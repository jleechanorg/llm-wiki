## Summary

When a party has only 1 member (the leader) and `removePlayer` is called, the party is now automatically disbanded instead of returning `false`. Leaders with other members are still protected from being removed.

## Root Cause

`removePlayer` in `PartyStore` had a blanket `if (member.role === 'leader') return false` guard that prevented the leader from ever being removed. When the leader was the only member, this left an orphaned single-member party that could never be cleaned up.

## Changes

- **`party_store.ts`**: Relaxed the leader-removal guard to only block removal when `party.members.length > 1`. When the leader is the last member, removal proceeds and the party is deleted from the in-memory map, the `partysByPlayer` lookup, and the SQLite DB.
- **`party_store.test.ts`**: Updated the "should not allow removing leader" test to cover the 2-member case. Added a new test "should disband party when single-member leader removes themselves" that verifies the party is fully removed and the player->party mapping is cleared.
