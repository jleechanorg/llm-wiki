## High-level description of changes
Adds `.github/workflows/skeptic-gate.yml` copied from the AO template at `packages/cli/src/templates/skeptic/skeptic-gate.yml`.

Flow:
1. GHA posts skeptic-gate-trigger comment on each PR push
2. AO lifecycle worker detects trigger, runs `ao skeptic verify`
3. AO posts `VERDICT: PASS` or `VERDICT: FAIL` comment
4. GHA polls (up to 28min), exits PASS/FAIL accordingly
