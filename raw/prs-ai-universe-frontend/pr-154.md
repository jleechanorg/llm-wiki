## Raw Body
**Problem:**
When clicking the "New Chat" button, the sidebar visually appeared to keep the topmost conversation selected (via keyboard focus ring), even though the active conversation was correctly cleared to null.

**Root Cause:**
In ConversationSidebar.tsx, the `focusedIndex` state (used for keyboard navigation) was only updated when `selectedConversationId` was truthy. When clicking "New Chat" cleared the selection to null, the focusedIndex remained at its previous value, causing the visual focus ring to persist.

**Solution:**
Added an else clause to reset `focusedIndex` to -1 when `selectedConversationId` becomes null/undefined, ensuring no conversation appears focused when starting a fresh chat.

**Additional Fixes:**
- Updated synthesis normalization tests to use correct field name `tokensUsed` instead of `tokens` (matching ModelOpinion interface)
- Added backward compatibility in normalizeModel to accept both field names for robustness
