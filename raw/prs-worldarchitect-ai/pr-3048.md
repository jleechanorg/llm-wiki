## Raw Body
Implements the optional Xanathar's Guide to Everything rule for going without a long rest. After 24 hours without a long rest, characters must make a DC 10 Constitution save or gain 1 level of exhaustion. The DC increases by 5 for each additional 24 hours without rest.

Changes:
- Added sleep deprivation Con save table with escalating DCs
- Added last_long_rest_world_time tracking in player resources
- Added proactive rest recommendation triggers for the LLM
- Added narrative integration guidance for fatigue descriptions
