## Summary
Follow-up to #5110 to address reviewer feedback about INFO-level guards still evaluating expensive payload formatting under default logging.

### Changes
- `mvp_site/world_logic.py`
  - In `_handle_set_command`, moved heavy payload/state dump logs from INFO to DEBUG.
  - Cached `debug_enabled` once and reused it for all expensive log guards.
  - Kept lightweight INFO markers (`received`, `raw payload`, `complete`) intact.
- `mvp_site/firestore_service.py`
  - In `update_campaign_game_state`, moved final full-state dump log from INFO to DEBUG.
  - Kept lightweight success INFO log intact.

## Why
`logging_util.setup_unified_logging()` sets root level to INFO by default, so INFO guards do not prevent expensive `_truncate_log_json(...)`/formatting costs. DEBUG guards ensure those costs are skipped unless explicitly debugging.

## Validation
- `python3.11 -m py_compile mvp_site/firestore_service.py mvp_site/world_logic.py`

  - Result: `193 passed, 23 subtests passed`

## Scope
No business-logic changes. Logging level/guard behavior only for heavy serialization logs.
