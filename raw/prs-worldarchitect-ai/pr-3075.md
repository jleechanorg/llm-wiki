## Raw Body
The Xanathar's optional rule for staying awake was being incorrectly applied during long rest actions. The save only applies when a character has been awake 24+ hours AND chooses to continue staying awake.

Taking a long rest means the character IS resting (sleeping), so:
- No exhaustion save should be rolled
- Exhaustion level decreases by 1 (the normal long rest benefit)

Added clear anti-pattern example and correct behavior to prevent this misinterpretation by the LLM.
