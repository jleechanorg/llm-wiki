|----------|--------|-------|

## Context
Investigation of Jan 9 Codex quota spike (81 conversations vs 17 avg) found that Gemini failures were falling back to Codex, consuming significant quota. By switching fallback to cursor-agent, we preserve Codex quota for the dedicated workflow.
