## Changes

## Bug Details
- **Location**: mvp_site/agent_prompts.py line 2512
- **Issue**: With MAX=0, 1 correction attempt was falsely saying "fix" instead of "exceeded"
- **Root cause**: `max(1, max_attempts)` made effective_max=1 even when max_attempts=0
