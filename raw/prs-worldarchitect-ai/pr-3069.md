## Raw Body
## Summary

Implements a **sanctuary mode** that provides temporary protection from life-ending events after completing major narrative arcs or questlines.

### Key Features

- **Automatic Activation**: Triggers when a major arc is completed (quest chain finale, story chapter, campaign milestone)
- **Duration**: Approximately 21 turns (~1 week in-game time)
- **Protection Scope**: Blocks lethal complications, ambushes, faction attacks, and assassination attempts
- **Passive Activities Allowed**: Companion conversations, planning, shopping, training, peaceful exploration
- **Breaking Conditions**: Player-initiated major aggression (attacking factions, declaring war, raiding strongholds) breaks sanctuary
- **Player Notifications**: Clear narrative indicators on activation, expiration, and breaking

### Implementation Details

- **GameState methods**: `activate_sanctuary_mode()`, `is_sanctuary_active()`, `break_sanctuary_mode()`, `get_sanctuary_status()`, `get_sanctuary_turns_remaining()`
- **Constants**: `SANCTUARY_MODE_DURATION_TURNS`, `SANCTUARY_BREAKING_ACTIONS`, `SANCTUARY_SAFE_ACTIONS`
- **Living World Instruction**: Full sanctuary protocol section with restrictions, allowed activities, and state delta format
- **Narrative System**: Sanctuary triggering protocol and LLM context integration
- **Agent Prompts**: `build_sanctuary_status()` method for including sanctuary status in LLM context

### Bug Fix

- Fixes duplicate else block syntax error in narrative_response_schema.py
