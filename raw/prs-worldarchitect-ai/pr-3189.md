## Changes:
- DC now scales based on knowledge familiarity:
  - DC 2-5: Things character should know (class abilities, party members)
  - DC 6-8: Easy to remember (recent events, basic geography)
  - DC 9-12: Requires thought (tactical assessment)
  - DC 13-16: Complicated planning (multi-step plans)
  - DC 17-20: Elaborate strategies (heist planning)
  - DC 21-25: Mastermind-level (multiple contingencies)

- DC always explained in narrative with category and reasoning
- Quality tiers now based on success/failure margin vs DC
- Added "Confused" tier for severe failures (failed by 10+)

## Plan Freeze Mechanic:
- Failed planning checks lock that topic for N hours
- Freeze duration scales with DC:
  - DC 2-8: 1 hour
  - DC 9-12: 2 hours
  - DC 13-16: 4 hours
  - DC 17-20: 8 hours
  - DC 21+: 24 hours
- Freeze can be broken by new information or different approach
