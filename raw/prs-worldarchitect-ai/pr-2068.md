Resolves bug where XP progress displayed incorrectly (e.g., "1775/14000" instead of "1775/9000" for level 6→7).

- Updated post-combat report section to reference table and show correct calculation

- Before: Level 6 character shows "1775/14000" (incorrect - 14,000 is total to reach level 6)
- After: Level 6 character shows "1775/9000" (correct - 9,000 XP needed from 6→7)
