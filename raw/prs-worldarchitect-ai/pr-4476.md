## Summary
Expanded character creation instructions with detailed equipment scaling guidelines by level and background, plus canonical JSON field mappings for equipment and spells storage.

## Changes
- Added **Starting Equipment Guidelines - Level & Background Scaling** section with:
  - Level-based equipment tiers (1, 2-3, 4-6)
  - Background-based quality modifiers (Poor, Standard, Wealthy)
  - Specific gold piece ranges and item counts per tier
  - Equipment scaling rules and examples by archetype
  
- Added **Equipment & Spells: JSON Field Mapping** section with:
  - Canonical `equipment` object format with slot-based structure (armor, shield, main_hand, off_hand, jewelry, backpack, etc.)
  - Alternative simplified `inventory` array format for backward compatibility
  - Canonical `spells_known` array format with spell objects including level and ritual flags
  - Alternative spell formats (`cantrips`, `spells`, `spells_prepared`, `spell_slots`) for backward compatibility
  - Class-specific spell rules (Wizard vs Sorcerer vs Warlock differences)
  - **Critical rule**: Respect pre-specified equipment from campaign templates and god mode characters

## Benefits
- **Consistency**: Provides clear, enforceable rules for equipment distribution across all character levels and backgrounds
- **Fairness**: Ensures poor backgrounds aren't overpowered at low levels while allowing progression at higher levels
- **Clarity**: Explicit JSON field mappings eliminate ambiguity about where to store equipment and spells
- **Backward Compatibility**: Supports both canonical and alternative formats for existing systems
- **Template Respect**: Critical rule prevents overwriting intentional equipment specifications from campaign creators
