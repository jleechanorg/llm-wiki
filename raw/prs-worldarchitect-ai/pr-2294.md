## Problem
Campaign Mwkd1WEFKuV5YufFwFNw with 100 story entries (~118k tokens) failed with Context too large error despite truncation being applied.

**Root cause:** PR #2284 added 20% output reserve in validation (_get_safe_output_token_limit) but didnt update truncation to match.

| Component | Before | After |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Truncation reserve | Fixed 12k tokens | **20% ratio** (~23.6k) |
| Validation reserve | 20% ratio (~23.6k) | 20% ratio |
| **Gap** | **11.6k tokens** | **0** |

Content passed truncation but failed validation due to this 11.6k token gap.

## Solution
Centralized _calculate_context_budget() function ensures both truncation and validation use identical logic.
