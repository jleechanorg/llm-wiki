## Problem
Campaign `kuXKa6vrYY6P99MfhWBn` had 33MB of data with 30MB (88.8%) being `debug_info`.

The bloat came from storing full LLM context in every story entry:
- `raw_request_payload`: ~20KB per entry
- `system_instruction_text`: ~8KB per entry
- `raw_response_text`: ~8KB per entry

## Solution
- **Remove** these fields from `debug_info` storage
- **Keep** `system_instruction_files` (list of filenames) and `system_instruction_char_count`
- **Log** the full values at DEBUG level instead for troubleshooting

## Expected Impact
- Per AI entry: ~37KB → ~1KB
- New campaigns will have ~97% smaller `debug_info`
- Full request/response still available in Cloud Logging
