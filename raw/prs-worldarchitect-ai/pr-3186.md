## Raw Body
Add comprehensive D&D 5e combat statistics to the stats display, similar to Baldur's Gate 3:

- Proficiency bonus (based on character level)
- Initiative (DEX modifier)
- Movement speed
- Spell Save DC (8 + proficiency + spellcasting mod)
- Spell Attack bonus (proficiency + spellcasting mod)
- Weapon attack/damage with proper modifier calculations
  - Finesse weapons use higher of STR/DEX
  - Ranged weapons use DEX
  - Melee weapons use STR

Added helper functions:
- get_proficiency_bonus(): Level-based proficiency lookup
- get_spellcasting_ability(): Class-based spellcasting stat
- extract_equipped_weapons(): Parse equipped weapon data
- SPELLCASTING_ABILITY_MAP: Class to ability mapping

Closes #combat-stats
