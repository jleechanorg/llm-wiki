## Summary
- **Root cause**: `_inject_levelup_narrative_if_needed` gated only on raw `rewards_pending.level_up_available`, while `_inject_levelup_choices_if_needed` uses `_resolve_level_up_signal` (including stale guards: explicit `level_up_in_progress=False`, `level_up_pending=False`). That split produced **LEVEL UP narrative / indicator messaging without `level_up_now` / `continue_adventuring` in the planning block** when stale modal flags suppressed the canonical signal but leftover `rewards_pending` remained.
- **Fix**: Gate narrative injection on `_resolve_level_up_signal`, pass through optional `rewards_box` (same as choice injection), and derive `new_level` from `rewards_pending` or `resolved_new_level`.
