## Changes

- **`ralph/ralph-pair.sh`** (new) — ralph with deterministic verification:
  - After coder phase, runs `verifyCommand` for each unpassed story
  - Auto-sets `passes: true` when verifyCommand succeeds
  - When verify story VN passes, also marks paired implement story SN
  - Only exits when ALL stories verified
  - 100% deterministic — no extra LLM calls for verification

- **`ralph/CLAUDE.md`** — changed from 'work on ONE story per iteration' to 'use your judgement for how many stories to tackle per iteration'
