## Summary

- Adds an LLM-driven Custom Campaign Wizard as an alternative to the existing "paste your prompt" campaign creation flow
- Custom Campaign now appears first (left card, selected by default), Dragon Knight second (right card)
- **One-click launch**: All three campaign modes (Dragon Knight, Guided Wizard, Paste Your Prompt) launch immediately when selected ‚Äî no multi-step navigation needed
- New `CampaignWizardAgent` routes wizard rounds through the agent priority chain at position 1b (after GodMode, before CharacterCreation)

## One-Click Campaign Launch

Each campaign option is now a single click:

| Option | Click Action | Backend Behavior |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Dragon Knight** | Click card ‚Üí instant launch | Uses DK defaults (Ser Arion, World of Assiah, companions, defaultWorld) |
| **Custom + Guided Wizard** | Click radio ‚Üí instant launch | Sets `campaign_wizard_mode="wizard"`, wizard agent presents 7 planning block rounds |
| **Custom + Paste Your Prompt** | Click radio ‚Üí instant launch | Empty fields trigger random character/setting generation |

## The 7 Wizard Rounds

The wizard walks players through 7 hardcoded rounds in fixed order. Each round presents LLM-generated contextual choices as planning blocks. Every round includes a "Let AI pick the rest for me" escape hatch.

| Round | Key | Description |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| 1 | **Setting** | Genre/era (Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Modern, Historical, Post-Apocalyptic, Steampunk) |
| 2 | **Area or IP** | Specific world/universe/IP based on chosen setting (e.g., Forgotten Realms, Star Wars, or "Original World") |
| 3 | **Gender** | Character gender (Male, Female, Non-binary, "Surprise Me") |
| 4 | **Name** | 3-4 thematic names fitting Setting + Gender + Area/IP, plus "Custom Name" |
