Extends D&D 5e character stats with additional combat and character info:

New stats display:
- Hit Dice: Shows class hit die (d6-d12) with current/max tracking
  - Barbarian: d12, Fighter/Paladin/Ranger: d10
  - Most classes: d8, Wizard/Sorcerer: d6
- Unarmed Strike: Attack and damage for punches (everyone can fight!)
  - Monks get improved 1d4 damage dice
  - Properly handles negative STR modifiers with parentheses
- Proficiencies: Armor, Weapon, Tool, and Language proficiencies
- Damage Defenses: Resistances, Immunities, Vulnerabilities
- Senses: Darkvision and other special senses

Implementation:
- stats_display.py:
  - get_hit_dice(): Maps classes to hit dice (HIT_DICE_BY_CLASS)
  - calculate_unarmed_strike(): STR mod + proficiency, monks get 1d4
  - Extended build_stats_summary() with new sections
- main.py: Updated /api/campaigns/{id}/stats endpoint with new data
- frontend_v1/app.js: Added hit dice badge to stats panel

Tests:
- Added comprehensive test coverage for all new functions
- Tests for hit dice by class, multiclass, level clamping
- Tests for unarmed strike with monks and negative modifiers
- Tests for proficiencies, resistances, and darkvision display
