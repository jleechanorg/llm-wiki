## Raw Body
This reverts commit 8257621cd926b18552e98515589d3dac03af9037.

The item spawning prevention in scan_narrative_for_unlisted_items() was too aggressive - it detected items mentioned in story context (like "control gem" as a quest objective) and incorrectly blocked with rejection message even when no item was being used.

The false positive was firing after successful spells, replacing valid LLM narratives with "You reach for the gem, but realize you don't have one."

The feature needs redesign to distinguish:
- Items actively USED in current action
- Items merely MENTIONED in narrative context (goals, plans, lore)
