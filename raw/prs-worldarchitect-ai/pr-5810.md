> **Overview**
> **Prevents orchestration worktree disk bloat** by changing `TaskDispatcher._get_worktree_base_path()` to default under `tempfile.gettempdir()` (`.../orch_worktrees/orch_{repo}/`) instead of `~/projects`, with an `ORCHESTRATION_WORKTREE_BASE` override for persistent storage.
> 
> Adds `scripts/disk_cleanup.sh`, a *dry-run by default* cleanup utility (optional `--apply`) that prunes common stale worktree/cache directories (age-gated, 14+ days) across orchestration, project dirs, actions-runner snapshots, and selected dev caches.
> 
> Documents the incident and cleanup approach via new `docs/disk_cleanup_methodology.md` and `docs/system_disk_breakdown_2026-03-01.md`.
