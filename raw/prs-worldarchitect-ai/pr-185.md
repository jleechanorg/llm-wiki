## Summary

## Changes

- Added complete character dossier for Alexiel including:
  - Full psychological profile with detailed personality analysis
  - Backstory covering her creation by Lucifer and defection to the Imperium
  - Secret three-layer power system (Null status, hidden magic, Nullification Field)
  - Complete mechanical character sheet with stats and abilities

- Added complete character dossier for Prince Artorius val Raziel including:
  - Personality profile as the idealistic "People's Prince"
  - Backstory of his conflict with his pragmatic father Raziel
  - Paladin mechanics with Celestial Wings and combat abilities
  - Full psychological deep dive analyzing his heroic nature

## Benefits

- **Fills critical historical gaps**: These characters are frequently referenced throughout the existing documentation but lacked their own detailed profiles
- **Family context**: Properly positions the parents' dossiers before their children (Cassian, Valerius, Sariel) in the Celestial Imperium section
- **Narrative consistency**: Provides definitive psychological and mechanical details for characters central to the world's recent history
- **Campaign utility**: DMs now have complete references for these important historical figures
