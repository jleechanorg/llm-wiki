> [!NOTE]
> Centralizes world-time handling in a new module with ISO/UTC parsing and month normalization, integrates it into the story loop to enforce forward progression, and updates prompts/tests accordingly.
> 
> - **Core (`mvp_site/world_time.py`)**:
>   - New centralized world-time utilities: `_safe_int`, month normalization (strips punctuation), ISO timestamp parsing to UTC, `world_time_to_comparable`, `check_temporal_violation`, `apply_timestamp_to_world_time`, `ensure_progressive_world_time`, and prompt-friendly formatter.
> - **Engine Integration (`mvp_site/world_logic.py`)**:
>   - Replaces inline temporal helpers with imports from `world_time`.
>   - Applies timestamp→`world_time` conversion and normalization in the action loop.
>   - Adjusts temporal warning logic to surface a warning even when retries are disabled.
> - **Prompts/Docs (`mvp_site/prompts/game_state_instruction.md`)**:
>   - Instruct LLMs to include `world_data.timestamp_iso`; backend derives structured `world_time` and preserves timeline if omitted.
> - **Tests**:
>   - Add coverage for ISO parsing (with timezone to UTC), extraction from `timestamp_iso`, auto-populating `world_time`, microsecond normalization, and tolerant month parsing (e.g., `Kythorn.`).
