### Dragon's Favor System (Turns 1-50)
- **Dragon's Whisper (25% HP):** Dragon offers rescue telepathically
- **Dragon's Rescue (0 HP):** Automatic intervention saves you
- **Dragon's Call (Turn 51+):** Dragons seek you out if no prior contact
- Alignment determines which dragon: Aurum (virtue), Umbrax (ruthless), both (neutral)

### Champion's Inheritance (NEW)
- **Trigger:** Save refugees WITHOUT dragon help
- **Reward:** +3 good-aligned gear in every slot from Alexiel's Cache
- Lady Annalise reveals family connection and leads to hidden vault
- 9 legendary items: Dawnbreaker, Aegis, Plate of Unbroken Oath, Crown of Clarity, Mantle, Signet, Striders, Grips, Heart of the Faithful
- Represents the "third path" of true independence

- Hidden in Ivory Spire, greater psychic potential than Sariel
