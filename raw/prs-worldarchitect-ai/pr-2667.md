## Agent Selection Priority
1. GodModeAgent (explicit override)
2. CombatAgent (in_combat = true)
3. RewardsAgent (rewards pending from combat OR encounter OR narrative victory)
4. StoryModeAgent (default)

## RewardsAgent Triggers
- `combat_phase == "ended"` AND `combat_summary` exists
- `encounter_state.encounter_completed == true`
- `rewards_pending` exists in game_state
- **Narrative victory detected in DM notes or story outcome**

## Narrative Victory Detection (NEW)
Detects rewards needed when:
- DM notes mention "defeated", "killed", "destroyed", "deleted", "dominated"
- Story describes enemy neutralization without formal combat
- Spell effects resolve enemy (Dominate Monster, Power Word Kill, etc.)

This enables automatic XP rewards for ALL victory types including:
- Spell-based defeats (Dominate Monster on Planar Auditor)
- Story-choice victories (hostile takeover, soul harvesting)
- Social manipulation wins (persuasion, deception)
- Stealth/heist comple
