## Raw Body
When XP is awarded outside the normal rewards pipeline (e.g., God Mode, narrative milestones), level-up detection was not triggered because RewardsAgent only activates when specific state conditions exist:
- combat_phase == "ended" with combat_summary
- encounter_completed with encounter_summary
- explicit rewards_pending

This fix adds _check_and_set_level_up_pending() that runs for ALL modes after state updates. It compares stored level vs expected level from XP and sets rewards_pending with level_up_available=True when level-up is available, triggering RewardsAgent to offer the level-up to the player.

Root cause discovered in Undertale campaign where player had 8006 XP (Level 5 threshold is 6500) but level remained at 4 until manually requested in God Mode.
