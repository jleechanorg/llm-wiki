> [!NOTE]
> **High Risk**
> Touches authentication enforcement and bypass paths; mistakes could either block legitimate traffic or re-enable unintended bypasses in production. Startup fail-fast based on environment variables can also cause production outages if configs are mis-set.
> 
> **Overview**
> Adds a startup guard (`_validate_production_environment`) that **crashes the app on boot** when `ENVIRONMENT=stable` and any test auth-bypass flags (`TESTING_AUTH_BYPASS`, `ALLOW_TEST_AUTH_BYPASS`, `SKIP_AUTH`) are enabled.
> 
> Hardens `check_token` to *fail-closed* in `PRODUCTION_MODE=true` by blocking header/query bypass mechanisms, and fixes the `SMOKE_TOKEN` path to be **preview-only** (restricted to `/mcp` and the exact stream route matcher).
> 
> Updates/extends tests to cover the new stable-environment validation and to assert bypass headers are rejected in production mode; adds a Ruff per-file ignore entry for `test_main_enhancements.py`.
