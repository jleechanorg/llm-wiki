## Raw Body
### Background

Deploy has been failing repeatedly because the orphan-kill step in Stage 4 kills the **staging** gateway (port 18810) along with prod orphans. This triggers a launchd KeepAlive restart loop on staging, which appears dead during the monitor-agent health check — causing a 5+ minute hang and deploy failure.

### Root Cause

 in Stage 4 indiscriminately kills ALL gateway processes, including the staging gateway. Since staging's KeepAlive immediately respawns it, the orphan check sees it as a new orphan on the next iteration, kills it again, and the loop starves the monitor-agent check.

### Fixes

1. **Orphan kill protects staging** (): lsof identifies PIDs listening on port 18810 — those are launchd-managed and excluded from the orphan kill.
2. **Startup wait increased**: Stage 1: 20s→35s, Stage 4: 20s→45s. Measured: gateway takes 30-40s to bind and respond after restart.
3. **Single-instance check uses port filter** (): switched from  (counts staging too → always 2) to  (counts only prod port listener).
