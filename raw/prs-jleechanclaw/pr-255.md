> **Overview**
> Adds a new **Pass 3.5 merge-gate enforcement** to `ao-backfill.sh` that scans all open PRs and, when criteria aren’t met (CI, mergeable, unresolved threads, CodeRabbit state, evidence PASS when relevant, OpenClaw approval), posts a `**MERGE BLOCKED**` comment and a `CHANGES_REQUESTED` review (rate-limited and auto-dismissed once compliant).
> 
> Tightens auto-merge gating by treating `NEUTRAL`/`SKIPPED` checks as passing, failing closed when CodeRabbit hasn’t reviewed, and making the evidence PASS regex require `evidence`/`/er` context; also makes timestamp parsing portable across BSD/GNU `date`.
> 
> Replaces ad-hoc merge hard-gates in `action_executor.py` with a new `orchestration.merge_gate.check_merge_ready()` module (plus tests) that centralizes merge readiness checks, including filtering inline comments to *unresolved* threads and classifying only structured
