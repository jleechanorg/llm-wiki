> **Overview**
> Automatically triggers GitHub review-thread auto-resolution after fix pushes and merges to unblock PRs with stale “changes requested” threads.
> 
> `RetryAction` now carries an optional `pr_url` from escalation events, and `action_executor` parses that URL to call `auto_resolve_threads_for_pr` after a successful retry send; `MergeAction` similarly calls auto-resolve after a successful merge. Both flows record the resolved/skipped counts in action details and treat resolve failures as non-fatal.
