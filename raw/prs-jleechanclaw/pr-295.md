## Raw Body
Replaces broken body>0 heuristic with paired-review temporal detection. Data from 9+ PRs shows CR never posts APPROVED with body>0. New approach: genuine = COMMENTED(body>0) within 10s before APPROVED, or first/only review. Fake = orphan APPROVED after CHANGES_REQUESTED. Supersedes closed PR #292.
