The silence stretched, broken only by the soft crackle of the fire in the hearth. Nocturne met his unwavering gaze without a hint of concession. She allowed her own presence, an unyielding, chilling force, to fill the space between them. She was not a petitioner; she was a bearer of truth, a deliverer of vital, terrifying intelligence.
