### Detailed Family History: Gaia Julia Caesar (82 BCE Context)

In this divergent timeline of **The Pax Julia**, your family history is the intersection of divine myth and brutal revolutionary politics. At age 16, you are the focal point of a bloodline that Sulla intends to extinguish.

#### 1. The Divine Origin: The Gens Julia
Your family, the **Gens Julia**, is one of the oldest **Patrician** (noble/aristocratic) clans in Rome. You claim direct descent from **Iulus** (Ascanius), the son of the Trojan hero **Aeneas**, who was himself the son of the goddess **Venus**. 
*   **The Miasma Connection:** In this world, the Julian descent from Venus is not a metaphorical claim to prestige; it is the source of the **Fated Miasma**. This 'Stolen Fire' is a biological and psychic inheritance that allows Julians to perceive and manipulate alternate timelines, a power that manifests as your **Auctoritas**.

#### 2. The Political Pivot: The Marian Alliance
While the Julians are ancient, they were politically faded until your aunt, **Julia**, married **Gaius Marius**. 
*   **Gaius Marius (Your Uncle):** A 'Novus Homo' (New Man) who served as Consul an unprecedented seven times. He reformed the Roman legions into a professional force and became the champion of the **Populares** (the faction favoring the rights of the common people over the Senate).
*   **The Inheritance:** Marius's death left a power vacuum. You are viewed by the **Marian Remnants** (like Decimus and his Tenth Legion veterans) as the spiritual successor to Marius—the 'niece' who carries both the Julian divine right and the Marian revolutionary fire.

#### 3. The Current Crisis: The Sullan Proscriptions
In this year (**82 BCE**), the civil war has turned against your family. **Lucius Cornelius Sulla** has entered Rome as Dictator and issued the **Proscriptio** (Proscriptions).
*   **Proscriptio:** These are official lists of 'enemies of the state.' Anyone on the list is stripped of citizenship, their property is seized, and they can be killed by anyone for a reward. 
*   **The Julian Hunt:** Sulla fears the Miasma. He views the Julians as a 'cancer of change' and has marked your entire branch of the family for death. Your contemporary and kinsman, the young **Gaius Julius Caesar** (the historical Caesar), is also currently a fugitive for refusing to divorce his wife, Cornelia (daughter of another Marian leader).

#### 4. The Future Path: The Pax Julia
Your family history is currently a story of 'The Hunted,' but your visions show a future where the Julii Ptolemaic dynasty rules the sun and the stars. You are the 'Unbroken Colt' of the **Eagle's Brood**, destined to bridge the gap between the iron discipline of Rome and the ancient mysticism of Egypt (through the future alliance with Cleopatra).

**Key Cultural Terms Defined:**
*   **Gens:** A Roman clan or group of families sharing a common name and claiming descent from a common ancestor.
*   **Patrician:** The elite class of Roman families who originally held all political and religious power.
*   **Novus Homo:** 'New Man' — the first man in his family to be elected to the Roman consulship.
*   **Populares:** A political faction that relied on the people's assemblies and tribunes to challenge the traditional power of the Senate.
*   **Optimates:** The conservative 'Best Men' faction (led by Sulla) who sought to maintain the power of the Senate and traditional aristocratic privilege.
