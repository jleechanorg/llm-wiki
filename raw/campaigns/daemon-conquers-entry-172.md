The interior of Blackwood Keep, while grand, felt small and somewhat muted compared to the Mayor's sprawling estate in Silverport, or even Riverbend's more robust fortifications. As they moved towards the great hall, Daemon's sharp eyes took in the details: the quality of the tapestries, the number of guards, the general state of maintenance.
