Game Master:

Understood. Itachi Uchiha has reached **Level 6**. This marks another significant advancement in his path towards ultimate control and power.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 6:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 6
    *   **HP Gain:** 6 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 8 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 43 to **51 HP**. He is now at 51/51 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 6 Ninja (Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack + Illusion Wizard), Itachi gains significant advancements:

    1.  **Expertise (Choice):** Itachi's understanding of various fields deepens further. He gains **two** additional Expertise Tags. He can choose to:
        *   **Option A:** Choose two entirely new Expertise Tags (e.g., Deception, Acrobatics, Persuasion, Intimidation, Medicine, Survival, etc.).
        *   **Option B:** Choose one existing Expertise Tag (like Investigation, which he currently has at Advantage) to gain **Super-Advantage** in (roll 3d20, take the highest result), and one new Expertise Tag.
        *   **(Note: Stealth already has Super-Advantage from Level 2 Expertise.)**

    2.  **Malleable Illusions:** Itachi's mastery over the very fabric of illusion becomes profound. When he casts an illusion spell that has a duration of 1 minute or longer, he can now use a **Secondary Action (1 CP)** to change the nature of that illusion (e.g., altering its appearance, sounds, or other sensory details within the spell's original parameters), provided that he can see the illusion. This allows for dynamic, adaptable deceptions.

Please let me know your choice for Itachi's Level 6 Expertise.
