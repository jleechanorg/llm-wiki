His eyes dart nervously, not at his screen, but at the surging tide of students, and then, almost involuntarily, they flick towards Sariel. Sariel closes the distance, her movements precise, drawing minimal additional attention. Her voice, low and calm, cuts through the din, acknowledging the unspoken without directly addressing it.

He offers a quick, almost desperate nod, before subtly slipping away into the thinning crowd, his movements faster, more furtive than before.
