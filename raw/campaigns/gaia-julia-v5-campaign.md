## Summary

Gaia Julia Caesar is the daughter of the God-Emperor, stripped of her divine right and cast into the filth of the Third Servile War (73 BCE). Her "Siren" powers -- the genetic gift of Venus -- lie dormant due to poison, trauma, or a mystic's curse. She has been reduced to Level 1 Bard, torn stola, bruised skin, forced to rely on raw wits in the pleasure tents of rebel-held countryside. This is "The Gilded Trap" -- a fall from grace survival horror campaign.
