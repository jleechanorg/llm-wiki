Location: Hidden outside Man Tibbet's Farm, Early Evening

The promise of a "large family" at Man Tibbet's farm drew Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) forward, a cold, calculating drive for efficiency guiding her. The initial, vague information from the terrified villager was now augmented by her own keen observations: a subtle thrum beneath the ground near the barn, a glint from a suspicious slit in the farmhouse window. This was more than a simple farm; it was a guarded position. The complexity merely sharpened her focus. Information was paramount before action.

She settled into a deep shadow at the edge of the copse, her Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate merging with the deepening gloom. Her eyes, pools of intense concentration, fixed on the farmhouse. With a soft mental exertion, she channeled her formidable intellect (Intelligence 18), expending a portion of her inner energy for a deeper, more invasive **Apex Senses** scan. Her mind became a non-physical probe, dissecting the mundane reality around her, seeking out the hidden truths.

The walls of the farmhouse dissolved into a schematic of heat signatures and faint mental presences. Inside the main farmhouse, she detected a cluster of several individuals – the "large family" – mostly adults, with the smaller, more agitated energy of children. They were gathered, likely preparing for the evening meal, their concerns mundane.

But then, her focus sharpened on the second floor. Two distinct, stronger presences. They were armed, their forms radiating a disciplined, vigilant stillness. One was positioned near the narrow embrasure she had spotted, a silent sentinel peering out into the darkness. Their equipment, though not fully discernible in detail, felt professional – not the rusty tools of farmers. These were not local militia, but trained individuals, perhaps mercenaries, or a private security force. Their presence confirmed her suspicion: Tibbet's farm was being actively protected.

The scan extended to the barn. The low, resonant thrum she had felt was indeed a **magical ward**, subtly woven into the barn's foundation. It pulsed with a faint arcane signature, designed not to harm, but to detect. Its energy felt linked, subtly, to the two guards within the farmhouse, suggesting a coordinated defensive system. It was likely a perimeter alarm. This explained the presence of the earlier trained mastiffs; they were simply the outer layer of a more sophisticated defense.

A flicker of cold satisfaction, almost a grim amusement, touched Nocturne's lips. Kaelen's "easy pickings" were proving to be anything but. This was no simple abduction; it was a raid on a defended position. The information was invaluable. The presence of these professional guards, and the arcane ward, meant the road patrol she had detected earlier was almost certainly connected, forming a larger, coordinated defense network around Oakhaven, perhaps centered on this very farm. The Underdark buyers were clearly investing more than simple coin in their "stock."

She had 2 uses of Apex Senses remaining. Her EP was now 21/36 (5 EP spent for Apex Senses).
