Location: Hidden outside Widow Elara's Cottage, Early Evening

Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) focused her gaze on the humble cottage, her eyes now pools of intense concentration. The faint light from the window, the wisp of smoke from the chimney, the gentle gurgle of the creek—these mundane details dissolved as her will sharpened. She expended a portion of her internal energy, channeling her formidable intellect (Intelligence 18) into a non-physical probing, an **Apex Senses Deep Scan** (1 use expended). Her mind became a conduit, stretching beyond the physical, mapping the interior with chilling clarity.

A single occupant. An elderly woman, likely Widow Elara herself, sat hunched by the hearth, a worn blanket draped over her shoulders. The flickering firelight cast long, dancing shadows, making her appear frail and solitary. There were no other heat signatures, no hidden rooms, no signs of unexpected visitors or hidden alarm mechanisms. The cottage was small, a single main room serving as living space and sleeping area, with a tiny, attached pantry. The furniture was sparse but well-maintained: a wooden table, two chairs, a simple bed. There was no resistance, no hidden strength, only quiet vulnerability.

Nocturne's senses then extended to the immediate exterior. The soft earth around the cottage was undisturbed, save for the faint, worn path leading to the front. The thicket where she hid offered excellent, impenetrable cover right up to the back wall. The wind, still gentle, carried the scent of woodsmoke and old linens. The sounds were those of a quiet countryside evening: crickets, a distant owl, the murmur of the creek. The disciplined patrol on the main road, though still a calculated awareness, remained a quarter-mile distant, its presence faint and methodical. They had not been alerted by the earlier commotion, or perhaps simply had not yet approached close enough to investigate.

The scan confirmed everything her pragmatic mind had anticipated. This was a low-risk, high-efficiency target. The 'stock' was present and isolated. The environment offered ample concealment for the approach and extraction. The operation could be swift, quiet, and clean. The fleeting irritation from the earlier mastiff incident faded, replaced by the cool satisfaction of a variable perfectly controlled. The path to wealth, unburdened by unnecessary complications, lay open.
