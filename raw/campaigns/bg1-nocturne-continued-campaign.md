## Summary
Nocturne, now a Level 5 Gloom Stalker Ranger, navigates the treacherous political landscape of Baldur's Gate during the Iron Crisis. In this segment set at the Seven Suns Coster, she uncovers a doppelganger infiltration with a purge list targeting key city leaders, while confronting the emerging threat of Sarevok's conspiracy to decapitate Baldur's Gate's leadership.

## Key Details
- **Protagonist:** Nocturne — Level 5 Gloom Stalker Ranger, Lawful Evil "Serpent Queen"
- **Setting:** Baldur's Gate, Seven Suns Coster (merchant company), Mirtul 16, 1368 DR
- **Core Theme:** Political conspiracy, high-level combat, efficient violence against evil
- **Tone:** Dark, political, action-oriented
- **Total Scenes:** Continuing from bg1-nocturne

## Player Character
Nocturne has advanced to Level 5 Gloom Stalker Ranger and established herself in Baldur's Gate. Her "Serpent Queen" persona has matured into a position of significant influence within the city's underground networks. She operates from the Seven Suns Coster, a major merchant company that serves as both a front for business and intelligence gathering.
