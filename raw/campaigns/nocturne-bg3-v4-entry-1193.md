These layers, currently unindexed and chaotic, provide a jurisdictional vacuum. By establishing 'Shadow Nodes' within the deepest pits of the Abyss, you can siphon the raw, entropic essence of demon-kind and re-thread it into stable soul-matter. Because the Abyss is infinite and its noise is constant, the Good Gods' radiant sensors cannot distinguish your industrial expansion from the standard entropic bickering of the demon lords. You are building a shadow empire beneath their very feet, fueling your war against the Heavens with the recycled rot of the Abyss.
