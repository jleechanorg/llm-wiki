## Summary

Alexiel Assiah is a high-stakes D&D 5e campaign set in the world of Assiah (Year 78 DR - Alexiel Era). The player controls Alexiel, the biological "daughter" of Lucifer who was designed to be the ultimate weapon of the rebellion. Alexiel is a Null - a being without magic publicly, but secretly a magical prodigy who can manifest a Nullification Field. After the massacre at Silverwood violated her moral boundaries, she has abandoned the Unchained Host and now stands at the Imperial border, hunted.

## Key Claims

- Alexiel is Level 6 Gestalt (custom class blending Null and Prodigy abilities)
- The world is fractured: Celestial Imperium to the east, Unchained Host to the west
- The Celestial Imperium is ruled by Lord Regent Raziel from Aeterna
- The Unchained Host is a multi-species army (orcs, beast-kin, fallen angels) led by Lucifer
- The Thousand Year War has reached a stalemate
- Alexiel's Nullification Field can level the playing field against celestial beings
- She was the Sword of the Host until Silverwood massacre made her defect
- Currently at Dragon's Tooth Foothills near Fortress Vigil and Imperial border
