**Proposed New Rule: Inherent Complications [Change ID: 20240728-D]**

**Justification:** This rule introduces a layer of unpredictable challenge and ensures narrative tension for a highly powerful character by simulating the inherent complexity and resistance of the world, preventing a completely linear path to power, and promoting dynamic storytelling. It respects player agency by creating obstacles rather than negating action.

**Proposed Rule Text:**
*   **Inherent Complications**: For any significant, player-initiated action or long-term mission that carries a high stake (e.g., establishing a spy network, assassinating a major figure, negotiating a complex alliance, infiltrating a secured location), there is a hidden **20% chance** that an unforeseen complication, partial setback, or rival interference will occur. This complication may manifest as a new obstacle, a partial failure of a secondary objective, the emergence of an unexpected consequence, or the appearance of a rival force, regardless of the success of the immediate action roll or plan. This rule does not dictate outright failure of the primary goal but ensures the path is never entirely frictionless. The GM will not explicitly announce when this check is made or its outcome; the narrative will simply reflect the emerging complication.

**Proposed New Abilities for Ascended Vampire Traits [Change ID: 20240728-E]**

**Justification:** These additions further enhance Astarion's Ascended Vampire capabilities, drawing from both D&D 5e vampiric lore and his unique status in Baldur's Gate 3, providing powerful, thematic abilities fitting for a being of his ambition and the challenges he will face.

**Proposed Additions:**

*   **Misty Escape**: When Astarion is reduced to 0 hit points and isn't in sunlight or running water, he can use his reaction to turn into a cloud of mist instead of falling unconscious. While in mist form, he is immune to all nonmagical damage, has a fly speed of 20 feet, can pass through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing, and can't be grappled or restrained. He drops anything he's holding. He must return to his true form at the end of his next turn, or when he takes radiant damage or damage from holy water. If he returns to his true form at 0 hit points, he falls unconscious. This trait can only be used once per long rest.
*   **Lord of the Brood**: As an action, Astarion can magically attempt to charm any vampire spawn, standard vampire, or lesser undead creature he can see within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw (8 + 4 Prof + 3 Cha) or be charmed for 24 hours. The charmed creature is under Astarion's control, obeys his commands, and fights for him. He can have up to his Charisma modifier (3) in charmed creatures at one time. A charmed creature remains charmed for 24 hours or until he uses this ability again on a different creature. If a creature under his control is reduced to 0 hit points, it instantly perishes, and Astarion regains 5 hit points.
*   **Corrupted Senses**: Astarion's passive Perception is increased by 5. Additionally, he can pinpoint the location of any living creature within 60 feet of him, even through total cover, as long as it has blood flowing through its veins.
