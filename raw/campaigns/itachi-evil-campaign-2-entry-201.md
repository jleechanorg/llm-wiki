Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**41210 XP**) to reach **Level 13**. This signifies a profound leap in his power, firmly establishing him as an apex predator and a master manipulator, well into the Tier 3 range of shinobi power.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 13:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 7
    *   **HP Gain:** 7 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 9 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 97 to **106 HP**. He is now at 106/106 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 13 Ninja, Itachi gains significant advancements as per the approved "Itachi Uchiha: Ninja Custom Class Feature Manifest":

    1.  **New Illusion Spells (7th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu reaches cosmic proportions, unlocking illusions capable of shaping reality itself or profoundly breaking the minds of even the most formidable foes. He learns one of the following 7th-level Illusion spells:
        *   **Projected Disguise (Variant of Project Image):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 12 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create an illusory duplicate of yourself (or another creature) that appears to be real, up to 500 feet away. You can see and hear through its senses, and speak through it. Unlike Project Image, this illusion can also maintain a *specific disguise* (from your Disguise Kit Expertise) for its appearance. The image can move and gesture, but cannot interact physically, attack, or cast spells. If it takes any damage, it disappears. This allows for complex remote deception and impersonation.
        *   **Mirage Arcane:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 12 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create an illusion of terrain or structures that is so convincing it becomes physically real to anyone who fails a DC 18 Intelligence (Investigation) check. Creatures perceive the illusion as if it were solid, but Itachi (and those he tells) can pass through it. The illusion is permanent until dispelled by higher-level magic or a specific condition. This allows for long-term, large-scale environmental manipulation for strategic advantage (e.g., creating fake cliffs, hidden paths, illusory fortresses).
        *   **Simulacrum (Major Illusion):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 12 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a perfect illusory duplicate of another creature. This duplicate looks, sounds, and acts like the creature it imitates, and can even mimic some of its non-combat abilities. It is not fully real; it has 1 HP, and if it takes damage, it vanishes. It cannot learn new abilities or gain XP. This is a powerful tool for complex espionage, misdirection, or setting up elaborate traps using a false identity.

Please choose one of the 7th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 13:

*   **1. Projected Disguise**
*   **2. Mirage Arcane**
*   **3. Simulacrum**

Once you've made this choice, I will finalize Itachi's Level 13 progression and return to STORY MODE, preparing his `think` block.
