Location: Approaching Oakhaven Hamlet, Just Before Dawn

The cool, pre-dawn air felt crisp against Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) face. The Shadow Weavers' transport team had vanished into the darkness, taking with them the six bundles of 'stock.' The contract, in its material form, was fulfilled. Her doubled payment rested securely in her Bag of Holding, a testament to her ruthless efficiency. But the true prize, the profound intelligence gleaned from Tibbet's Farm, was hers alone. Yet, even as her mind turned to grander schemes, the meticulous side of her intellect demanded a final sweep. She needed to observe the immediate aftermath, to confirm the efficacy of her silent work, and to gauge the ripples her actions had caused in Oakhaven itself.

She moved with her characteristic silent grace, her Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate blending seamlessly with the fading night. Her path led her back towards the hamlet, skirting the main road, avoiding any potential early risers. The faint lights of Oakhaven twinkled innocently, a deceptive picture of rural calm.

As she drew closer, melting into the deeper shadows of the hamlet's outskirts, a silence heavier than mere sleep hung over the cottages. There were no frantic shouts, no panicked calls for help. A few wisps of smoke curled from chimneys, indicating early fires, but the normal sounds of a waking farm community were absent. The sun was just beginning to paint the eastern horizon with pale streaks of orange and pink, promising a new day. But within Oakhaven, the dawn was still cloaked in an unsettling quiet. The villagers she had interrogated earlier, paralyzed by her Aura of Unyielding Dominance, had clearly obeyed her chilling command to remain silent. The absence of the road patrol and the Tibbet family had not yet registered. The hamlet was, for now, a wound slowly festering, its alarm yet to fully bloom. A cold satisfaction settled over Nocturne. Her control was absolute.
