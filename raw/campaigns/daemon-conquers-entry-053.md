Daemon (Level 2, Age 30) stood in the central courtyard of his newly acquired Keep, the eight newly designated 'warriors' standing before him, a mix of former guards and hardened commoners. He pointed towards the armory, his voice a low, gravelly command. "Arm yourselves. You will learn to wield steel for *my* purpose. Discipline will be absolute. Failure, swiftly punished." He knew true training would take time, but a foundation of fear and basic instruction could be laid immediately. He sent them to the armory, instructing them to take only what they could effectively use, and to begin simple drilling in the yard, their movements watched closely by his unblinking gaze.

Next, his attention turned to the trembling mass of townsfolk designated as servants. He barked orders, assigning them to the kitchens, the cleaning of the great halls, the upkeep of the grounds. His commands were short, brutal, and left no room for question. "This Keep will shine. Food will be prepared. My needs will be met. You will work until you drop, or you will regret it." He established a perimeter, ordering the few remaining guards who hadn't fled to patrol, reinforcing the message that his rule was absolute and immediate. Stonehaven was no longer a baron's fief; it was a conqueror's domain, run by fear and efficiency.
