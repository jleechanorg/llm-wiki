**The Departure.**

The journey is a stark lesson in the region's decay. The road to Beregost is clogged with evidence of the Iron Crisis. You pass merchant wagons with shattered axles, the iron brittle and gray. You see refugees clutching farming tools that have snapped in half. The tension on the road is palpable; every traveler eyes the others with suspicion, hands hovering near weapons.

Your *Hunger* keeps you alert, scanning the treeline for threats, but the road is quiet today—likely due to the heavy Flaming Fist patrols you spot periodically. Roric keeps pace easily, his earlier wounds forgotten, while Khalid and Jaheira walk with the synchronized rhythm of veteran partners.

By late afternoon, the sprawling town of **Beregost** appears on the horizon. It is a hub of trade, dominated by the massive temple of Lathander and the smoke of burning smithies. Even from here, you can hear the ring of hammers—smiths desperately trying to forge iron that won't break.

"B-Beregost," Khalid stammers, wiping dust from his brow. "We should... we should rest here tonight. Thunderhammer Smithy is famous; perhaps they have news of the mines?"

Jaheira looks at the sinking sun. "It is a logical stop. But we must be careful. Towns mean ears, and Sarevok's coin buys many listeners."
