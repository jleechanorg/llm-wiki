## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign set shortly after the events of BG3, featuring Nocturne as a Level 5 Paladin. Uses the custom "Destiny" ruleset with Aptitudes, Influence, and Milestones. The city is in flux after the Absolute crisis, providing a perfect environment for an ambitious individual to rise. Campaign includes extensive world generation with noble houses, factions, and siblings.

## Key Claims
- Setting: Baldur's Gate shortly after BG3, with the city recovering from the Absolute crisis
- Unlikelihood Ratio calculated at 5%, well within the 10% threshold for heroic campaigns
- Nocturne is a 16-year-old Paladin with high stats (Physique 16, Intelligence 18, Wisdom 16)
- First contract: Kidnap peasants for the Shadow Weavers to sell as slaves
