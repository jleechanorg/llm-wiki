- You have fundamentally changed the city's architecture. You executed the official Lord Commander and replaced the City Watch with a fanatical militia (The Blackguard). You must convince them that your 'Blackguard math' is the only thing keeping their investment safe.

**Consequence of Failure:**
- If you ignore the Auditor or fail to manipulate the ledger, the Bank declares default. Tywin's funding ceases, the Stormlands' blockade tightens, and Rhaegar will have no choice but to revoke your command to appease the creditors.
