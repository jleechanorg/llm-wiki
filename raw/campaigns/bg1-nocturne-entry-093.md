"Quiet," Roric hisses. "Talk, and you live. Scream, and I take your ears."

The kobold freezes, trembling so violently his armor rattles. "I talk! I talk! Don't cut!"

You descend the ladder, stepping over the corpse of the Chieftain to join the interrogation. **Minsc** and **Kagain** stand guard, watching the tunnels, while **Dynaheir** inspects the green fire of the dead shaman.

"Skinny elf! With the glowing sword! Mulahey keeps him for... for sacrifices!"
