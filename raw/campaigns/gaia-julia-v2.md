## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in an alternate Rome where the Julians control 70% of power. Player is Gaia Julia Caesar, a 16-year-old Level 12 Imperator (gestalt Paladin Conquest / Divine Soul Sorcerer). She hides her true power behind a "Masked Level 1" persona as "Gaia of the Gens Vibia." She is Lawful Evil, a sadistic voyeur who views mortals as cattle. Custom Imperator class has Subtle Spell for covert spellcasting. 1,042 entries.

## Key Claims
- True Level 12, Masked Level 1 — creates perfect mechanical tension
- CHA 22 (+6), DEX 20 (+5), STR 10 (+0)
- Custom Imperator class: gestalt Paladin/Sorcerer with Aura of Conquest (frightened targets frozen)
- 100,000 gold starting wealth, 20 elite Level 9 guards ("The Twenty")
- Five companions (First Cohort): Voros, Cinnaminus, Marcus Antonius, Lucius Vorenus, Servilia
- Companions are 2 levels below (Level 10), guards are 3 levels below (Level 9)
- Spartacus is present at the ludus — player can subvert or let rebellion happen
