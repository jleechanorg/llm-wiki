Location: Outskirts of Oakhaven, Near the Barn, Early Evening

The growls of the mastiffs vibrated through the damp earth, a low, ominous counterpoint to the chirping crickets. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) remained perfectly still within the copse, her concealing garb a second skin. Her mind, as sharp and precise as a honed blade, immediately cataloged the unexpected obstacle. These were not mere farm animals; their disciplined stance, the intelligent sweep of their heads as they scented the air, spoke of training. Kaelen's oversight, or perhaps a deliberate omission, was irrelevant now. Only the problem, and its most elegant solution, mattered.

Her gaze, devoid of any sentiment, measured the distance to the barn, the density of the undergrowth, the way the wind whispered through the tall grass. A cold satisfaction settled over her, akin to the feeling a master craftsman might have when presented with a complex, unmarred piece of raw material. This was a deviation from the plan, yes, but also an opportunity to prove her mastery of adaptation. The task was clear: infiltrate Oakhaven, scout, and prepare for the harvest. Noise was unacceptable.

Her formidable intellect (Intelligence 18) began to cycle through possibilities, filtering them through the lens of ruthlessness (Agreeableness 1) and absolute efficiency.

***

**Nocturne's Internal Monologue & Planning:**

*The dogs are alert, and my scent is drifting towards them. A direct advance, even carefully, risks immediate detection and a loud confrontation. That would compromise the entire operation.*

* **Option 1: The Ghostly Path (Stealth Bypass)**
    *   *Approach:* Attempt to use the shifting wind and available cover to circle wide around their patrol area, slipping into the hamlet from an unguarded approach. This relies heavily on my ability to move silently despite my armor.
    *   *Pros:* Completely avoids direct conflict, maintains absolute silence, preserves Energy Points and Hit Points. The purest form of discreet infiltration.
    *   *Cons:* My plate armor imposes a disadvantage on stealth. While I am agile (Coordination 14), these dogs are trained. A single misstep, a twig snapped, could mean immediate failure and a barking alert. My focus on efficiency means I abhor wasted effort or unnecessary risk.
    *   *Confidence:* High risk of exposure. Success is possible, but my internal calculations show a significant chance of a critical error. The stakes of this entire contract hinge on discretion. This feels almost too risky for the primary objective.

* **Option 2: The Silent Dissolution (Lethal Neutralization)**
    *   *Approach:* Engage the mastiffs directly, but with absolute precision and silence, eliminating them before they can raise an alarm. This would involve a swift, targeted strike. My knowledge of anatomy (Expertise: Anatomy & Weak Points (Combat)) would be crucial here.
    *   *Pros:* Eliminates the threat permanently, granting uncontested access. Can be done swiftly if executed perfectly. It appeals to my desire for decisive action and absolute control.
    *   *Cons:* Requires perfect execution. Any failure means noise, potentially alerting the hamlet and even their handlers. Even for me, unnecessary bloodshed is inefficient if a more subtle path exists.
    *   *Confidence:* High confidence in my ability to execute, but the margin for error is slim. It provides certainty, however.

* **Option 3: The False Trail (Distraction)**
    *   *Approach:* Create a diversion away from my position, drawing the dogs off while I slip in. Perhaps something small thrown, or a whispered sound to misdirect.
    *   *Pros:* Avoids direct confrontation, can be low-risk if the distraction is convincing.
    *   *Cons:* Requires careful timing and a suitable distraction. Might still draw unwanted attention from the hamlet's residents if too loud or obvious. It's a less direct form of control, leaving the dogs active.
    *   *Confidence:* Moderately confident. It feels less clean than outright neutralization, but potentially safer than pure stealth.

*The thought of a botched stealth attempt, or the risk of a noisy distraction, gnawed at the edges of her mind. Perfection was paramount. The most effective strategy would be the one that guaranteed silence and efficiency, leaving no trace.*

***

Nocturne's eyes narrowed, a calculating gleam reflecting the fading light. She settled on her preferred course, the one that offered the most control and the least chance of lingering variables.
