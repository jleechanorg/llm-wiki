## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set during the liberation of Astapor and March on Meereen in the Game of Thrones universe. Visenya Belaerys, a Level 1 Dragon Scholar (Wizard), is the secret last scion of the Fourteen Flames' most powerful Valyrian line — the "true Apex" who outranks Daenerys Targaryen. Rescued from a Lysene pleasure house, she hides her identity while manipulating events from the shadows.
