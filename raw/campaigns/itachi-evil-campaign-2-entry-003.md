Their Sharingan, a power born of intense emotion, only magnifies their instability, turning them into walking powder kegs of resentment and ambition. They are an inefficient, wasteful mechanism, their power squandered on petty squabbles and a self-destructive insistence on a past that must die. I am the surgeon, performing a necessary, brutal removal for a greater, perfectly designed future—my future. Their cries are not those of innocents, but of a flawed system being purged.

*What is the optimal path for this purge?*
My primary objective is total eradication, clean and silent where possible, to maintain the façade for Konoha. Secondarily, I must identify and secure any unique Uchiha artifacts, forbidden jutsu scrolls, or any other knowledge that could serve my long-term ambitions.
