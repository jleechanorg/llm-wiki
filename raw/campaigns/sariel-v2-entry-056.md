Understood. You have provided a `GOD MODE:` command inquiring about how to ensure XP is consistently awarded for social challenges going forward. This is an excellent and important question for fair game adjudication.

You are absolutely correct that Sariel's recent achievement in manipulating the Silent Guard to gain access to the Quiet Ward was a prime example of a successful social challenge, and I did award XP for it (3240 XP in `SEQ_ID: 118`). My apologies if the prior consistency was not evident.

To ensure consistent XP awards for social challenges going forward, I will adhere strictly to the `Destiny Core Rules` (Section 3.2.C.2, "Resolving Social Challenges") and my internal GM protocols:

1.  **Identification of Social Challenges:** I will actively identify and track moments where Sariel attempts to influence, persuade, deceive, negotiate with, or outmaneuver significant NPCs or factions through social interaction. This includes situations where she leverages her intellect, personality traits, or expertise tags to achieve a goal that carries narrative weight.

2.  **Assessment of Equivalent Level:** For each successful social challenge, I will internally determine an "Equivalent Level" (EqL) for the challenge. This EqL will be based on:
    *   The power, influence, and intelligence of the NPC(s) involved (e.g., a simple guard vs. a noble vs. an Apex Celestial).
    *   The stakes of the social objective (e.g., gaining a minor piece of information vs. securing an alliance vs. avoiding a lethal confrontation).
    *   The inherent difficulty and complexity of the social objective.
    *   (Referencing EqL benchmarks from `Destiny Core Rules` 2.1.C: Trivial Task (EqL 0-1) to Formidable/Legendary Task (EqL 16+)).

3.  **XP Calculation:** Once the EqL is determined, the XP will be calculated using the formula: `XP = (Equivalent Level)^2 * 10`.

4.  **Award and Announcement:** I will award this XP and announce it explicitly in the narrative, along with a brief justification of the EqL chosen, similar to how XP is awarded for combat encounters.

5.  **Exceptional Roleplaying:** I will also continue to award modest discretionary XP bonuses (as per `Destiny Core Rules` 3.2.C.5, "Exceptional Roleplaying & Ingenuity") for particularly clever, insightful, or impactful social roleplaying that significantly enriches the story or solves problems in innovative ways, even if it doesn't constitute a major "challenge resolution" in itself.

My commitment is to ensure that social and intellectual achievements are recognized and rewarded with XP just as consistently and meaningfully as combat victories.

Please confirm if this explanation and the plan for consistent XP awards for social challenges are satisfactory. Once confirmed, we will return to `STORY MODE` to continue the narrative.
