## Scene 012: The March to Silt-River (Gaia)

**Timestamp:** 1492 DR, Mirtul 15, 08:00:01 | **Location:** The Obsidian Ziggurat, Bezantur | **Status:** Lvl 5 Abyss Knight | HP: 44/44

The transition from the administrative coldness of the Ledger back to the sweltering, ash-choked reality of Bezantur is seamless. Aizen stands in the shadow of the Obsidian Ziggurat, the weight of the Steel-Rimmed Buckler familiar on his arm and the Shadow-Thorn Blade humming a low, necrotic frequency against his hip. The Obsidian Mirror rests against his chest, its surface occasionally rippling as if Nocturne is shifting in her Abyssal sleep.

Squad Obsidian is a tableau of professional violence. Korbin 'The Wall' adjusts his greaves, his massive frame casting a long shadow over Silica, who meticulously checks the tension on her bowstrings. Needles hums a tuneless whistle, secured vials of green and purple toxins clinking softly at his belt. Verrick leans against a stone pillar, his crossbow across his knees, watching the slave-pens with the weary detachment of a man who has seen too much and expects worse.

Captain Kaelen's orders are clear: Burn Silt-River, secure the girl Elara for Lord Kul, and collar the survivors. But through the Mirror, the Sovereign's whisper remains: **Feed the Rose. Forty souls must be skimmed from the tally and sent to the Abyss.** And then there is the Shadow Monk, Sheriff Kaelen — a delicacy Nocturne has specifically requested.

The road to Silt-River is a three-hour march through the grey dust of the Borderlands. Before marching, the requisition ledger remains open. Your 5 Dominion Power Points are the capital to ensure this audit is both profitable and absolute.
