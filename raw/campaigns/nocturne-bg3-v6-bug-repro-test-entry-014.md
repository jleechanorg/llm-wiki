## Scene 014: The Intelligence Gathering

**Timestamp:** 2026 DR, 1 15, 08:05:00 | **Location:** Emerald Grove Gates (Inner Entrance) | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

Nocturne moves through the inner sanctum of the gatehouse, her footsteps making no sound against the flagstones — a ghost in torn silk. Zevlor leads her toward a small stone table cluttered with maps and half-eaten rations.

**"You're safe now, child,"** he says, his voice a low rumble of exhausted compassion. He doesn't notice that she isn't actually shivering; he only sees the stillness of a girl who has seen too much.

**"The road... there were so many of them,"** Nocturne whispers, the Abyssal harmonic in her voice vibrating with just enough sincerity to soothe. **"They talked about the 'Absolute.' They said the thorns wouldn't stop them. Are we... are we really safe here?"**

Zevlor's shoulders slump. **"We have the gate, and the Druids have their Rite. But the goblins... they grow bolder every night."** He points to the western ridge. **"The caves there are thin. If they find the back passage, or if the gate mechanism fails..."**

He catches himself, looking at her delicate frame and shaking his head. **"But you shouldn't worry about that. We have enough able bodies to hold the line."**

Through her 'victim' lens, Nocturne sees the cracks: desperate for more men, exhausted scouts, the Rite causing internal strife. The gate mechanism is old and prone to jamming. The Shadow Druids are becoming increasingly vocal about 'cleansing' the grove of outsiders. Zevlor mentions the gate mechanism is old, prone to jamming if not maintained, and that the Shadow Druids are taking over the inner sanctum.
