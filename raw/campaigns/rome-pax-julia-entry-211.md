- **Reasoning:** The Doctore is the most experienced combatant in the ludus. His higher level reflects his mastery over all gladiator styles and his ability to suppress the younger champions through pure technique and discipline.

**4. The Brotherhood (Veteran Gladiators): Level 4-5**
- **Examples:** Barca, Gannicus (if present), and the senior fighters.
- **Reasoning:** At Level 5, they gain 'Extra Attack,' making them a formidable force for your eventual rebellion. They are the 'Elite' tier of your future army.

**5. The Novicii (Recruits): Level 1-3**
- **Reasoning:** These are the 'meat' Batiatus mentioned. They are skilled compared to commoners but lack the 'Singularity' or 'Champion' traits that define the lead cast.

**Summary for Strategy:**
Your 20 Marian Veterans (Level 5) are individually superior to the majority of the ludus recruits but would be challenged by the named champions (Spartacus/Crixus). This creates a perfect tactical tension for the subversion arc.
