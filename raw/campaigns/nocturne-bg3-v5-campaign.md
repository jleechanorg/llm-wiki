## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set during the events of BG3, featuring Nocturne Sosuke as a 16-year-old fallen noble sold to "The Velvet Cage" (The Bloom Shadow brothel). The campaign opens with a direct BG3 integration — playing in parallel with the Absolute cult's final squeeze on Baldur's Gate. A psychological noir revenge plot with BG3 companion interactions.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne: Level 1 Bard (College of Perdition), STR 8, DEX 14, CON 8, INT 16, WIS 10, CHA 16 — designed as a "porcelain weapon"
- "Siren's Leverage" trait: +2 bonus to CHA (Deception) and CHA (Persuasion) against humanoids sexually/romantically attracted to females
- The Cassalanter Ascension Gala happens tonight at midnight — the Cassalanters are "renewing the pact" with Zariel
- BG3 integration: Gortash's Coronation in 3 days; refugees flooding Outer City; BG3 party is Level 9-10 but "socially clumsy"
- All 11 BG3 companions can appear as allies/romantic interests
