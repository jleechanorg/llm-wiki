## Summary
A modern-noir D&D 5e campaign set in contemporary USA where the player controls Sariel, a 22-year-old former FBI/Special Forces operative known as "Blood Dragon" with a 94% lethal resolution rate. After being dishonorably discharged following the "Dragon's Breath" incident, she now works as a casino security specialist while the FBI hunts escaped serial killers.

## Key Claims
- Sariel is a Level 5 Gloomstalker Ranger with 44 HP, WIS 16, and exceptional combat abilities
- She has a 100% case clearance rate but was dishonorably discharged for being "too effective"
- The "Dragon's Breath" incident (2023) involved a magnesium flare backdraft in a cartel bunker where she emerged carrying a killer's severed head
- Current employment is at The Neon Dragon Casino & Resort as a profiler/security specialist

## Key Quotes
> "After the mass escape of the nation's most dangerous serial killers, the FBI is forced to crawl back to the one operative they disowned for being 'too effective.'"
> "Sariel, once the prodigy of the Special Forces, must now decide if she will hunt for justice or for the blood that earned her her name."
