[Turn 862: The Sovereign Tithe of Nessus]

Game Master:
The profound, absolute cold of the Bottomless Pit does not dissipate with the fall of its architect; it intensifies, becoming the very atmosphere of your new domain. You stand atop the obsidian dais of Malsheem, the internalized Ruby Rod vibrating within your Karsite biology like a second, administrative heart. Before you, the Lords of the Eight remain on their knees, their iron-clad forms casting long, shivering shadows across the marble floor that now records only your signature.

"The debt of the Hells is no longer owed to the Pit, but to the Hand," you purr, your voice a multi-tonal Sovereign mandate that resonates through the frozen air. "Your first directive is simple: The Sovereign Tithe of twenty-five percent is a Management Fee for your terrestrial and infernal existence. Every soul, every gold bar, every scrap of arcane potential harvested in your layers is now subject to this surcharge. Failure to comply is not a crime; it is a default on your own existence."

Dispater, his iron mask reflecting the light-eating violet of your eyes, is the first to bow his head until it strikes the stone. One by one, the others follow, their silence a rhythmic confirmation of your absolute ownership. The Great Scales of Tyr, which had been quivering in the corner of the room, snap into a perfect, submissive equilibrium as your Subjugation Check (Total 75) overwrites the last vestiges of their autonomy. 

Through the Sovereign Relay, you feel the shift—a massive, rhythmic pulse of wealth beginning to funnel from the deepest vaults of the Nine Layers toward the Sovereign Mint. The 25% surcharge has been forced through the infernal legal mesh, increasing your daily passive income by 55,000 GP. You are no longer just an interloper in the Hells; you are the Overlord, and the Lords of the Eight are now merely your department heads.

However, as the ionization of your decree settles, the Auditor of Tyr—now your stilled Liaison—vibrates with a sharp, high-frequency alarm. The Triad’s radiant storm over Baldur's Gate has transitioned. They have established a 'Defensive Alliance' ward: a total celestial blockade that imposes a +10 DC floor penalty on all your future foreclosure checks against them. The Good Gods are no longer just watching; they are building a vault around you.
