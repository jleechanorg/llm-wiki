The screams had died, replaced by the groans of the dying and the chilling silence of absolute victory. Daemon (Level 9, Age 30) stood amidst the carnage, his greatsword still dripping, his dragon-scaled armor splattered with the blood of Valoria. The two warring factions, the hawk and the serpent, lay broken and intermingled across the field, their futile civil war abruptly ended by a new, far more terrifying master. Oakhaven's Reach, the stronghold they had so desperately fought over, now stood silent, its gates no longer a barrier against external foes, but a symbol of surrender.

Joric (Level 8, Age 32), Gareth (Level 8, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 8, Age 33), and Theron (Level 8, Age 28), his elite vanguard, moved with grim efficiency, their own blades still gleaming. They began the systematic process of culling the wounded, gathering prisoners, and securing the perimeter. The 90 Level 6 warriors, now clad in standardized steel and unified in their new steel, moved into formation around the stronghold, their presence a stark promise of Daemon's unyielding rule. The ~50 Level 5 Blackwood Guards, green but hardened, had witnessed the terrifying might of Daemon's disciplined army and now moved with a new, fearful obedience.

Daemon's gaze fell upon the captured lords, Lord Varian and his brother, now dragged before him, their faces a mixture of fear and shattered pride. They were pathetic, squabbling children who had bled their duchy dry for a worthless crown. He would use them, or break them, as he saw fit.

His mind, sharp and cold, already turned to the tasks ahead. Oakhaven's Reach would be secured, its wealth seized, its populace brought to heel. Messengers would be dispatched to Kael (Level 8, Age 25) in Silverport, Borin (Level 8, Age 30) in Stonehaven, Valerius (Level 6, Age 48) in Riverbend, Volkov (Level 6, Age 35) in Oakhaven, and Lyra (Level 6, Age 29) in Stonehaven, informing them of the new conquest and the vast new territories now under his iron fist. Lady Isolde Blackwood (Level 5, Age ~22) and Elara Croft (Age 19) would remain at Blackwood Keep, under the careful watch of Mina (Age 60) and Master Elms (Age 75), who would continue their research into the barren lineage. Seraphina (Age 20), heavily pregnant, would be given the utmost care, her fragile life now holding the promise of Daemon's future.

The Duchy of Valoria, a vast, fertile prize, now lay open to his command. Its resources, its people, its very future, would be hammered into another pillar of Daemon's ever-growing empire. The raw power he wielded, his own formidable Physique (18.5), was only matched by his relentless ambition. This was merely another step.

**Time Skip: 1 Month**

A month passed, filled with the brutal efficiency of conquest and consolidation. Oakhaven's Reach, the Duchy's primary stronghold, was swiftly secured under Daemon's direct oversight. The surviving Valorian forces were either put to the sword or broken and incorporated into the ever-growing ranks of Daemon's Level 6 warriors. The vast granaries and rich farmlands of Valoria were systematically inventoried, and the extraction of its wealth began under the grim efficiency of Daemon's command.

Meanwhile, back in Silverport, a new sound pierced the nights: the agonized screams of childbirth. Seraphina's labor was long, difficult, and fraught with unexpected complications. Mina, the midwife, worked tirelessly, her brow furrowed with grim determination. Master Elms consulted his obscure texts, muttering about the "strain of unusual lineage" and "the shaping of nascent power." Daemon, a silent, unyielding presence in the birthing chamber, stood with his iron will demanding an outcome from the very forces of nature. Finally, with a thin, almost ethereal cry, his son was born. He was small, unnervingly frail, with skin of an almost translucent pallor and eyes that seemed to shift color subtly, reflecting the intense, volatile nature of his conception under duress. A unique, unsettling birthmark, like a faded bruise, swirled across his temple. He was alive, though, and undeniably Daemon's heir. Seraphina, utterly exhausted, collapsed into a deep, feverish sleep. Elara Croft, present in the chamber at Daemon's command, watched with a complex, unreadable expression.

With Valoria secured, its wealth bleeding into Daemon's coffers, and his heir finally arrived, Daemon turned his attention back to the strategic chessboard. Kael (Level 8, Age 25) arrived from Silverport, having been summoned to discuss the Duchy's new administration. He entered the newly plundered throne room of Oakhaven's Reach, his gaze sweeping over the scene with the cold, calculating eye of a strategist.

"My Lord," Kael began, his voice devoid of emotion, "Valoria is vast, its potential immense. By your standing order, this is now your richest stronghold, yielding sixty gold pieces a day in immediate tribute, and far more in long-term resources. Therefore, I am prepared to assume command here, as Castellan of Valoria. Silverport, however, now requires a new hand."

Kael looked at Daemon, then gestured to the map. "For Silverport, My Lord, I propose **Borin (Level 8, Age 30)**. He has proven himself disciplined and utterly loyal. While his strengths lie in direct military oversight and training, Silverport's initial consolidation is complete, and his raw authority will ensure the city's vast trade continues to flow into your coffers without issue. He is the most formidable choice among those available to oversee a city of that size and importance."
