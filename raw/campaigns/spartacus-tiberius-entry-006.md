### 🏛️ Special Roman Mechanics (Low-Magic Variant)

1.  **Aura of Roman Discipline (Level 6):** Your presence hardens the resolve of those around you. Allies within 10 feet (extending to 30 feet at Level 18) gain a bonus to **AC and all Saving Throws** equal to your Charisma modifier (+3). This represents tactical positioning and the fear of failure under your gaze.
2.  **Ancestral Fury (Divine Smite Reskin):** Instead of holy light, your 'Smites' are moments of cold, calculated lethality. When you hit, you exploit a flaw in the enemy's guard. Damage is **Force** or **Psychic** (representing terror), rather than Radiant.
3.  **Commanding Voice (Spellcasting Replacement):** You do not cast 'Bless' or 'Command' through magic. You do it through authority. These function as limited-use tactical shouts that replicate spell effects through sheer presence (CHA-based).
4.  **Gladius Mastery:** Since you are DEX-based, you treat the **Gladius (Shortsword)** as a masterwork weapon. You can use your reaction to parry, adding +2 to your AC against one melee attack (replaces *Shield* spell).
