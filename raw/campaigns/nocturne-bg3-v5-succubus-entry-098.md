[Session 1: The Scion's Gambit]

Game Master:
The stone archway vibrates with the resonance of your voice, the 'Black Diamond' mask reflecting the flickering torchlight with a clinical, terrifying stillness. You do not wait for Zevlor's permission. With a sharp, clicking whistle, you signal the vanguard. 

The six thralls move as one, their boots churning the mud in perfect, unsettling synchronization. They march past the cowering Aradin, their glassy eyes fixed forward, a living wall of meat and malice that forces the tiefling guards to scramble back in a mixture of terror and awe. 

Zevlor's hand white-knuckles the hilt of his blade, his singular eye wide as he watches a sixteen-year-old girl command a goblin unit into the heart of his sanctuary. He looks at you, then at the silver-clad Lae'zel and the shimmering arcane aura of Gale. The 'Uncanny Valley' dread of your presence, saturated with the brine and Abyssal sparks of Malcanthet's favor, finally snaps his resistance. He sees not a child, but a prodigious architect of survival who has already outpaced his own tactical mind.

"Open the inner gate!" Zevlor bellows, his voice cracking with the weight of the concession. "Clear the way for the scion's... escort!"

The massive interior doors groan as they swing open, revealing the Hollow beyond. You march through the threshold, the vanguard leading the way, while the defenders of the Grove watch in stunned silence. You haven't just entered the Grove; you have conquered its hierarchy before the first sun has even set.
