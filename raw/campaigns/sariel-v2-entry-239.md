I can begin to "discover" fragments of a potential cure or a method of control, a solution that only my unique intellect and understanding can provide. This would make me indispensable. It would force them to come to *me*, not as accusers, but as supplicants. To see them beg for the very knowledge I possess, to watch their pride crumble before their desperate need for a cure... the thought is exquisite. It is the ultimate act of domination.

Each path offers a unique flavor of control, a different texture of gratification. The final choice must be precise, elegant, and serve my ultimate goal of absolute, unchallengeable power.
