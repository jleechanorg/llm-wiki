## Summary
A post-Baldur's Gate 3 solo D&D 5e campaign. Astarion, now an Ascended Vampire, is Level 12 (Ranger 5 Gloom Stalker / Rogue 7 Assassin). Set in 1492 DR in Baldur's Gate, he contemplates alliances (Gale, Shadowheart, or new contacts) while establishing his lair in the Upper City sewers. The campaign opens with character build finalization, clarifying sunlight immunity, necrotic damage output, and expanded Ascended Vampire traits. Core theme: the loneliness of supreme power and strategic alliance-building.

## Key Claims
- Astarion's final build: DEX 22 (+6), CHA 16 (+3), CON 16 (+3), INT 14 (+2), WIS 14 (+2), STR 10 (+0)
- Ascended Vampire traits confirmed: immunity to sunlight, necrotic damage output via bite, Regeneration 10hp/turn, Legendary Resistance 3/day, Charm (DC 14)
- Class features: Gloom Stalker gives Umbral Sight (90ft darkvision + invisibility in darkness), Dread Ambusher (+initiative, extra attack/round 1), Extra Attack; Assassin gives Sneak Attack 4d6, Assassinate (crit on surprised), Evasion, Uncanny Dodge
- Fighting Style: Defense (+1 AC in armor); Spells: Fog Cloud, Hunter's Mark, Longstrider, Cure Wounds, Darkvision, Pass Without Trace, Spike Growth
- The campaign heavily features DM MODE character sheet refinement, not yet transitioned to full story mode
