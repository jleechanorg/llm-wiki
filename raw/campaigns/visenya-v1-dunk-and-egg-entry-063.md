He nods, but it's a slow, heavy movement. The honesty you're asking for is a different kind of armor, and for a man who has spent his life trying to be invisible in the Flea Bottom alleys, showing his heart to a hall of lords feels more dangerous than a tourney lance. He mutters a name under his breath—not a title, but just 'Arlan'—and for a moment, the giant seems very alone on the golden road.
