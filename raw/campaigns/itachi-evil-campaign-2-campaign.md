## Summary
A dark Naruto RPG campaign where the player controls Itachi Uchiha as an evil character — not secretly good, but genuinely malevolent. Starting at age 13 during the Uchiha Clan massacre, this campaign explores the "what if" of an Itachi who truly embraced darkness rather than the canonical path of a tragic hero.

## Key Claims
- Itachi is 13 years old at the Uchiha Clan massacre, evil (not secretly good)
- He possesses both Sharingan and Mangekyō Sharingan active from the start
- His custom "Ninja" class combines Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack at L5 + all Illusion spells
- Uses the Destiny ruleset (D&D 5e based)

## Key Quotes
> "Your chosen setting is Naruto, and you will be playing as Itachi Uchiha. The core premise is that this Itachi is evil and not secretly a guy, starting at the point of the Uchiha Clan massacre."
> "He sees himself as a surgeon, performing a necessary, brutal excision for the 'greater good'—his own meticulously planned, absolute, and unyielding order."

## Character Goals
1. **Uchiha Excision:** Systematically eliminate the Uchiha clan to remove a volatile element
2. **Konoha's Hidden Hand:** Manipulate Konoha's power structures from the shadows
3. **Great Unifying Order:** Transcend village politics to forge a unified world order
