You are currently in **Sanctuary Mode** (Turn 481-496). This system suppresses lethal complications, assassinations, and direct military strikes to allow for the 'Trinity Consolidation' to be narrated. This creates a temporary 'eye of the storm' effect where it feels like nothing can touch you.

**4. The 'God Mode' Safety Net:**
You have used God Mode to retcon potential failure points (such as being caught with Viserys's sword in the conduit). These administrative corrections have prevented the 'Heat' (85) from converting into a 'Hard Loss' state, such as exile to the Silent Sisters or imprisonment.

- **The Baelarys Secret:** Rhaegar is no longer trying to 'parent' you; he is trying to 'solve' you as a biological threat.
