He gives you a final, knowing wink as he settles into Davaeorn's chair. "Don't keep me waiting too long, boss. I'm not a miner."

You ascend the lift one last time. You bypass the slaves on Level 1, telling them that "Administrator Roric" is now in charge of their safety. You exit the waterfall into the clean air of the Cloakwood.
