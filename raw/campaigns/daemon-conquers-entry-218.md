Daemon (Level 8, Age 30) listened to Kael's (Level 8, Age 25) breakdown of costs, his gaze still fixed on the gold tally. "Show me the payback, Kael," Daemon rumbled, his voice sharp. "When does the gold I spend return to my coffers? And going forward, you will always provide this assessment."

Kael’s eyes flickered with a faint, almost imperceptible gleam of satisfaction. This was the language Daemon understood, the ruthless calculation of investment and return. "Understood, My Lord," he affirmed, swiftly reorganizing his thoughts.

"For options that directly generate more gold, the calculations are more precise:

*   **Option 4: The Merchant's Grip (Direct Trade Investment)**
    *   "An investment here of **10,000 to 20,000 Gold Pieces** is projected to return an additional **20 to 40 Gold Pieces per day**. This means a payback period of approximately **9 to 33 months**, My Lord. The lower the initial investment and the higher the daily return, the faster the payback. It accelerates once routes are established."

*   **Option 5: The Earth's Bounty (Resource Extraction Expansion)**
    *   "An investment of **15,000 to 25,000 Gold Pieces** for expanding mines or developing new resource veins would yield an estimated **15 to 30 Gold Pieces per day** in equivalent value. This translates to a payback period of approximately **17 to 56 months**, My Lord. It is slower due to the labor and time involved in extraction, but the resources are foundational."

"For the other options, My Lord," Kael continued, "the returns are not measured in direct gold, but in strategic advantage, which is harder to quantify financially, but no less vital:

*   **Option 1: The Iron Fist (Military Investment)**
    *   "Outfitting our army or hiring mercenaries: This has no direct gold payback. Its return is in saved lives during conquest, faster victories, reduced casualty costs in the long run, and the ability to take stronger targets. It is an investment in overwhelming force, which prevents future losses."

*   **Option 2: The Unseen Hand (Intelligence & Influence)**
    *   "A spy network and bribery: The initial **5,000 to 8,000 Gold Pieces** and ongoing **500 to 1,000 Gold Pieces per month** does not return gold directly. Its payback is in invaluable intelligence, allowing for bloodless takeovers, reduced resistance, and the subtle dismantling of enemies from within. It saves you from costly wars."

*   **Option 3: The Enduring Foundation (Infrastructure & Defense)**
    *   "Fortifying Keeps and improving infrastructure: This investment of **10,000 to 30,000 Gold Pieces** yields long-term stability and increased prosperity, making your current holdings harder to challenge. Its payback is in sustained daily income, reduced need for large garrisons, and impenetrable defenses. It protects the wealth you have already seized, and allows more gold to remain in your coffers rather than spent on repairs or lost to rebellion."

Kael finished, the meticulous numbers laid bare, the strategic purpose of each coin understood.
