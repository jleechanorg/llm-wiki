## Scene 006: The Viper's Promise

**Timestamp:** 1492 DR, Month 1 Day 15, Dawn | **Location:** Shattered Sanctum - Minthara's War Room | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

Nocturne leans closer, violet eyes flaring with predatory luminescence that bleeds into the whites of her irises. Her thumb brushes against the pulse point in Minthara's wrist, feeling the sharp, rhythmic thrum of drow blood.

**"The Absolute's design is efficient, General, but it lacks... finesse,"** she whispers, the brine and ozone thickening until it tastes of a coming storm. **"I will go to the grove. I will play the part of the porcelain victim, the drowning girl seeking refuge. I will subvert their 'thorns' from within, rotting their resolve until the gates don't just open — they fall in supplication."**

Minthara's gaze is a searing heat against Nocturne's marble-cool skin. She is looking for the lie but finds only the terrifying sincerity of ambition. The prospect of a 'decisive victory' — not just a slaughter, but a total psychological collapse — appeals to her sense of drow superiority.

**"A viper in their garden,"** Minthara murmurs, a dark, hungry smile tugging at the corner of her mouth. **"You are a wicked creature, Nocturne. I see now why the Absolute has marked you. Do this. Break them from within. When the signal fires rise, my goblins will sweep through the ruin you've prepared."**

She releases Nocturne's hand with a warning: **"But remember: the Absolute sees all. If this subversion is merely a mask for cowardice, I will personally ensure your 'perfect' skin is flayed for my rafters."**
