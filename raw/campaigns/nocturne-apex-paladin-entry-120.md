The air was sharp and cool, scented with damp earth and the subtle, lingering metallic tang of fresh blood. Kaelen Flinn had vanished back into the night, leaving behind the chilling silence and the promise of payment. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) waited a precise minute, ensuring his retreat was absolute, before moving. Her gaze, sharp and analytical, fixed on the base of the dead drop tree where he had placed the purse. The negotiation, though unspoken, had been successful. Kaelen's fear, coupled with his syndicate's demand for efficiency, proved a potent lever.

She approached the tree, her Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate blending seamlessly with the deep shadows. Her fingers, quick and precise, closed around the heavy leather purse. The weight was satisfying. She pulled open the drawstrings, and the gleam of gold, more than the initial sum, confirmed Kaelen's compliance. Her fee was indeed doubled. This small victory reinforced her belief in the power of calculated ruthlessness.

With the payment secured in her Bag of Holding, Nocturne's mind, a relentless engine of strategic thought, shifted focus. Kaelen would arrange transport, but his timetable remained uncertain. Idleness was anathema. The night was still young, and the original hamlet of Oakhaven, a mere mile away, remained unexamined in detail. Its reaction to the absence of its guards and the Tibbet family was a variable she wished to map. Information was power, and no detail was too small if it could yield future advantage.

She moved with fluid grace, leaving the dead drop tree and the concealed captives behind. Her path led her away from the main road, angling towards the quiet heart of Oakhaven. Her Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate was a silent whisper against the tall grasses. The hamlet appeared as a cluster of faint, flickering lights, a deceptive picture of rural peace. She would observe, gather data, and ensure there were no lingering threats or unforeseen developments in the very place she intended to harvest again, should the need arise.
