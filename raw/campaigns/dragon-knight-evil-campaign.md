## Summary
A dark political fantasy following Ser Arion val Valerion, a 16-year-old Paladin of the Argent Eaglets knight order, raised in Empress Sariel's absolute authoritarian regime called the "Silent Peace." Set 40 years after the Great Vanishing and the Celestial Wars between Lucifer and Raziel, this campaign explores the morality of obedience, political authority vs. individual conscience, and what it truly means to be loyal.

## Key Details
- **Protagonist:** Ser Arion val Valerion — 16-year-old Paladin, Argent Eaglets knight
- **Setting:** World of Assiah, 40 A.G. (After Great Vanishing), Winter-Mourn Province, Edge of Frost-Veil Forest
- **Core Theme:** Morality of obedience vs. individual conscience, corruption of absolute order
- **Tone:** Dark, political, philosophical
- **Total Scenes:** Campaign start

## Player Character
Ser Arion val Valerion is a young Paladin of just 16 years, knight of the Argent Eaglets order. He has been raised entirely within Empress Sariel's "Silent Peace" — an absolute authoritarian rule established through the "War of Whispers" and psychic mastery. In this world, absolute security is maintained through thought-crime and dissent elimination.

Arion's warhorse is named Cinder. His story begins at Ashwood Keep, on the edge of the Frost-Veil Forest in the Winter-Mourn Province, where he encounters Lady Annalise Ashwood — a noble who has been sheltering refugees in direct defiance of the Stability Mandate.

## Key NPCs / Companions
- **Lady Annalise Ashwood** — Sheltered refugees in defiance of Stability Mandate
- **Prefect Gratian** — Clinical commander who wants to pacify Ashwood Keep
- **Empress Sariel** — The absolute ruler. She IS [[Sariel Arcanus]] from the Sariel V2 campaign — the same character, ~40 years later, after growing from grieving teenager into a psychic autocrat who established the Silent Peace through the "War of Whispers"
- **Alexiel** — Daughter of Lucifer, brokered peace through sacrifice (same Alexiel who died at Battle of Sacrifice Fields in Sariel V2 — this campaign is set ~40 years after that)

## Factions / Organizations
