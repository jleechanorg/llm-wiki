## Summary

A D&D 5e campaign set in an alternate-history Rome where Julius Caesar is Dictator Perpetuo and the Third Servile War is raging. The player character is Gaia Julia Caesar (alias "Livia of Capua"), 16-year-old daughter of the God-Emperor, secretly Level 12 Siren Bard. She was captured by rebels and must use intelligence, seduction, and manipulation to survive and orchestrate the rebellion's downfall. Tone: erotic thriller, reverse horror, political wargame.

## Key Claims

- Gaia is Level 12 Siren (Bard/Paladin Gestalt) posing as Level 1 commoner
- The Velvet Iron mechanic: Subtle Spell always active for Enchantment/Illusion
- Imperial Education: Expertise in Deception/Persuasion/Intimidation/Performance
- The Lotus Ten: 10 loyal slaves (Kael/assassin, Valerius/pyro, Titus+Nero+Aulus/shields, Elara/alchemist, Nia/bard, Lydia+Sabina+Caelia/distractions)
- Campaign Bible structure: 4 parts — Siren & Hidden Blade, Lotus Court & Julian Monolith, Factions/World Lore, The Cage/Rules/First Breath
- Arc I: Survive initial capture, seduce Spartacus, neutralize Crixus
- Arc II: Run rebellion from Spartacus's tent, start civil war between Crixus and Spartacus
- Arc III: Crassus/Pompey corner the army — defect or march on Rome
