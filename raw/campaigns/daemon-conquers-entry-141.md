Each day, the recruits became a fraction tougher, their movements more coordinated, their fear of Daemon slowly transforming into a grim, desperate discipline.

The air, thick with the scent of salt and prosperity, was about to be choked with blood and fear. The training had been brutal, effective, and had undeniably changed Daemon's force.
