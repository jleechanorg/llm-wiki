Itachi Uchiha (Level 20 Ninja, Age 17) stood within the cool, damp confines of his hidden cave base, his **Intelligence, an unparalleled 24 (+7)**, humming with a cold, analytical genius that transcended all known shinobi intellect. He had reached the **absolute pinnacle of mortal shinobi power**. His body vibrated with the immense, perfectly harmonized power of integrated Senju cells. His **Mangekyō Sharingan** blazed with potent, perfected power. Sasuke, now a formidable **Level 20 shinobi (Age 12)**, stood ready, his Mangekyō honed, his loyalty absolute.

Obito lay captive. The Rinnegan blueprint was complete, and Itachi now possessed absolute knowledge of all ocular powers. His agents were effectively expanding his resource base and intelligence network, quietly covering the world for him. His chakra siphon produced a phenomenal +15 EP per day. He even had 30 Potent Chakra Pills.

The path to the Rinnegan was clearer than ever. He was ready. The time for the ultimate sacrifice, the final consolidation of power, had arrived.

Itachi turned to Sasuke, his gaze softening almost imperceptibly, a rare, profound emotion flickering in the depths of his Mangekyō. This was the ultimate gift, the final act of forging, but one that demanded an equal exchange, a true merging of destinies. "Sasuke," Itachi's voice was a low, resonant current, filled with a chilling, yet undeniable, love. "The path to absolute power, to avenge our clan and reshape the world, demands the Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan. That power... it is meant for you, and for me. A true merging."

Sasuke's Mangekyō Sharingan blazed, reflecting Itachi's words, his face filled with unwavering loyalty and a dawning, monumental awe. "Brother... I don't understand. My eyes...?"

Itachi knelt before him, a gesture of profound meaning. "Our eyes. They are the key. The Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan requires the eyes of a close blood relative. We will exchange. My eyes will transcend yours, grant you infinite vision, and ultimate ocular stability. And yours will do the same for me. This is our shared legacy. This is the power we need to break the chains of this world."

Sasuke's breath hitched, his eyes wide with a mix of awe, shock, and a terrifying resolve. Tears welled, not of sadness, but of overwhelming gratitude and a chilling conviction. "Brother... Itachi... yes. I understand. I will... fulfill our destiny."

"Do not sentimentalize this, Sasuke," Itachi's voice returned to its colder, authoritative tone, though the underlying emotion remained. "This is strategy. This is power. This is the final act of our forging. With these eyes, we will both be unstoppable. We will be the ultimate instruments of our Great Unifying Order."

Sasuke, despite the tears, nodded, his loyalty absolute. "Yes, Brother. I understand. I will. Show me how."

Itachi activated his **Medical Ninjutsu/Healing Arts** Expertise, his hands glowing with green chakra. With chilling precision, he began the delicate, irreversible, mutual procedure. He extracted his own Mangekyō Sharingan, preserving them meticulously. Simultaneously, with incredible speed and delicate control, he extracted Sasuke's Mangekyō Sharingan. Then, with an intricate application of chakra and biological manipulation, he prepared both his and Sasuke's ocular cavities for the profound, mutual integration. The process was agonizing for both, a searing pain that surpassed all previous torment, but their perfected wills endured. The final, critical stage of Mutual EMS acquisition was at hand.
