## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign where the player controls Sosuke Aizen, a 16-year-old level 4 wizard with INT 20, secretly running a high-end brothel called "The White Garden" (The Hollow). Character is based on Bleach's Aizen with god-mode modifications. Contains explicit sadistic content with "fade to black" mechanics. 1,194 entries. Aizen seeks to "break the queen" — dominating strong women as his primary sadistic compulsion.

## Key Claims
- Aizen is 6th child of House Sosuke (high nobility), plays "Quiet Sosuke" scribe role
- INT 20 (+5), CHA 14 (+2), has Metamagic Adept (Subtle Spell, Quickened Spell)
- Secretly owns The White Garden / The Hollow — a sanctuary for broken wills
- Kyoka Suigetsu (Hogyoku) allows complete hypnosis
- Target profile: young, attractive women with power/status (Paladins, Heiresses, Wizards)
- 450gp/month income, 2 diamonds, 1 withered, 5 "fodder" disposable flowers
- Starting gold: 2000gp + daily gold accumulation
