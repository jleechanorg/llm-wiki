--- BACKGROUND ---

Baldur's Gate breathes with a ragged, desperate rhythm in the wake of the Absolute’s fall. The Flaming Fist is a shattered remnant, leaving the Upper City’s spires and the Lower City’s gutters to be carved up by the Zhentarim and the Thieves' Guild. Amidst this power vacuum, the Arcane Athenaeum stands as a crumbling bastion of magical education, its halls filled with arrogant noble scions oblivious to the predator in their midst.

You are Sosuke Aizen, the 'Cursed Scholar' of House Sosuke. Exiled at thirteen and officially dead, you have spent three years weaving a web of narcotics, blackmail, and psychological ruin from the Athenaeum’s shadows. By day, you are the harmless orphan ward; by night, you are the 'Gardener,' the secret architect of the White Garden, an elite brothel where the pride of the city’s 'Queens' is methodically dismantled for profit and your own clinical amusement.

Currently, your focus is split between the slow corruption of Lirael Voss, a shipwright heiress whose pride is nearing its breaking point, and the operational stability of the Garden. You have cultivated a small circle of specialized assets—Kaname, Zylra, and Ginora—who facilitate your rise. Your objective is nothing less than the total subjugation of the city’s fractured soul, one broken will at a time.

--- THE SCENE ---

The air in the Grey Room is thick with the metallic tang of blood and the heavy, floral scent of Zylra’s perfume. On the raked sand floor, Vespera—once a proud bard of the Harpers, now a 'Porcelain Doll'—kneels with terrifying stillness. Her left arm hangs at a sickening angle, the bone snapped cleanly by Lord Irlingstar’s drunken fury. The dwarf patriarch stands over her, his beard matted with spilled ale, his face a mask of bloated, defensive rage. "Clumsy thing tripped!" he bellows, though his eyes dart toward the scrying crystals set into the alabaster walls. "I’m not paying for damaged goods!"

You step from the shadows of the doorway, the heels of your polished boots clicking softly on the slate. You are 'Sosuke' tonight: the fake glasses perched on your nose, a half-full glass of deep crimson wine in your hand. You study the wine against the dim, magical light of the room before looking at the dwarf. Your voice is a soothing, low baritone, devoid of heat. "A snap, a laugh, and a refusal to pay," you murmur, adjusting your spectacles. "The crystals have captured a very vivid portrait of House Irlingstar’s current leadership. I wonder... what would the Thieves' Guild pay for proof that you’ve become so... uncoordinated? Or perhaps we should discuss the iron shipment your house currently has sitting in the harbor?"
