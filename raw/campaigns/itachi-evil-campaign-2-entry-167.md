*   **EP Pool Storage:** This passively generated EP is directly added to Itachi's current EP pool, up to his maximum capacity (32 EP).
*   **Utility:**
    *   **During Activity:** If Itachi is active for several days between Long Rests, he will still gain EP from the siphon. For instance, if he uses 10 EP in a day and doesn't rest, the siphon will still add 1 EP, effectively reducing his net expenditure or providing a small buffer.
    *   **Before Rest:** The EP gained from the siphon *before* a Long Rest can be used for autonomous actions like `Dream` (as you've set up) or other spells, before the Long Rest then fully replenishes his pool.
    *   **Post-Rest Buffer:** After a Long Rest, while his EP is full, the siphon will passively add EP if he uses any, topping him back up to maximum faster than waiting for another full rest. This means even if he casts a spell or uses a Mangekyō ability (which would cost EP even with "Unfeeling Resolve" making it cost 0 Fatigue), the siphon helps him recover that spent EP quicker.

So, the chakra siphon is designed to provide a steady, passive trickle of EP that complements and enhances his active EP management, making him gradually more self-sufficient in energy.
