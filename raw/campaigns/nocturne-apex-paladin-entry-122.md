The Shadow Weavers' transport team, three cloaked figures, moved with practiced efficiency, locating the bundles in the undergrowth. Nocturne watched from her concealed perch, a silent, unmoving observer. Her mind was already calculating the optimal time for her departure, the path back to Baldur's Gate, and the precise words she would use to present this new, chilling truth to her father.
