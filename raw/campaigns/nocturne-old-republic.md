## Summary
A Star Wars: The Old Republic AU campaign set in 3640 BBY where Emperor Vitiate has evolved beyond the Sith Empire's traditional Dark Council structure. He has implemented "Project: Apotheosis" — a eugenics and Force-breeding program creating the "Golden Lineage" or "House of Vitiate" — biological vessels of his own divine essence. The protagonist Nocturne is one of Vitiate's children, publicly an "Imperial Princess" but secretly a "broken vessel" whose raw power threatens to fracture into the volatile Vaylin persona. She possesses the forbidden Essence Devourer trait, allowing her to consume Force connections to unlock power tiers.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is a Level 1 Sith Prodigy (Siren) with CHA 16, capable of consuming defeated Force users to unlock tiers of power through the "Consumption Tree" system
- The campaign introduces four new siblings: High Marshal Arcann (military), Lord Thexan (diplomacy), Lady Valtoria (Alchemist), Lord Draven (Beastmaster)
- Key political factions: Council of Twelve (Traditionalists), Obsidian Circle (Neo-Imperialists), The Eclipsed (Nihilists), Revanite Cell (Shadow)
- The "Essence Devourer" allows hunting specific Force user "flavors" for permanent mechanical rewards: Seer's Eyes (precognition), Juggernaut's Heart (durability), Assassin's Shadow (stealth), Holocron Eater (knowledge)
- The Emperor's "Harvest" is a ritual to consume the galaxy's life force; Nocturne's siblings are competing to be the "vessel" he eventually consumes
