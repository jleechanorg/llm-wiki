## Summary
A D&D 5e psychological horror campaign set in the Tyranny (Obsidian Entertainment) world of Terratus. Nocturne, a Level 6 Bard (College of Swords), is a Lawful Evil INTJ "Ascendant Architect" who views the world as a flawed schematic to be optimized through mathematical precision. The campaign explores themes of manipulation, mentorship-as-control ("Itachi experiment"), and the refactoring of reality through forbidden knowledge.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne uses a dual-mask system: "Courtly Shadow" (social persona) and "Dispassionate Strategist" (crisis persona)
- The "Itachi Experiment" targets Verse (Scarlet Chorus Fury) — cruel mentorship to stress-test her and create dependency
- Core drives: "Hogyoku Complex" (utility precedes lovability), hatred of "sloppy success," and the "Refactoring of Reality" via Spire magic
- The Edict of Execution looms as a countdown timer that Nocturne intends to weaponize as a restructuring event
- Two companions recruited: Verse (Fury) and Lantry (Scribe), with Barik (Shield-bearer) identified as next target
