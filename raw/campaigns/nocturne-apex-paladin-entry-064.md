Location: Oakhaven Main Road, Early Evening

The silence on the main road was profound, broken only by the chirping of crickets and the distant murmur of the hamlet. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood over the two fallen patrol guards, their professional gear a stark contrast to the rustic surroundings. Their elimination had been swift and silent, a testament to her honed skill and brutal efficiency. The magical sentinel orb, now inert, lay discarded by the roadside. This obstacle, a clear sign of more complex defenses, had been removed with surgical precision.

Her gaze, sharp and analytical (Intelligence 18), swept over the bodies. They were well-equipped, suggesting a higher value than simple bandits. Her meticulous nature (Conscientiousness 4/5) demanded a quick, thorough search before disposing of the evidence. There might be intelligence, or simply useful resources.

Action: [Nocturne searches the bodies of the neutralized patrol guards.]
Nocturne knelt, her movements fluid and economical. She quickly went through their pouches and equipment. Each guard carried a standard shortsword, a light crossbow with a quiver of 20 bolts, and wore well-maintained dark leather armor. In their coin purses, a modest sum of gold. One guard had a small, folded piece of parchment, a simple duty roster for patrols around Oakhaven, confirming their purpose as local security. The sentinel orb was a minor magical device, inert without its user's arcane command, and likely of little value to her.

*   **Loot Acquired:**
    *   2 Shortswords (standard quality)
    *   2 Light Crossbows
    *   2 Quivers with 20 bolts each
    *   **55 gp** (total from both guards' purses)
    *   1 Duty Roster (parchment, confirming patrol times and routes, and that only 2 guards are on this specific route)
    *   2 Sets of Dark Leather Armor (functional, but nothing superior to her own).

The newly acquired gold was a small but satisfying addition to her pouch. The duty roster, however, was far more valuable, providing concrete intelligence on the immediate patrol schedule.

With the loot secured, Nocturne turned her attention to the bodies. Leaving them exposed was an unacceptable risk, a loose end that could lead to discovery and compromise. Her Physique (16) allowed her to handle the task with grim efficiency. She dragged each body into the dense undergrowth bordering the road, ensuring they were completely obscured from casual view, pushing them deeper into the thicket, covering them with loose branches and leaves. It was a temporary measure, but effective for the immediate future.

The road was now truly clear, the silence absolute. The tactical situation had been restored to her advantage. With the patrol route understood and the immediate threat eliminated, her options for the remainder of the night's work had expanded significantly. The information gleaned from the villagers, from her Apex Senses, and now from the patrol's roster painted a clearer picture of Oakhaven's true defenses.

Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) had 1 CP remaining.
Her EP pool was 16/36.
Her Channel Divinity was used (Flawless Opening) and would refresh on a Short or Long Rest.
