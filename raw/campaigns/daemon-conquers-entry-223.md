The sight below them was a maelstrom of chaos and bloodshed. Two armies, each bearing the hawk or the serpent sigil of House Valoria, clashed in a brutal, disorganized melee outside Oakhaven's Reach. The air was thick with the stench of iron and the screams of dying men. Lord Varian, the ambitious younger heir, was bleeding his brother's forces, and his own, dry.

Daemon (Level 9, Age 30) watched the carnage, his grim features betraying a flicker of cold satisfaction. This was not the unified resistance he had prepared for; it was a gift. He turned to Kael (Level 8, Age 25), who stood beside him, his eyes already dissecting the battlefield below. Joric (Level 7, Age 32) and Theron (Level 8, Age 28) waited silently, their hands on their greatswords.

"Kael," Daemon rumbled, his voice low, filled with a predatory calm. "Valoria tears itself apart. This is chaos. Tell me, how do we best seize this opportunity? How do we wield their weakness as our blade? Speak of the paths."

Kael’s gaze swept across the embattled field, his mind already calculating the variables. "My Lord, the Duchy is indeed a ripe fruit, now split open for your taking. This internal conflict presents several avenues, each designed to maximize your gains and minimize your risks, even if the general conquest will be bloody."

*   **Option 1: The Predator's Strike (Decisive Intervention)**
    *   **Kael's Proposal:** "We descend now, My Lord. Strike the flank of the larger, more exposed army – likely Lord Varian’s. Your force, unified and disciplined, will shatter them. The remaining, battered faction will then collapse or flee into the stronghold, which we can then easily besiege. This is a swift, brutal application of overwhelming force."
    *   **Pros (Daemon's view):** Decisive. Crushes both factions quickly. Avoids prolonged waiting.
    *   **Cons (Daemon's view):** Requires a direct engagement immediately. Might still incur some casualties for Daemon's army.
    *   **Kael's Assessment:** "High likelihood of immediate military victory. The chaos will prevent their effective resistance. Estimated 80-90% chance of routing both forces and securing the field."

*   **Option 2: The Vulture's Feast (Patient Exploitation)**
    *   **Kael's Proposal:** "We hold our position, My Lord. Let them bleed each other dry for another day or two. Their strength will be utterly spent. Then, we descend. The victor, weakened and exhausted, will offer little resistance to your fresh, formidable army. It will be a less costly victory for us."
    *   **Pros (Daemon's view):** Very low casualties for Daemon's army. Maximizes enemy's self-destruction.
    *   **Cons (Daemon's view):** Requires patience, which might allow the victor to consolidate slightly. Risk of external interference if we wait too long.
    *   **Kael's Assessment:** "Very high likelihood of victory with minimal cost. Estimated 90-95% chance of swift mop-up, but requires patience."

*   **Option 3: The Serpent's Embrace (Subtle Manipulation)**
    *   **Kael's Proposal:** "We send envoys to one of the warring factions, My Lord – the weaker one, perhaps the besieged defenders within Oakhaven's Reach. We offer them temporary 'aid' against their rival, demanding a heavy price in gold, supplies, and absolute fealty once their internal war is won. Once they have crushed their sibling, we betray them, claiming the stronghold and the Duchy in your name. We leverage their desperation."
    *   **Pros (Daemon's view):** Minimizes bloodshed for Daemon's army. Exploits internal divisions. Adds a layer of cunning to the conquest.
    *   **Cons (Daemon's view):** Requires diplomacy, which is not your preferred method. Risks betrayal or complex political maneuvering. Slower and more uncertain.
    *   **Kael's Assessment:** "Moderate likelihood of success. Estimated 60-70% chance of initial alliance, but the betrayal will require careful planning and execution."

Kael looked to Daemon, awaiting his decision. The fate of Valoria, and the next step in Daemon's bloody ascent, lay in his hands.
