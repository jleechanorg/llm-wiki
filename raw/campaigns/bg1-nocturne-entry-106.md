**Garrick has left the party.**

You turn back to the cage. You slash the rusted lock with *Varscona*, the magical blade shearing through the iron like butter. **Xan** steps out, dusting off his grey robes with an air of tragic dignity. "Out of the cage and into the fire," Xan sighs, fastening the blade to his hip. "We are all doomed, of course. Sarevok's reach is long, and our graves are likely already dug. But I suppose it is better to die fighting than rotting in a kobold's larder."
