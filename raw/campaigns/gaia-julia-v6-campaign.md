## Summary
A D&D 5e alternate history campaign set in 71 BCE Roman Republic during the Spartacus revolt. The player controls Gaia Julia Caesar, a 12-year-old daughter of Emperor Julius Caesar who possesses the divine blood of Venus (Miasma). As a Level 4 College of Swords Bard with the "Glass Cannon" archetype, she is tasked with stabilizing the Spartacus revolt while her Praetorian Hand guards protect her.

## Key Claims
- Gaia Julia is the youngest daughter of Emperor, possessing Miasma (divine Venus blood) granting unique powers
- She serves as an Imperial agent disguised as a servant among mercenaries, on a mission to manipulate the Spartacus revolt
- Her Praetorian Hand consists of five elite veterans: Valerius, Felix, Titus, Cassius, and Brutus
- The campaign takes place primarily in Capua and Rome during 71 BCE

## Key Quotes
> "To the world, you are merely a talented young servant accompanying a band of rugged mercenaries. In reality, you are a Level 4 Bard (College of Swords), a 'Glass Cannon' who wields beauty, brilliance, and a hidden blade with equal lethality."
> "Your mission is simple yet impossible: stabilize the chaos of the Spartacus revolt, protect Rome's image, and prove to your cold, distant father that his youngest daughter is his truest heir."
