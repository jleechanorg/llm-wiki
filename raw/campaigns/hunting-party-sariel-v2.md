## Summary

Sariel, a 16-year-old FBI prodigy and secret vigilante with 100 kills, operates under a masked level system hiding her true Level 9 abilities behind a Level 1 facade. Assigned to track Richard Harris and other serial killers escaped from "The Pit" maximum-security silo in Cheyenne, Wyoming, she navigates team dynamics where allies are polarized by her youth and beauty while evil individuals can sense her predatory nature. Her Sterling Dynasty lineage (bastard daughter of Arthur Sterling) remains a closely guarded secret known only to Agent Odell.

## Key Claims

- **Masked Level System**: Sariel hides Level 9 abilities behind Level 1 perception; Heat mechanic causes masked level to grow as she performs impressive feats
- **True Genius**: IQ of 200, GENUINE lack of self-awareness about her own capabilities — she believes she is normal
- **Vigilante History**: Secretly executed exactly 100 violent criminals before joining FBI; evil individuals can sense her predator status
- **Sterling Dynasty**: Secret bastard daughter of Arthur Sterling (head of Sterling Global); 4 older half-siblings (Marcus, Cassian, Eleanor, Genevieve)
- **Heat Mechanic**: Big wins and feats generate Heat; as Heat accumulates, her masked level rises and attracts attention from allies and enemies
- **Trust System**: Successful missions build Trust with specific allies, granting Persuasion bonuses that scale with relationship level
- **INT-based Casting**: Genius intellect replaces Wisdom for Ranger spellcasting; spells flavored as tactical hacking/tech abilities
