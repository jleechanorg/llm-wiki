## Summary
A Game of Thrones solo D&D 5e campaign where the player controls Ramsay Bolton -- a half-Bolton, half-Stark bastard -- as a custom CHA-based martial class called the Dreadblade Shadow. The campaign opens at the Red Wedding, where Ramsay betrays his father Roose Bolton to save Robb Stark and his men. Themes: honor among evil men, the complexity of moral identity, and the North's fragility.

## Key Claims
- Ramsay's custom class, the Dreadblade Shadow (Levels 1-30), uses Charisma for weapon attacks, grants Umbral Ambush bonuses, and layers in Bard/Ranger spellcasting
- Ramsay claims his mother was a Stark bastard and possesses an ancient direwolf signet ring proving his bloodline
- After rescuing Robb, Ramsay becomes his second-in-command, facing protest from Northern lords who remember his Dreadfort reputation
- Ramsay wields Oath-Eater, a Valyrian steel longsword stolen from his father Roose, and The Whisper, a companion dagger
- Ramsay's retinue includes Kyra (mistress, Level 5 Gloomstalker), Skinner, Sour-Alyn, and Grunt (Level 5 elites), plus 40 soldiers rising to 80
