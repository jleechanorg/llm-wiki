Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood amidst the unsettling silence, the two mastiffs lifeless at her feet. The quick work had been satisfying in its brutal efficiency, but the distant, questioning calls of human voices, followed by hurried footsteps, were an annoyance. The element of surprise was irrevocably lost, at least for this entry point. Her internal estimation of the project's 'cleanliness' dropped slightly. Nevertheless, the mission parameters remained. The hamlet needed to be scouted, targets identified, and the extraction planned. This new complication merely required a rapid adaptation.

Her mind, precise and calculating (Intelligence 18, INTJ), immediately discarded the idea of a full, discreet entry. That ship had sailed with the dogs' barks. Now, it was about managing the fallout, perhaps leveraging the confusion, or simply overpowering whatever meager resistance presented itself. The critical factor was time; the longer she lingered, the more organized any local defense might become. Her remaining CP (1) on this turn was negligible for immediate action, but her full EP pool (31/36) and intact HP (43/43) meant she was ready for direct engagement if necessary.
