- **Base Modifier:** +8 (+5 CHA, +3 Prof).
- **Roll:** 1 (Natural 1).

**4. Comparison to Standard DCs:**
- **DC 10 (Easy):** Passing a commoner on the street without notice.

- **DC 20 (Hard):** Convincing a Sullan Augur you are a commoner.

**Conclusion:** The DC was set at 15 to reflect the high stakes of your 'Singularity' status. The failure was a result of the Natural 1, which bypassed your high modifiers. This creates a narrative complication where your 'Wolf's teeth' have been spotted, resetting your success streak and forcing a shift in tactics.
