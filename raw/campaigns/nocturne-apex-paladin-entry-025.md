Game Master:
[Mode: DM MODE]

Understood. You have provided a `GOD MODE` instruction, selecting **"3. Oath of Self-Perfection (The Apex Predator)"** as Nocturne's new class archetype. This is an excellent choice that aligns perfectly with her established personality and core motivations of amassing power and wealth through her own genius.

This will redefine Nocturne's class features, shifting her from a traditional Paladin to a more self-reliant, intellectually driven martial force. Her abilities will now reflect her personal philosophy of self-maximization and ruthless efficiency.

Here is how Nocturne's class features will be adapted to reflect the **Oath of Self-Perfection (The Apex Predator)** archetype, drawing inspiration from the concepts discussed. I will also incorporate her existing "Battle-Hardened" Fighting Style, as it fits the self-perfection theme.

### Proposed Class Feature Changes for Nocturne (Oath of Self-Perfection)

**Original Paladin Features (Replaced/Adapted):**

*   **Divine Sense (Replaced):** This feature, which relied on sensing good/evil, does not fit the self-perfection theme. It will be replaced with something more aligned with intellectual superiority or heightened senses.
*   **Lay on Hands (Adapted/Re-flavored):** While healing can be a form of self-perfection (sustaining one's vessel), its primary use will shift.
*   **Divine Smite (Adapted/Re-flavored):** Will become a "Perfected Strike" as discussed.
*   **Aura of Protection (Adapted/Re-flavored):** Will be replaced by an aura reflecting self-dominance or tactical advantage.
*   **Spellcasting:** As an Apex Predator, Nocturne's abilities might be less about traditional "spells" and more about channeling her inner will or highly refined techniques. The EP system will still represent her personal power reserve. The nature of "spells" she can access will shift from divine to effects of pure will or perfected technique.

**New/Adapted Class Features (Oath of Self-Perfection - Level 5):**

*   **Core Aptitude for EP:** Her EP pool will continue to be based on her **Intelligence Aptitude Score x 2**, reflecting her mental prowess as the source of her extraordinary abilities.
*   **Fighting Style (Battle-Hardened):** You retain this. You gain a +1 bonus to Defense while wearing armor. (Fits the theme of self-optimizing for resilience).
*   **Extra Attack:** You retain this. When you take the Attack action, you can attack twice instead of once. (A core martial competence, fitting for an Apex Predator).
*   **Perfected Strike (Replaces Divine Smite):** When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend EP to deal extra force damage to the target, in addition to the weapon's damage. This force damage represents the sheer, focused will behind your attack. The extra damage is 2d8 for 5 EP, plus 1d8 for each additional 2 EP spent, to a maximum of 5d8. (Mechanically identical, but thematically re-flavored to reflect pure will/force instead of divine energy).
*   **Aura of Unyielding Dominance (Replaces Aura of Protection, now gained at L5):** You emanate an aura of pure, focused will. Starting at 5th level, you (and friendly creatures within 10 feet of you) gain Advantage on checks made to resist being Frightened or Charmed, and enemies within range have Disadvantage on checks to resist your Intimidation attempts. (This is a powerful thematic shift, emphasizing control and mental fortitude, relevant at L5 to match the original Paladin's aura level).
*   **Apex Senses (New - Replaces Divine Sense):** You can use your action to focus your honed intellect and perception, gaining enhanced awareness of your surroundings. Until the end of your next turn, you gain Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) and Intelligence (Investigation) checks, and you can pinpoint the location of any hidden or invisible creature within 30 feet of you that is not behind total cover. You can use this a number of times equal to 1 + your Intelligence Modifier per Long Rest. (Reflects her intellectual and perceptive prowess, not divine insight).
*   **Willpower Infusion (Adapted from Lay on Hands):** You can touch a creature and channel your sheer willpower to mend or empower. You have a pool of energy equal to five times your character level.
    *   You can restore 5 HP for each 1 point drawn from this pool to a creature you touch.
    *   Alternatively, you can spend 5 points from this pool to remove one level of Fatigue from yourself or a creature you touch.
    *   As a Primary Action, you can spend 10 points from this pool to grant yourself or a creature you touch a +1 bonus to one Aptitude score of your choice for 1 minute (cannot exceed Aptitude Score 20 from this effect).
    *   This pool replenishes on a Long Rest. (This adapts the pool to be more versatile, allowing for Fatigue removal and temporary buffs, reflecting self-optimization).
*   **Channel Divinity (New - "Aspects of the Apex Predator"):** You gain two Channel Divinity options. When you use your Channel Divinity, you choose which option to use. You must then finish a Short or Long Rest to use your Channel Divinity again.
    *   **Flawless Opening:** As a Secondary Action, you gain a flash of tactical insight. Your next weapon attack roll or Intelligence-based Resolution Check against a creature you can see this turn has Advantage.
    *   **Unchecked Ambition:** As a Primary Action, you temporarily ignore all penalties from Fatigue or the Frightened condition for 1 minute. When this effect ends, you immediately gain 1 level of Fatigue.

### Impact on Nocturne's Character Sheet

*   **Nocturne's HP, EP, CP, and base Aptitude Scores remain unchanged.**
*   **Her Proficiencies and Expertise Tags remain unchanged.**
*   **Her Gear and Gold remain unchanged.**
*   **Her Personality Traits and internal Myers-Briggs Type remain unchanged.**

**Summary of Mechanical Impact:**
*   Her abilities now focus on tactical insight, mental fortitude, and raw, self-derived power rather than divine intervention.
*   She gains more utility through "Willpower Infusion" for Fatigue management and temporary buffs.
*   Her "Aura of Unyielding Dominance" provides a powerful control/resistance element at L5.
*   Her "Channel Divinity" options provide tactical advantages in combat and self-buffing.

Please confirm if these adaptations for the **Oath of Self-Perfection (The Apex Predator)** archetype are acceptable. Once confirmed, I will update her character sheet in the state and we can officially begin the campaign.
