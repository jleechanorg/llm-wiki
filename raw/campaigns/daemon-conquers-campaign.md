## Summary
A heroic fantasy campaign with a dark edge, following Daemon — a brute-strength knight of evil alignment whose ultimate goal is to become ruler and king. Set in "a land of dragons," this campaign uses the Destiny Core Rules system to explore the classic fantasy trope of an evil protagonist rising through strength and ambition.

## Player Character
Daemon is a knight of considerable physical prowess and commanding presence. His alignment is firmly Evil, but in the context of heroic fantasy rather than villainous cruelty. His drive is domination — he seeks to rule, to become king. This is not petty villainy but grand ambition: the desire to command, to lead, to possess a throne.

The "brute-strength" descriptor suggests a character who relies on raw power rather than subtlety or magic. In a land where dragons fly and fantasy kingdoms rise, Daemon's path to kingship will likely involve conquest, political maneuvering, or both.
