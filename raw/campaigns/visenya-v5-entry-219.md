Game Master:
Explanatory Audit: The Iron Bank's Financial Architecture

To answer your question: Lord Tywin has not 'paid off' the debt in a single lump sum (the total debt is far too massive, even for the Rock). Instead, he has **assumed** the debt. 

Here is the breakdown of the current financial geometry:

1. **The Debt Assumption:** In Sequence 373, Tywin Lannister officially moved the Crown's liabilities to the Lannister ledger. This means House Lannister is now the **Debtor** and the Iron Bank remains the **Creditor**. Tywin is now responsible for the periodic interest and principal payments that the Regency could no longer afford.

2. **The Bank as Overseer:** The Iron Bank remains in the picture because they are the ultimate 'Risk Managers.' Even though Tywin is paying, the Bank’s collateral is the **stability of the Seven Kingdoms**. If the city falls into chaos or if the 'Blood Dragon' turns out to be an uncontrollable variable that destroys trade, Tywin’s ability to pay becomes a risk. The Auditor (Nestoris) is here to ensure that the environment Tywin is investing in remains a 'going concern.'

3. **The Collateral Variable:** The Bank views King's Landing and its trade routes as the underlying asset. If the 'Blood Dragon' (you) executes too many people or disrupts the economy, you are damaging the Bank's security. This is why Nestoris was so focused on your 'stability' and why he accepted your 'Blackguard math'—he needed proof that the city was a machine that produces gold, not just a fire that consumes it.

4. **Strategic Leverage:** By keeping the Bank in the picture, Tywin maintains a 'Doomsday Clock' over Rhaegar. If the Regent ever defies Tywin, the Hand can simply stop the payments, causing the Iron Bank to immediately fund Stannis Baratheon or the North to replace the Targaryens. 

**Summary:** Tywin is handling the payments, but the Bank stays in the picture as the ultimate judge of whether the kingdom is worth the credit. You have successfully convinced them that your 'Blood Dragon' era is a stable investment, which is why you were granted access to their intelligence network.
