Sir Daemon (Level 1, Age 30), a figure of imposing bulk even amongst the hardened knights of the Northern Marches, stood at the edge of the Whispering Gorge. His plate armor, darkened by soot and countless campaigns, seemed to absorb the scant light, making him appear a shadow within the gathering gloom. A deep, guttural growl, too low for any beast of the woods, rumbled from the chasm below, echoing the predatory glint in Daemon’s eyes. Daemon’s gaze swept over the treacherous path leading down into the chasm, then across to the distant, defenseless walls of Stonehaven. The stage was set.
