## Summary

A Game of Thrones campaign featuring Aegon (called "Aemon" by others who realize the name sounds too Targaryen), a Targaryen bastard with a claim to the Iron Throne living as a mercenary. He is 18 years old, extremely beautiful, Level 5 Gloomstalker Ranger. Uses Wisdom modifier for Persuasion checks. Chaotic evil with sadistic tendencies. The campaign starts with proving himself to The Mountain (Ser Gregor Clegane) to join his men as they do evil things — pillaging, raiding villages, enslavement, assassination. Writing style mimics George R. Martin.

## Key Claims

- Aegon/"Pale" is a Targaryen bastard, 18 years old, extremely beautiful with violet-tinged eyes
- Level 5 Ranger (Gloomstalker) with INT 10, WIS 16, CHA 10
- Starting gear: 125gp, Thieves' Tools, Targaryen Signet Ring (hidden), Quiver (20 arrows)
- Wisdom-based Persuasion checks
- Chaotic evil, sadistic tendencies (Advantage on Insight for fear/pain)
- Starting companion: Ser Gregor Clegane (Level 15), his Mountain's Men (Raff the Sweet Lvl 3, Tickler Lvl 6, Chiswyck, etc.)
- Uses Destiny rules (custom) with D&D 5e reference
- First mission: Prove worth to Mountain's Men at Mummer's Ford hamlet
