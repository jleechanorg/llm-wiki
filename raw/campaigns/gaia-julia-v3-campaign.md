## Summary

Gaia Julia Caesar ("Daughter of the Phoenix, Singularity of the Julian Line") is a Level 1 Sovereign Gestalt -- a psionic mental titan combining Psi Sovereign (Aberrant Mind Sorcerer) with Voice of Empire (Eloquence Bard). Set in 44 BCE Rome/Capua with "Prodigy" profile and psionic "Miasma" visuals. She arrives with 100,000 gold, 20 Elite Legionnaires, 20 Gloomstalkers, and the fanatical Lotus Ten cult. The campaign focuses on political maneuvering with vast resources and royal bloodline power.
