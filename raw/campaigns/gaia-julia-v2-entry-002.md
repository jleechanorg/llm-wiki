Here are the detailed dossiers for The First Cohort, your inner circle. These are the broken souls you have collected, fixed, and bound to your will. In a world of level 1-5 mortals, they are your elite lieutenants, the only ones who know (or suspect) the true extent of your power.

I. Voros (The Thracian Beast)

Archetype: Barbarian (Path of the Zealot).


Origin: The Ludus of Batiatus (Capua).


The Hook: You found him in a holding cell, chained and scheduled for execution because he bit a guard's ear off. You bought him not for labor, but because you saw a monster that needed a gentle hand.


The Dynamic: Beauty and the Beast. To the world, he is a scarred nightmare. To you, he is a large, dangerous dog. You wash the grime off him yourself. He views you as a fragile, glowing idol that must be kept from breaking.

Current Role: Your shadow. He stands behind you at the gate of Batiatus right now. He hates Batiatus for his past treatment, but he obeys your command to stay silent.


The Leash: Devotion. You promised him he would never wear chains again, as long as he wears your favor.

II. Cinnaminus (The Prince of Rats)

Archetype: Rogue (Arcane Trickster).


Origin: The Rooftops of the Subura.

The Hook: You caught him trying to cut your purse. Instead of screaming, you twisted his wrist and realized he wasn't stealing gold, but your family seal.


The Dynamic: The Queen and the Spider. He knows every sewer and secret tunnel in Rome. He falls in love with you conceptually—you are the "Noble Girl" from the stories he wants to save. You exploit this mercilessly, treating him like a younger brother you send into burning buildings.

Current Role: Intelligence. He is currently invisible in the shadows near the Ludus, counting Batiatus's guards and looking for exit routes.


The Leash: Unrequited Worship. He brings you rumors like a cat brings dead birds.

III. Marcus Antonius (The Young Stallion)

Archetype: Fighter (Battle Master) / Bard.


Origin: The Taberna of the Weeping Virgin.


The Hook: You found him drunk on cheap wine, fighting three creditors while reciting bad poetry. You didn't run; you threw a chair to even the odds.



The Dynamic: Bonnie and Clyde. He is your mirror: noble-born but loves the gutter. He challenges your hedonism; if you drink three cups, he drinks four. It is a toxic, electric romance.

Current Role: The Face / Distraction. He is currently disguised as the captain of your "mercenary" guard (The Twenty), drinking with the locals to spread rumors about your vast wealth.


The Leash: Obsession/Competition. He stays because you are the only woman more dangerous than he is.

IV. Lucius Vorenus (The Disgraced Centurion)

Archetype: Paladin (Oath of the Crown).


Origin: Working as a bouncer in a brothel.


The Hook: He recognized you as Caesar's daughter and tried to take you into "protective custody". You talked him down using Auctoritas.


The Dynamic: The Old Guard. He represents the Rome that Was—rigid and honorable. He looks at you and sees a spoiled, dangerous child mocking tradition, but he stays because he swore an oath to your father.

Current Role: Drillmaster. He commands "The Twenty," ensuring your elite Praetorians maintain their cover as common sellswords while keeping their discipline iron-clad.


The Leash: Duty. He is the only one who tells you "No".

V. Servilia (The Vestal Drop-out)

Archetype: Warlock (Great Old One - The Miasma) / Cleric.


Origin: The Temple of Vesta.


The Hook: You broke into the archives to steal scrolls; she caught you and critiqued your lockpicking.


The Dynamic: The Witches. She is your intellectual equal, fascinated by your Miasma. She interprets your dreams and encourages your darkest impulses. "Do not weep for hammers," she tells you when you break people.


Current Role: Arcane Support. She is back in Rome (or in your travel carriage), managing the magical wards that keep the Senate's diviners from locating you.

The Leash: Knowledge. She wants to understand the Singularity you represent.
