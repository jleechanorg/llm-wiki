This is a one-time, significant cost for a permanent, undeniable upgrade to their fighting capability. Alternatively, to hire a large, decisive mercenary force for a single, overwhelming strike, you would look at an upfront payment of **5,000 to 10,000 Gold Pieces**, depending on their experience and numbers, with additional ongoing pay."

*   **Option 2: The Unseen Hand (Intelligence & Influence)**
    *   **Cost:** "To establish a comprehensive, regional spy network, capable of gathering vital intelligence and executing subtle acts of sabotage and bribery, would demand an initial outlay of **5,000 to 8,000 Gold Pieces**. *   **Option 3: The Enduring Foundation (Infrastructure & Defense)**
    *   **Cost:** "To significantly fortify even one of your Keeps – say, Silverport or Riverbend – with improved walls, watchtowers, and expanded garrisons, would cost **10,000 to 15,000 Gold Pieces per Keep**. To embark on wider infrastructure improvements across all your territories – roads, expanded mines, enhanced farmlands – you would be looking at an investment of **20,000 to 30,000 Gold Pieces** for initial, impactful projects, with long-term, gradual returns."

Kael finished, his eyes returning to Daemon's, awaiting the next command. The numbers were laid bare, the strategic choices defined by the gold in Daemon's coffers.
