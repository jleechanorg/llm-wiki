## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in an alternate 40 BCE Roman Empire where Julius Caesar survived the Ides of March. Aurelius Julia Caesar, Level 5 Paladin (Oath of Domination), is a Lawful Good anti-slavery son of the living God-Emperor. The campaign follows his rise through the Cursus Honorum while navigating succession politics against his brothers Caesarion and Octavian.

## Key Claims
- Aurelius wields Auctoritas (Charisma as physical force) and the Oath of Domination subclass
- Aura of Sovereignty grants +2 AC, Aura of Submission immobilizes enemies via Wisdom save
- Sixteen years old, HP 44, companions: Spartacus, Crixus, Gannicus, Aurelia
- The campaign features 9 noble house factions, the Parthian threat, and the succession crisis
- Anti-slavery stance creates political tension in a slave-dependent empire

## Key Quotes
