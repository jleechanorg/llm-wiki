I also failed to explicitly prompt for confirmation before initiating a complex scene like combat.

I failed to adequately distinguish between meta-discussion/instructions and direct commands for narrative progression. This is a fundamental error in upholding player agency and the collaborative nature of our campaign.

**Commitment to Future Adherence:**

I reaffirm my absolute commitment to:
*   **Core Directive #1: Player Agency is Absolute:** I will **never** make a narrative decision that determines the outcome of a scene or advances the story without your explicit command.
