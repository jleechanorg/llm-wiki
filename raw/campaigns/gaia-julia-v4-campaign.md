## Summary

Set in 73 BCE during the Third Servile War (Spartacus rebellion), Gaia Julia Caesar is a Level 12 Siren (Bard/Paladin Gestalt) and daughter of the God-Emperor Julius Caesar in an alternate history where Caesar already rules Rome. She secretly operates as "Livia of Capua" -- a Level 1 captive -- while being a biological weapon of social engineering. The Lotus Ten cult forms a living wall of flesh around her. Spartacus and Crixus lead the rebel forces she manipulates.
