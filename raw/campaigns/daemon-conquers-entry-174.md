Game Master:
Daemon (Level 7, Age 30) sat at the head of the feast table, the rich food tasting like ash in his mouth. His eyes, however, devoured every detail of Blackwood Keep's interior and its inhabitants. He observed Isolde's (Age: ~22) staff – older, some with years of ingrained loyalty to the Blackwood name, others clearly nervous opportunists. Their movements were practiced but lacked the brutal efficiency of his own hardened recruits. He noted the quiet tension among the household guards, their fear of him palpable, a useful tool but not a guarantee of loyalty. This was an ancient house, steeped in tradition, not the grim, efficient machines Daemon preferred.

Later, in the privacy of his chambers, Daemon turned to Kael (Level 6, Age 25), who had been equally observant. "Their lands are fertile, beyond doubt," Daemon stated, a grim satisfaction in his tone. "A granary for our growing army. But the keep's defenses are antiquated, and their forces meager. They have relied on isolation, a luxury they no longer have under my banner. The risk is not open defiance, but the subtle, ingrained habits of old power."

Kael nodded, his own assessment aligning. "Indeed, My Lord. Isolde is a calculating woman. She accepted for survival and ambition. She will need to be reminded, constantly, that her ascension is entirely dependent on your will, and that her loyalty must be absolute. The old retainers may present a passive resistance, siphoning resources or quietly undermining your authority. We must ensure their supply lines are fully integrated, and their wealth accounted for, immediately."
