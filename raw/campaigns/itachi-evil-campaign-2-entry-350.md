### **Finalization Protocol: Differential Report**

**Proposed Changes for [Change ID: 20240122-A]**

**Source Document Word Count (Relevant Sections):** Approx. 150 words (EMS mentions, Mangekyō ability descriptions)
**New Draft Word Count (Relevant Section):** Approx. 500 words (New Mutual EMS mechanic, updated ability lists for both, and new benefits)

**A. Change Description: Introduction of Mutual Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan (EMS)**

*   **Rationale:** To define the unique mechanics of the player's chosen "mutual eye exchange" for EMS, where both Itachi and Sasuke gain Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan by integrating the other's eyes, thereby inheriting all unique Mangekyō abilities from their sibling. This fundamentally alters the power progression for both characters, aligning with the "absolute control" and "ultimate weapon" themes.
*   **Location:** This new mechanic will be integrated into the character manifests for both Itachi and Sasuke, and referenced in the core `Destiny` ruleset where EMS is mentioned.

**B. Proposed New Mechanic: Mutual Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan (EMS) Acquisition**

When two Uchiha siblings with Mangekyō Sharingan perform a mutual, willing eye exchange and integration:

1.  **Acquisition:** Both Uchiha (Itachi and Sasuke in this case) gain the **Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan (EMS)**.
2.  **Ocular Stability:** The EMS grants both users:
    *   **Eternal Vision:** Permanent protection from the blindness associated with Mangekyō overuse.
    *   **Stable Mangekyō:** All Mangekyō abilities (both original and inherited) consume 0 Fatigue.
3.  **Ability Inheritance & Potency (Shared Powers):**
    *   Each user gains **all unique Mangekyō abilities** of the other. These inherited abilities function as if they were natively awakened, consuming the user's own EP.
        *   **Itachi's New Abilities (from Sasuke):** Sasuke's distinct Amaterasu variation, and Sasuke's unique Chidori manifestation.
        *   **Sasuke's New Abilities (from Itachi):** Itachi's Tsukuyomi, Itachi's Amaterasu, and Itachi's Susano'o (Perfected Form, with Totsuka Blade and Yata Mirror).
    *   The harmonious fusion of powers imbues *both* Itachi's and Sasuke's Mangekyō with **increased potency**: all their Mangekyō abilities (original and inherited) gain a **+2 bonus to their Spell Save DC and a +2 bonus to their damage rolls/temporary HP generated**. (This stacks with Itachi's existing +1 from "Perfected Chakra Control" for his original abilities, making it +3 total for Itachi's original abilities, and +2 for his newly inherited ones and all of Sasuke's).
4.  **EP Cost Adjustments:**
    *   **Itachi:** His "Chakra System Optimization" (Level 20) already grants -3 EP cost to all Illusion spells. This now applies to all his *inherited* Illusion-category Mangekyō abilities as well (e.g., Sasuke's Amaterasu variation if categorized as Illusion, Itachi's original Tsukuyomi/Amaterasu/Susano'o).
    *   **Sasuke:** His inherited Mangekyō abilities (from Itachi) will use standard EP costs (minus any base cost reductions if Mangekyō itself has a passive reduction, but not Itachi's specific optimization).
5.  **Consequences:** The exchange is a profound, irreversible physical and spiritual alteration for both. Both will be temporarily incapacitated during the procedure and require recovery.
6.  **XP Gain:** Both Itachi and Sasuke will gain significant XP for this ultimate achievement.
