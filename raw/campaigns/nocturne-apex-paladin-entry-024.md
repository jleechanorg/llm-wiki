Understood. You have provided a `GOD MODE` instruction to "lets change nocturne class. Give me some ideas for a dark paladin."

This is a significant change that will redefine Nocturne's abilities and thematic identity. I will now propose a few distinct concepts for a "dark paladin" class, tailored to Nocturne's established personality (INTJ, Agreeableness 1/5, Neuroticism 4/5, ruthless, calculating) and her pursuit of power and wealth. These concepts will adapt the spirit of a paladin's divine connection to a more sinister, self-serving, or amoral path.

Please consider these ideas and let me know which resonates most, or if you'd like to combine elements or suggest a new direction. Once a concept is chosen, we will then adapt her specific features (spells, auras, smites) to fit the new archetype.

---

### Dark Paladin Archetype Ideas for Nocturne

Here are three distinct concepts for a "Dark Paladin," each offering a different flavor of corrupted power:

**1. Oath of Domination (The Tyrant's Blade)**
*   **Concept:** This archetype represents a paladin who has sworn themselves not to a deity or ideal of justice, but to the principle of absolute control and dominance. Their power stems from their unyielding will, their capacity to instill fear, and their conviction that they are destined to rule. They might draw power from a malevolent entity of order (like Bane or Vecna, or even a twisted interpretation of a lawful neutral power) or simply from their own burgeoning ego and ambition.
*   **Key Themes:** Authority, fear, control, absolute order (often through tyranny), manipulation of lesser wills.
*   **Potential Features (Thematic Adaptation):**
    *   **Divine Smite (Renamed/Re-flavored):** "Soul-Crushing Smite" - still deals radiant/necrotic damage, but might also impose fear or a minor penalty on the target's next action, reflecting the overwhelming force of her will.
    *   **Lay on Hands (Re-flavored):** "Iron Will's Command" - could still heal, but perhaps also be used to remove conditions like Frightened or Charmed from allies, or to impose a similar condition on enemies as a mental assault. Maybe a version that drains life to heal self.
    *   **Auras:** "Aura of Dread" - instead of protecting, it might impose Disadvantage on attack rolls for enemies within range against Nocturne, or make them Frightened on a failed Wisdom check. "Aura of Obedience" - allies within range gain Advantage on checks to resist being charmed or intimidated.
    *   **Channel Divinity (Examples):** "Commanding Presence" (Force an enemy to make a Wisdom save or drop their weapon/obey a simple command). "Terrifying Visage" (All enemies in an area make a Wisdom save or are Frightened).

**2. Oath of the Shadow Pact (The Corrupted Conduit)**
*   **Concept:** This archetype has forged a pact with a dark power – a demon lord, an elder evil, a primordial force of shadow or death, or even a powerful devil – exchanging their soul's purity for immense power. Their abilities are tainted, often drawing on necrotic energies, shadows, or corrupting influence. They are not necessarily tyrants, but agents of a greater darkness, or simply individuals who have found a shortcut to power through forbidden means.
*   **Key Themes:** Forbidden knowledge, infernal bargains, necrotic energy, life drain, subtle corruption, pacts.
*   **Potential Features (Thematic Adaptation):**
    *   **Divine Smite (Renamed/Re-flavored):** "Necrotic Smite" - deals necrotic damage instead of radiant, and might siphon a small amount of HP back to Nocturne on a successful hit.
    *   **Lay on Hands (Re-flavored):** "Whispers of the Grave" - could still heal, but perhaps also be used to deal necrotic damage to an enemy by touch, or grant temporary hit points by draining nearby life force.
    *   **Auras:** "Aura of Blight" - might cause minor necrotic damage to enemies entering or starting turn in range, or impose Disadvantage on enemy saving throws against necrotic effects. "Aura of Shadow Veil" - allies in range gain concealment in dim light/darkness.
    *   **Channel Divinity (Examples):** "Invoke Dark Bargain" (Temporarily gain a powerful but risky bonus, e.g., Advantage on all attacks for one round but take damage at end of turn). "Corrupting Touch" (Touch a creature, on failed Health save, they gain a level of Fatigue or a temporary penalty to an Aptitude).

**3. Oath of Self-Perfection (The Apex Predator)**
*   **Concept:** This archetype has no fealty to a deity or external power, but rather to their own inherent potential and the brutal philosophy of self-maximization. Their "oath" is to themselves – to become the ultimate, most efficient, and most powerful being possible. Their abilities manifest as raw, focused power, an unnatural resilience, and a chilling intellect that grants them superiority over all others. This perfectly aligns with her personality inspirations (Aizen, Itachi, Johan, Tywin) and her core goal of amassing power and wealth through her own genius.
*   **Key Themes:** Ego, self-reliance, intellectual superiority, raw power, ruthless efficiency, personal evolution.
*   **Potential Features (Thematic Adaptation):**
    *   **Divine Smite (Renamed/Re-flavored):** "Perfected Strike" - pure force damage (or a damage type representing pure destructive will) that might also bypass a small amount of an enemy's DR or Defense.
    *   **Lay on Hands (Re-flavored):** "Willpower Infusion" - could heal, but more importantly, remove Fatigue, or grant temporary increases to Aptitude scores or CP for self or allies, reflecting her ability to push limits.
    *   **Auras:** "Aura of Unyielding Dominance" - instead of protection, enemies within range have Disadvantage on checks to resist Nocturne's Intimidation attempts or resist her special abilities. "Aura of Calculated Advantage" - Nocturne gains a bonus to her initiative checks.
    *   **Channel Divinity (Examples):** "Flawless Opening" (Gain a tactical insight, granting Advantage on your next Intelligence or attack roll against a chosen enemy). "Unchecked Ambition" (Temporarily ignore all penalties from Fatigue or conditions for one minute, but take a level of Fatigue afterwards).
