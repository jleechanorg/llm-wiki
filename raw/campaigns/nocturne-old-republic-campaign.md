## Summary
A Star Wars The Old Republic alternate universe campaign set in 3640 BBY on Dromund Kaas. Nocturne, a Level 1-6 Sith Prodigy and "Siren" of House Vitiate, is an Essence Devourer involved in Project Apotheosis. The campaign explores the Golden Lineage, Vitiate dynasty politics, and galactic harvest themes.

## Key Claims
- Project Apotheosis aims to elevate Nocturne through essence consumption
- The Golden Lineage represents pure Vitiate bloodline inheritance
- House Vitiate controls the Sith Empire through Emperor Vitiate
- Essence Devourer trait allows consumption of life force

## Key Quotes
> "The Emperor sees all. The Emperor hungers always." — House Vitiate mantra

> "One soul becomes many. Many souls become power." — On essence consumption
