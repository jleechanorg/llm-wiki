## Summary
A dark, predatory adventure following Nocturne, a Lawful Evil Human Ranger (Gloom Stalker) raised by Gorion at Candlekeep in 1368 DR. Set during the Iron Crisis on the Sword Coast, the campaign explores political intrigue between Baldur's Gate and Amn while the protagonist navigates a morally bankrupt world with predatory efficiency.

## Key Details
- **Protagonist:** Nocturne — Human Ranger (Gloom Stalker), Lawful Evil "Serpent Queen"
- **Setting:** Sword Coast (Candlekeep → Baldur's Gate), 1368 DR, Mirtul 1, Iron Crisis economic collapse
- **Core Theme:** Survival through predatory efficiency in a corrupt world; political upheaval and economic collapse
- **Tone:** Dark, predatory protagonist, political intrigue
- **Total Scenes:** Starting point of campaign

## Player Character
Nocturne is a Gloom Stalker Ranger raised by Gorion at the ancient fortress library of Candlekeep. Born in 1368 DR, she carries the moniker "Serpent Queen" — a title earned through her cold, calculating approach to survival. Her alignment of Lawful Evil manifests not as mindless cruelty, but as a pragmatic understanding that the world is a web of predators and prey, and she intends to be the former.

The campaign introduces a unique mechanical hook: "The Hunger" — a state-tracking system where Nocturne gains +2 to all stats when satiated and suffers -2 to all stats during withdrawal. This creates narrative tension around resource management and survival.
