She is the end of the Discord, or the end of the world.*

We will not move against her. Not yet. We will watch the fire she has lit, and pray it only burns our enemies."

Tywin's eyes narrowed. He saw the Regent's dread. *He's afraid of his own blood,* the Lion realized. *And if a man of Rhaegar's level is afraid, then I must be twice as prepared to strike.*
