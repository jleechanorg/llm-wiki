## Summary
A D&D 5e survival horror campaign identical in structure to the V5 Succubus campaign. Nocturne Sosuke, a Level 1 Bard (College of Perdition), is de-powered from Level 6 after inverting the Cassalanter pact. Malcanthet (Queen of Succubi) replaces Zariel as her patron. Begins at the Ravaged Beach after the Nautiloid crash.
