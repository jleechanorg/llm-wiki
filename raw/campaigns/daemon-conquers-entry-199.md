He set off for Silverport with Elara, accompanied by Joric (Level 7, Age 32) and Theron (Level 6, Age 28), his two most trusted elite warriors, their presence a silent shield. Elara, quiet and submissive, complied, her fear of Daemon's displeasure outweighing any personal discomfort. By day, the small retinue pushed on, Daemon's mind already assessing the next strategic moves, even as the biological imperative gnawed at him.
