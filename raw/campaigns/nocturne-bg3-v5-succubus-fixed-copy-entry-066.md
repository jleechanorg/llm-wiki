- **Hit Points:** You rolled a **5** on your Hit Die. With your Constitution modifier (-1), you gain **4** HP. Your maximum HP is now **16**.
- **Proficiency Bonus:** Remains **+2**.

### 🎭 Bard College: The Abyssal Siren
You have formally joined the **College of the Abyssal Siren**. You now weaponize desire and psychological weight with surgical precision.
- **The Queen's Curriculum:** You gain proficiency with **Medium Armor** and the **Scimitar**. You can now use your Ritual Scimitar as a spellcasting focus.
- **Siren’s Authority (Expertise):** You have mastered the art of the long game. You gain **Expertise** in **Deception** and **Persuasion**, doubling your proficiency bonus for these skills.
- **The Gilded Tether:** You can now bind humanoids to your will through your magic or spare them at 0 HP to make them **Thralls**. (Current Limit: 3 + 3 = 6 Active Thralls).
- **Abyssal Arts (Modified Blade Flourish):** When you attack, your speed increases by 10ft. You can expend Bardic Inspiration to activate **The Lash** (Psychic damage), **The Caress** (Pull target), or **The Command** (+AC).

### 🔮 Spellcasting Expansion
- **Level 2 Spell Slots:** You have unlocked **Level 2 Spell Slots (2 total)**.
- **New Spell:** You can choose one 2nd-level spell (or lower) from the Bard list. *Common choices for a Siren include: Suggestion, Hold Person, Invisibility, or Phantasmal Force.*
