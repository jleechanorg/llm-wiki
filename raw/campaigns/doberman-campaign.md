## Summary
A corporate intrigue and psychological manipulation campaign adapted from the K-drama "Military Prosecutor Doberman." Following Sariel, a disgraced chaebol heiress working for the NILFGAARD-esque IM Defense corporation, the campaign explores themes of power, control, and the ultimate desire for dominance through subtle orchestration rather than overt force.

## Key Details
- **Protagonist:** Sariel — Human Bard (College of Swords), Level 1
- **Setting:** Seoul, South Korea, K-drama adaptation world
- **Core Theme:** Corporate manipulation, psychological power dynamics, ultimate control
- **Tone:** Corporate intrigue, psychological manipulation, K-drama adaptation
- **Total Scenes:** Campaign start

## Player Character
Sariel is a disgraced chaebol (Korean conglomerate family) heiress who has fallen from grace and now works for Noh Tae-nam at IM Defense — a powerful corporation that serves as the "Nilfgaard" of this world, led by the formidable Noh Hwa-young.

Her character is defined by subtle menace wrapped in perfect etiquette. She speaks softly with flawless manners, observes weaknesses like a scholar studies texts, and possesses a compulsive urge to orchestrate humiliating power dynamics. She is, in essence, "The Serpent in the Service" — a manipulator who seeks ultimate control.

## Key NPCs / Companions
- **Noh Tae-nam** — Her direct superior at IM Defense
- **Noh Hwa-young** — Leader of IM Defense, "Nilfgaard" equivalent
