## Summary

A modern-day tactical thriller set in the "Hunting Party" Netflix TV show universe. Sariel is a Level 5 Gloomstalker Ranger, former FBI Special Agent in Charge (SAC), and secretly a serial killer with a "Code." She was dishonorably discharged for excessive force but is reinstated to hunt 12 escaped serial killers from Blackgate prison. The campaign opens at the Neon Dragon Casino where FBI agents Marcus Vance and Sarah Miller recruit her with a $50M operational budget and $5M personal fee.

## Key Claims

- Sariel has 100+ confirmed serial killer kills as a vigilante before joining the FBI, earning the "Blood Dragon" moniker
- She negotiates extreme terms: $50M operational budget, $5M personal fee, full discretion, no oversight
- Her INT is 18, making her an Intelligence-based spellcaster with profiling capabilities
- She has a "Dark Passenger" — a secret compulsion to kill criminals who escape justice, independent of FBI orders
- The Blackgate Twelve escape was a coordinated IED attack with inside intel — a "Total Collapse Protocol"
- She commands a team of 3 Level 5 companions: Kaelen "Ghost" Vance (Rogue), Dr. Aris Thorne (Cleric), Jax "Breaker" Malone (Fighter)
- She is extremely beautiful and uses her appearance as a weapon/unsettling factor
