Daemon (Level 7, Age 30) stood before a large, newly unrolled map of the surrounding lands, the latest scout reports scattered across its surface. His armor gleamed, a silent testament to the month of relentless training he had just overseen. Kael (Level 6, Age 25), his Castellan of Silverport, stood nearby, his keen eyes already assessing the intel. Joric (Level 6, Age 32), Gareth (Level 6, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 6, Age 33), Theron (Level 6, Age 28), and Borin (Level 7, Age 30) were in the outer courtyard, overseeing the hardened Level 3 recruits.

"Kael," Daemon rumbled, his voice cutting through the silence of the Mayor's war room. "We are stronger. Our coffers swell. The reports are in. Tell me, what is the next move? Where does my blade fall next? What target offers the greatest return for the least resistance, or perhaps, the greatest strategic value for the long game? And these whispers of noble daughters... is there a weakness to exploit through blood as well as steel?"

Kael stepped closer to the map, his finger tracing a path along the trade routes leading from Silverport. "My Lord, the options are plentiful, and your scouts have proven their worth."

He indicated a region to the east. "To the east lies the **Barony of Ironhollow**. Its lord is an aging, paranoid recluse, his keep strong but his forces depleted by recent banditry. The land is rich in ore, and its mines would significantly boost our raw material income. It would be a swift, brutal campaign, cementing our presence inland."

His finger then moved north. "Alternatively, to the north, beyond a rugged mountain pass, is the **Free City of Brightwater**. It's smaller than Silverport, but a hub of artisan crafts and rare materials. Its defenses are better, but its mayor is known to be fearful and susceptible to intimidation. Conquest would be harder, but the unique resources could open new avenues of power."

Kael then gestured to a separate report. "Regarding political alliances, My Lord, the scouts have returned with information on **Lady Isolde of House Blackwood**. Her family controls fertile farmlands to the south, vital for supplying a growing army. She is young, ambitious, and her house is currently isolated, seeking an advantageous match. A marriage, while distasteful to some, could secure a vast food supply and a loyal, if terrified, ally, without shedding a drop of blood."

He looked up, meeting Daemon's gaze. "Each path offers different gains, My Lord. Immediate wealth and raw materials from Ironhollow, specialized resources from Brightwater, or a bloodless alliance and food supply from House Blackwood."
