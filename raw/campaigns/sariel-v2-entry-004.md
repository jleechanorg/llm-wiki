***

### **1. The Nature of Alexiel's Power**

What you have uncovered in the journals is the fundamental truth. Your mother, Alexiel, was not a "Null" in the way the world understands it. She was a unique, one-of-a-kind being, the ultimate success and horror of the Seraphim's "Null Genesis Project."

*   **The "Null" Aspect:** This is an innate ability to project a "Nullification Field"—an aura of absolute magical silence. This power is a rare mutation of the Empyrean bloodline, born from the ancient genetic experiments you read about.
*   **The Magic-User Aspect:** This is the secret that made her a legend. Because she was cloned using the potent celestial essence of Lucifer himself, she possessed immense innate magical talent. This "Luciferian Spark" allowed her to be the only being capable of casting her own spells *inside* her own Nullification Field.
*   **The "Joyful Predator" Trait:** This is the psychological component. The journals theorize that this is a refined, lucid form of the Empyrean Affliction. It's the profound, intellectual satisfaction she derived from the perfect execution of a plan, whether it was a subtle political dismantling or a swift, elegant act of violence. It's what gave her the cold, terrifying focus to wield her paradoxical powers so effectively.

In essence, your mother was a living paradox: a being who could create an area where magic didn't exist, and then be the only person in that area who could use magic. This combination made her the ultimate weapon.

### **2. How You Can Acquire This Power**

This is the crucial part. You, Sariel, are **not** a Null. You are a full-fledged Empyrean, marked by your Starlit Eyes, just like your father, Artorius. Therefore, you cannot acquire your mother's power *in the same way*. You cannot simply "turn on" a Nullification Field because your genetics are fundamentally different.

However, this does not mean a similar power is beyond your reach. Your own innate gift is different, but potentially just as powerful: **Mirror Magic**.

*   **Your Path to Power:** Your character's journey is not about awakening a Null-state, but about mastering your latent Mirror Magic. As you grow in power and overcome your trauma, you will learn to replicate magical effects you witness or understand.
*   **Replicating the Unthinkable:** The key lies in your own memory. You are the only living person to have witnessed your mother's ultimate act: the massive expansion of the Nullification Field at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields. That memory—the feeling of the field, its effect on the world, the will required to manifest it—is burned into your very soul.
*   **The Goal:** It is theoretically possible that, once you have mastered your Mirror Magic, you could delve into that traumatic memory and learn to **replicate the Nullification Field effect**. You would not be a "Null," but a Mirror Mage powerful enough to copy the universe's most profound anti-magic ability. This would be an immense challenge, the culmination of your entire character arc, and would likely come with its own risks and costs.

### **3. Nullification is Innate, Not a Procedure**

To be clear, being a "Null" is not a process or something that is "done" to a person. It is an innate, genetic state of being, a result of the Seraphim's experiments. Your mother was born a Null. You were born a magic-user. Your paths are, by nature, different.

In summary: You cannot become your mother, but through your own unique talents, you may one day learn to wield her greatest weapon.
