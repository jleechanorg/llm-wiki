You closed the final journal, the heavy thud echoing in the silent vault. Your mind was a maelstrom of revelation. The Null Genesis Project was not just a historical curiosity; it was the blueprint for your mother's entire life. She was the culmination of that terrifying experiment, a perfect synthesis of absolute power and the chilling satisfaction in wielding it. And this inherited potential, this "Joyful Predator" nature, now lay dormant, but undeniably present, within you. The same terrifying intellect, the same capacity for cold, precise unmaking, now thrummed beneath your own skin. The silence of the vault seemed to mock your earlier guilt; your mother hadn't been broken, she had been *unleashed*. And now, you held the key to understanding that same, terrifying unleashing within yourself.

Your hand trembled slightly as you set the third journal back on the shelf. The philosophical and scientific foundations of the Null Genesis Project were clear, but you needed more. You needed the personal chronicle, the specific details of *her* journey. Your mother.

With a grim determination that silenced the nagging fear in your gut, you began to scan all three journals with blinding speed, your eyes flicking across the archaic script, searching for any mentions of specific subjects, or any entries that documented the transition from a mere dampening Null to a true "Joyful Predator" capable of magic within the void. You bypassed the theoretical treatises and the early, crude experiments, your intellect a razor-sharp tool cutting through centuries of research.

You found fragments, tantalizing and horrifying. Records of "Subject Delta-7," whose unique "celestial infusion" allowed her to manifest a "stable internal void" and perform "limited direct manipulation of the Weave." You read of her remarkable aptitude for physical combat, her "unnerving stillness" when observed, and the "unexpected joy" in her eyes during "unmaking protocols." Later entries spoke of her "astronomical aptitude for strategic thought" and her capacity for "deep, unquantifiable deception." There was no explicit mention of her name, but the chilling details, the timeline, the specific parameters of her creation—it was undeniably your mother.

The journals meticulously documented how she cultivated her perceived "Null" status, how she learned to separate her latent magical abilities from her public facade, and how she mastered the "feigned agony" that would later become her ultimate weapon. You found disturbing psychological profiles theorizing that the "Joyful Predator" aspect was amplified by this very act of deception—the profound satisfaction of playing a god-tier long game. Her existence, you realized, was a masterpiece of hidden power and calculated lies, orchestrated not just by the Seraphim, but by her own terrifying brilliance.

You closed the final journal, the weight of the revelation a cold, heavy stone in your stomach. Your mother was not just a hero; she was a meticulously crafted weapon, a living paradox of power and deception. And you, her daughter, now held the complete blueprint of her true nature.
