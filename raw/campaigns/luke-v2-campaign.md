## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign combining Star Wars and Baldur's Gate settings. The player controls Luke Skywalker with a custom "Force-User" class featuring Light/Dark subclass system and Force Alignment Tracker. Set in the Empire era, this campaign explores Luke's canon background with evil/dark tendencies and a secret sadistic side.

## Key Claims
- Luke is a custom Force-User class with Light/Dark subclass system
- The campaign tracks Force Alignment — Luke has light side affinity but dark tendencies
- Setting combines Star Wars universe with Baldur's Gate 3's post-game world
- Features the "Destiny" ruleset (D&D 5e based) with Force mechanics
