Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**61790 XP**) to reach **Level 15**. This is a monumental achievement, placing him firmly in the Tier 3 range of shinobi power, capable of dealing with major national threats and ancient evils, and nearing the peak of mortal potential.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 15:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 7
    *   **HP Gain:** 7 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 9 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 113 to **122 HP**. He is now at 122/122 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 15 Ninja, Itachi gains significant advancements as per the approved "Itachi Uchiha: Ninja Custom Class Feature Manifest":

    1.  **Potent Mangekyō Mastery (Custom):** The profound insights from his Rinnegan research and his relentless self-forging have culminated in a terrifying mastery over his Mangekyō Sharingan. The **Fatigue gained from any Mangekyō Sharingan ability activation is permanently reduced to 0 levels.** This reinforces and makes explicit the full extent of his "Unfeeling Resolve" trait for all future Mangekyō power usage.

    2.  **New Illusion Spells (8th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu reaches its penultimate peak, unlocking illusions capable of utterly twisting reality or imprisoning even the most formidable minds. He learns one of the following 8th-level Illusion spells:
        *   **1. Illusory Reality (Variant of Hallucinatory Terrain/Programmed Illusion):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 15 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create an illusion of terrain or objects that is so convincing it becomes physically real not just for perception, but for a limited time (e.g., 1 minute) or for a specific number of interactions (e.g., 3 physical interactions). This allows for creating real obstacles, pits, walls, or even temporary structures that fool even physical interaction. The illusion can support weight, block movement, or trigger environmental effects. After the duration or interactions, it reverts to being purely illusory. (This is a more potent version of Mirage Arcane's 'physical reality' aspect, allowing it to affect objects, not just terrain).
        *   **2. Trap Soul (Variant of Imprisonment/Soul Cage):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 15 EP.
            *   **Effect:** Target a creature Itachi can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 19 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or its soul is momentarily ripped from its body and trapped within an illusory dimension of Itachi's choosing (e.g., a mental prison, a torturous landscape). The target's body falls unconscious. While trapped, Itachi can interrogate or torment the soul directly. The effect lasts until Itachi ends it (no concentration) or for up to 1 hour. The soul can attempt a new Wisdom (Willpower) check at the end of each of its turns to escape. If successful, the soul returns to its body, and the target is stunned for 1 round. If the soul is trapped for the full duration, the target gains 2 levels of Fatigue.

Please choose one of the 8th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 15:

*   **1. Illusory Reality**
*   **2. Trap Soul**
