## Scene 001: Character Review — Nocturne Sosuke

**Timestamp:** Session 1 | **Location:** Minthara's War Room, Shattered Sanctum | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

The velvet curtains of the *Bloom Shadow* ripple as Nocturne stands before the polished silver mirror — a vision of porcelain perfection and ruined silk. She is **Nocturne Sosuke**, the "Black Diamond," a sixteen-year-old paradox of aristocratic grace and Abyssal hunger. Her skin has begun to take on the unsettlingly flawless, marble-like texture — the first sign of the *Uncanny Valley* she is descending into.

At **Level 5**, Nocturne displaces the air in the room. Her violet eyes reflect torchlight with a glass-like clarity that makes battle-hardened veterans look away. She stands in **Minthara's War Room** within the Shattered Sanctum, her small hand pinned beneath the Drow Commander's cool palm. The smell of ozone and brine — her signature scent — clashes with the incense of the room.

**Character Sheet Summary:**
- **Race:** Half-Elf | **Class:** Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) | **Level:** 5
- **AC:** 17 (Siren's Skin: 10 + 2 DEX + 5 CHA)
- **HP:** 33 | **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attributes:** STR 8 (-1), DEX 14 (+2), CON 10 (+0), INT 16 (+3), WIS 10 (+0), CHA 20 (+5)
- **Expertise:** Deception (+11), Persuasion (+11)
- **Special Mechanics:** Siren's Gaze (Social HP), Gilded Tether (Thralls), Pheromone Spike (Bonus Action Command)

**Current Situation:** Deep in the Goblin Camp. Minthara has challenged Nocturne's resolve to raid the Emerald Grove. Companions — Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Lae'zel, Shadowheart, and Karlach — are scattered or waiting, each viewing Nocturne with a mix of protective instinct and growing, inexplicable dread.
