Game Master:

Understood. You have provided a `GOD MODE:` command to give Sariel a special subclass of Wizard that is lore-appropriate. This is an excellent idea that perfectly aligns with her developing abilities and discoveries.

Based on our established lore (`World of Assiah v2.1.8 - Sariel's True Nature: The Path of the Mirror Mage`), Sariel's unique talent is **Mirror Magic**. This is a latent, legendary ability that allows her to perfectly replicate magical energies she observes.

I propose formalizing this as Sariel's Wizard subclass: **School of the Mirror**.

Here's how this subclass will function mechanically and narratively:

***

### Sariel's Subclass: School of the Mirror

This subclass represents Sariel's unique, inherited magical art – the ability to perfectly replicate observed or understood magical effects, bypassing conventional study.

**Features at Level 2 (Current Level):**

*   **Mirror Reflection (Level 2 Feature):**
    *   **Effect:** When Sariel successfully casts a spell of 1st level or higher, she can, as a bonus action on the same turn, store a faint "reflection" of that spell's energy within her mind. Once stored, for the next minute, she can cause one visible object within 30 feet to briefly shimmer with the spell's visual effect (no mechanical effect). This is primarily a flavor/roleplaying ability to establish her unique magic.
    *   **Purpose:** This feature represents Sariel's nascent ability to replicate. At higher levels, this will evolve into her more potent Mirror Magic abilities.

*   **Echoed Insight (Level 2 Feature):**
    *   **Effect:** Sariel has an uncanny ability to glean information from magical sources. When Sariel examines a magical item or spellbook for at least 1 minute, she can make an Intelligence (Arcana) check (DC determined by the item/spell's complexity). On a success, she gains Advantage on her next Intelligence (Investigation) check related to the item's history or purpose, or on her next Intelligence (Arcana) check to identify or understand a spell within the book.
    *   **Purpose:** This ties into her "Textual Replication" potential at higher levels, allowing her to gain initial insight into magical phenomena.

**Future Features (Unlocked at Higher Levels, as per `Sariel's True Nature` lore):**

*   **Visual Replication (Higher Level):** The ability to perfectly copy and cast a spell immediately after seeing it performed.
*   **Textual Replication (Higher Level):** The ability to instantly understand and cast a spell directly from a magical scroll or spellbook.
*   **Mnemonic Replication (Apex Level):** Her most profound and dangerous ability: to touch a person and read their memories, allowing her to find and perfectly copy a spell that person has either cast or witnessed (this is the ability she seeks to unlock from Kaelan).

***

This subclass integrates Sariel's established lore and provides a clear mechanical path for her unique Mirror Magic to develop as she levels up.

**Please confirm:** Do you approve the addition of the **School of the Mirror** subclass with these features for Sariel? Once approved, I will integrate it into her character sheet and resume the story.
