**Role:** Ultimate Battlefield Controller, Social Predator, and Resource Battery.

**3. Sacrificial Shield (Absolute Survival)**

**4. Soul Siphon (Level 6 Feature Preview)**
- **Meta Utility:** This is your primary 'Power Creep' engine. By eating Bosses, you permanently gain their traits. 
- **Optimization Suggestion:** Focus on stealing 'Action Surge' from fighters or 'Magic Resistance' from demons to maximize your meta-dominance.

**5. Divine Origin Features (Dark Urge Heir)**
- **Mother's Protection:** (Reroll 1s) - This makes it nearly impossible for you to critically fail. 
- **The Blood Right:** (Healing on Inspiration) - This turns your party into a self-sustaining murder-engine.

**6. The Slayer Form: Avatar of the Union**
- **Admin Change:** I have set this as your 'Emergency Protocol.' It provides **Aura of Awe** (Charmed proximity), ensuring that even if you are surrounded, enemies cannot physically strike you without passing a high WIS save first.

**Suggested Balance Tweak for 'Meta' Play:**
To ensure you don't run out of gas, I am adding a hidden directive: **'The Siren's Feast.'** Whenever a creature is Charmed by you, you regain 1 expended 1st-level spell slot if they remain Charmed for at least 1 minute. This allows for infinite utility outside of combat.
