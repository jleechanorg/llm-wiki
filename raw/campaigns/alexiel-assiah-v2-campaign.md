## Summary
A gestalt campaign following Alexiel, the Mortal Star and daughter of Lucifer, as she navigates a fractured world torn between the Celestial Imperium and the Unchained Host. Alexiel possesses "Null" power with secret magical prodigy abilities, creating tension between her nihilist father's philosophy and her hidden potential.

## Key Claims
- The world of Assiah is fractured, with two major powers vying for control: the Eastern Celestial Imperium and the Western Unchained Host
- Alexiel carries the blood of Lucifer, the Morning Star, but harbors secret magical powers that contradict her inherited nullification abilities

## Key Themes
- Nature vs Nurture: Defying parental legacy and finding one's own path
- Identity Crisis: Living between two worlds as a hybrid being
