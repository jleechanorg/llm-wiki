**Differential Report for Astarion's Traits - Part 2 [Change ID: 20240728-B]**

**Justification:** These additions and clarifications are necessary to accurately represent the full scope of an Ascended Astarion's powers as presented in Baldur's Gate 3, ensuring his character mechanics reflect his unique transformation and the thematic elements of his ascension.

**Proposed Changes:**

*   **ADD:** The following new action to Astarion's "Ascended Vampire Traits" list:
    *   **Ascended Bite (Melee Weapon Attack):** As an action, Astarion can make a special melee attack against one creature he has grappled, that is incapacitated, or that is charmed by him. Alternatively, as a bonus action, he can make this attack against a creature within 5 feet that is restrained, incapacitated, or charmed by him.
        *   *Attack:* +10 to hit (Proficiency Bonus +4 + Dexterity Modifier +6), reach 5 ft., one creature.
        *   *Hit:* 1d4 piercing damage + 1d6 necrotic damage. The target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the necrotic damage taken, and Astarion regains hit points equal to that amount. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0.

*   **ADD:** The following resistances to Astarion's "Ascended Vampire Traits" list (modifying "Vampiric Resilience"):
    *   **Vampiric Resilience (Revised):** Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks not made with silvered weapons, **and resistance to necrotic and poison damage.**

*   **REVISE:** The existing "Charm" trait for enhanced potency and correct DC:
    *   **Charm (Revised):** As an action, he can target one humanoid he can see within 30 feet. If the target can see him, it must succeed on a **DC 15 Wisdom saving throw** (8 + 4 Prof + 3 Cha) or be charmed for 24 hours. The charmed target regards him as a trusted friend and will heed his requests, even those detrimental to itself, though it will not directly harm itself or its immediate loved ones. Each time he or his companions do anything harmful to the target, or if he issues a command that directly causes the target to self-harm, the effect ends. When the effect ends, the target might know it was charmed by him. He has advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to influence a creature charmed in this way.
