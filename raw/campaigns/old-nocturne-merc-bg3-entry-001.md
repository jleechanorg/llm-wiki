## Scene Summary
The campaign opens in the Smirking Boar cellar, 1492 DR. The city teeters after Elturel's fall, refugees flooding the Outer City. Nocturne is introduced as a Level 6 College of Swords Bard, youngest bastard daughter of a powerful noble house — exiled for her cold, philosophical evil and sadistic, ritualistic hunger for murder. She operates as a mercenary seeking contracts others deem too distasteful. Guild lieutenant "Smiley" Arvax offers her first job: kidnapping a tiefling refugee family from Rivington.
