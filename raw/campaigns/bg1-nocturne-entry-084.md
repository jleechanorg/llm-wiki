You do not take the map. Instead, you let it hang in the air between you, suspended by the tension of your silence. You look around the opulence of the Temple—the gold-inlaid altar of Helm, the fine vestments of the priest, the polished plate of the guards.

"The future of the Sword Coast," you repeat, your voice mocking. "That sounds expensive, Mayor. And 'slaughter' sounds messy."

You step closer, invading his personal space just as you did with Silke. The *Hunger* sharpens your gaze, dissecting his desperation. "You want me to walk into a hole in the ground that is eating your soldiers. You want me to kill something that scares a garrison of Amnish troops. That isn't charity work, Ghastkill. That is a contract."

You slam your hand onto the altar, right next to the map. "Two thousand gold pieces. One thousand now, to outfit my team. One thousand upon completion. Pay it, or send your guards down there to die."

**Intimidation Check: 16 (Success vs DC 15 / Failure vs DC 20)**

Ghastkill blanches, clutching the holy symbol at his neck. "Two... two thousand? Madwoman, the garrison hasn't been paid in a tenday! The trade routes are dead! We don't have that kind of coin in the entire town treasury!"

He looks at your face, expecting you to back down. You do not. You simply stare, the *Satiated* predator waiting for the prey to stop thrashing.

"I... I cannot give you two thousand," Ghastkill stammers, sweat beading on his bald pate. He looks frantically at the priest, then unlocks a heavy iron chest beneath the altar. "But I can give you everything we have left. **Nine hundred gold pieces**. Four hundred now—that is every coin in this chest—and five hundred when you bring me the head of the leader."

He dumps a heavy sack onto the map. "Take it. Please. Just stop the killing."

It isn't the two thousand you demanded, but it is likely every copper piece the desperate town possesses. You weigh the sack in your hand. Heavy. Acceptable.

"Done," you say, snatching the map. "We leave in the morning. Try not to lose the rest of my money while I'm gone."

You turn to your party. **Roric** is grinning at the audacity. **Kagain** nods approvingly at the negotiation tactics. **Minsc** is confused but happy that evil will be kicked. **Dynaheir** looks weary, but ready.

You have the contract. You have the advance. You have the team. Now, you need to be at full strength to survive the dark.
