The victory had been brutal, leaving him aching and bruised, but the satisfaction was immense. He was wounded, but not broken. With a heavy sigh, he found a relatively clean section of the cavern floor, leaning his greatsword against a rock and sliding off his helm. The cool, damp air on his face was a welcome relief from the stifling heat of his armor. He closed his eyes, focusing on the deep, rhythmic breaths that would knit his flesh back together. His newly acquired 'Crushing Blow' ability felt potent, ready to be unleashed again. The long, arduous climb out of the gorge, and then the inevitable march on Stonehaven, demanded his full power.
