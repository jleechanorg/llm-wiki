Game Master:
You surveyed the bound, unconscious form of Lord Xykon. His furious eyes, though currently closed in forced oblivion, held the blueprint for a terrifying power. To simply leave him, or to merely interrogate him, felt… inefficient. To truly wield the "Harvest" and command its insidious influence, you needed its architect. Not a puppet, but an extension of your own magnificent will. And what better extension than a creature of the night, bound utterly to you?

"Rise and serve," you purred, a dark, possessive command. You leaned in, pressing your *Ascended Bite* against his throat, not to drain him dry, but to infuse him with your own corrupted vitae, to force the transformation, to bend his very essence to your will. This was no mere feeding; this was creation, a violent rebirth.

Xykon's body convulsed, even in unconsciousness. A faint, almost imperceptible shimmer of resistance rippled across his skin – his raw, inherent power fighting back against the unholy intrusion.

A growl, low and guttural, escaped Xykon's bound throat. The shudder intensified, his muscles spasming. He had resisted. Not the bite itself, but the forced transformation, the bending of his very being into your thrall. He had burned his last **Legendary Resistance (0/3 remaining)**, spending it not on his life, but on his will, on his refusal to become your puppet.

His eyes snapped open, blazing with a raw, desperate defiance and a chilling comprehension of your intent. He was no longer unconscious. He was still bound, still gagged, but fully aware, and now filled with a singular, burning hatred. You had tried to break him, to make him your tool, and he had resisted, costing him his last defense. He was still alive, for now, but the attempt had roused him to full, furious consciousness.
