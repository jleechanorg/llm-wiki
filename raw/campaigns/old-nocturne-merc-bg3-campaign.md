## Summary
A Baldur's Gate campaign with a noir tone, set in 1492 DR. Nocturne is a Level 6 College of Swords Bard operating as a Guild mercenary. The campaign explores a kidnapping contract, the exotic heritage market (enslavement), and criminal enterprise analysis. Known for the philosophical evil of the "Serpent-King" serial killer backstory.

## Key Claims
- The Smirking Boar serves as Guild operations base
- Smiley Arvax is the Guild lieutenant contact
- Exotic heritage market represents moral depths
- Deception checks drive social encounters

## Key Quotes
> "Not evil for evil's sake. Evil for *meaning*." — On the Serpent-King philosophy

> "The contract's clear. The conscience isn't." — On the kidnapping job
