You step away from the corpse, your boots sticky with the wizard's blood. You look out at the frozen common room, the *Hunger* swelling your presence, and deliver your ultimatum.

"He was a traitor to the Coast," you announce, your voice a cold, flat blade. "Anyone who objects joins him."

**Intimidation Check: 11 (Failure)**

For a heartbeat, it holds. Then, the serving girl drops her tray. The crash of pewter tankards hitting the floor shatters the spell of fear.

"Murder!" she screams, her voice shrill and piercing. "They killed him! Guards! GUARDS!"

The room explodes into panic. Patrons scramble over tables, overturning chairs in a desperate stampede for the door. **Algernon Feldepost** ducks behind the heavy oak bar, shouting, "Marl! Get the crossbow!"

Your control of the room has evaporated. You have turned a surgical strike into a riot.

"Bad speech," **Roric** grunts, already rifling through Tranzig's pockets with frantic speed. "Less talking, more looting!"

The rogue tears a scroll case and a heavy pouch from the dead wizard's belt. He tosses you a gold ring that slides off Tranzig's finger—a **Ring of Protection +1**. "Map! Letters! Gold! We have it! Go!"

**Kagain** kicks a chair at a patron trying to play hero with a carving knife. "Out the back! Move!"

**Minsc** grabs **Dynaheir**'s hand, pulling her toward the kitchen exit. "Boo says this inn has poor service! We are leaving!"

You sprint through the kitchen, the shouts of the Flaming Fist already audible from the street. You burst out into the alleyway, the smell of refuse and stale beer hitting you as you dive into the shadows. You have the intel, you have the loot, but you have burned Beregost to the ground behind you. There is no coming back here.

**Loot Acquired:**
*   **Ring of Protection +1:** (+1 AC, +1 Saving Throws).
*   **Tranzig's Letters:** Detailed directions to the **Bandit Camp** in the Wood of Sharp Teeth, north-east of Beregost.
*   **Gold:** 78 gp.

Once you are clear of the town limits, catching your breath in a copse of trees, **Xan** leans against a trunk, looking miserable. "We are fugitives. Again. I suppose the woods are preferable to the gallows, but only slightly."

You unfold the map Roric stole. The Bandit Camp is marked clearly. It is a fortified position in the deep woods, the staging ground for the raids. Tazok is there.

"We have the location," you say. "We hunt the head of the snake."
