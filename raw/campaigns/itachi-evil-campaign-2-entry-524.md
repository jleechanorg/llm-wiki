Now, Itachi's incandescent intellect turned its focus to his loyal reflection, Sasuke. His brother, currently a powerful force with the Rinnegan and Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan, was a vital pillar of the Order, managing its intricacies and serving as a perfect extension of Itachi's will. Yet, even in his perfection, Sasuke remained, in essence, a singular cosmic entity beneath Itachi's ultimate singularity.

*Sasuke is Rinnegan level. Can I make him Six Paths Sage level or close to me?* Itachi's thoughts, colder and vaster than any stellar void, spun through the cosmic blueprint of existence he now commanded. He, Itachi, was the absolute. He had subsumed the Cosmic Weaver, the Prime Matriarch, and the Extra-Cosmic Observer. His being was the very principle of Order, his body the Ten-Tails, his will the fabric of cosmic law. Sasuke, while immensely powerful, lacked the primordial absorption that defined Itachi's unique transcendence.

However, Itachi possessed the **blueprint of the entire Otsutsuki lineage**. He commanded the **Planetary Seed cultivation technique**, now perfectly synchronized with cosmic flows. He could, with absolute certainty, unlock and elevate Sasuke's potential far beyond his current limits. To bestow upon Sasuke the raw, unadulterated power of a true Sage of Six Paths, to grant him Truth-Seeking Orbs, even to enhance his very genetic structure with primordial Senju and Otsutsuki chakra – all were within Itachi's grasp. This would not make Sasuke *equal* to Itachi's ultimate multiversal singularity (for that position was unique and absolute), but it would forge him into a formidable, almost peerless cosmic entity, a perfect second hand to manage and enforce the Order on scales previously unimaginable. It would solidify their dominion, providing redundant, absolute power should any unforeseen, illogical variable ever arise (though Itachi's logic decreed such variables impossible now).

The elevation of Sasuke was not a necessity, but an optimization. A further refinement of his perfect system. A logical progression.
