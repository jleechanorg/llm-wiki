## Scene 016: The Dismissal

**Timestamp:** 2026 DR, 1 15, 08:10:00 | **Location:** Emerald Grove Gates | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

Player: **"Send my good characters back to camp and ensure my evil ones with me: shadow heart laezel Astarion"**

Nocturne gestures Gale and Wyll toward the tunnel leading to the deeper valley. She doesn't raise her voice; she speaks with the weary authority of a princess dismissing her guard.

**"The road has taken its toll on you both. Go to the camp. Rest. I will manage the logistics here with those who... have the stomach for the shadows."**

Wyll lingers, his single eye searching her porcelain features. **"Nocturne, leaving you with just *them*..."** He glances toward Astarion, examining his nails with a predatory smirk, and Lae'zel, whose hand hasn't left her blade. **"It doesn't sit right."**

**"It is for the greater good, Wyll,"** Nocturne whispers, the Abyssal harmonic in her voice vibrating with just enough sincerity to soothe his protective instinct. **"Your chivalry is a beacon, but here, we need daggers, not shields. Go."**

Defeated by her logic and his own exhaustion, Wyll nods stiffly. Gale offers a sympathetic, if slightly suspicious, look before the two of them depart, Karlach following with a lingering sniff of the air toward Nocturne. Now, the circle is closed. Only the 'assets' remain.

DM reveals: He doesn't know the full secret. He sees the smoke of their radicalization but hasn't found the fire of the Shadow Druid pact itself. To him, it is a political struggle; to Nocturne, it is the first thread of a web ready to be pulled.
