I will now follow the **Finalization Protocol** to integrate these changes.

**1. Proposal:**
Integrate the "Yearly Progression Model" and "Special Growth: The 'Lesson Learned' Check" into the Destiny Core Rules, specifically under the "Character Attributes" section.

**2. Conceptual Approval:** (Implicitly granted by your command.)

**3. Differential Draft & Justification:**

**Differential Report - Change ID: 20240530-A**

*   **Source Text (Destiny Core Rules, II. Character Attributes):**
    *   Currently, Rule 2.5 is "Influence & Expertise System."
    *   Currently, Rule 2.6 is "Internal Conflict System."
    *   There is no explicit rule detailing passive aptitude growth or the use of Aptitude Potential in progression.

*   **Proposed New Text (Additions to Destiny Core Rules, Section II. Character Attributes):**
    *   **New Rule 2.7: Aptitude Progression Model**
        *   **2.7.1 Aptitude Potential (Refined):** Each character possesses an innate "Aptitude Potential" coefficient (rated 1-5) for each Aptitude. This represents their natural talent and inherent capacity for growth. **Aptitude Potential is a static value and does not change.**
        *   **2.7.2 The Yearly Progression Model:** Beyond explicit Aptitude Score Improvements (Rule 3.5), attribute scores also change fractionally each year based on a character's age and potential. These changes apply to a character's **base aptitude score** and are then summed with any explicit Aptitude Score Improvements gained at levels.
            *   **Growth Years (Ages 26-35):** All Aptitudes gain + (Potential / 10) points per year.
            *   **Plateau Years (Ages 36-45):** Physical Aptitudes (Physique, Coordination, Health) change by + ((3 - Potential) * 0.1) points per year. Intelligence changes by -0.1 points per year. Wisdom changes by +0.2 points per year.
            *   **Decline Years (Ages 46+):** Physical Aptitudes change by + ((2 - Potential) * 0.2) points per year. Intelligence changes by -0.2 points per year. Wisdom changes by +0.1 points per year.
            *   *(Note: Fractional aptitude scores are rounded down for the purpose of calculating modifiers, but tracked precisely for future growth.)*
        *   **2.7.3 Special Growth: The "Lesson Learned" Check:** Wisdom can also be actively developed. After successfully making an Intelligence check to learn from a *significant life failure* (CN 12-18 based on severity), a character may gain a permanent +0.5 increase to their Wisdom score, representing growth through hardship and reflection. This check can only be attempted once per major failure.

*   **Justification:** This amendment provides a robust, dynamic system for long-term character progression beyond discrete level-up choices. It formalizes the role of "Aptitude Potential" and character age in attribute development, adding depth and realism. The "Lesson Learned" check offers a narrative-driven way to improve Wisdom, linking character growth directly to impactful story events and choices. This aligns with the "Character Depth & Display" directive (Part 6.A).
