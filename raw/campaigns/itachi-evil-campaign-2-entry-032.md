Game Master:
[Mode: DM MODE]

Understood. You have approved the analysis and the proposed solution for XP tracking. I will implement the "XP Event Immediate Reporting Protocol" to ensure all XP gains are explicitly stated immediately after the relevant action from this point forward.

Before returning to the narrative, Itachi has gained a Class Level due to accumulating 540 XP. He is now **Level 2**.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 2:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 6
    *   **HP Gain:** 6 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 8 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 10 to **18 HP**. He is now at 18/18 HP.

*   **New Class Features (from Assassin Rogue path):**
    1.  **Cunning Action:** As a Secondary Action (1 CP), Itachi can now choose to perform one of the following actions:
        *   **Dash:** Gain extra movement equal to your Speed.
        *   **Disengage:** Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of the turn.
        *   **Hide:** Attempt to become hidden.
        This grants Itachi incredible flexibility and mobility in combat and stealth.
    2.  **Expertise (Choice):** Itachi's training and natural talent have deepened. He can now choose **two** additional skills to gain an **Expertise Tag** in (granting Advantage on relevant checks, as per `Destiny` 2.5.B).
        *   **Alternatively,** he can choose one skill he *already* has an Expertise Tag in (such as Stealth, which he already possesses from his Sharingan/prodigy status) to gain **Super-Advantage** in that skill (roll 3d20, take the highest result, reflecting unparalleled mastery).

    **Itachi's Expertise Choice:** Please decide which option Itachi takes for his Expertise:
    *   **Option A:** Choose two new Expertise Tags (e.g., Investigation, Deception, Acrobatics, Persuasion, Intimidation, Medicine, etc.).
    *   **Option B:** Choose one existing Expertise Tag (like Stealth) to gain Super-Advantage in, and one new Expertise Tag.

Please let me know your choice for Itachi's Expertise.
