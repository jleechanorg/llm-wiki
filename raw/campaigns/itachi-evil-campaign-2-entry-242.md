*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 5

    2.  **Increase two different Aptitude Scores by 1 each.**
    3.  **Choose one Feat.**

This ability represents the culmination of his Mangekyō Sharingan's power, reflecting his profound understanding of its true potential, hardened by his unique path.
    *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 20 EP.
    *   **Duration:** Up to 1 minute. Can be dismissed early. Requires concentration.
    *   **Effect (Basic Manifestation - Full Body):** Itachi is encased in a colossal, spectral humanoid avatar of chakra. He gains:
        *   **Damage Resistance:** Resistance to all non-magical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
        *   **Temporary Hit Points:** Gains 100 temporary hit points that absorb damage first. If these are depleted, the Susano'o fades.
        *   **Increased Reach:** Melee attacks made from within Susano'o gain 10 feet of reach.
        *   **Offensive Capabilities:** While inside Susano'o, Itachi can make powerful melee attacks with its spectral weapons (e.g., a sword, orb) dealing 4d8 Slashing or Force damage.
    *   **Fatigue Cost:** 0 levels of Fatigue (due to Potent Mangekyō Mastery).
    *   *Note: Further stages of Susano'o (e.g., armored form, Perfect Susano'o, unique weapons like the Totsuka Blade/Yata Mirror) will be unlocked at higher levels or through specific narrative milestones/research, as per this ability's progression.*
