### ⚖️ The Dynasty of Aeterna (Nuanced Profiles)
1. **Emperor Malphas (The Eternal):** He is the 'Divine Enforcer' of the species. His 'Evil' is a cold, calculated pragmatism. He holds the **Covenant with the Eater of Worlds** (Shroud Entity), sacrificing billions to prevent the 'End of the Cycle' from consuming the entire galaxy. He views himself as the ultimate shepherd.
2. **Empress Xylia:** Not merely a spy, but a genetic curator. She manages the **Transcendence Tradition**, ensuring the psionic purity of the species. Her cruelty stems from a fear that one weak link in the gene-seed will cause a species-wide psychic collapse into the Warp.
3. **Prince Xeres:** He commands the **Colossus-class ships**. He doesn't enjoy slaughter; he believes **Exterminatus** is a mercy compared to a world being slowly digested by Shroud-entities or falling to the Prethoryn Scourge. He is a man of grim duty.
4. **Princess Hespera:** Your rival. She believes the Emperor's centralized power is attracting Shroud-horrors. She seeks to decentralize and create 'Psi-Corps' enclaves to stabilize the galaxy, viewing your family's absolute rule as a dangerous psychic beacon.
5. **Prince Aethelred:** An advocate for **Genetic Ascension** who believes psionics are too unstable. He views your 'evil' family as addicts to Shroud-power and wants to replace psychic uncertainty with biological perfection.

### 🚀 Stellaris Mechanics Integrated
- **Ecumenopolis (Aeterna):** Your throne world is a planet-wide city, maximizing alloys and consumer goods production at the cost of total ecological devastation.
- **The Shroud (The Warp):** Accessible via **Jump Drives**. You can reach out to entities like the *Whisperers in the Void* or the *Instrument of Desire* for boons, but the 'Shroud Mark' always carries a price.
- **Galactic Community:** Aeterna is the 'Galactic Custodian,' using its 'Diplomatic Weight' (Psychic Might) to pass resolutions that label rivals as 'Galactic Crises.'
- **Megastructures:** The **Solar Architects** are currently constructing a **Dyson Sphere** in the nearby Sol-Prime system to fuel the Spire's psychic resonators.
- **Traditions & Perks:** Your species has unlocked **Mind Over Matter** and **Transcendence**, but you are currently facing the **'Synthetic Dawn'** uprising in the outer rim (the Cog-Priests).

### 🏰 Noble Houses & Planets
- **House Valthor (Krios IV - Continental World):** Lords of the industrial tithe. They are currently struggling with 'Deviancy' levels due to the Shroud Psychic Scream.
- **House Thalassa (Ocean World):** Controls the 'Crystal Focus' mines essential for psionic amplifiers.
- **Lord Tiberius:** Governor of a **Resort World**, managing the decadent pleasures that keep the high-nobility from falling into psychic despair.
