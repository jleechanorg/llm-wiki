Understood. You have provided a `GOD MODE` directive to apply Level 17 benefits and then a `think` command regarding the current situation with Obito and Zetsu.

Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**92760 XP**) to reach **Level 17**. This is a monumental achievement, placing him among the most powerful mortal beings in the world, truly a legendary figure.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 17, as per the approved "Itachi Uchiha: Ninja Custom Class Feature Manifest":

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 6
    *   **HP Gain:** 6 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 8 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 129 to **137 HP**. He is now at 137/137 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 17 Ninja, Itachi gains significant advancements:

    1.  **New Illusion Spells (9th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu culminates, unlocking illusions capable of shaping reality at a fundamental level, creating entirely new worlds, or profoundly altering the minds of even cosmic entities. He learns one of the following 9th-level Illusion spells:
        *   **1. Weird (Mass Fear/Phantasm):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 20 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a phantasmal illusion that takes a form chosen by you (e.g., a terrifying monster, a landscape of agony, a horde of your victims) in a 30-foot radius sphere centered on a point Itachi can see within 120 feet. Each creature in the sphere must succeed on a **DC 24 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or become Frightened (see `Destiny` 6.4) for up to 1 minute. While Frightened, a creature is incapacitated. At the end of each of its turns, the target must make a Wisdom (Willpower) check. If it succeeds, the spell ends. This is a massive, area-of-effect mental assault capable of devastating multiple elite targets.
        *   **2. Illusory Prison (Variant of Imprisonment/Forcecage):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 20 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You conjure a powerful, reality-bending illusion that creates a seemingly unbreakable prison around a target creature Itachi can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 24 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or be trapped within the illusory prison. The prison is physically impassable to the target (as if it were a solid object), and it appears as a terrifying mental construct tailored to the target's deepest fears. The target is Incapacitated while trapped. The illusion persists indefinitely until Itachi dismisses it or until dispelled by truly cosmic magic. The illusion can support weight, block movement, or trigger environmental effects only for the trapped target.

Please choose one of the 9th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 17:

*   **1. Weird**
*   **2. Illusory Prison**

Once you've made this choice, I will finalize Itachi's Level 17 progression and return to STORY MODE to address your `think` command.
