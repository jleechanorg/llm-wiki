### Analysis of Nocturne's Power Level vs. BG3 Campaign Start

A standard Baldur's Gate 3 campaign begins at Level 1, with basic gear and no magic items. Characters gradually acquire better equipment and advance through levels, typically reaching Level 5-6 by the end of Act 1, and higher levels later in the game.

Nocturne, as currently defined, starts at **Level 5**. This alone immediately places her well beyond the starting power curve of a BG3 campaign. Furthermore:

*   **Aptitude Scores:** Her Aptitude scores (Physique 16, Coordination 14, Health 15, Intelligence 18, Wisdom 16) are uniformly very high, providing strong modifiers from the outset across all critical areas. Her Intelligence of 18 means a significant EP pool and a high Spell Save CN for her Paladin spells.
*   **Gear:** She possesses **Masterwork Plate Armor (DR 3)**, a **+1 Masterwork Longsword**, a **Masterwork Shield (+2 Defense)**, and a **Bag of Holding**. This is an exceptionally powerful set of equipment for a Level 5 character. The DR 3 from her plate armor is particularly strong, significantly reducing incoming physical damage. A +1 magic weapon is usually a rare find at this stage, and a Bag of Holding is a utility item of immense value.
*   **Class Features:** As a Level 5 Paladin, she has **Extra Attack**, **Divine Smite** (a potent damage burst), and a substantial **Lay on Hands** healing pool. These are all significant combat multipliers.
*   **Expertise Tags:** The four Expertise Tags grant Advantage on a wide range of relevant checks, making her highly competent in her chosen fields.
*   **Aptitude Potentials:** Her potentials are all high (4 or 5 after conversion), meaning the new "Yearly Progression Model" will ensure she continues to grow significantly year by year, reinforcing her "prodigy" theme.

**Conclusion:** Yes, Nocturne is **exceptionally strong** for the start of a campaign, especially when calibrated against the typical progression and power level of a Baldur's Gate 3 campaign. She is effectively starting with the power of a mid-to-late Act 1 character, but with superior, pre-defined gear.

### Calibration Options

To bring Nocturne's initial power closer to a typical (albeit still strong, given her background) starting point for a fresh campaign, while retaining her "prodigy" essence and noble background, I propose the following options for adjustment. Please choose one, or suggest an alternative:

**Option A: Gear & Minor Resource Adjustment**
*   **Rationale:** Keeps her Level 5 power and prodigy stats, but adjusts her equipment to be more in line with what a newly exiled noble might retain – high quality, but not immediately magical or legendary.
*   **Changes:**
    *   **Masterwork Plate Armor:** Becomes **Fine Plate Armor**. Defense bonus remains +8, but **DR is reduced to 1**. Stealth Disadvantage remains. (It's still excellent armor, but less universally resilient).
    *   **Masterwork Longsword (+1):** Becomes a **Masterwork Longsword (non-magical)**. Damage remains 1d8/1d10, but the **+1 bonus to attack and damage is removed**. (Still a high-quality weapon, but not magically enchanted).
    *   **Bag of Holding:** This is a rare item. While she could have acquired one, it might be too much for a starting character.
        *   **Sub-Option A1:** Replace the Bag of Holding with a **Standard Adventurer's Pack** and a **Sturdy Backpack** (effectively reducing her carrying convenience and capacity).
        *   **Sub-Option A2:** Keep the Bag of Holding, but reduce her starting gold to 200 gp (to reflect the cost/resource drain of acquiring such a valuable item during her exile).
    *   **Potion of Greater Healing (x3):** Reduced to **Potion of Healing (x3)** (Restores 2d4+2 HP).

**Option B: Slight Aptitude Adjustment & Gear Adjustment**
*   **Rationale:** Gently tunes down her raw aptitude slightly while also adjusting gear, to make her formidable but not quite as universally dominant. Her high potentials still ensure strong growth.
*   **Changes:**
    *   **Reduce two Aptitude Scores by 1:** For example, **Health (15 -> 14)** and **Physique (16 -> 15)**. This would slightly reduce her HP, HP modifier, and Physique modifier, but her other high scores and potentials remain. (This will also slightly reduce her maximum HP and her Lay on Hands pool).
    *   **Apply all gear changes from Option A** (Fine Plate Armor with DR 1, non-magical Masterwork Longsword, and either Sub-Option A1 or A2 for the Bag of Holding/Gold, and Potion of Healing x3).

**Option C: Keep as Is, Scale World Faster**
*   **Rationale:** Respects your initial character design choices completely. The consequence is that the campaign will be inherently more difficult from the outset, with more powerful threats, complex political maneuvering, and high-stakes encounters appearing much earlier than in a typical campaign.
*   **Changes:** No changes to Nocturne's current character sheet. The GM (AI) will simply scale up initial encounters and world reactions to match her formidable capabilities.

Please confirm which of these options (or a modification of your own) you'd prefer for Nocturne's starting state.
