Location: Outskirts of Oakhaven, Near the Barn, Early Evening

The trembling villagers stood before Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16), their terror a potent, almost tangible thing. Their fear had yielded valuable information about Oakhaven's layout and its most isolated targets, Man Tibbet's farm and Widow Elara's cottage. The immediate problem of the mastiffs was solved, but the hamlet was now aware, even if vaguely, of a disturbance. Nocturne's internal clock was ticking; the window for a truly clean, unobserved operation was closing fast. Her mind, a whirlwind of calculated efficiency, decided to perform a swift, broad scan.

She took a moment, her eyes closing briefly, the sounds of the approaching villagers fading into a distant hum. Her consciousness expanded, reaching out, touching the very fabric of the immediate surroundings. This was not mere perception, but an **Apex Senses** deployment, a focused application of her honed intellect (Intelligence 18) and awareness. It consumed a fraction of her inner energy, but the strategic advantage it offered was immeasurable.

As her senses sharpened, the world around her resolved into stark, objective data. The distant calls of the villagers were merely sound waves, their frantic energy a predictable response to anomaly. She felt the subtle vibrations of the earth, mapping the shallow, winding creek that led past Widow Elara's cottage to the west – a potential, quiet ingress point. To the north, the larger, more robust presence of Man Tibbet's farmhouse registered, a solid structure with a few outbuildings and a distinct, faint scent of uncleaned livestock pens.

Beyond the immediate approach, her enhanced perception revealed something else, a pattern of movement that wasn't quite natural for a peaceful farming hamlet. Near the main road, perhaps a quarter-mile back towards Baldur's Gate, she detected faint, irregular patrols – not official Flaming Fist, but a small, disciplined group, moving with a subtle, practiced caution. Three, perhaps four, individuals. Their presence suggested a more organized defense than the villagers had implied, or perhaps a different interested party entirely. They hadn't heard the dogs' barking in full, but they were scanning the approach to Oakhaven with a distinct purpose. They were still too far to be directly alerted, but their presence was a new variable, suggesting the Shadow Weavers perhaps employed local muscle for 'protection,' or that another faction was already active in the area.

Nocturne's eyes opened, a cold, almost imperceptible smirk touching her lips. The intelligence was confirmed: Oakhaven was not as 'unprotected' as Kaelen had suggested. This merely added another layer of complexity, another set of variables to control. The approaching villagers were merely a distraction now. The true concern was this unexpected, professional presence on the main road. The mission was becoming more engaging.

She still had 4 uses of Apex Senses remaining (1 + 4 Wisdom Mod = 5 total, 1 used). Her EP was 31/36, CP 1.
