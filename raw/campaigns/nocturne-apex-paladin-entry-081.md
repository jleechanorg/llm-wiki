Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Near Root Cellar, Early Evening

The air was still, thick with the scent of damp earth and the subtle, metallic tang of fresh blood. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood over the second fallen farm guard, his body a silent, inert mass against the root cellar. The chilling precision of her strike, empowered by her focused will and mastery of combat, had ensured his death was as swift and soundless as the first. The complex, multi-layered defense of Man Tibbet's farm had been systematically dismantled, piece by piece, by her calculated efficiency. All professional threats, from the road patrol to these internal sentinels, were now neutralized.

Her gaze, sharp and analytical, swept the surrounding area. The farmhouse remained quiet, the muffled sounds of the unsuspecting family a constant reminder of the primary objective. The arcane ward near the barn pulsed faintly, a silent observer of the violence it had failed to detect. With the immediate tactical situation firmly under her control, Nocturne's meticulous nature demanded a swift collection of any valuable intelligence or resources from the fallen guard.

Action: [Nocturne searches the body of the neutralized guard and conceals him.]
Nocturne knelt, her movements fluid and practiced. She quickly and efficiently patted down the guard's dark leather armor and went through his pouches. Like his comrade, he carried a standard shortsword, a light crossbow with a quiver of 15 bolts, and wore practical dark leather armor. In his coin purse, she found a sum of gold, slightly less than the first. He also had a small, finely crafted silver whistle, designed to produce a sound barely audible to the human ear but piercingly loud to trained animals – a tool for coordinating with patrol beasts or signaling.

*   **Loot Acquired:**

    *   1 Shortsword (standard quality)
    *   1 Light Crossbow
    *   1 Quiver with 15 bolts
    *   **70 gp** (from coin purse)
    *   1 Silver Dog Whistle (finely crafted)

The silver whistle piqued her interest – a tool for animals, but potentially for other purposes, or a subtle communication device. The gold was a pleasant, expected bonus. With the loot secured in her Bag of Holding, Nocturne turned her attention to the body. Leaving it exposed was an unacceptable lapse in control. Her powerful physique made the task effortless as she gripped the guard's heavy boots. She dragged him away from the root cellar, deeper into the wild, dense shrubbery bordering the farm, ensuring his form vanished completely from sight. She broke off several large branches, meticulously arranging them over the corpse, leaving no trace of the violence that had just transpired.

She stood, dusting off her hands, her gaze sweeping the now-empty space. Two down. The farm's immediate perimeter was secured. Her EP was low (6/36), and her Channel Divinity was expended, but her HP remained full. The 'large family' was hers for the taking. The path to wealth and power lay open, unhindered by any vigilant eyes.
