## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in Assiah (world between Heaven and Hell). Player is Ser Arion val Valerion, a 16-year-old Level 1 Paladin (Oath of the Crown) sworn to Empress Sariel's "Silent Peace." Campaign explores moral corruption as Arion shifts to Neutral Evil, power-hungry and sadistic. He discovers the refugee camp can be enslaved and sold to the Adamant Vault mines. 1,000 entries.

## Key Claims
- Setting: World of Assiah after Great Vanishing, ruled by Empress Sariel via psychic power
- Arion's stats: CHA 16, STR 16, INT 14, others redistributed
- Neutral Evil alignment with power-hungry, sadistic motivation
- Successfully proposed "Labor Transfer" instead of massacre — 300 refugees to sell
- Negotiated 15% cut (450gp) from the finder's fee
- Killed witness messenger, scouted servants' tunnel for infiltration
- Fighting Style: Defense selected (+1 AC, total AC 19)

## Key Quotes
> "The 'Silent Peace' is a privilege you are currently forfeiting with your stuttering, boy."

> "Dispersal is a finality that yields the Empress nothing. To put them to the sword here is to double that waste."
