    2.  **New Illusion Spells (9th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu culminates in absolute control over reality perception. He learns a **second** 9th-level Illusion spell. (He already knows `Weird`).

        Please choose one of the following 9th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 18:
        *   **1. Illusory Prison (Variant of Imprisonment/Forcecage):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 20 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You conjure a powerful, reality-bending illusion that creates a seemingly unbreakable prison around a target creature Itachi can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 24 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or be trapped within the illusory prison. The prison is physically impassable to the target (as if it were a solid object), and it appears as a terrifying mental construct tailored to the target's deepest fears. The target is Incapacitated while trapped. The illusion persists indefinitely until Itachi dismisses it or until dispelled by truly cosmic magic. The illusion can support weight, block movement, or trigger environmental effects only for the trapped target.
        *   **2. True Sight (Ocular Enhancement - Pseudo-Illusion):**
            *   **Activation:** Secondary Action (1 CP), spend 15 EP.
            *   **Effect:** Itachi gains the ability to see through normal and magical darkness, perceive invisible creatures and objects, automatically detect visual illusions and succeed on saves against them, and perceive the true form of shapeshifters or creatures hidden by magic, all out to a range of 120 feet. This represents a perfected insight into reality that illusion masters possess. Lasts for 1 hour.

Please choose one of the 9th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 18:

*   **1. Illusory Prison**
*   **2. True Sight**
