## Summary
A Dance of the Dragons solo D&D 5e campaign. Visenya "Silver" Belaerys is a 16-year-old claiming to be a minor Targaryen bastard, secretly of the Apex bloodline. Set during the Sowing of 129 AC on Dragonstone, she claims Vermithor the Bronze Fury -- the second largest dragon in the world -- while maintaining a "Lucky Peasant" persona. The campaign went through multiple identity resets, refining Visenya from a soot-stained nobody to a disreputable drunk Targaryen cousin.

## Key Claims
- Visenya's class is Dragon Lord (Sorcerer subclass), using CHA 18 + DEX 16 + CON 14 spread, with Subtle Spell as a free mechanic
- Her Noblest Dialect grants Advantage on CHA checks when speaking High Valyrian to Valyrians or dragons
- The Ascension Meter (0-100) tracks her rising power; using Apex Aura directly raises the meter and attracts the Architects of Silence
- Gerrick the Smith is her anchor companion -- a loyal, kind-hearted Dragonseed who believes she is merely clever
- Rhaenyra suspects Visenya's true blood but is simultaneously impressed and wary
