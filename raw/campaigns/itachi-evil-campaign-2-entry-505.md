Sasuke's Rinnegan blazed, his young face a mask of grim, zealous resolve. "As you command, Brother. The ultimate power. For our Order."

Itachi and Sasuke began the monumental ritual. Leveraging Itachi's absolute command over the Gedo Mazo and his **Ten-Tails Resonance** ability, they initiated the arduous process of drawing the collected Tailed Beasts from within the colossal shell, fusing their fragmented essences into a single, terrifying entity. Yet, their wills, forged in pure, unfeeling resolve, remained unyielding. It was a cosmic birth, a forced evolution of primordial chakra under their absolute dominion.

He held the Ten-Tails, completely. The culmination was absolute.

But in that same instant, a chilling, profound disturbance ripped through the cosmic tapestry. It was not the isolated ripple of a single Watcher. Itachi's Rinnegan, now enhanced by the Ten-Tails' power, registered a **gargantuan, multi-pronged energy signature** tearing through dimensional space, far vaster and more immediate than the lone scout. The "Watcher" was merely a prelude. A full-fledged **Otsutsuki Invasion Force** was now descending upon Earth, drawn by the immense, chaotic surge of the Ten-Tails' revival. The air crackled with a terrifying, unknown pressure. A new, far greater challenge had arrived.
