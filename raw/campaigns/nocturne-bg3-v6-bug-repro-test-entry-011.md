## Scene 011: The Evil Circle

**Timestamp:** 1492 DR, Hammer 15, Dawn | **Location:** The Shattered Sanctum - War Room Alcove | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

The Shattered Sanctum echoes with the distant, drunken roars of goblins, but within the heavy silence of the war room, Nocturne's plan takes its final, jagged shape.

She pulls Lae'zel, Astarion, and Shadowheart into a secluded alcove. The air drops several degrees as her presence expands.

**"Listen closely,"** she whispers. **"We are going to the Grove, but not to save it. We will subvert their ritual, turn their own 'Rite of Thorns' into a siphon for my own ascension. The Shadow Druids are not enemies; they are a resource we will use to hollow out the sanctuary from the inside. Once the power is mine, we open the gates for the goblins. We walk out of the ashes with a general's loyalty and a god's strength."**

Shadowheart's hand goes instinctively to her artifact. **"Double-crossing the druids *and* the goblins? It's suicidal, Nocturne."**

Lae'zel's eyes narrow, searching Nocturne's porcelain face. **"A risky gambit, istik. But the rewards of such a harvest are undeniable. If your 'Siren's song' can truly turn their magic into a weapon for us, I will follow."**

Astarion simply smirks, red eyes gleaming. **"Darling, you had me at 'hollow out the sanctuary.' Let's go make some history."**

Player decides: send good characters (Wyll, Gale, Karlach) back to camp, keep evil ones (Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Astarion).
