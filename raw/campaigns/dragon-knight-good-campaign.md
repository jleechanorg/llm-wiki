## Summary

The Dragon Knight Good campaign is set in Assiah, 40 years after the Celestial Wars. The player character, Ser Arion val Valerion, is a Level 1 Paladin and noble house scion who serves Empress Sariel—the daughter of legendary Alexiel. The campaign explores the moral dilemmas of serving an unjust regime under the "Silent Peace," a psychic dominance that suppresses all dissent while maintaining prosperity.

## Campaign Scope

- **Character:** Ser Arion val Valerion, Level 1 Paladin, noble house scion (STR16/CON14/CHA16)
- **Setting:** Assiah world, Winter-Mourn Province, 40 A.G. (After the Celestial Wars)
- **Key NPCs:** Prefect Gratian (masked enforcer), Lady Annalise Ashwood (March Lord sheltering refugees), Empress Sariel (ruler)
- **Locations:** Imperial Road, Ashwood Keep, Ridge Overlooking Ashwood Settlement, Refugee Camp
- **Scope:** Character's first mission—to "pacify" refugees sheltered by Lady Annalise

## Key Themes

- **Silent Peace Tyranny:** Psychic dominance that maintains prosperity through total suppression of dissent
- **Moral Dilemma:** Following unjust orders vs. personal conscience
- **Noble vs. Commoner:** The Crown's ruthless logic vs. human compassion
- **Celestial Politics:** Legacy of the cosmic struggle between Order (Imperium) and Freedom (Shattered Host)
- **Just vs. Necessary Evil:** Is the Empress's harsh logic justified by results?
