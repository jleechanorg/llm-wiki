Game Master:
You establish a perimeter in a dense copse of elms a mile south of Beregost. The town walls are visible through the trees, buzzing with the activity of a garrison on high alert. Flaming Fist patrols scour the main roads, stopping wagons and questioning travelers. They are looking for a party of six led by a ranger in studded leather.

They are not looking for a lone elf wandering with a mournful expression, nor a nondescript rogue slipping through the drainage ditches.

"The Proxy Strategy," you announce. "Roric, you know the fences. Xan, you have the face of a stranger. Go. Sell the gems. Buy healing. Do not get caught."

**Roric** grins, donning a hooded cloak. "In and out. Like a whisper."

**Xan** sighs, adjusting his robes. "I suspect I shall be arrested and hanged as an accomplice, but it is preferable to sitting here waiting for the inevitable wyvern attack."

They vanish toward the town.

**Three Hours Later.**

The wait is tense. **Kagain** sharpens his axe, grumbling about trusting an elf with gold. **Minsc** teaches **Boo** how to forage for nuts. You watch the road, the *Hunger* restless.

Then, a rustle in the brush. Roric and Xan return, unharmed.

"Too easy," Roric reports, dumping a heavy sack of coins and supplies onto the grass. "The Fist is tearing the Feldepost Inn apart looking for clues. They aren't watching the back alleys."

Xan nods, looking relieved. "I sold the gemstones to a fence Roric knew—a halfling with very loose morals. We purchased supplies at the Temple of Lathander. The priests were... charitable, despite the chaos."

**Transaction Report:**
*   **Sold:** Miscellaneous gems and jewelry from the Bandit Camp.
*   **Bought:** 5 Potions of Healing, 20 Arrows, Rations.

"Also," Roric adds, his expression darkening. "The town is buzzing with news. **Tazok** isn't just in the Cloakwood. Reports say the Iron Throne has fortified an old dwarven mine deep in the forest. They've hired **Davaeorn** to ward it. It's not a bandit camp anymore, Nocturne. It's a fortress."

You have the supplies. You have the gold. And you have the target.
