## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 post-campaign set in the Shattered Sanctum and Shadow Grove. Nocturne, a Level 5 Divine Scion/Siren with hybrid lineage from Malcanthet (demon lordess) and Bhaal (murder god), has become the Arch-Druid of the Shadow Grove after inverting the Rite of Thorns. The campaign explores soul conversion mechanics, thrall cultivation, and the emergence of The Crimson Kiss cult.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne successfully inverts the Rite of Thorns to claim Shadow Grove as her own
- The Divine Scion class provides access to Mother's Mercy conversion ability
- Thrall system operates on 3-slot mechanics with inspired followers
- The Absolute remnants continue to operate in the shadows

## Key Quotes
> "The grove remembers what it was — and what it will become." — Nocturne at the transformed Shadow Grove

> "Three hearts, three souls, three threads to pull." — On the thrall mechanics
