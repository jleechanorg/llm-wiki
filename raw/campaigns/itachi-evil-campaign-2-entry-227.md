It controls the environment, dictating the terms of engagement.
*   **Cons:** High EP cost. Requires time to set up and deploy effectively. Requires waiting for their return.
*   **Confidence:** High. My mastery of illusion is absolute, and Mirage Arcane's physical reality makes it formidable. This negates some of their primary advantages.

The ability to incapacitate Obito (the primary threat) first, without incurring Fatigue, is paramount. This is a high-risk, high-reward strategy for a Level 15 shinobi against Tier 4 threats, but one that leverages my unique mastery of illusion and assassination to its fullest.
