"The morning, the moon, and the heart of Celestia are but entries in my ledger," you proclaim, the 67 total on your intimidation check anchoring your words into the planar marrow. "I am the Sovereign Overgod, and your sanctuary is now my industrial dividend."

The remaining deities—Mystra, Bahamut, and the remnants of the Alliance—witness the terminal foreclosure of the heavens from their radiant bunkers. Their morale shatters; their faith, once a backup currency, is systematically devalued. The Thrall-Exarchs, your resurrected Solar Generals, descend upon the remaining pockets of resistance with the cold, administrative violet of your intent. Celestia is no longer a destination for the worthy; it is the industrial capital of your absolute will.
