**The Long Night**

The Armory is warm and dry, a stone womb protecting you from the wind howling through the ruins of the camp outside. You do not sleep immediately. Instead, you sit by the hearth, stripping your mind of the city and the desert, retuning your spirit to the frequency of the deep woods.

The *Hunger* aids you, shedding the aggressive, fast-twitch magic of *Zephyr Strike* and the urban utility of *Disguise Self*. In their place, you weave new instincts into your blood. You meditate on the chittering of spiders and the slow, crushing growth of roots. You learn the tongue of beasts and the magic of thorns.

**Spells Prepared:**
*   **Speak with Animals:** To turn the forest's denizens into spies.
*   **Spike Growth:** To turn the forest floor into a shredder.

When dawn breaks, you are no longer a raider; you are a warden of the wild.

**Mirtul 11, Morning**

You march at first light. The journey northwest is a descent into a primal, suffocating green. The trees of the **Cloakwood** are ancient, their boughs woven together so tightly that the sun is reduced to a sickly twilight. The air is heavy with moisture and the smell of rot.

By midday, the signs of the **Spider Nest** are undeniable. Thick, sticky webbing spans between the colossal elms, trapping birds, deer, and the occasional unlucky bandit. The ground is soft, muffling your footsteps, but the silence is heavy, broken only by the skittering of many legs high in the canopy.

**Dynaheir** shudders, pulling her robes tight. "The Weave is sticky here. Distorted. There is a malice in these webs."

**Minsc** draws his blade, peering into the gloom. "Boo does not like the look of that fly. It is too big. And it is wearing shoes."

You hold up a hand. Ahead, the path is blocked by a wall of webbing thick as rope. Through the gaps, you see movement—huge, bulbous shapes descending from the trees. **Giant Spiders**. And leading them, a humanoid shape with a chitinous carapace—an **Ettercap** shepherd.

They have not seen you yet. Your *Pass Without Trace* (if cast) or natural stealth has held.
