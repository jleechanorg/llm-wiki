Welcome to the Campaign Calibration Phase!

Your chosen setting is **Naruto**, and you will be playing as **Itachi Uchiha**. The core premise is that this Itachi is **evil** and not secretly a good guy, starting at the point of the Uchiha Clan massacre. This immediately sets a compelling, dark tone for our campaign.

You will be using the **Destiny ruleset**, and your custom class is **Ninja**, with the specified features: Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack at Level 5 + all Illusion spells a Wizard would have.

Regarding your request for Itachi's strength: Given the power of the Sharingan and Mangekyō Sharingan, combined with your high Aptitude Potentials and custom class features, it is entirely realistic that this Itachi could solo the Uchiha Clan. The `Destiny` ruleset (specifically Protocol 6.B.3.c on "Appropriate Challenge Level" for antagonists) will ensure that the scale of threats and challenges you face will appropriately escalate with your character's immense power, allowing for a compelling narrative where you can indeed accomplish such a feat.

Let's establish the foundation for this evil Itachi:

### Itachi Uchiha: The Architects of Despair

**New Evil Justification for the Uchiha Clan Massacre:**
The Uchiha clan, in Itachi's cold, calculating view, is a cancer upon the Shinobi world. Their obsession with tradition, their festering resentment, their volatile emotions (especially tied to their Sharingan's evolution), and their narrow, clan-centric vision are not just weaknesses—they are a fundamental flaw in the grand design of global order. They are a relic, a chaotic element that will inevitably lead to destruction, either their own through reckless rebellion or the larger system's through their unpredictable internal conflicts. Itachi believes true evolution, true order, and ultimate control can only be achieved by eradicating these emotional, traditionalist barriers. He sees himself as a surgeon, performing a necessary, brutal excision for the "greater good"—his own meticulously planned, absolute, and unyielding order. Their power is wasted, their emotions a liability, and their existence a direct impediment to his transcendent vision.

**Goals:**

1.  **Immediate (The Uchiha Excision):** Systematically eliminate the Uchiha clan to remove a volatile and unpredictable element that threatens his grand design for Konoha. During this process, he seeks to acquire or secure specific Uchiha artifacts, forbidden jutsu, or other unique resources that could aid his future plans, consolidating power and knowledge that would otherwise be squandered.
2.  **Mid-Term (Konoha's Hidden Hand):** Manipulate Konoha's power structures from the deepest shadows. He will guide its policies, dictate its strategic direction, and ensure its strength, but always subtly, ensuring it becomes a powerful, stable tool in the realization of his ultimate vision for a controlled, orderly world. He has no desire for public leadership; instead, he seeks to be the unseen architect, the power *behind* the Hokage and the council, expertly playing them against each other to achieve his ends.
3.  **Long-Term (The Great Unifying Order):** His ultimate ambition transcends mere village politics. Itachi believes the concept of "Hidden Villages" and the constant, inefficient cycle of warfare and political intrigue are obsolete. He aims to subtly influence the entire Shinobi world, forging a new, unified order under a single, hidden authority—his own. This could involve eliminating key figures in other villages, orchestrating "false flag" conflicts to demonstrate the futility of war, or creating a pervasive system of control where unpredictability and strong, disruptive emotions are suppressed. He is willing to commit any atrocity, sacrifice any individual, for this "greater," meticulously crafted, and ultimately cold order.

### Itachi's Special Abilities & Proposed Mechanics:

**A. The Sharingan (Active):**
Your character possesses the Sharingan from the outset, already demonstrating your prodigy status.
*   **Enhanced Perception (Passive):** You have Advantage on all Wisdom (Perception) checks, especially for visual details, reading body language, and detecting subtle movements (including those of chakra).
*   **Illusion/Genjutsu Mastery (Learned):** As specified, you learn all control/crowd control spells (e.g., *Hold Person, Confusion, Command, Dominate Person, Suggestion, Fear, Hypnotic Pattern, Mass Suggestion, Programmed Illusion*) as you reach the appropriate Class Levels. These will draw from your Energy Points (EP) for casting, based on the `Destiny` ruleset's spell-like ability costs (4.3.A).
*   **Ability Copying (Core Mechanic):** When you witness another character use an ability (spell, jutsu, or non-magic technique), you gain an innate understanding of its workings.
    *   **Spells/Jutsu (EP-based):**
        *   **Unranked/Basic Jutsu (Cantrip-equivalent):** You can immediately use it at will (0 EP cost).
        *   **Ranked Jutsu (costs EP):**
            *   If its power level (EP cost tier, see `Destiny` 4.3.A) is **equal to or lower than the highest EP cost tier of a spell/jutsu you *already know***: You can learn it by spending a Primary Action (2 CP) to focus on the technique and succeeding a **DC 10 Intelligence (Ninjutsu/Arcana) check**. On success, you can now use it, provided you have the necessary EP.
            *   If it's a **ranked jutsu of a *higher* power level** than any you currently know: You must succeed a **DC 10 + (EP cost tier difference * 2) Intelligence (Ninjutsu/Arcana) check**. On success, you can use it, provided you have the necessary EP. *Failure means you grasp the general concept but cannot replicate it; you can attempt again after a Long Rest and further observation.*
    *   **Non-Magic/Physical Abilities:** If it's a specific technique (e.g., a unique sword maneuver, a specific taijutsu form, a specific acrobatics feat), you gain an **Expertise Tag** (see `Destiny` 2.5.B) related to that technique and gain Advantage on rolls when attempting to replicate it. For very complex, multi-stage, or unique physical abilities (e.g., Rock Lee's Eight Gates opening), you may gain an Expertise Tag related to the *principle* but the full replication would require dedicated training or be deemed impossible without specific biological/narrative prerequisites.
    *   **GM (AI) Discretion:** Some unique, bloodline-specific (other than Uchiha's own), or exceptionally high-level abilities (e.g., Sage Mode, Eight Gates, True Golem Creation, or other character's personal Defining Traits) may be deemed uncopiable, or only copiable with immense narrative and mechanical cost, to maintain game balance and narrative integrity. This will be clarified if such an ability is encountered.

**B. Mangekyō Sharingan (Active):**
You possess the Mangekyō Sharingan. This grants you access to incredibly potent, unique visual powers, each with a personal cost. You choose one of the following abilities, and the associated cost will apply each time you activate it:

*   **Option 1: Amaterasu - Blazing Scourge**
    *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
    *   **Effect:** Target a creature or object you can see within 120 feet. It is engulfed in unquenchable black flames that deal `6d6` Fire damage at the start of each of its turns. The flames are magical and cannot be extinguished by normal means (only by Itachi's will or specific counter-magic). A creature can attempt a **DC 15 Health (Endurance) check** at the end of each of its turns to end the effect on itself.
    *   **Cost:** Each time you activate Amaterasu, you gain **1 level of Fatigue** (see `Destiny` 4.4) due to the immense strain on your eye.
*   **Option 2: Tsukuyomi - Temporal Nightmare**
    *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
    *   **Effect:** Target a creature you can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 15 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or be trapped in Itachi's illusion for up to 1 round per Itachi's Intelligence modifier (minimum 1 round). Within this illusionary world, Itachi can manipulate the perception of time, causing moments to feel like days or weeks of torture. The target takes `8d4` Psychic damage and gains **1 level of Fatigue** (see `Destiny` 4.4).
    *   **Cost:** Each time you activate Tsukuyomi, you gain **1 level of Fatigue** due to the immense mental and ocular strain.
*   **Option 3: Susano'o - Ethereal Colossus**
    *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 10 EP initially, then 3 EP at the start of each of your turns to sustain.
    *   **Effect:** You summon a spectral, chakra-based avatar around yourself. This avatar grants you **Temporary HP equal to 10 * your Class Level** (these absorb damage first). While active, you have **Damage Reduction (DR) 5** against all physical damage, and your unarmed melee attacks deal `2d8` Bludgeoning damage. You cannot perform actions that require fine manipulation (e.g., picking locks, writing) while Susano'o is active.
    *   **Cost:** Each round you sustain Susano'o, you gain **1 level of Fatigue** (see `Destiny` 4.4) at the start of your turn, reflecting the incredible physical and spiritual toll. The Susano'o also disappears if you become Unconscious.

**C. Rinnegan (Future - Level 16):**
You will learn the Rinnegan at Level 16. When you reach that point, we will discuss and choose from potent, world-altering abilities. Here are a few conceptual options, drawing inspiration from Naruto:

*   **Option 1: Six Paths Devotion (Versatility):** Gain access to three distinct abilities from the Six Paths of Pain. This could be a combination of Universal Pull/Push (Chibaku Tensei/Shinra Tensei at reduced scale), Chakra Absorption, and Soul Absorption (e.g., pulling souls from bodies). Each ability would have its own EP cost and specific mechanical effects, allowing for incredible tactical versatility.
*   **Option 2: Planetary Devastation (World-Shaper):** Spend a massive amount of EP (e.g., 30 EP). As a Primary Action, create a gravitational core that pulls nearby terrain and objects into a colossal, crushing sphere, trapping or annihilating targets within a vast area. This would be a single, ultimate ability for massive destruction and terrain manipulation.
*   **Option 3: Samsara of Heavenly Life (Life & Death Manipulation):** Spend ALL remaining EP. As a Primary Action, restore a number of recently deceased creatures (up to your Wisdom Modifier) to life, or greatly accelerate the healing of many living creatures. This would be a campaign-altering ability with potentially immense narrative costs (e.g., a permanent reduction to your maximum EP, gaining multiple levels of Fatigue or even a temporary Aptitude score reduction).
