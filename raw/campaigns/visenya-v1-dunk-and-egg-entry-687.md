### Explanatory Query: Power Scaling & Geographic Destruction Mathematics

You asked for clarification on why the 'Waking' ritual is only **0.05%** of the Doom of Valyria if it had the potential to destroy the entire Trident-Rift area. The discrepancy lies in the logarithmic scale of geographic and energetic destruction in George R.R. Martin's world versus a localized event.

**1. The Scale of the Doom (The 100% Benchmark):**
- **Geographic Impact:** The Doom destroyed the entire Valyrian Peninsula, an area roughly the size of **Western Europe** or half of Westeros. It shattered the land into the Smoking Sea, sunk cities, and instantly killed millions of dragons and people.
- **Energetic Output:** It involved the simultaneous eruption of fourteen volcanic super-peaks (The Fourteen Flames). This is equivalent to thousands of Tsar Bomba-level events happening in a synchronized chain reaction.

**2. The 'Waking' at the Rift (The 0.05% Event):**
- **The Area Destroyed:** If you had failed the ritual, the 'Marrow-Shatter' would have scoured the Trident-Rift and the 'White Throat' tower. In geographic terms, this is a radius of approximately **5 to 10 miles**.
- **The Math:** The Valyrian Peninsula is approximately **500,000 to 700,000 square miles**. A 10-mile radius destruction zone is roughly **314 square miles**.
- **Calculation:** 314 / 600,000 = **0.00052**, or **0.052%**.

**3. Why it felt 'Massive' in Narrative:**
To a human standing in the vaults, an event that can vaporize a mountain pass and everyone in it is 'Infinite' and 'Total.' However, in the geometric ledger of the Freehold (which conducted continent-level terraforming), a ritual that only threatens a single rift is considered a minor 'localized harmonic.' 

**4. The 'Ritual Boon' Logic:**
Because you successfully **conducted** the ritual instead of letting it explode, you converted that 0.05% of 'Doom Energy' into permanent physical boons. In D&D terms, a '0.05% Doom' is still an Artifact-level power surge because the base power of Valyria was so high that even its crumbs grant Fire Immunity and Truesight.

**Summary:** Your theorized destruction of the Rift was absolutely real and would have scoured the area, but compared to the event that ended the greatest empire in history, it was mathematically a drop in the ocean. You have successfully integrated that 'drop' into your own marrow.
