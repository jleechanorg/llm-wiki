**Protocol Change Request: Kael's Expanded Strategic Autonomy**

**1. Proposal:**
Establish a new protocol: Castellan Kael (Level 9) is granted expanded strategic autonomy. He will now make independent decisions on the utilization of the spy network, the appointment of non-combat Castellans (or other administrative roles in established territories), and the overall non-military consolidation of Daemon's empire. Daemon retains final authority on all direct military conquest targets and the leadership of major campaigns. Kael will report on these autonomous decisions and their outcomes periodically.

**3. Differential Draft & Justification:**

**Differential Report - Change ID: 20240530-D**

*   **Source Text (Current behavior of Kael's autonomy, as per 20240529-D and 20240529-G):**
    *   `Castellan Autonomy Protocol`: Castellans operate autonomously in daily management, reporting periodically, and interrupting for critical decisions or major threats.
    *   `Castellan Proposal Protocol`: Kael proposes candidates for Castellan roles, with Daemon making the final decision.
    *   Spy network usage and other strategic decisions are typically made by Daemon.

*   **Proposed New Text (New Protocol Addition and Modifications):**
    *   **New Protocol Addition: "Kael's Grand Strategist Protocol"**
        *   **Invocation:** This protocol applies specifically to Castellan Kael (Level 9+), reflecting his advanced strategic capabilities and Daemon's trust.
        *   **Expanded Authority:** Kael is empowered to make independent, strategic decisions regarding:
            *   **Utilization of the Spy Network:** How to deploy agents, gather specific intelligence (non-military targets, internal dissent, economic weaknesses), and conduct subtle influence/sabotage operations (within the established budget for the network).
            *   **Appointment of Non-Combat Castellans/Administrators:** For any newly conquered territories or vacant administrative roles (excluding direct military command positions), Kael can now independently appoint suitable individuals from Daemon's forces, without prior approval from Daemon (unless the appointee is another elite warrior or a particularly high-profile figure).
            *   **Non-Military Consolidation & Resource Management:** Kael will independently devise and implement detailed plans for consolidating newly conquered territories (after Daemon's military conquest is complete), maximizing resource extraction (beyond general directives), and streamlining economic flows.
        *   **Daemon's Retained Authority:** Daemon retains absolute and final authority over:
            *   The selection of all primary military conquest targets.
            *   The direct command and leadership of all major military campaigns.
            *   The appointment of elite warrior Castellans (Borin, Joric, Gareth, Ragnar, Theron).
            *   Any decision requiring significant, large-scale resource expenditure beyond the established budgets for Kael's autonomous operations.
        *   **Reporting:** Kael will continue to provide periodic reports on the success and challenges of his autonomous initiatives, and will immediately alert Daemon to any major threats or opportunities that require Daemon's direct military intervention.

*   **Justification:** This protocol significantly enhances Kael's role as a true Grand Strategist for the empire, offloading complex administrative and intelligence-gathering decisions from Daemon. It allows Daemon to focus his formidable will on direct military conquest, while Kael ensures the efficient and ruthless consolidation and expansion of the empire's non-military power. This streamlines gameplay, adds depth to Kael's character, and reflects Daemon's pragmatic delegation of authority. It aligns with the player's stated desire for Kael to "make bigger decisions" and manage aspects beyond direct combat, while keeping military targets as Daemon's purview.
