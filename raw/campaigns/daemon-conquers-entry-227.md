### Clarification on Level 10 Advancement Choices:

As per the **Destiny Core Rules, Section III. Character Progression System, Rule 3.5 (Aptitude Score Improvement & Feats)**, a character gains an "Advancement Choice" (either increasing Aptitude Scores or choosing a Feat) at Class Levels **4, 8, 12, 16, and 19.**

Since Daemon has just reached **Level 10**, this is **not one of the designated levels** for an Aptitude Score Improvement or Feat choice. He gained his last choice at Level 8 (Physique +2).

Therefore, Daemon does **not** get to make a specific Level 10 advancement choice. His power has increased through his general level-up (HP, potential class features, Combat Prowess Bonus), but not through a specific ASI/Feat choice at this level.
