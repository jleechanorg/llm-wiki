Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Farmhouse Interior, Early Evening

The farmhouse was utterly silent, a tableau of unconscious, bound forms amidst the comforting domesticity of a roaring hearth. Man Tibbet, his wife, and their three children lay inert, transformed into silent bundles. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood amidst her work, her movements economical, her gaze sweeping over the secured 'stock'. Her EP was low, her Channel Divinity expended, but her HP remained full. The primary phase of acquisition was complete.

The iron-bound strongbox had yielded its coin and, more importantly, the deed and the letter from **House Durin's Folly**. The dwarven house, ostensibly focused on mining and banking, maintained a hidden rivalry with her own House Valerius. The ledger's meticulous records of "Security Contingency Fund" and "Arcane Oversight" payments, linked to a lesser noble family tied to Durin's Folly, screamed of a strategic outpost, not a simple farm. And then, the Orbs of Scrying – hidden, magical surveillance tools – sealed the truth. This was a clandestine observation post, likely monitoring the eastern approaches to Baldur's Gate, a potential asset in the subtle wars of influence that defined the city's Upper Echelon.

Her initial contract, a crude abduction for coin, had unexpectedly peeled back a layer of deeper political intrigue. This wasn't merely about kidnapping peasants; it was about uncovering a rival house's secret operations.
