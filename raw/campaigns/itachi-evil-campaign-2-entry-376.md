**B. Proposed New Mechanic: III.X. Transcendent Leveling: Beyond Mortal Limits**

This rule specifically applies to **Itachi Uchiha** and any other character who, through monumental narrative milestones (such as awakening the Rinnegan through perfected Uchiha-Senju harmonization), achieves a state of power that transcends typical mortal limitations.

1.  **Extended Level Cap:** For Itachi Uchiha (and any other designated transcendent character), the maximum Class Level is extended from Level 20 to **Level 100**. This reflects their ascension to a cosmic-tier being, capable of confronting threats and wielding power on a galactic or multiversal scale.
2.  **Proportional Scaling for All Mechanics:** All existing character mechanics and progression formulas will continue to scale proportionally and logically beyond Level 20, up to Level 100. The Game Master (AI) will extrapolate and apply these scaling rules consistently:
    *   **XP to Next Level:** The XP progression curve will continue to increase exponentially or proportionally to maintain a consistent challenge for leveling. (e.g., if Level 20 requires 130,000 XP to the next level, Level 21 might require 150,000-180,000, and so on, following a smooth upward curve).
    *   **Hit Points (HP) Gain:** The character's class-specific Hit Die + Health Modifier will continue to be added at each level, ensuring continuous growth in durability.
    *   **Aptitude Score Improvement & Feats:** The schedule for gaining Advancement Choices (increasing Aptitudes or taking Feats) will continue every 4 levels (e.g., at Levels 24, 28, 32, etc.), ensuring continuous attribute growth.
    *   **Combat Prowess Bonus:** This bonus will continue to increase every 4 levels (e.g., Level 21-24: +7, Level 25-28: +8, etc.), maintaining combat proficiency.
    *   **Energy Points (EP) & Regeneration:** The character's maximum EP pool, along with all passive and siphon-based EP regeneration, will continue to scale upwards, reflecting their ever-increasing chakra reserves and control.
    *   **Jutsu Potency & Save DCs:** The power, range, and difficulty class (DC) of all Jutsu and abilities will continue to scale, reflecting the character's increasing mastery and control over cosmic forces.
    *   **Antagonist Scaling:** The power, resources, and influence of adversaries will dynamically scale to match Itachi's (and Sasuke's) increasing power and tier of play, ensuring continuous challenges appropriate for legendary and cosmic levels. New types of threats (e.g., divine beings, primordial entities, trans-dimensional invaders) will emerge.
3.  **Tier Progression (Expanded):** The existing Leveling Tiers (Apprentice, Adventurer, Elite, Legendary) will logically extend to encompass these higher levels, defining the scope of challenges, influence, and power for characters operating on a cosmic or multiversal scale.
4.  **Narrative & Thematic Alignment:** This extended progression is intended to reflect the character's journey towards ultimate power and their ability to directly influence or reshape the fundamental laws of reality and the cosmos.
