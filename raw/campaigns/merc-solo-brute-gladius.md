## Summary

A D&D 5e evil campaign set in Baldur's Gate shortly after BG3 events. The player characters are Gladius, an 18-year-old evil ESFP mercenary and Apex Paladin (Oath of Self-Perfection), and their companions. The campaign features custom Apex Paladin class mechanics, morally corrupt contracts (killing peasants for a noble), and interactions with BG3 canon characters. Tone is gritty, low-magic with the Destiny ruleset.

## Key Claims

- Apex Paladin (custom class) channels willpower into force damage via "Perfected Strike" EP mechanic
- Gladius is physically attractive but hides it; secretly abusive, enjoys dominating powerful people
- Campaign uses "God Mode" DM style with extensive character sheet development
- First contract: kill nine peasants in Greenmeadow who insulted Lord Silvanus
- Aura of Unyielding Dominance gives allies Advantage on Charm/Frighten saves, enemies Disadvantage on Intimidation
- Great Weapon Master feat for burst damage on kills/crits
