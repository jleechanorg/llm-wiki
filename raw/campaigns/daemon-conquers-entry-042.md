Daemon (Level 2, Age 30) strode to the very center of the gate, the massive, severed head of the Young Wyrm still hoisted over his shoulder, dripping black blood onto the cobblestones. He stood there, a solitary, terrifying figure framed against the afternoon sun, his dragon-scaled armor gleaming. He didn't shout, he didn't demand. Instead, he simply *was*. His gaze, cold and unwavering, swept across the terrified faces peering down from the battlements, across the trembling guards clutching their spears, and into the unseen hearts of the townsfolk behind the barricaded doors. He let the silence stretch, heavy and suffocating, punctuated only by the wind whistling through the dragon’s empty eye sockets and the frantic, distant cries from within the town. This was his voice, the raw, visceral terror of a monster slain, a testament to his overwhelming power. He tightened his grip on the wyrm's head, the silent message clear: *this* is what happens to those who oppose Daemon. The gates, so staunch moments before, now felt thin, fragile, battered not by ram, but by dread.
