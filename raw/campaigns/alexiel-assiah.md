## Summary
A D&D 5e high-fantasy celestial warfare campaign set in the world of Assiah during the Thousand Year War between the Celestial Imperium (led by Lord Regent Raziel) and the Unchained Host (led by Lucifer). The protagonist Alexiel is Lucifer's biological "daughter" — a construct designed as his perfect heir — who secretly possesses magical powers despite being publicly known as a "Null." The campaign follows her defection from the Host after witnessing the massacre at Silverwood, her infiltration of the Imperium, and her eventual role in the Starfall Gambit to assassinate Lucifer.

## Key Claims
- Alexiel is a Level 6 Warrior/Secret Sorcerer gestalt with INT 20 and CHA-based combat, featuring the "Three Layers" system: public Null mask, Reluctant Champion facade, and true Joyful Predator identity
- The campaign uses a 5-tier progression system: Levels 1-6 (local), 7-12 (regional), 13-18 (continental), 19-24 (legendary), 25-30 (transcendent/cosmic)
- Lucifer ascends to Tier 5 as a 6-winged Seraphim at the Battle of Mourning Fields — the highest power tier in the setting
- The Starfall Gambit is a multi-layered assassination operation against Lucifer at the Obsidian Spire, designed by Alexiel and Raziel together
- Prince Artorius is Alexiel's romantic interest and moral compass; their children (Cassian, Darius, Sariel) are born in the timeline

## Campaign Arc
1. **Chapters 1-5**: The Defection — Silverwood massacre triggers Alexiel's switch to the Imperium; survival challenge through Dragon's Tooth; capture and interrogation by Artorius; Seraph's Watch dungeon crawl; betrothal to Artorius
2. **Chapters 6-10**: The Mourning Fields — Lucifer ambushes; Artorius killed; Starfall Guard decimated; Alexiel fully unleashes Joyful Predator; grief forges bond between Alexiel and Raziel
3. **Chapters 11-15**: The Starfall Gambit — Vow of 40 elite soldiers; infiltration of Obsidian Spire; Alexiel reveals all secrets to Raziel; Lucifer killed; Alexiel rejects godhood and divine destiny
