Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Farmhouse Interior, Early Evening

The farmhouse was utterly silent, a tableau of unconscious bodies amidst the comforting domesticity of a roaring hearth. Man Tibbet, his wife, and their three children lay still on the worn floor, their forms rendered inert by Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) precise, non-lethal strikes. The chilling efficiency of her actions had secured the 'stock', leaving the house quiet and the family oblivious. Her EP was low, her Channel Divinity expended, but her HP remained full. The primary phase of acquisition was complete.

Her gaze swept over the prone forms. While subdued, they were not yet secured for transport. Her meticulous nature demanded order and thoroughness, even in abduction. She reached into her **Bag of Holding**, her fingers selecting the necessary tools: more lengths of sturdy rope, and several more canvas sacks, carefully folded. This was a larger family, requiring more preparation than Widow Elara.

Nocturne moved systematically, a dark, efficient shadow in the flickering firelight. She bound Man Tibbet and his wife first, securing their wrists and ankles with tight, professional knots, then pulled the sacks over their heads, muffling any potential cries should they stir. The children were next, smaller, easier to manage. Each was bound and sacked, transformed from individual lives into silent, anonymous bundles. The process was swift, leaving no trace of struggle, only an eerie stillness. The 'stock' was now fully prepared for transport.

With the family secured, Nocturne’s intellect (Intelligence 18) turned to the second part of her internal command: search the farm for valuables and clues. This farm was far more than a simple rural dwelling. The professional guards, the arcane ward, the detailed map she'd already acquired—all pointed to a hidden significance, a value beyond mere crops and livestock. The Underdark buyers were paying a premium, and that implied a reason. She moved with a purpose that transcended simple theft, seeking not just coins, but information, leverage, and perhaps even a deeper source of power.

She started in the main living area, her keen eyes (Wisdom 16) scanning every nook and cranny. The furnishings were plain, sturdy. Nothing of obvious value hung on the walls or sat on the shelves. She checked beneath floorboards, behind loose bricks in the hearth, and inside empty barrels. Her search was thorough, almost clinical. In a hidden compartment beneath a loose floorboard near the hearth, she found a small, iron-bound strongbox. Masterwork Lockpicks (newly acquired) would prove useful later.

She then moved to what appeared to be Man Tibbet’s study or office, a small room off the main living area. Here, the utilitarian nature of the farmhouse gave way to something more organized. A sturdy wooden desk, a ledger, and a few locked drawers. Her gaze lingered on the ledger. This could contain financial information, perhaps revealing the source of the farm’s unexpected wealth, or its connections to the professional guards.

The subtle scent of dried herbs and a faint metallic tang caught her attention near a closed, heavy door. It led to a small, cool storage room. Her Apex Senses, though currently low on EP, had already told her nothing major was here, but a deeper inspection wouldn't hurt. This farm was an enigma, and every piece of data she could acquire would serve her ultimate goal of power and wealth.
