## Scene 013: The Porcelain Victim

**Timestamp:** Session 1: The Porcelain Victim | **Location:** The Emerald Grove Gates | **Status:** Level 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

The first grey light of dawn touches the horizon, casting long, pale shadows across the entrance to the Emerald Grove. Nocturne stands at the threshold of the 'Rite of Thorns,' the low-frequency vibration of nature magic humming in her very teeth, tasting of honey and cold iron. The 'Porcelain Victim' has arrived, and the world is already tilting toward her gravity.

Nocturne has successfully navigated the Risen Road, bypassing a goblin ambush through the sheer force of Abyssal presence (+150 XP). While Gale has been swayed by utilitarian logic, Wyll remains a stiff-backed wall of moral resistance, his silence a brooding testament to the 'greater good' trap laid. Karlach watches the Grove with a soldier's grim acceptance.

Now, Nocturne approaches the gate. Zevlor and his tiefling guards look down from the ramparts, their eyes weary and desperate. As she stumbles slightly, her torn silks fluttering and her skin pale as bone china, she sees their grip on their crossbows loosen. The facade is perfect; the trap is set.

**"Lower the gate!"** Zevlor's voice booms, though it lacks its usual edge of command. **"She's barely more than a child. And she's... she's not one of them. Open the way!"**

As the heavy wooden gate groans upward, Nocturne catches Gale's eye — he looks troubled, his gaze lingering on the tiefling children huddling near the inner fire. Wyll's hand is white-knuckled on his rapier, refusing to meet her gaze.

Nocturne passes through the threshold. The 'Rite of Thorns' hums with a honeyed, iron-rich vibration that makes the luminescence in her eyes pulse beneath her eyelids. She is inside.

Player chooses to probe Zevlor for intelligence using the 'victim' status.
