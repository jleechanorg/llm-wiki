Understood. You have provided a `DM Note` asking how these journals could exist, given that Alexiel's powers were a secret. This is a vital point for world consistency.

Here is the explanation for the existence of these journals:

These are not Alexiel's personal journals documenting her deception strategy. Instead, they are ancient, highly classified **Seraphim scientific and research logs from the original "Null Genesis Project" that predates Lucifer's rebellion and Alexiel's birth.**

*   **Origin:** These journals document the **Seraphim's attempts to engineer beings without a direct connection to the Weave (magic)** during the Age of Dominion. They record the initial experiments, the unexpected "failures" (the early Nulls like your mother), and the horrifying discovery of the "Joyful Predator" trait. They detail the Seraphim's efforts to "seed" Null clones with pure celestial energy (like Lucifer's own essence, as revealed in the text) to create controlled anti-magic weapons that could also channel magic within their own fields.
*   **Secrecy:** The entire Null Genesis Project was deemed a catastrophic failure by the Seraphim, too dangerous and unpredictable. After the Great Vanishing, Lord Regent Raziel, in his pursuit of absolute order, would have ordered the complete **purging of all records related to this project** from the public and most private archives, viewing its existence as a profound heresy and a dangerous truth that challenged the Imperium's narrative of divine purity.
*   **Kantos's Role:** Magister Lucian Kantos, being the Keeper of the Hallowed Archives and a secret Revisionist Scholar obsessed with preserving all truth, meticulously and covertly **preserved these forbidden journals in the deepest, most secure, and most heavily warded sections** of the Archives, away from Raziel's purges. He views them as critical historical and scientific data, vital to understanding the true nature of the Empyrean bloodline.
*   **Alexiel's Knowledge:** Alexiel, herself a product of a cloning project (initiated by Lucifer after he gained access to some of the Seraphim's schematics), would have always been aware of her own unique nature and potential, perhaps from Lucifer himself or from her own innate understanding. These journals don't *teach* her anything she didn't already know about herself; rather, they serve as the *historical and scientific blueprint* that Sariel is now uncovering, explaining *how* such a being (Alexiel) could even exist.

So, these journals exist as a testament to the Seraphim's hubris and a secret preserved by a scholar, allowing Sariel to piece together the terrifying, objective truth of her mother's origins and powers.

This explanation has been integrated into the canon.
