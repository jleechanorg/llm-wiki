Game Master:
You do not accept a partial victory. The *Hunger* demands total domination of the battlefield's resources.

"Strip them," you order **Roric**. "Armor, weapons, boots. If it has value, it is ours."

The rogue grins and sets to work. The Acolytes, terrified and surrounded, offer no resistance as they are stripped down to their tunics in the cold mountain air. Their leather armor, scimitars, and pouches are piled onto a tarp. 

You turn your attention to the corpse of **Amarande**. The Archdruid lies in the ash, his stag headdress broken. You rifle through his belongings with clinical efficiency.

**Loot Secured:**
*   **Ring of Fire Resistance:** An iron band set with a ruby. (Resistance to Fire damage). Ironic, given the circumstances.
*   **Potion of Invulnerability:** A vial of thick, grey liquid.
*   **Studded Leather +1:** Finely crafted, reinforced with ironwood.
*   **Gold:** 120 gp.

With the human element neutralized, you turn to the beasts. The two **Black Bears** are restless, pacing near their shivering masters, sniffing the blood in the air. You whisper the incantation for *Speak with Animals*, and the growls of the bears shift in your ears, becoming intelligible, guttural speech.

*"Fire... pain... Pack-Leader dead... new Alpha?"*

You step forward, locking eyes with the larger bear. You do not flinch. You project the aura of the apex predator.

"The Stag was weak," you growl in the tongue of beasts. "He let the fire take the woods. I am the Fire-Bringer. I am the Alpha now. Serve me, and you eat. Fight me, and you burn."

**Animal Handling Check: 24 (Critical Success)**

The bear pauses, sniffing the scent of Amarande's blood on your hands. It lowers its head, a gesture of submission. *"Stronger... Alpha. We hunger."*

You point to the pile of dead Giant Spiders. "Eat."

The bears descend on the spider carcasses, tearing into them with savage glee. You have turned the guardians.

Finally, you kneel before the shivering Acolytes. "The mine," you state. "The Iron Throne. Where is the entrance, and what is Davaeorn planning?"

The older acolyte stammers, his teeth chattering. "The... the river! Follow the river north! The mine entrance is hidden behind a waterfall, guarded by the Black Talon elites! Davaeorn... he is flooding the lower levels! He plans to drown the slaves if the mine is breached!"
