**Roman Explanations:**
- **Nymphaeum**: A monument or shrine consecrated to nymphs, often centered around a natural or artificial fountain.
- **Furia**: The Roman equivalent of the Greek Furies, chthonic deities of vengeance and divine retribution. In this world, they are spectral constructs used by the Augurs to hunt Miasma-users.
- **Luctus**: A Latin term for 'grief' or 'mourning.' This specific spectral hound represents the psychic weight of those whose timelines have been snuffed out by Julian interventions.
