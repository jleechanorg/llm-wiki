Location: Approaching Man Tibbet's Farm, Early Evening

The silence on the main road was now absolute, the two fallen guards meticulously hidden, their equipment enriching Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) already impressive gear. The duty roster, a small but potent piece of intelligence, confirmed the rhythm of local security—a rhythm she had now disrupted. The initial contract, once presented as a simple "easy picking," had evolved into a complex, multi-layered problem, and Nocturne felt a cold, sharp thrill. The higher the complexity, the greater the challenge, and implicitly, the greater the potential reward.

Her mind, a tactical engine of unparalleled efficiency, discarded the notion of returning to Elara's cottage immediately. The single captive was secured, a static asset. Man Tibbet's farm, however, with its "large family," its hidden guards, and its arcane ward, represented the true prize. With the road patrol neutralized and their schedule known, a significant variable had been removed. The path to the larger 'stock' was clearer, albeit still fraught with internal defenses.

She moved north from the main road, the Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate whispering faintly as she glided through the tall, moon-dappled grass. Every shadow was her ally, every dip in the terrain a natural cover. Her steps were economical, silent, a testament to her honed Coordination (14). Her gaze was fixed on the cluster of buildings that formed Man Tibbet's farm, now a darker, more imposing silhouette against the bruised canvas of the sky.

As she drew closer, the faint sounds of life from within the farmhouse became more distinct: a child's distant laugh, the murmur of adult voices, the clatter of pots – mundane sounds that belied the professional security lurking within. Her **Apex Senses** scan from before had revealed two trained guards on the second floor, one positioned near a subtle embrasure, and a magical ward pulsing near the barn. This was a network, not a simple farm.

Nocturne found a new vantage point, a low ridge overlooking the entire farm complex. From here, she could meticulously map out potential entry points, identify the most efficient paths to neutralize the internal guards, and plan the extraction of the large family. The new intelligence on the road patrol, combined with her detailed scan of the farm, meant she could now devise a truly flawless operation. The hunt was on, and this time, there would be no unforeseen barks, no embarrassing compromises. Only precision.
