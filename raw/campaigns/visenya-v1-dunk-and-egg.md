## Summary
A reimagined D&D 5e adaptation of George R.R. Martin's "Dunk and Egg" stories set in Westeros, where the player character is Aegon (nicknamed "Egg"), a young girl secretly a Targaryen princess disguised as a squire. She travels the Seven Kingdoms with Ser Duncan the Tall. The campaign focuses on character creation, the Ashford Tourney, and the bond between the hedge knight and his mysterious squire.

## Key Claims
- Egg is a 10-year-old Targaryen princess disguised as a boy, her silver-gold hair hidden beneath a straw hat
- Custom class: "Dragon Scholar" — INT/WIS primary, CHA checks can use INT or WIS modifier
- Ser Duncan ("Dunk") is a towering hedge knight (nearly 7 feet tall) traveling to the Ashford Tourney
- The campaign uses God Mode for mechanical refinement — ability scores, class features, spell progression negotiated mid-scene
- Character creation dominates scenes 1-20: stat allocation (16 INT, 16 WIS, 14 DEX, 8 CON), class features up to level 30
