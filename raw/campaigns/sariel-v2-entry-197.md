### **Clairvoyance (3rd Level Illusion)**
*   **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
*   **Range:** Miles (Unlimited if you use a focus)
*   **Components:** V, S, M (a focus worth at least 100 gp, such as a jeweled horn for hearing or a glass eye for seeing)
*   **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
*   **Description:** You create an invisible sensor within range in a location familiar to you (a place you have visited or seen before) or known to you (through a description). The sensor remains there for the duration, and you can choose one of the following sensory modes for it:
    *   **Hearing:** You can hear sounds as if you were in the sensor's location.
    *   **Seeing:** You can see as if you were in the sensor's location.
    You can switch between hearing and seeing as an action. A creature that can see invisible objects sees the sensor as a dim, shimmering aura.

### **Major Image (3rd Level Illusion)**
*   **Casting Time:** 1 action
*   **Range:** 120 feet
*   **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fleece)
*   **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
*   **Description:** You create the image of an object, a creature, or some other visible phenomenon that is no larger than a 20-foot cube. The image appears at a spot within range and lasts for the duration. The image is purely visual; it creates no sound, smell, or other sensory effects. You can use your action to move the image anywhere within range. As the image changes location, it appears to be a real phenomenon (for example, if you create an image of a creature that moves, it appears to walk, fly, or crawl as appropriate).
