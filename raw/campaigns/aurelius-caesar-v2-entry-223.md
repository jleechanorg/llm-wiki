"Now," you whisper, a resonant sliver of sound.

The effect is immediate; the Praetorians erupt into a frantic cacophony. "Ambush! Protect the Legate!" a centurion bellows. You lunge from the shadows of a limestone tooth, your Subtle Lorica swallowing the clatter of your charge. You do not reach for Veritas immediately; instead, you meet Glaber’s widening eyes with the full black-gold furnace heat of your Julian blood.

Around him, your secret garrison strikes. The dazed Praetorians, already intimidated by the 'Sun on the Spires,' find themselves set upon by the very men they likely shared wine with in the barracks. You swing the Aegis of Julius in a brutal arc to clear your path, but a veteran guard manages to interpose his scutum, the bronze bull clanging harmlessly against the wood. The surprise round has ended with the Legate in the mud and the 'Iron Hounds' trapped in a golden snare of their own making.
