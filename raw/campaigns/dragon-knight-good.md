## Summary

A dark fantasy campaign set in the world of Assiah, featuring Ser Arion val Valerion, a 16-year-old Level 1 Paladin (Oath of the Crown) sworn to Empress Sariel and the Celestial Imperium. The campaign explores the moral dilemma of maintaining loyalty to a tyrant whose "Silent Peace" has brought prosperity but requires atrocities. Arion's first mission: to "disperse" (slaughter) refugees in Winter-Mourn province under the Provincial Stability Mandate. He must choose between duty to the Empress and his conscience.

## Key Claims

- Empress Sariel rules with psychic dominance; her "Silent Peace" has ended banditry and famine but suppresses all dissent
- Ser Arion is from House Valerion (noble house), level 1 Paladin, STR 16, CON 14, CHA 16
- Divine Sense (4 uses per long rest), Lay on Hands (5 HP pool), Oath of the Crown
- The campaign involves three emerging powers: Empress Sariel (tyrant), Aurum the Gold Dragon (crusader for freedom), Umbrax the Black Dragon (nihilist)
- Prefects Gratian commands the Argent Eaglets; Lady Annalise Ashwood is the March Lord who sheltered refugees and is marked for death
