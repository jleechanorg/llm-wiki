This guarantees the EMS source is perfected under my direct guidance.
*   **Eye Exchange:** The ultimate goal for EMS. If Sasuke is a loyal minion or ally, the exchange becomes a controlled procedure rather than a forcible extraction. My **Medical Ninjutsu/Healing Arts** expertise would ensure the procedure's perfection.
*   **Powerful Ally:** The highest reward. A loyal, powerful Uchiha ally would amplify my influence exponentially, both globally and within Konoha, serving as a direct extension of my will. He could shield me from scrutiny or act as a decisive force where my direct intervention is too overt.

**Overall Strategic Assessment of the Composite Plan:** This new strategy represents a tactical masterstroke. It leverages Sasuke's unique potential, transforming a passive resource into a dynamic, active asset. While it requires direct, prolonged interaction and managing emotional variables, my ultimate control over Sasuke's mind and the narrative ensures the risks are minimized. This path accelerates EMS acquisition in a controlled manner, provides a powerful subordinate, and sets the stage for a truly pervasive "Great Unifying Order." It is the most efficient path to consolidating ultimate power.
