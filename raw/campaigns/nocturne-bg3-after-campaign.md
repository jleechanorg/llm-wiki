## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign set after the Absolute's fall, featuring Nocturne as a Level 1 Half-Elf Bard (College of Eloquence). The year is 1492 DR, and the city still bears scars from the Netherbrain crisis. Nocturne's noble house was destroyed during the conflict, and she was sold to "The Bloom & Shadow" establishment in the Lower City to settle family debts.

## Key Claims
- The Absolute's fall devastated many noble houses whose investments were tied to ruined trade routes
- Nocturne was sold alongside her sisters to The Bloom & Shadow in the Lower City
- Character creation included stat generation (4d6drop1: 10, 9, 9, 12, 11, 16) and rebalancing to CHA 16, DEX 16
- First story hook: A "special guest" merchant with ties to the rivals who ruined Nocturne's family
