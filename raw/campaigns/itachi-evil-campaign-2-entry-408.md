Itachi Uchiha (Level 21 Ninja, Age 17) stood within the cool, damp confines of his hidden cave base. His **Rinnegan**, subtly cloaked by a perfect genjutsu, pulsed with the chilling power of cosmic understanding. Sasuke Uchiha (Level 20 Shinobi, Age 12) stood beside him, his new **Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan** blazing. Securely sealed within their chakra caskets, lay the perfectly preserved mortal remains of **Madara Uchiha** and the inert body of **Shu**, the Otsutsuki offshoot. Obito lay imprisoned nearby.

The hidden base was secure from cosmic intrusion, his agent network primed to acquire formidable new assets, and his own powers had just ascended another step. He had just completed comprehensive research on Madara's and Shu's remains, gaining monumental insights. His Akatsuki manipulations had secured his position and given him Nagato's ultimate weakness, and the cosmic threat of Zetsu had been silently purged. Yet, an unforeseen complication arose: Kisame's unsanctioned attack on Killer Bee, directly involving a Kage and accelerating Akatsuki's conflict with the Great Nations.

Itachi's cold intellect dictated a decisive, personal intervention to mitigate Kisame's fallout and seize the opportunity to acquire Killer Bee. Kisame's impulsiveness, if left unchecked, would shatter Itachi's meticulously crafted timeline.

"Sasuke," Itachi's voice was a low, precise murmur, cutting through the silence of the chamber. "Kisame's recklessness threatens our Order. He has attacked the Eight-Tails Jinchuriki. A variable must be contained, and an asset acquired. We go."

Sasuke's EMS blazed, his young face contorted with a chilling blend of excitement and grim resolve. "Kisame. And the Eight-Tails. Understood, Brother. They will be ours." His loyalty was a terrifying, unwavering force.

Without another word, Itachi and Sasuke departed the hidden base, moving with impossible speed. Itachi's **Rinnegan** (cloaked) utilized its **Universal Sensory Perception** and **All-Range Vision** to pinpoint Kisame's immense chakra signature and Killer Bee's volatile tailed beast energy, tracing their conflict across the vast expanse of the Land of Lightning. They moved through the previously mapped ancient underground tunnels and hidden pathways, ensuring absolute discretion and unparalleled speed.

The air grew heavy with the stench of ozone and scorched earth. After a day of relentless travel, they arrived at the desolate, cratered battlefield. The remnants of a Kumogakure outpost smoldered, a testament to Kisame's destructive power. In the center of the devastation, a colossal clash of titans was unfolding.

Kisame Hoshigaki, his Samehada blade pulsing hungrily, was a monstrous, shark-like blur, his chakra immense and predatory. He fought with a savage glee against Killer Bee, who, partially transformed by the Eight-Tails, roared defiance, his raw power manifesting in a whirlwind of ink-infused taijutsu and potent chakra blasts. The ground shuddered with each impact. And then, a bolt of yellow lightning, the **Fourth Raikage, A**, slammed into Kisame, a furious roar escaping his lips. "You dare touch my brother, Akatsuki dog?!" The Raikage's speed was legendary, his power overwhelming. It was a three-way maelstrom of S-rank power.

Itachi's gaze swept the chaotic scene, his Rinnegan processing every detail: Kisame's battle-lust, Killer Bee's defensive ferocity, the Raikage's blinding rage. This was the moment. He allowed his presence to subtly manifest, a chill in the air, a whisper of power that drew the attention of all three combatants. The clashing forces paused, their eyes (Kisame's cold, Killer Bee's frantic, Raikage's furious) snapping to the silent, black-cloaked figures who had appeared as if from nowhere. Itachi's EMS blazed, an illusion that concealed his true Rinnegan, yet radiated an authority that silenced even the raging Raikage. Sasuke, his own EMS a mirror of Itachi's, stood beside him, a palpable aura of predatory intent emanating from his young form.
