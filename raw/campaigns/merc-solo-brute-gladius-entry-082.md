The raw, unadulterated terror on the Stonehand couple's faces was a potent, exquisite sensation for Gladius, far more satisfying than any physical pleasure. Their wills, once so stubbornly independent, were now utterly shattered, their simple strength dissolving into pathetic, quivering fear. The silence in the dwelling, broken only by their ragged breathing, was a testament to his chilling success. The distant, muted sounds of the waking village seemed miles away, irrelevant to the intimate horror he had meticulously crafted within these stone walls.

"Face down. Hands behind your backs," Gladius commanded, his voice a low, gravelly rumble. He didn't raise it, didn't need to. The words were a flat, undeniable order, imbued with the absolute authority of his presence. He watched their eyes, wide with a despair that mirrored the terror of their child, flicker to the limp form on the straw mat. The implicit threat, the knowledge that their obedience was all that held their child's life in the balance, was a tangible leash he now held.

The couple, broken and defeated, complied instantly. The burly farmer, whose rough hands Gladius had seen tilling the fields just hours ago, crumpled to the floor, his face pressing against the cold stone. His wife, a soft, choked sob escaping her lips, followed, her movements clumsy with fear. They lay there, utterly still, pathetic bundles of submission.

Gladius moved with the fluid, unhurried grace of a predator securing its prey. He drew another length of hemp rope from his pack, the material rough against his calloused hands. He knelt first by the man, his large hands working with practiced efficiency, binding the wrists tightly behind the broad back, then cinching the ankles. The farmer offered no resistance, only shallow, desperate breaths. Gladius repeated the process with the woman, her body trembling under his touch, her whimpers barely audible. He tightened the knots, ensuring no escape, no struggle. They were now inert, helpless, utterly at his mercy.

He stood back, surveying the scene. The unconscious child, the bound and terrified parents—all laid low, brought to their knees by his will. A cold hum settled deep in his gut, a profound, almost euphoric satisfaction. Their simple lives, their loud, defiant strength, had been dismantled piece by piece. This was the raw, undeniable pleasure of dominance, the visceral proof of his own power to reduce even the seemingly robust to quivering, helpless things. The contract was almost complete, and the final act promised a chilling culmination.
