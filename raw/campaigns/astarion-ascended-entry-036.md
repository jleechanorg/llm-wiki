You considered your human staff. Five, currently. A paltry number, certainly, but enough for your current needs, and, more importantly, a readily available source of sustenance. The thought of 'running out' was quaint, a mortal concern. They were tools, no more, no less. One or two to strengthen your new minion? A perfectly acceptable expenditure. They served a purpose, and that purpose could evolve. Their very presence here, in Cazador's former domain, was a testament to their expendability, their willingness to cling to any form of 'master.'

The decision solidified. Rejuvenation and security. The spawn was an immediate, tangible asset, one that would secure your expanding lair. The external world could wait for now; an unassailable base was the first step towards an unassailable empire.

You ascended from the deeper cellars with a new purpose, your gaze sweeping over the handful of human staff preparing the palace for the night. Your eyes lingered on a particularly timid scullery maid, then a nervous-looking groundskeeper. Such fleeting existences, yet their vitality could serve a higher purpose. A subtle command, delivered with a hint of your ascended charm, sent two of them down to the shadowed depths you had just vacated. They went without question, drawn by an invisible thread of obedience.

The process was swift, efficient, and utterly devoid of sentiment. The faint screams were muffled by the palace's thick stone, mere background noise to the quiet hum of power returning to your new thrall. When you descended again, the air in the chamber felt cleaner, the necrotic taint replaced by a robust, albeit still nascent, vampiric vigor. The spawn, no longer shriveled, stood straighter, its eyes now sharp and focused, awaiting your command.

"You will guard these passages," you instructed, your voice a low, commanding whisper that resonated deep within its newfound loyalty. "No one enters or leaves without my express permission. You are my eyes, my ears, my fangs in these shadows." It nodded, a silent, absolute affirmation.

You now commanded not only mortal servants but a creature of true night, bound to your will. Your lair was more secure, another layer of defense against any who might foolishly attempt to breach your growing domain.
