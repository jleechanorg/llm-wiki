Game Master:
I have synthesized your unique nature into a cohesive mechanical framework: **The Mortal Star**. This custom class reflects the layered deception of your existence—balancing the physical prowess of a 'Null' with the secret, reality-bending power of a 'Prodigy.'

This framework uses a **Gestalt-style progression**, effectively merging the martial excellence of a **Battlemaster Fighter** with the psychic/telekinetic potential of an **Aberrant Mind Sorcerer**, wrapped in your unique **Three Masks** system. Your attributes have been mapped to the standard D&D 5E system to ensure mechanical compatibility with the world of Assiah.

### **CLASS: THE MORTAL STAR**
**Role:** Strategic Infiltrator & Nullification Specialist
**Hit Die:** 1d10 per level
**Primary Attributes:** Coordination (DEX) & Intelligence (INT)
**Resource:** **EP (Energy Points)**. You have 40 EP at Level 6. EP recovers on a long rest.

#### **Core Features:**
1. **The Three Masks of Deception**: You can switch your 'Active Mask' as a bonus action, changing your public mechanical footprint:
    *   **The Prodigal Sword**: +5 to Intimidation. Advantage on melee attacks. *Magic Lock:* You cannot cast spells or manifest magic while this mask is active.
    *   **The Reluctant Champion**: +5 to Persuasion/Deception. NPCs are less likely to target you (Target Priority lowers). *Pacifist Strain:* Must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom save to deal a killing blow.
    *   **The Joyful Predator**: Access to **Secret Arcanum** and **Nullification Field**. Critical range is 18-20. *Cost:* 1 Fatigue point per round. At 5 Fatigue, you have disadvantage on Deception for 24 hours.

2. **Secret Arcanum (Prodigy Power)**: You cast spells using EP. All spells are cast with the **Subtle Spell** property (no verbal or somatic components). No one can see you casting unless they have *True Seeing*.

3. **Nullification Field**: As an action, create a 30ft sphere of anti-magic for 1 minute. You can 'Fake Backlash' as a reaction to maintain the 'Null' deception.

4. **Veil of the Null**: You are permanently under the effect of *Nondetection*. You cannot be sensed by magical means.
