## Summary

Shadow Heart is a Baldur's Gate 3 continuation campaign where the player takes on the role of Shadowheart—the Chosen of Shar—after the events of BG3. The player chose the evil narrative path and now continues as a Level 12 custom Death Cleric with Extra Attack and Soul Harvest abilities. The campaign explores Shar's Ascent through a dark chronicle of necrotic power, soul manipulation, and the continuation of Shadowheart's unique theological journey.
