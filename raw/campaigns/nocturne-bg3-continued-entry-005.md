You reach into your pouch, your fingers brushing against the cold, minted iron of Avernus. You withdraw a single **Soul Coin**. It hums with a mournful, dissonant vibration, the trapped essence within screaming silently against its cage.

"The Absolute demands your death," you say, holding the coin up to the rain. It sizzles, steam rising in curling wisps. "But I deny the request."

You slam the Soul Coin against the **Whispering Idol** at your hip. 

*CRACK.*

The sound isn't physical; it's a psychic thunderclap that rolls across the deck. The coin dissolves into a stream of burning, crimson hellfire that spirals up your arm, mingling with the violet lightning of your **Omni-Attunement**. It hurts—a searing, exquisite agony that feels like liquid power flooding your veins—but you channel it instantly, forcing it outward.

You thrust your hand toward the convulsing True Soul Leader. **"INFERNAL SIPHON: STASIS."**

The red energy slams into him, not as a blast, but as a cage. Spectral chains of burning iron erupt from the deck, wrapping around his limbs and throat. More importantly, the energy sinks *into* him, seizing his nervous system. You can visualize the Absolute's kill-signal like a spreading black rot in his brainstem, and you watch as the infernal magic meets it, walls it off, and freezes it in place.

He gasps, a sound like tearing parchment, and then goes rigid. His eyes remain open, pupils dilated, but the black bile stops flowing from his lips. He is caught in a perfect, horrifying equilibrium between life and death—a living statue powered by a damned soul.

**Nyx** leans in, her tail thrashing with excitement as she waves a hand in front of his unblinking eyes. "By the Hells... Boss, you didn't just hold him. You *paused* him. His neural decay has completely halted. It's like you put his biology in a time loop."

**Gant** grunts, nudging the True Soul's boot with his own. "He's stopped twitching. Is he conscious?"

"He is whatever I need him to be," you reply, the crimson glow fading from your hand, leaving smoke drifting from your fingertips.

From the ridge above, **Silas** calls down, his voice cutting through the wind. "Visuals are... intense, Boss. If anyone was watching from the river, they definitely saw that. We need to move."

**Vesper** wipes the rain from her face, looking from the frozen True Soul to you with a mixture of fear and dark amusement. "So... we keeping him as a pet? Or are you going to crack open that head of his for the codes now that he can't die on us?"

You lean close to the frozen man's ear. "The cargo codes," you whisper. "Now."

Unable to resist, unable to die, the True Soul's mouth moves mechanically, bypassing his conscious will entirely. "Protocol... Omega... Nine... Black. Stasis... field... active."

You have the key.
