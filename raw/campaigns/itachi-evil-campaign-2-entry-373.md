Sasuke's EMS blazed. "Understood, Brother." With swift, ruthless precision, he moved between the three unconscious shinobi. He had the blueprint of their weaknesses, and the insidious mechanism of the dormant spiritual fragment that fed from them. This was the final piece, the essence of the ultimate rival.

"Sasuke," Itachi commanded, his voice now imbued with a profound gravity. "Guard me. Absolutely. My focus will be absolute. Nothing. Nothing will interrupt this."

Sasuke, his EMS blazing with unwavering devotion, snapped into a defensive stance, his gaze sweeping the desolate peaks, his chakra sensing every minute shift in the spectral wind. "As you command, Brother. I am your shield."

Itachi knelt before a section of the ancient vault where the arcane seals concentrated most heavily. His Rinnegan, though still hidden, pulsed with an internal, terrifying power. He activated the **Human Path**, but not to absorb a soul into himself. His **Medical Ninjutsu/Healing Arts** Expertise, combined with his Rinnegan's understanding of life and death, allowed him to swiftly manifest a small, translucent **chakra-infused spiritual vessel** from purified natural energy, forming it in his hand. It glowed faintly, humming with intricate sealing formulas only he could comprehend.

With his Rinnegan, Itachi then pierced the very essence of the arcane seals. He didn't attempt to break them directly. Instead, he subtly twisted the flow, reversing the insidious mechanism that fed Madara's spiritual fragment. With the **Human Path**, he began to draw the dormant fragment. A faint, almost imperceptible shimmer of dark energy, like a wisp of primordial shadow, began to detach from the seals and slowly, inexorably, flow towards the prepared vessel in Itachi's hand. It was a delicate, dangerous dance of wills, a silent tug-of-war with a cosmic entity's residual essence. Itachi's perfect concentration held firm.

The subtle transfer took agonizing minutes. The air around Itachi grew heavy, thick with the residual will of the legendary Uchiha. Sasuke, standing guard, felt an oppressive aura emanating from his brother, a pressure that even his EMS struggled to fully comprehend, yet he did not waver. Finally, with a soft, almost inaudible *click*, the wispy fragment fully coalesced within the spiritual vessel. Itachi instantly clapped his free hand over it, sealing it with a series of intricate, unbreakable seals, binding it absolutely within the shimmering orb. The vessel pulsed once, then settled into a dull, contained thrum. The ultimate rival's essence was now captured, a perfectly controlled variable.

Itachi rose, his Rinnegan (still cloaked) serene. The vault seals, now devoid of their insidious feeder, remained intact, their power diminished but still formidable. The ultimate acquisition was complete.
