The Dynamic Aptitude System
A character's core attributes are dynamic, evolving with age and experience based on innate potential. The five Aptitudes are: Physique, Coordination, Health, Intelligence, and Wisdom.
A. Aptitude Potential: Each character has an innate "Aptitude Potential" coefficient (rated 1-5) for each Aptitude, representing their natural talent.
B. The Yearly Progression Model: Attribute scores change fractionally each year.
Growth Years (26-35): All Aptitudes gain + (Potential / 10) points per year.
Plateau Years (36-45): Physical Aptitudes change by + ((3 - Potential) * 0.1). Intelligence changes by -0.1. Wisdom changes by +0.2.
Decline Years (46+): Physical Aptitudes change by + ((2 - Potential) * 0.2). Intelligence changes by -0.2. Wisdom changes by +0.1.
C. Special Growth: The "Lesson Learned" Check: Wisdom can also be actively developed by successfully making an Intelligence check to learn from significant life failures (CN 12-18 based on severity).
