Mechanical Clarification: Tiberius's Low-Magic Ability Suite

To align with your request for 'similar but weaker' Paladin abilities reskinned for the Spartacus setting, I have mapped your spellcasting resources to 'Commanding Shouts' and 'Tactical Manuevers'. You retain the utility of standard Paladin spells (like Bless, Shield of Faith, or Cure Wounds), but they are executed through mundane Roman discipline and leadership rather than divine magic. 

### Updated Ability List (Level 3)
- **Commanding Shouts (Spells):** You have 3 uses of 'Commanding Shouts' per short rest (replacing standard spell slots). 
    - **Inspirational Focus (Bless):** You shout orders to three allies. For 1 minute, they add 1d4 to attack rolls and saving throws.
    - **Defensive Formation (Shield of Faith):** You direct an ally to raise their guard. Their AC increases by +2 for 10 minutes.
    - **Lictor's Decree (Command):** You speak with the authority of the Crassus name. A target must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom save or obey a one-word command (Drop, Flee, Grovel).
    - **Strategic Assessment (Detect Magic):** You spend 10 minutes analyzing a scene to detect 'irregularities' (traps, hidden poisons, or tactical oddities).
- **Field Medicine (Lay on Hands):** You have a pool of 15 HP. As an action, you can apply legion-grade bandages and cauterization to restore HP or cure a poison/disease.
- **Ancestral Fury (Divine Smite):** When you hit with a melee attack, you can expend a 'Commanding Shout' use to deal an extra 2d8 Force/Psychic damage as you strike with cold, lethal precision.
- **Aura of Roman Discipline (Level 6 Preview):** Passive +3 bonus to AC and Saving Throws for you and allies within 10ft.

### Level 1-30 Progression Plan
- **Levels 1-20:** Standard D&D 5e Paladin progression, with 'Aura of Roman Discipline' replacing 'Aura of Protection'.
- **Levels 21-30 (Epic Tier):** 
    - **Level 21-25:** Proficiency increases to +7. HP increases by +40. Auras add +1 to attack rolls.
    - **Level 26-30:** DEX and CHA caps rise to 24. Smite damage dice increase to d10s. Your presence becomes so terrifying that your 'Command' shout can affect multiple targets simultaneously.

Your character data has been updated to reflect these 'Shouts' while maintaining the Paladin mechanical chassis.
