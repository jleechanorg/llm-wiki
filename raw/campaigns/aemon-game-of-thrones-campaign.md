## Summary

A dark, morally corrupt Game of Thrones D&D 5e campaign where Aegon Targaryen, a secret heir to the Iron Throne disguised as a sellsword, serves under Ser Gregor Clegane (The Mountain) in the Westerlands. The player character operates in a god mode with chaotic evil alignment, taking on evil missions including pillaging, raiding, enslavement, and assassination against the "good" characters of Westeros. The campaign mimics George R. Martin's writing style and aims to create plot hooks for encounters with most GOT characters starting in book one.

## Key Claims

- Aegon Targaryen carries Valyrian blood, secretly claiming the Iron Throne
- At age 18, Aegon is a Level 5 Ranger (Gloomstalker subclass) who thrives in darkness
- The Mountain's men care only for efficiency of slaughter and silence of the dead
- Aegon was made extremely beautiful and uses Wisdom-based persuasion
- The mercenary company accepts evil missions: pillaging, raiding, enslavement, assassination
- Campaign set in 298 AC (timeline of the main books)
