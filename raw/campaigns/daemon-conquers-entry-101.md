Daemon (Level 6, Age 30) considered Kael's words, his gaze hard. The notion of a prolonged siege, drawing unwanted attention, grated against his nature. The idea of subtle infiltration, however, resonated with a brutal satisfaction. "Option Three, then," Daemon rumbled, his voice decisive. "The Shadowed Approach. But first, the path. I will take three of our strongest warriors. They will be our eyes. They will find this hidden way."

He turned to the three Level 5 Great Weapon Master warriors who had recently returned from their initial scouting mission to Riverbend, their faces already grim from the last assignment. "You three. You know the terrain, you know the city's outer shell. Now you will find its soft underbelly. Seek out the forgotten tunnels, the neglected sewers, any weakness along the river or under the walls. Move like shadows. Be unseen. If a path exists, you will find it. If it does not, you will return, and we will reconsider."

The chosen warriors, veterans of Daemon's brutal campaigns and now hardened by their own newfound prowess, nodded with a grim understanding. The task was dangerous, requiring more cunning and stealth than their usual direct assaults, but Daemon's orders were absolute. They gathered minimal supplies, shed any unnecessary gear that might impede their clandestine movement, and slipped out of Oakhaven under the cover of early morning, their objective: the hidden secrets of Riverbend.

**Scouting Mission: Riverbend Hidden Paths**

The three Level 5 Great Weapon Master warriors spent the next three days meticulously probing Riverbend's perimeter. They crawled through damp undergrowth, navigated treacherous riverbanks, and silently observed the city's ancient foundations. Their keen eyes, sharpened by years of survival and recent experience, searched for any anomaly: an unusual grate, a suspiciously dry culvert leading into the earth, a faint, unused path along the water that might suggest an old smuggler's route.

Their efforts were exhaustive, silent, and dangerous, constantly skirting the edges of the Duke's patrols.

After nearly three days of relentless, covert searching, the lead scout, a wiry man named Joric (Level 5, Age 32), signaled a halt. He pointed to a section of the riverbank, partially obscured by dense reeds and ancient, crumbling stones. It smelled of stagnant water and old, forgotten secrets. It was a path.
