You cast **Pass Without Trace**. The shadows lengthen, wrapping **Minsc's** gleaming armor and **Kagain's** bulk in a shroud of silence. **Xan** clutches his moonblade, looking at the damp alley walls with disdain. "A sewer entrance. How fitting for our station."

**The Basement**

You drop into the cool, damp air of the storage cellar. The smell is wrong immediately. It doesn't smell of spices and silk; it smells of rotting meat and ozone. 

Your **Umbral Sight** cuts through the gloom. The basement is a labyrinth of crates and barrels. But in the center of the room, you see a disturbing tableau. Several men—merchants by their dress—are bound and gagged, thrown into a makeshift pen made of crates. Guarding them is a figure that looks exactly like one of the merchants, but its movements are wrong—fluid, alien, shifting.

It is a **Doppelganger**, currently in the shape of a human guard, pacing nervously. It stops, sniffing the air. It senses something, but it cannot see you through the veil of shadows.

"Food?" it hisses to itself, its voice a wet, gurgling approximation of human speech. "Or the Master?"

It does not see you. The *Pass Without Trace* spell holds. You are thirty feet away, hidden behind a stack of Amnish rugs. The creature is alone with the prisoners.
