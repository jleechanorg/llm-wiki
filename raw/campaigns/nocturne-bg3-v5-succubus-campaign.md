## Summary
A D&D 5e survival horror campaign set in Baldur's Gate 3 (Descent Edition). Nocturne Sosuke, a Level 1 Bard (College of Perdition), is de-powered from Level 6 after inverting the Cassalanter pact. Malcanthet (Queen of Succubi) replaces Zariel as her patron. The campaign begins at the Ravaged Beach after the Nautiloid crash, with the tadpole suppressing her infernal energy.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne's patron changed from Zariel to Malcanthet after inverting the Cassalanter pact
- Malcanthet's Favor (Plot Armor) -- instead of falling unconscious at 0 HP, becomes Incorporeal and drops to 1 HP
- Siren's Leverage trait: +2 to Charisma checks against humanoids sexually/romantically attracted to females
- College of the Abyssal Siren features Thrall mechanics, Soul Thief progression, and Pheromone Spike
- All BG3 companions appear: Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Karlach, Lae'zel, Shadowheart, Minthara
