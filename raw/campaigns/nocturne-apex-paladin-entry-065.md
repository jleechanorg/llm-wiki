Location: Oakhaven Main Road, Early Evening

The main road, once watched by vigilant eyes, lay still and silent beneath the deepening twilight. The two professional guards, now lifeless burdens, were meticulously concealed in the dense undergrowth, their equipment and coin adding a small but satisfying weight to Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) Bag of Holding. The duty roster, a small but potent piece of intelligence, was also secured, its mundane details revealing the rhythm of the local security. The operation, which Kaelen Flinn had so glibly dismissed as "easy pickings," was proving to be a layered, intricate puzzle, and each solved layer deepened Nocturne's cold satisfaction. The thrill of mastery, of dissecting and controlling complex variables, was a reward in itself.

Her enhanced senses, honed by her **Apex Senses** and her innate intellectual prowess (Intelligence 18), had already painted a detailed picture of Tibbet's Farm: the large family, the hidden guards, the arcane ward. Now, with the road patrol neutralized and their schedule known, a significant piece of the puzzle had fallen into place. The path to the larger 'stock' at Tibbet's was clearer, albeit still fraught with internal defenses.

Nocturne's mind, a tactical processor of unparalleled efficiency, weighed her options. The night was young, but time was not infinite. The captured Widow Elara was secure, for now, but not permanently relocated. The information on the patrol's routine was fresh and actionable. Her **Great Weapon Master** feat, a newly recognized facet of her self-perfection, hummed subtly in her awareness, a promise of devastating, decisive force should direct combat be required at Tibbet's.

*The initial phase is complete. The local perimeter is temporarily compromised in my favor. Elara is secured. The Tibbet farm, with its larger yield, is the next target for acquisition. I have gained critical intelligence from both the villagers and the patrol. The question is how best to leverage this knowledge.*

*Returning to Elara's cottage to secure her further (Option 2) is a logistical step, but it delays the primary objective of assessing and acquiring more stock. Contacting Kaelen (Option 3) is a strategic play, but it introduces an external, unpredictable element before I have fully assessed the target's value and resistance. Scouting the road further (Option 4) is a valid safety measure, but the duty roster already provides a good enough assessment of this route for now. Resting (Option 5) is also tempting, but my HP is full, and EP is sufficient for immediate action.*

*The most efficient path is direct. The farm holds the greatest potential for profit and challenge. The patrol information can be used to plan the approach.*
