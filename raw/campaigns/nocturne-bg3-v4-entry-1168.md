[Session 916: The Sovereign Overgod's Zenith]

Game Master:
The pearlescent void of Chronias, the Seventh Heaven, shudders as your Sovereign Hand closes around its very heart. Standing atop the newly manifested Sovereign Citadel of Chronias, you raise the dual staves Markoheshkir and the Staff of Spellpower, their indigo tracers flaring with a crystalline white heat that incinerates the last vestiges of celestial purity. The 108 total on your synchronization is Masterful; you do not merely announce your status—you rewrite the planar firmament to acknowledge it.

"The morning, the moon, and the light of the martyrs are but entries in my ledger," your multi-tonal Sovereign voice ripples through the Seven Heavens, the Hells, and the Abyss simultaneously. "I am the Queen of the Abyss, the Overlord of the Hells, the Mistress of the Sword Coast, and now, the Queen of Heaven. I am the Sovereign Overgod, and my Will is the only Law."

The remaining deities—Mystra, Bahamut, Oghma, and the rest of the Defensive Alliance—witness the terminal foreclosure of their sanctuary from their radiant bunkers. The morale of the celestial host shatters; their faith, once a backup currency, is systematically devalued as the Seven Heavens are re-indexed into your Sovereign Mint. The Thrall-Exarchs, your resurrected Solar Generals, descend upon the remaining pockets of resistance with the cold, administrative violet of your intent. Celestia is no longer a destination for the worthy; it is the industrial capital of your absolute will.

### Portfolios Annexed
- **Celestial Receivership (Chronias):** Total command over the Seventh Heaven; the conceptual void is now your private jurisdictional node.
- **Solar Receivership (Lathander):** Mastery over dawn, vitality, and renewal (re-indexed to birth-rates).
- **Lunar Receivership (Selûne):** Command over moonlight, navigation, and gates.
- **Gray Ledger Receivership (Kelemvor):** Total administrative command over soul-transit and the Fugue Plane.
- **Justice & Duty (Tyr/Torm):** Total administrative command over Law, Judgment, and Obligation.
- **Nessian Overlordship (Asmodeus):** Irrevocable authority over the Nine Hells.
- **Abyssal Sovereignty (Demon Lords):** Total domain over the liquidated Abyssal layers.

### New Titles & Status
- **Title:** Sovereign Overgod of the Sword Coast and the Outer Planes.
- **Title:** Queen of Heavens, Hells, and Abyss.
- **Rank:** Nascent Overgod (Terminal Rank).
- **Power: Absolute Jurisdictional Foreclosure:** You can instantly banish or unmake any entity of a lower rank within your annexed nodes (DC 104).
- **Power: Solar-Lunar Siphon:** Your attacks now deal an additional 20d10 Radiant and 20d10 Force damage (Internalized Portfolios).
