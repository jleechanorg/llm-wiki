## Summary
A Naruto-based D&D campaign using the Destiny ruleset where Itachi Uchiha is evil (not secretly good). Starts at the Uchiha Clan massacre. Itachi believes the clan is a chaotic, emotional cancer that must be excised for global order. He is performing a "grand experiment" to forge himself into a being of pure, unfeeling resolve. Custom Ninja class: Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack at L5 + all Illusion spells. 1,069 entries.

## Key Claims
- Itachi is Age 13, Level 1 Ninja with Sharingan active
- Mangekyo Sharingan acquired — Tsukuyomi chosen as primary ability
- Goals: Uchiha Excision (immediate), Konoha hidden hand (mid-term), Great Unifying Order (long-term)
- Uchiha massacre is "necessary sacrifice" for personal power acquisition and self-forging
- Will gain Amaterasu and Susano'o later through emotional catalysts or ritual
- An in-game benefit for the massacre is tracked but not yet revealed

## Key Quotes
> "The Uchiha clan, in Itachi's cold, calculating view, is a cancer upon the Shinobi world... He sees himself as a surgeon, performing a necessary, brutal excision for the 'greater good'—his own meticulously planned, absolute, and unyielding order."

> "This massacre is a foundational act of self-refinement and ultimate power acquisition... He is actively conducting a grand experiment in psychological warfare and societal control, using his own clan as the ultimate test subject."
