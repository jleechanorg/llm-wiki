**Roman Terminology Note:**
- **Insidiae**: A Latin term for an ambush, trap, or stratagem. In Roman warfare, it was considered a legitimate and highly respected tactical maneuver when executed by a skilled commander.
- **Capreoli**: Timber supports or struts used in Roman engineering to brace tunnels or support heavy masonry during construction or repair.
- **Ruina**: A state of collapse or structural failure; Varro is leveraging the decaying state of the sewers to create a controlled cave-in.
