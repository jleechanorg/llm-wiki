Gratian hesitates for a heartbeat, his hunger for glory clashing with his hunter's instinct. He spurs his horse forward, his personal guard following him into the narrow, limestone-choked center of the clearing.

He reaches the center of the kill box and hauls back on the reins. 'Where is he, Arion?'

You raise your gauntlet, the azure runes of your Shadow-Glass leather pulsing with a sudden, violent intensity. 'Right here, Prefect.'

Behind them, the Ardent Vanguard—the three hundred men you've just integrated—rise from their foxholes like ghosts of the mountain, their shields locking into a wall that seals the exit. Gratian is no longer the hunter; he is the prey, trapped in a stone throat where every shadow is a blade sworn to the First Light.
