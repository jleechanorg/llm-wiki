## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in the World of Assiah (Celestial Imperium). Sariel Arcanus, a Level 2 Wizard (INTP), is a 15-year-old daughter of Alexiel — the legendary "Mortal Queen" who was secretly a demigod with a Nullification Field. Alexiel died at the Battle of Sacrifice Fields saving Sariel and hundreds of children from a demonic ritual. Sariel carries crushing guilt over revealing her mother's moral code under torture. She discovers Mirror Magic might let her replicate her mother's Nullification Field by accessing her traumatic memories. Her goal: reach Sergeant Kaelan, the only witness to her mother's true power, in the heavily-guarded Quiet Ward.

## Key Claims
- Alexiel was not a true Null but a unique fusion: Null genetics + Lucifer's celestial essence = Nullification Field + magic use
- The "Joyful Predator" trait is a refined Empyrean Affliction — cold intellectual satisfaction in absolute control and unmaking
- Sariel's Mirror Magic could theoretically replicate the Nullification Field by accessing her traumatic memory of the Sacrifice Fields
- Attempting to access this memory caused psychic backlash (nosebleed, physical trembling, emotional overwhelm)
- The Silent Guard are incorruptible Apex Celestials; their weakness is rigid predictability and blind spots for subtle actions
- Sergeant Kaelan is held in the deepest section of the Quiet Ward, guarded by rotating Silent Guard pairs
- The Imperium is in crisis: Raziel, Artorius, Kyra Vian, and other leaders are all dead; Prince Titus is de facto leader
