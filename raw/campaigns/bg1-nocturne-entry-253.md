Dynaheir narrows her eyes but holds her tongue. The logic is sound, even if the morality is grey.

**The Ascent**

You lead the party up the narrow cellar stairs, the *Pass Without Trace* spell muffling the clatter of Kagain's plate and the heavy tread of the berserker. You emerge into the back hallway of the main trading floor.

Through the open archway, you see the main hall. It is quiet. Three "merchants"—Doppelgangers wearing the faces of the men you just left bound in the cellar—are standing near the counters, idly sorting through ledgers. They move with a predatory grace that no clerk possesses. They do not see you.

You slip past them, a shadow moving behind the pillars, guiding the pack up the grand staircase to the second floor.

**The Office**

The upper hallway is empty. You find the door marked *Proprietor*. It is locked, but **Roric** picks it in seconds. You slip inside and bolt it behind you.

The office is lavish, decorated with teak furniture and silk tapestries. On the north wall hangs a painting of a storm-tossed ship—*Umberlee's Mercy*.

You tear the painting from the wall. Behind it sits a heavy iron wall safe.

You insert the brass key you took from Jhampah's boot. The mechanism clicks smoothly. The heavy door swings open.

**The Hoard of the Seven Suns**

*   **Trade Bars:** Heavy ingots of Amnish gold, stamped with the seal of the coster. Value: **1,000 gp**.
*   **Gemstones:** A velvet bag containing loose rubies and pearls. Value: **800 gp**.
*   **Wand of Lightning Bolts:** A rod of crackling electrum. (6 Charges).
*   **Ledger:** A black book detailing payments to "The Iron Throne" and "Sarevok Anchev." It confirms the takeover was a hostile acquisition to destabilize the city's economy.

"Rich," Roric whispers, scooping the gold bars into the Bag of Holding. "We just robbed the victims *and* the villains."

Suddenly, heavy footsteps echo in the hallway outside. A voice—sounding exactly like Jhampah's—calls out. "Who is in there? I heard the door!"

The doppelganger proprietor is outside. The doorknob rattles.
