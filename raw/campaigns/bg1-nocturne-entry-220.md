"Strip it," you command. "Everything."

The *Hunger* drives you to consume the resources of the fallen. You direct the looting with a cold, hierarchical precision. You are the Alpha; you eat first. Then the wolves. Then the sheep.

**The Gold:**

**The Gear:**
You lift the **Bracers of Defense**. They are bands of polished mithral etched with shielding runes. You toss them to **Roric**.
"Lighter than leather," you note. "Speed is your armor. Wear them."
The rogue grins, swapping his studded leather for the bracers. He flexes his arms, moving faster, lighter. (AC Set to 14 + Dex).

Next, the **Robe of the Evil Archmagi**. You hold up the black fabric; it hums with a necrotic, malevolent power. **Xan** reaches for it, but recoils, his hand smoking slightly. "Dark weave," the elf hisses. "It rejects me. And the witch."
"Then it is currency," you say, stuffing the valuable robe into the bag. It will fetch a king's ransom in Baldur's Gate.

**The Scrolls:**
You sort through the parchment. You hand the **Scroll of Web** to **Xan**. "Control," you state. "Keep them still so I can kill them."
The offensive scrolls—**Fireball** and **Lightning Bolt**—you toss carelessly to **Dynaheir**. "Tools for the backline. Try not to hit us."

**The Sanctum:**
You turn the room upside down. Kagain smashes open a display case to find two **Potions of Healing** and a **Potion of Master Thievery**. You keep the thievery potion and one healing draft; Kagain gets the other healing potion for his tanking duties. Minsc gets nothing but the satisfaction of victory.

The room is stripped bare. The letters from Sarevok are in your pocket. The mine is decapitated.
