### **Nocturne's Class: Oath of Self-Perfection (The Apex Predator)**

This archetype represents a warrior of supreme will and intellect who has no fealty to external powers, but rather to their own inherent potential and the brutal philosophy of self-maximization. Their "oath" is to themselves – to become the ultimate, most efficient, and most powerful being possible. Their abilities manifest as raw, focused power, unnatural resilience, and a chilling intellect that grants them superiority over all others.

**Core Aptitude for Extraordinary Abilities (EP):** Intelligence (EP Pool = Intelligence Aptitude Score x 2)

**Key Features (Level 5):**

*   **Fighting Style (Battle-Hardened):** You gain a +1 bonus to your Defense score while wearing armor.
    *   *Rationale:* Represents self-optimization for resilience and combat durability.
*   **Extra Attack:** When you take the Attack action on your turn, you can attack twice instead of once.
    *   *Rationale:* A fundamental martial mastery, essential for an Apex Predator.
*   **Perfected Strike (Replaces Divine Smite):** When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend Energy Points (EP) to deal extra force damage to the target, in addition to the weapon's damage. This force damage represents the sheer, focused will behind your attack. The extra damage is **2d8 for 5 EP**, plus **1d8 for each additional 2 EP spent**, to a maximum of 5d8.
    *   *Rationale:* Focuses on pure, destructive force channeled through personal will, rather than divine energy.
*   **Aura of Unyielding Dominance (Replaces Aura of Protection, gained at L5):** You emanate an aura of pure, focused will within a **10-foot radius**. You (and friendly creatures within range) gain **Advantage on checks made to resist being Frightened or Charmed**. Additionally, enemies within range have **Disadvantage on checks to resist your Intimidation attempts**.
    *   *Rationale:* Emphasizes psychological control, mental fortitude, and a commanding presence that dominates lesser wills.
*   **Apex Senses (Replaces Divine Sense):** You can use your action to focus your honed intellect and perception, gaining enhanced awareness of your surroundings. Until the end of your next turn, you gain **Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) and Intelligence (Investigation) checks**, and you can pinpoint the location of any hidden or invisible creature within 30 feet of you that is not behind total cover. You can use this a number of times equal to **1 + your Intelligence Modifier per Long Rest**.
    *   *Rationale:* Reflects intellectual and perceptive prowess, allowing for superior information gathering and threat assessment.
*   **Willpower Infusion (Adapted from Lay on Hands):** You can touch a creature and channel your sheer willpower to mend or empower. You have a pool of energy equal to **five times your character level** (25 points at L5). This pool replenishes on a Long Rest.
    *   You can restore 5 Hit Points (HP) for each 1 point drawn from this pool to a creature you touch.
    *   Alternatively, you can spend **5 points** from this pool to remove one level of Fatigue from yourself or a creature you touch.
    *   As a Primary Action, you can spend **10 points** from this pool to grant yourself or a creature you touch a **+1 bonus to one Aptitude score of your choice for 1 minute** (cannot exceed Aptitude Score 20 from this effect).
    *   *Rationale:* A versatile resource pool reflecting self-optimization and the ability to push limits and bolster allies, rather than divine healing.
*   **Channel Divinity (Aspects of the Apex Predator):** You gain two Channel Divinity options. When you use your Channel Divinity, you choose which option to use. You must then finish a Short or Long Rest to use your Channel Divinity again.
    *   **Flawless Opening:** As a Secondary Action, you gain a flash of tactical insight. Your next weapon attack roll or Intelligence-based Resolution Check against a creature you can see this turn has **Advantage**.
    *   **Unchecked Ambition:** As a Primary Action, you temporarily ignore all penalties from Fatigue or the Frightened condition for 1 minute. When this effect ends, you immediately gain **1 level of Fatigue**.
    *   *Rationale:* These reflect specific tactical insights and the capacity to push oneself beyond normal limits, even at a cost.
