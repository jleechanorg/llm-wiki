**A. Change Description: Comprehensive Rinnegan Acquisition Benefits (Spell Save DC Revision)**

*   **Rationale:** To define the full, monumental benefits of Itachi's successful Rinnegan awakening, integrating his Uchiha/Senju lineage, Level 20 power, and research breakthroughs. This clarifies the mechanical effects of the ultimate ocular power, transforming him into a cosmic-tier entity with truly divine aptitudes and irresistible techniques.
*   **Location:** This new mechanic will be integrated into Itachi's character manifest under a dedicated "Rinnegan" section, alongside his other Level 20 features.

**B. Proposed New Mechanic: Rinnegan Acquisition Benefits (Revised Spell Save DCs)**

Upon successful awakening of the Rinnegan, Itachi Uchiha gains the following monumental benefits, reflecting his unmatched mastery and the harmonious fusion of Uchiha-Senju lineage:

1.  **Ocular Transformation & Universal Perception:** Itachi's eyes permanently transform into the Rinnegan. This eye is always active and provides the following passive benefits:
    *   **True Sight (Cosmic Level):** Itachi gains permanent, unblockable True Sight out to 120 feet. This allows him to see through all forms of darkness, perceive invisible creatures and objects, automatically detect and see through all visual illusions and genjutsu (even those of a cosmic nature), and perceive the true form of shapeshifters or creatures hidden by any magic or ability.
    *   **Universal Sensory Perception (Expanded):** Itachi gains an innate, omnipresent understanding of chakra flow across the world. He can instantly perceive chakra signatures, emotions, and pinpoint locations within a **100-mile radius**, and can focus his perception to perceive the flow of nature energy and subtle changes in the world's natural balance with absolute clarity. He can even perceive chakra through barriers or long distances with precision.
    *   **All-Range Vision (Tactical Omni-Perception):** His vision is no longer impeded by smoke, fog, or similar non-physical obscuration, and he gains a chillingly precise understanding of tactical battlefield layouts, predicting movements and weaknesses with supernatural accuracy.

2.  **Core Aptitude Enhancement (Divine Elevation):** The cosmic power of the Rinnegan profoundly and permanently elevates Itachi's fundamental capabilities.
    *   **Physique:** Increases by **+4** (from 15 to **19**, +4 Modifier).
    *   **Coordination:** Increases by **+4** (from 19 to **23**, +6 Modifier).
    *   **Health:** Increases by **+4** (from 17 to **21**, +5 Modifier).
    *   **Intelligence:** Increases by **+4** (from 26 to **30**, +10 Modifier).
    *   **Wisdom:** Increases by **+4** (from 21 to **25**, +7 Modifier).

3.  **EP Pool & Regeneration (Cosmic Wellspring):**
    *   **Maximum EP:** Itachi's Max EP permanently increases by **+50**. (His current Max EP based on INT 30 (new) is 60. So, 60 + 50 = **110 Max EP**).
    *   **Chakra Siphon (Passive - Amplified):** The siphon now generates **+25 EP every 24 hours (game-time)** (instead of +15 EP).
    *   **Senju Life Force (Passive Regeneration - Perfected Cosmic Flow):** Itachi now passively regains **20 EP per hour** (instead of 5 EP/hour), even when not resting. This is a constant, almost infinite wellspring of chakra, reflecting his ultimate connection to natural energy.

4.  **Chakra Control & Jutsu Costs (Absolute Cosmic Efficiency):**
    *   **All Jutsu Cost Reduction:** The EP cost reduction for Itachi's Illusion spells (from Chakra System Optimization) is now increased: **all of Itachi's jutsu (Ninjutsu, Genjutsu, Taijutsu abilities with EP cost) now cost 5 less EP to activate (minimum 0 EP, replacing the previous -3 EP for Illusions only).** This reflects absolute chakra mastery over all forms of energy.
    *   **Spell Save DC Enhancement (REVISED):** All of Itachi's Spell Save DCs (for all his Jutsu, including Tsukuyomi, Weird, etc.) permanently increase by an **additional +4**. This is a direct enhancement to the potency of *all* his techniques. (This is a total +7 DC for his original abilities, +6 for inherited Sasuke abilities from EMS, and +4 for all other Rinnegan/Ninjutsu abilities where it is applicable, on top of normal calculation).

5.  **New Active Abilities (The Six Paths & Outer Path - Full Manifestation):** Itachi gains access to the fundamental powers of the Rinnegan. Each of these represents a Primary Action (2 CP) unless stated otherwise, with EP costs reflecting their immense power. All are considered Ninjutsu.

    *   **Deva Path (Shinra Tensei / Bansho Ten'in):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 10-50 EP (scales with power/area).
        *   **Effect:** Manipulate gravitational forces. Can repel or attract objects/individuals (Bansho Ten'in - up to DC 30 Strength Save to resist for targets). At higher EP cost, can create widespread destructive forces (Shinra Tensei - up to city-block level devastation with DC 25 Constitution Save or take 10d10 Force damage) or miniature celestial bodies for sealing (Chibaku Tensei - immense EP cost, can trap targets in a small moon-like construct with DC 30 Strength Save to avoid).
    *   **Asura Path (Mechanized Form & Weaponry):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 10 EP.
        *   **Effect:** Transforms part or all of Itachi's body into mechanical weaponry. Grants immense physical strength (+8 to Physique for attacks/checks), allows for firing homing missiles (4d6 explosive damage, can fire up to 3 as Secondary Action), or forming additional limbs/blades. Lasts 1 minute, or until dismissed.
    *   **Human Path (Soul Absorption & Mind Reading):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 15 EP.
        *   **Effect:** Place hand on a creature's head. Target makes **DC 30 Wisdom saving throw**. On a fail, Itachi can instantly read their entire mind, extracting all knowledge (memories, secrets, skills) in a single moment. On a critical fail, Itachi can extract their soul, instantly killing them and absorbing their remaining chakra (gain 20 EP).
    *   **Animal Path (Summoning - Mastered):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 5-30 EP (scales with summoning's power/number).
        *   **Effect:** Summon various powerful, often monstrous, creatures that share Rinnegan eyes (e.g., giant multi-headed dogs, chameleons, birds, rhinoceroses). Summoned creatures act immediately on Itachi's turn. Can summon up to 3 creatures at once with higher EP cost.
    *   **Preta Path (Chakra Absorption - Universal):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 0 EP.
        *   **Effect:** Absorb any chakra-based attack (ninjutsu or chakra-enhanced taijutsu) targeting Itachi or an ally within 30 feet, nullifying its effects and restoring EP equal to half the absorbed spell's EP cost (or a flat 5-10 EP for taijutsu). Can also drain chakra directly from a touched target (drain 2d10 EP per round of contact).
    *   **Naraka Path (King of Hell / Restoration & Interrogation):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), Cost: 15 EP (for restoration/interrogation) or 5 EP (for judgment/damage).
        *   **Effect:** Summon the King of Hell. Can restore damaged bodies, heal grievous wounds, or perfectly restore lost eyes (including non-Sharingan eyes, but cannot regenerate lost Mangekyō/Rinnegan). Can also be used for absolute interrogation (forcing a target to speak the truth via judgment, dealing 8d8 psychic damage to liars or those who refuse to speak, DC 25 Wisdom save for half damage).
    *   **Outer Path (Life & Death Manipulation / Cosmic Chains):**
        *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP) or more, Cost: Varies immensely (minimum 30 EP for basic effects like chakra receivers, up to 100+ EP for resurrection).
        *   **Effect:** The most powerful of the Rinnegan abilities. Can manifest chakra receivers/chains to control chakra, suppress abilities, or bind powerful targets (DC 30 Strength Save to resist). Can connect to the Gedo Mazo (if available). Can even grant life/resurrect the dead (at immense EP cost and with severe consequences for the user, potentially requiring a life sacrifice from the user itself, at GM discretion for specifics).
        *   **Limbo: Border Jail (Advanced Outer Path - Requires Future Mastery):** This specific ability (invisible, untraceable clones of immense power) is so advanced that it remains a future unlock, requiring specific narrative milestones, further mastery of the Rinnegan's true depth, and potentially a connection to the true Madara's residual chakra.

6.  **Senju Integration - Active Manifestation Potential:** Itachi's existing Senju Integration benefits ("Nature Manipulation," "Medical Ninjutsu/Healing Arts," "Biological Adaptation") are now primed for active, conscious development into specific active techniques (e.g., Wood Release, Hashirama-level healing, advanced Sage Mode) through dedicated training or specific future catalysts. The Rinnegan provides the ultimate understanding to unlock these with significantly reduced effort.

This is a comprehensive set of benefits, transforming Itachi into a true cosmic force, poised to reshape the world.
