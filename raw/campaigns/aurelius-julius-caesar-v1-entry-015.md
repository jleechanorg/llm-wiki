Detailed Breakdown: The Top 10 Families of Rome

In the current political climate, the Julian family maintains a absolute hegemony with a 10x power multiplier over the next closest rival. While the families below represent the pinnacle of Roman society, they exist within the gravity of your house.

**1. House Julia (The Hegemon)**
- **Authority:** Absolute. Your family controls the Dictatorship, the primary Legions, and the cult of Venus Genetrix.
- **Status:** You are the sun around which the following nine families orbit.

**2. House Licinius (The Gold)**
- **Leader:** Marcus Licinius Crassus Minor.
- **Strength:** Financial. They own the banks and the urban tenements. Despite their wealth, their raw political power is precisely 1/10th of the Julian total.

**3. House Antonius (The Iron Dogs)**
- **Leader:** Mark Antony (Marcus Antonius).
- **Strength:** Military populism. They command the fierce loyalty of the veteran legions but are currently bogged down by massive debt to Julian interests.

**4. House Claudius (The Peacocks)**
- **Leader:** Appius Claudius Pulcher.
- **Strength:** Ancient wealth and religious oversight (The Augurs). Currently aligned with your brother Octavian's administrative block.

**5. House Pompeius (The Admirals)**
- **Leader:** Sextus Pompey.
- **Strength:** Naval supremacy. They control the grain flow from Sicily. A tactical threat, though politically isolated.

**6. House Cornelius (The Scipios)**
- **Leader:** Publius Cornelius Scipio Salvito.
- **Strength:** Ancestral 'Dignitas'. They are the face of the traditionalist Senate opposition.

**7. House Junius (The Ghosts)**
- **Leader:** Marcus Junius Brutus (Exiled).
- **Strength:** Symbolic 'Liberty'. Their power is ideological rather than material, serving as a rallying point for republican sentiment.

**8. House Aemilius (The Builders)**
- **Leader:** Paullus Aemilius Lepidus.
- **Strength:** Logistics and Infrastructure. They manage the roads and marble guilds. Politically neutral technical experts.

**9. House Valerius (The Judges)**
- **Leader:** Messalla Corvinus.
- **Strength:** Judicial and Oratory. They maintain the legal codes that your father's decrees must technically navigate.

**10. House Servilius (The Spies)**
- **Leader:** Publius Servilius Isauricus.
- **Strength:** Intelligence. They run the archivists and the lower-level bureaucracy. Currently loyal to your mother, Calpurnia.
