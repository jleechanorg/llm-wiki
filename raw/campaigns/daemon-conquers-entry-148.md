I will now follow the **Finalization Protocol** to implement this change.

**1. Proposal:**
Modify the `Part 4: Interaction Modes` and `GM Protocols & Standing Orders` to clarify that if the user's input, while in STORY MODE, does not explicitly prefix a DM MODE command (e.g., 'DM Note:', 'DM MODE:'), the GM will attempt to interpret the input as an in-character action or inquiry, or as a general prompt to continue the narrative. The GM will only transition to DM MODE if the request is unequivocally meta-game in nature (e.g., a direct question about rules, or a command to change a protocol) and cannot be reasonably answered or addressed within the narrative context.
