**Timestamp:** 2026 DR, Mirtul 15, 08:15:00 | **Location:** The Sacred Pool Threshold | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

The stone path leading down to the Sacred Pool is slick with moisture, the air growing heavy with the cloying scent of wild lilies and the oppressive vibration of the Rite. Nocturne moves with a deceptive frailty, her hand resting lightly on Astarion's arm as if for support, though her grip is as steady as the marble it mimics. Shadowheart and Lae'zel follow a few paces behind, their presence acting as a dark escort that frames her 'vulnerability' with a sharp, lethal edge.

Two druid guards stand at the threshold of the stone stairs, their staves crossed. They look ready to bark a command, but the words die in their throats as Nocturne stumbles forward. Her violet eyes are wide, glassy with a simulated terror that reflects the flickering green braziers of the pool.

**"Please,"** she whispers, the Abyssal harmonic in her voice making the very air seem to shiver. **"The shadows... they're coming. I need... I need to see the one in charge."**

The guards exchange a glance, their resolve melting before the uncanny perfection of her ruin. They pull their staves back, allowing her to pass into the inner sanctum.
