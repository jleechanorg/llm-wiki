But even gifts could be poisoned if handled clumsily. A direct charge, however satisfying, could be prolonged, messy, and prolonged meant exposure. You did not wish to attract the entire Watch to your nascent empire, not when Lord Xykon and Agent Morwen were diligently unraveling the Order of the Silent Mind and your Crimson Guard was growing.

How to ensure swift, undeniable control? Your core strengths were stealth, surprise, and overwhelming initial damage. Your *Assassinate* feature, combined with *Dread Ambusher* and *Extra Attack*, promised a first-turn massacre against a surprised foe. That changed everything.
