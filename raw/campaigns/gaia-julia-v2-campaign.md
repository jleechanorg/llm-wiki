## Summary

The Gaia Julia V2 campaign is set in 82 BCE Roman Republic / Capua. The player character, Gaia Julia Caesar ("Gaia of the Gens Vibia"), is a Level 12 Imperator -- a gestalt of Paladin (Conquest) and Sorcerer (Divine Soul). Lawful Evil, she is a noble/spy who commands 100,000 gold and 20 elite disguised guards ("The Twenty"). The "Golden Republic (Pax Julia Alternate)" setting has the Julian family dominating 70% of Rome via the "70% Rule." Low-magic gritty realism with political manipulation as the core mechanic.
