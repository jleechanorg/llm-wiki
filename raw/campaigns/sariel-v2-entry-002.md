***

### **1. Major Conflict: The Timeline**

*   **The Issue:** There is a significant contradiction regarding when the campaign begins relative to Alexiel's death.
    *   **Assiah Lore Document:** States that Alexiel died in Year 1580 and the "Present Day" of the campaign is Year 1620, placing a **40-year gap** between her death and the start of our story.
    *   **Campaign Start (`SEQ_ID: 1 & 2`):** Explicitly states the premise is "The day after her legendary mother sacrificed herself," setting the campaign in the immediate, raw aftermath of the tragedy. It also uses a "Year 11 of the New Peace" calendar, which is inconsistent with the lore's Imperial Calendar.

*   **Proposed Resolution:**
    I recommend we adopt the **Campaign Start's timeline**. The premise of playing Sariel in the immediate aftermath of her mother's death, dealing with the fresh grief and the raw accusations of her brothers, is far more narratively compelling and provides stronger immediate motivation.
    *   **Action:** We will officially retcon the lore document's 40-year time gap. Our campaign begins on **Kythorn, Day 2, Year 11 of the New Peace**, which is the day after the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields. The children's ages (Sariel 19, Twins 20) as established in our campaign start will be considered canon.

    **Please confirm: Do you agree to use the immediate "day after" timeline from our campaign start?**

### **2. Minor Discrepancies & Clarifications**

*   **Sariel's Starting Level:**
    *   **The Issue:** The lore document lists Sariel's starting character sheet at Level 2, while our campaign began with her at Level 1.
    *   **Proposed Resolution:** I suggest we continue with her as Level 1 at the start of the game. This provides a more satisfying character progression arc. The XP you were just awarded for your discoveries has correctly advanced you to Level 2, so we are now aligned with the lore's intended power level for her at this stage. No further action is needed if you agree.

*   **Rowan's Last Name:**
    *   **The Issue:** The NPC data in our campaign start lists your companion as "Rowan Thorne." The character dossier in the main lore document lists his name simply as "Rowan."
    *   **Proposed Resolution:** This is a minor detail. Do you have a preference? We can either keep "Thorne" as his surname or remove it to align with the dossier.

*   **Commander Kyra Vian's Status:**
    *   **The Issue:** The detailed battle narrative of the "Starfall Gambit" (the final battle against Lucifer) states that Commander Kyra Vian died in a mutual kill with the Apostate Samael. However, the political summary of the current era states that after Alexiel's death, the Imperium is led by a triumvirate including a still-living Kyra Vian.
    *   **Proposed Resolution:** We need to decide which is canon. Is Kyra Vian alive and part of the Imperial leadership, or did she die heroically in the final battle? Her being alive adds another powerful political player to the world.

***

Please review these points and provide your decisions. Once we have a unified canon, we can proceed with the story with full confidence and consistency.
