Itachi Uchiha (Level 6 Ninja, Age 13) maintained his high vantage point, his gaze cold and calculating. The Konoha investigation into the Uchiha compound was proceeding precisely as he had orchestrated, the fabricated narrative taking root. The subtle genjutsu had worked; Danzo and the elders were already accepting the convenient lie of an internal clan implosion.

*Did I make a deal with Danzo? If not, should I?* Itachi's mind, a flawless instrument of strategic recall, accessed the recent past. Yes, a deal had been struck. A cold, pragmatic arrangement, initiated by the Konoha leadership (spearheaded by Danzo, with the Third Hokage's reluctant acquiescence) to contain the Uchiha threat by ordering their extermination. In exchange, Sasuke's life was guaranteed. Itachi had accepted, not out of loyalty to Konoha, but because this pact provided the perfect cover for his "grand experiment"—the purging of the clan and the forging of Sasuke into his ultimate tool. Danzo, in his own obsession with Konoha's dark underbelly, was merely a convenient pawn, a shadow to hide Itachi's true, deeper manipulations.

The question was not *if* to deal with Danzo, but *how* to best leverage this existing, implicit alliance for his own, transcendent goals. Danzo's Root organization, his network of spies and hidden operatives, possessed a certain utility for Itachi's mid-term goal of Konoha's Hidden Hand.
