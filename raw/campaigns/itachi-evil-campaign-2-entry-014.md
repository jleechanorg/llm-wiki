Location: Uchiha Police Force Headquarters, Uchiha Compound, Konohagakure, Night

Having flawlessly dispatched Captain Ryota and secured his Sharingan, Itachi Uchiha (Level 1 Ninja, Age 13) felt a cold satisfaction. The initial phase of his "grand experiment"—the systematic eradication of the weaker, unsuspecting Uchiha—was nearing completion. Now, within the Uchiha Police Force Headquarters, he faced a more concentrated, organized resistance. Yet, their predictable patterns and emotional vulnerabilities were merely further variables for his superior intellect to dissect.

The hum of Itachi's Sharingan intensified as he analyzed the chakra signatures radiating from the upper floors. Two more high-ranking jōnin, clearly alerted by the faint disturbance of Ryota's sudden silence, were on their way down, their movements swift and purposeful. They were cautious, but their caution was steeped in arrogance, a belief that no one could possibly infiltrate *their* fortress. A fatal flaw, Itachi mused.

He melted deeper into the shadows, Ryota's body now a mere obstacle on the floor. From his hip, "The Silent Edge" Chokutō slipped into his hand, its presence a comforting weight, its blade a promise of surgical precision. His presence was a void, his very being a testament to his ruthlessness. He would isolate these two, as well, before they could raise a wider alarm. This was the precise, surgical strike he preferred, ensuring minimum chaos and maximum efficiency for his ultimate ascension.

He listened, the sounds of their approaching footsteps growing louder, distinct voices now audible, though muffled. "Ryota should have checked in by now," one muttered, a hint of unease in his tone. "Perhaps he's just... tired." The other scoffed, but Itachi sensed a subtle shift in their chakra, a tightening of their guard. They were skilled, but their arrogance and complacency were their undoing.

Itachi positioned himself, a shadow within shadows, just around a corner where the staircase opened into the main hall. He would let the first one pass, then strike, relying on the speed and precision of "The Silent Edge" and his mastery of silence.

The first jōnin, a grim-faced man named Kaito, rounded the corner, his eyes scanning the empty hall, a hand hovering near his own kunai pouch. He walked past Ryota's unmoving form without truly seeing it, his attention drawn to a flickering gas lamp further down the corridor. In that instant, Itachi moved.

"The Silent Edge" flashed, a blur of dark steel. The +1 bonus for attacking an unaware target was applied. The blade found the precise, vital point in Kaito's neck, a clean, silent cut that severed his life force before a sound could escape his lips. Kaito crumpled, dead before he hit the floor. Itachi, with the cold efficiency of his "grand experiment," immediately secured Kaito's Sharingan eyes, adding them to his growing collection.

He turned his attention to the second jōnin, who was now nearing the corner, calling out Kaito's name with growing urgency.
