## Summary
A D&D 5e survival horror campaign set in Baldur's Gate 3 (Descent Edition). Nocturne Sosuke, a Level 1 Bard (College of Perdition), is de-powered from Level 6 after a failed infernal ritual. The campaign begins at the Ravaged Beach after the Nautiloid crash, with the tadpole suppressing her immense infernal energy. Malcanthet (Queen of Succubi) has replaced Zariel as her patron.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne's patron changed from Zariel to Malcanthet after inverting the Cassalanter pact
- Malcanthet's Favor (Plot Armor) — instead of falling unconscious at 0 HP, becomes Incorporeal and drops to 1 HP
- Siren's Leverage trait: +2 to Charisma checks against humanoids sexually attracted to females
- College of the Abyssal Siren features Thrall mechanics, Soul Thief progression, and Pheromone Spike
- All BG3 companions appear: Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Karlach, Lae'zel, Shadowheart, Minthara
