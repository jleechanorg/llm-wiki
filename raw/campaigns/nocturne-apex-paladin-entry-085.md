Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Farmhouse Interior, Early Evening

The farmhouse main room, moments ago filled with the warmth of domesticity, was now utterly silent, frozen in a tableau of pure terror. Man Tibbet, his wife, and their three children stared at Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16), their eyes wide, their bodies rigid. Her presence, a silent, dark silhouette against the flickering hearth, emanated a chilling aura of absolute, unyielding dominance. They were not merely afraid; they were paralyzed, utterly subdued by her will. All professional threats outside had been systematically eliminated; now, only these unsuspecting, vulnerable lives remained, the primary 'stock' for her lucrative contract.

Her EP was critically low (6/36), and her Channel Divinity was expended, but her HP remained full. These were not warriors or mages; they were simple farmers, their fear a far more potent weapon than any rusty tool. Her mind, a relentless engine of strategic thought, processed the scene with cold, precise efficiency. The objective was to secure them, swiftly and cleanly, maintaining the silence that was her hallmark.
