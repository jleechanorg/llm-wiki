The air was still, thick with the scent of damp earth and the subtle, metallic tang of fresh blood. With the immediate tactical situation firmly under her control, Nocturne's meticulous nature demanded a swift collection of any valuable intelligence or resources from the fallen guard. Now, with all external and internal professional threats neutralized, the path to the "large family" was clear.
