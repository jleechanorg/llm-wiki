For two relentless weeks, Silverport’s Mayor’s Estate became a nexus of brutal training and desperate hope. By day, Daemon (Level 8, Age 30) drove his 90 Level 4 recruits and the ~50 Level 3 Blackwood Guards through grueling drills, their collective grunts a testament to his formidable Physique and relentless will. Joric (Level 7, Age 32) and Theron (Level 7, Age 28) tirelessly enforced his commands, their own formidable prowess a constant example. Borin (Level 7, Age 30), Gareth (Level 7, Age 29), and Ragnar (Level 7, Age 33) continued their duties, ensuring the vast machinery of Daemon's empire churned efficiently.

In the midst of this, the summoned experts arrived. Mina (Age 60), the stern midwife, and Master Elms (Age 75), the sharp-eyed scholar, were brought before Daemon. They assessed Elara (Age 19) with a careful, meticulous detachment, asking questions and performing examinations. Daemon, in turn, continued his efforts with Elara, and now, with the newly arrived Seraphina (Age 20), the robust fisherman's daughter. Seraphina, terrified yet seemingly resilient, offered no resistance to her grim purpose.

Mina and Master Elms, after their examinations, delivered a cautious report. "My Lord," Master Elms began, his voice dry, "the 'curse' on the Blackwood line appears to be less a magical affliction and more a deeply ingrained, subtle physical predisposition. A weakness, perhaps, passed through generations, affecting the very seed of life." Mina added, "Elara, though healthy, carries some faint echoes of this. Seraphina, however, shows no such sign. Her vitality is undeniable, and while we cannot guarantee, her chances appear far more… promising." They suggested specific tonics, dietary changes, and practices for both women, methods to bolster fertility.

No definitive pregnancy was yet announced, but a flicker of a path forward had been illuminated. The empire still awaited its heir, but hope, cold and pragmatic, now stirred.
