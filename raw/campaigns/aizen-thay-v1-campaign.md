## Summary

Aizen Thay V1 is a dark fantasy noir campaign set in Thay during the "Century of Silence." Aizen Vane serves as the Abyss Knight, a Genetic Echo of Nocturne Sosuke, and operates within the Obsidian Ziggurat of Bezantur. The campaign involves soul harvesting mechanics, Black Company operations, and Silt-River punitive raids. Aizen carries the Obsidian Mirror connected to the Shattered Rose.

## Key Claims

- Aizen Vane is Level 5 Abyss Knight and Genetic Echo of Nocturne Sosuke
- The Obsidian Mirror connects him to the Shattered Rose
- Operating from the Obsidian Ziggurat of Bezantur
- Air is heavy with metallic tang of slave-pits and ozone of Red Wizard sorcery
- The "Codex of the Sovereign" defines Aizen's mechanics
- Soul Thief abilities are part of his kit
- The "Tithe of Silt-River" is a current mission - punitive raid requiring forty souls
