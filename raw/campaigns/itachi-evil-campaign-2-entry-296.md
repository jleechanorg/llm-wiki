The stage was set. The path to the Rinnegan was clearer than ever. He was ready.

*How can I get EMS without Sasuke?* Itachi's mind, a flawless instrument of strategic analysis, immediately accessed the vast, chilling knowledge he had meticulously acquired, particularly from his recent breakthrough research into all Sharingan. He knew Sasuke was the traditional path, the "perfect vessel," and that EMS conventionally required the eyes of a close blood relative. However, his absolute understanding of ocular powers had revealed alternative, more complex, and potentially more controlled pathways.

**Options for Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan (EMS) Acquisition (without Sasuke):**

1.  **Artificial EMS Integration (Synthesized Ocular Power) [CHOICE_ID: ArtificialEMS_1]:**

This is a direct, controlled, and independent method, requiring no reliance on Sasuke or other Uchiha. It aligns with his desire for absolute control over his destiny.
    *   **Cons:** Extremely complex and dangerous. Even with his unparalleled intellect, directly engineering an EMS is unprecedented and carries inherent, monumental risks of catastrophic failure, blindness, or irreversible chakra imbalance. Consumes immense time and resources (collected Sharingan).
    *   **Confidence:** High. Conceptually sound based on his knowledge, but the practical execution is immensely challenging.

2.  **Rinnegan First, then EMS Derivation (Transcendence-Driven Perfection) [CHOICE_ID: RinneganThenEMS_2]:**
    *   **Pros:** Prioritizes the acquisition of the Rinnegan (now that he has the complete blueprint and integrated Senju cells). His research indicated that the Rinnegan grants understanding of creation and destruction, potentially allowing him to then "perfect" his own Mangekyō into an EMS *through Rinnegan's power*, bypassing the traditional biological EMS requirement entirely. This is a path of ultimate transcendence.
    *   **Cons:** Assumes Rinnegan provides this capability, which is a theoretical leap. Delays EMS, and thus perfect Mangekyō stability, until after the even riskier Rinnegan acquisition.
    *   **Confidence:** Moderate-High. Intellectually appealing, but relies on a further theoretical power manifestation.

3.  **Exploit Surviving Uchiha (Remote Harvesting) [CHOICE_ID: ExploitSurvivingUchiha_3]:**
    *   **Pros:** Utilizes his autonomous agents to discreetly identify and monitor surviving Uchiha beyond Konoha (whom his agents are already monitoring). If any are found to possess Mangekyō or develop it, he could theoretically harvest their eyes for EMS or research, avoiding direct engagement with Sasuke.
    *   **Cons:** Highly unreliable; surviving Uchiha with Mangekyō are exceedingly rare. Risks drawing unwanted attention or direct confrontation with powerful, unknown individuals.
    *   **Confidence:** Low. A contingency, but far from an efficient primary path.

4.  **Continue Sasuke's Forging (Traditional Path Optimization) [CHOICE_ID: ContinueSasukeForging_4]:**
    *   **Pros:** Acknowledges Sasuke as the most direct, albeit emotionally complex, path to EMS. Continues and intensifies the subtle psychological manipulation via `Dream` to accelerate Sasuke's Mangekyō development, ensuring he reaches the necessary emotional state for EMS. While not "without Sasuke," it optimizes the original plan.
    *   **Cons:** Relies on Sasuke's progression, which is outside direct control and time-consuming. Entails maintaining the facade and long-term deception.
    *   **Confidence:** Very High. The most established and controllable path for EMS, despite the emotional baggage.

My cold intellect demands the most efficient path to consolidating power and moving into the next phase of Konoha's Hidden Hand. Every action must serve my grand design.
