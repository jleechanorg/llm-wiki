## Summary
A fantasy campaign set in "Assiah," Year of New Peace. Sariel Arcanus is a Level 1 Wizard (possibly Leanhaun?) aged 15-19, known as the "Quiet Prodigy" and Keeper of Secret. The campaign explores coming-of-age at House Arcanus magical academy, with hidden identity themes.

## Key Claims
- House Arcanus is the primary magical institution
- Zenith Spire serves as the central tower
- Aeterna represents the larger world
- DM mode vs Story mode creates meta-narrative

## Key Quotes
