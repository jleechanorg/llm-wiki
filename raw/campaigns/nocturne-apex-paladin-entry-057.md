The secured form of Widow Elara lay silent on the worn floor, a canvas sack obscuring her head, her muffled breathing the only sound apart from the soft crackle of the hearth. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood over her captive, the newly acquired journal and locket tucked securely into her Bag of Holding. The efficiency of the capture was satisfying, a precise cut in a complex operation. Yet, a calculating thought began to form in her mind, turning over the 'stock' she had just secured.

**Nocturne's Internal Monologue & Planning:**

*One individual. Elderly. Frail. The contract specified 'peasants' and hinted at a 'premium for fresh stock' for the Underdark buyers. What is the value of this particular acquisition?*

*The buyers are 'particular' and 'from below, deep.' Their interest is likely in labor, or perhaps a more esoteric purpose that requires a certain 'freshness' or vitality. An elderly woman, while easily subdued, might not command the highest price. Her physical utility would be limited. *Kaelen implied a 'premium,' which means a higher-than-average price per head. If the standard 'peasant' rate is, say, 50 gold, a 'premium' might double that. But for an elderly woman, the premium might be negligible, or even a discount on the base rate. She is 'stock,' yes, but perhaps not 'prime stock.' This single acquisition, while clean, will not significantly move the needle on my wealth accumulation. It is merely a proof of concept, a test of the method.*

*This suggests the need for more. Man Tibbet's farm, with its 'large family,' would offer a greater quantity, though with increased risk. The efficiency of this single capture is high, but the overall yield for the contract needs to be considered. The integrity of the 'stock' for the buyers is also a factor; a quick, clean capture minimizes damage and stress, which might be part of their 'particular' requirements.*

*The journal is an unknown variable. Its precise script suggests a life beyond mere farming. Could she be more than she appears? Perhaps a hidden value, an expertise, or a secret that would make her worth more than her physical form implies? That is a secondary concern for now, but a compelling one.*

*My immediate priority is to ensure the successful completion of this phase of the contract. Secure the captured asset, then reassess the quantity needed and the most efficient path to acquire it. The road patrol is still a factor to consider for any larger-scale operation.*

***

Nocturne's focus returned to the present. The individual, now a silent, bound bundle, was secure. The initial acquisition was complete. The question of her individual value was secondary to the overall success of the operation. Now, it was time to move the asset and ensure the chain of control remained unbroken.

The cottage was quiet, save for the muffled breaths of the captive and the soft crackle of the hearth. The night was still young, and there was more work to be done.
