Daemon (Level 5, Age 30) had no time for complacency. Oakhaven was conquered, its wealth stripped, its people bent to his will, and his castellans were now drilling new recruits. But there were other villages, other resources, ripe for the taking in the surrounding lands. He would leave Kael to the dull work of administration and training, while he personally oversaw the expansion of his direct influence. He needed more gold, more proof of his undeniable power, and he needed to break more wills.

For the next five days, the eastern lands echoed with screams and the glint of steel. Daemon led his three chosen warriors and a rotating, terrified contingent of twenty Basic Recruits from Oakhaven on a relentless campaign. Village after village was fallen upon, plundered with brutal efficiency. Daemon moved like a force of nature, his greatsword a bloody blur, personally executing any who dared to resist or show cowardice among his ranks. The 'recruits' were driven forward, their terror used as a shield and a tool to overwhelm the few, desperate defenders of these settlements. Gold, supplies, and any valuable items were ruthlessly confiscated, leaving behind only ashes and despair. Each raid was a short, sharp shock, consolidating his control through fear.

By the end of this brutal five-day spree, the immediate region around Oakhaven had been thoroughly bled dry.
