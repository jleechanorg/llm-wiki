Crixus stops in the center of the clearing, raising a hand for silence. The chatter dies instantly, leaving only the crackle of the campfires and the distant, thin whistle of the wind. "Look at this man!" Crixus bellows, his voice echoing off the cavern walls like a thunderclap. "You see a Roman noble? You see a boy of sixteen? You are fools! Spartacus steps beside you, his eyes scanning the horizon where Glaber’s fires still smolder. "He did not just risk his neck for a slave," the Thracian states firmly. "He shamed an Empire to save a soul. From this day, the noble walks among us not as a hostage, but as a brother of the Spires."

Aurelia stands at your side, her face radiant with a devotion that has shed its last remnants of mortal doubt. She doesn't speak, but her amber eyes reflect the flickering firelight with a quiet, fierce pride that claims you more surely than any legion's oath.
