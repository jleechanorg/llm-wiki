**The Long Rest**

"We're not done," you reply, staring into the fire. The *Hunger* is quiet, but you can feel the edges of it fraying. The *Satiated* clarity is fading. You have hours, maybe a day, before the itch returns.

**Mirtul 16, Morning.**

You gather the pack in the courtyard as the sun burns off the morning mist. The road to **Baldur's Gate** stretches north, a ribbon of mud and danger. You have the Mayor's letter, the Iron Throne's documents, and a small army at your back.

"To the city!" Minsc cheers, brandishing his sword at a passing seagull. "Evil hides behind walls, but walls cannot stop justice! Or hamsters!"

"The blockade waits," Xan sighs. "Guards, bribes, and inevitable bureaucracy. I can hardly wait."
