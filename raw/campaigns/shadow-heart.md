## Summary

A D&D 5e campaign following Shadowheart after the events of Baldur's Gate 3. The player took evil narrative decisions and is a Chosen of Shar. Starting at Level 12 with the best BG3 gear. Custom Death Cleric class with Extra Attack and special Shar-granted powers. Three plot arcs offered: Consummation of Shadow, Extinction of False Light, and difficult path to godhood.
