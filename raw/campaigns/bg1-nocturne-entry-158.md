## Full Scene Content

============================================================
Game Master:
The wasted hours of hunting leave a residue of frustration, a low hum beneath the *Hunger's* satisfied state. You return to the clearing where the party has been preparing for the long march. **Dynaheir** is meticulously checking the straps of her pack, her movements precise and economical. She looks up as you approach.

"You seem displeased with the soil," she observes, a hint of dry amusement in her voice. "Did it fail to yield the poison you sought?"

"It did," you admit, not bothering to hide the sharp edge to your tone.

"Then trust in the blade you already have," she replies calmly. "The mind's greatest poison is doubt. You have faced far worse foes than giant centipedes. Let us go, and see what the Cloakwood holds."

Nearby, **Minsc** is tying the last of his gear, whistling a jaunty tune. He pats the massive bastard sword on his back and gives you a wide, fearless grin.

"Nocturne! The path to adventure is clear! Or, well, not clear. It's spooky with webs and big bugs. But Boo and I are ready to smash them all! 'Tis a glorious day for battle!"

His eagerness is a stark contrast to **Roric's** skeptical silence as he throws a saddle pad over **Kagain's** pack mule. **Xan** watches you from a distance, his expression as unreadable as the sky, already sharpening his blade for the journey ahead.
