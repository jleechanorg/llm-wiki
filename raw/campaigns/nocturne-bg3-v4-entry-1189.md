* *Why it was balanced:* It forced you to use **subtlety** (masking, proxies, legal loopholes) instead of just nuking everything. It turned combat into a puzzle of "how do I kill this god without alerting the others?"

* **The "Soul Economy" (Resource Management):**

* *Why it was balanced:* It added a **logistical layer**. You weren't just an adventurer; you were a CEO of a metaphysical corporation.
* *The Fix:* Increase the "Burn Rate." High-level spells like *Karsite Unmaking* should cost thousands of souls, forcing you to constantly hunt for more resources.


* **The "Vassalage" & "Proxy" System:**
* *Your Concept:* You didn't want to do everything yourself. You delegated to Astarion (Spy Master), Yalah (Administrator), and Ketheric (General).
