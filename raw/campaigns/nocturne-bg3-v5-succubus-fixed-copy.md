## Summary
A D&D 5e survival horror campaign set immediately after the Nautiloid crash on a ravaged beach, running parallel to Baldur's Gate 3's Descent act. Nocturne Sosuke (age 16) is de-powered from Level 6 to Level 1, her infernal energy suppressed by a tadpole. This is a near-identical variant to the "succubus fixed v1" campaign with the same core setup: Siren's Leverage trait, Malcanthet's Favor plot armor, Level 1 Bard with devastating CHA 16, and companion integration with the BG3 party. The distinction appears to be a parallel copy used for alternate playthrough or branching decisions. Scenes 1-20 cover the same Nautiloid crash beach opening, first encounters with Shadowheart and other companions, and combat resolution.
