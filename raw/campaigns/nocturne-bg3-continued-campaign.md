## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign featuring Nocturne "The Shadow Monarch," a Level 8 Bard (College of Swords) with Neutral Evil alignment. Set on the deck of a captured Moonrise Ship at Wyrm's Tooth during the post-Elturel Fall period. Features unique mechanics including The Hunger (sated provides +2 to all stats) and Omni-Attunement (5 items attuned, limit broken).

## Key Claims
- Character uses CHA 24 (God-Mode) with Whispering Idol to override Tadpole control
- Operation Scalpel was successful; True Soul Leader captured and mind-controlled
- Empire assets: Shadow Bank (Manager Vessic), House Umbrage (Regent Lyra Umbrage), subverted Law (Vice-Marshal Rokas)
- Black Lotus squad (Vesper, Gant, Silas, Nyx) serves as primary forces
