**Background (Estranged Scion Adapted):**
Sariel comes from immense old money, a family of quiet influence and dark secrets. Her past crime has estranged her, but the family name still opens doors, even as she rejects their world. Her probation and work with the police leverage both her intellect and her family's hidden connections.

**Backstory:**
Sariel's early life was one of extreme privilege and isolation, her prodigious intellect setting her apart from both her peers and her emotionally distant parents. At the tender age of twelve, a chillingly precise organizational genius blossomed into a horrific act: a meticulously planned mass murder that claimed over a thousand lives. The motivation remains a labyrinth of psychological complexity, a blend of a nascent dark impulse, a detached fascination with control, and perhaps a twisted experiment in human behavior.

Due to her age and the sheer, unfathomable nature of the crime, combined with her family's unspoken influence, her sentence was mitigated. Now fifteen, she carries the burden of that event—a cold, calculated regret born not necessarily of remorse for the lives taken, but for the disruption to her own carefully constructed world and the subsequent loss of freedom. Her probation offers a perverse form of atonement and a new outlet for her intellect: hunting other predators, a task she approaches with the same detached, analytical precision that once led her down a darker path. Her family, horrified by her actions but still unwilling to fully disown their scion, maintains a distant, watchful presence, their wealth a gilded cage and a hidden resource.

This 'Arcane Operative' class perfectly encapsulates Sariel's unique blend of cunning, intelligence, and a hidden connection to subtle, almost magical, influence in a modern world. It allows her to excel in investigation, social manipulation, and direct action, while providing a framework for her genius to manifest as powerful, adaptable abilities.
