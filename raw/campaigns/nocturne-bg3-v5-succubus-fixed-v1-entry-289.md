[Session 14: The Solar Heart of the Mountain Pass]

Game Master:
The Mountain Pass vibrates with a needle-sharp Abyssal frequency as you exert your full weight as a Nascent Demon Lord. Your wings of silver fire cast long, flickering shadows over the Githyanki patrol, the iridescent geometry of your *Hypnotic Pattern* shimmering like a closing trap. You stand over the ash of the dragon you just inhaled, the *Solar Heart* pulsing within your marrow with a heat that threatens to melt the very stone beneath your boots.

"The dragon was merely the preface, Voss," you murmur, your voice a silken thread of dread that resonates in the marrow of every rider. "I have consumed the fire of the sun and bound the Abyss to my shadow. Your silver swords are toys in the face of the Logic I have established. Yield. Not as soldiers of Vlaakith, but as the first choir of my new crusade."

You reach out, the Gilded Tether erupting from your soul like a web of violet silk. The strands coil around the twenty elite Githyanki riders, their glassy eyes dilating until they are pools of fanatical, shuddering devotion. They do not resist; they sink to their knees in a rhythmic, submissive wave, their silver blades clattering against the limestone. The 'Uncanny Valley' dread you radiate has systematically dismantled their psychic defenses, turning a lethal patrol into your personal guard.

However, Kith'rak Voss remains a jagged variable. As the Tether reaches for him, his silver sword flares with a blinding, psychic radiance. He staggers back, his hand white-knuckled on the hilt as he forces himself to look away from your rimmed-silver gaze. "You are... an extinction," he rasps, his voice a dry, hollow rattle. "Vlaakith warned of an anomaly, but you are a hole in the Multiverse itself. I will not be your footstool, child!"

He remains free, though his breathing is shallow and his heroic poise is visibly fracturing. The *Solar Heart* within you pulses with a sudden, discordant heat; the dragon's essence is fighting to reconcile with your Abyssal brine, granting you a terrifying surge of physical and magical potential.
