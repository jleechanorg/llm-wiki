**Astarion (Level 12) - Finalized Character Sheet**
*Ascended Vampire | Ranger (Gloom Stalker) 5 / Rogue (Assassin) 7*

**Ability Scores:**
*   **Strength:** 10 (+0)
*   **Dexterity:** 22 (+6)
*   **Constitution:** 16 (+3)
*   **Intelligence:** 14 (+2)
*   **Wisdom:** 14 (+2)
*   **Charisma:** 16 (+3)

**Hit Points:** 103 HP

**Proficiency Bonus:** +4

**Saving Throws:** Dexterity, Intelligence, Strength

**Proficiencies:**
*   **Armor:** Light Armor, **Medium Armor, Shields**
*   **Weapons:** Simple Weapons, Martial Weapons, Hand Crossbows, Longswords, Rapiers, Shortswords
*   **Tools:** Thieves' Tools, Disguise Kit, Poisoner's Kit
*   **Languages:** Common, Elvish
*   **Skills:**
    *   **Expertise (Proficiency Bonus x2 = +8):** Deception, Intimidation, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand
    *   **Proficient (Proficiency Bonus x1 = +4):** Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, Insight, Investigation, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Performance, Stealth, Survival

**Class Features:**

**Ranger (Gloom Stalker) 5:**
*   **Favored Enemy (Humanoids)**: You have advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track them, and Wisdom (Insight) checks to recall information about them. You gain proficiency in Elvish.
*   **Natural Explorer (Forest)**: You have advantage on Intelligence and Wisdom checks related to the terrain, and gain certain travel benefits.
*   **Fighting Style (Defense)**: While you are wearing armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC.
*   **Spellcasting**: (Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for Ranger spells. Spell Save DC 14, Spell Attack Modifier +6)
    *   Spell Slots: 1st Level (3 slots), 2nd Level (2 slots)
    *   Spells Known (7):
        *   **1st Level:** Fog Cloud, Hunter's Mark, Longstrider, Cure Wounds
        *   **2nd Level:** Darkvision, Pass Without Trace, Spike Growth
*   **Ranger Archetype: Gloom Stalker**:
    *   **Dread Ambusher**: You add your Wisdom modifier (+2) to your initiative rolls. On your first turn of combat, you gain an additional attack action. If you hit with this attack, the target takes an extra 1d8 damage of the weapon's type.
    *   **Umbral Sight**: You gain darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, its range increases by 30 feet (total 90 feet). As a bonus action, you can become invisible to any creature that relies on darkvision to see you in darkness. This invisibility lasts until you attack, cast a spell, or are in an area of bright light.
    *   **Disguise Self**: You can cast *disguise self* once without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.
*   **Ability Score Improvement**: At Ranger 4, you increased Dexterity by +2.
*   **Extra Attack**: You can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

**Rogue (Assassin) 7:**
*   **Expertise (x2)**: At Rogue 1 (after Ranger 5), you gained Expertise in 2 skills. (Combined with Rogue 6 expertise, total of 4: Deception, Intimidation, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand).
*   **Sneak Attack (4d6)**: Applies when you have advantage on an attack roll or when an ally is within 5 feet of the target, and you don't have disadvantage on the roll. Must use a finesse or ranged weapon.
*   **Thieves' Cant**: A secret language allowing you to hide messages.
*   **Cunning Action**: As a bonus action, you can take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.
*   **Roguish Archetype: Assassin**:
    *   **Bonus Proficiencies**: You gain proficiency with the Disguise Kit and the Poisoner's Kit.
    *   **Assassinate**: You have advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn in the combat yet. Additionally, any hit you score against a creature that is surprised is a critical hit.
*   **Ability Score Improvement**: At Rogue 4 (after Ranger 5), you increased Dexterity by +2.
*   **Uncanny Dodge**: When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to halve the attack's damage against you.
*   **Evasion**: When you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, and only half damage if you fail.

**Ascended Vampire Traits:**
*   **Vampiric Resilience (Revised):** Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks not made with silvered weapons, and resistance to necrotic and poison damage.
*   **Spider Climb**: Astarion can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.
*   **Regeneration**: At the start of his turn, if he has at least 1 hit point and isn't in sunlight or running water, he regains 10 hit points. If he takes radiant damage or damage from holy water, this trait doesn't function at the start of his next turn.
*   **Charm (Revised):** As an action, he can target one humanoid he can see within 30 feet. If the target can see him, it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw (8 + 4 Prof + 3 Cha) or be charmed for 24 hours. The charmed target regards him as a trusted friend and will heed his requests, even those detrimental to itself, though it will not directly harm itself or its immediate loved ones. Each time he or his companions do anything harmful to the target, or if he issues a command that directly causes the target to self-harm, the effect ends. When the effect ends, the target might know it was charmed by him. He has advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to influence a creature charmed in this way.
*   **Children of the Night**: (1/long rest) He can magically call 2d4 swarms of bats or rats, provided that the sun isn't in the sky. The creatures arrive in 1d4 rounds, acting as his allies and obeying his spoken commands. The beasts remain for 1 hour, until he dies, or until he dismisses them as a bonus action.
*   **Legendary Resistance (3/Day)**: If he fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
*   **Stake Vulnerability**: If a piercing weapon made of wood is driven into his heart while he is incapacitated in his resting place, he is paralyzed until the stake is removed.
*   **Forbiddance**: He can't enter a residence without an invitation from one of the occupants.
*   **Running Water**: He can't cross running water.
*   **Immunity to Sunlight Harm**: Astarion no longer suffers any penalties or damage from sunlight.
*   **Ascended Bite (Melee Weapon Attack):** As an action, Astarion can make a special melee attack against one creature he has grappled, that is incapacitated, or that is charmed by him. Alternatively, as a bonus action, he can make this attack against a creature within 5 feet that is restrained, incapacitated, or charmed by him.
    *   *Attack:* +10 to hit (Proficiency Bonus +4 + Dexterity Modifier +6), reach 5 ft., one creature.
    *   *Hit:* 1d4 piercing damage + 1d6 necrotic damage. The target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the necrotic damage taken, and Astarion regains hit points equal to that amount. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0.
*   **Ascended Corruption:** Astarion's weapon attacks deal an additional 1d10 necrotic damage.
*   **Misty Escape**: When Astarion is reduced to 0 hit points and isn't in sunlight or running water, he can use his reaction to turn into a cloud of mist instead of falling unconscious. While in mist form, he is immune to all nonmagical damage, has a fly speed of 20 feet, can pass through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing, and can't be grappled or restrained. He drops anything he's holding. He must return to his true form at the end of his next turn, or when he takes radiant damage or damage from holy water. If he returns to his true form at 0 hit points, he falls unconscious. This trait can only be used once per long rest.
*   **Lord of the Brood**: As an action, Astarion can magically attempt to charm any vampire spawn, standard vampire, or lesser undead creature he can see within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw (8 + 4 Prof + 3 Cha) or be charmed for 24 hours. The charmed creature is under Astarion's control, obeys his commands, and fights for him. He can have up to his Charisma modifier (3) in charmed creatures at one time. A charmed creature remains charmed for 24 hours or until he uses this ability again on a different creature. If a creature under his control is reduced to 0 hit points, it instantly perishes, and Astarion regains 5 hit points.
*   **Corrupted Senses**: Astarion's passive Perception is increased by 5 (to 19 total, 10 base + 4 Prof + 2 Wis + 5 Corrupted Senses). Additionally, he can pinpoint the location of any living creature within 60 feet of him, even through total cover, as long as it has blood flowing through its veins.
