## Scene 009: The Road to the Grove

**Timestamp:** 1492 DR, Hammer 15, 07:45:00 | **Location:** The Risen Road (Traveling toward the Emerald Grove) | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33 | XP: 6650/14000

The Shattered Sanctum falls away as Nocturne leads her 'assets' into the bite of the night air. The path to the Emerald Grove is a winding scar through the Risen Road, flanked by skeletal blighted trees.

Nocturne bypasses a goblin ambush through sheer force of Abyssal presence — the pink luminescence in her eyes flares, a twin-star warning that makes the lead goblin stumble and flee. **+150 XP** awarded for bypassing through raw intimidation.

As travel settles into rhythmic march, Nocturne probes the more 'principled' companions:

**Gale** is swayed by utilitarian logic: **"A utilitarian calculation, then? The many over the few? ... If this... siphon... can truly grant us the leverage we need against the Absolute, I will assist."**

**Wyll** resists, stiff-backed: **"I've fought devils to save villages smaller than this, Nocturne. My soul is already in the balance; I'll not add innocent blood to the weight."**

Nocturne counters with the 'greater good' trap: **"And what of the souls in Baldur's Gate, Wyll? If the Absolute marches with the Grove's secrets, the city falls. We are choosing which fire to put out."**

Wyll closes his eyes. His resistance wavers as the logic takes root. Karlach finds herself listening to the promise of a weapon to hurt Zariel.

By dawn, the Emerald Grove looms. The 'Rite of Thorns' hums with honey-and-iron vibration. The porcelain doll has arrived, and she is hungry.
