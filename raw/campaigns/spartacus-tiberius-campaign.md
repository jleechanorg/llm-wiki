## Summary

Spartacus Tiberius is a low-magic historical D&D 5e campaign inspired by the Spartacus TV series (specifically War of the Damned). The player controls Tiberius, the ambitious and ruthless son of Marcus Crassus, entangled in the brutal world of Roman politics, gladiatorial arenas, and Spartacus' slave rebellion. The campaign emphasizes gritty realism, political intrigue, and intense combat without high fantasy elements.

## Key Details

- **Player Character:** Tiberius (son of Marcus Crassus)
- **Starting Level:** 3
- **Setting:** Republican Rome, 72-71 BC
- **Tone:** Gritty historical, political intrigue, brutal combat
- **Campaign Status:** Active (79 scenes, character creation phase)
- **Magic Level:** Low-magic (modified spell-less mechanics)

## Player Character

**Tiberius** is a young Roman noble with a taste for cruelty, skilled in swordplay, deception, and command. He is a Modified Paladin (reskinned as "Legion's Vindicator") sworn to Rome's glory via ancestral oaths. In this low-magic Roman epic, divine spells are stripped—no Fireball or Cure Wounds, pure grit.

**Approved Build (Level 3, Human Variant, Noble Background):**
- STR 16 (+3), DEX 12 (+1), CON 13 (+1), INT 10, WIS 8 (-1), CHA 15 (+2)
- AC 18 (Lorica segmentata + shield)
- HP 28
- Fighting Style: Dueling (+2 damage with gladius/shield)
- Divine Smite: 2d8 radiant "ancestral fury" (3 uses/short rest)
- Lay on Hands: 15 HP pool (field medicine)
- Oath of Conquest (reskinned "Oath of Rome"): Conquering Presence, Guided Strike
- Equipment: Lorica segmentata, Gladius, Shield, Javelins x4, 250gp, healing potions

## Modified Paladin: Legion's Vindicator

The campaign uses a modified Paladin class stripped of standard divine magic:

- **Smite = Arena Finishers:** Resource-based burst damage on melee hits (no spell slots)
- **Lay on Hands = Field Medicine:** Legion training applied as healing/treatment
- **Auras/Oaths = Commanding Presence:** Terror tactics and inspiring allies
- **Channel Divinity:** Conquering Presence (frighten foes) and Guided Strike (+10 attack)
