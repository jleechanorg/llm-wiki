**Mechanical Explanation: Haste and Spellcasting**

No, you cannot cast two spells in one turn using the extra action provided by the *Haste* spell. Per the D&D 5e SRD (System Reference Document) rules for *Haste*:

> "That action can be used only to take the **Attack** (one weapon attack only), **Dash**, **Disengage**, **Hide**, or **Use an Object** action."

Because the **'Cast a Spell'** action is not included in this specific list of allowed actions, the extra action cannot be used for magic. This is why in Sequence 1334, your attempt to cast a second *Hold Person* was interpreted as a Sling attack instead. 

To cast two full spells in a single turn, you would typically need the Fighter's *Action Surge* feature. If you use a Bonus Action to cast a spell, you are also restricted to casting only a Cantrip with your main Action that turn.
