The faint light of dawn filtered through the grimy windows of the disused weaver's shop, casting long shadows across Nocturne’s (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) face. Inside her meticulously secured hideout, the silence was profound, broken only by the distant sounds of Baldur's Gate stirring to life. The ciphered journal lay open on her desk, its chilling contents now fully revealed. House Durin's Folly, ostensibly a rival in commerce, was in fact a nexus of ancient death magic, Shadowfell incursions, and a looming "Convergence" orchestrated by "The Architect" in the Underdark. This was not a minor skirmish; this was a war for the very fabric of the city's power, perhaps even its existence.

A cold, grim satisfaction settled over Nocturne. Kaelen's paltry contract had opened a door to a game of far grander scale, one worthy of her intellect. The peasants, now mere cargo in the Shadow Weavers' distant hands, faded into irrelevance. The true currency was the intelligence she had plundered. This was her path back, not as a penitent exile, but as an indispensable asset.

*   **1. How to present to father?**
    *   **Direct audience:** Fastest, most impactful. Risky if Julian intercepts or if father still sees me as a liability rather than an asset. But he knows my ruthlessness is efficient.
    *   **Indirect approach (via Lysandra):** Safer, but slower. Lysandra understands arcana and family loyalty. She might be a conduit to father, vouching for the intel's veracity, and subtly undermining Julian's position. But it risks diluting my personal impact.
    *   **Assessment:** A direct, controlled presentation to father is optimal. The urgency of the "Convergence" dictates speed. I am an Apex Predator; I do not rely on proxies for such critical plays. Julian will attempt to obstruct, but my father will appreciate the directness of a crisis brought to his attention by the one who *solved* it.

*   **2. What is the sequence of evidence presentation?**
    *   Start with the undeniable: **Deed of Tibbet Farm, Ledger** (payments for 'Security Contingency', 'Arcane Oversight' linked to Durin's Folly). Establishes the 'outpost' aspect, a breach of trust.
    *   Follow with the tools: **Orbs of Scrying**. Proves surveillance, an act of aggression.
    *   Then, the profound, chilling truth: **Shadowfell Maps, Darkwood Casket, Obsidian Shard, Ciphered Journal with Cipher Key**. This reveals the full, terrifying scope of their activities – death magic, extraplanar dealings, a major threat.
    *   Culminate with the urgency: The **Underdark Rendezvous details** and the **"Convergence" in three nights** with "The Architect." This transforms abstract intel into an immediate, looming crisis.

*   **3. What is the precise demand?**

    *   A formal acknowledgment of my unique capabilities.
    *   **Assignment to a leadership role** in addressing this threat, granting me resources and authority outside of Julian's command structure. This ensures my continued indispensability and a clear path to consolidating power.
    *   Perhaps a direct request for House Valerius's resources (agents, funds, magical support) to investigate/intervene in the Underdark rendezvous.
