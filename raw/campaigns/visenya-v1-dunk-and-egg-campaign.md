## Summary

Visenya V1 is a D&D 5e campaign set in a reimagined version of George R.R. Martin's Dunk and Egg novellas. The player controls Visenya—a young girl secretly bearing Targaryen royal blood—serving as a squire to the hedge knight Ser Duncan the Tall. The world has been adapted to allow women as squires, and the player requested a female Aegon renamed Visenya. The campaign is a Dragon Scholar class built around high INT/WIS with full spellcaster progression.
