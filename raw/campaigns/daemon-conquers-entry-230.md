The newly plundered throne room of Oakhaven's Reach, still thick with the scent of blood and fear, found Daemon (Level 10, Age 30) seated upon its crude throne. His formidable Physique (18.5) radiated an aura of unyielding power. The conquest of Valoria, swift and absolute, filled him with a cold satisfaction. And in Silverport, the thin, frail cry of his newborn son, Valerius, echoed in his mind – a testament to his will, a lineage secured.

Kael (Level 9, Age 25), his Castellan of Valoria, stood before him, his gaze sweeping over the conquered halls with the calculating eye of a strategist. Joric (Level 8, Age 32), Gareth (Level 9, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 9, Age 33), and Theron (Level 9, Age 28), his elite vanguard, stood like grim statues at the room's perimeter, their greatswords silent but deadly.

"Kael," Daemon rumbled, his voice a low, commanding growl that filled the silence. "Valoria is ours. The boy is born. The empire grows. What next? How do we best secure this new acquisition, and where does my blade fall to expand my dominion further?"

Kael inclined his head, his cold eyes gleaming with strategic ambition. "My Lord, the Duchy of Valoria is a vast prize. Its immediate consolidation is paramount. Its fertile lands can feed your entire army, and its populace can swell your ranks beyond anything we've yet seen. Before we look outward too far, we must sink our roots deep here."

Kael pointed to the vast Valorian lands on the map. "My Lord, I propose a dual approach. First, an intense, brutal program to **fully integrate Valoria's populace and resources**. This means a systematic census, immediate taxation, and forcing their young men into rigorous military training under your direct oversight. We break them completely, and remake them into loyal subjects and formidable soldiers. This will be a grinding, month-long process, but it will transform Valoria into an unshakeable pillar of your empire. Its daily gold contribution will rise, and its manpower will become limitless."

"Concurrently, My Lord, our scouts have new intelligence. To the southeast lies the **Republic of Aethelgarde**. A loose confederation of wealthy merchant cities and independent artisan guilds, relying on internal squabbles for defense, rather than unified might. It boasts immense wealth, and its diverse population could yield a rich harvest of unique skills and resources. It would require a coordinated military campaign, perhaps even leveraging our nascent spy network to sow discord among its squabbling leaders, making its defeat swift and profitable."

Kael paused, his gaze meeting Daemon's. "Valoria needs to be truly claimed, My Lord. And Aethelgarde offers the promise of vast new wealth and skilled labor, ripe for the taking once Valoria is secured."

Daemon listened, a grim satisfaction in his eyes. The dual approach resonated with his pragmatic brutality. The simultaneous integration of Valoria and the preparation for Aethelgarde would not only secure his newest acquisition but also ensure constant momentum. And the expanded authority for Kael... that was simply efficient.

"Very well, Kael," Daemon rumbled, his voice devoid of doubt. "The dual approach. Implement the full consolidation of Valoria. Grind them into my empire. And begin your preparations for Aethelgarde, using your network to sow discord. Choose your agents, use your gold, and break them from within. The military targets, however, remain mine. Only I will decide when the blade falls, and where. But all other matters – the spies, the appointments, the resources – that is now your domain. Prove your worth, Grand Strategist."

Kael’s eyes, usually cold, flickered with a raw, almost predatory ambition. To be given such wide authority, such trust, was a reward greater than any gold. He bowed deeply, a rare, genuine spark in his gaze. "It shall be done, My Lord. Your empire will swell."

**Time Skip: 1 Month**

The next month was a period of relentless, synchronized expansion and consolidation. In Valoria, Daemon (Level 10, Age 30) personally oversaw the brutal integration. The screams of the populace echoed through Oakhaven's Reach as a systematic census was conducted, wealth extracted, and all able-bodied men were forced onto the training grounds. Daemon’s elite vanguard – Joric (Level 8, Age 32), Gareth (Level 9, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 9, Age 33), and Theron (Level 9, Age 28) – drove the training relentlessly, forging the new Valorian recruits into a nascent fighting force. The Duchy, once a symbol of internal strife, was being remade, violently, into a unified engine for Daemon’s empire. Its agricultural wealth began to flow, and its new daily gold contribution stabilized at 60 GP.

Concurrently, Kael (Level 9, Age 25), now acting with his expanded strategic autonomy, initiated the unseen campaign against Aethelgarde. From Silverport, where Borin (Level 8, Age 30) now served as Castellan, Kael began deploying agents from his nascent spy network. Whispers of dissent were sown among Aethelgarde's merchant guilds, false rumors of betrayal circulated among its artisan leaders, and key figures were subtly bribed or compromised. Kael operated with ruthless efficiency, preparing the ground for Daemon's eventual military conquest by weakening the Republic from within.

Reports from Valerius (Level 6, Age 48) in Riverbend, Volkov (Level 6, Age 35) in Oakhaven, and Lyra (Level 6, Age 29) in Stonehaven affirmed their continued autonomous governance, maintaining order, training recruits, and ensuring gold flow. Lady Isolde Blackwood (Level 5, Age ~22) diligently oversaw her lands, securing food supplies and managing her integrated guard. In Silverport, Seraphina (Age 20) and the infant Valerius were under the constant care of Mina (Age 60) and Master Elms (Age 75), who continued their research into Isolde's barrenness.
