## Summary

A D&D 5e campaign set in the Spartacus TV show universe (War of the Damned era). The player character is Tiberius, son of Marcus Crassus. Campaign follows Spartacus TV show authenticity — gritty combat, intense drama, betrayals, high-stakes Roman intrigue. Custom low-magic Modified Paladin class, Level 3 at start.

## Key Claims

- Tiberius is DEX-based Modified Paladin (Roman Legate variant)
- Stats: STR 8, DEX 16, CON 14, INT 12, WIS 8, CHA 16
- Oath of Rome (Conquest reskin) — Commanding Voice replaces spellcasting
- Great Weapon Master feat replaced with focus on Aura mechanics
- Aura of Roman Discipline: +CHA to AC and Saves for nearby allies
- Heritage: seeking father's approval, sadistic, cruel, charismatic, intelligent
- First scene: scouting gladiators and pirates making a deal at Sinuessa en Pitta docks
