## Summary

A dark fantasy campaign using custom "Destiny Core Rules" where Daemon, a Level 1 brute strength warrior with evil alignment, seeks to become a ruler. The campaign opens with Daemon at the edge of the Whispering Gorge in the Cragged Peaks, contemplating Baron Valerius's keep of Stonehaven across the way. A young dragon has claimed the gorge, and Daemon sees it as a potential tool or symbol of his power. He scouts the area, chooses to confront the wyrm directly, and charges into its lair. Combat ensues — Daemon lands a critical hit (24 damage) on the young wyrm, which retaliates with dragonfire for 16 damage (Daemon drops to 9/25 HP). The campaign is a study in brutal, direct ambition.

## Key Claims

- Daemon is a Level 1 brute strength warrior, Physique 15, Health 15, low Intelligence (9), high Wisdom (14)
- Personality: Openness 2, Conscientiousness 5 (disciplined/organized/ruthless), Extraversion 4 (commands presence), Agreeableness 1 (no empathy), Neuroticism 1 (emotionally stable/cold)
- Core Ambition: To become a ruler
- Expertise Tags: Heavy Weapons, Intimidation, Tactics
- Young wyrm in the gorge is Level 4, approximately 60 HP
- Baron Valerius (Level 3) rules Stonehaven, refused Daemon's demands for tribute and fealty
