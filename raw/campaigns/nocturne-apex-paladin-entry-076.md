Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Barn Side, Early Evening

The scent of damp earth and fresh blood, swiftly buried beneath layers of leaves, hung faintly in the air. Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) stood, her movements economical, her gaze sweeping the newly secured perimeter of the barn's side. The first internal guard was gone, seamlessly absorbed by the shadows and earth. His professional gear, the unexpected lockpicks, and the detailed map were now tucked within her Bag of Holding, valuable additions to her growing arsenal of resources. The map, especially, was a keen piece of intelligence, confirming the hidden routes and points of interest around Oakhaven. This was a far more organized operation than Kaelen had let on, and each layer she peeled back revealed a deeper, more intriguing challenge.

Her mind, a relentless engine of strategic thought, hummed with a cold satisfaction. The farm's defenses were complex, but not impenetrable. Two layers of professional security – the road patrol and now the first internal guard – had fallen to her calculated precision. The family within the farmhouse remained oblivious, their muffled sounds a constant, tempting invitation.

Now, her focus narrowed to the second guard. The lone sentinel, currently inside, likely near the embrasure on the second floor of the farmhouse. This guard's continued presence was the primary impediment to a clean, efficient acquisition of the "large family." She had used her lure once, and it had proven effective. The question was whether to repeat the tactic, or to shift her approach now that the farm's internal structure and the magic ward's location were known. Her objective was wealth, and that meant securing the maximum amount of "stock" with minimal chaos.
