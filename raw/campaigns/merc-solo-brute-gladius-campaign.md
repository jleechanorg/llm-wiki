## Summary
A D&D 5e solo campaign set in Baldur's Gate shortly after the events of Baldur's Gate 3. The player controls Gladius, a Level 5 Apex Paladin known as "The Hammer" — a physically perfect mercenary who hides his exceptional features beneath a veneer of brutish functionality. The campaign explores themes of hidden identity, class resentment, and hedonistic brutality.

## Key Claims
- Gladius is Level 5 Apex Paladin with 49 HP, STR 18 (+4), DEX 16 (+3), Great Weapon Master feat
- He is secretly a disowned scion of House Al'Veri from Amn, hiding his noble origins
- His face is physically perfect but he deliberately hides it to avoid manipulation
- Uses the Destiny ruleset (D&D 5e based) with custom Apex Paladin class features

## Key Quotes
> "The Caged Adonis — A physically perfect predator hiding his nature beneath a veneer of brutish functionality"
> "To achieve absolute superiority over others, not merely through physical strength, but by psychologically breaking and controlling those the world perceives as 'powerful'"
