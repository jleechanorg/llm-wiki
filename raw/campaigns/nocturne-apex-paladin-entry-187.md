Inside, Lysandra, a slender figure draped in scholarly robes, sat hunched over a collection of ancient scrolls, her face illuminated by the soft glow of a perpetually enchanted lantern. Nocturne wasted no words. She laid the Darkwood Casket, the obsidian shard, the Shadowfell maps, and the ciphered journal open with its key, directly onto Lysandra's pristine worktable. "Myrkul... or something older," she murmured, her gaze tracing the marked ley lines. "And an Architect? This is... unprecedented." Her voice was barely a whisper, a stark contrast to the casual questions she usually fielded. A deep, intellectual fascination, tinged with dawning horror, captivated her.

The challenge was laid bare. Lysandra, the keeper of arcane knowledge, now held a piece of truth that demanded her full, terrifying attention.
