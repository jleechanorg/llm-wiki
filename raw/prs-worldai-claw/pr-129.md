## High-level description

Adds 18 integration tests for companion party management endpoints:

### Endpoints tested
- `POST /sessions/:id/companions/:companionId/join-party` — join party
- `POST /sessions/:id/companions/:companionId/leave-party` — leave party
- `GET /sessions/:id/party` — list party with full companion objects
- `DELETE /sessions/:id/companions/:companionId` — cleanup party on delete
