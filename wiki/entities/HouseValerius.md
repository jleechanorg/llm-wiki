## Description
House Valerius is a noble house in Baldur's Gate with a sigil of a coiled serpent striking through a stylized rose. Their core ideology is power through influence, ruthless pragmatism, and maintaining a veneer of respectability. They have primary economic and political influence within the Upper City and the Parliament of Peers, with significant investments in trade and infrastructure.

## Key Details
- Category: Noble House / Faction
- Ideology: Power through influence, ruthless pragmatism, family above all else
- Influence Area: Upper City and Parliament of Peers
- Public Perception: Respected and feared, known for keen intellect and sharp business dealings
- Key Figurehead: Lord Cassian Valerius (Age 55), Nocturne's father who orchestrated her exile
- Campaign appearances: [[nocturne-apex-paladin-campaign]]
