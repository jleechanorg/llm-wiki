## Description
Threat tracking system (0-10) measuring how noticed the Sovereign is by other powers. Higher aggro means more frequent Logic Sieges and Emperor attention.

## Threat Levels
| Level | Status | Effects |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 0-3 | Dormant | Stealth possible |
| 4-6 | Noticed | Moderate pressure |
| 7-8 | Targeted | Regular Logic Sieges |
| 9 | Hunted | Emperor attention |
| 10 | Purge | Coordinated elimination |

## Starting Condition
New Sovereigns start at **Aggro 8 (Lethal)** — immediately targeted for Logic Sieges.
