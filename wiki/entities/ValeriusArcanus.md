## Profile

- **Age:** 18 (canon, adjusted from 20)
- **Title:** Twin Prince of House Arcanus
- **Role:** Sariel's twin brother
- **Archetype:** The Cold Analyst
- **Alignment:** True Neutral
- **MBTI:** ISTP

## Character Overview

Valerius Arcanus is the twin brother of Sariel and the third child of Alexiel Arcanus and Prince Artorius. Where Cassian's grief manifests as explosive rage, Valerius has retreated into cold, analytical silence. He processes his mother's death as a strategic failure, dissecting every report from the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields trying to understand how the legendary Magister could have been outmaneuvered.

His silence is described as more damning to Sariel than Cassian's open rage. Where Cassian's anger is at least visible and therefore arguable, Valerius's quiet rejection is a wall that offers no path to reconciliation.

## Character Traits

- **Outward:** Emotionally distant, consumed by analysis and tactical review
- **Inward:** Processes grief through logic, treating mother's death as a puzzle
- **Role:** Represents the intellectual, detached response to loss
- **Contrast:** His cold silence contrasts with Cassian's volcanic anger
