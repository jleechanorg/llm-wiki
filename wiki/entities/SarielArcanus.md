## Character Overview

Sariel Arcanus is the daughter of Alexiel Arcanus (the legendary "Mortal Queen") and Prince Artorius. At age 15, she was kidnapped by the warlord Mordan and tortured. Under duress, she revealed her mother's absolute moral code—protecting children—which Mordan exploited to spring a trap at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields. Alexiel sacrificed her own life to stop the ritual and save Sariel. Sariel now carries crushing guilt, believing her torture-induced confession directly caused her mother's death.

She is an intellectual scholar with exceptional Intelligence, possessing Starlit Eyes and a personal data-slate. Her scholarly skills include Arcana, History, Investigation, and Insight.

## Story Arc

Sariel's journey in the campaign begins in immediate aftermath of her mother's death. She explores the Deep Archives to understand the truth behind Alexiel's powers and death, seeking out Sergeant Kaelan (the Starfall Patient) as a witness to the final battle. The campaign explores her grief, guilt, and gradual transformation from grieving scholar to someone with unshakeable resolve.
