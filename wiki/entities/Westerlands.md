## Overview

The Westerlands is a region in the western part of Westeros, ruled by House Lannister. The Mountain's Warcamp is located here, where Ser Gregor Clegane's mercenary company operates. It is known for its wealth, gold mines, and rugged terrain.

## Key Details

- **Ruled By:** House Lannister of Casterly Rock
- **Notable Features:** Gold mines, mountainous terrain
- **Current Occupant:** Ser Gregor Clegane and his men
- **Location:** Western part of the continent
