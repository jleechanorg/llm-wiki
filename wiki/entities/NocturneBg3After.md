## Description
Nocturne in the BG3 After campaign is a sixteen-year-old Half-Elf Bard (College of Eloquence) who was sold to The Bloom & Shadow establishment after her noble house was destroyed during the Absolute crisis. Her name was chosen for the night, for secrets whispered in the dark, and for the silver-tongued grace she's developed to survive. She is a different Nocturne from other campaigns, with stats STR 10, DEX 16, CON 9, INT 12, WIS 11, CHA 16.

## Story
Her family's bankruptcy didn't just strip her of her title; it stripped her of her agency. In a city recovering from a mind-flayer invasion, many seek to forget in the arms of those the city has forgotten. Tonight, a "special guest" — a merchant with ties to the rivals who ruined her family — has requested her company.
