Northern Hispania is the campaign setting region, encompassing the northern territories of the Iberian Peninsula during Roman expansion. This frontier region is home to various Celtic and Iberian tribes who resist Roman conquest. The rugged terrain includes mountain passes like Vindius Pass that serve as strategic chokepoints. The region is rich in mineral resources and presents both opportunities and challenges for Roman legions seeking to expand the empire's borders. Aurelius operates in this region as he pursues his conquest objectives.
