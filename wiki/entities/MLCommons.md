## Overview

MLCommons is an industry consortium running AI safety preparedness benchmarks. They develop and maintain standard benchmarks for AI model evaluation.

- **Benchmarks**: AI safety and preparedness benchmarks
- **Industry consortium**: Cross-company collaboration on AI evaluation
- **Safety preparedness**: Measuring AI systems' safety properties
