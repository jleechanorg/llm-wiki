## Description

Prefect Gratian is a high-ranking enforcer of Empress Sariel's Silent Peace, commanding the Imperial forces in Winter-Mourn Province. He serves as the primary antagonist in the Dragon Knight Good campaign, representing the cold, calculated face of authoritarian power. His bone-white mask and black warhorse make him an intimidating presence, and his voice is described as "cold and metallic."

## Role in the Campaign

In the Dragon Knight Good campaign, Prefect Gratian leads the mission to "pacify" the refugee camp at Ashwood Keep. He serves as the commanding officer of the young knights (the "Eaglets"), including the player character Ser Arion val Valerion. His orders are clinical and absolute: "Any who resist the Mandate are to be dealt with as traitors."

He represents the moral challenge the player must confront: the embodiment of "following orders" without question. Gratian is not cruel for its own sake—he genuinely believes in the Silent Peace's logic that sacrifices a few for the many.

## Relationships

- **Empress Sariel:** Loyal servant, executes her will without hesitation

- **Lady Annalise Ashwood:** Target for "pacification," viewed as a threat to stability
