Lord Eddard Stark, known as Ned, is the head of House Stark and Lord of Winterfell, serving as a Level 12 character in the campaign. He is known for his unwavering sense of honor and his role as the voice of truth in King's Landing. Ned's journey involves his friendship with Robert Baratheon and his discovery of the true parentage of the royal children. His confrontation with Arthur Dayne at the Tower of Joy is a pivotal moment in his history, where he defeated the legendary Sword of the Morning through cunning and the aid of Howland Reed.
