## Overview
The Treaty of Nexus ended the bipolar conflict between the [[CelestialImperium]] and [[ShatteredHost]], creating the current multi-polar world.

## Key Facts
- Status: Ended major war
- Parties: Celestial Imperium, Shattered Host
- Outcome: Multi-polar world emergence
- Effect: Created current strategic situation

## Consequences
- Post-war multi-polar world emerged
- Current tensions center on succession questions, rising independent factions
