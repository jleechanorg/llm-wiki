## Overview
The CNCF is the Linux Foundation organization that hosts cloud-native open-source projects including Kubernetes, OPA (graduated), Envoy, Prometheus, and dozens of other infrastructure tools. OPA's graduation under CNCF established policy-as-code as a first-class cloud-native concern.

## Key Properties
- **CNCF graduated projects**: OPA, Kubernetes, Envoy, Prometheus, ArgoCD, Flux
- **Policy-as-Code**: CNCF hosts OPA which established Rego/policy engine as cloud-native pattern
- **Graduated status**: OPA achieved CNCF graduated status (highest tier)
- **Governance**: CNCF provides legal, operational, and marketing support for member projects
