## Description

The Uchiha Compound is the fortified residential district of the Uchiha clan in Konohagakure. This is where the Uchiha family lived as a closely-knit clan within the larger village. In this campaign, it serves as the location of the Uchiha Clan massacre — the starting point for Itachi's dark journey.

## In This Campaign

The campaign begins at the Uchiha Compound during the night of the clan massacre. This is where 13-year-old Itachi executes his family, beginning his path toward his vision of a unified world order. The compound represents both the height of Uchiha power and the site of their complete destruction.

## Historical Significance

The Uchiha Compound was home to one of the most powerful clans in the shinobi world. Their Sharingan bloodline ability made them formidable warriors, but internal politics and resentment toward the village leadership led to the coup attempt that Itachi — in this evil version — decides to eliminate entirely rather than support.
