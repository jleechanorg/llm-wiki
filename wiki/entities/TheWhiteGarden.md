## Overview

The White Garden is a sanctuary of pristine, broken wills operated by Sosuke Aizen. Located in the Hollows area of Baldur's Gate, it serves as both an elite brothel and a front for harvesting secrets and psyches of the city's elite. Within it are specialized rooms like the Grey Room where "Flowers" (broken-will assets) are cultivated.

## Key Details

- **Owner:** Sosuke Aizen (as "The Gardener")
- **Location:** The Hollows, Baldur's Gate (Little Calimshan in V2)
- **Purpose:** Secret harvesting of secrets, psychological manipulation
- **Staff:** Kaname, Zylra, Ginora (assets in V2)
- **Notable "Flowers":** Vespera, Lirael Voss
- **Grey Room:** A room with scrying crystals for blackmail operations
