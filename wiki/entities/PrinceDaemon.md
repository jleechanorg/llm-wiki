## Summary
Daemon II Blackfyre is the Targaryen prince who becomes Aegon's husband and is seated as the "Prince Who Was Promised" by R'hllor's fire. He begins as a mortal Lvl 20 knight and is elevated to Tier 3 Divine Vessel (Lvl 30) through R'hllor's prophetic spark.

## Key Characteristics
- **Starting Level**: 20
- **Elevated Level**: 30 (Tier 3 Divine Vessel)
- **Role**: Husband of Aegon; the "iron" to her "rhythm" in the Song of Ice, Fire, and Starlight
- **Divine Spark**: Bestowed by R'hllor at the "Solar Eclipse" ceremony

## Arc Summary
1. **Mortal Years**: Knight of the Reach; "Permanent Doubt" clouds his spirit
2. **Elevation**: R'hllor incinerates him with golden starlight at the Palace of the Stars, seating the "Fire of Prophecy" in his marrow
3. **God-Marriage**: Married to Aegon in a formal ceremony witnessed by R'hllor (in mortal avatar)
4. **Divine Partnership**: "I am the iron, and you are the rhythm. Together, we shall be the shield that the Great Other cannot solve."
5. **Guardian of the North**: Leads the Vanguard of the Silver Song; secures the Shivering Wastes garden
