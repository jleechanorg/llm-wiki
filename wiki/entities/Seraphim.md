Divine beings, direct children of The Creator. Ruled during the Age of Dominion under the "Edict of Chains" enforcing absolute order.

## History
Enslaved races deemed "inherently chaotic" (humans, elves, dwarves, orcs) for millennia. Disappeared during the Great Vanishing.
