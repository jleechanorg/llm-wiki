## Overview
The Obsidian Covenant is an organized successor-state within the [[ShatteredHost]], known for its sophisticated philosophy that attracts nobles, mages, and intellectuals.

## Leadership
- Led by: Malakor the Argent Tongue

## Key Facts
- Type: Organized successor-state
- Parent: [[ShatteredHost]]
- Membership: Nobles, mages, intellectuals
- Philosophy: Sophisticated interpretation of Lucifer's ideals

## Relationships
- Part of: [[ShatteredHost]]
- Opposes: [[CelestialImperium]]
