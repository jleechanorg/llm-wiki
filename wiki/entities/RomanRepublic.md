The Roman Republic is the governing system that Aurelius Julia Caesar V2 opposes through his anti-slavery crusade. In 40 BCE, Rome is a powerhouse that maintains its dominance through military conquest and the institution of slavery. The Republic's economy depends heavily on slave labor, making anyone who opposes slavery a threat to the established order. Aurelius's moral stance places him directly at odds with the Senate, the consuls, and the entire power structure of Roman society. The campaign explores the tension between individual moral conviction and systemic injustice.
