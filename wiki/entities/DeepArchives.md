## Profile

- **Name:** Deep Archives
- **Type:** Location (magical vault/library)
- **Setting:** Beneath Zenith Spire, Aeterna
- **Keeper:** Magister Kantos

## Overview

The Deep Archives is a secret vault located beneath the Zenith Spire in Aeterna. It is where Alexiel Arcanus kept her personal journals and research—documents that reveal the true nature of her powers, her creation of Sariel, and the horrifying science behind her demigod abilities.

Magister Kantos is the ancient keeper of these secrets, having guarded them for decades. He served as Alexiel's confidant and is now the reluctant gatekeeper of the truth Sariel seeks.

## Campaign Significance

In the opening scenes of the Sariel V2 campaign, Kantos explains to Sariel the true nature of Nulls—genetically flawed beings the Imperium dismisses as powerless. He reveals how her mother's "disdain for Nulls" was actually the perfect cover for Alexiel's own hidden nature. These revelations settle into the silence of the vault like a "final, chilling piece of the puzzle."

Sariel's objective after leaving the Archives shifts from academic discovery to finding Sergeant Kaelan—a living witness to the events surrounding her mother's death.
