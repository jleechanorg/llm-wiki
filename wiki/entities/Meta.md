Meta is Facebook's parent company and creator of the Llama open-source language model series. Llama models are available through OpenRouter and OpenClaw Gateway in WorldAI.

## In WorldAI
- Meta Llama 3.1 70B/405B Instruct available via OpenRouter
- Open-source models accessible via OpenClaw Gateway
