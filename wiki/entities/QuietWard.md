## Profile

- **Name:** Quiet Ward
- **Type:** Location (fortress-level medical ward)
- **Setting:** Starfall Sanitarium, Aeterna
- **Security:** Fortress-level, requires authorization

## Overview

The Quiet Ward is the deepest, most secure level of the Starfall Sanitarium—a fortress-level medical facility where the most dangerous or sensitive patients are kept. It is not a place of healing but a place of containment.

Kantos warns Sariel that the Quiet Ward is "not a library. It is a fortress." Access requires explicit authorization—Raziel had given explicit orders barring entry. The horrors that lay in its deepest levels are only hinted at.

## Campaign Significance

Sariel's quest for truth leads her to demand that Kantos take her to Sergeant Kaelan, "The Starfall Patient," who is held in the Quiet Ward. Kaelan is a witness to the events at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields—the only person besides Sariel who saw the full truth of what happened when Alexiel died.

Kantos knows the danger but recognizes the same "relentless, unyielding determination" in Sariel that he once saw in her mother. He knows he cannot stop her.
