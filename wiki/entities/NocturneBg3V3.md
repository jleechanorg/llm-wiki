## Character Overview

Nocturne was never meant to be a hero -- she was meant to be merchandise. Born into a once-proud noble family, she was sold into "The Bloom Shadow" Upper City brothel to settle debts she didn't create. In the degrading nights of servitude, she didn't break -- she studied. She learned to read the predatory curiosities of men like Horgus Gwent and turned their own mechanisms against them. By hijacking the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual intended to sacrifice her sisters Elara and Lyra, she didn't just save her kin -- she liquidated her enemies and caught the eye of Archdevil Zariel herself.
