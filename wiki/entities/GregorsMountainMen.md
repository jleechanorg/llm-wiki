## Overview

Gregor's Mountain's Men is the mercenary company led by Ser Gregor Clegane, known as "The Mountain." The company accepts morally corrupt missions including pillaging, raiding, enslavement, and assassination. They operate openly in the Westerlands during 298 AC.

## Key Details

- **Leader:** Ser Gregor Clegane (The Mountain)
- **Operating Region:** Westerlands, particularly the Mountain's Warcamp
- **Mission Types:** Pillaging, raiding, enslavement, assassination
- **Reputation:** Cares only for efficiency of slaughter and silence of the dead

## Campaign Role

The player character Aegon Targaryen joined this mercenary company as his cover identity while secretly harboring his claim to the Iron Throne. The company serves as the vehicle for dark missions targeting the "good" characters of Westeros.
