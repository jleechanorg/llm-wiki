## Overview

Lord Kaito is the public leader of House Sosuke in Baldur's Gate. He appears to be in charge of the family's affairs, but in reality his son Sosuke Aizen is the true architect of the family's future, operating from the shadows. Kaito values steel over scrolls and is unaware of the true extent of Aizen's machinations.

## Key Details

- **Title:** Lord Kaito of House Sosuke
- **Relationship:** Father of Sosuke Aizen
- **Public Role:** Head of House Sosuke
- **Reality:** Figurehead while Aizen controls operations
