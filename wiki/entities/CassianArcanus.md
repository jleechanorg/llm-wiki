## Character Overview

Cassian Arcanus is the elder twin brother of Sariel and one of the three children of Alexiel Arcanus and Prince Artorius. He is consumed by grief and rage over his mother's death, directing his fury at the remnants of Mordan's forces and the Shattered Host.

Secretly, Cassian holds Sariel responsible for being "the bait in the trap" that led to their mother's sacrifice. This tension between the siblings—overt in Cassian's volcanic anger and covert in Sariel's guilt—is a significant emotional undercurrent of the campaign.

## Character Traits

- **Outward:** Rages openly against enemies of the Imperium, leads military efforts against Mordan's remnants
- **Inward:** Blames Sariel for revealing their mother's weakness under torture
- **Role:** Represents the angry, justice-seeking response to loss
- **Contrast:** His hot grief contrasts with Valerius's cold analytical silence
