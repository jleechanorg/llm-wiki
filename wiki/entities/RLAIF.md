## Overview

RLAIF (Reinforcement Learning from AI Feedback) is a training method where AI systems supervise other AI systems. It is used in the RL phase of Constitutional AI training.

## Key Properties

- **Type**: Training method — RL from AI Feedback
- **Used in**: Constitutional AI (RL phase)
- **Advantage**: Reduces human labeling burden by using AI preference models as reward signal
