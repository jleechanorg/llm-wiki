## Description

The Refugee Camp is a temporary settlement in the valley below Ashwood Keep in Winter-Mourn Province. It represents the human cost of Empress Sariel's Silent Peace—displaced people who have been deemed "parasitic drains on resources" and marked for elimination.

## Appearance and Conditions

The camp consists of "ragged tents, shivering families huddled around small fires." The smoke rises as "a thin, pathetic trail of grey against the white horizon." The refugees are described as having endured significant hardship, lacking the resources to survive the harsh Winter-Mourn climate.

## Role in the Campaign

The Refugee Camp is the destination of the player's first mission in the Dragon Knight Good campaign. Under the command of Prefect Gratian, Ser Arion val Valerion must "ensure the dispersal is... thorough" of these refugees. This mission forces the young paladin to confront the moral implications of the Silent Peace firsthand.

The camp represents everything the Imperial logic claims to protect against (instability, resource drain) while simultaneously representing the human cost of that very logic.
