## Overview

The Edgering Ruins is the opening campaign location for the Tyranny campaign. It is a ruined structure where political negotiations between the Disfavored and the Scarlet Chorus break down into violence, establishing Nocturne's dominance through precise, mathematical elimination of rivals.

The ruins serve as a neutral ground where the two factions' officers (Drastus of the Disfavored and Krimp of the Chorus) meet to argue, with Nocturne as the deciding force. The scene is described in terms of tactical analysis and mathematical precision—Discordia's blade wiping clean with silk, the "single, terrifying truth" of Nocturne's supremacy.

## Campaign Significance

The Edgering Ruins scene establishes Nocturne's character through action: the quick elimination of the political variable (Drastus and Krimp) and the emergence of Verse as an observer who recognizes what she is seeing. The ruins are a place of transition, where old political arrangements crumble and Nocturne's new order begins.
