## Overview

ISACA is a professional body for IT governance, offering certifications including AAIA (AI Assurance Analyst), AAIR (AI Risk Professional), and AAISM (AI Security Manager).

## Key Properties

- **Certifications**: AAIA (AI Assurance Analyst), AAIR (AI Risk Professional), AAISM (AI Security Manager)
- **Focus**: IT governance, audit, risk, security
- **AI governance**: Professional certification pathway for AI governance roles
