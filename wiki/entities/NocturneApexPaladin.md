## Description
Nocturne as a Paladin is a 16-year-old Level 5 character with exceptional Aptitudes: Physique 16 (Potential 20), Coordination 14 (Potential 18), Health 15 (Potential 19), Intelligence 18 (Potential 22), Wisdom 16 (Potential 20). She has high HP (43/43), EP (36/36), and combat prowess (+3). Her personality traits show very low Agreeableness (1/5) — ruthless, enjoys murder, aligns with cold pragmatism.
