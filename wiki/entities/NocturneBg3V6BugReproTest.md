## Description

A 20-scene bug-reproduction test campaign for the BG3 V6 system, used to verify character state restoration and campaign continuity mechanics. Nocturne Sosuke is at Level 5 Bard (HP 33/33) in Minthara's War Room in the Shattered Sanctum. The campaign is titled "Bug-Repro-Test" and is primarily a technical validation run rather than a narrative campaign — the focus is on verifying companion integration (Minthara, Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Astarion), character assets, and the Emerald Grove infiltration path.

## Key Details

- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Class:** Bard | Level 5
- **Location:** Minthara's War Room, Shattered Sanctum
- **HP:** 33/33
- **Companions:** Minthara, Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Astarion
- **Scenes:** 20

## Story

The campaign opens with Nocturne standing before a silver mirror in The Bloom Shadow, then immediately transitions to Minthara's War Room — the Shattered Sanctum headquarters. The technical focus is apparent from Scene 1: the GM is running a "Firebase Sanity Check" on character state and companion assets.

Scene 20 shows successful infiltration of the Emerald Grove under a "porcelain victim" pretense, with the "good" companion group (Wyll, Gale, Karlach) redirected. The campaign validates that the character can successfully execute the Minthara-priority path through the Emerald Grove while maintaining the victim persona.

This campaign is primarily infrastructure for testing campaign continuity and character restoration — it is referenced by the cross-campaign synthesis as the "V6 Bug-Repro-Test" used to verify the recurring Nocturne Sosuke character state across sessions.
