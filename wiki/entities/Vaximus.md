## Description

Vaximus is Nocturne's trusted henchman and retrieves valuable items and information for her operations. In the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, he is tasked with retrieving the family's reclaimed ledgers and the Cassalanter soul coins after the successful ritual hijacking.

## Role in the Campaign

Vaximus appears in the opening scene of the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, frantically stuffing the family's reclaimed ledgers and Cassalanter soul coins into a satchel. He provides practical perspective on the immediate aftermath of the ritual, warning that the Flaming Fist will arrive within twenty minutes and that the Zhentarim will "smell the power vacuum before the bodies are even cold."

## Capabilities and Personality

Vaximus serves as Nocturne's reliable agent for retrieval and audit operations. His practical concerns—securing assets, planning escape routes, verifying deeds—ground the high-stakes supernatural events in tangible logistics.
