## Summary
Robb Stark (Level 2 Fighter → Level 8 by late campaign) is the heir of Winterfell. In the Visenya V6 campaign, he is the primary target of Visenya's "Shattered Compass" arc — a slow-motion destruction of his Stark identity. He begins as a boy trying to sound like a man and ends as a "buckling beam" of a man, functionally addicted to Visenya's presence and lying to his father with increasing fluency.

## Character Arc
- **Early**: First impression of Visenya: "I forgot how to breathe... She looks like she could command an army with a whisper"
- **Mid**: Isolated in the Wolfswood; confides his burdens; becomes a "boy unspooled"
- **Late**: Fully converted; kills his mentor (a Karstark captain) at Visenya's command
- **Endgame**: Fails to kill Catelyn ("the stone was too thick") but brands her; participates in Ned's parricide; competes with Jon for Visenya's exclusive favor

## Key Moments
- **The Heavy Crown**: Visenya plants seeds of resentment about Ned's rigid honor
- **The Branding**: Cannot kill his mother but can perform the branding — his first visible failure
- **The Parricide**: Kills Ned with Ice alongside Jon/Jaehaerys
