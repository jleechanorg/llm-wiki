# House Stark

Warden of the North, led by Lord Eddard Stark. Specialty: heavy infantry and absolute moral rigidity. The North remains an isolationist "Ice Cage" paying no taxes under the Targaryen cold-war regime.

## Key Members
- **Eddard Stark** — Lord, inflexible honor makes him predictable
- **Robb Stark** — His son, believes his direwolf makes him dangerous
- **Jon Snow** — Rhaegar's legitimized son with Lyanna Stark, Level 6 Paladin/Fighter

## The Hook
Eddard's inflexible honor is highly predictable, and Jon Snow's true Targaryen lineage is a mathematical vulnerability waiting to be exploited.

## Faction Status
- **Specialty**: Heavy infantry and absolute moral rigidity
- **The Vibe**: Cold, silent resentment
- **Hostility**: Antagonistic to Targaryen rule but isolationist rather than aggressive
