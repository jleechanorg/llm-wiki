## Overview

Partnership on AI (PAI) is a multi-stakeholder AI governance organization with members including Amazon, Meta, Google, IBM, Microsoft, and others. It runs the Safety-Critical AI Program.

## Key Properties

- **Members**: Amazon, Meta, Google, IBM, Microsoft, and others
- **Safety-Critical AI Program**: "Seeks to establish social and technical foundations that will support the safe development and deployment of AI"
- **Mission**: Precautionary approach to AI safety — "We don't have to wait for such incidents to arise. As our work shows, precaution can be taken as early as the research stage"
- **Focus**: Safety-critical AI, responsible AI development
