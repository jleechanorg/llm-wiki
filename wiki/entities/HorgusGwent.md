## Overview
Horgus Gwent is a wealthy noble and regular client of the Bloom Shadow brothel who serves as the catalyst for the V4 revenge plot. He possesses a Soul Coin bearing Nocturne's sister Lyra's face and brings the Cassalanter Ascension Gala invitation that sets the story in motion. His remark "They bottled them... vintage currency" reveals the fate of Nocturne's sisters.
