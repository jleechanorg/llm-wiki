## Overview

Fortress Vigil is an imperial fortress that lies in the Dragon's Tooth Foothills at the Imperial border. Its gleaming marble is visible from the Dragon's Tooth Mountains where Alexiel stands after her defection from the Unchained Host. An Imperial patrol approaches her with steel drawn, making survival dependent on which mask she chooses to wear.

## Key Details

- **Type:** Imperial fortress
- **Location:** Dragon's Tooth Foothills, Imperial border
- **Appearance:** Gleaming marble
- **Status:** Border fortress, patrols in area
