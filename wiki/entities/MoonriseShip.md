## Description
The Moonrise Ship is a black-painted cog from Moonrise that has been captured by the Shadow Monarch's forces. It serves as the current location where Operation Scalpel was executed — the successful raid that captured the True Soul Leader. The ship drifts listlessly against the Guild blockade at Wyrm's Tooth, its crew either mesmerized or dead.
