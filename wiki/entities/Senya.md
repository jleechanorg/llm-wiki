## Overview
Senya is Nocturne's mother in the Nocturne Old Republic campaign, bestowed the title of Sovereign of Judgment. She is described as having a golden aura that pulsates with warmth and adoration.

## Key Details
- **Category:** Person / NPC / Nocturne's Mother
- **Campaign(s):** [[nocturne-old-republic]]
- **Title:** Sovereign of Judgment
- **Physical Description:** Golden aura
- **Gift from Nocturne:** The **Well of Mercy** on Belsavis — a reality-anchor that allows her to heal any soul, no matter how fractured, plus a palace woven from golden glass that will never fade
