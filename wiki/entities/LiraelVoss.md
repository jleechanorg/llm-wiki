## Overview

Lirael Voss is a shipwright heiress in Baldur's Gate whose pride is reaching its breaking point. She is a target of Sosuke Aizen's slow corruption scheme in the V2 campaign. Aizen is methodically breaking down her resistance as part of his broader plans for the city.

## Key Details

- **Role:** Target of Aizen's manipulation
- **Background:** Shipwright heiress
- **Status:** Being slowly corrupted
