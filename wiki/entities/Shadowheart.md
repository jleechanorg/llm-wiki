## Character Overview

Shadowheart is the player character of the Shadow Heart campaign—a Baldur's Gate 3 continuation where the player takes on Shadowheart after the events of BG3. The player chose the evil narrative path and is continuing as the Chosen of Shar.

As a custom Death Cleric class built collaboratively between player and DM, Shadowheart combines martial prowess (Extra Attack) with necrotic divine power (Soul Harvest):

### Custom Class Features

**Martial Cleric (Extra Attack):**
Grant Shadowheart the standard Fighter's Extra Attack feature (two attacks when taking the Attack action), emphasizing her combat prowess beyond standard Cleric limitations.

**Soul Harvest:**
Powers centered on draining life force, manipulating souls, and empowering Shadowheart through necrotic energy. This emphasizes her Death Cleric aspect and connection to Shar's dark theology.

## Shar's Chosen

Shadowheart serves Shar—the goddess of darkness, loss, and forgetting. As a Chosen, she has been granted special divine abilities. The campaign explores the continuation of her theological journey as a Death Cleric, with the custom Soul Harvest path emphasizing the necrotic aspect of Shar's portfolio.
