## Description
Ser Arion val Valerion is a 16-year-old Paladin of the Argent Eaglets knight order, raised in Empress Sariel's authoritarian "Silent Peace." His journey at Ashwood Keep forces him to confront the morality of obedience versus individual conscience.

## Background
Born and raised entirely within the Silent Peace — Empress Sariel's absolute rule established through the "War of Whispers" and psychic mastery — Arion has known only a world of absolute security where dissent is thought-crime. As a knight of the Argent Eaglets, he has been trained to serve without question.

His assignment to Ashwood Keep, where Lady Annalise shelters refugees in defiance of the Stability Mandate, presents his first true moral dilemma. Under the clinical command of Prefect Gratian, Arion must decide what loyalty truly means.

## Personality
Arion is principled but untested. He believes in honor, duty, and loyalty — but these abstract concepts are now confronting harsh reality. He is young, earnest, and struggles to reconcile the perfect world he was raised in with the imperfect world he discovers.
