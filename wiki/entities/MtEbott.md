## Profile

- **Name:** Mt. Ebott
- **Type:** Location (mountain)
- **Setting:** World of Undertale
- **Feature:** The hole leading to the Underground

## Overview

Mt. Ebott is the mountain above the Underground—the site where Frisk and countless others have fallen into the monster realm. Legends say that those who climb Mt. Ebott never return, establishing the mountain as a place of mystery and danger in the human world.

The hole at the top of Mt. Ebott is the entry point to the Underground. Frisk falls through this hole, entering the purple-glowing world of monsters below. The fall itself is the beginning of the Undertale campaign.
