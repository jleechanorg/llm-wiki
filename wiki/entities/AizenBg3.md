# Aizen Bg3

**Campaign:** 596 entries | **Character:** Aizen Sosuke | **Arc:** Strategic mastermind, Hogyoku wizard

## Premise

Set in post-Netherbrain Baldur's Gate. Aizen Sosuke is a 16-year-old prodigy adopted into House Sosuke, whose true identity is the mastermind behind "The White Garden" — a sanctuary where broken wills are harvested for secrets and psyches. He has already marked the city's leadership with his Kyoka Suigetsu (complete hypnosis). Waiting for the moment to shatter "the lens through which they see reality."

## Character Concept

From Entry 1:

> *"Aizen has spent his youth weaving a web of complete hypnosis and strategic vice. Through his secret ownership of 'The White Garden,' a sanctuary of pristine, broken wills, he has begun to harvest the secrets and psyches of the city's elite. He views the world not as a home, but as a dull machine in need of a perfect sovereign. With the Hōgyoku—his pursuit of evolutionary transcendence—always on his mind, he has already 'marked' the city's leadership, waiting for the moment to shatter the lens through which they see reality."*

## "Hogyoku" Complex

The Hogyoku (from Bleach) represents: must be indispensable, must transcend, must become so integrated into the system that removal would destroy it.

Manifestations:
- Kyoka Suigetsu (complete hypnosis) — total control over perception
- "White Garden" — systematic harvesting of elite psyches
- Strategic vice — patience as weapon

## God Mode Usage

Entry 45 shows active god mode use for **mechanical refinement** — checking for imbalance, optimizing spell DC, adjusting equipment. The player is co-authoring the power trajectory.
