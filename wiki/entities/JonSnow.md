## Summary
Jon Snow (Level 3 Paladin/Fighter → Level 8 by late campaign) is the legitimized son of Rhaegar Targaryen and Lyanna Stark, raised by Eddard Stark as his bastard. In the Visenya V6 campaign, he begins as Visenya's "secondary anchor" and ends as her most intellectually engaged weapon — "Jaehaerys," the King-Consort in the Shadows. He is the only Stark child whose emotions are shielded from Visenya's Information Geometry, making him a "fascinating puzzle."

## Character Arc
- **Early**: First to perceive Visenya's Static; his Level 3 Paladin instincts recognize the predator others cannot
- **Mid**: Drawn to Visenya's clinical, intellectual flirtation about his Valyrian features; Robb becomes jealous of their armory conversations
- **Late**: The "Bastard's Mirror" arc completes — Jon fully converts, taking the name Jaehaerys and wearing the Crown of Roses
- **Endgame**: Bound to Visenya through shared parricide (killing Ned together with Robb); the Obsession Paradox makes him compete with Robb for exclusive favor

## Key Quotes

- "I would build a world where shadows are the law... I would be your sword, Elyse." (Fever Dream vision)
