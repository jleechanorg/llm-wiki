## Description

The Shattered Rose is Nocturne Sosuke's pocket-dimension dominion carved from the corpses of the Abyss's 666th Layer. It serves as her prison (sealed by Ao's Geometric Firewall) and her court — a realm of infinite indulgence and beautiful ruin where demon lords and former heroes serve as her thralls. The air smells of ozone, expensive perfume, and divine blood. Rivers run with liquid starlight beneath a bruised violet sky.

## Key Traits

- **The Honor Guard:** Level 20+ entities frozen in ecstatic devotion — Lae'zel (Knight of Silver & Ash), Shadowheart (Handmaiden of Ecstasy), Astarion (Prince of Bleeding Hearts), Gale (Broken Weaver), Malcanthet (Gilded Shield)
- **Economy of Sensation:** Built on "Fresh Experience" — souls harvested from the Material Plane are currency
- **The Obsidian Mirror:** Two-way portal allowing the Sovereign to watch and communicate
- **The Sovereign's Supply Chain:** Cut by Ao; needs living souls as "high-octane fuel" to calculate a breach
- **The Tasting Menu:** Quality of harvested souls affects Court favor (peasant = indifferent; paladin = frenzy of ecstasy)
