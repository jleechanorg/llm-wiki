## Early Career (Year 61–78 DR)

Alexiel was created by **[[Lucifer]]** as the ultimate weapon of the Unchained Host. Born in Year 61 DR, she was a biological construct — a peerless warrior and secret sorcerer, a prodigy whose true nature was hidden even from her creator. For sixteen years she served as the Host's "Prodigal Sword," all the while cultivating the persona of the "Reluctant Champion" — a dutiful warrior who hated the violence she excelled at.

The breaking point came at the **Sacking of Silverwood (Year 4 B.G.)** when, at Lucifer's command, Host forces slaughtered innocent children. This violated Alexiel's one unbreakable moral line. She defected to the Imperial side.

## Imperial Period — Alexiel Arcanus

She met and married the honorable **Prince Artorius**, son of **Lord Regent Raziel**. Her marriage was both a political masterstroke and a genuine, complex bond — though their entire relationship was built on the lie of her "Reluctant Champion" persona; Artorius never knew the "Joyful Predator" that thrilled at perfect battle execution.

## Later Life

The long war reached its climax with **Prince Artorius's death** at the Battle of Mourning Fields (2 B.G.). Alexiel, alongside Raziel, orchestrated the **Starfall Gambit** — killing Lucifer and his remaining Apostates in Year 0. In the aftermath, she secretly absorbed her creator's divine power, becoming a hidden demigod.

She chose to rule from the shadows as the **"Mortal Queen,"** hoping to build lasting peace for her children. This peace lasted ten years.

## The Trap and Death

At age 15, her daughter **[[Sariel Arcanus]]** was kidnapped by the warlord **Mordan**. Under torture, Sariel revealed Alexiel's absolute moral vow: protecting children. Mordan exploited this to spring a trap at the **Battle of the Sacrifice Fields**, using hundreds of captive children as leverage. Faced with an impossible choice, Alexiel overloaded her **Nullification Field** to stop a profane demonic ritual and save the children — sacrificing her own life.
