## Overview
The Gralhund Villa is the mansion of the Cassalanter Family in Baldur's Gate. In the Nocturne campaigns, it serves as the target of Nocturne's infiltration during the Ascension Gala, where she seeks to reclaim her sisters' Soul Coins and disrupt the family's infernal pact with Zariel.
