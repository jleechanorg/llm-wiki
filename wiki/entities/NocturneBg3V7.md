## Overview
Nocturne is the player character in the Nocturne BG3 V7 campaign. A Level 5 Divine Scion/Siren with hybrid lineage from Malcanthet (demon lordess) and Bhaal (murder god), she has achieved the position of Arch-Druid of Shadow Grove after inverting the Rite of Thorns.

## Attributes
- **Level:** 5
- **Class:** Divine Scion/Siren
- **Lineage:** Malcanthet + Bhaal hybrid
- **Position:** Arch-Druid of Shadow Grove

## Abilities
- **Mother's Mercy:** Soul conversion ability
- **Divine Scionpowers:** Granted by dual heritage
- **Thrall System:** Commands up to 3 thralls
