## Summary
House Peake is the primary antagonist force in the Marches campaign arc. Led by Lord Peake (Lvl 12), they field heavy infantry called "the Swarm" — approximately 200 spears in the immediate pincer, with potential for thousands through Marcher Lord mobilization.

## Key Details
- **Leader**: Lord Peake (Lvl 12)
- **Force**: 200 heavy infantry (Eastern Pincer), with regional mobilization potential
- **Special Unit**: High Watch Infantry (CR 5 Unit)
- **Signal System**: Mirror-based communication between beacons for tactical coordination

## Campaign Actions Against House Peake
- **Widow's Crossing**: 30 outriders executed, 1 Peake Captain captured
- **Southern Mouth of Jagged Pass**: 60 infantry captured, then culled to 5 officer cadre (55 executed)
- **The Grey Sentinel**: 20 outriders ambushed; 19 executed, 1 Sergeant captured
- **Signal Spoof**: Peake eastern pincer anchored at "the Needle" via captured command codes

## Total Casualties Inflicted on House Peake
- 104 executed
- 30+ scattered
- 7 officers captured (1 Captain, 6 Sergeants)
