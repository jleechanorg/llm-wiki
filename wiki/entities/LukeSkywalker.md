## Description

Luke Skywalker appears in this D&D 5e campaign with a custom "Force-User" class combining Star Wars elements with the Baldur's Gate setting. This version of Luke maintains his canon background from the original trilogy but adds complexity — he possesses both light side affinity and dark tendencies, with a secret sadistic side that emerges during gameplay.

The campaign uses a custom Force-User class with a Light/Dark subclass system, tracking Luke's moral progression through a Force Alignment Tracker. This creates a tension between his heroic origins and corruptible nature, allowing for nuanced storytelling about the nature of power and morality.

## In This Campaign

The campaign combines the Star Wars universe with the post-Baldur's Gate 3 setting, creating a unique crossover where Force abilities interact with the D&D 5e ruleset under the "Destiny" ruleset. Luke's journey explores the classic Jedi question of darkness versus light, but with the added complexity of his secret sadistic tendencies.

## Character Concept

- **Class:** Custom Force-User with Light/Dark subclass system
- **Affinity:** Light side with dark tendencies
- **Core Mechanic:** Force Alignment Tracker monitors moral progression
- **Background:** Canon Luke with hidden dark side
