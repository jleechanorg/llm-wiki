## Overview
Kagha is a Shadow Druid leader within the Emerald Grove, representing the radical faction that has taken over the Inner Sanctum. In V6, Olodan (Shadow Druid Envoy) has made contact with her, advancing the plan to deliver the Grove to the Shadow Druids. She is described as radicalized but "not yet damned."
