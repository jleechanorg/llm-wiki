## Overview
The Shattered Sanctum is a ruined druidic temple in Baldur's Gate, serving as the headquarters for the druids. In V6, it contains Minthara's War Room where tactical decisions are made for the goblin assault on the Emerald Grove, and the Sacred Pool where the Rite of Thorns is performed.
