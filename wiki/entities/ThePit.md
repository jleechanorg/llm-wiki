## Description

"The Pit" is a post-explosion tactical chaos zone in Cheyenne, Wyoming. This area serves as the primary setting for the Hunting Party Sariel V2 campaign, representing a lawless territory where traditional rules of engagement don't apply.

## In This Campaign

The 16-year-old Sariel operates in The Pit alongside her team of companions. The environment is dangerous, unpredictable, and allows for extreme action. The area's chaotic nature makes it ideal for vigilante operations, as law enforcement has limited ability to operate there.

## Characteristics

- **Post-explosion:** The area was created by a major explosion, leaving it in a state of tactical chaos
- **Lawless:** Traditional law enforcement struggles to operate in the area
- **Strategic:** The chaos makes it a valuable location for those who can navigate its dangers
- **Dangerous:** High risk environment requiring exceptional skills to survive
