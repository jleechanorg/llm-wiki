Aurelius Julia Caesar V2 is a Level 5 Paladin dedicated to anti-slavery activism in 40 BCE Italy. As a Lawful Good character, he fights against the Roman Republic's institution of slavery, making him a target of the established order. He is the son of Julius Caesar, carrying his father's ambition but directing it toward moral causes. His wife Aurelia was rescued from slavery, fueling his personal crusade against the institution. Based in Capua, Aurelius navigates the complex political landscape of ancient Italy while opposing the very system that built the Republic.
