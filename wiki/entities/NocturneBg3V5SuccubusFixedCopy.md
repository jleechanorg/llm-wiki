## Description

A 669-scene extended run of the V5 Succubus campaign arc. Nocturne Sosuke opens on the Ravaged Beach at 1 HP with an inverted Malcanthet contract. The campaign extends significantly further than the base V5 Succubus (278 scenes), reaching the Shattered Rose obsidian heart sanctuary and the Sanctum Protocol — a state of absolute clinical stillness where Nocturne's internalized Crown reaches a pressurized vacuum of bruised indigo static.

The terminal scene (669) is titled "The Stygian Ingress and the Vampire's Debt," suggesting a late-game engagement with vampire factions and infernal ingress into Stygia (the Fourth Layer of the Nine Hells).

## Key Details

- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Class:** Bard | Level 1 → Level 13+ (Nascent Demon Lord range at Scene 669)
- **Background:** Malcanthet patron (inverted), 730 days at The Bloom Shadow
- **Scenes:** 669

## Story

Extends the V5 Succubus arc beyond the Goblin Camp and Emerald Grove into deeper infernal territory. The Sanctum Protocol activation at Scene 669 represents a major milestone: the internalized Crown reaches a state of absolute stillness within the obsidian heart of the Shattered Rose sanctuary.

The "Stygian Ingress" of the final scene title places the campaign's terminus at the border of Stygia — the Fourth Layer of the Nine Hells, realm of the Archdevil Levistus — suggesting the campaign arc extends infernal politics into the Hellscape itself.
