## Overview

MetaGPT is a meta-programming framework (Hong et al., 2023) that encodes Standardized Operating Procedures (SOPs) for multi-agent collaboration. Its key innovation is the "assembly line paradigm" assigning diverse roles to agents to reduce cascading hallucinations.

## Key Properties

- **Architecture**: Meta-programming framework encoding SOPs
- **Key innovation**: "Assembly line paradigm" assigning diverse roles to agents
- **Verification**: Reduces cascading hallucinations via human-like domain expertise verification
- **Paper**: arXiv (multi-agent collaboration)
- **Reference**: Voyager paper cites MetaGPT as related work
