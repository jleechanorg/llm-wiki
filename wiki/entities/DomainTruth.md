## Definition
A fundamental truth of reality that cannot be denied when a deity is present in their domain. Core power mechanism in [[DivineAscensionCeremony]]. Examples: "Fire burns not in my presence," "Steel bends to my will."

## Properties
- **Acquisition**: Granted upon domain selection during ascension ceremony
- **Effect**: Reality warps to accommodate truth when deity is present
- **Domain Connection**: Each domain provides multiple suggested truths
