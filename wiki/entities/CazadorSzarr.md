## Description
Cazador Szarr is a vampire lord and the primary antagonist of the bg3-astarion campaign. Two hundred years ago, he turned Astarion into a Vampire Spawn and forced him to lure victims to his master.

## Background
Cazador Szarr has existed for centuries as a powerful vampire. His mansion in Baldur's Gate served as a hunting ground where he cultivated a network of Vampire Spawn to do his bidding.

## Relationship with Astarion
Astarion was turned into a Vampire Spawn by Cazador approximately 200 years before the campaign. During this time, Astarion was compelled to lure victims to Cazador's master. This traumatic experience defines Astarion's greatest fear: returning to Cazador's control.

## Capabilities
As a vampire lord, Cazador possesses:
- Superhuman strength and durability
- Control over lesser vampires and spawn
- Darkvision and regeneration
-Hypnotic presence

## Campaign Role
Primary antagonist and source of Astarion's traumatic past in the bg3-astarion campaign.
