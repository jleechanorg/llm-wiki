## Overview

CAMEL (Communicative Agents Framework) is a research framework (Guohao Li et al.) for multi-agent cooperation via role-playing. The paper introduces "inception prompting" for autonomous cooperation without human intervention.

## Key Properties

- **Paper authors**: Guohao Li, Hasan Abed Al Kader Hammoud, Hani Itani, Dmitrii Khizbullin, Bernard Ghanem
- **Key concept**: Inception prompting — role-playing framework where agents autonomously cooperate toward goals
- **Approach**: Two agents (assistant + user) engage in progressive dialogue without human guiding the conversation
- **Goal**: Study emergent behaviors in multi-agent systems without task-specific prompting
