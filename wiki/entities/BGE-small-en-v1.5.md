BGE-small-en-v1.5 is a sentence embedding model from BAAI (Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence). Produces 384-dimensional dense embeddings, used by FastEmbed in the Local Intent Classifier.

## Specifications
- Dimensions: 384
- Size: ~133MB
- Source: HuggingFace Hub (BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5)
