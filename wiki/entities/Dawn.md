Dawn is a legendary greatsword of unparalleled renown in Westeros, a +5 weapon that deals radiant and force damage. It is forged from the heart of a fallen star, giving it its distinctive pale white coloring and inner glow. The sword has been passed down through generations of the Dayne family, from father to son, for thousands of years. When Arthur Dayne wields Dawn, the blade seems to glow with the light of dawn itself, earning him his epithet "the Sword of the Morning." This weapon is considered the most famous in all the realm.
