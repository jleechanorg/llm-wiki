# Faction Nocturne Bg3 V3

**Campaign:** 606 entries | **Character:** Nocturne Sosuke | **Arc:** Brothel survivor → soul-industrialist → nascent overgod

## Premise

Set in post-Netherbrain Baldur's Gate. Nocturne, a 16-year-old Half-Elf Bard sold into "The Bloom & Shadow" brothel after her noble family's bankruptcy. She hijacks the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual, liquidates her enemies, and catches the eye of Archdevil Avernus. From there, she builds a soul-industrial complex that eventually reaches multiversal scale.

## Key Phases

### Phase 1: Survival and Extraction (Entries 1-100)
- Hijack the Cassalanter ritual to save sisters Elara and Lyra
- Redirect soul-tithe to become Avernus's proxy
- Acquire House Sosuke as primary creditor
- Establish soul-stealing as core mechanic

### Phase 2: Industrialization (Entries 100-300)
- Scale soul processing to 750/month
- Industrialize damnation — "a farm" for nobles
- Reach Level 20, acquire Helldusk Armor + Staff of Spellpower
- DC 30 mask prevents Solar intervention

### Phase 3: Cosmological Dominance (Entries 300-606)
- Bypass gods, become the system itself
- 1.649 billion souls, 2.4 trillion followers
- Author "Law of the Spire" — fundamental re-indexing of reality
- Final request: "turn everywhere into a breeding parase, hell, abyss, faerun, heavens"

## Character Choices (Key Evidence)

- **Entry 100:** *"Turn everywhere into a breeding parase"* — final player request showing full system-capture ambition
- **Entry 500:** Player initiates simulation of their own torture to confront Zariel — choosing to experience powerlessness to master it
- **Entry 600:** *"Author the absolute re-indexing of reality"* — optimizing the multiverse itself

## Player Choice Pattern Evidence

This campaign demonstrates:
- Economic translation of every crisis (souls as "livestock," "processing rate," "archival dividends")
- Scale escalation without framework change (from brothel to multiverse with same industrial logic)
- "Made by the system" origin (merchandise in a ledger)
- Third-option reframing (turning torture simulation into dominance display)
