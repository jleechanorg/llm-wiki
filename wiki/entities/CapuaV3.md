CapuaV3 is the city setting for the v3 campaign, representing a divergent timeline version of the ancient Roman city. This version of Capua serves as the operating ground for Aurelius Julius Caesar V3 under his alias Aurelius Varenus. The city maintains its historical significance as a center of commerce and gladiatorial training, but in this timeline operates under different political circumstances. The divergent history creates unique opportunities for Aurelius's merchant cover operations and his covert leadership of The Praetorian Hand.
