Gale of Toril is a wizard and companion to Astarion, continuing their journey from Baldur's Gate 3 into the post-Absolute setting. As a mage of exceptional talent, Gale possesses extensive knowledge of arcane magic and retains his eager appetite for Netherese magical secrets. His relationship with Astarion has evolved from travel companions to something more complex as they navigate the challenges of a post-war world. Gale's wizardly abilities complement Astarion's vampiric powers, making them a formidable duo.
