## Overview
Garl is a half-orc tavern keeper encountered during the V5 campaigns. He provides early-game information and supplies to Nocturne after the Nautiloid crash, appearing in the initial survival sequences at or near the Ravaged Beach area before the party moves toward the Emerald Grove.
