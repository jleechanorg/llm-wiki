## Overview
The Emerald Grove is a druid sacred site in Baldur's Gate, home to the tiefling refugees and druids of the Emerald Circle. In the Nocturne campaigns, it serves as the destination after the Nautiloid crash, with the Rite of Thorns as a key plot element in V6 where Nocturne plans to subvert and siphon its power.
