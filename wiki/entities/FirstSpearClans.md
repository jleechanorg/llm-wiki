## Overview
The First-Spear Clans are former slaves who forged an independent nation through warrior tradition, now struggling with transition from revolutionary movement to stable governance.

## Sub-factions
- **High Council**: Established leadership focused on nation-building, pragmatic diplomacy and stability, led by War-Chief Vorlag
- **Tusk-Sworn**: Warrior tradition faction

## Key Facts
- Type: Independent power
- Origin: Former slaves
- Focus: Transition from revolutionary movement to stable governance
- Leader: War-Chief Vorlag

## Relationships
- Independent from: [[CelestialImperium]], [[ShatteredHost]]
