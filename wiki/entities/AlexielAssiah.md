## Overview

Alexiel, known as "The Mortal Star," is the player character of the Alexiel Assiah campaign set in Year 78 DR. She is a biological construct designed by Lucifer to be the ultimate weapon of the Unchained Host. Publicly a "Null" (no magic), secretly she is a magical prodigy capable of manifesting a Nullification Field. She served as the Sword of the Host until the massacre at Silverwood violated her moral boundaries, causing her defection.
