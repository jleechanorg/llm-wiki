## Overview
Jesse Vincent coined the dorodango framing for codegen software — the Japanese art of polishing a ball of mud into a high-gloss sphere. His insight: codegen software is disposable; polish what comes out, and when fundamentally wrong, throw it away and rebuild from spec.

## Key Properties
- **Type**: Developer, blogger
- **Key features**: Coined dorodango framing for codegen software, author of the "dorodango" blog post (Feb 2026)
- **Key insight**: "Software is cheap now. Specs are the expensive part."
