## Description
Astarion Ancunín is an Elf Vampire Spawn and Ranger, formerly a respected magistrate who was lured and turned by the vampire lord Cazador Szarr. After 200 years of forced service, he now seeks freedom, power, and revenge.

## Background
Originally a magistrate in his elven homeland, Astarion was magically lured and transformed into a Vampire Spawn by the notorious Cazador Szarr. For 200 years, he was forced to serve as a lure — drawing victims to the vampire's lair. Despite this horrific captivity, Astarion never fed on sentient blood, preserving some fragment of his former self.

His core motivation is twofold: freedom from the potential return of Cazador's control, and gaining enough power to never be vulnerable again.

## Personality
Astarion presents a sardonic, manipulative exterior wrapped in charm and vanity. He uses humor as armor against his deep trauma. He is pragmatic, self-serving, and hedonistic — enjoying pleasures he was denied for centuries. Underneath the wit lies a traumatized survivor who trusts no one and plans for every contingency.

## Capabilities
- **Race:** Elf (200 years old)

Protagonist of the bg3-astarion campaign, beginning on the Nautiloid mind flayer ship.
