## Description
Seoul is the primary setting for the doberman campaign, adapted from the K-drama "Military Prosecutor Doberman." This modern Korean city serves as the backdrop for corporate intrigue, political manipulation, and the ultimate struggle for control.

## Significance
Within Seoul, the power dynamics revolve around the chaebol (conglomerate) system and corporations like IM Defense that wield enormous influence. Sariel's operations within this environment leverage the city's blending of traditional hierarchy and modern corporate power.
