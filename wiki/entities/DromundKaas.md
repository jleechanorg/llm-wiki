## Overview
Dromund Kaas is the capital planet of the Sith Empire in the Star Wars TOR timeline. A world of perpetual storms and dark jungle, it serves as the Imperial stronghold in 3640 BBY.

## Significance
The Imperial capital where Emperor Vitiate rules and where Nocturne's story unfolds. The planet's dark atmosphere reflects the Empire's nature.
