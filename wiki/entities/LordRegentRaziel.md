## Overview

Lord Regent Raziel is the ancient ruler of the Celestial Imperium, a bastion of rigid hierarchy and golden magic. He governs from Aeterna, the geometric wonder that serves as the Imperium's capital. The Thousand Year War between the Imperium and the Unchained Host has reached a stalemate.

## Key Details

- **Title:** Lord Regent Raziel
- **Rules:** The Celestial Imperium
- **Capital:** Aeterna
- **Opponent:** Lucifer and the Unchained Host
