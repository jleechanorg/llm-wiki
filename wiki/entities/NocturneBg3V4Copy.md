## Description

A parallel run of the BG3 V4 campaign, also set in Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis. Nocturne Sosuke opens in The Bloom Shadow brothel as the "Black Diamond," sold after the Cassalanter destruction of House Sosuke. The character arc follows the same trajectory as V4 — brothel captive → social infiltrator → infernal sovereign — but diverges in execution and timing.

**Key divergence:** V4 Copy reaches Level 18 Bard/Infernal Sovereign by Scene 821, ending in the High Hall Grand Sanctum with 172 souls, pregnant, masked behind a DC 32 presence concealment, with unlimited L1-L6 spell slots. The campaign terminates at Scene 821, while V4 continues to Scene 1207 and beyond.

## Story

Identical opening to V4: Horgus Gwent arrives drunk at Nocturne's chamber in The Bloom Shadow, drops a Soul Coin bearing her sister Lyra's face, and hands her the invitation to the Cassalanter Ascension Gala. The revelation — that House Sosuke wasn't just ruined but harvested — is the catalyst for a long-con infiltration of the Cassalanter operation.

By Scene 200, Nocturne has pivoted from captive to operative, running intelligence operations from the Basilisk Gate dead-drop network. The Cassalanter frame job is in motion, and the Gralhund annexation has begun.

By Scene 400, she is Level 7 Bard operating from the Gralhund Annexed Sanctum, executing the Absolute's soul audit as a double agent — reporting to the Elder Brain while redirecting its resources toward her own infrastructure.

By Scene 600, she is Level 11 Grand Duke at the Moonrise Towers docks, pregnant, with the Arros Silencer activated against her registration. The Shadow-Cursed Verdict has been survived. Ketheric Thorm is in submission.

The terminal scene (821) shows her as Level 18 Bard/Infernal Sovereign at the High Hall Grand Sanctum, performing the Essence Siphon — a clinical, surgical extraction of trapped noble souls from sculptures, funneling them directly into the Gralhund Signet as mythic-tier fuel for the "Utopia factories." The debt ledger closes. The soul does not go to the Hells or the Heavens; it is indexed and archived.
