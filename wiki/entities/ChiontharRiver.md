## Overview
The Chionthar River is a major waterway in Baldur's Gate where the Nautiloid crashed. In the V5 campaigns, the crashed nautiloid lies in smoking ruins across the Chionthar River, and the Ravaged Beach is the shore where Nocturne washes up after the crash.
