## Overview

Aizen Vane is the player character in the Aizen Thay V1 campaign. He is the Abyss Knight, a Genetic Echo of Nocturne Sosuke. He operates within the Obsidian Ziggurat of Bezantur and serves the Sovereign through calculated dismantling and Soul Thief abilities.

## Key Details

- **Title:** Abyss Knight, Genetic Echo of Nocturne Sosuke
- **Level:** 5
- **Equipment:** Obsidian Mirror (connected to Shattered Rose)
- **Mission:** Tithe of Silt-River (punitive raid requiring 40 souls)
- **Codex:** "Codex of the Sovereign" defines his mechanics
