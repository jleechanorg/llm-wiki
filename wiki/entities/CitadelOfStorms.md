## Overview
The Citadel of Storms is the penthouse fortress of Emperor Vitiate on Dromund Kaas. A massive structure of dark stone and Sith architecture, it serves as the seat of power for the Sith Empire.

## Significance
This is where Nocturne's training and missions originate. The Citadel contains the Emperor's presence and the resources of Project Apotheosis.
