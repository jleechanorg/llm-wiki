## Summary
Social HP is a game mechanic representing an NPC's resistance to social interactions. Unlike physical HP, Social HP can be targeted through persuasion, deception, and intimidation. The Preventive Guards module provides ANTI-BLITZ PROTECTION to prevent players from spamming social interactions to rapidly drain NPC Social HP.

## Key Properties
- **Cooldown Enforcement** — SERVER-ENFORCED cooldown blocking prevents rapid-fire social attacks
- **Damage Capping** — Damage is capped at current HP to prevent over-damage exploits
- **Silent Integrity** — Social HP integrity enforcement operates silently - users never see corrections
