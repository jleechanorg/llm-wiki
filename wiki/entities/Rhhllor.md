## Summary
R'hllor is a Tier 1 Primordial deity — the Lord of Light, god of fire and shadow. In this campaign he is not an adversary to defeat but a cosmic partner to negotiate with. He descends to the Palace of the Stars in a bronze-skinned mortal avatar to witness Aegon's "logic over sacrifice" philosophy.

## Key Characteristics
- **Tier**: 1 Primordial (Lvl 50+)
- **Domain**: Fire, light, sacrifice, prophecy
- **Traditional Method**: Blood sacrifice and prayer to fight winter
- **Aegon's Counter-argument**: Industrial civilization conducts heat better than blood sacrifice; the "Silver Song" is a more efficient geometry

## Arc Summary
1. **Initial Contact**: Descends as golden solar eclipse; the air "screams" with his 95% Apex Attention
2. **Solar Eclipse Proposal**: Aegon proposes he provide raw heat while she provides geometric architecture — he accepts
3. **God-Marriage Witness**: Takes mortal avatar (bronze-skinned man with ember-eyes) to attend Aegon's wedding
4. **Prophecy Bestowal**: Seats the "Fire of Prophecy" in Daemon II Blackfyre's marrow
5. **Partnership**: Provides heat to stabilize the Wall and industrialize the Shivering Wastes

## Key Negotiations
- **Social Skill Challenge**: R'hllor's Social HP starts at 18/20 (Resisting). Aegon reduces it to 8/20 through Persuasion rolls.
- **Core Argument**: "You seek a Prince Who Was Promised, but your fire dissipates without a vessel. I have built the lens through which it must shine."
- **Nat 20 Moment**: On bestowing the prophetic spark, Aegon rolls Nat 20 + 54 + 15 = 89 vs DC 65 — Masterful Success

## Notable Quotes
