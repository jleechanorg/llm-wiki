## Profile

- **Full Name:** Nocturne
- **Titles:** The Fatebinder, The Ascendant Architect, The Ghost in the Machine, The Courtly Shadow, The Dispassionate Strategist
- **Age:** 16 (physically) / Ancient (intellectually)
- **Role:** Player Character
- **Class:** Bard (College of Swords) flavored as Wizard of the Order of Scribes
- **Level:** 16
- **Alignment:** Lawful Evil (The Systematizer)
- **Weapon:** Discordia (named blade)

## Character Overview

Nocturne is a Fatebinder—a judge/diplomat in the Tyranny setting—who presents as a 16-year-old but carries an ancient intellect. They are the "Ghost in the Machine": a deceptive grime-covered traveler whose eyes scan everything, tracking geometric flaws, caloric inefficiencies, and probability lines of Edict magic.

Nocturne recites mathematical proofs that force reality to correct itself. Their blade Discordia serves as a conductor for magic, conducting the flow of force the way a baton directs an orchestra.

## Two Masks

**1. The Courtly Shadow (Social Persona):**
Soft-spoken, hyper-competent, unassuming. Uses Johan Liebert-style mirroring to create dependency loops. Makes allies feel seen so deeply they lower mental defenses. Goal: "If I am essential, they cannot discard me."

**2. The Dispassionate Strategist (Crisis Persona):**
Cold, administrative evil. Tywin Lannister + Aizen Sosuke hybrid. Intellectualizes fear into systems and probability calculations. Combat bark: "Your stance is off by three degrees. You were dead before you swung."

## Core Drives

- **The "Hogyoku" Complex:** Must be useful/essential. Weaponized belief that friendship without utility signals parasitism.
- **The "Itachi" Experiment:** Clinical urge to stress-test raw talents like Verse—cruel mentorship designed to create dependency.
- **The Refactoring of Reality:** Seeks forbidden knowledge to "patch" Terratus—viewing the world as "full of bad code."

## Nocturne Variant Pattern

Many campaigns use a "Nocturne" character sharing the **Two-Masks/Snake-Eyes archetype** — a brilliant, manipulative INTJ with dual personalities. Key shared traits:

- **Personality mashup:** Tywin Lannister + Aizen Sosuke + Johan Liebert + Griffith
- **Two perfect masks:** Social persona (soft, essential) + Crisis persona (cold, administrative)
- **Core drive:** Must be useful/essential; views relationships through utility lens
- **Background:** Often orphaned/sold/exploited, uses grime as disguise to hide beauty
