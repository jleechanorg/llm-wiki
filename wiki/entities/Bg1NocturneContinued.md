## Description

A 54-scene BG1 campaign continuation following the events of the main BG1 Nocturne campaign. The character switched class from Bard to Gloom Stalker Ranger, beginning at Level 5 in the main campaign and continuing at Level 3 Ranger in this continuation. The campaign is set in 1368 DR during the Iron Crisis, in Baldur's Gate and the surrounding Sword Coast.

## Key Details

- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Class:** Gloom Stalker Ranger | Starting Level 3
- **Opening location:** Seven Suns Coster, 2nd Floor Proprietor's Office
- **Timestamp:** 1368 DR, Mirtul 16, Evening
- **Scenes:** 54

## Story

The continuation begins with Nocturne at Level 3 Ranger — a significant class pivot from the Bard progression of the main BG1 campaign. She is operating out of the Seven Suns Coster's 2nd floor office in Baldur's Gate, financially solvent (10,487.6gp at Scene 1), and running intelligence operations from the Harbor Master's Office.

By Scene 54 (the terminal scene), she remains at Level 3 Ranger with 38/38 HP, having accumulated 1,200 XP toward the 9,000 needed for Level 4. The campaign terminates at a relatively low level compared to the main BG1 Nocturne run, suggesting this continuation focused on a specific narrative arc rather than full progression.

The campaign uses standard BG1 D&D 5e mechanics with the Gloom Stalker subclass — shadow-based ambush tactics, favored enemy bonuses, and the characteristic "first strike" mechanics of the Gloom Stalker archetype.
