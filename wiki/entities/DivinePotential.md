## Definition
Numeric custom_campaign_state metric that tracks a character's proximity to divine ascension. Threshold: ≥100 triggers the [[DivineAscensionCeremony]].

## Properties
- **Threshold**: 100 triggers ascension ceremony
- **Related Metrics**: divine_potential accumulates through powerful deeds
- **Display**: Shown in character sheet alongside level and abilities
- **Alternative Triggers**: Level 25+ or narrative divine milestone also activate ceremony
