## Description
Nocturne Sosuke in the bug-repro-test campaign is the same character as in other Nocturne campaigns — a sixteen-year-old Half-Elf Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) at Level 5 with CHA 20. This variant is used specifically to test SYSTEM RESTORE functionality for restoring game state. The campaign focuses on the "Nerf" narrative where a Level 6 Abyssal Sovereign is trapped in a Level 5 cage.
