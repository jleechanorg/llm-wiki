## Description

Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village) is the primary shinobi village in the Naruto world. In this campaign, it serves as both Itachi's home and the target of his manipulation — he seeks to control the village from the shadows rather than rule it publicly.

## In This Campaign

After the Uchiha Clan massacre, Itachi infiltrates Konohagakure's power structures. His goal is not to become Hokage but to be "the unseen architect, the power behind the Hokage and the council." He manipulates village policies, dictates strategic direction, and ensures its strength as a tool for his ultimate vision of world domination.

## Significance

Konohagakure represents the "system" that Itachi seeks to control rather than destroy. Unlike the Uchiha, which he views as a chaotic element requiring excision, Konoha is a tool — powerful enough to serve his purposes if properly manipulated.
