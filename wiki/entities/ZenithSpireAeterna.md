## Profile

- **Name:** Zenith Spire, Aeterna
- **Type:** Location (magical fortress/capital)
- **Setting:** World of Assiah, Imperium
- **Role:** Imperial seat of power, home to House Arcanus

## Overview

The Zenith Spire is the central tower and seat of power in Aeterna, the capital of the Imperium. It houses the Imperial court, the Triumvirate's command, and the Deep Archives beneath its floors. The Spire is the political and magical heart of the realm—a fortress-tower where power is concentrated, secrets are kept, and the fate of the Imperium is decided.

In the Sariel V2 campaign, Sariel Arcanus's chambers are located in the House Arcanus wing of the Zenith Spire. The Deep Archives (a vault beneath the Spire) contains Alexiel's journals. The entire Spire is currently in mourning following Alexiel's death.

## Key Areas Within the Spire

- **House Arcanus Wing:** Where Sariel lives with her brothers
- **Deep Archives:** Secret vault beneath the Spire containing Alexiel's research journals
- **Imperial Court:** Where political maneuvering occurs
- **Command Level:** Where the Triumvirate (including Titus val Raziel) governs

## Significance to Campaign

The Zenith Spire is both home and battlefield for Sariel. It is where she must navigate the grief of the court, the whispers of faction members, and her own journey toward truth. The Spire's Deep Archives were her first stop on the path to understanding her mother's powers.
