## Overview

PromptLayer is a prompt registry platform with RBAC and SSO for enterprise prompt governance. It enables versioning prompts outside the codebase with release labels.

## Key Properties

- **Prompt Registry**: Versioning prompts outside codebase with release labels
- **RBAC**: Granular roles for engineers, operators, and reviewers
- **SSO**: Enterprise identity provider integration
- **Self-hosting**: On-premise deployment for compliance
- **Key quote**: "Enable secure team access with enterprise identity providers and centralized login flows"
