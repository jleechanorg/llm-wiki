## Description

The Council of Four is one of the major powers in post-Baldur's Gate 3 Baldur's Gate. This faction is in a state of reformation following the events of the game, working to restore order and governance to the city after the chaos of the Absolute's defeat.

## In This Campaign

The Council of Four represents the legitimate authority structure that Gladius operates around and potentially against. As a mercenary working for various employers, he may find himself in opposition to or in service of the Council's interests. The campaign's political landscape is shaped by the Council's reformation and the power vacuum left by the Absolute's defeat.

## Significance

The Council of Four represents the "legitimate" power structure that Gladius resents and seeks to subvert. His hidden identity as a disowned noble gives him a personal stake in the political dynamics of the city, even as he presents himself as an apolitical mercenary.
