## Overview
Ancient British tribe in eastern England, led by Queen Boudica. The Iceni were a prosperous Celtic tribe who eventually rebelled against Roman occupation.

## In-Game Context
The player's tribe and people. Boudica was the queen of the Iceni before and during the rebellion against Rome.

## Historical Background
- Location: Modern-day Norfolk and Suffolk, England
- Capital: Probably near modern-day Norwich
- Economy: Agriculture, horse breeding, trade
- Known for: Resistance to Roman occupation

## In Story
The Iceni people follow Boudica in her rebellion against Roman forces. Their lands were invaded and their royal family humiliated by Roman occupiers, sparking the uprising.
