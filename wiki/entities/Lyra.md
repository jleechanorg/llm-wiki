## Description

Lyra is Nocturne's younger sister, age 14, rescued from the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual. She was held at Sunder-Stone Villa as "Refined Collateral" alongside her sister Elara. In the opening scene, Lyra is described as "a ghost of herself, staring at [Nocturne's] glowing palms with a quiet, hollow terror."

## Role in the Campaign

Lyra represents the most vulnerable member of Nocturne's family—the innocent caught in the machinery of the Cassalanter's infernal scheme. Her hollow terror at seeing Nocturne's ember-glow palms suggests she recognizes the cost of their rescue: Nocturne now carries the mark of the infernal power they escaped.

## Relationship with Nocturne

Lyra's young age makes her the most innocent casualty of the Cassalanter's predation. Her reaction to Nocturne's transformation—from victim to proxy of Avernus—filters through fear and confusion rather than the pragmatic acceptance that Elara demonstrates.
