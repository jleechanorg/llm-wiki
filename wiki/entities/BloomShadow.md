## Overview
The Bloom Shadow is an Upper City brothel in Baldur's Gate run by Madam Zylra. In the Nocturne campaigns, it serves as the primary setting where Nocturne Sosuke was held captive after being sold by the Cassalanter Family. The establishment caters to wealthy clients including Horgus Gwent, who brings the inciting incident that drives the V4 revenge plot.
