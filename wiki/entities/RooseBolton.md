## Summary
Roose Bolton (Level 12) is the lord of the Dreadfort and Visenya's key military ally. Described as "a fellow Vivisector" — a Northern lord who mirrors Visenya's own approach to cruelty but lacks her elegance. His flaying expertise and psychological warfare capabilities make him a grim complement to her information geometry. In the endgame, he begins playing both sides — corresponding secretly with Tywin Lannister and the Night King.

## Key Character Traits
- **The Quiet Sadist**: "A quiet, sadistic terror that mirrors your own, but lacks your elegance"
- **Calculating**: Has "seen your wildfire and calculated that you are a high-risk asset"
- **Pragmatic betrayal**: Will peel away from Visenya when the audit weakens her

## Key Moments
- **The Dreadfort Alliance**: Formalized early in the campaign; becomes Visenya's forward command post
- **The Flayed Writ**: Delivered via Bolton riders — Ser Marlon Manderly's finger with his signet ring
- **Endgame betrayal**: Secret correspondence with Tywin Lannister and the Night King
