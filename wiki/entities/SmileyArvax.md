## Overview
Smiley Arvax is a Guild lieutenant in the Old Nocturne Merc BG3 campaign. They serve as the contact point for Nocturne, assigning contracts and managing the mercenary's activities.

## Significance
Despite the name "Smiley," Arvax is a dangerous criminal operator. They represent the Guild's interface with hired muscle and facilitate the kidnapping contract.
