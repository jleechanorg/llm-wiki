## Overview

House Sosuke is a noble house in Baldur's Gate known for ruthless efficiency. It is currently led by Lord Kaito, who appears to be in charge, while the true architect is his son Sosuke Aizen operating from the shadows. The house is a pillar of the city's establishment, though Aizen uses it for his own dark purposes.

## Key Details

- **Leader (public):** Lord Kaito
- **True Leader:** Sosuke Aizen
- **Reputation:** Ruthless efficiency, values steel over scrolls
- **Assets:** The White Garden (secretly owned by Aizen)
