## Description

House Sosuke is the accursed noble house that recurs across multiple campaigns as the lineage of both Nocturne and Aizen. In the BG3 timeline, they are a Baldur's Gate noble family. In the Thay timeline, they operate under the alias "House Vane" while serving Red Wizard interests. After Nocturne's ascension and subsequent sealing by Ao, the house was hunted to extinction by fearful gods and rival lords — making every Sosuke descendant a "debris of that collapse."

## Appears In

- [[aizen-bg3]] — House Sosuke as Baldur's Gate nobility; Aizen adopted into it
- [[aizen-thay]] — House Vane (secretly Sosuke) in Bezantur, Thay; five Great Houses as rivals
- [[nocturne-bg3]] — Nocturne as scion; House Valerius variant
- [[aizen-godhood-continued]] — Continuation of the Sosuke bloodline's ascent

## Key Traits

- **The Accursed Bloodline:** Gods and rivals hunt survivors; carrying the name is a death sentence
- **Singularity Descendants:** Members tend toward extreme power potential (Nocturne: Level 60+; Aizen: godhood-aspirant)
- **Weaponized Beauty:** The Sosuke line produces unnaturally attractive individuals
- **Patron Bond:** The Obsidian Mirror connecting Sosuke descendants to the sealed Sovereign
- **Exile as Origin:** Every Sosuke PC begins in exile — cast out for being "too dangerous"
- **Five Great Houses of Thay (Rivals):** Thul, Nym, Dydra, Agneh, Kren
