## Overview

Aegon Targaryen is the player character of the Aemon Game of Thrones campaign. He is a secret heir to the Iron Throne who disguises himself as a sellsword while carrying the blood of old Valyria. Despite his noble lineage, he operates in the shadows as a member of The Mountain's mercenary company.

## Key Details

- **Character Class:** Level 5 Ranger (Gloomstalker subclass)
- **Alignment:** Chaotic evil with sadistic tendencies (god mode)
- **Age:** 18
- **Resources:** HP: 44/44, XP: 6500/14000, Gold: 25gp
- **Abilities:** Shadow-walking, thrive in darkness, Wisdom-based persuasion
- **Physical Description:** Extremely beautiful, carries Valyrian blood

## Campaign Role

Aegon serves under Ser Gregor Clegane ("The Mountain") as an audition for his men. His secret claim to the Iron Throne could either set the world on fire or get him silenced forever. The player requested missions involving pillaging, raiding, enslavement, and assassination against the "good" characters of Westeros.
