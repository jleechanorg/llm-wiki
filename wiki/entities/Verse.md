## Profile

- **Full Name:** Verse
- **Title:** Fury of the Scarlet Chorus
- **Role:** Key NPC, target of Nocturne's "Itachi Experiment"
- **Faction:** Scarlet Chorus
- **Setting:** The Tiers, Terratus

## Character Overview

Verse is a Fury of the Scarlet Chorus—the chaotic, numerous army led by the Voices of Nerat. She is described as raw, talented, emotional, and messy: a fighter who uses passion and fury rather than technique. In Nocturne's worldview, she represents the "Sloppy Success" he hates—the kind of talent that succeeds without the rigorous study and optimization he demands of himself.

Verse first appears at the Edgering Ruins, emerging from shadows with fluid, predatory movements. She watches Nocturne with "sharp, dangerous curiosity" and shows no fear—only a challenge that is both a question and a promise.

## The "Itachi Experiment"

Verse is the subject of Nocturne's pathological need to stress-test raw talent. Nocturne looks at her and sees a project, not a person. His "evil mentor" compulsion drives him to create situations that test her endurance and will—putting her in impossible positions to see if she has the "hatred" or "will" required to survive.
