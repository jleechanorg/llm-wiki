## Overview
Nocturne is the player character in the Nocturne Post BG3 Zhent campaign. A Level 5 Ranger (potentially Beast Master) with Zhentarim connections, they operate in the post-war criminal landscape of Baldur's Gate.

## Attributes
- **Level:** 5
- **Class:** Ranger (Beast Master?)
- **Affiliations:** Potential Zhentarim
- **Base:** Elfsong Tavern Penthouse

## Background
Operating from the Elfsong Tavern Penthouse in Baldur's Gate's Upper City, Nocturne navigates the power vacuum left by the Absolute's defeat.
