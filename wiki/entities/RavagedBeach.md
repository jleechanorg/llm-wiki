## Overview
The Ravaged Beach is the site of the crashed Nautiloid in Baldur's Gate 3, where the campaign begins after the mind flyer crashes. This location serves as the starting point for the V5 and V6 campaigns, featuring the Intellect Devourer combat and initial Shadowheart encounter.
