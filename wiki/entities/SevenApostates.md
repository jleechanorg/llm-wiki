## Overview
The Seven Apostates are Lucifer's seven generals who led the divine rebellion during the Celestial Wars.

## Members
1. **Lucifer** — Morning Star, overall leader
2. **Samael** — The Unbeliever
3. **Abaddon** — Unchained Fury
4. **Lord Marshal Xylos** — Crimson Flame
5. **Leliel** — The Whispering Prince
6. **Uriel** — The Broken Wall
7. **Azrael** — The First Sword

## Key Facts
- Role: Generals of the divine rebellion
- Affiliation: [[ShatteredHost]]
- Era: Celestial Wars
