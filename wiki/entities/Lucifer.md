## Overview
Lucifer, also known as the Morning Star, was the leader of the divine rebellion known as the Celestial Wars that shattered the cosmic order. His uprising against the celestial hierarchy created the current multi-polar world of Assiah.

## Affiliation
- Leader of the [[ShatteredHost]]
- Original leader of the [[SevenApostates]]

## Key Facts
- Title: Morning Star
- Role: Leader of divine rebellion
- Status: Leading scattered remnants post-war
- Opposes: [[CelestialImperium]]
