## Overview

Ser Gregor Clegane, known as "The Mountain," is one of the most feared men in Westeros. He is the commander of a band of sellswords and the lord of Clegane's keep in the Westerlands. He cares nothing for lineage or honor, only for efficiency of slaughter and the silence of the dead.

## Key Details

- **Title:** Ser Gregor Clegane, "The Mountain"
- **Known For:** Extreme violence, size, and brutality
- **Operating Area:** Westerlands
- **Men:** Mercenary company ("Mountain's Men")
- **Attitude Toward Aegon:** Testing Aegon through audition
