# House Lannister

Golden opulence masking deep insecurity. Led by Tywin Lannister. Specialty: economic dominance and political extortion.

## Key Hook
Tywin's obsession with legacy makes him highly susceptible to deals that guarantee his bloodline's prominence. The "Lion's Pact" is blackmail leverage Visenya holds over Tywin.
