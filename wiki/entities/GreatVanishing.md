Cataclysmic event where The Creator and all pure-blooded Seraphim disappeared from reality. Defines the boundary between Age of Dominion and Age of Rebellion.

## Description
Ended the divine order era without explanation. Left half-celestial children (Empyrean bloodline) to inherit a fracturing world.
