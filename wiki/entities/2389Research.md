## Overview
2389 Research, Inc. is an AI integration startup founded by Harper Reed and Dylan Richard in June 2024 in Chicago. They built three independent Attractor implementations — Mammoth, Smasher, and Tracker — all converging on the same three-layer architecture.

## Key Properties
- **Type**: AI research startup
- **Key features**: Three Attractor implementations, DOT-pipeline expertise, Dippin language
- **Founded**: June 2024, Chicago
- **Founders**: Harper Reed, Dylan Richard
- **Products**: Mammoth (Go pipeline runner), Smasher (Rust pipeline runner), Tracker (Go orchestration engine), dotpowers, Coven
