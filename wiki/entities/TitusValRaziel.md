## Profile

- **Full Name:** High Commander Titus val Raziel
- **Title:** Leader of the Triumvirate, High Commander of the Imperial Legions
- **Role:** Military leader, protector of Alexiel's children
- **Archetype:** The Grieving Guardian
- **Alignment:** Lawful Good
- **MBTI:** ESTP

## Character Overview

Titus val Raziel is the High Commander of the Imperial Legions and leader of the Triumvirate that governs the Imperium. He had come to see Alexiel Arcanus as a sister, and her death has devastated him. Now he serves as the sole protector of her children and legacy, trying to hold the shaken Imperium together while it threatens to fracture into factions vying for power.

He guards Alexiel's children—particularly Sariel—with fierce determination, having been one of the few who knew the truth of her demigod nature. His grief is tempered by duty; he must be strong for the realm even as he mourns.

## Role in the Campaign

Titus represents the last bastion of stability in an Imperium thrown into mourning and political turmoil. He is torn between protecting Sariel from the dangers of investigating her mother's death and supporting her right to know the truth. When Sariel demands to be taken to the Quiet Ward to see Sergeant Kaelan, Kantos knows that only Titus's authority could authorize such access—but Sariel's determination rivals that of her mother, and ultimately the decision falls to her.

## Key Characteristics

- **Military Prowess:** Veteran commander of the Imperial Legions, experienced in both battlefield tactics and political navigation
- **Protective Instinct:** Views Alexiel's children as his sacred charge
- **Grief-Strength duality:** Weakened by loss but sustained by duty
- **Moral Center:** Lawful Good alignment reflects genuine concern for the realm and its people
