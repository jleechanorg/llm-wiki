## Overview
The Crimson Kiss is an emerging cult faction centered around Nocturne, formed in the post-campaign aftermath. Drawing on her dual heritage of Malcanthet and Bhaal, the cult represents the convergence of demonic allure and divine murder.

## Background
As Nocturne solidified her power over the Shadow Grove and the Absolute remnants scattered, followers began to gather around her — attracted by promises of transformation and liberation from the old order.
