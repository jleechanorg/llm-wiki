## Description

The Praetorian Hand is an elite group of five veteran soldiers assigned to protect Gaia Julia Caesar in this campaign. They serve as her shield, her scalpel, and her fist — bound to her by blood and oath. Their members are Valerius, Felix, Titus, Cassius, and Brutus.

## In This Campaign

These five elite veterans operate as Gaia's personal guard and field operatives. While she operates under cover as a servant among mercenaries, the Praetorian Hand remains close at hand to provide protection and execute her directives. They represent the Imperial interest in the Spartacus revolt region and serve as Gaia's connection to Rome.
