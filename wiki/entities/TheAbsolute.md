## Overview
The Absolute is a cult in Baldur's Gate 3 led by the Dark Urge, seeking to bring the elder brain's fl mind to Baldur's Gate. In the Nocturne campaigns, goblin forces serve the Absolute as "True Souls," and the tadpole suppresses Nocturne's power in V5-V6, making her appear as a potential recruit.
