The DragonsTooth Mountains are a treacherous mountain range serving as a boundary between the territories of the Celestial Imperium and the Unchained Host. Named for their jagged, fang-like peaks that pierce the sky, these mountains are home to scattered settlements and dangerous wildlife. The range provides strategic importance in the fractured world of Assiah, acting as a natural barrier that divides the Eastern and Western powers. Many travelers attempt to cross these peaks to move between territories, but the journey is fraught with danger from both environmental hazards and hostile forces patrolling the passes.
