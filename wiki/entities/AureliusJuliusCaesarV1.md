Aurelius Julius Caesar V1 is a Level 5 Paladin following the Oath of Domination, with exceptional stats of INT 18 and CHA 18. He operates in Northern Hispania as a conquistador figure, ruling through strength and authority rather than consent. His high intelligence and charisma make him a formidable leader and manipulator. He commands Legio IX Hispana, one of the Roman legions stationed in the Iberian Peninsula, and seeks to control the strategically important Vindius Pass. He wields Veritas, a +1 Damascus Gladius that symbolizes his commitment to truth through power.
