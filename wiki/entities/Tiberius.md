## Profile

- **Full Name:** Tiberius
- **Title:** Son of Marcus Crassus, Roman Patrician
- **Role:** Player Character
- **Class:** Modified Paladin (Legion's Vindicator)
- **Level:** 3
- **Background:** Noble (Crassus Family)
- **Race:** Human (Variant)

## Character Overview

Tiberius is a young Roman noble with a taste for cruelty, skilled in swordplay, deception, and command. Son of the powerful Marcus Crassus, he is entangled in the brutal world of Roman politics, gladiatorial arenas, and Spartacus' slave rebellion. The campaign is inspired by the Spartacus TV series (War of the Damned), and Tiberius embodies the ruthless ambition and political cunning of Roman nobility.

## Character Build (Level 3 Approved)

**Attributes:** STR 16 (+3), DEX 12 (+1), CON 13 (+1), INT 10, WIS 8 (-1), CHA 15 (+2)
**HP:** 28 | **AC:** 18 (Lorica segmentata + shield)
**Feat:** Sentinel
**Skills:** Athletics, Intimidation, Persuasion, History

**Combat:**
- Fighting Style: Dueling (+2 damage with gladius/shield)
- Divine Smite: 2d8 radiant "ancestral fury" (3 uses/short rest)
- Lay on Hands: 15 HP pool
- Oath: Conquest (reskinned "Oath of Rome")
- Channel Divinity: Conquering Presence (frighten), Guided Strike (+10 attack)

**Equipment:** Lorica segmentata, Gladius, Shield, Javelins x4, 250gp, healing potions, superior healer's kit

## Role in the Campaign

Tiberius navigates the political and military conflict between Rome and Spartacus's slave rebellion. As a Modified Paladin without traditional spell slots, he represents Roman virtue through oath-bound combat rather than divine magic.
