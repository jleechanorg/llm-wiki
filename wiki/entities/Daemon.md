## Description
Daemon is a brute-strength knight of evil alignment whose ultimate ambition is to become ruler and king. Set in a land of dragons, this evil protagonist seeks to rise through strength and dominate.

## Background
Daemon is a knight of considerable physical prowess and commanding presence. His Evil alignment drives him toward domination — the desire to command, to rule, to possess a throne. This is not petty villainy but grand ambition: conquest and kingship.

## Personality
Daemon is ambitious, commanding, and physically dominant. He relies on raw power rather than subtlety or magic. He views the world as a hierarchy where the strong rule the weak, and he intends to reach the top.

## Capabilities
- **Class:** Knight (brute-strength build)
- **Alignment:** Evil
- **System:** Destiny Core Rules

## Campaign Role
Protagonist of the daemon-conquers campaign, set in "a land of dragons."
