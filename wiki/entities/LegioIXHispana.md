Legio IX Hispana is the Roman military legion stationed in Hispania under Aurelius's command. This legion has a distinguished history of service in the Iberian Peninsula and serves as Aurelius's primary instrument of conquest. The legion consists of heavily disciplined Roman soldiers equipped with the best equipment available, including gladius swords and scuta shields. Under Aurelius's command, the legion pursues expansion objectives in northern Spain, seeking to control key positions like Vindius Pass. The troops benefit from Aurelius's high stats and paladin abilities, which boost their morale and combat effectiveness.
