## Description
The Shadow Monarch is Nocturne at Level 8 Bard (College of Swords) with Neutral Evil alignment. She operates from a captured Moonrise Ship at Wyrm's Tooth, having built an empire through ruthless strategy. Her stats when sated (The Hunger mechanic) are: STR 12 (+1), DEX 20 (+5), CON 16 (+3), INT 18 (+4), WIS 16 (+3), CHA 24 (+7). She uses God-Mode mechanics including Omni-Attunement (5 items attuned, limit broken) and the Whispering Idol to override Tadpole control.
