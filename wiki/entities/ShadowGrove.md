## Overview
The Shadow Grove is a druidic grove in the Shadowfell,formerly the Emerald Grove before Nocturne inverted the Rite of Thorns. Now the domain of Arch-Druid Nocturne, it has been transformed from a place of light and nature into something darker and more powerful.

## History
Originally known as the Emerald Grove, the location was tied to the Rite of Thorns — a ritual that protected the natural world. Nocturne's inversion of this rite claimed the grove as her own, transforming its essence.
