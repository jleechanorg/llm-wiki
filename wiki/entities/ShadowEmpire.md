## Description
The Shadow Empire is the collection of assets and operations controlled by the Shadow Monarch (Nocturne). It represents a growing network of financial, political, and military power that Nocturne has built through ruthless strategy. The empire operates across multiple domains in Baldur's Gate and beyond.

## Key Details
- Category: Faction / Criminal Empire
- Leader: [[ShadowMonarch]]
- Status: Growing/Active
- Campaign appearances: [[nocturne-bg3-continued-campaign]]
