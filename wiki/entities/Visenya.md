## Profile

- **Full Name:** Visenya
- **Title:** Squire (secretly: Targaryen Princess)
- **Role:** Player Character
- **Class:** Dragon Scholar (custom INT/WIS spellcaster)
- **Level:** 1
- **Setting:** Seven Kingdoms of Westeros, Year 209 AC (Spring)

## Character Overview

Visenya is a young girl serving as a squire to the hedge knight Ser Duncan the Tall. She secretly carries Targaryen royal blood beneath her humble straw hat, traveling the Seven Kingdoms incognito. In a reimagined version of George R.R. Martin's Dunk and Egg stories where women can be squires, Visenya is the equivalent of "Egg"—just female.

The character requested a female Targaryen name (choosing Visenya, after the legendary Targaryen queen) and a custom Dragon Scholar class focused on high INT and WIS, with full spellcaster progression and Subtle Spell as a class feature.

## Character Build

**Attributes:** INT 16, WIS 16, DEX 14, CHA (remaining), CON 8
**Class Features:** Full spellcaster progression, INT-based, CHA checks substitute INT/WIS modifier
**Planned:** Dragon companion at Level 6 (narratively earned, growing and leveling with Visenya)

## The Secret Heritage

Like Egg in the original Dunk and Egg stories, Visenya travels incognito as a hedge knight's squire while secretly being of royal Targaryen blood. This secret identity creates tension between her humble present and her potentially royal future, and shapes how others perceive and treat her.
