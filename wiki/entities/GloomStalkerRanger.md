## Description
The Gloom Stalker is a Ranger subclass in D&D 5e that draws power from the Shadowfell, specializing in stealth, ambush tactics, and operating in darkness. It is the signature class of Nocturne in the bg1-nocturne campaign.

## Features

### Dread Ambusher
At 3rd level, the Gloom Stalker gains advantage on initiative rolls and can make an extra attack on the first turn of combat. This represents their ability to strike from the shadows before enemies can react.

### Umbral Sight
The Gloom Stalker can see in magical darkness without disadvantage. They are effectively invisible to creatures that rely on darkvision, gaining immunity to the blinded condition in darkness.

### Mind's Eye
At higher levels, the subclass gains abilities to become more shadow-like, including the ability to cast Greater Invisibility as a spell.

## Campaign Usage
Nocturne uses the Gloom Stalker Ranger class in the bg1-nocturne campaign, combining the subclass with her Lawful Evil "Serpent Queen" persona for maximum predatory effectiveness.
