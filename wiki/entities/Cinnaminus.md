## Description

Cinnaminus is one of Gaia Julia Caesar's companions in the Gaia Julia V2 campaign. As part of "The Twenty" (Gaia's elite disguised guards), Cinnaminus serves the young Caesar scion in the alternate Roman setting of 82 BCE.

## Role in the Campaign

In the Gaia Julia V2 campaign (The Golden Republic / Pax Julia Alternate), Cinnaminus accompanies Gaia through the gladiator ludus of Capua and the political machinations of Rome. The campaign centers on the Julian family's "70% Rule" - dominance over 70% of Rome.
