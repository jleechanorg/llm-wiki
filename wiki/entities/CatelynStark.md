## Summary
Catelyn Stark (Level 4) is the wife of Eddard Stark and mother to Robb, Sansa, and Arya. Her visceral hatred of Jon Snow (the reminder of Ned's infidelity) is a known vulnerability Visenya exploits. By the endgame, she is broken — kneeling in the Winterfell great hall after being forced to witness her husband's murder and her son's parricidal conversion.

## Key Character Traits
- **Sharp, instinctive distrust**: First to sense Visenya as a threat ("a southern viper in my home")
- **Mother's hatred**: Her venom toward Jon is a "rot" that Visenya pokes throughout
- **Broken by endgame**: The brand of the three-headed dragon on her shoulder; Visenya's last torture was asking her to describe the world she would build if SHE were queen

## Key Moments
- **The Distrust**: Opposes Visenya from the start, but is gradually won over by Sansa's adoration
- **The Branding**: Forced to kneel while Robb brands her; Ned watches in chains
- **Endgame**: The last remains of Northern resistance in Winterfell are unspooled

## Stats
- **Level**: 4 (Aristocrat)
- **House**: Tully by birth, Stark by marriage
