## Overview
Zevlor is a druid leader at the Emerald Grove gates in the V6 campaign. He serves as the initial contact when Nocturne arrives at the Grove gates in her "porcelain victim" infiltration act. He provides intelligence about the gate mechanism, Shadow Druid radicalization, and the Sacred Pool's location.
