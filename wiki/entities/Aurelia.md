Aurelia is the wife of Aurelius Julia Caesar V2, originally a slave who was rescued by Aurelius from captivity. Her past as a slave fuels her husband's anti-slavery crusade and represents the personal stakes of his moral battle against the Roman Republic's institution of slavery. She provides emotional support and motivation for Aurelius's fight against the systemic injustice of the time. Her rescue serves as a turning point in Aurelius's life, solidifying his commitment to the anti-slavery cause.
