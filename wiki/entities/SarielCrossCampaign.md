## Description

Sariel is a recurring character who appears in radically different settings — from the fantasy world of Assiah as a grieving wizard-scholar (Sariel Arcanus), to modern-day Wyoming as a 16-year-old FBI prodigy/vigilante (Hunting Party), to a killer variant. Despite the setting shifts, the core identity persists: a brilliant, beautiful young woman with a terrible secret and a dual identity (masked vs. true level).

## Appears In

- [[sariel-assiah]] — Sariel Arcanus, Level 1 Wizard; "The Echo of Starlight"; INTP; daughter of Alexiel; navigating Assiah during Lucifer's Thousand Year War
- [[hunting-party-sariel]] — Sariel, Level 9 Rogue/Ranger (Masked: Level 1); FBI prodigy who has secretly executed 100 criminals; The Pit setting
- [[sariel-killer]] — Killer variant iteration

## Key Traits

- **Unearthly Beauty:** Polarizing attractiveness across all iterations; used as camouflage
- **Masked Power / Smurfing:** Hides true capabilities (Level 9 appearing as Level 1 in Hunting Party; suppressed magic in Assiah)
- **INT-Based Profiling:** Uses intellect rather than instinct; calculates trajectories, deduces patterns
- **Vigilante Double Life:** Federal authority by day, lethal operator by night
- **The Terrible Secret:** Each iteration carries a hidden truth that would destroy her if revealed
- **INTP Core:** Analytical, introverted, driven by curiosity and systems understanding
