## Description
Ashwood Keep is a fortress on the edge of the Frost-Veil Forest in the Winter-Mourn Province. It serves as the site where Ser Arion val Valerion confronts a moral dilemma: Lady Annalise Ashwood has been sheltering refugees in defiance of the Stability Mandate, and Prefect Gratian has been sent to pacify the keep.

## Significance
For Arion, Ashwood Keep represents the collision between the perfect world he was raised in and the imperfect reality he now faces. The keep is where his understanding of loyalty, duty, and conscience will be tested.
