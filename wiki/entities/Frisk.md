## Character Overview

Frisk is a young human child who fell through a hole into Mt. Ebott and entered the world of monsters known as the Underground. Their soul shines with a unique red brilliance and is fueled by pure DETERMINATION. In the Undertale campaign, every step Frisk takes decides the fate of both monsters and humans alike.

The Determination Adept class prioritizes social interaction and survival over direct combat. High Charisma reflects the ability to "Act" and find peaceful resolutions; Wisdom allows sensing the true nature of monsters encountered.

## The DETERMINATION Soul

Determination is the central thematic force of the Undertale campaign. It is described as a tangible force that keeps the soul whole even in the face of death. It allows Frisk to persist where others would fade and is the engine of save/load mechanics that give the player agency over fate itself.

The campaign's core distinction is between the Mercy path (using Persuasion/Insight to resolve encounters through dialogue) and the Kill path (combat resolution).
