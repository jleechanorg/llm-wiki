The Tower of Joy is a legendary location in the red mountains of Dorne, where Ser Arthur Dayne made his final stand during Robert's rebellion. This ancient tower, forgotten by most in Westeros, became the site of one of the most famous battles in recent history. Arthur Dayne and two of his Kingsguard brothers defended this tower against Lord Eddard Stark and six of his companions. The battle resulted in only Ned Stark and Howland Reed surviving, with Arthur Dayne falling after a legendary duel. The tower itself is a symbol of honor and the tragic cost of blind loyalty.
