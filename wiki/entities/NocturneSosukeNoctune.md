## Description
Nocturne Sosuke, also known as the "Black Diamond," is a sixteen-year-old Half-Elf Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) at Level 5 with CHA 20. Her skin has an unsettlingly flawless, marble-like texture, and her violet eyes reflect torchlight with glass-like clarity. Her signature scent is ozone and brine, and she displaces the air in any room she enters. She is a de-powered goddess in a mortal cage, with the tadpole in her brain the only thing keeping the dam from breaking.
