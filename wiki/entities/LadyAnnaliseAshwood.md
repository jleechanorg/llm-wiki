## Description

Lady Annalise Ashwood is the March Lord of Ashwood Keep in the Winter-Mourn Province. She is a noble who has prioritized humanity over imperial law, sheltering refugees that the Empire views as "parasitic drains on resources." Her defying the Stability Mandate makes her a target for elimination under Empress Sariel's Silent Peace.

## Role in the Campaign

In the Dragon Knight Good campaign, Lady Annalise is the focal point of the player's moral dilemma. She represents the compassionate alternative to imperial authority—choosing to help the vulnerable rather than follow the cold logic of the Stability Mandate. The player's first mission is essentially to kill or disperse these refugees she has sheltered.

The refugees in the valley below Ashwood Keep form a pathetic sight: "ragged tents, shivering families huddled around small fires." Lady Annalise's choice to shelter them has marked her for death.

## Background

As a March Lord, Lady Annalise holds significant local authority but operates under the Imperial framework. Her decision to prioritize human compassion over Imperial mandate represents a rare crack in the Silent Peace's otherwise absolute control.
