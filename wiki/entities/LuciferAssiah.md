## Overview

Lucifer, called "The Morning Star," is the charismatic and nihilistic leader of the Unchained Host in the Alexiel Assiah campaign (Year 78 DR). He designed Alexiel to be the ultimate weapon of the rebellion. His multi-species army of orcs, beast-kin, and fallen angels seeks to dismantle the divine order. Alexiel was his "daughter" - a biological construct - until her defection.

## Key Details

- **Title:** The Morning Star
- **Role:** Leader of the Unchained Host
- **Personality:** Charismatic, nihilistic
- **Creation:** Designed Alexiel as ultimate weapon
- **Army:** Multi-species (orcs, beast-kin, fallen angels)
