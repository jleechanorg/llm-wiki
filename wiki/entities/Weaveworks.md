## Overview

Weaveworks is a GitOps platform for Kubernetes deployment and policy management. It pioneered GitOps — Git-centric deployment with approval workflows.

## Key Properties

- **GitOps**: Git-centric deployment with approval workflows
- **Kubernetes**: Container orchestration and deployment
- **Policy management**: Git-based policy enforcement
- **Flux**: Weaveworks created Flux CD tool (now a CNCF project)
