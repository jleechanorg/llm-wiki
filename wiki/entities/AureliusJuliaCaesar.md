## Description

Aurelius Julia Caesar is the "Sun of Rome" — the youngest and most ambitious son of the living God-Emperor Julius Caesar in an alternate timeline where the Ides of March failed. He is a Lawful Evil Decadent Conqueror whose Auctoritas manifests as Heat, and whose beauty is a biological weapon. His campaign (980+ entries in v1) explores the fratricidal cold war among the Julian siblings for succession.

## Key Traits

- **Auctoritas (The Golden Gravity):** CHA as physical force; can use CHA instead of STR for Intimidation and Strength saves
- **The Miasma:** Spending spell slots causes skin to glow with sunlight; allies gain temp HP, enemies can't look directly
- **The Decadent Conqueror (ESFP-ENTJ):** Public warmth hiding Lawful Evil entitlement; tyranny made fun
- **Blood of Venus:** Supernatural beauty that "stops conversations"; beauty as armor
- **Oath of Domination (Custom Paladin):** Aura of Submission (enemies Speed 0), Aura of Gravity (difficult terrain for enemies)
- **Apotheosis Path:** 5-tier progression from Tribune to God-Emperor (Level 30: becomes Lesser Deity)
- **Hedonistic Urge Mechanic:** Exhaustion if denied victory; "Divine Fervor" buff on major submissions
- **The Julian Vipers:** Family dynamics with Caesarion, Octavian, Julia Major, Julia Minor
