## Definition
A multiversal faction organization that a player's organization elevates to upon achieving Sovereign Protocol status. The Hegemony represents the player's faction extended across multiple universes.

## Structure
- **Leader**: The Sovereign
- **Members**: Sub-Deities (elevated NPCs with portfolios)
- **Scope**: Multiple universes under unified command

## Sub-Deity System
Sub-Deities are key NPCs elevated to divine status with assigned portfolios:
- **War**: Military operations and combat strategy
- **Logistics**: Resource management and supply chains
- **Culture**: Propaganda and societal control
- **Research**: Technology and magical advancement
