Veritas is a +1 Damascus Gladius wielded by Aurelius Julius Caesar V1, representing his commitment to truth through power. The blade is crafted using the traditional Damascus steel technique, creating distinctive layered patterns in the metal. This weapon combines the Roman gladius design with superior materials and enchantments, providing +1 to attack and damage rolls. The name Veritas (Latin for "truth") reflects Aurelius's philosophy that power brings clarity and that truth is established through strength rather than negotiation.
