## Description
The D&D 5th Edition System Reference Document (SRD) is the baseline mechanical framework for WorldArchitect.AI campaigns. It establishes that SRD rules override narrative preferences, ensuring consistent mechanical resolution across all game interactions.

## Key Properties
- **Mechanical Authority**: SRD rules take precedence over narrative
- **Six Abilities**: STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA
- **Proficiency System**: Level-based bonus scaling from +2 to +6
- **Standardized Formulas**: Attack, Save, Skill, Spell DC all follow consistent pattern
