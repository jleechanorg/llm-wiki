## Description

Voros is one of Gaia Julia Caesar's companions in the Gaia Julia V2 campaign. As part of the "The Twenty" (Gaia's elite disguised guards), Voros serves as a loyal follower of the young Caesar scion in the alternate Roman setting of 82 BCE.

## Role in the Campaign

In the Gaia Julia V2 campaign (The Golden Republic / Pax Julia Alternate), Voros accompanies Gaia as she maneuvers through the political landscape of Capua and Rome. The campaign focuses on political manipulation, building a power base in blood, and the Julian family's dominance of 70% of Rome ("70% Rule").
