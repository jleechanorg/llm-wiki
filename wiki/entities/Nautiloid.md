## Description
The Nautiloid is a mind flayer ship — a colossal organic vesselcrew by illithids. It serves as the opening setting for the bg3-astarion campaign, where Astarion and other potential companions find themselves captive aboard during the escape from the module's beginning.

## Significance
The Nautiloid represents Astarion's transition from Cazador's servitude to a new kind of danger. It's where he first encounters other potential companions (Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Gale, Wyll, Karlach, Halsin) and begins his journey toward freedom and power.
