## Overview
The Goblin Camp is occupied by the Absolute's goblin forces under Minthara's command. In the V6 campaign, Nocturne is held here as a "True Soul" prisoner, using her position to infiltrate the Emerald Grove from within. The camp serves as the base for the goblin assault on the Emerald Grove.
