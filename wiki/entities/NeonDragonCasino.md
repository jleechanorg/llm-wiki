## Description

The Neon Dragon Casino & Resort is a luxury gambling establishment in this modern-noir campaign. It serves as Sariel's current place of employment, where she works as a profiler and security specialist. The casino represents the intersection of wealth, crime, and entertainment that characterizes this campaign's setting.

## In This Campaign

The campaign opens at the Neon Dragon Casino & Resort, where Sariel's unique skills as a former "Blood Dragon" operative have been recognized. The FBI, struggling to track escaped serial killers, has approached her to use her talents in a civilian capacity while they officially deny her involvement.

## Significance

The casino serves as a neutral ground where law enforcement and criminal elements intersect. Sariel's role as a profiler allows her to apply her behavioral analysis skills to identify high-risk individuals within the gambling population, while her security work keeps her connected to the criminal underworld.
