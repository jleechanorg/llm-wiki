## Overview
Nocturne is the player character in the Nocturne Old Republic campaign. A Level 1-20+ Sith Prodigy serving as "Siren" of House Vitiate, they are an Essence Devourer involved in Project Apotheosis who ultimately consumes Emperor Vitiate and ascends to God-Empress status.

## Attributes
- **Level:** 1-20+ (scales from Lvl 1 Sith Prodigy to Lvl 20 Sovereign of the Void)
- **Class:** Sith Prodigy (Siren) evolving into Sovereign of the Void
- **Title:** Siren of House Vitiate, then God-Empress
- **Trait:** Essence Devourer — can consume defeated Force users to unlock tiers of power through the "Consumption Tree" system
- **Key Mechanic:** "Vaylin" — a volatile psionic storm within Nocturne, the source of her Essence Devourer powers

## Character Arc
1. **Lvl 1-6:** Subject 7, Siren of House Vitiate, navigating Sith Academy politics on Dromund Kaas
2. **Lvl 7-19:** Consuming Sith Lords, assembling Void Engine blueprints, building power
3. **Lvl 20:** Final consumption of Vitiate — "The unmaking of a god" — and ascension to God-Empress
4. **Post-God-Empress:** Distributing gifts to family (Arcann, Thexan, Senya, Tenebrae) across the galaxy as the Author of the Omniverse
