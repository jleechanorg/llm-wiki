## Overview
Nocturne is the player character in the Nocturne Old Republic campaign. A Level 1-6 Sith Prodigy serving as "Siren" of House Vitiate, they are an Essence Devourer involved in Project Apotheosis.

## Attributes
- **Level:** 1-6
- **Class:** Sith Prodigy
- **Title:** Siren of House Vitiate
- **Trait:** Essence Devourer

## Background
Born into the Vitiate dynasty bloodline, Nocturne carries the legacy of Emperor Vitiate himself. The Empire's attention falls upon them as a potential vessel for the Emperor's continued power.
