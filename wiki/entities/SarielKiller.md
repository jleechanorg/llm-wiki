# Sariel Killer

**Campaign:** 685 entries | **Character:** Sariel | **Arc:** Manufactured serial killer → sovereign architect

## Premise

Modern-day campaign. Sariel is a 15-year-old female serial killer who organized a mass murder event (1,000 people) at age 12. Released due to being a minor, now on probation — working with police to catch other killers (like Dexter). Wealthy old-money family, estranged. Genius-level intelligence (200 IQ), extremely good looking. Evil INTJ inspired by Itachi, Aizen, Johan Liebert, Tywin, Griffith.

## Key Phases

### Phase 1: Psychological Profiling (Entries 1-100)
- Analyzing crime scenes for pattern recognition
- Identifying the "Whisper Killer" as psychic predator exploiting emotional vulnerabilities
- Building psychological profile from autopsy reports
- Systematic testing of Detective Thorne's boundaries

### Phase 2: Investigation to Action (Entry 100)
- Player requests pivot from psychological investigation to vigilante/gang warfare
- "God Mode: Execute rapid resolution to Whisper Killer case"
- Combat mechanics introduced
- Police characters retained as supporting elements

### Phase 3: Root Cause (Entries 100-400)
- Discovery of "The Foundry" — the organization that manufactured Sariel
- "Behavioral templates they used to tune me at age 10"
- Site Alpha — maritime "womb" of Project Genesis
- "Burn the roots, not the branches"

### Phase 4: Transcendence (Entries 400-685)
- **Entry 400:** Conditions include "Traumatized (severe), Relentless Infamy"
- Level 5 while psychologically damaged — damage as permission structure
- Transcendence into "Sovereign Architect" at Level 300
- 172,000 universes, 100 trillion nodes, 172,000 "God Power"
