## Summary
The Grey Sentinel is a chalk-rise ridge in the Dornish Marches, serving as the site of a decisive ambush victory. Aegon uses Hypnotic Pattern to incapacitate 15 of 20 Peake outriders, then the retinue counter-charges and executes the remainder (keeping only officers).

## Key Details
- **Location**: Northern Marches, a low windswept ridge
- **Ambush Type**: Magical (Hypnotic Pattern) + cavalry counter-charge
- **Outcome**: 20 Peake outriders neutralized; 19 executed, 1 officer captured
- **Tactical Element**: The high bronze grass provides a natural, shimmering veil for the company

## Notable Dice Rolls
- **Hypnotic Pattern WIS Saves**: 20x(1d20+1) vs DC 17 — 15/20 failed (15 incapacitated, 5 saved)
- **Ser Roland Crakehall Attack**: 14 vs AC 15 — Miss
- **Dunk Attack**: 22 vs AC 15 — Hit, 7 damage
- **Prince Daeron Attack**: 17 vs AC 15 — Hit, 9 damage
- **Binding Coordination**: 20 vs DC 12 — Success (40 veterans bind 15 incapacitated targets before Pattern expires)
