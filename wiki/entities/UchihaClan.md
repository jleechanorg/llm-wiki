## Description

The Uchiha clan is one of the most powerful clans in the shinobi world, known for their Sharingan bloodline ability. In this evil campaign, the clan becomes the target of genocide by their own member — Itachi Uchiha, who views them as "a cancer upon the Shinobi world."

## In This Campaign

The campaign begins with the Uchiha Clan massacre. Itachi views the clan as a chaotic element whose elimination is necessary for his vision of a unified world order. The Uchiha's obsession with tradition, their festering resentment toward the village, their volatile emotions tied to their Sharingan evolution — all these he sees as fundamental flaws requiring surgical excision.

## Significance

The Uchiha represent the first step in Itachi's grand plan. Their elimination removes a volatile element and provides him with the power (Mangekyō Sharingan) necessary for his future ambitions.
