## Description
Minthara is a Drow Paladin commander who serves as the leader of the goblin camp's forces in Baldur's Gate 3. She operates from the Shattered Sanctum's War Room, where she coordinates the raid on the Emerald Grove. Her cool, dark-skinned hand rests over Nocturne's on the tactical map as she searches for any sign of weakness in the "surface weakling" she suspects Nocturne to be.
