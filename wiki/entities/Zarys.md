## Overview
Zarys is a Zhentarim contact in the Nocturne Post BG3 Zhent campaign. They serve as the connection point between Nocturne and the Zhentarim organization in the post-war Baldur's Gate criminal landscape.

## Significance
As a contact, Zarys represents the potential path to Zhentarim affiliation and provides quest hooks for the criminal enterprise expansion storyline.
