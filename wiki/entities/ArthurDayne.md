Ser Arthur Dayne, known as the Sword of the Morning, is the greatest swordsman in the history of Westeros and serves as a Level 20 gestalt Fighter/Paladin in the Kingsguard. His legendary skill with Dawn, the radiant greatsword passed down from his ancestors, made him the most feared and respected knight of his age. Arthur's journey in the campaign involves his unwavering loyalty to the Targaryen dynasty and his pivotal role in the events surrounding Robert's rebellion. He serves faithfully in the elite Kingsguard, the royal protection order, until the fall of the Mad King Aerys II.
