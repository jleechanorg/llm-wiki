## Description
The Argent Eaglets are a silver-themed military order serving the Silent Throne under Empress Sariel. This knight order embodies precision, honor, and ruthless efficiency in service to the throne.

## Background
Founded in the early years of Empress Sariel's reign, the Argent Eaglets were established as an elite strike force capable of operating beyond conventional military constraints. Their silver armor and eagle insignias have become synonymous with the Silent Throne's will.

## Membership
Members of the Argent Eaglets undergo rigorous training in both combat and political acumen. The order accepts only those who demonstrate unwavering loyalty to the throne and the ability to make difficult decisions without hesitation.
