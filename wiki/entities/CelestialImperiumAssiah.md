## Overview

The Celestial Imperium is a bastion of rigid hierarchy and golden magic located to the east in the Alexiel Assiah campaign (Year 78 DR). It stands in opposition to the Unchained Host. The Imperium is ruled by the ancient Lord Regent Raziel from the geometric wonder of Aeterna. Alexiel currently stands at its border, having fled from the Host.

## Key Details

- **Location:** Eastern Assiah
- **Ruler:** Lord Regent Raziel
- **Capital:** Aeterna
- **Opposes:** The Unchained Host
- **Force:** Divine order and golden magic
