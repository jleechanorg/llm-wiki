## Description

An exact duplicate run of the V5 Succubus campaign, also 278 scenes, also terminating at Level 13 Nascent Demon Lord in the Shattered Sanctum. Shares the same opening: Nocturne Sosuke on the Ravaged Beach with 1 HP, Prone, Malcanthet's Favor available. Shares the same terminal scene (278): the Religious Conversion roll at DC 20, 1,006,527gp, and the Aura of the Sovereign. The V5 Succubus Copy appears to be a parallel session or recording of the same campaign arc.
