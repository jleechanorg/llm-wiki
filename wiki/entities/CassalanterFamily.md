## Overview
The Cassalanter Family is a powerful noble house in Baldur's Gate that made an infernal pact with Zariel, harvesting the souls of "ruined nobility" to power their fortune. They destroyed Nocturne's family and sold her to the Bloom Shadow brothel. Their wealth comes from soul coins, and they hold an annual Ascension Gala to renew their pact.
