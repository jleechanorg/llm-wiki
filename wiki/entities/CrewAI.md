## Overview

CrewAI is an enterprise platform for orchestrating "crews" of collaborative AI agents. It provides four pillars: Orchestrate, Build/Integrate, Observe/Optimize, Manage/Scale.

## Key Properties

- **Architecture**: Enterprise platform for collaborative AI agent crews
- **Deployment**: Three options — AMP Cloud, AMP Factory (on-prem), OSS
- **Scale**: 450M+ agentic workflows/month, 4,000+ new sign-ups weekly, 60% Fortune 500 adoption
- **Four pillars**: Orchestrate, Build/Integrate, Observe/Optimize, Manage/Scale
