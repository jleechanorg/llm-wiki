## Description

Sunder-Stone Villa is the former Cassalanter Family manor in Baldur's Gate, now claimed by Nocturne after she hijacked their soul-tithe ritual to Archdevil Zariel. The obsidian-walled estate represents both Nocturne's triumph and the turning point in her transformation from victim to power broker.

## Role in the Campaign

In the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, the villa serves as the player's home base after destroying the Cassalanter Family. The property includes the Whispering Cellar, the site of the pivotal ritual where Nocturne turned the family's own infernal machinery against them.

After claiming the villa, Nocturne renamed it to House Sosuke, establishing a new noble identity in Baldur's Gate. The Zhentarim partnership secured the property's tunnels as "Sovereign Zhentarim Territory" for 99 years.

## Key Features

- **Whispering Cellar:** Obsidian ritual room where the Cassalanter soul-tithe took place; now contains cooling soul-fire
- **Upper City location:** The villa is in the prestigious Upper City of Baldur's Gate
- **Formerly Cassalanter property:** The family devoted themselves to Zariel before their destruction
- **Noble authority:** The property grants Nocturne legitimate standing in Baldur's Gate society
