## Overview
The Smirking Boar is a tavern in Baldur's Gate serving as the operations base for The Guild's mercenary activities. The cellar contains the hidden meeting spaces where contracts are negotiated and jobs are assigned.

## Significance
This location represents the noir underside of Baldur's Gate — where criminals gather away from official eyes and where the kidnapping contract originates.
