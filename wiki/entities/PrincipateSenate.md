## Overview
The Principate Senate is the traditionalist power base of the oldest families in the [[CelestialImperium]], composed of political intrigue masters managing the Empyrean Affliction under [[Raziel]]'s guidance.

## Leadership
- Guided by: Lord Regent [[Raziel]]
- Wing: Revisionist Scholars (progressive, questioning official doctrine under Magister Lucian Kantos)

## Key Facts
- Type: Traditionalist political power base
- Parent: [[CelestialImperium]]
- Members: Oldest families
- Focus: Political intrigue, managing Empyrean Affliction
