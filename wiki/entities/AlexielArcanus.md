- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **MBTI:** INTJ

## Character Overview

Alexiel Arcanus was the Magister of the Watch and Lady of House Arcanus, publicly known as the "Mortal Queen"—a legendary war hero who ended the Host Wars. Her public legacy was that of a martyr who made the ultimate sacrifice at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields to stop a profane demonic ritual, saving hundreds of innocent children including her own daughter.

Her secret truth was far more extraordinary: Alexiel was a hidden demigod of immense power who chose to live as a mortal. Her greatest secret was a Nullification Field she could project, allowing her to nullify magical and demonic phenomena. Her death was not heroic sacrifice in a fair battle—it was the result of a trap specifically designed by the warlord Mordan to exploit her single, absolute moral code: the protection of children.

## The Secret Trap

At age 15, Sariel was kidnapped by Mordan. Under torture, she revealed her mother's unbreakable moral vow: Alexiel would do anything to protect children. Mordan used this knowledge to set a trap at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields, using hundreds of captive children as leverage. Faced with an impossible choice, Alexiel overloaded her Nullification Field to stop the ritual and save the children, sacrificing her own life in the process.

## Legacy

Alexiel's death sent shockwaves through the Imperium. High Commander Titus val Raziel was devastated, having come to see her as a sister. The court is now a viper's nest of factions seeking to exploit the vacuum. Her children—Sariel, Cassian, and Valerius—are left to navigate the aftermath, while the truth of her demigod nature remains hidden from most.
