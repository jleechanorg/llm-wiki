## Description
Sariel is a disgraced chaebol (Korean conglomerate family) heiress turned corporate operative for IM Defense in Seoul. Known as "The Serpent in the Service," she speaks with flawless etiquette while observing weaknesses like a scholar, driven by a compulsive urge to orchestrate humiliating power dynamics.

## Background
Once part of a powerful chaebol family, Sariel suffered a fall from grace that led her to work for Noh Tae-nam at IM Defense — a corporation that serves as the "Nilfgaard" equivalent of this world, led by the formidable Noh Hwa-young. Her goal is ultimate control, not through overt force, but through subtle manipulation and orchestration.

## Personality
Sariel speaks softly with flawless etiquette, presenting a gentle exterior while internally cataloging every weakness she observes. She has a compulsive need to orchestrate humiliating power dynamics, deriving satisfaction from making others dependent on her goodwill. She is patient, calculating, and never makes a move without significant leverage.

## Capabilities
- **Class:** Bard (College of Swords), Level 1
- **Key spells:** Vicious Mockery (delivers "soul-crushing truth"), Charm Person, Suggestion, Disguise Self, Detect Thoughts
- **Equipment:** Concealed combat baton (functions as rapier), compact pistol (functions as hand crossbow), tailored ballistic suit, earpiece

## Campaign Role
Protagonist of the doberman campaign, set in Seoul, South Korea, adapting the K-drama "Military Prosecutor Doberman."
