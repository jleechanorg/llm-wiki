## Profile

- **Full Name:** The Whispering Cellar
- **Type:** Underground ritual chamber
- **Location:** Beneath Sunder-Stone Villa, Upper City Baldur's Gate
- **Campaign:** [[Faction Nocturne Bg3 V3 Campaign]]

## Description

The Whispering Cellar is an obsidian-lined underground ritual room beneath the Cassalanter Manor (Sunder-Stone Villa). It features cooling soul-fire and crystalline cages designed to hold victims during the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual to Zariel. The room was the site of Nocturne's defining act: hijacking the ritual mid-performance to liquidate her enemies and redirect the soul-tithe through her own palms.

After the ritual was subverted, the crystalline cages hiss as they depressurize. The soul-fire still cools in the obsidian walls. The screaming of the Cassalanter nobles echoes in the floorboards above.

## Key Events

- **Soul-Tithe Ritual:** The Cassalanter family's mechanism for collecting souls to pay Zariel (Archdevil). Nocturne redirected this mid-ritual.
- **Sisters' Rescue:** Elara and Lyra were held in crystalline cages here; freed when Nocturne broke the ritual.
- **Crime Scene:** The manor above is a massacre of dead high-nobles. Nocturne must navigate the aftermath.
