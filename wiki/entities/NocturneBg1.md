## Description
Nocturne is a Human Ranger of the Gloom Stalker archetype in the bg1-nocturne campaign, known as the "Serpent Queen" — a Lawful Evil predator navigating the corrupt political landscape of the Sword Coast during the Iron Crisis.

## Background
Raised by Gorion at the ancient fortress-library of Candlekeep in 1368 DR, Nocturne learned early that the world is a hierarchy of predators and prey. Her Lawful Evil alignment manifests not as sadistic cruelty, but as cold, calculating survival efficiency. She intends to be at the top of any food chain she enters.

By Mirtul 16, she has reached Level 5 Gloom Stalker Ranger and operates from the Seven Suns Coster in Baldur's Gate, where she uncovered a doppelganger conspiracy targeting the city's leadership.

## Personality
Nocturne is patient, observant, and supremely pragmatic. She evaluates every situation for advantage and disadvantage with serpentine patience. She does not kill unnecessarily — not from mercy, but from efficiency. Every action serves her long-term positioning.

## Capabilities
- **Class:** Gloom Stalker Ranger (Level 5)
- **Abilities:** Dread Ambusher, Umbral Sight
- **Special mechanic:** The Hunger (stat modifier based on satiation state)
- **Planned:** eventual Rogue multiclass

## Campaign Role
Protagonist of the bg1-nocturne and bg1-nocturne-continued campaigns.
