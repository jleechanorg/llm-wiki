## Description

Cheyenne, Wyoming serves as the primary setting for the Hunting Party Sariel V2 campaign. This campaign takes place in a specific area known as "The Pit" — a post-explosion tactical chaos zone that has become a dangerous but strategically important area.

## In This Campaign

The 16-year-old version of Sariel operates in Cheyenne alongside her team of companions. The city represents the American heartland's darker underbelly — a place where FBI operations, vigilante justice, and criminal activity collide in the shadows.

## The Pit

"The Pit" is described as a post-explosion tactical chaos zone in Cheyenne. This area has become a lawless territory where traditional rules of engagement don't apply, making it perfect for Sariel's brand of vigilante justice. The environment is dangerous, unpredictable, and allows for the kind of extreme action that characterizes this campaign.
