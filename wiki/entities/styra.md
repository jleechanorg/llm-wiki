## Overview
Styra is the company founded by the OPA authors that provides commercial OPA distribution, an enterprise governance platform, and policy-as-code framework support. Styra Governace Platform extends OPA with visual policy editors, RBAC, and centralized policy management across distributed microservices.

## Key Properties
- **Founded by**: OPA core authors
- **Product**: Styra Governance Platform — enterprise OPA distribution
- **CNCF**: OPA is a CNCF graduated project; Styra provides commercial support layer
- **Policy-as-Code**: Extends Rego with visual editors and pre-built policy libraries
