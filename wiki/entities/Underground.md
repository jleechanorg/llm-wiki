## Profile

- **Name:** The Underground
- **Type:** Location (sealed monster realm)
- **Setting:** Beneath Mt. Ebott
- **Ruler:** Unknown (to be encountered)

## Overview

The Underground is the sealed realm where monsters have lived for centuries after being defeated by humans in a great war. It is accessed by falling through a hole at the top of Mt. Ebott. The realm is vast, featuring diverse environments from the initial Ruins to deeper areas populated by increasingly powerful monsters.

The Underground is filled with monsters of all kinds, each with their own personalities and stories. The air is cool, smells of dust and old magic, and is lit by magical purple-glowing stonework rather than sunlight.

## Campaign Significance

Frisk begins their journey in the Underground after falling into Mt. Ebott. The campaign is a D&D 5e adaptation of the Undertale experience, where every encounter can potentially be resolved through mercy or violence. The Underground's inhabitants range from friendly to hostile, and navigating this social landscape is the heart of the campaign.

## Key Areas

- **The Precipice:** Where Frisk first arrives after falling
- **The Ruins:** The entrance area, home to Toriel
- **Golden Flower Patch:** First area inside the Ruins
