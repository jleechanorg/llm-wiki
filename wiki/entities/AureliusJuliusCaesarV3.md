Aurelius Julius Caesar V3 is a Level 6 Bard College of Swords who operates under the alias Aurelius Varenus in Capua within a divergent timeline. His blade-wielding bard abilities combine magical song with combat prowess, making him a unique force in the political landscape of Rome. Using disguise and deception as his primary tools, Aurelius navigates the complex political environment of a divergent timeline Rome through his merchant cover identity. He leads The Praetorian Hand, an elite squad of operatives, as he works toward his goals through cunning rather than direct confrontation.
