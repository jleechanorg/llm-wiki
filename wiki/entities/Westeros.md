## Overview

Westeros is the continent that serves as the primary setting for the Aemon Game of Thrones campaign. It is a land of political intrigue, noble houses, and brutal power struggles. The continent is ruled from King's Landing and the Iron Throne, with various houses vying for power.

## Key Details

- **Notable Regions:** Westerlands (where campaign begins), King's Landing
- **Timeline:** 298 AC (beginning of the main book series)
- **Notable Houses:** House Targaryen, House Lannister, House Stark
- **Major Threat:** The War of the Five Kings is approaching
