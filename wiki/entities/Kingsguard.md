The Kingsguard is the elite royal protection order of Westeros, consisting of seven of the greatest knights in the realm. These warriors swear lifelong vows to protect the king and the royal family, forswearing all other allegiances including their own houses. Arthur Dayne serves as the Lord Commander of this prestigious order, representing its highest honor. The Kingsguard's white cloaks and white armor make them instantly recognizable symbols of the crown's authority. Their oath requires them to defend the king even at the cost of their own lives.
