## Overview
StrongDM is an enterprise security company that pioneered the Attractor pattern and Dark Factory methodology. Their CTO Justin McCarthy's team built CXDB, Healer, and a digital twin universe of SaaS applications for agent testing.

## Key Properties
- **Type**: Enterprise security company
- **Key features**: Attractor NLSpec specs, AttractorBench benchmark, CXDB observability, Healer agent, digital twin universe
- **Key people**: Justin McCarthy (CTO), Jay (digital twin builder), Navan (bootcamp lead)
- **Team size**: 3 people built the entire factory
