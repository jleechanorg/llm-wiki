## Description

Elara is Nocturne's older sister, age 18, rescued from the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual. She was held at Sunder-Stone Villa as "Refined Collateral" for sacrifice to Archdevil Zariel. After being freed by Nocturne's hijacking of the ritual, Elara struggles to reconcile her gratitude with the horror of what her sister did to save her.

## Role in the Campaign

Elara appears in the opening scene of the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, stumbling out of the crystalline cages after the ritual's "Refined Resonance" is siphoned into Nocturne. Her "noble poise" is "shattered" as she catches herself on Vesper's arm. Her response to Nocturne's actions—accepting that "this is the price of our name"—shows her willingness to embrace a darker path.

## Relationship with Nocturne

Elara represents the family Nocturne fought to save. Her acceptance of Nocturne's methods, even when horrified by them, creates a complex dynamic where familial loyalty coexists with moral unease.
