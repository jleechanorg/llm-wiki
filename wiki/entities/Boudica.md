## Overview
Player character in the Boudica's Uprising campaign. Queen of the Iceni tribe in ancient Britain, leading a rebellion against Roman occupation.

## Character Profile

### Background
Boudica, Queen of the Iceni, was once a proud ruler of her people in ancient Britain. When Roman forces invaded her lands, humiliated her family, and flogged her daughters, she swore a blood oath of vengeance.

## Story Role
Boudica draws upon ancient druidic pacts and the fury of her people, forging a pact with an entity of retribution—channeling the rage of the wronged into supernatural power. She rises as both a warrior and a bard, rallying tribes with her voice and leading them with her blade.
