## Description

A 179-scene post-Absolute campaign with SYSTEM RESTORE mechanics. The campaign is titled "The Shadow Monarch's Ascension" and begins with a full character and campaign state restore — Nocturne is Level 8 Bard (College of Swords) at the Deck of the Moonrise Ship, in the Wyrm's Tooth area, on Tarsakh 5, 1492 DR. The SYSTEM RESTORE format indicates this is a continuation of a prior campaign that was saved and restored mid-session.

The campaign escalates to Divine Rank 2 (Demigod) by Scene 179, with a Portfolio of War, Omni-Attunement at Infinite, and the Idol's Aura active. The Shadow Monarch title is the operative identity throughout.

## Story

The SYSTEM RESTORE at Scene 1 establishes that this campaign is a mid-campaign continuation. The Restore Summary indicates the prior state: "The Shadow Monarch's Ascension" with Nocturne operating in Baldur's Gate during the post-Elturel crisis.

By Scene 40, she is Level 8 Bard in the Coiled Serpent Containment Antechamber (Lower City), with AC 18, DC 25 (Siphon Boost), and 45/52 HP. The Hunger is sated. True Seeing extends to 58 meters.

By Scene 90, she is Level 13 Bard (College of Swords) at the Morphic Pool (Elder Brain Dock), with Absolute Knowledge (+2 INT/WIS), 91/91 HP, and 7/7 Omni-Attunement active. The Elder Brain is being audited from within.

Scene 140 is a DM MODE override moment: the campaign enters "APOTHEOSIS TIER 2" with Intermediate Deity (Rank 6) status, 666 HP, AC 45, DC 40, and the title "The Hidden Lord" with a Portfolio of Tyranny, Secrets, City, and Ambition. The scene includes a direct communication with the player about linear scaling to Level 50 using 5th Edition progression.

The terminal scene (179) shows Nocturne as Divine Rank 2 Demigod in the High Hall Throne Room (Upper City), with 700 HP, unlimited Inspiration and spell slots (L1-L8 Infinite, L9 4/4), and Divine Presence (Awe) active. Her Reign has reached Day 45. She is the Shadow Monarch, seated on the throne, with the Hunger sated and all attunements infinite.
