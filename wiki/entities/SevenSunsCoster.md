## Description
The Seven Suns Coster is a major merchant company located in Baldur's Gate. It serves as Nocturne's base of operations during her time in the city, providing both business cover and intelligence gathering opportunities.

## Significance
In this segment, the Seven Suns Coster becomes the site of a critical discovery: a doppelganger infiltration with a purge list targeting key city leaders. The 2nd floor office where Nocturne operates becomes ground zero for uncovering Sarevok's conspiracy to decapitate Baldur's Gate's leadership.
