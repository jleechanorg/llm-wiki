Assiah is the fractured world where the campaign takes place, torn between two major powers: the Eastern Celestial Imperium and the Western Unchained Host. The world was shattered during the Celestial Wars led by Lucifer, creating a multi-polar cosmic order where divine beings and mortals coexist in uneasy tension. The geography includes mountain ranges like the DragonsTooth Mountains and grand cities like Aeterna, the geometric wonder capital of the Celestial Imperium. This setting provides the backdrop for Alexiel's journey as she navigates between her father's rebellion and her hidden potential as a magical prodigy.
