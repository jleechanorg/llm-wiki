## Overview
The dominant imperial power occupying ancient Britain during the campaign period (1492 DR / ~60 AD).

## In-Game Context
The antagonist faction of the Boudica campaign. Roman forces invaded the Iceni's lands, humiliated Boudica's family, and flogged her daughters—triggering the rebellion.

## Historical Context
- Empire: Roman Empire at its height of power
- Territory: Britain, Gaul, Mediterranean
- Military: Professional army, superior tactics and equipment
- Occupation: Britain was conquered and occupied since 43 AD

## In Story
The Roman legions are the occupying force that the player character (Boudica) seeks to overthrow. Their cruelty and oppression serve as the catalyst for the rebellion.
