## Summary
A D&D 5e Baldur's Gate 3 campaign set during the events of BG3's Act 1, immediately following the Nautiloid crash. The protagonist Nocturne Sosuke is a Level 5 Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) — a de-powered goddess trapped in a mortal cage by the illithid tadpole. She is the "Sovereign of the Shattered Rose," a Malcanthet-pacted succubus whose power is being suppressed. The campaign takes place in the Goblin Camp's Shattered Sanctum where she operates under Minthara's command, coordinating the raid on the Emerald Grove alongside BG3 companions (Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Lae'zel, Shadowheart, Karlach).

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is Level 5 (narratively Level 6 suppressed) with CHA 20, AC 17 (Siren's Skin: 10+DEX+CHA), HP 33 — a porcelain weapon of social and magical domination
- The "Siren's Gaze" system tracks targets' Social HP; when broken, they become vulnerable to the "Gilded Tether" (Thrall binding)
- At Level 5, she commands 3 Thralls max; each active Thrall reduces her Max HP by the creature's CR
- The Abyssal Siren class progression extends to Level 30 Demon Lord apotheosis, with physical evolution from "Porcelain Doll" (L1-4) through "Uncanny Valley" (L5-9) to "Sovereign" (L15-19) to "Ascended" (L20)
- The tadpole narrative represents the "dam" containing her Level 6+ power; every level-up is a structural failure of that containment
