They melted into the dreary morning, heading towards the merchant's expected route, their eagerness for profit overcoming any lingering internal squabbles. Mistle, already somewhere in the distance, would be keenly observing their approach, her analytical wariness now overlaid with an undeniable attraction to Nocturne.
