## Summary
Visenya commands both Robb and Jon to kill Eddard Stark together — a shared trauma to "reset" the brothers' rivalry (the "Obsession Rift") by giving them a greater sin than their jealousy. Both comply. Ice descends; Jaehaerys' blade finds the heart. The "Stark Wall" has not just been breached — it has been used to baptize Visenya's Knights in a shared, irrevocable parricide.
