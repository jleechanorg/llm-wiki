Game Master:
[Mode: DM MODE]

I understand. You have provided a new standing directive: "whenever i conquer an area enslave people, train warriors, rest servants, divide among my castles."

**Understanding & Execution:**

This is a new behavioral protocol for Sir Daemon following any future conquest. It outlines a standardized post-conquest routine for managing the conquered populace and resources. I will integrate this as follows for all subsequent narrative developments related to conquering new areas:

1.  **"enslave people"**: Upon conquering an area, I will narrate Daemon's immediate actions to forcibly subjugate the local populace. They will be treated as his personal property and labor force. This reinforces the existing "terrified populace" dynamic and clarifies their status.
2.  **"train warriors"**: A portion of the able-bodied subjugated populace will be immediately put into mandatory, brutal military training under the direction of Daemon's veteran warriors (or Daemon himself, if present). This will continue the "Basic Recruits" pipeline.
3.  **"rest servants"**: A portion of the subjugated populace will be designated for "rest" or basic maintenance duties. This acknowledges their need for recovery before being assigned to more strenuous labor or military training, preventing complete exhaustion and ensuring a continuous pool of exploitable labor/potential recruits. I will track this as a "Labor Pool" with a sub-status of "Rested" or "Active."
4.  **"divide among my castles"**: All resources (including newly subjugated people, loot, and trained recruits) will be systematically distributed and allocated among Daemon's controlled keeps (currently Stonehaven and Oakhaven), as per his strategic needs and current capabilities. This will be reflected in follower and resource updates.

This directive formalizes Daemon's ruthless administrative policies and will directly influence the narrative of future conquests and resource management.

Please confirm if this understanding and integration plan for Daemon's post-conquest actions is acceptable.
