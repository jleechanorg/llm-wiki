He had revealed his true desires to Vader, and the Sith Lord's approval resonated with his burgeoning strength.

Now, a new, cunning ambition took root. To gain even deeper insight, to wield even greater control, Luke needed more than just a loyal weapon; he needed knowledge. The Jedi Master, a wellspring of Force techniques honed over decades, was the key. He stepped closer to her, his voice a low, resonant purr. “Master,” he commanded, the title now a grotesque mockery of its former meaning. “You will secretly impart to me all of your Force techniques, every subtle nuance of the Light Side, every defense, every application of foresight. The **Jedi Master's** eyes, vibrant with that chilling, violet glow, widened almost imperceptibly, a flicker of comprehension. She gave a curt nod, her posture becoming subtly more rigid, as if preparing for a new, intricate task. Over the next few days, cloistered in a secluded training chamber, she moved with chilling precision, demonstrating complex Force forms and reciting ancient Jedi tenets, only for Luke to mentally twist them, finding their inherent weaknesses, their vulnerabilities to a Dark Side assault. He absorbed her knowledge like a sponge, a grim satisfaction building within him as he corrupted the very essence of her teachings. He justified it: *This is necessary to understand my enemy. Their weaknesses will be my greatest weapon.* A faint, fleeting sense of unease, a ghost of his former self, tried to surface at the profound perversion, but he ruthlessly suppressed it. This was the true path to order.

**Darth Vader's** presence was distant, a heavy anchor in the wider installation, but not immediately close. Luke worked with a cold focus, aware of the ever-present risk of detection, yet confident in his ability to conceal this intellectual plunder. He was not only powerful; he was cunning. And when the time came, he would unleash this augmented power upon the Rebels, a testament to his own terrifying capabilities, not just Vader’s.
