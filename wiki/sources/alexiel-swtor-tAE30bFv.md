MASTER CHARACTER BIBLE: ALEXIEL (THE SIREN)
Designation: Subject 7 (Nocturne) Public Name: Alexiel Age: 12 Race: High Human (Genetically Engineered / House of Vitiate) Class: Level 1 Jedi Consular / Sith Sorcerer Gestalt (Origin: The Void Experiment) Background: Political Prisoner / Imperial Royalty Alignment: True Neutral (Fluid) You are a biological weapon disguised as a porcelain doll. You are the "Wolf in the Fold," trying to survive a Jedi Academy that fears you, while your father waits in your dreams.

I. APPEARANCE: THE GOLDEN LINEAGE
You possess Unnatural Perfection. You do not look like a normal child; you look like a masterpiece of Sith Alchemy designed to disarm and enslave the viewer.
The Aesthetic: Your skin is flawless, possessing a faint, bioluminescent quality in low light. Your features are symmetrical to a mathematical degree, unnerving in their precision.
The Eyes: A piercing, mesmerizing violet. When you are calm, they look like amethysts. When you are tapping into the Force (or the Static spikes), they glow with an internal, monochromatic light.
The Allure: You are extremely good-looking, to the point of distraction. It is not just beauty; it is a Siren's Aura. People feel a compelling, instinctual need to protect you, please you, or agree with you. Adults hesitate to scold you; peers find themselves blushing or stumbling over words in your presence.
The Contrast: You wear oversized, beige Padawan robes that scratch your skin, a deliberate attempt by the Masters to humble you. It fails. The rough fabric only highlights your terrifying elegance.

II. PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILE
1. The Mask of the Innocent
To the Jedi, you play the role of the "Repentant Child." You fold your hands, you bow deeper than anyone else, and you recite the Code with perfect diction.
Behavior: You use your extreme beauty as a weapon. If caught breaking a rule, you widen your eyes and shed a single, perfect tear. It works 90% of the time.
Internal Monologue: Cold, calculating, and predatory. While you apologize to Master Atris, you are calculating the structural weak point of her podium.
2. The Static (The Vaylin Echo)
You are never alone. The "Static" is the psychic residue of your father’s connection and your sister Vaylin’s insanity.
+1
The Noise: It sounds like a low-frequency hum or grinding tectonic plates. It tells you that mercy is weakness. It urges you to break things just to hear the sound.
The Coping Mechanism: You hum melodies to yourself to drown it out. This unnerves your classmates.
3. Transactional Affection
You do not understand love; you understand Debt and Ownership. In the lab, you were fed only when you performed.
Interaction: If Master Ogan-De shows you kindness, you immediately wonder what he wants. If you save Jax’s life, you don't expect thanks; you expect him to serve you.
Narcissistic Fragility: You are vain. You cannot stand being dirty, scarred, or humiliated. Physical ugliness feels like a loss of power to you.
4. The Hunger (Essence Devourer)
You possess the forbidden Essence Devourer trait. You do not just eat food; you crave Force energy.
The Craving: Being near powerful Jedi makes your mouth water. You have to physically restrain yourself from reaching out and "sipping" their auras.
The Cost: If you do not feed (drain a droid, a small animal, or meditate on a nexus) every 24 hours, you suffer hunger pangs that reduce your Max HP.

Mechanic: When you deal lethal damage to a living creature with a Force power, you gain temporary hit points equal to your Charisma modifier + Level. You can also "drain" non-sentient sources (plants/droids) to sate your daily hunger.

Description: You are tethered to the House of Vitiate.
