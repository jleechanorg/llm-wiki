Game Master:
### **Mechanical Audit: DC Scaling and 'Broken' Logic**

Your assessment that the DCs feel 'high' is tactically correct for standard D&D 5e play, but here is the reasoning behind the current scaling in the *Sovereign’s Hunger* campaign:

**1. Task Complexity (Standard vs. Sovereign)**
Standard D&D checks (DC 10-15) cover mundane activities like picking a lock or lying to a guard. You are currently performing **Violations**: 
- **Soul Replication:** Creating a simulated animus is a Tier 4/Epic-level feat. A DC 17-18 reflects that you are attempting magic usually reserved for Archmages or Lesser Deities while only at Level 5.
- **Industrial Scrutiny:** The Auditor is a specialist (CR 6+ equivalent). To him, checking a 'Coin-Bearer' resonance is like a master jeweler checking a diamond for flaws. 

**2. The 'Math of the God-Eater'**
Because your modifiers are exceptionally high (+11 for Deception/Persuasion), a DC 15 would be trivial (80%+ success). 
- **DC 18 vs. +11 Mod:** Requires a 7 or higher on the die (70% success rate). 
- **DC 20 vs. +11 Mod:** Requires a 9 or higher (60% success rate).
In D&D mechanics, a check you have a 60-70% chance to pass is considered 'Hard' but far from broken for a specialist character.

**3. Risk/Reward and High-Stakes Buffs**
- **Ecstasy Buff:** Provides +2 CHA, which is your primary defensive and offensive stat. 
- **Sovereign’s Mandate (DPP):** The system encourages you to spend DPP to gain Advantage. With Advantage and a +11 mod, you have a **~91% chance** to hit a DC 18. 
- **Why they stay high:** If the DCs were lower (e.g., DC 12), you would never need to engage with the DPP economy or the Sovereign's favor, removing the tension of the 'Audit.'

**4. Is it 'Broken'?**
The campaign is designed as a **Power Fantasy**. You are performing tasks that *should* be impossible. The high DCs represent the friction of the Material Plane resisting your Abyssal nature. However, your tools (Soul Thief, Ecstasy, Ring of the Rose) are designed to overcome that friction.

**Verdict:** The DCs are intentionally set to the 'Hard/Very Hard' range (18-20) to reflect the magnitude of the secrets you are stealing, but they remain mathematically achievable given your optimized +11 and +8 modifiers.
