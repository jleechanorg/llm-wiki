## Summary

Aizen Bg3 V2 is the second iteration of the Aizen Baldur's Gate campaign, now set after the Flaming Fist has shattered following the Absolute's fall. Aizen operates as the "Cursed Scholar" of House Sosuke - officially dead at age 16 and exiled. He runs the White Garden by night as "The Gardener" while maintaining a cover as a harmless orphan ward at the Arcane Athenaeum. The campaign expands on his network of assets and focuses on corrupting Lirael Voss, a shipwright heiress.

## Key Claims

- Aizen is exiled at 13, officially dead, and now 16 years old
- The Flaming Fist is shattered, creating power vacuum filled by Zhentarim and Thieves' Guild
- The Arcane Athenaeum is a crumbling bastion where Aizen poses as a noble orphan
- His assets include Kaname, Zylra, and Ginora who facilitate his rise
- Lirael Voss is a shipwright heiress whose pride is being slowly corrupted
- Vespera is now a "Porcelain Doll" - once a proud bard of the Harpers
