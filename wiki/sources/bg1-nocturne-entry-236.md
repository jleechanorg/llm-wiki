**Commander Scar.**

He watches your approach, his eyes lingering on the bloodstains on Kagain's armor and the magical aura radiating from your gear. He nods to the runner, dismissing him, and steps forward.

"You're the ones from Nashkel," Scar states, not asking. "Mayor Ghastkill's letter spoke highly of you. He says you cleared the mines and broke the back of the bandit army in the Cloakwood."

"We did what needed doing," you reply, your voice cool.

Scar crosses his arms. "Good. Because I have a problem that needs doing, and my own men are too loud or too corrupt to handle it. The **Seven Suns**... something is wrong. The merchants inside are acting strangely. They've stopped shipments, liquidated assets, and refuse to speak to the Dukes. I suspect the Iron Throne has infiltrated them, or worse."

He leans in, lowering his voice. "I can't raid a legitimate business without proof. I need you to go inside. Find out what happened to the owners. If they are traitors, arrest them. If they are hostages... save them. And if they are something else... kill them."

**Roric** eyes the building. "Infiltration? Or a hostile takeover?"

"Whatever works," Scar grunts. "Just bring me proof I can take to Duke Eltan."
