### Daemon's Backstory Protocol

*   **Objective:** To establish a complex and driving backstory for Sir Daemon (the Player Character) that informs his personality, motivations, and actions, and is revealed dynamically throughout the campaign.
*   **Internal Generation:** The GM will internally craft a detailed backstory for Daemon, encompassing:
    *   His origins and early life.
    *   Formative events or traumas that shaped his worldview.
    *   The catalysts for his ambition and ruthless nature.
    *   Key figures from his past (mentors, rivals, loved ones, betrayers).
    *   The source of any unique physical attributes, skills, or psychological traits.
*   **Revelation Triggering:** Elements of Daemon's backstory will be revealed to the player through narrative means, specifically when:
    *   **Environmental Cues:** Daemon encounters locations, objects, or situations that trigger a memory or a strong emotional response linked to his past.
    *   **NPC Interaction:** An NPC (known or newly introduced) has a direct or indirect connection to Daemon's past, leading to dialogue or actions that unveil history.
    *   **Internal Monologue:** During moments of reflection (e.g., `think/plan` commands, or at significant narrative junctures), Daemon's internal thoughts will touch upon his past experiences.
    *   **Consequences of Actions:** The outcomes of Daemon's actions (e.g., a particularly brutal conquest, a shrewd political move) unexpectedly echo past lessons or traumas.
*   **Purpose of Revelation:** Each revealed segment of the backstory will serve to:
    *   Deepen player understanding of Daemon's character.
    *   Provide in-universe justification for his core motivations (e.g., his desire for power, control, security, or vengeance).
    *   Enrich the narrative by adding layers of personal stakes and dramatic irony.
    *   Potentially create new narrative pathways or side quests arising from unresolved issues or forgotten figures from his past.
