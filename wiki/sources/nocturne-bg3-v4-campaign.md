## Summary
A D&D 5e noir psychological horror campaign set in Baldur's Gate, integrated with BG3 events. Nocturne Sosuke, a fallen noble turned "Black Diamond" of Madam Zylra's brothel, navigates revenge against the Cassalanters who destroyed her family. The campaign combines social engineering, seduction, and calculated violence against a backdrop of soul coins, infernal pacts, and BG3 companion interactions.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is a Level 1 Bard (College of Perdition) with STR 8, DEX 14, CON 8, INT 16, WIS 10, CHA 16 — a "porcelain weapon" built for social combat
- The Cassalanters power their fortune through soul coins harvested from "ruined nobility," including Nocturne's sisters
- The campaign integrates all BG3 companions as potential allies or romantic interests
- The "Siren's Leverage" trait gives +2 bonus to Charisma checks against humanoids sexually/romantically attracted to females
- Horgus Gwent possesses a Soul Coin bearing Nocturne's sister Lyra's face
