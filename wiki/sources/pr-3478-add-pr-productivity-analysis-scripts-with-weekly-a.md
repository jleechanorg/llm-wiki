## Summary
Adds two new scripts for analyzing PR productivity and comparing to industry benchmarks:

### scripts/prs_per_month.py
- Analyze PRs by month or week across GitHub organizations
- Calculate hourly productivity metrics (PRs/hr, lines/hr)
- Supports weekly breakdowns since October 2025
- Shows productivity assuming 20-30 hours/week work schedule

### scripts/compare_productivity.py  
- Compare developer stats to industry benchmarks
- Benchmarks for: General dev, Senior dev, AI/ML dev, Senior AI/ML
