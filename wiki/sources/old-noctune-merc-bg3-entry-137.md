Leaving the Captain's quarters, you descend the narrow, creaking stairs into the belly of the *Silent Wave*. Beyond the living cargo, stacked against the bulkhead, are the 'Artifacts'. Heavy crates reinforced with iron bands. You approach one, your Masterwork Rapier sliding the lid open with a groan of protesting wood.

Inside, nestled in straw, are not mere trinkets, but the stripped wealth of a fallen city. Golden religious icons from Elturel's temples, silver candelabras, and... a small, heavy iron chest. You flip the latch.

It is filled with cold, heavy coins made of infernal iron. *Soul Coins*. The currency of Avernus. Each one hums with a faint, trapped whisper of agony. This is the 'special goods' Vessic was waiting for. Portable, absolute power, minted from the souls of the damned.
