The screams had died, replaced by the groans of the dying and the chilling silence of absolute victory. Daemon (Level 9, Age 30) stood amidst the carnage, his greatsword still dripping, his dragon-scaled armor splattered with the blood of Valoria. The two warring factions, the hawk and the serpent, lay broken and intermingled across the field, their futile civil war abruptly ended by a new, far more terrifying master. Oakhaven's Reach, the stronghold they had so desperately fought over, now stood silent, its gates no longer a barrier against external foes, but a symbol of surrender.

Joric (Level 8, Age 32), Gareth (Level 8, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 8, Age 33), and Theron (Level 8, Age 28), his elite vanguard, moved with grim efficiency, their own blades still gleaming. They began the systematic process of culling the wounded, gathering prisoners, and securing the perimeter. The 90 Level 6 warriors, now battle-hardened and unified in their new steel, moved into formation around the stronghold, their presence a stark promise of Daemon's unyielding rule. The ~50 Level 5 Blackwood Guards, while still green, had witnessed the terrifying might of Daemon's disciplined army and now moved with a new, fearful obedience.

Daemon's gaze fell upon the captured lords, Lord Varian and his brother, now dragged before him, their faces a mixture of fear and shattered pride. They were pathetic, squabbling children who had bled their duchy dry for a worthless crown. He would use them, or break them, as he saw fit.

His mind, sharp and cold, already turned to the tasks ahead. Oakhaven's Reach would be secured, its wealth seized, its populace brought to heel. Messengers would be dispatched to Kael (Level 8, Age 25) in Silverport, Borin (Level 8, Age 30) in Stonehaven, Valerius (Level 6, Age 48) in Riverbend, Volkov (Level 6, Age 35) in Oakhaven, and Lyra (Level 6, Age 29) in Stonehaven, informing them of the new conquest and the vast new territories now under his iron fist. Lady Isolde Blackwood (Level 5, Age ~22) would also be informed of the expansion, and Seraphina (Age 20), his heavily pregnant mistress, would be given every comfort to ensure the safe arrival of his heir.

The Duchy of Valoria, a vast, fertile prize, now lay open to his command. Its resources, its people, its very future, would be hammered into another pillar of Daemon's ever-growing empire. The raw power he wielded, his own formidable Physique, was only matched by his relentless ambition. This was merely another step.

### Clarification on Level 9 Advancement Choices:

As per the **Destiny Core Rules, Section III. Character Progression System, Rule 3.5 (Aptitude Score Improvement & Feats)**, a character gains an "Advancement Choice" (either increasing Aptitude Scores or choosing a Feat) at Class Levels **4, 8, 12, 16, and 19.**

Since Daemon has just reached **Level 9**, this is **not one of the designated levels** for an Aptitude Score Improvement or Feat choice. He gained his last choice at Level 8 (Physique +2).

Therefore, Daemon does **not** get to make a specific Level 9 advancement choice. His power has increased through his general level-up (HP, potential class features, Combat Prowess Bonus), but not through a specific ASI/Feat choice at this level.
