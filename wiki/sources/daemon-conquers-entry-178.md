Game Master:
Daemon (Level 7, Age 30) listened to their counsel, then fixed his gaze on Isolde (Age: ~22). "And your warriors, Lady Isolde?" he pressed, his voice firm. "What is their number, their quality? Perhaps we should integrate a contingent of my own Level 3 recruits, perhaps even one of my veterans, to oversee their training and ensure their loyalty. They can answer to you directly when I am not present, managing the defense and security of Blackwood Keep. You will be responsible for the efficiency of their training and their discipline, just as you will for the land's bounty. House Blackwood will, from this day forward, manage its own affairs under your direct command, so long as it serves my empire."

Isolde's eyes widened almost imperceptibly, a flicker of surprise quickly replaced by a shrewd, ambitious glint. "My Lord," she said, her voice gaining strength, "my household guard numbers perhaps fifty men, mostly older, loyal, but not honed for true warfare. Your proposal is... wise. Their training, overseen by your veterans, would strengthen our defenses immeasurably. I accept this responsibility."

Kael (Level 6, Age 25) nodded in approval. "An excellent strategy, My Lord. Integrating their forces under Lady Isolde's direct command, with our oversight, ensures both local control and unwavering loyalty to your person. It secures the south without deploying your core army."

Daemon gave a curt, satisfied nod. The pieces were falling into place. Blackwood Keep was now not just a granary, but a fortified bastion, autonomously managed, further solidifying his grip on the burgeoning empire.
