The Duke, his face ashen, dropped his sword with a clatter. His eyes, wide with primal fear, fixed on Daemon's dragon-scaled form. He raised his hands, trembling. "No! Please! I surrender!" he stammered, collapsing to his knees amidst the opulent furnishings.

Daemon advanced, his boots crunching over the shattered remains of the door. He seized the Duke by the scruff of his silken tunic, hauling him to his feet with a single, effortless motion. The Duke dangled, whimpering, a helpless, terrified puppet in Daemon's iron grip. The capture was complete.
