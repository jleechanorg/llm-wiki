Daemon (Level 5, Age 30) stood tall in Oakhaven Keep, his vision already stretching beyond the conquered baronies. His warriors were strong, but his army was still nascent. He needed information, and he needed more disciplined bodies. He turned to the three Level 4 Great Weapon Masters who had accompanied him on the recent raids, their faces hardened by the brutal campaign.

"You three," Daemon commanded, his voice a low, gravelly rumble. "Travel west, towards Riverbend. Do not engage. Do not reveal yourselves. Simply observe. I want to know its defenses, its patrols, its strengths, and its weaknesses. Learn what kind of prey it is. Report back in thirty days. If you encounter anything significant, send word immediately." The three warriors, grim-faced but resolute, nodded and moved to prepare. Kael (Level 4, Age 25), Castellan of Oakhaven, would remain behind with his single remaining veteran warrior to oversee the Keep.

With the scouts dispatched, Daemon's gaze shifted to the teeming, terrified masses in the training yard. "For the next thirty days," he roared, his voice echoing through the Keep and beyond, "we will forge steel! You will eat, sleep, and breathe the blade. Every waking moment will be spent in training. Those who break will be discarded. Those who endure will become the backbone of my true army!" He would personally oversee this process, moving between Oakhaven and Stonehaven, ensuring his Castellans pushed their respective populations to the breaking point, transforming cowering civilians into fear-driven, obedient instruments of his will. This was not just about building an army; it was about shaping an empire, one brutal, disciplined step at a time.

**Time Skip: 30 Days of Brutal Training and Scouting**

For the next thirty days, the twin baronies of Stonehaven and Oakhaven hummed with a grim, relentless activity. Daemon (Level 5, Age 30) was a relentless presence, moving between the Keeps, his dragon-scaled armor a symbol of absolute authority. He personally oversaw the training of the Basic Recruits, often stepping into the mud-churned yards to deliver brutal demonstrations of combat, or to publicly punish those who faltered or rebelled. Under his and his Castellans' unforgiving eyes, the terrified populace was drilled incessantly in basic formations, crude weapon handling, and the brutal discipline of Daemon’s burgeoning army. The whimpers of fear slowly hardened into grunts of effort, and their movements, though still clumsy, gained a terrifying, desperate efficiency.

Meanwhile, the three Level 4 Great Weapon Master warriors Daemon had dispatched ventured towards Riverbend. They moved with the stealth and efficiency of predators, observing the roads, the patrols, and the general state of the larger city. After thirty days, they returned, grim-faced, with valuable intelligence.

*   The populace is well-fed and seemingly loyal to their current Duke, who is known for his strict but fair rule.
