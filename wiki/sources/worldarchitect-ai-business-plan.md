## Summary
First AI RPG platform built for authentic gameplay with enterprise-grade state consistency. Targets the AI gaming market projected at $27.47B by 2029 with 42.3% CAGR. Differentiates through solved memory issues, D&D 5e mechanics, and BYOK pricing model.

## Key Claims

- **State Consistency**: 0% desynchronization achievement vs competitors' memory degradation issues
- **Market Position**: Only platform combining authentic D&D 5e mechanics, superior state consistency, and BYOK transparent pricing
- **Technical Moat**: SCORE Framework achieves 23.6% higher coherence, 89.7% emotional consistency, 41.8% fewer hallucinations
- **Financial Projections**: Break-even at month 24, growing from 500 users ($180K revenue) in 2025 to 8,000 users ($2.88M) in 2028
