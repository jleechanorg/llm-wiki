The campaign follows the rise of Aizen Sousuke, a character whose ambition and intellect have allowed him to transcend mortality and ascend to godhood. The story has progressed through several major arcs:

The Rise to Power: Starting as a mortal nobleman in Baldur's Gate, Aizen uncovered a plot by the Cult of the Dead Three led by his own family. He defeated his father, converted his mother (Seraphina), and in a climactic battle, consumed the portfolio of the god Bane, becoming a nascent God of Tyranny. He later consumed the portfolio of Myrkul, becoming a Greater Deity of Tyranny and Death.
The Great Deception Begins: Aizen orchestrated a plan to secretly "retire" the Overgod Ao, becoming The Absolute and the hidden master of all reality. To amuse himself, he created the "Godswar Game" in Faerûn. He cast his subordinate, Gale (a mortal wizard he elevated to godhood), as the public villain and new God of Magic, who claimed to have "consumed" the goddess Mystra.
The False Resistance & The Godswar: Aizen orchestrated a "false resistance" to oppose Gale. This involved his own avatar playing the part of a "defeated, redeemed god," a resurrected and re-empowered Mystra (now a mortal archmage), her legendary Chosen, and a "Third Force" of powerful, purified heroes from the world of Cerilia, led by the former tyrant Raesene. This led to a major battle in Neverwinter where Gale was ultimately defeated and killed by Raesene's forces.
The Recasting: Following Gale's failure, Aizen brought all key players to his personal "pleasure world" of Mystara. He resurrected Gale as a weak, cursed mortal and made him Mystra's "pet." He then re-ascended Mystra to full godhood, casting her as the new primary villain for the Faerûn game. Her new directive is to be a subtle, insidious tyrant. Her first major act was to successfully orchestrate a major schism within the Church of Tyr.
