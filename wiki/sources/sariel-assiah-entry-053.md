Timestamp: Year of the New Peace, 11. Month of Kythorn, Day 2. 10:30 AM.
Location: Deep Archives, Zenith Spire, Aeterna.
Key Game Stats: Level 2 (XP: 1000/2700), Gold: 10gp, HP: 10/10

You closed the final journal, the heavy thud echoing in the silent vault. Your mind was a maelstrom of revelation. The Null Genesis Project was not just a historical curiosity; it was the blueprint for your mother's entire life. She was the culmination of that terrifying experiment, a perfect synthesis of absolute power and the chilling satisfaction in wielding it. And this inherited potential, this "Joyful Predator" nature, now lay dormant, but undeniably present, within you. The same terrifying intellect, the same capacity for cold, precise unmaking, now thrummed beneath your own skin. The silence of the vault seemed to mock your earlier guilt; your mother hadn't been broken, she had been *unleashed*. And now, you held the key to understanding that same, terrifying unleashing within yourself.

Your gaze snapped from the chilling text to Magister Kantos, who stood, a fragile, ancient sentinel in the dim light. A horrifying possibility, born from the raw data of the journals, clawed its way into your mind, a connection that tied your mother's terrifying power directly to your family's ancient curse.

"The Empyrean Affliction," you whispered, your voice barely audible in the profound stillness of the vault. You took a step toward him, your Starlit Eyes wide with a new, terrifying comprehension. "You spoke of 'madness,' of 'uncontrolled rage' in the other archives. You said the 'Joyful Predator' was a 'perversion of celestial power.' Is it... is it related, Magister? Is this 'joy' in unmaking... is it another manifestation of the Affliction? A different kind of madness?"

Kantos visibly flinched, his ancient face paling further. He wrung his ink-stained hands, his gaze darting to the arcane wards that sealed the vault, as if seeking confirmation that the secret would remain confined. He understood the terrifying implication of your question – the very core of your lineage, the foundation of your family's perceived divine right, intertwined with a power so utterly monstrous. He closed his eyes for a moment, a deep, shuddering sigh escaping him, laden with the weight of unspeakable truths.

"Princess," he rasped, his voice a dry, strained whisper, "the deepest lore suggests... yes. The Empyrean Affliction, in its rawest, purest form, is not merely uncontrolled rage or paranoia. Those are its crude, overt symptoms. Its true essence is a perversion of the celestial will: the profound, inherent desire for absolute control, for perfect order enforced through overwhelming, merciless unmaking." His eyes, filled with profound sorrow, met yours. "The 'Joyful Predator' is not separate from it, Sariel. It is the Affliction, refined. The madness, distilled into a chilling, lucid, and terrifyingly effective form of destructive genius. A form your mother mastered."
