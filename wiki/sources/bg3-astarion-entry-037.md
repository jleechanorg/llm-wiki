The thought of binding a foe with magic, or relentlessly tracking them, sends a shiver of dark satisfaction through him.

With his new abilities settling into his consciousness, Astarion's gaze sweeps over the gruesome scene. The Githyanki and the burly man, still breathing heavily, seem to share his pragmatic thought, their eyes also scanning the fallen creatures, though they await his lead.
