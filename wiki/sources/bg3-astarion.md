## Summary

A D&D 5e campaign where the player plays Astarion Ancunín from the beginning of Baldur's Gate 3. The campaign begins on the Nautiloid, covers the crash to the Ravaged Beach, and follows Astarion's perspective through early companion recruitment. Initial character is a Rogue; player changes to Ranger mid-creation. Key mechanical elements include vampire spawn abilities (Bite, Sunlight Sensitivity, Fey Ancestry), Sneak Attack, Expertise in Stealth/Deception, and the tadpole's role in Astarion's potential freedom from Cazador.

## Key Claims

- Astarion is a Sardonic Vampire Spawn, formerly a magistrate in Baldur's Gate, turned by Cazador Szarr 200 years ago
- His core motivation is regaining freedom and independence from Cazador; the tadpole offers a unique path to achieving this
- His greatest fear is returning to Cazador's control, becoming a mindless thrall, or being exposed and destroyed
- The campaign covers the full BG3 Act 1 from Astarion's perspective — Nautiloid captivity, crash, beach survival, companion encounters
- Player modifies the character: changes from Rogue to Ranger, adjusts stats to STR 8, DEX 17, CON 16, INT 8, WIS 10, CHA 16, and requests "Make me a ranger" during character creation

## Key Quotes

> "To regain his freedom and independence from Cazador, to secure his own survival, and ultimately, to gain power and control over his own destiny so he is never a thrall again." — Astarion's core motivation
