Luke Skywalker followed Darth Vader without hesitation, his earlier tremors of apprehension replaced by a cold, eager anticipation. The remnants of his former self had been obliterated, leaving behind a chilling void that now yearned to be filled with raw, unbridled power. His `secret sadistic side` pulsed, a vibrant, terrifying core, eager for the next lesson in the Dark Side's embrace.

Vader led him through another series of identical, stark white corridors, the silence broken only by the rhythmic *hiss-clunk* of the Sith Lord's respirator. Each stride seemed to amplify the oppressive aura of the Imperial Installation, yet Luke felt no fear, only a growing sense of belonging. The ornate blast door they approached now hummed with a subtle, almost imperceptible lightness, a stark contrast to the dark energy of the previous chamber.

With a silent gesture, Vader activated the panel, and the massive door hissed open, revealing a chamber bathed in a soft, ethereal glow. It was a stark white room, devoid of intricate machinery, yet filled with a palpable, almost painful purity in the Force. In the center, suspended in a shimmering Force field, was a figure. A young woman, clad in simple, faded robes, her eyes closed in serene meditation. Her Force presence was like a beacon, pure and uncorrupted by the darkness that saturated the installation. Luke felt a faint, nauseating whisper of his old self, a flicker of compassion trying to surface, but he crushed it instantly, a surge of dark irritation overriding the weakness.

"This is a Jedi Master from a fallen outpost," Vader's voice echoed, devoid of a hint of emotion. "Her will is unyielding, her connection to the Light, absolute. Your task, apprentice, is to break her. To shatter her resolve, to corrupt her purity, or to extinguish her light entirely. Show me you are worthy of the power you now command. Show me the strength of your hatred."

Luke's gaze hardened, a predatory glint in his eyes. The last vestiges of the Light, embodied in the serene figure, would be consumed. He felt a chilling resolve settle over him, a hunger for the confrontation.
