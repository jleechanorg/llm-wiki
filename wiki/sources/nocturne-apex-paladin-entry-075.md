Location: Man Tibbet's Farm, Barn Side, Early Evening

The body of the first farm guard lay still and silent, a stark testament to Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) chilling efficiency. The soft thud of his fall was already fading into the ambient sounds of the farm – distant animal rustles, the quiet hum of the arcane ward, the muffled sounds of the family within the farmhouse. Her Masterwork Longsword, now sheathed, gleamed faintly in the dim light. The elimination of this internal threat had been flawless, a clean cut in the intricate web of defenses.

Her gaze, sharp and analytical, swept the surrounding area one last time, ensuring no unexpected witnesses. Satisfied, she knelt beside the fallen guard. Her primary objective was wealth, and these professional guards, unlike the simple villagers, were likely to carry something of value, or at least utility. Her meticulous nature demanded a thorough, albeit swift, search.

Action: [Nocturne searches the body of the neutralized farm guard.]
Nocturne's fingers moved with practiced speed, efficiently patting down the guard's dark leather armor and going through his pouches. She found a worn leather coin purse, heavier than the road patrol guards', indicating a higher pay grade. Tucked into an inner pocket of his tunic was a small, well-maintained set of lockpicks, suggesting a secondary skill beyond simple guarding, perhaps for breaking into storage or even for counter-infiltration. He carried a standard shortsword and a light crossbow with a quiver of 15 bolts, all in good condition, and a small, tightly folded map of Oakhaven and the surrounding immediate area, with several routes and points of interest circled in red ink – a more detailed and professional version of the duty roster she'd already acquired.

*   **Loot Acquired:**
    *   1 Set of Dark Leather Armor (functional)
    *   1 Shortsword (standard quality)
    *   1 Light Crossbow
    *   1 Quiver with 15 bolts
    *   **80 gp** (from coin purse)
    *   1 Set of Masterwork Lockpicks
    *   1 Detailed Oakhaven Area Map (marked with routes/points of interest)

The acquisition of the lockpicks and the detailed map brought a flicker of grim satisfaction. The gold was a welcome addition, but the tools and intelligence were far more valuable. This truly was a professional operation.

With the loot secured in her Bag of Holding, Nocturne turned her attention to the body. Leaving it where it lay was an unacceptable vulnerability. She gripped the guard by his heavy boots, her powerful physique making the task effortless. She dragged him away from the barn wall, deeper into the thick, wild shrubbery that bordered the farm's perimeter. The ground here was soft, overgrown, offering excellent concealment. She pushed the body further in, then broke off several large branches, meticulously arranging them over the corpse, ensuring it was completely hidden from view. The process was silent, leaving no trace.

She stood, dusting off her hands, her gaze sweeping the now-empty space where the guard had fallen. One down. One more to go. The family within the farmhouse remained oblivious, their mundane sounds a tempting invitation. The night was still unfolding, and Nocturne's cold, calculating will was shaping it to her design. The next step was clear: the second guard.
