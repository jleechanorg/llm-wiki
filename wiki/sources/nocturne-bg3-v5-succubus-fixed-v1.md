## Summary
A D&D 5e survival horror campaign set immediately after the Nautiloid crash on a ravaged beach, running parallel to Baldur's Gate 3's Descent act. Nocturne Sosuke (age 16) is de-powered from Level 6 to Level 1, her infernal energy suppressed by a tadpole. She retains her devastating CHA 16 and the Siren's Leverage trait but is physically fragile (CON 8, HP 7). The Malcanthet contract replaces Zariel as her infernal patron. Scenes 1-20 cover the post-crash beach survival, first encounters with BG3 companions (Shadowheart), combat with Intellect Devourers, and the GotM (Game of Thrones mode) integration variant. A "Malcanthet's Favor" trait provides plot armor (reduced to 0 HP triggers incorporeality instead of unconsciousness).
