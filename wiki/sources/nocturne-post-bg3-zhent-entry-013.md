The Stitcher's fingers cease their work, the needle hovering half an inch from the leather pauldron. She doesn't look up immediately, letting your words hang in the sterile air of her workshop. 'Credentials,' she says, her voice devoid of inflection. 'Everyone who finds this place claims credentials. Most are dead within a week for wasting my time.' She sets down her tools with deliberate precision, each movement economical and without waste.

Your gloved hand reaches into the concealed pouch within your armor, withdrawing the small disc of blackened metal that Zarys provided upon your acceptance into the Zhentarim's elite ranks. You place it on the polished obsidian counter between you. The Zhentarim crest - a black shield crossed by silver chains - catches the dim light, the marking of one who has proven their worth through blood.

'Better,' the Stitcher acknowledges, though her expression remains unchanged. 'But symbols can be stolen. What is your purpose tonight, Silent Blade? The Zhentarim doesn't send their best to browse like common mercenaries.'

You lean forward slightly, your voice low but confident. 'I have a mission requiring superior ranged capability. Zarys sent me specifically for titanstring. The Ironhead Legion needs to learn that distance offers no safety from Zhentarim justice.'

At the mention of both Zarys and the Ironhead Legion, the Stitcher's posture shifts almost imperceptibly - the subtle relaxation of someone recognizing legitimate business rather than curiosity seekers. 'Zarys wouldn't send anyone to me for a routine extermination. Your reputation precedes you - the Elfsong job, the warehouse incident. You understand the value of precision.'

She rises from her stool and moves to a locked weapons rack at the back of the workshop. 'I have exactly one titanstring bow ready for deployment. It was commissioned by a client who... met with an untimely end before collecting it. The string itself is harvested from titan sinew, treated with alchemical processes that render it nearly unbreakable. It provides exceptional range and power - beyond what ordinary mortals can achieve.'

She returns with a bow of exquisite craftsmanship, the wood dark as obsidian and the string humming with barely contained tension. 'Five thousand gold. Non-negotiable. For that price, I include a quiver of twenty arrows guaranteed not to shatter under the bow's full draw.' Her eyes meet yours. 'Can you afford to become death itself, Silent Blade?'
