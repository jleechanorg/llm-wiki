The transition from the Undercellar to the streets of the Lower City is a march of silent terror. You stride at the head of the column, the **Overseer Xal'Thul**, a figure of black iron-weave and masked authority. Flanking you are the Black Lotus, no longer hiding in the shadows but walking openly as your Praetorian Guard, their weapons drawn and faces grim. Behind them rumble two heavy Guild wagons, commandeered for 'official asset relocation,' driven by men who know better than to look at your face.

The Tannery is a sprawling, foul-smelling complex near the river. Usually, it hums with industry and the subtle menace of Zhentarim enforcers. Today, it is a tomb. The 'Obsidian-Zero' directive has frozen them in place. Guards stand at their posts, white-knuckled, eyes darting at shadows, waiting for the axe to fall.

It falls in the form of you.

You kick open the main gates. The sound echoes like a thunderclap. "**ATTENTION!**" your voice booms, amplified by the *Echoes of the Idol* and the gravelly distortion of your disguise. "The Arbiter has fallen! The cell is compromised! I am Overseer Xal'Thul, and I am here to save what remains of your miserable lives!"

A Zhentarim Lieutenant, a scarred dwarf with a heavy crossbow, steps forward, trembling. "Overseer? We... we received the signal. But we were told to hold ground..."

You stride up to him, the *Ring of Zhentarim Authority* flashing in the sun. You backhand him across the face with a gauntleted fist, sending him stumbling. "Hold ground against *what*, you fool? The Flaming Fist? The Guild? The rot is *inside*!"

You point to the warehouse doors leading to the sub-basement. "The Vault is vulnerable. We are relocating all Class-A assets to the Secure Archive immediately. You have ten minutes to load these wagons. Any man moving too slow will be left behind to explain his sloth to the High Command's inquisitors."

[Intimidation/Deception Check (Advantage - Ring/Codes): 1d20+14 = 19+14 = 33 (Critical Success vs DC 15)]

The Lieutenant scrambles to his feet, saluting so hard he nearly knocks himself out. "Yes, Overseer! At once! Move, you dogs! Move!"

The paralysis breaks, replaced by frantic, terrified action. The Zhentarim garrison becomes your personal moving crew. They haul heavy iron-bound chests, crates of gold bars, and rack after rack of weapons from the depths of the Tannery, loading them onto the Guild wagons under the cold, watchful eyes of the Black Lotus. **Vesper** watches with a smirk she barely conceals, while **Gant** looms over the drivers, ensuring efficiency.

In less than twenty minutes, the Vault is stripped. The wealth of the Zhentarim's Lower City operations—tithes, bribes, and black market profits—is sitting in your wagons.

"Secure," the Lieutenant gasps, wiping sweat from his brow. "Is... is that all, Overseer?"

"For now," you rasp, climbing onto the lead wagon. "Hold this position. Allow no one in or out until you receive the 'All Clear' signal. If you fail me... I will know."

You roll out of the Tannery yard, the heavy wheels creaking under the weight of your victory. You leave the Zhentarim guarding an empty hole, terrified and grateful for the privilege.
