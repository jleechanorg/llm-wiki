## Summary
System for guiding RPG character transition from mortal to hidden deity status. Implements a multi-layer deception protocol where players maintain mortal identities while accessing cosmic power. Setting-agnostic - works with any pantheon/cosmic power structure. Triggers on divine_potential ≥100, level 25+, or narrative divine milestone.

## Key Claims
- **Multi-Layer Deception**: Players maintain 3 layers (Mask/Persona/Source) to hide divine status
- **Divine Dissonance**: Using power while masked creates detectable disturbance in reality's fabric
- **Domain Truths**: Reality laws that bend to player's will when present
- **Setting-Agnostic**: Replaces D&D pantheon examples with campaign equivalents

## Key Quotes
> "Divinity is a dangerous gift. The Pantheon does not welcome newcomers."

> "The Central Tension: Every use of divine power while wearing the Mortal Mask creates 'Divine Dissonance' — a disturbance in reality that the Pantheon can detect."
