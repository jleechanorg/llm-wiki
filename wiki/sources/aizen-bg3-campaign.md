## Summary

Aizen Bg3 is a dark psychological noir campaign set in post-war Baldur's Gate after the defeat of the Absolute. The player controls Sosuke Aizen, a sixteen-year-old prodigy who secretly owns "The White Garden," a sanctuary where he manipulates and extracts secrets from the city's elite. The campaign features god mode mechanics, hypnosis, psychological manipulation, and a slow-burn takeover of the fractured city.

## Key Claims

- Aizen is a Level 4 Wizard secretly controlling House Sosuke while his father Lord Kaito handles public affairs
- The White Garden is a front for harvesting secrets and psyches of Baldur's Gate's elite
- The Hogyoku drives Aizen's pursuit of evolutionary transcendence
- Lord Garton Irlingstar is being blackmailed over his iron shipment
- Vespera is a "Flower" or broken-will asset of the Garden
- Post-Absolute Baldur's Gate has a power vacuum being filled by the Zhentarim and Thieves' Guild
