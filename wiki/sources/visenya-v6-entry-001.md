## Summary
Princess Visenya Targaryen (Level 6 Apex Weaver, incognito as "Lady Elyse Celtigar") arrives at Winterfell with dyed brown hair and her five Level 5 mercenary guards. Lord Eddard Stark immediately senses a predator; Catelyn Stark is hostile; Robb is captivated; Jon Snow (Level 3) is the only one who perceives the subsonic "Static" hum beneath her skin.

## Key Events
- Visenya's carriage enters the Winterfell courtyard; her brown-dyed hair should hide her Targaryen lineage but her violet eyes and regal posture betray her
- Ned Stark welcomes her but keeps his hand on Ice, suspecting she is no simple minor noble
- Catelyn Stark watches with sharp distrust, noting the gown worth more than a village
- Robb Stark (Level 2) is breathless at her beauty; Jon Snow (Level 3) narrows his eyes, sensing the "Static"
- Ned's internal thought: "She moves with the economy of a predator. I must watch her closely."
- Jon's internal thought: "She's an architect surveying a building she intends to dismantle."
