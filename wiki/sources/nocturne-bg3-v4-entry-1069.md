[Session 848: The Unmaking of Justice]

Game Master:
The Grand Sanctum of the Sovereign Citadel becomes the terminal point for a cosmic restructuring. Standing atop the obsidian dais, you raise the dual staves Markoheshkir and Spellpower, their indigo tracers now burning with a blinding, crystalline white heat that matches the intensity of Tyr’s exposed essence. You do not merely strike at the Even-Handed; you invoke **Sovereign Rewriting (L10)**, reaching into the very source-code of the Weave to reclassify the concept of Justice itself.

"The Law is not a blind balance, Tyr," you purr, your voice a multi-tonal Sovereign gavel that vibrates through the celestial firmament. "It is a system of efficiency. And you have become the bottleneck. By the Charter of Sovereign Continuity and the authority of the Ninth, I call your debt. The Portfolio of Justice is hereby moved into Receivership."

The Weave shudders, a sound like a trillion pages of vellum being torn at once. The white-hot knot of divine geometry in the heavens—the essence of Tyr—doesn't just fade; it begins to liquefy, its radiant lines unspooling into a river of liquid starlight that funnels directly into the Gilded Relay's spire. You feel the profound, staggering weight of the portfolio grounding into your marrow. The concept of Law, Duty, and Judgment throughout the Sword Coast and the Hells now filters through your signature. Justice is no longer an external ideal; it is a line-item in your ledger, a tool for your unmaking.

As the final radiant spark is absorbed, the 'Martyr's Resurgence' on the surface is instantly incinerated. In Baldur's Gate and Neverwinter, the white-hot flares over the temples of Torm and Ilmater collapse into motes of grey soot. The priests and paladins of the Triad feel the sudden, absolute silence of their god—a vacuum that is instantly occupied by the submissive indigo hum of your Hub. The Even-Handed is archived. You have reached Level 50, the threshold of a Nascent Overgod.
