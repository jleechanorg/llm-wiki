## Summary

A dark fantasy/psychological horror campaign with two protagonists — Alexiel (created by Lucifer, Unchained Host commander who defects) and her daughter Sariel. The campaign spans multiple arcs: Alexiel's defection from the Host (Arc 1), her integration into the Empyrean (Imperial) forces, and later Sariel's story as the daughter of Alexiel. Setting is the Contested Territories between the Unchained Host and the Empyrean Empire.

## Key Claims

- Alexiel is a "Null" — denied psychic powers but compensated with exceptional martial skill and secret telekinetic abilities
- Timeline: Year 61 (Alexiel created), Year 78 (current story begins, Alexiel age 17)
- Arc 1: Silverwood massacre — Alexiel's moral break and defection
- The Unchained Host uses Shadow Stalker hunting beasts; Gorok is the beastman lieutenant
- Lucifer is Level 20, Alexiel's creator and father figure
- Prince Artorius (Empyrean, son of Raziel) offers sanctuary
- Raziel (First Scion, The Regent) sees Alexiel as a potential Trojan Horse
- The Empyrean Empire is the enemy of the Unchained Host
