## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in Game of Thrones "The Grinding" era (298 AC). Visenya Targaryen, age 12, is an INTJ "Apex Predator" class with the archetype "The Bored Godling / The Vivisector." Chaotic evil, she views the world as entertainment and herself as the only one worth watching. Her companion is an Entropy dragon in spirit form.
