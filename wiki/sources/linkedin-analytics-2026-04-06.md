## Summary

LinkedIn analytics showing 113,732 total impressions (+338.7% vs prior 90 days). Community/interactive posts dominate, funny/relatable content punches above weight, story posts outperform pure info posts.

## Key Claims

- Top posts: Community calls-to-action (#1 with 12,767 impressions), "I broke GitHub" story (#2 with 8,396 impressions), CodeRabbit funny screenshot (#3)
- Sundays and Mondays/Tuesdays are strongest posting days
- Tuesdays are sneaky strong for LinkedIn algorithm
- Content Insights:
  - Community/interactive posts dominate
  - Funny/relatable dev screenshots have high impressions-per-effort ratio
  - "Story" posts (personal narrative + technical detail) are best combo
  - Detailed how-to content is mid-tier
  - Short cryptic posts with images do fine but don't break out

## Tactical Recommendations

- Post Sunday evening or Monday morning for spike potential
- Lead with story hook ("I broke X this weekend" format)
- More CodeRabbit / funny dev screenshots
- Community calls-to-action boost comment counts which drives reach
- Avoid posts that are just links or images with no context
