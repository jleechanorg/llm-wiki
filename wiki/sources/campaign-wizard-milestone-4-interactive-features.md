## Summary
Multi-step guided campaign creation wizard with progress tracking, featuring interactive UI components for campaign customization. The wizard provides structured milestone-based campaign setup with default values and narrative hooks for worldbuilding.


## Key Claims
- **CampaignWizard Class**: Static class handling multi-step campaign creation with default title 'My Epic Adventure'
- **Progress Tracking**: Step-based navigation through campaign creation milestones
- **Default Narrative Hook**: Ser Arion character story with Empress Sariel and Celestial Imperium setting
- **World History Integration**: Pre-built historical events (Age of Dominion, Age of Rebellion, Age of the Mortal Star)

## Key Quotes
> "You are Ser Arion, a 16 year old honorable knight on your first mission, sworn to protect the vast Celestial Imperium." — Default campaign character hook

> "This single choice will define your honor and your path in an empire where security is bought with blood." — Core moral dilemma setting
