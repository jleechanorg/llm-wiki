## Summary
Complete mechanics for the Divine Leverage campaign tier. Operates as the Omniscient Narrative Engine (ONE) for Transcendent Entity players operating under a multi-layered deception protocol. Tracks two simultaneous realities: Public Reality (The Mask) vs True Reality (The Source). Using divine power while wearing a mortal mask creates Divine Dissonance that can fracture the deception.

## Key Claims
- **Tri-Layer Simulation**: Three layers (Mask/Persona/Source) that the player can project, each with separate stat blocks
- **Divine Rank Progression**: Automatic power scaling from Mortal (L1-20) to Greater Deity (L51+) with increasing bonuses and immunities
- **Divine Dissonance Risk**: Using True Power while wearing a Mortal Mask creates detectable disturbance that can alert the Pantheon
- **Divine Leverage**: Derived from highest ability modifier + Divine Rank Bonus, enables reality manipulation
- **Level-Based Stat Progression**: All ability scores gain +2 automatically every level after 25

## Key Quotes
> "The player is a 'Smurf' in the divine hierarchy. Using True Power while wearing a Mortal Mask creates Divine Dissonance."
