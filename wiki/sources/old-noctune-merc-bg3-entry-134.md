You weigh the purse in your hand. It is heavy—the payroll for a smuggling run, the bribes for the dockmaster, the personal spoils of a captain. You do not open it. You do not count it.

Instead, you turn to the Black Lotus squad. **Vesper** is wiping her blade on a dead sailor's tunic. **Gant** is cracking his knuckles, his eyes dark. **Silas** has descended from the rigging, and **Nyx** is watching you with a hungry curiosity.

"The Zhentarim hoards wealth like a dragon with a toothache," you say, your voice cutting through the twilight air, amplified by the *Echoes of the Idol*. "They believe gold is for the leaders, and scraps are for the blades."

You toss the heavy purse to Vesper. She catches it with a reflex snap of her hand, the coins clinking mutely inside. The weight surprises her.

"Two hundred gold pieces, at a guess," you continue, your gaze sweeping over them. "A bonus. Immediate. Tax-free. Because under the Shadow Monarch, the blade eats first."

Vesper looks at the pouch, then at her squad, and finally back to you. The professional detachment in her flinty eyes cracks, replaced by a gleam of genuine, mercenary avarice—and something firmer. Respect.

"The blade eats first," Vesper repeats, a slow grin spreading across her face. She ties the pouch to her belt. "We like this doctrine. Consider the Black Lotus... enthusiastic."

**Gant** nods, a deep rumble in his chest. "Orders?"

"Secure the prisoners in the fo'c'sle," you command. "Gant, Nyx—ensure they don't find their courage. Silas, watch the docks. Vesper, you're with me. We have a cargo to inspect."

You turn towards the cargo hold, the keys in your hand cold and heavy. The ship is yours. The squad is yours. And below decks, the true value of the *Silent Wave* awaits.
