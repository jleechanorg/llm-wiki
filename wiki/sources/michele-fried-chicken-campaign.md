## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign (adapted for modern setting) where Michele, a Bard (College of Culinary Arts), opens a Korean fried chicken restaurant "Crispy Dreams" in Silver Lake, Los Angeles. The story follows the challenges of navigating gentrification tensions, securing permits, competing with celebrity chefs, and building community support.

## Key Characters
- **Michele** (Lvl 4 Bard) - The protagonist, undocumented immigrant and culinary visionary
- **Marcus** (Lvl 3) - Kitchen manager and loyal crew member
- **Kim** (Lvl 1) - Marketing specialist with a hidden San Francisco past
- **Chef Jin** (Lvl 4) - Korean fried chicken specialist who joins as equity partner
- **Sarah** (Lvl 1) - College student and customer service lead
- **Julian** (Lvl 10) - Rival celebrity chef who runs the "Golden Bird" pop-up

## Key Organizations
- **Crispy Dreams** - The fried chicken restaurant
- **Silver Lake Shift** - Neighborhood activist group initially opposed to the restaurant
- **Grover's Poultry** - Premium chicken supplier
- **Mr. Park's Korean Grocery** - Starch and specialty ingredient supplier
- **The Golden Bird** - Julian's competing pop-up restaurant

## Campaign Arc
1. **Setup** (Scenes 1-6): Character creation, location acquisition, hiring initial crew
2. **Permits & Community** (Scenes 7-15): Liquor license application, Silver Lake Shift confrontation
3. **Korean Pivot** (Scenes 16-18): Hiring Chef Jin, pivoting to Korean fried chicken
4. **Crisis** (Scenes 19-22): Burglary, Julian's sabotage, Chloe Luxe confrontation
5. **Struggle & Despair** (Scenes 23-30): Business for sale, nearly giving up
6. **Vindication** (Scenes 31-40): Viral exposure of Julian, community partnership secured
7. **Expansion** (Scenes 41-42): City partnership interest, operational scaling challenges

## Key Themes
- Gentrification and community vs. business development
- Undocumented immigrant struggles in LA
- Social media influence on small business
- Redemption and second chances (Kim's arc)
- Community organizing and grassroots support
