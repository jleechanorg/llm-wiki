Their silence, a testament to their shattered will, offered no information, only the raw, visceral scent of despair. A cold, decisive clarity settled in Gladius’s gut. Gladius felt a chilling hum beneath his skin, the ultimate satisfaction of bending their deepest love into an instrument of their own torment. He watched their eyes, saw the dawning, absolute horror as his other hand, holding the Silent Strangler’s Cord, moved. The dark cord slid into place with an almost tender precision, around the small neck.

The exquisite performance was complete.
