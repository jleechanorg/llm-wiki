He felt a deep, chilling hum beneath his skin; this was the raw, immediate pleasure of total dominance, of twisting their love into an instrument of their own torment.

"Last chance," Gladius’s voice rumbled, a low, gravelly current, cutting through their ragged breaths. He maintained his grip on the child, allowing the unspoken threat to fully permeate the space. He watched their eyes, searching for the crack, the moment their stubbornness would finally shatter. The farmer’s body convulsed, a silent, desperate effort to speak, but only a choked sob escaped. Only the profound, intimate humiliation of their helplessness.

A faint sneer touched Gladius’s lips. *Pathetic.* Even in their broken state, they clung to their meager secrets, their simple defiance. It was a frustrating, yet ultimately satisfying, display. Their silence, born of overwhelming despair rather than strength, merely amplified his power. They truly had nothing of consequence, no hidden leverage for him to exploit, nothing but their lives and their profound, simple love for their child. Their strength was their weakness. The dawn, now painting vibrant oranges and purples on the eastern sky, illuminated their utter defeat. Gladius felt no anger, only a cold, decisive clarity. The performance was complete. The final act awaited.
