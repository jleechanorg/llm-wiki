## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign where the player controls Nocturne, a 16-year-old half-elf bard who was sold into a high-end brothel after her family's bankruptcy during the Absolute crisis. The campaign begins at level 9 after hijacking a Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual to Zariel. Themes: survival, revenge, power acquisition, moral corruption, infernal bargains. 1,212 scene entries across multiple campaigns.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is a College of Perdition bard, marked by Zariel after redirecting an archdevil's ritual
- The Cassalanter family orchestrated the family's ruin through debt manipulation
- Sisters Elara (18) and Lyra (14) were held at Sunder-Stone Villa as "Refined Collateral" for sacrifice
- Zhentarim partnership secured, tunnels become "Sovereign Zhentarim Territory" for 99 years
- House Sosuke established as new identity after claiming the Cassalanter manor
- BG3 origin characters (Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Gale, etc.) appear as potential companions
