## Summary
Three weeks pass. Visenya systematically dismantles the Stark household from within. Robb is now functionally addicted to her presence and lies to his father with increasing fluency. Jon is drawn to her as the only person who treats him as an intellectual equal. Sansa is a devoted acolyte. Arya has been neutralized through combat training. Ned and Catelyn are at war with each other — Visenya has turned their own communication patterns against them.

## Key Events
- **Robb Stark**: A "boy unspooled" — lies to Ned with increasing, agonizing fluency; each lie is a new brick in the wall between him and his heritage
- **Jon Snow**: Treated with clinical, intellectual flirtation about his "Valyrian bone structure"; Robb is jealous of the long conversations Jon has with Visenya in the armory
- **Sansa**: Her room becomes a shrine to Southern etiquette; she mimics Visenya's walk, tilt of head, and calculated silences
- **Arya (Level 1)**: Neutralized through "physics of the strike" training with Visenya's parrying dagger; views Visenya as the only "Lady" who doesn't smell of perfume and embroidery
- **Catelyn**: Whispered "concerns" about Ned's secrecy regarding Jon framed as lack of trust in her strength
- **Ned**: Praised for his "burden of silence" to his face; Catelyn's sharpness interpreted as failure of Northern endurance
