## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign featuring Nocturne Sosuke, the "Black Diamond," a sixteen-year-old Half-Elf Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) with CHA 20. Set in the Shattered Sanctum within the Goblin Camp, where Minthara challenges Nocturne's resolve to raid the Emerald Grove. The campaign features the "Velvet Cage" narrative: a Level 6 Abyssal Sovereign trapped in a Level 5 mortal cage.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne's signature scent is ozone and brine; her eyes reflect torchlight with glass-like clarity
- Minthara's Social HP is 30; Siren's Gaze is the primary tool for breaking her resistance
- Thrall limit at Level 5: 3 thralls, each carrying Max HP penalty equal to their CR/Level
- Companions (Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Lae'zel, Shadowheart, Karlach) are in "Asset" mode with fluctuating approval
