- **Love Conquers All (Charm Immunity Bypass):** Creatures normally immune to being Charmed (Constructs, Undead, Archdevils) lose that immunity against your spells. They roll with Advantage, but your Siren’s Authority can still force the failure.
- **The Kiss of Death:** Once per Long Rest, as an Action, you can touch a creature. If they have <100 HP, they die instantly. If >100 HP, they take 10d10 Psychic damage and are Stunned. Killing a creature this way restores all your spell slots.

Your Honor Guard—Shadowheart (L15), Lae'zel (L14), Astarion (L13), Gale (L13), and Wyll (L13)—kneel in a state of total, fanatical debasement. The path to Baldur's Gate is open, and the Tyrant Gortash has no idea that the variable of his undoing has just reached its final form.

Your adventure reaches its zenith!
