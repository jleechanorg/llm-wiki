Darth Vader remained a silent, imposing sentinel, his rhythmic breathing a constant, heavy counterpoint to the scene. Luke felt an immense, cold wave of profound approval from his master, a non-verbal affirmation that solidified his terrifying triumph. The pieces were all in place. The Rebellion’s fate was sealed.
