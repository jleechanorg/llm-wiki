## Summary

A D&D 5e adaptation of the Undertale video game where the player controls Frisk, a human child who has fallen into the Underground. The character is built as a "Determination Adept" (Sorcerer/Bard hybrid) with high Charisma and Wisdom, optimized for the Mercy/compassionate path. The campaign follows Frisk's journey through the Ruins, encountering Flowey the flower, meeting Toriel, and learning about puzzles and the nature of the Underground.

## Key Claims

- The soul trait "Determination" is the primary character attribute, represented mechanically as a unique resource
- Frisk's build prioritizes social interaction and survival over combat, with high Persuasion, Insight, and Perception
- The Ruins contain ancient puzzles and traps that serve as tests for those who fall into the Underground
- Flowey presents as friendly but is secretly malevolent, attempting to "kill or be killed" in the Underground's harsh logic
- Toriel acts as the caretaker of the Ruins, providing guidance and protection for fallen children
- Dice rolls drive narrative outcomes with DC-based success/failure checks for skill challenges
