    -   Confidence: Medium. It's a safer path, but less satisfying.

3.  **Defer Training and Focus on Current Mission:** Prioritize the immediate objective of infiltrating the Rebel base and leading the surgical strike. Reassess the Jedi Master's training potential after this major triumph.
    -   Pros: Direct obedience to Vader's last command; focuses energy on the primary objective; allows for future strategic evaluation of the Jedi Master's utility.
    -   Cons: Delays the potential for accelerated personal power growth; leaves a valuable asset's full potential untapped for now.
    -   Confidence: High. It is the most pragmatic and immediate path.
