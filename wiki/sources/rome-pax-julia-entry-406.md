## SRD‑only 4th‑level paladin spells

From the 5e SRD lists, paladins get exactly:[1][2][3]

- Banishment  
- Death Ward  
- Locate Creature  

These are the only 4th‑level paladin spells in SRD‑restricted material.[2][3][1]

## Full 5e core (PHB) 4th‑level spells

Including the full Player’s Handbook (beyond SRD), paladins also have:[4][5]

- Aura of Life  
- Aura of Purity  
- Staggering Smite  

These three are PHB paladin‑only spells at 4th level and do not appear in the SRD.[5][4]

## Later 5e books / expansions

With Xanathar’s and similar 5e supplements, paladins additionally gain at 4th level:[6][4]

- Find Greater Steed  

Aggregators and guides consistently treat this as a key high‑impact paladin 4th‑level spell.[7][6]
