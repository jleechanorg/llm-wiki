## Summary
Privacy notice for Lee-Chan Consulting (dba WorldAI), describing how personal information is collected, stored, used, and shared when users visit https://worldarchitect.ai or use the WorldAI AI-powered RPG engine and platform.

## Key Claims
- **Data Controller** — Lee-Chan Consulting is responsible for processing personal information
- **Service Description** — WorldAI is an AI-powered RPG engine enabling users to create, explore, and interact with immersive narrative-driven game worlds powered by large language models
- **No Third-Party Collection** — Does not collect information from third parties
- **No Sensitive Information** — Does not process special/sensitive personal information (racial origins, sexual orientation, religious beliefs)
- **Security Commitment** — Implements organizational and technical processes to protect personal information, though no guarantee against all cyber threats
- **International Transfers** — Information may be transferred internationally
- **Data Retention** — Retains information as described in the policy
- **User Rights** — Users have privacy rights depending on their geographic location

## Key Quotes
> "We are responsible for making decisions about how your personal information is processed."

> "Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices."
