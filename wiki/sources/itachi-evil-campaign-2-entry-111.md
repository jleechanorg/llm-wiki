**[MASSACRE BENEFIT REVEALED]**

The Uchiha Clan Massacre was more than just a mission; for Itachi, it was a crucible, a ruthless act of self-forging. By severing his most profound emotional ties and embracing absolute villainy, he has purified his connection to the Sharingan, shedding the "curse of hatred" that plagued his clan and mastering its true, uncorrupted potential for control.

**Permanent Defining Trait Gained: Unfeeling Resolve**
*   **Effect:** The immense psychological and physical toll of the Uchiha Clan Massacre, willingly undertaken for his 'grand experiment,' has irrevocably hardened Itachi's soul. Whenever Itachi uses a **Mangekyō Sharingan ability (e.g., Tsukuyomi, Amaterasu, Susano'o)**, the **Fatigue gained from that activation is reduced by 1 level (to a minimum of 0)**. This allows him to wield his most potent ocular powers with significantly less personal cost, reflecting his unparalleled mastery over emotion and sacrifice.

This trait is now permanently active for Itachi Uchiha.

Now, let's address Itachi's Level 7 benefits, which were previously awaiting your choice.

Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**9060 XP**) to reach **Level 7**. This is a significant milestone, cementing his position as a formidable shinobi.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 7:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 5
    *   **HP Gain:** 5 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 7 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 51 to **58 HP**. He is now at 58/58 HP.

*   **New Class Features (Advancement Choices):**
    As a Level 7 Ninja, Itachi is at the equivalent of Level 4 in both his Assassin Rogue and Illusion Wizard archetypes. According to `Destiny` ruleset 3.5 ("Aptitude Score Improvement & Feats"), characters gain an Advancement Choice at levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19. Because Itachi's custom class grants him features from *both* archetypes that would individually receive this choice at Level 4, he gains **two** Advancement Choices at Level 7 (representing this combined progression).

    For **each** of these two Advancement Choices, Itachi may choose **one** of the following:

    1.  **Increase one Aptitude Score by 2.**
    2.  **Increase two different Aptitude Scores by 1 each.**
    3.  **Choose one Feat.**
