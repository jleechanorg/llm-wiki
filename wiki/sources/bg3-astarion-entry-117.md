He glanced at his companions. Lae'zel, grim and unyielding, stood like a statue of coiled aggression, her golden eyes sweeping the clearing, her disdain for weakness palpable. His crimson eyes fixed on Lae'zel, then Gale, a flicker of his usual charm attempting to mask the desperate hunger. "Right, now that we've confirmed our... *benefactor* here is utterly useless," he drawled, his voice a low, sardonic purr, though a tremor of raw need ran beneath it. Absolutely not!" he choked, his voice laced with profound horror and revulsion. "We are not *monsters*! We don't sacrifice innocents for... for personal gain! Find your strength in courage, in morality, not in barbarism!" He scrambled away from you, his eyes wide with a mixture of fear and profound disappointment.
