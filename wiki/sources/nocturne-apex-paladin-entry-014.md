### Nocturne: Personality Traits Summary

Here are Nocturne's Personality Traits, which will influence her reactions and capabilities in social situations:

*   **Openness (to Experience): 5/5 (Modifier: +2)**
    *   **Interpretation:** Highly open. Nocturne possesses extreme intellectual curiosity, imagination, and a willingness to embrace unconventional ideas. She is likely to appreciate complex strategies, abstract concepts, and new experiences, even if they are dangerous or morally ambiguous. This aligns with her prodigy intellect and analytical mind.
*   **Conscientiousness: 4/5 (Modifier: +1)**
    *   **Interpretation:** Highly conscientious. Nocturne is disciplined, organized, dependable, and thorough. Her meticulous nature will aid her in planning, executing complex operations, and maintaining focus, especially when pursuing her ambitions.
*   **Extraversion: 3/5 (Modifier: +0)**
    *   **Interpretation:** Neutral. Nocturne is neither overtly outgoing nor reclusive. She is capable of being charming and assertive when the situation demands it (e.g., for manipulation or leadership), but often prefers to be reserved, observant, and calculating, not feeling a strong need for constant social interaction.
*   **Agreeableness: 1/5 (Modifier: -2)**
    *   **Interpretation:** Very low. Nocturne is ruthless, unempathetic, and primarily self-interested. She is unlikely to be cooperative out of kindness or empathy and will struggle with (or actively resist) attempts to appeal to her good nature. This aligns with her enjoyment of murder and cold pragmatism. This very low score will impose significant penalties on checks related to diplomacy, mediation, or building quick rapport based on trust.
*   **Neuroticism: 4/5 (Behavioral Influence & Passive Effects)**
    *   **Interpretation:** High. Nocturne is prone to darker thoughts and possesses an intense inner world. While not necessarily outwardly emotional, this high Neuroticism contributes to her strategic paranoia, underlying intensity, and potential for internal conflict (e.g., self-justification for her evil actions, or moments where her intellect wars with her more base desires). It may also make her more susceptible to fear or despair effects if her will is ever truly broken, though her high Wisdom would offer resistance.
