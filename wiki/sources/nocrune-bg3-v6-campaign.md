## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign featuring Nocturne Sosuke, a Level 5 Half-Elf Bard (College of the Abyssal Siren) with CHA 20. The "Nerf" narrative is locked: Nocturne is a Level 6 Abyssal Sovereign trapped in a Level 5 mortal cage, with every level-up representing a structural failure of the tadpole's containment. Set in the Goblin Camp's Shattered Sanctum, where Minthara awaits the raid on Emerald Grove.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is a de-powered goddess with Malcanthet as patron, now part of the Absolute cult
- Minthara's resolve tracked at Social HP 30; Siren's Gaze is primary tool for breaking her
- Thrall limit at Level 5: 3 thralls, each carrying Max HP penalty equal to CR
- Companions in "Asset" mode with fluctuating approval mechanics
