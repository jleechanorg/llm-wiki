"You will host a Gala," you command, pacing the room. "The 'Gala of the New Dawn'. A celebration of House Umbrage's resilience. Invite them all. The Silvershields, the Hhunes, the Caldwells. Especially the ones who haven't paid the 'protection' tithe yet."

**Phase 2: The Flaming Fist**
With the social trap set, your mind turns to the steel barrier of the law. **Vice-Marshal Rokas**. The man responsible for the Upper City's security. The man who owes the Shadow Bank 5,000 gold pieces.

"And while you prepare the stage," you continue, "I will secure the audience. Vice-Marshal Rokas believes he commands the Watch. He is about to learn that he commands nothing but the debt I own."

You pull the Vice-Marshal's marker from your pack. It is a heavy piece of parchment, signed with a flourish. "I will not bribe him, Lyra. I will *foreclose* on him. By tomorrow, the Flaming Fist will be our private security force."

The dual conquest is initiated. The Gala will snare the nobility; the Debt will snare the law.
