## Summary

A D&D 5e dark fantasy noir campaign set in Thay, the "Century of Silence" (+100 years post-Nocturne's ascension). Protagonist Aizen Vane (born Aizen Sosuke, "The Spare") is a Level 5 Abyss Knight serving the Shattered Rose through the Obsidian Mirror pact with Nocturne Sosuke. The campaign centers on the "Tithe of Silt-River" — a Black Company punitive raid that doubles as a soul-harvest for the Sovereign. Key mechanics include Soul Thief (harvest helpless targets for XP/HP/slots), Smite of the Void, Aura of Entropy, and the DPP requisition economy.

## Key Claims

- Aizen is the "Genetic Echo" and last scion of House Sosuke, exiled for being "dangerous" to his family
- The Obsidian Mirror is a physical pact object — a shard of black glass framing a two-way psychic link to Nocturne in the Abyss
- The Soul Thief mechanic requires targets to be Helpless (0 HP, Paralyzed, Stunned, Unconscious, or Enthralled); contact is intimate (caress of cheek or hand over heart); the body desiccates to ash and the soul flows like liquid mercury
- Nocturne watches every Soul Thief through the Mirror and tastes what Aizen tastes; feeding her maintains the Abyssal connection
- Three rebel factions introduced: Order of the Broken Chain (militant abolitionists), The Silver Wing (romanticized idealism), The Silent Dawn (pragmatic smugglers)
- The Sovereign's Treasury (DPP) can be spent on permanent personal gear, not just mission assets — Ring of the Shattered Rose purchased for 5 DPP
