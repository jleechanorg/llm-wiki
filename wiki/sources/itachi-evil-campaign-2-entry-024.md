Itachi Uchiha (Level 1 Ninja, Age 13) stood before the heavy, sealed door to the Uchiha Clan archives. The air here was heavy with the scent of old parchment and the faint, residual chakra of countless generations of Uchiha who had passed through. This was more than just a vault; it was a repository of the clan's accumulated knowledge, its forbidden techniques, and perhaps, its most damning secrets. These were the very resources he sought to consolidate, to strip from the hands of the "uncontrollable variable" and repurpose for his own grand design.

The door was a formidable barrier of reinforced wood and steel, crisscrossed with intricate sealing tags—a standard defense against external intrusion, but also, subtly, against the clan's own volatile members. Itachi's Sharingan traced the lines of the seals, discerning the flow of chakra that bound them, and the complex mechanism of the lock itself. It was designed to withstand brute force and unrefined chakra, but not the surgical intellect of a true prodigy. His mind, honed by years of relentless training and an unparalleled drive for control, effortlessly dissected the intricate layers of protection.

*How to best breach these archives?* The objective was not merely entry, but *clean* entry. Any alarm now, after the meticulous work, would be an unacceptable inefficiency in his "grand experiment." The seals were complex, but solvable. The lock, mechanical and arcane, yielded to analysis under his enhanced perception.
