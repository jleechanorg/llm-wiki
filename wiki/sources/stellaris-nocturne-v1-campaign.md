## Summary
The Shroud-Daughter's Ascension — a Stellaris/Warhammer 40K hybrid TTRPG campaign. Player is Nocturne, an 18-year-old princess and psionic prodigy, daughter of the immortal Emperor Malphas. The campaign covers 60 turns of imperial court intrigue, psychic politics, and galactic empire management.

## Key Setting Elements
- **Aeterna Domain**: Galaxy-spanning empire of psionically ascended humans
- **The Shroud**: Dimension of pure thought / Warp equivalent, source of psychic power
- **20 Factions**: Including Golden Custodians, Shroud-Walkers, Iron Nulls, Bio-Ascendant Cult, etc.
- **Royal Family**: Emperor Malphas, Empress Xylia, Prince Xeres, Princess Hespera, Prince Aethelred, Princess Vespera, Prince Kael, Arch-Duke Mortis, Lady Hecate, Prince Orion
- **Mechanics**: Stellaris ecumenopolis, megastructures, Great Tithe, Exterminatus, psychic ascension

## Character: Nocturne
- Age 18, extremely beautiful, planetary-scale wardrobe
- Lvl 1-2 Psionicist (Sorcerer: Aberrant Mind)
- Stats: STR 8, DEX 14, CON 10, INT 16, WIS 14, CHA 16
- Psionic Spells: Arms of Hadar, Dissonant Whispers, Mind Sliver
- Multi-Domain Prodigy trait with unrealized potential in military, political, intellectual domains
