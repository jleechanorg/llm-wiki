Daemon (Level 8, Age 30) stood, a grim statue in the morning light, his thoughts cold and precise. He had two women, taken as vessels for his heir: Lady Isolde (Level 3, Age ~22), his political wife, and Elara (Age 19), his first mistress. Both, despite his relentless efforts, remained barren. The "curse" of the Blackwood line, as Isolde had called it, now extended to a woman with no direct blood ties, yet from the same region. Was it the land? A lingering, unseen blight? Or simply a peculiar, frustrating defiance of his iron will over the very laws of nature?

It was an inefficiency, a puzzle for his pragmatic mind. Two strong, healthy women, and no conception. Yet Seraphina (Age 20), the third, chosen from outside Blackwood's influence, now carried his child, her belly swelling with the promise of his dominion. The difference was stark. It confirmed the problem lay with the others, and not with him.

The problem of the "barren heir" was not fully resolved, but its immediate threat was bypassed. The subtle humiliation of being thwarted by biology still gnawed at him, but Daemon focused on the result. Seraphina was pregnant. That was the only truth that mattered. The others were simply discarded tools in that particular endeavor, their continued presence a testament to his 'mercy.'
