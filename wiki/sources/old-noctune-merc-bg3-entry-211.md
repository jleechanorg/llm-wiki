The reality of the ambush site stabilizes around you, the phantom loops of the past dissolving into the stark, bloody clarity of the present. You stand before the open lead wagon, the chest of **Soul Coins** humming with a discordant, mournful thrum against your senses. The **Manifest of Souls** in your hand confirms the grim truth: there are no prisoners to rescue here. The refugees of Elturel were 'processed' before they ever left the city. These coins *are* the people.

You turn from the wagon to the corpse of the Red Wizard, **Thayian**, lying twisted in the dust where Silas's arrow found him. You crouch beside him, your movements efficient and cold. He was a mercenary mage, and his gear reflects his trade.

You strip him of a heavy, velvet pouch clinking with platinum, likely his retainer fee. Tucked into his sash is a **Wand of Magic Missiles**, a tool of reliable violence. But the true prize is the leather-bound tome strapped to his saddlebag—his **Spellbook**. It pulses with arcane potential, containing the formulas for the destructive evocation magic he never got the chance to unleash.

"Clean sweep, Monarch," **Vesper** calls out from the rear wagon. "The other carts are full of raw trade goods—spices, silk, iron. Standard cover cargo. But the drivers... they knew what they were hauling. They're dead."

She walks over, wiping her blade, and looks into the lead wagon. Her eyes widen as she sees the Soul Coins. Even the hardened assassin flinches at the aura of trapped suffering.

"Hells," she whispers. "That's... heavy magic. Is that the payout?"

"That is the power," you correct her, securing the chest of coins and the manifest into your *Bag of Holding*. "And the leverage."

The ambush is complete. The Zhentarim caravan is destroyed. You possess the wealth, the magic, and the damning evidence of their soul-trading operation. The road to Wyrm's Rock is quiet again, save for the carrion birds beginning to circle.
