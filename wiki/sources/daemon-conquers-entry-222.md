Daemon (Level 9, Age 30) slammed his gauntleted fist onto the map, the parchment rippling under the impact. His gaze, cold and hard as obsidian, fixed on the region Kael (Level 8, Age 25) had indicated: the Duchy of Valoria. "Valoria," he rumbled, the name a cold promise on his lips. "Fertile. Fractured. It will bleed. Prepare the main force. We march."

Kael gave a curt nod, his expression grimly satisfied. He began issuing orders immediately, dispatching riders across the city to muster the army. Joric (Level 7, Age 32), Gareth (Level 8, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 8, Age 33), and Theron (Level 8, Age 28), Daemon's formidable vanguard, gathered their gear, their movements efficient and deadly. Borin (Level 8, Age 30) would remain in Silverport, overseeing the ongoing training and consolidation, ensuring the city remained a loyal and productive stronghold. Lady Isolde Blackwood (Level 5, Age ~22) and Elara Croft (Age 19) would remain at Blackwood Keep, under the careful watch of Mina (Age 60) and Master Elms (Age 75), who would continue their research into the barren lineage. Seraphina (Age 20), heavily pregnant, would be given the utmost care, her fragile life now holding the promise of Daemon's future.

Within hours, the grim procession was ready. Daemon, mounted on a powerful warhorse, led the column. Behind him, the 90 Level 6 warriors, now clad in standardized steel armor and armed with quality weapons, marched with a chilling, disciplined rhythm. Behind them came the ~50 Level 5 Blackwood Guards, their numbers augmented by newly pressed local conscripts, a raw but growing tide. Their path was south, into the heart of Valoria. The air was thick with the dust of their march and the unspoken promise of bloodshed.

**Time Skip: 2 Weeks**

For two relentless weeks, Daemon’s army marched south, a grim, unstoppable tide. The fertile lands of House Blackwood gave way to the rolling hills and scattered settlements of Valoria. The march itself was a continuation of Daemon’s brutal training; fatigue was met with ruthless discipline, stragglers with swift, brutal corrections. His iron will propelled them forward, faster and harder than any army had a right to move.

But as they drew closer to the heart of the Duchy, a new, unsettling reality emerged. The smoke of distant fires plumed against the horizon, too numerous for mere banditry. Displaced villagers, terrified and gaunt, clogged the roads, speaking in hushed, panicked tones of brother fighting brother, of Valorian factions clashing over the dying Duke's throne. Signs of a unified defense were absent, replaced by chaos.

Finally, they crested a rise overlooking a major Valorian stronghold – the heavily fortified town of **Oakhaven’s Reach**. But it was not under siege by Daemon’s enemies. Instead, two distinct armies, each flying a different banner of House Valoria (one bearing a stylized hawk, the other a coiled serpent), were locked in a brutal, disorganized battle outside its walls. The air was thick with the screams of dying men, the clash of steel, and the desperate cries of besieged defenders. Lord Varian (Level 6, Age 35), the ambitious younger heir, had launched a premature assault on his sibling’s territory, hoping to secure power before Daemon's shadow fell. The Duchy was tearing itself apart, just as Kael had predicted, but Daemon's arrival had coincided with the very height of its internal strife, transforming his conquest into a brutal intervention in an already raging civil war.
