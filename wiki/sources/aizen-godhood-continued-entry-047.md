*   **The Intent:** To strip the remaining deities of their divine power and transform them into Level 6 mortals, scattering them across Faerûn.
*   **Perceived Pros:**

    *   **New Game Dynamics:** Unleashes a fresh wave of controlled chaos and unpredictable interactions across Faerûn, providing endless amusement. Former gods, now mundane, struggling with mortal concerns.
    *   **Living Trophies:** Each mortal god becomes a constant, visible reminder of my absolute authority, a walking monument to their own defeat.
    *   **Entertainment Value:** The potential for their struggles, their attempts to regain power, their inevitable failures – all provide long-term, engaging observation.
    *   **No Direct Threat:** As mortals, they pose no meaningful threat to my dominion.
*   **Perceived Cons / Risks:**
    *   **Management Overhead (Minor):** Tracking their mortal lives, should I choose to, adds a layer of complexity. However, this is negligible given my absolute omnipresence.

*   **Success Estimation (The Absolute's Confidence):** Flawless. The act is trivial. The outcome, the long-term entertainment, is the variable.
