## Summary
A modern-noir D&D 5e campaign featuring a younger version of Sariel (16 years old) who is an FBI prodigy secretly working as a vigilante with 100 confirmed kills. Set in Cheyenne, Wyoming at "The Pit" (a post-explosion tactical chaos zone), this version emphasizes vigilante justice and the moral complexity of a teenage assassin.

## Key Claims
- Sariel is 16 years old, an FBI prodigy operating as a secret vigilante
- She has 100 kills as a masked vigilante, operating alongside a team of companions
- The team includes: Bex Henderson, Oliver Odell, Jacob Hassani, Jennifer Morales, and Shane Florence
- Setting is Cheyenne, Wyoming, specifically "The Pit" — a post-explosion tactical chaos area
