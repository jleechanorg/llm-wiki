Now, a profound stillness settled over him, the initial surge of grim determination replaced by a dizzying mix of apprehension and a dark, thrilling anticipation.

Was he strong enough to control the darkness he now embraced, or would it control him?

Vader remained a silent, unmoving sentinel, his rasping breaths filling the void. He offered no further words, no immediate commands, allowing Luke to grapple with the enormity of his choice. There was no turning back. Luke felt the truth of those words in his very bones, a cold, unyielding weight.

Then, Vader, as if sensing the subtle shift within his new apprentice, finally moved.
