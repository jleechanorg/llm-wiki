Protection turned into a weapon of slow, agonizing death.
    -   Pros: A direct, perverse application of defensive power into offensive torment. Highly versatile in combat and for capturing/torturing targets. Appeals to the deep-seated desire to turn their strength into their weakness.
    -   Cons: Might be less flashy than lightning or direct mind control. Could require significant Force expenditure for larger effects.
    -   Confidence: Medium. Effective, but perhaps less psychologically satisfying than some other options.
