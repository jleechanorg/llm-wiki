You felt the potential for immense control, a subtle, intellectual dominance that promised to dismantle any opposition, any obstacle, not through raw force, but through perfect understanding. This was the "chilling capacity," the inheritance you both feared and craved, now manifesting as a terrifying clarity of purpose. You were learning to wield the unmaking, and the experience, while unsettling, was exhilarating.
