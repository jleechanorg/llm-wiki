## Summary

A D&D 5e Star Wars campaign where the player is Luke Skywalker, about to be offered a place at Darth Vader's side by the dark side. Luke is a Level 5 Force-User (Jedi) with light side affinity but secret dark tendencies — a hidden sadistic side. The campaign opens with custom class creation and builds to the iconic Vader offer scene, allowing Luke to explore the pull between light and dark. Alignment can shift based on choices.

## Key Claims

- Custom Force-User class with Light Side and Dark Side subclasses and alignment tracking
- Luke's starting affinity is Light Side with hidden dark tendencies that create internal conflict
- Initial Force abilities: Force Push/Pull, Force Sense, Force Jump
- Luke's secret dark side is a core character trait — he has a hidden sadistic side
- The campaign is set around 3 ABY (After Battle of Yavin), consistent with The Empire Strikes Back timeline
- Darth Vader is actively recruiting Luke, demonstrating power (crushing a durasteel wall) to prove the dark side's strength
- Luke can use INT for logical persuasion actions when profiling or tactical analysis
