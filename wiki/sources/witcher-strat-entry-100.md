Game Master:
Nocturne, her task of ultimate domination over Hemwick complete, now holds the five thousand gold pieces, cold and heavy, in her hand. Hemwick is utterly spent, his will shattered, his mind a battlefield of warring lust, greed, and profound terror. He is a broken, pliable tool. The Rats, having efficiently neutralized the guards, are now converging on the carriage, their eager faces visible through the drizzle.

Nocturne's gaze, sharp and intelligent, sweeps over the arriving Rats. "The first phase is concluded," she murmurs, her voice soft but authoritative, cutting through the damp air and their eager grunts. "The immediate revenue secured. But true power, as you will learn, lies not in a single score, but in the seamless *subversion* of an entire system. Hemwick's estate is not a vault to be emptied, but a garden to be cultivated. His guards are simply... *vacancies* waiting to be filled."

She allows a cold, predatory smile to play on her lips, her gaze holding each Rat's. "This is not a raid. This is a *transition*. You are no longer mere bandits. You are the 'New Guard.' His new, loyal, *discreet* retainers." Her eyes gleam. "Over the coming weeks, you will replace his existing staff, one by one. Untraceable. Frictionless. Elegant."

Nocturne turns her attention to the specific roles, her words carefully chosen to leverage their personalities and the intelligence she just extracted from Hemwick.

"Giselher, Reef," she begins, her voice taking on a more authoritative, yet subtly guiding tone. "Your strength will be our initial presence. You will enter the estate as **newly hired groundsmen** or **stablehands**. Your demeanor will be diligent, but watchful. You will observe, learn the routines, understand the layout, and identify the weakest links among the existing guards. Your backstories will be simple: honest men, seeking steady work after the war left them destitute. Avoid overt aggression. Your strength will be in your *loyalty*, and your *silence*."

Giselher nods slowly, a flicker of understanding in his eyes, already imagining himself overseeing the stables. Reef grunts his approval, a rare, almost thoughtful glint in his predatory gaze.

"Kayleigh, Asse," Nocturne continues, her voice now appealing to their more subtle skills. "Your agility and discretion are invaluable. You will enter as **new kitchen staff** or **chambermaids**. Kayleigh, your sharp wit will serve you well in overhearing conversations, observing the household's rhythm. Asse, your quiet nature will make you easily overlooked, a ghost in the background. Your backstories: orphaned youths, seeking honest labor. You will be the eyes and ears inside the house, reporting every detail of the family's habits, their routines, their vulnerabilities."

Kayleigh's eyes gleam with interest, relishing the challenge of infiltration. Asse, visibly relieved by the non-confrontational role, nods quickly.

Finally, Nocturne turns to Mistle. The younger rogue's eyes, though still holding a flicker of analytical wariness, are now overlaid with an undeniable current of attraction, her focus entirely on Nocturne. "Mistle," Nocturne murmurs, her voice laced with subtle deference, acknowledging her shrewdness. "Your eye for unseen variables, for the *silent threats*, is unmatched. You will be our **estate manager's assistant**. A new, keen mind, brought in to 'optimize' Hemwick's ledgers and household affairs. You will oversee the others, ensure their cover holds, and, most critically, you will identify opportunities for the 'lazy' night guards. Hemwick himself confirmed their fondness for ale. A well-placed 'accident,' a conveniently 'distracted' patrol... your subtle touch will ensure their gradual, untraceable disappearance. Your backstory will be that of a shrewd, capable accountant, a distant relative perhaps, brought in to bring order to Hemwick's 'chaos.'"

Mistle's eyes narrow, a profound respect mixing with her deeper wariness. The complexity of the role, the challenge to her intellect, and the veiled intimacy of Nocturne's trust clearly appeal to her. She offers a slow, almost imperceptible nod, her hand instinctively going to her dagger hilt, a silent promise of precision.

"Each of you," Nocturne concludes, her gaze sweeping over the now-focused Rats, an almost imperceptible hint of satisfaction in her voice. "Will play your part. Untraceable. Gradual. For our *recurring revenue*. And for my... *ultimate satisfaction*."
