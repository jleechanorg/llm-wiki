Your head throbs, and a cold dread coils in your stomach – not just from the terrifying situation, but from the alien presence behind your eye: a wriggling, squirming tadpole, driving you towards an unthinkable transformation.

You tear your gaze from the horror of the engine, scanning your immediate surroundings. Other unfortunate souls are strapped to similar slabs, their expressions a mixture of terror and dawning madness. One in particular catches your eye: a fierce-looking Githyanki woman, her golden eyes burning with fury and desperation, struggles against her own restraints. Beyond her, the vessel itself groans and shudders, the air thick with ozone and the stench of blood and burning flesh. It's a living nightmare, and every instinct screams for escape.
