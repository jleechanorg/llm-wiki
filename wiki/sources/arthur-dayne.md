## Summary

A D&D 5e Game of Thrones campaign where the player controls Ser Arthur Dayne, the Sword of the Morning, at the Tower of Joy during Robert's Rebellion (283 AC). Arthur is a Level 20 Gestalt Fighter (Battle Master) / Paladin (Oath of the Crown), wielding the legendary greatsword Dawn (+5, radiant, force damage). The campaign recreates the canonical confrontation between Arthur and Ned Stark, but with Arthur prevailing and revealing Lyanna's secret: she married Rhaegar willingly and bore his child.

## Key Claims

- Arthur Dayne is the strongest warrior in existence — Level 20 Gestalt with even-numbered stats (STR 20, DEX 18, CON 18, INT 12, WIS 14, CHA 20)
- Dawn is a +5 greatsword dealing 2d6+9 slashing + 2d8 radiant + 2d8 force damage
- Paladin Aura grants +5 AC and +5 to saving throws for all allies within 30 feet
- Low magic setting — offensive magic is rare; Arthur's spell list is defensive/utility only
- Arthur disarmingly defeats Ned, Howland Reed, Martyn Cassel, and Theo Wull non-lethally
- The campaign reveals Lyanna was not kidnapped — she and Rhaegar married eloped in secret
- Arthur successfully heals Lyanna after childbirth using all Lay on Hands + healing spells
