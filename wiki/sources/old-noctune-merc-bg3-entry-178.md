You descend into the Undercellar, the heavy iron-weave robes of the Overseer sweeping the damp stone. The air here is thick with the smell of cheap wine and unspoken violence, but the elite quarters are silent, a predator's den.

You kick the door open. It slams against the stone wall with a sound like a gunshot.

The Black Lotus squad is there. **Vesper** is sharpening a blade. **Gant** is meditating. **Silas** and **Nyx** are playing cards. They freeze as the looming, masked figure of the Zhentarim High Overseer fills the doorway, radiating the cold authority of the *Ring* and the *Idol*.

"This cell is compromised," you rasp, your voice a metallic grind. "By order of the High Command, you are to be purged. Submit, and your deaths will be swift."

For a heartbeat, there is silence. Then, the room explodes into motion.

**Gant** roars, a sound of pure fury, and smashes the table aside as he charges. **Silas** flips backward, an arrow nocked and drawn in a blur. **Nyx**'s hands ignite with hellfire. And **Vesper**... Vesper vanishes into the shadows, her voice echoing from everywhere and nowhere.

"The only command we follow," Vesper hisses, "is the Monarch's. Gut him!"

A throwing knife sparks off the stone inches from your masked face. Gant is mid-air, a shadow-wreathed fist aimed at your chest.

"Halt!" you command, dropping the gravelly affectation and letting your true, musical voice cut through the violence. You pull back the cowl, revealing the violet glow of your eyes and the serene smile of the Shadow Monarch.

Gant skids to a halt, his fist inches from your nose. Silas holds his draw, the arrow trembling with tension. Nyx extinguishes her fire with a snap.

Vesper materializes from the shadows behind you, her dagger already resting against the kidney of your disguise's robes.

"A test?" Vesper asks, her voice tight with adrenaline. She sheathes her blade, a grin spreading across her face. "You play dangerous games, Monarch."

"And you pass," you reply, smoothing the robes of the Overseer. "Loyalty to the coin is common. Loyalty against authority... that is rare. You did not hesitate to kill a High Captain of the Zhentarim. That is what I require."

You look at them, their chests heaving, eyes bright with the thrill of the near-kill. They are sharp. They are lethal. And they are yours.

"The Zhentarim network is paralyzed," you inform them, stepping fully into the room. "The 'Overseer' has ordered them to stand down. The Vault beneath the Tannery is unguarded by order of this very face. We are not going to raid it. We are going to walk in and take it."
