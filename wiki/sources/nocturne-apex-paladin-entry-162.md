Location: House Valerius Estate, Lord Cassian's Private Study, Morning

The study air, usually a sanctuary of quiet authority, now thrummed with the raw shock radiating from Lord Cassian Valerius (Age 55). The assembled evidence—the deed, the ledger, the Orbs of Scrying, the Shadowfell maps, the malevolent obsidian shard, the chilling Darkwood Casket, and the decoded journal detailing the Architect's "Convergence" in three nights—lay spread across his polished desk, a damning testament to a conspiracy far grander and more terrifying than he had conceived. His usual impassive mask was utterly shattered, replaced by a pallor and eyes wide with a mixture of profound shock and cold, dangerous fury. He looked from the artifacts to Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16), a grim recognition dawning in his gaze.

Nocturne allowed the silence to stretch, a powerful weapon in itself. She offered no further words, no elaboration, no demand. Her presence was absolute, unwavering, devoid of any weakness or emotional appeal. She simply watched him, a silent observer of his internal struggle, her own features an unreadable mask. He understood. He understood the betrayal, the scale of the threat, and the chilling truth that his exiled daughter had just revealed a vulnerability that his vast network had entirely missed. She was not begging for his attention; she was demanding his recognition, forcing his hand through irrefutable, devastating facts. The weight of his realization filled the room.
