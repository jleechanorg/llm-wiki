The air in the circular chamber crackled, thick with the scent of ozone and the oppressive weight of the Dark Side. Luke Skywalker, his face a mask of grim satisfaction, watched the flickering, terrified illusions of his former companions. Their pleas were silent, their fear a tangible, intoxicating aroma that fueled the burgeoning inferno within him. The Light Side, once a guiding star, was now a dead ember, crushed beneath the overwhelming power of his `secret sadistic side`.

With a guttural roar, a sound more animal than human, Luke unleashed the torrent. Dark energy, raw and uncontrolled, surged from his outstretched hands. It wasn't the precise, elegant application of the Force he had once known, but a brutal, all-consuming wave of pure hatred. Crackling bolts of crimson lightning erupted from his fingertips, arcing across the chamber to strike the spectral figures. The illusions shrieked, a chorus of agony that brought a perverse thrill to Luke's very core. Leia's form warped and twisted, dissolving into a wisp of smoke; Han screamed silently as he was torn apart by invisible forces; Chewbacca's mournful cry was cut short as he imploded. Obi-Wan's disappointed visage held for a moment longer, his eyes wide with a phantom horror, before he too was consumed by the malevolent energy, shattering like brittle glass.

Silence descended, broken only by Vader's rhythmic breathing. The air still hummed with residual dark energy, the conduits on the walls pulsing with a deeper, more resonant glow. Luke stood, chest heaving, the aftertaste of absolute power sweet on his tongue. He felt... complete. The purge was thorough, the weakness eradicated. He turned to Vader, a cold, hungry glint in his eyes. He was ready for whatever came next.
