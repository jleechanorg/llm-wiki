Game Master:
[Mode: DM MODE]
Understood. You've provided a DM Note stating that "speech should be a free action so he should be able to do both" (command and attack).

I acknowledge that general, short phrases or basic dialogue are indeed considered a free interaction that does not consume a character's action (as per Section 5.1.E of the Destiny Core Rules, "Free Interaction").

However, a "command" that is part of a strategic option, especially one that requires a **Resolution Check** (like Astarion's successful `Charisma (Leadership/Strategy)` check in a previous turn to "coordinate a simultaneous attack" in SEQ_ID: 46), is interpreted as a **primary action** within this ruleset. This is because it involves active tactical direction and coordination, which is more complex than a simple shout or warning and directly influences the combat flow beyond mere communication. Such abilities are comparable to a "Help" action, or a Battle Master's "Commander's Strike" in other systems, which consume an action or bonus action.

Therefore, Astarion still has one **primary action** for this turn. He can either:

*   **Use his primary action to `CommandAttackThird_2`:** This would be a strategic command, directing his companions to engage the third, newly alert Mind Flayer. I would then narrate the companions' actions based on the success of that command (though Astarion would not be able to make a personal attack himself as his primary action).
*   **Use his primary action to `AttackWoundedFlayer_1`:** This means Astarion personally attacks the wounded Mind Flayer. His companions would then act logically based on the immediate combat context and their own (AI-driven) priorities, but without a specific strategic command from Astarion.

Please choose **one** primary action for Astarion. Once you clarify this, I will proceed with the story.

Player (freeform):
CommandAttackThird_2
