[Session 877: The Global Gestation Decree]

Game Master:
The Grand Sanctum of Malsheem vibrates with a subsonic, rhythmic roar as your Sovereign Relay expands to encompass the totality of your holdings. You stand at the edge of the obsidian dais, the **Secular Regalia** casting a blinding, multi-tonal radiance that bleeds from dawn-gold to moonlight-silver, painting the bowing Lords of the Eight in the colors of their new mistress. 

"The harvest is no longer a localized function," you purr, your voice rippling through the Gilded Relay like a planetary foreclosure. "It is the biological baseline of the multiverse."

At your command, the **Utopia Breeding Grounds** erupt across the Sword Coast, the Nine Hells, and the re-indexed Abyssal layers. In Baldur’s Gate, the scent of jasmine and ozone becomes a permanent atmospheric weight; every citizen is now a line-item in your gestation program. In the Hells, the scorched plains of Avernus and the frozen wastes of Cania are transformed into shimmering, climate-controlled meadows of indigo grass. The process is Masterful; the 73 total on your expansion audit ensures that the transition is seamless, the biological math grounding into your marrow with the weight of a world-ending debt.

You turn your gaze toward your newly elevated Council of the Indigo Eye. "Consolidate," you mandate. "Own your layers. Own your nodes. Mass your legions and prepare for the divine friction."

Shadowheart, Astarion, Gale, and the others vanish in pillars of violet fire to oversee their respective jurisdictions. Within the hour, the relay begins to pulse with their reports. The **Sosuke Guard** has expanded to **50,000 Elite God-Stalkers**, each one bolstered by the divine residue of your recent unmakings. Your **Infernal Legions** have swollen to **2,000,000 disciplined Soldiers**, backed by **250,000 Masked Erinyes** who move as light-eating smudges in the air. The quality is absolute; your troops possess the rigid discipline of the Hells and the tactical unmaking capabilities of your own bloodline. The board is set for the Time of Troubles v2.0.
