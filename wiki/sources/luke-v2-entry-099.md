The hum of the secluded training chamber faded as Luke Skywalker, accompanied by the now-intelligent and loyal **Jedi Master**, moved deeper into the Imperial Installation. His strategic focus, sharpened by days of clandestine learning, sought a practical application for his newly corrupted Force Empathy. The subtle, insidious pleasure of psychological domination beckoned. *This is necessary,* he rationalized, *to truly understand the enemy's breaking points. Their suffering is merely data, a means to achieve ultimate order.*

They entered a smaller, starker room: an interrogation chamber. In the center, restrained by binders to a reinforced chair, sat a man of mid-30s, his face grim but defiant. This was **Roric Vane**, a captured Rebel operative, likely a technician or scout from the very base Luke intended to infiltrate. His eyes, wary but unbroken, met Luke’s with a flicker of recognition, then confusion, seeing a figure so akin to the 'hero' of the Rebellion.

Luke stepped forward, his Force presence shifting, no longer masked, but subtly amplifying. He extended a hand, focusing the power of his **Corrupted Force Empathy**. He didn't merely sense **Roric's** emotions; he plunged into them, seeking the threads of his loyalty, his fear, his hope, and began to twist. He amplified the man's despair, conjured phantom betrayals by his allies, twisted his memories of freedom into crushing futility. **Roric's** eyes widened, a silent scream tearing through his mind, and his body convulsed against the binders. Sweat beaded on his brow, and a whimper escaped his lips, not of physical pain, but of profound, internal agony.

His strategic mind registered the potency of the ability. *Effective.* The **Jedi Master** stood silently beside Luke, her violet eyes observing with keen interest, her presence a cold, approving echo of his own dark purpose. **Darth Vader's** distant, heavy presence was a constant, approving anchor in the Force. A faint, almost imperceptible pang of unease, a ghost of his former self, tried to surface at **Roric's** suffering, but Luke ruthlessly suppressed it. *He chose his side. This is the consequence. The galaxy demands this.*
