## Summary
A legendary hero campaign following Ser Arthur Dayne, the Sword of the Morning, as a Level 20 gestalt Fighter/Paladin in the Game of Thrones universe. He serves in the elite Kingsguard and wields the radiant Dawn, the most famous greatsword in the realm.

## Key Claims
- Arthur Dayne is the greatest swordsman in Westeros, serving faithfully in the Kingsguard protecting the royal family
- His journey involves confronting Ned Stark and the events surrounding Robert's rebellion

## Key Themes
- Honor and Duty: The weight of oaths sworn to the crown
- Chivalry: Living up to the ideal of knighthood in a treacherous world
