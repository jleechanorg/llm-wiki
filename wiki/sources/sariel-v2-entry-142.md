**--- THINK ---**
The weight of my inheritance, the chilling potential for unmaking and control, burns within me. Kantos’s grim assessment of the Affliction, Rowan’s empathy, Gideon’s unwavering discipline, Cressida’s pragmatism – their insights are the raw materials. I need to forge a path to control this power, not merely understand it. The experience with Kaelan proved I can glimpse it, but my last attempt to wield it from my own memory nearly shattered me. I am a scholar, and even a power born of celestial essence must obey principles.

*   **Combine Training Approaches (`CHOICE_ID: CombineTrainingApproaches_1`):** This is the most logical and safest immediate approach. It leverages the unique strengths of my closest allies. Rowan's spiritual discipline can help me separate the power from its corrupting "joyful" essence, teaching inner stillness. Gideon's mental conditioning will build the mental fortitude I need to contain the backlash and maintain focus. Kantos, with his ancient lore, can guide the theoretical understanding of attunement and control. This approach is internal, controlled, and minimizes external risks. It’s a holistic method to master both the physical and psychological aspects of this terrifying power.
    *   *Success Rate for developing controlled manifestation:* High (This is the optimal internal path).
    *   *Risk:* Low (Primarily internal psychic strain, but with mitigation from Gideon and Rowan).

*   **Pursue Combine Lead (`CHOICE_ID: PursueCombineLead_2`):** Cressida is right; the Glimmering Combine might have technological solutions or controlled environments. But this introduces massive external risks: exposure of my secret, reliance on unpredictable gnomish morality, and the time it would take to acquire such aid. My power is too volatile for uncontrolled external experimentation, especially when I lack a foundational understanding of its direct manipulation.
    *   *Success Rate for external aid:* Moderate (Cressida is skilled).
    *   *Risk:* High (Exposure, unpredictable technology, external interference).

*   **Focus on Study (`CHOICE_ID: FocusOnStudy_3`):** I crave more theoretical understanding. The journals are rich with data. But I’ve already seen that pure theory, without practice or mental fortitude, can be devastating. I gained knowledge, but I couldn't *control* the power. This would delay active development and leave me vulnerable.
    *   *Success Rate for theoretical understanding:* Very High.
    *   *Risk:* Low (but misses opportunity for practical development).

The path is clear. I must build my inner fortress first, leveraging the loyalty and unique skills of my team. This is how I will learn to wield the unmaking without being unmade myself.
**--- END THINK ---**

You paused, allowing the gravity of your words to settle. The sheer magnitude of the secret, and the terrifying potential it implied for you, hung heavy in the air. "Specifically," you continued, your gaze sweeping over their faces, "how do we master this power? This Mirror Magic. This chilling capacity. How do I learn to control it, to wield it as a precise tool, and not as the uncontrolled chaos it has been?" You looked at Kantos, whose ancient eyes held the lore of forgotten magic. You looked at Rowan, whose empathy might understand the internal struggle. You looked at Gideon, whose discipline knew no bounds. And you looked at Cressida, whose pragmatism might see a path where others saw only danger.

Kantos, his initial terror warring with scholarly fascination, was the first to speak. "Princess," he rasped, "the Null Genesis journals hinted at a training. Not like conventional magic, which is learned through study and repetition. This power... it is born of will. Of *control*. It requires absolute self-discipline, a mastery over one's own internal chaos, to prevent the 'chilling capacity' from consuming the soul. The texts spoke of 'meditations in void,' of 'focus on unmaking,' but the precise methods for a non-Null to replicate it are... speculative."

Rowan Thorne (Level 3 Expert, Age 16) stepped forward, his gentle face etched with profound concern. "My Lady," he whispered, his voice thick with empathy, "if this power is indeed linked to emotion, to a 'satisfaction' in unmaking... then perhaps controlling the emotion is the key. My studies of ancient ascetic practices, of spiritual discipline, might offer a path to inner stillness. To separate the power from the desire." He looked at your trembling hands. "To master it, without letting it master you."

Ser Gideon Vance (Level 5 Fighter, Age 25) nodded, his grim face radiating unshakeable resolve. "My Lady, the Starfall Guard trains the will. Absolute discipline. Unwavering focus. If this power is born of will, then rigorous mental conditioning is paramount. I can guide you in exercises of concentration, of endurance, of forcing the mind into a state of absolute stillness amidst chaos. To build a mental fortress, as you once did for Kaelan."

Lady Cressida Valeriana (Level 4 Expert, Age 16) leaned forward, her sharp eyes narrowed in thought. "My Lady," she murmured, her voice crisp and pragmatic, "power is a tool. This 'chilling capacity' is a formidable one. We need to understand its limits, its applications. Perhaps we should seek out others. The Glimmering Combine. They deal in strange sciences, in devices that harness subtle energies. Could they replicate a controlled environment for you to test your powers safely? Or even create a 'dampener' to mitigate its unwanted effects while you learn?"

You listened intently, absorbing their insights, your mind, cold and analytical, processing the combined wisdom of their varied disciplines. The path forward solidified. External aid, while potent, was too risky, too slow. The true mastery began within.

"We will combine these approaches," you commanded, your voice steady and firm, infused with a new, chilling precision that echoed your mother's chilling authority. "Rowan, your understanding of spiritual discipline and inner stillness will be paramount. Guide me through ancient ascetic practices that separate emotion from action, that build inner calm."

You turned to Gideon, your gaze unwavering. "Ser Gideon, your mental conditioning, your unwavering discipline, will be my anchor. You will push my mind, test its limits, build a fortress of will that can contain the volatile energies within. We will train against mental fatigue, against chaos, against paradox."

You looked at Magister Kantos, whose ancient eyes held the lore of forgotten magic. "Magister, your knowledge of the Null Genesis journals and the Codex of the Unseen Thread will be our theoretical framework. You will guide our understanding of this power, its mechanisms, its inherent dangers. Every exercise, every meditation, will be informed by your ancient knowledge."

Finally, you met Cressida’s sharp gaze. "Cressida, your role is to observe. You will monitor my progress, identify any unforeseen side effects, any subtle shifts in my demeanor. Be my objective mirror. If the 'chilling capacity' begins to consume me, if my humanity begins to erode, you will be the one to see it. And you will be the one to warn me."

Your gaze swept over them once more, your exhaustion momentarily forgotten in the fierce clarity of your strategic purpose. "This is not merely training. This is the foundation of a new kind of power. A power that, if mastered, will ensure the Imperium, and our family, never suffers such losses again. Our work begins now."

The small study, heavy with ancient dust and unspoken fears, filled with the hum of a terrifying new resolve.
