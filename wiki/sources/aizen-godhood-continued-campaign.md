## Summary

Aizen Godhood Continued is a campaign set at a cosmic scale, where Aizen has achieved godlike status and observes Faerun from his Cosmic Throne. The campaign is in "Story Mode" with Aizen as The Absolute. Mystra is introduced as a new villain orchestrating subtle tyranny, and Aizen watches the schism she created within the Church of Tyr unfold as part of his grand game.

## Key Claims

- Aizen is The Absolute - at the peak of his power
- Aizen sits on a Cosmic Throne observing Faerun
- Sequence ID 132 indicates a checkpoint system
- Mystra is the new villain, manipulating the Church of Tyr
- The Triumvirate is an existing alliance that must contend with a crippled ally
- Aizen views the world as a game board where he orchestrates events
