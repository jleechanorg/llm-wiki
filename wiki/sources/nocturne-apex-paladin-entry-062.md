Location: Hidden near Oakhaven Main Road, Early Evening

Nocturne (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) observed the two road patrol guards, their movements precise, their vigilance sharp. Her **Apex Senses** scan had revealed their professional training, their coordinated movements, and the subtle arcane pulse of their sentinel orb – confirmation of a sophisticated, interconnected defense network. Kaelen’s initial assessment of "easy pickings" was a grave misjudgment, a flaw in intelligence that demanded a higher compensation. The mission had escalated from a simple abduction to a complex infiltration, and these two guards were the first visible layer of that complexity.

Her mind, a relentless engine of strategic thought, processed the implications. The Underdark buyers were clearly investing heavily in their "stock," suggesting a value far beyond that of typical peasants. This increased complexity, rather than deterring her, sharpened her focus. More risk, more challenge, implied greater potential reward. The priority shifted: these two had to be neutralized. Bypassing them was merely a temporary measure, a delay to an inevitable confrontation. To proceed with the larger acquisition at Tibbet's Farm, this immediate threat needed to be eliminated, and silently, to prevent further alarm.

***

**Nocturne's Internal Monologue & Planning for Neutralization:**

*Two trained guards. Vigilant. Armed. The objective: immediate, silent, complete neutralization. Noise is unacceptable. A clean strike is paramount.*

*My capabilities for a silent takedown are high. My Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate provides stealth advantage in this dim light. My combat prowess is formidable. The challenge is ensuring both are silenced before either can react or raise an alarm.*

*I need to approach unseen, then strike with overwhelming force. My **Perfected Strike** (EP cost) is ideal for ensuring a lethal blow on the first target. But what about the second? He will react. The speed of my second attack must be absolute. I also have **Channel Divinity: Flawless Opening** to guarantee a hit on the first strike. This will ensure critical damage.*

*The sequence must be: close the distance under stealth. Activate Flawless Opening. Target the lead guard, perhaps the one who tossed the sentinel orb – he seems marginally more alert, more 'aware.' Strike with Perfected Strike. Immediately pivot and attack the second. The element of surprise, though compromised by the prior barking in Oakhaven, can still be leveraged here to ensure silence.*

*This is a calculated risk. But the highest confidence lies in my ability to execute. Efficiency demands their removal now, before they become a larger variable in the larger operation.*

***

Nocturne’s eyes, pools of cold resolve, fixed on the approaching patrol. Her Masterwork Longsword was already drawn, its metal catching the faint moonlight. She began to move, a predatory glide, using every shadow and undulation of the terrain to her advantage.

They passed within twenty feet of her concealed position, their low murmurs indiscernible, their attention still fixed on the road ahead. Now.

As the lead guard passed, Nocturne lunged, a sudden burst of motion that was almost too fast for the eye to follow. She simultaneously activated her **Channel Divinity: Flawless Opening** (Secondary Action, 0 CP used this turn), focusing her tactical insight on the guard's vital points. Her longsword, charged with her willpower, plunged forward.

He crumpled silently, his body hitting the dirt with a soft thud. His companion, hearing the faint sound, began to turn, a question forming on his lips.

Nocturne was already moving. Her **Extra Attack** (part of the same Primary Action) seamlessly flowed into the second strike, targeting the turning guard's exposed back.

The second blade found its mark, deep and swift. The guard gasped, a choked, dying sound that died in his throat as he too collapsed without a scream. Two bodies lay still in the growing darkness, their presence on the road extinguished. The magical sentinel orb, still hovering faintly, dimmed its arcane light, its purpose unfulfilled.

Nocturne stood over the fallen, her breath even, the faint scent of blood mixing with the evening air. The silence returned, profound and absolute. The professional threat was eliminated, and without a sound to alert Tibbet's Farm or any other lurking dangers. The road was clear. The efficiency of the operation was, for now, restored to her chilling standards.
