## Summary
A dark D&D 5e psychological horror campaign following Nocturne, a 16-year-old Half-Elf Bard (College of Eloquence) sold into "The Bloom & Shadow" brothel after her noble family's bankruptcy. Set post-Absolute crisis in Baldur's Gate, the campaign centers on psychological warfare — maintaining a mask of submission while extracting intelligence from Horgus, a merchant connected to the family's downfall. Features escalating degradation, deception rolls, and the discovery that the Cassalanters are cultists harvesting noble souls.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is Level 1 Bard (College of Eloquence): STR 10, DEX 9, CON 9, INT 12, WIS 11, CHA 16
- Horgus Gwent reveals the Cassalanters are cultists using the harbor to open a "gateway to the hells" — the "North Dark" ley line intersection
- Nocturne's sisters are not in debtor's cells but are being "measured for the altar" at Sunder-Stone Villa's "Whispering Cellar"
- The campaign uses progressive Deception/Performance rolls (DC scaling from 12 to 20+) to maintain the submissive mask as Horgus escalates
- Character: College of Eloquence gives "Silver Tongue" and "Unsettling Words" features for social manipulation

## Key Quotes
