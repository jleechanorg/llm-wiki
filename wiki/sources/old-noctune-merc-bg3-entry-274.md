You leave the Umbrage Estate, the golden light of the setting sun casting long, bloody shadows across the white stone of the Upper City. The air is cooling, but the political heat is rising. You move with the inexorable momentum of a landslide, your destination the grim fortress of the Flaming Fist.

The guards at the main gate cross their halberds, but you do not stop. You flash the *Ring of Zhentarim Authority*—a subtle threat that you play the game at a level they cannot comprehend—and simply walk past them. They are too confused by your confidence to stop you. You ascend the stone stairs to the Vice-Marshal's private suite.

**Vice-Marshal Rokas** is a bull of a man, squeezed into a uniform that strains at the shoulders. He is pacing his office, shouting at a subordinate about patrol rotations. When you open the door and close it behind you, locking it with a decisive click, the subordinate flees through the side entrance, sensing a predator.

Rokas turns, his face flushing with anger. "Who in the Nine Hells are you? This is a restricted—"

"Sit," you command. The word is not a request; it is a physical weight, amplified by the *Echoes of the Idol*. It slams into him, forcing the breath from his lungs.

He stumbles back against his desk, his hand drifting to his sword. "Guard!"

"Call them," you dare him, stepping into the light. "And I will show them this."

You slam the debt marker onto his desk. It is a heavy parchment, sealed with the mark of the now-defunct moneylender Vessic replaced. It details a sum of **5,000 gold pieces**—gambling debts, illicit purchases, hush money. It is a career-ending document. It is a death sentence in a city looking for corruption to purge.

Rokas stares at the paper, his face draining of color. The bluster vanishes, replaced by the cold sweat of a man watching the gallows being built. "Where... where did you get this?"

"I own the bank, Rokas," you purr, leaning over the desk, your violet eyes burning into his. "I own the debt. And now, I own the man."

He looks up, desperate. "I can pay. Give me time. A month. I have investments..."

"You have nothing," you correct him. "You are insolvent. If I hand this to Grand Duke Ravengard, you will be stripped of your rank and thrown in the cells you currently command. Your family will be destitute. Your name will be mud."

You reach out, straightening his collar with a terrifying gentleness. "But I am not unreasonable. I do not want your gold, Vice-Marshal. I want your *vision*."

"My... vision?"

"I want you to look where I tell you to look," you whisper. "And to be blind where I need shadow. The river patrols on Tarsakh 5. They will be... redirected. The investigation into House Umbrage's finances. It will be... concluded. You will be the shield that guards my interests, and in return, this debt remains a secret between friends."

Rokas slumps, the fight leaving him. He nods, a broken man accepting his new master. "The river... yes. I can move the patrols. A training exercise. And Umbrage... the file is closed."

"Good," you smile, retrieving the marker but not destroying it. "Welcome to the new order, Vice-Marshal."
