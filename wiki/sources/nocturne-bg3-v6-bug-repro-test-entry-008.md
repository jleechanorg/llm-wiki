## Scene 008: The Alcove Declaration

**Timestamp:** 2026 DR, 1 15, 06:20:01 | **Location:** Shattered Sanctum - War Room Alcove | **Status:** Lvl 5 Bard | HP: 33/33

Nocturne pulls Lae'zel, Astarion, and Shadowheart into a secluded alcove. The air drops several degrees as her presence expands.

**"Listen closely,"** she whispers, violet eyes flashing with starry luminescence. **"We are going to the Grove, but not to save it. We will subvert their ritual, turn their own 'Rite of Thorns' into a siphon for my own ascension. The Shadow Druids are not enemies; they are a resource we will use to hollow out the sanctuary from the inside. Once the power is mine, we open the gates for the goblins. We walk out of the ashes with a general's loyalty and a god's strength."**

**Shadowheart's** hand goes instinctively to her artifact, her brow furrowing. **"Double-crossing the druids *and* the goblins? It's suicidal, Nocturne."**

**Lae'zel's** eyes narrow. **"A risky gambit, istik. But the rewards of such a harvest are undeniable. If your 'Siren's song' can truly turn their magic into a weapon for us, I will follow."**

**Astarion** simply smirks, red eyes gleaming. **"Darling, you had me at 'hollow out the sanctuary.' Let's go make some history."**
