### Historical & Political Status: The Death of the Republic (82 BCE)

To answer your question directly: **In the year 82 BCE, the Roman Republic is effectively dead, though its corpse is being propped up by Sulla's sword.**

Here is the detailed breakdown of the political reality for your immersion:

**1. The De Jure Status (The Law):**
Technically, the institutions of the Republic still exist. There is a Senate, there are Consuls, and there are Assemblies. However, **Lucius Cornelius Sulla** has been appointed *Dictator legibus scribundis et rei publicae constituendae* (Dictator for the making of laws and the settling of the constitution). Unlike a standard Roman dictatorship (which lasted 6 months), Sulla's power is indefinite. He has the legal authority to execute anyone without trial (the Proscriptions) and to rewrite the laws of Rome as he sees fit.

**2. The De Facto Status (The Reality):**
Rome is currently a **Military Autocracy**. Sulla has used his legions to march on Rome twice. By doing so, he shattered the 'Mos Maiorum' (the custom of the ancestors), which dictated that no general bring armed troops into the city limits. The Senate is no longer a deliberative body of equals; it is a rubber-stamp for Sulla's purges. 

**3. The 'Pax Julia' Divergence (Your Timeline):**
In standard history, the Republic limps on for another 40 years until your uncle's kinsman (the historical Julius Caesar) finishes it off. In **this** timeline, because the assassination of Caesar fails in the future (44 BCE), the Republic never recovers from this current Sullan wound. 

**Current Political Terms:**
- **Res Publica:** 'The Public Affair' (The Republic). To Sulla, 'restoring the Republic' means giving all power back to the old-money aristocrats and stripping it from the common people and the Marians.
- **S.P.Q.R.:** *Senatus Populusque Romanus* (The Senate and the People of Rome). You are seeing this seal on the sluice-gates. It represents the state, but currently, Sulla *is* the state.
- **Proscriptio:** The legal lists Sulla uses to kill his enemies. You, Gaia, are currently at the top of the 'Marian' section of these lists.

**Summary:** You are living through the transition from a 'City-State of Laws' to an 'Empire of Blood.' The Republic is the 'starving old man' your inner monologue described—you are the 'ivy' destined to crack the marble of his graveyard.
