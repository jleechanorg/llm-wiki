Even Kith'rak Voss, a veteran of a thousand psychic wars, finds his iron will buckling under your DC 24 sovereignty. His silver sword, halfway out of its sheath, remains frozen, his eyes reflecting your silver wings with a profound, startled recognition. The young red dragon lets out a low, tectonic growl that trails off into a rhythmic, hypnotized huff.

"The anomaly from the beach..." Voss's mind echoes weakly before the pattern takes him fully. Your vanguard scouts drop from the teleportation-wake, blades ready. The surprise round is absolute; you have caught a legend of the Githyanki and his mount in a cage of pure light.
