## Summary

A D&D 5e campaign set in an alternate-history Rome where Julius Caesar is Dictator Perpetuo and the Third Servile War (Spartacus rebellion) is underway. The player character is Aurelius Caesar, the 16-year-old youngest son of Caesar, posing as "Aurelius Varenus" — a wealthy merchant scion. Class: Level 6 College of Swords Bard with "Miasma" (Divine Blood of Venus) powers. Tone is political wargame, hedonistic thriller with low-magic Roman grit.

## Key Claims

- Caesar is Level 20 Warlock/Bard Gestalt; the Empire is autocratic with divine worship of the Julii
- The Miasma is genetic magic — the Julii have Domination/Charm bloodline
- Three sons competing for succession: Caesarion (East), Octavian (Rome), Aurelius (border wars)
- The Praetorian Hand is a 5-person black-ops kill team (Valerius/Sentinel, Felix/Sharpshooter, Titus/Dual Wielder, Cassius/Healer, Brutus/Tavern Brawler)
- 10 major noble houses and multiple factions (Liberators, Parthians, Germanic Tribes, Cult of Mithras)
- Aurelius is ESFP-ENTJ hinge — The Decadent Conqueror with beauty as weapon
