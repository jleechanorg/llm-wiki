## Summary
A Baldur's Gate 3 campaign following Astarion Ancunín, an Elf Vampire Spawn and former magistrate who was lured and turned by the vampire lord Cazador Szarr. After 200 years of forced service luring victims, he now seeks freedom, power, and revenge. The campaign begins on the Nautiloid ship, capturing the turmoil of escape and the struggle to regain autonomy.

## Key Details
- **Protagonist:** Astarion Ancunín — Elf Vampire Spawn, Ranger (planned multiclass with Rogue)
- **Setting:** Baldur's Gate 3 setting, begins on the Nautiloid (mind flayer ship)
- **Core Theme:** Freedom vs. servitude, power and control, trauma and survival
- **Tone:** Dark, sardonic, manipulative, hedonistic
- **Total Scenes:** Campaign start

## Player Character
Astarion Ancunín is a 200-year-old Elf who was once a respected magistrate in his elven homeland. He was lured and transformed into a Vampire Spawn by the notorious Cazador Szarr, forced to serve as a lure for 200 years — drawing victims to the vampire's lair. Despite this torment, Astarion never fed on sentient blood, maintaining some measure of his former self.

His core motivation is twofold: regaining freedom from Cazador's potential return, and gaining enough power to never be controlled again. His personality is sardonic, manipulative, vain, pragmatic, self-serving, charming, and hedonistic — a deeply traumatized individual who uses humor and charm as armor.
