## Summary
A D&D 5e low-magic campaign set in the world of Westeros during the "Dunk and Egg" era (circa 209 AC). The protagonist Visenya Targaryen is secretly the elder sister of Aegon "Egg" Targaryen, traveling incognito as a squire called "Silver" alongside Ser Duncan the Tall. She is the last scion of House Baelarys — a top-tier Old Valyrian family of "God-Mages" or "Apex Conductors" — possessing preternatural "Apex" blood that makes standard Targaryens feel like shadows in her presence. The campaign centers on the Ashford Tourney and Visenya's struggle to maintain her mortal disguise while secretly manipulating events.

## Key Claims
- Visenya is a Level 6 Baelarys Weaver (modified Swords Bard) with INT 20 and CHA 18, using the "Apex Presence" to dominate through voice and geometric precision
- The Apex Weaver class progresses from low-magic martial-caster (Levels 1-16) to full High Valyrian sorcery (Levels 17+), with epic progression to Level 30 "God Mode"
- Her "Silver Song" allows subsonic resonance that feels like a law of nature; her "White Lie" kit includes Glibness, Detect Thoughts, and Suggestion
- Aerion Targaryen is her brother whose "madness" is a reaction to her inferiority; he senses her Apex status and lashes out
- The campaign setting is low-magic where her "spells" are flavored as Ancestral Resonance — manipulating the "marrow" of the world through voice and will

## Key Quotes
> "You are the Sun that burns too bright, the Apex that makes him feel like a hollowed-out husk." — DM describing Aerion's reaction to Visenya
