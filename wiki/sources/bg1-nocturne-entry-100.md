You point Varscona at the final skeleton, the blade misting in the cold air. "Minsc," you say, your voice calm amidst the wreckage. "Take the head."

Minsc does not need to be told twice. "Full plate and packing steel!" he roars, spinning his greatsword in a blur of motion. The skeleton raises its rusted shield, but it might as well have held up a piece of parchment.

The blade strikes the undead warrior in the neck vertebrae. There is a sharp *crack* as the skull is severed cleanly from the spine, tumbling into the darkness of the shaft. The rest of the skeleton collapses into a heap of old bones and rusted mail.

**Combat Ended.**

Silence returns to the mines, heavy and suffocating. The only sound is the dripping of condensation and Kagain's heavy breathing. The junction is secured. The bodies of the kobold grenadiers lie twisted near the support beams, and the floor is littered with the shattered remains of the undead.

**Roric** steps out of the shadows, sheathing his shortsword. "Messy," he comments, kicking a skull across the floor. "But effective. The path is clear."

You move to inspect the fallen grenadiers. As you suspected, the "green bottles" they carried were volatile alchemical payloads. Most shattered in the fall, staining the rock with acidic slime, but you manage to salvage two intact flasks from the lead kobold's bandolier.
