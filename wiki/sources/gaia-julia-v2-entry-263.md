**1. The Unit Block System**
Your military force is organized into 'Blocks' of 10 soldiers each. 
- **The First Legionaries (10 Blocks):** Heavy Infantry (Level 10) providing the iron wall for your operations.
- **The Shadows (10 Blocks):** Special Ops (Level 10) for intelligence, sabotage, and surgical strikes.
- **The Twenty (2 Blocks):** Your personal Honor Guard (Level 11), providing absolute protection for your person.

**2. The Strategic Layer (The Minigame)**
When enabled, the campaign advances in 'Strategic Turns.' You will manage:
- **Upkeep:** Your 220 elite units require roughly 600-1,000 Gold Denarii per day for rations, equipment maintenance, and bribes. With 8.25M in the Shadow Reserve, your sustainability is currently decades-long.
- **FP (Faction Power):** A numeric score representing your influence. It determines your ranking against rivals like the Julian Main Branch (70%), Marcus Crassus, and the surviving Optimate clans.
- **Buildings:** You can use Lord Calavius or the Shadows to establish Training Grounds (XP boosts for units), Shadow Networks (Intel bonuses), or Hidden Caches.

**3. Mass Combat Mechanics**
Should your Shadow-Army face a Legion or a rival household in the field, combat shifts to a tactical grid:
- **Initiative:** Determined by the average DEX of the units.
- **Ranged/Charge/Melee Phases:** Each block acts in sequence to inflict HP damage on the enemy's formation.
- **Morale Checks:** Units that take significant damage must roll to avoid 'Breaking' (fleeing the field).

**4. Mantle Resonance**
