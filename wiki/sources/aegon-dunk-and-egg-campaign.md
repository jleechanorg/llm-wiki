## Summary
A D&D 5e campaign set in George R.R. Martin's "Dunk and Egg" universe — a reimagined version where young women can be squires. The player characters are Aegon (a female Targaryen princess disguised as a boy, age 10, secretly the "Egg") and her hedge knight Ser Duncan the Tall. The campaign begins at Ashford Meadow tournament as a grounded character study and escalates across 1,065 scenes into godhood-level cosmic warfare with 655M gold and 760K followers.

## Campaign Arcs

### Arc 1: Origins (Scenes 1-30, Lines 1-500)
Character creation as a custom "Dragon Scholar" class. Aegon is a 10-year-old Targaryen princess dressed as a boy, serving as squire to Ser Duncan the Tall. The opening at a roadside inn near Ashford mirrors the novella — Dunk mistakes Aegon for a stableboy and gives her a copper. Aegon uses Royal Insight (INT-based Persuasion) to haggle prices down and analyze the inn door hinge when repairs are needed. Early dice rolls are modest (d20+4 to +6), reflecting a low-level character.

### Arc 2: Tournament Season (Scenes ~30-300)
Travel to Ashford Meadow, tournament participation, initial combat encounters. Character earns XP through skill checks (Investigation, Persuasion) and first combat. Dragon Scholar class gains spell slots and a custom "Draconic Bond" with a hatchling companion that will grow to full dragon size at Level 6.

### Arc 3: The Marches Campaign (Scenes ~600-630, Lines ~13800-14200)
The retinue grows to 75 combatants (Belaerys Remnant allies + Summerhall Spears). Key tactics: mirror signal spoofing to freeze a 200-man Peake pincer, ambush at "The Grey Sentinel" using Hypnotic Pattern. Player executes prisoners to maintain mobility; only officers are kept captive. Character rises to Level 8 with a Level 3 dragon.

### Arc 4: The Silver Song (Scenes ~900-1000)
Transition from military commander to divine sovereign. Character consumes smaller gods for power. The Belaerys Remnant becomes the core military force. Key strategic phase: negotiating the "Solar Eclipse" alliance with R'hllor.

### Arc 5: Divine Ascension (Scenes ~1050-1065, Lines ~28300-28779)
- **Solar Eclipse Alliance**: Player proposes R'hllor provide raw heat; player provides Valyrian geometric architecture to focus it. Nat 20 Persuasion roll (1d20+54+15).
- **God-Marriage to Daemon II Blackfyre**: Player proposes to seat the "Prince Who Was Promised" in Daemon's bloodline. R'hllor bestows the prophetic spark, elevating Daemon to Tier 3 Divine Vessel (Lvl 30).
- **Industrialization of the Shivering Wastes**: Using R'hllor's heat and Director Vaelis's expertise, the character transforms the wastelands beyond the Wall into crystalline gardens.
- **Final Scene**: The Northern horizon glows with silver-gold brilliance as the Sovereign and Sun rewrite the laws of the North.
