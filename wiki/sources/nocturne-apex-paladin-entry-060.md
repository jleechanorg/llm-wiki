Location: Road to Oakhaven, Early Evening

The detailed internal map of Tibbet's Farm, complete with its arcane ward and two professional guards, solidified Nocturne's (Level 5 Paladin, Age 16) understanding. This was no simple peasant abduction; it was a contested operation. The thought of the road patrol, now confirmed as a "disciplined group" by her earlier Apex Senses scan, lingered. Their presence, combined with the farm's defenses, painted a picture of a larger, coordinated effort. Information was her most potent weapon. She needed to understand this patrol.

Leaving the heavily defended farm in shadow, Nocturne began to move south-east, her steps barely disturbing the tall grass as she angled towards the main road. Her Masterwork Shadow-Weave Half Plate absorbed the last vestiges of twilight, rendering her a near-invisible shadow. Every movement was economical, her Coordination (14) ensuring silence even over the uneven terrain. Her intellect (Intelligence 18) was already anticipating potential sightlines, sound traps, and the patrol's probable route. Her low Agreeableness (1/5) meant there was no hesitation, only a cold, predatory focus.

As she drew closer to the main road, the subtle hum of distant traffic from Baldur's Gate became more discernible. She found a dense copse of trees offering excellent concealment, allowing her to observe the road without exposing herself.

She settled into a deep shadow, her Apex Senses already a hum beneath her conscious thought, preparing for a focused scan. Two figures. Not three or four as initially estimated, but two. They were indeed "disciplined," moving with a synchronized gait, their heads constantly scanning the periphery. They wore dark leather armor, functional and unadorned, and carried shortswords at their hips, with crossbows slung across their backs. Their stance was relaxed, but their eyes missed nothing. They walked slowly, then paused, exchanging a few low words, their voices too faint to discern. They were not merely passing through; they were maintaining a presence, a subtle perimeter.

One of them reached into a pouch and pulled out a small, metallic orb. He tossed it lightly into the air, and it hovered for a moment, pulsating with faint, almost imperceptible arcane energy, before drifting slowly towards Oakhaven. A **magical sentinel**, likely designed to alert them to any significant intrusion further down the road, acting as an advanced tripwire. This confirmed their connection to the farm's more sophisticated defenses. They were professional security, likely employed by the buyers, or perhaps even Kaelen's Shadow Weavers directly, demonstrating a deeper commitment to this "stock" than previously revealed. Their level of preparedness was significant for a mere farming hamlet.

Nocturne pulled back further into the shadows, a slow, grim satisfaction spreading through her. This operation was far more complex, and therefore far more interesting, than the initial contract had implied. The Underdark buyers had invested heavily. The potential for wealth, and perhaps even deeper power, was growing with every layer of defense she peeled back. The night was still young, and the true hunt had just begun.

She had 2 uses of Apex Senses remaining. Her EP was 21/36, CP 1.
