You nod to the rogue. "Lead the way, Roric. If there is steel to be found, we take it."

Roric grins and moves to the rear of the armory, near the heavy stone hearth that likely served the quartermaster. He kneels, tapping the flagstones with the pommel of his new magical shortsword. "Hollow," he announces, pointing to a slab that looks indistinguishable from the rest.

He slides his fingers into a hairline crack, his **Gauntlets of Dexterity** finding purchase where a normal hand would slip. With a grunt of effort, he shifts the stone, revealing a dark crawlspace beneath.

"Paranoid bandits," **Kagain** rumbles appreciatively, peering into the hole. "My kind of people."

You drop into the crawlspace first, the *Hunger* alert for traps. There are none; this was a panic room, not a vault. The space is cramped, lined with racks of high-quality gear intended for the Black Talon elite.

**Cache Secured.**

The haul is substantial. You pass the items up to the waiting hands of **Minsc** and **Dynaheir**.

*   **Shield +1:** A heater shield reinforced with adamantine bands. Lighter and tougher than standard steel.
*   **Potion of Fire Breath:** A bubbling red vial that allows the drinker to exhale a cone of fire.
*   **20x Arrows +1:** More magical ammunition for your longbow.
*   **Chain Mail +1:** Finely linked mail that shimmers with a faint enchantment of protection. Lighter than plate, but sturdier than standard chain.
*   **Heavy Crossbow of Accuracy:** A masterwork weapon with a sighting lens (+1 to Hit).

**Xan** inspects the Chain Mail +1 with a critical eye. "Elven make," he notes, surprised. "Stolen from a caravan, no doubt. It will serve better than these rags I wear, if you do not claim it."

Minsc holds up the Shield +1. "A shiny plate for the good fight! But Minsc needs two hands for his sword. Perhaps the grumpy dwarf needs a shinier shell?"

You have stripped the camp to its bones. The Armory is empty. The leaders are dead. The army is ash. There is nothing left here but ghosts.

The map to the **Cloakwood Mines** burns in your pocket. The Spider Woods await.
