You are Luke Skywalker, a Jedi in training, and your world has just been shattered. The Empire, dominant and confident, now tightens its grip on the galaxy.

Darth Vader's obsession with you has reached its zenith, while Emperor Palpatine watches from Coruscant, seeing the events on Bespin as a great opportunity, and a great threat.

The Force: The Jedi Order is a ghost, a failed religion in the eyes of the galaxy. The Sith rule from the shadows, their power absolute. The Force holds its breath, waiting for your choice.

Campaign Module 1: The Emperor's Fist

Campaign Summary

You are Luke Skywalker, apprentice to the Sith Lord Darth Vader. On Bespin, faced with the terrible truth of your heritage and the futility of the Rebellion's struggle, you took your father's hand. For two years, you have been his student, your power in the Force growing under his brutal tutelage. You walk a razor's edge between light and dark, clinging to the belief that together, you can overthrow the corrupt Emperor Palpatine and bring true, lasting order to the galaxy. You are the commander of Vader's new elite task force—the "Emperor's Fist"—hunting down the Empire's most dangerous enemies.

Your loyalty is now tested. You have been dispatched to the Outer Rim to hunt down a rogue Inquisitor who has gone silent. Your father believes she is building a power base to challenge him; the Emperor believes she is a Rebel sympathizer. When you find her, you discover she is neither. She is a follower of a lost, pre-Jedi Force tradition that seeks true balance. She offers you a third philosophy, a path that is neither Jedi nor Sith, and a way to use your immense power to heal the galaxy rather than rule it.

You are now caught between three powerful and morally grey destinies. Do you uphold your new oath to your father and follow the path of Order, destroying a potential threat to the stability you hope to forge? Or do you break your new vows and join the rogue Inquisitor's quest for Balance, betraying your only family for a truth that might save the galaxy, or plunge it into even greater chaos?

World History (The Shadow of the Son Timeline)

The Turning Point: The Choice on Bespin (3 ABY) The history of the galaxy diverged in a single, desperate moment. On Cloud City, after losing his hand and learning the truth of his parentage, a broken Luke Skywalker did not let go. Faced with the choice between a lonely death and his father's promise of power and order, he chose his father. Darth Vader, triumphant, recovered his son from the gantry and brought him before a delighted, if wary, Emperor Palpatine. On the Millennium Falcon, Leia Organa's connection to the Force was met not with her brother's call, but with a terrifying, triumphant silence.

The Age of the Fist: A New Imperial Order (3 ABY - 5 ABY) The next two years saw the consolidation of Imperial power. The Rebellion, its greatest symbol of hope now a weapon of their enemy, was dealt a catastrophic blow to its morale. Led by a grieving but resolute Leia, they scattered, becoming a hunted and increasingly desperate remnant. Luke, under Vader's tutelage, was forged into a figure of terrifying legend. He learned to channel his anger and passion, his power growing exponentially, though he secretly clung to the light within him. He was made commander of a new elite legion, the "Emperor's Fist," tasked with hunting high-level Rebel targets and rogue Force-sensitives. Meanwhile, Palpatine, ever the master of shadows, saw the new father-son dynamic as a potential threat to his own rule and accelerated his own contingency plans on the secret Sith world of Exegol. The galaxy has fallen into a grim, stable peace under the Empire's absolute control, but new, forgotten Force traditions, sensing the profound shift in the cosmic balance, have begun to stir from the shadows.

Campaign Module 2: The Jedi's Shadow

Campaign Summary

You are Luke Skywalker, the last hope of the Jedi, but you are haunted by shadows. On Bespin, you learned the terrible truth of your father, Darth Vader, and the galaxy learned that even its brightest star could be nearly extinguished. A year has passed, a year you have spent in seclusion on Dagobah, trying to complete your training under a dying Master Yoda while wrestling with the darkness in your own blood.

Your path is now tested. While meditating, you are rocked by two powerful visions. The first is a horrifying glimpse of a secret Imperial facility on the volcanic world of Mustafar, where Force-sensitive children are being imprisoned and subjected to terrible experiments by agents of the Emperor. The second is a cryptic message from a mysterious, cloaked woman who claims to be from a hidden sect of the Jedi that survived the Purge. She offers you knowledge of the Force that Yoda never taught, techniques that could defeat Vader and the Emperor, but only if you abandon the "failed" path of the old Jedi Order and seek her out on the ancient world of Tython.

You are now caught between two desperate and conflicting paths of the light. Do you follow the Jedi's call to Compassion, launching a reckless, immediate rescue mission to save the innocent children from the horrors of the Empire, even if it means facing Vader unprepared? Or do you follow the promise of Knowledge, seeking out this mysterious master to learn forbidden techniques that might give you the power to win the war, but could cost you your soul and the lives of the children on Mustafar?

World History (The Shattered Hope Timeline)

The Turning Point: The Choice on Bespin (3 ABY) The canon of the galaxy holds. Luke Skywalker, horrified by the truth, rejects his father's offer and casts himself into the abyss, to be rescued by a desperate Leia Organa and the crew of the Millennium Falcon. The galaxy now knows that the son of Skywalker lives, but the revelation of his parentage sends shockwaves through both the Empire and the Rebellion.

The Galaxy in Hiding (3 ABY - 4 ABY) The year that followed was one of desperation. The Rebellion, scattered after the Battle of Hoth, is on the run, hunted relentlessly by Vader's fleet. Luke spends much of his time on Dagobah with a fading Master Yoda, but his training is now tainted by fear and doubt. Vader's obsession with finding his son grows, a weakness that the Emperor notes with keen interest. To ensure his own power, Palpatine secretly empowers a new rival to Vader: the Grand Inquisitor, a master of hunting Jedi, is resurrected through Sith alchemy and given command of a new generation of assassins, tasked with observing Vader and, if necessary, eliminating the Skywalker threat entirely. Across the galaxy, other Force traditions, long dormant, sense the coming climax between light and dark and begin to make their own moves, believing the time of both the Jedi and the Sith is over.
