    *   Their "levels" as powerful mortals would remain consistent with their lore (e.g., high-level wizards/sorcerers), but their effective power, freedom, and agency are now entirely dictated by Mystra (and thus, The Absolute).
4.  **Proposed Action:** I will clarify their specific status and estimated levels, as requested, within the context of the new reality.
