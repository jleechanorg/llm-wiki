Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**51090 XP**) to reach **Level 14**. This is a monumental achievement, placing him firmly in the Tier 3 range of shinobi power, capable of dealing with major national threats and ancient evils.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 14:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 5
    *   **HP Gain:** 5 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 7 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 106 to **113 HP**. He is now at 113/113 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 14 Ninja, Itachi gains significant advancements as per the approved "Itachi Uchiha: Ninja Custom Class Feature Manifest":

    1.  **Sleight of Hand Expertise (Rogue):** Itachi's dexterity and precision in subtle manipulation deepen. He gains **Advantage** on all Coordination (Sleight of Hand) checks. (This was not taken as a prior Expertise, so he gains it now).

    2.  **New Illusion Spells (7th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu reaches new, terrifying depths, unlocking illusions capable of shaping reality itself or profoundly breaking the minds of even the most formidable foes. He learns a **second** 7th-level Illusion spell. (He already knows Projected Disguise).

        Please choose one of the following 7th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 14:
        *   **1. Mirage Arcane:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 12 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create an illusion of terrain or structures that is so convincing it becomes physically real to anyone who fails a **DC 18 Intelligence (Investigation) check**. Creatures perceive the illusion as if it were solid, but Itachi (and those he tells) can pass through it. The illusion is permanent until dispelled by higher-level magic or a specific condition. This allows for long-term, large-scale environmental manipulation for strategic advantage (e.g., creating fake cliffs, hidden paths, illusory fortresses).
        *   **2. Simulacrum (Major Illusion):**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 12 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a perfect illusory duplicate of another creature. This duplicate looks, sounds, and acts like the creature it imitates, and can even mimic some of its non-combat abilities. It is not fully real; it has 1 HP, and if it takes damage, it vanishes. It cannot learn new abilities or gain XP. This is a powerful tool for complex espionage, misdirection, or setting up elaborate traps using a false identity.

Please choose one of these 7th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 14. Once you've made this choice, I will finalize Itachi's Level 14 progression and return to STORY MODE.
