## Summary
A Game of Thrones solo D&D 5e campaign set in 300 AC (alternate timeline where Robert died earlier). Visenya Targaryen, a 14-year-old Level 6 Apex Weaver (Bard/Mastermind gestalt), vacations incognito in the North as "Lady Elyse Celtigar." Known to the realm as the "Blood Dragon," she is Lord Commander of the Blackguard but uses her vacation to mathematically map Northern resentments. Core theme: a godling among mortals, testing how far she can bend the North before it breaks.

## Key Claims
- Visenya's Apex Weaver class perceives "Information Geometry" -- the stress lines and mathematical architecture of physical and social systems
- The Static Field drops ambient room temperature by 20 degrees and grants permanent Advantage on Intimidation/Insight
- The Heat System (0-100) tracks how close NPCs are to recognizing the Blood Dragon
- The Entropy Toll mechanic causes exhaustion if she witnesses a lie and does not correct it
- Her retinue includes Daenerys (aunt, moral buffer), Ser Davos (tactical executor), and the Master Weaver (embedded spymaster)
- Key target: Jon Snow, whose Targaryen features make him a fascinating puzzle she constantly tries to provoke
