## Summary

Nocturne is a Level 9 Bard (College of Perdition) in Baldur's Gate, 1492 DR. Her family was sold into "The Bloom Shadow" Upper City brothel to settle debts they didn't create. She survived by studying her captors, then hijacked the Cassalanter soul-tithe ritual meant to sacrifice her sisters Elara and Lyra, redirecting it to liquidate her enemies and catch the eye of Archdevil Zariel herself. Now a proxy of Avernus with ember-glow palms, she must navigate the power vacuum left by the Absolute's defeat.
