Their interaction was clear: EMS was not made redundant by the Rinnegan. Instead, it was the **ultimate foundation**. One was the perfected form of his bloodline's peak, the other, a divine evolution beyond it. He saw it as a tiered progression, not a replacement.

This solidified his conviction: Sasuke's destiny, his suffering, was absolutely essential for the perfected EMS, which would, in turn, serve as the ultimate stepping stone to the Rinnegan.
