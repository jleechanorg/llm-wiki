### [CHARACTER REVIEW - Nocturne Sosuke]

The velvet curtains of the *Bloom Shadow* seem to ripple as you stand before the polished silver mirror, a vision of porcelain perfection and ruined silk. You are **Nocturne Sosuke**, the "Black Diamond," a sixteen-year-old paradox of aristocratic grace and Abyssal hunger. Though your frame is delicate, your skin has begun to take on that unsettlingly flawless, marble-like texture—the first sign of the *Uncanny Valley* you are descending into.

At **Level 5**, you have already begun to displace the air in the room. Your violet eyes reflect the torchlight with a glass-like clarity that makes battle-hardened veterans look away. You stand in **Minthara's War Room** within the Shattered Sanctum, your small hand pinned beneath the Drow Commander's cool palm. The smell of ozone and brine—your signature scent—clashes with the incense of the room. 

You are a de-powered goddess in a mortal cage, and the tadpole in your brain is the only thing keeping the dam from breaking. Minthara expects a servant; Malcanthet expects a Queen.
