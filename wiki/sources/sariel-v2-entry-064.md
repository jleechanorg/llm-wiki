The horrific memory was a continuous loop, replaying itself within his shattered mind, each beat a fresh torment. You felt a profound pity for him, a scholar trapped in an endless, screaming loop of raw experience. This wasn't merely madness; it was a prison forged from pure, unendurable truth.
