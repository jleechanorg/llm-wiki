For three relentless months, Oakhaven's Reach, the heart of Valoria, became the grand forge of Daemon's (Level 10, Age 30) burgeoning army. Every day, from dawn till dusk, the vast training grounds outside the Keep echoed with the rhythmic pounding of feet, the clang of steel, and the guttural shouts of Daemon and his elite. His formidable Physique (18.625) was a constant, brutal inspiration, pushing the 90 Level 7 recruits, the ~50 Level 6 Blackwood Guards, and the ~100 Level 3 Valorian Recruits beyond what they thought possible. Joric (Level 8, Age 32), Gareth (Level 9, Age 29), Ragnar (Level 9, Age 33), and Theron (Level 9, Age 28), his personal vanguard, were tireless in their merciless drills, transforming the rabble into a truly cohesive and terrifying fighting force. Borin (Level 8, Age 30), meanwhile, ensured Silverport's martial might remained sharp.

In the midst of this relentless military grind, the most crucial event of all unfolded. Back in Silverport, under the watchful eyes of Mina (Age 60) and Master Elms (Age 75), Seraphina (Age 20) finally gave birth. The cries of a healthy, robust baby boy filled the chambers – a true testament to Daemon's relentless will. The infant, named **Valerius**, after the former Duke, bore no visible frailty, no strange marks; he was a strong, unblemished heir, the very embodiment of Daemon's ascendant power. News of the successful birth reached Daemon in Valoria swiftly, solidifying the cold, pragmatic joy of lineage secured. Seraphina recovered swiftly, her purpose fulfilled. Elara Croft (Age 19) remained barren, a silent, living reminder of the 'curse' that had been defied.

Concurrently, Kael (Level 9, Age 25), operating autonomously from Valoria, relentlessly drove the full integration of the Duchy. Its vast agricultural wealth flowed into Daemon's coffers, its populace was systematically broken and remade into loyal subjects, and its young men swelled the training ranks. His spy network, subtly active, continued to sow discord within the Republic of Aethelgarde, preparing it for future conquest. Reports from Valerius (Level 6, Age 48) in Riverbend, Volkov (Level 6, Age 35) in Oakhaven, Lyra (Level 6, Age 29) in Stonehaven, and Lady Isolde Blackwood (Level 6, Age ~22) in Blackwood, all affirmed their continued, brutal efficiency in managing their respective domains, ensuring gold flow and ceaseless recruit training.
