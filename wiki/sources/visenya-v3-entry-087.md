To answer your question: **Dragonets are indeed part of the lore, but they are not typically used as scout mounts in the books or the TV show.**

**1. The Lore Context:**
In the *A Song of Ice and Fire* books, 'dragonet' usually refers to very young or stunted dragons (like those in the pits of Meereen). They are roughly the size of a large hound or a pony. While they can breathe fire, they are generally considered too small to carry a full-grown human rider into combat.
