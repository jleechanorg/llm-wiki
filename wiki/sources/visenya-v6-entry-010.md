## Summary
Visenya orders Robb to brand his own mother, Catelyn Stark, with the three-headed dragon sigil — a "curated mercy" that preserves her life while permanently marking her as Visenya's property. Robb cannot complete the final execution (killing his mother) but can perform the branding. Jon watches with jealous silence.

## Key Events
- Visenya offers Catelyn as "the variable of the old world" that should be "deleted alongside the maesters"
- **Robb's failure**: He cannot bring himself to kill his mother — "The stone was too thick for a single stroke"
- He collapses beside Catelyn, sobbing: "Anything else. I will burn the world for you, but the stone... it's too thick here"
- Visenya pivots: instead of death, she forces Robb to **brand Catelyn** with a three-headed dragon iron
- Catelyn's scream is "a jagged, high-pitched wail that shatters the silence of the hall"
- Ned Stark (in chains) watches his heir "mark his wife like a common beast"
- **Jon Snow**: Watches from the shadows with "fanatical, silent jealousy" — he sees this as proof Robb cannot complete the mission

## Key Quotes
> "Spare her the finality of death, Warden. But give her the signature of her new Sun. Brand the Tully. Show your father that while she lives, she belongs to the Dragon."

## Character Notes
- **Robb Stark (Level 8)**: First visible failure — the Northern "Granite" hasn't fully melted; it has "fractured into a thousand jagged pieces of cognitive dissonance"
- **Jon Snow (Level 8)**: Sees this as a variable he can exploit — a path to being the favored knight
- **Catelyn Stark (Level 4)**: Broken; the brand is on her shoulder, still weeping clear fluid
