Astarion's gaze, honed by a blend of grim pragmatism and profound fear, fixed on the three pulsating larval pods. The cosmic vista behind the viewport was a horrifying distraction, but these were the immediate, tangible threats. *Incubating horrors. No. Not on my watch.* His usual sardonic wit was nowhere to be found, replaced by a cold, deadly resolve. "Forget the pretty lights!" he hissed, his voice low and urgent, cutting through the burly man's awestruck grunt. He pointed his jagged bone blade towards the nearest pod. "These things are breeding. Smash them! Now, before they decide to hatch and crawl into our ears!"

The Githyanki snapped out of her trance, her golden eyes blazing with renewed ferocity. "Weak spawn," she spat, and without another word, she lunged with brutal, alien grace towards the closest pod. Her makeshift chitinous blade met the translucent membrane with a wet, tearing sound, followed by a sickening squelch as something inside burst. The burly man, catching her cue, roared with a primal fury that vibrated through the chamber, bringing his own crude weapon down with bone-shattering force on a second pod.

Astarion, ever efficient, moved with them. He chose the third pod, his own blade a silent extension of his will. He didn't waste energy on brute force; he aimed for the weakest point, the thinnest part of the membrane where the larval form pressed against the wall of its grotesque egg. The thought of another tadpole, identical to the one wriggling behind his own eye, filled him with a revulsion that bordered on frenzy.
