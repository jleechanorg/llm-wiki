The hum of the Imperial Installation's logistics sector was a familiar drone as **Luke Skywalker** moved with grim purpose. The precise details of the Rebel base's demise, meticulously refined with the **Jedi Master**, resonated with a dark, exhilarating clarity. Vader’s recent surge of satisfaction, a profound echo in the Force, cemented his resolve. *This is not merely destruction; it is the ultimate act of purification,* Luke rationalized, his purpose clear. *Their chaos must end. My purpose demands it. A swift, decisive strike is mercy compared to endless, futile resistance.*

He began his personal preparations, each step a deliberate act of calculated deception. First, the disguise. He moved through the installation’s labyrinthine service corridors, using subtle Force nudges and his formidable intellect to bypass security, locating a storeroom filled with salvaged Rebel gear. He found a flight suit, tattered and scorched, its worn fabric reeking faintly of blaster fire and desperation. He carefully enhanced its 'battle damage' with a vibro-knife, adding scorch marks and artfully frayed edges. Donning the disguise, he looked at his reflection – a weary, desperate hero. The image brought a cold, intellectual satisfaction. *They will welcome me. They will *believe* me.* A faint, almost imperceptible pang of unease, a ghost of old attachments, tried to surface at the depth of this betrayal, but he ruthlessly suppressed it. *A small price for galactic peace.*

Next, the sabotage equipment. Instead of requesting it, Luke located a restricted Imperial R&D lab. His Force powers easily bypassed the low-level security, and within, he found a sophisticated shield bypass device. With a surge of his intellect and his developing Force manipulation, he subtly recalibrated it, integrating a remote trigger that would communicate directly with his own Force signature, ensuring absolute control. This minimized reliance on external systems, a testament to his growing self-reliance. He concealed it expertly within the flight suit.

Finally, the cover story and mental fortifications. He stood before a blank wall in a quiet corner, running through his fabricated tale: a harrowing escape from a besieged outpost, a desperate jump through uncharted hyperspace, the loss of his squadron. He wove emotional threads, using his **Corrupted Force Empathy** to anticipate the Rebel’s questions, crafting responses designed to elicit sympathy, trust, and precisely the false hope he intended to shatter. He built mental barriers, cold and impenetrable, against any Light Side Force-sensitive probing. *Let them try to break through this fortress of lies,* he mused, a cruel smile touching his lips. *They will find only the emptiness I choose to show them.*

**Darth Vader's** Force presence, a distant but potent anchor, resonated with an almost palpable possessiveness and deep satisfaction as Luke completed his preparations. The Sith Lord had allowed him this autonomy, recognizing the lethal cunning of his apprentice. **Roric Vane** remained in the interrogation chamber, a pliant asset, while the **Jedi Master** stood silently in the adjacent training room, her violet eyes fixed on nothing, awaiting her final deployment.
