Behind you, the Vanguard of the Shattered Rose moves with the rhythmic efficiency of a nightmare. Your Shadow Spies bleed into the peripheral chambers, their forms mere ripples of ozone and brine as they sweep for the relic. 

You reach the heart of the monastery: the Solar Machine. It is a colossal apparatus of gold and amber glass, suspended over a chasm of pure, concentrated light—the Blood of Lathander. The machine rotates with a slow, grinding majesty, focusing the sun's fury into a singular, psionic ward. 

"The light is a cage, First Disciple," the lead spy whispers, appearing from the rafters. "But the Githyanki have already cracked the glass. They sought to turn this into a lance to scour the Shadow-Curse, but the resonance is... unstable."

You glide down from the dragon, your marble-pale fingers reaching out to touch the amber glass. You attempt to seize the golden geometry, to un-zip the machine's administrative layer and turn its fury against the Absolute's enemies. But the Lathanderian magic is a sun-fire that recoils from your touch. The natural 1 on your weaponization check signals a violent dissonance; as your Abyssal will touches the light, the amber glass pulses with a blinding, holy gold. 

Nocturne is thrown back by the sheer, radiant pressure, her porcelain skin glowing with a searing heat that makes her violet eyes flare pink-crimson. The solar machine begins to spin faster, its rotation erratic and screaming with a mechanical agony that shakes the entire monastery. You have breached the vault, but the light is currently a wild, unmastered storm that threatens to incinerate everything it was meant to protect.
