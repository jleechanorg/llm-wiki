## Summary
A historical fantasy campaign following Aurelius Julia Caesar V2, a Level 5 Paladin dedicated to anti-slavery activism in 40 BCE Italy. Son of Julius, he fights against the Roman Republic's institution of slavery while based in Capua, facing the Spartacus rebellion's legacy.

## Key Claims
- Aurelius opposes slavery on moral grounds as a Lawful Good paladin, making him a target of the Roman Republic establishment
- His wife Aurelia was rescued from slavery, fueling his crusade against the institution

## Key Themes
- Moral Courage: Standing against systemic injustice
- Anti-Slavery: The fight for freedom in ancient Rome
