**Proposed Special Gear: "The Silent Edge" Chokutō**

This is a master-crafted chokutō, a straight-bladed sword designed for speed and precision, fitting Itachi's fighting style. It is superior to standard shinobi weaponry but not a legendary artifact.

*   **Name:** The Silent Edge (Itachi acquired it from a hidden Uchiha family armory, a blade perfected for assassination.)
*   **Type:** Light, Finesse Melee Weapon (Chokutō)
*   **Damage:** 1d6 Slashing
*   **Properties:**
    *   **Finesse:** You can use your Coordination modifier instead of Physique for attack and damage rolls with this weapon.
    *   **Light:** Can be wielded effectively in one hand, potentially allowing for dual-wielding if Itachi acquires the necessary Feats.
    *   **Precision Strike:** When Itachi makes a melee attack with this weapon against a target that is unaware of him, or is under the effects of the **Blinded** or **Stunned** condition, the attack roll gains a **+1 bonus**. This enhances his ability to deliver decisive blows in stealth or against incapacitated foes.
    *   **Silent Draw:** Drawing or stowing this weapon can be done as a Free Action (no CP cost) on Itachi's turn.

This weapon offers a small, situational bonus that aligns perfectly with Itachi's tactical preference for silent, precise elimination and his use of illusions (like Tsukuyomi, which can cause the Stunned condition, or other illusions that might blind foes). It provides a mechanical edge without making him overtly powerful.
