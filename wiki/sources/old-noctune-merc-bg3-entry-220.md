You leave the Grey Harbor behind, the scent of salt and blood fading into the humid bustle of the city. The journey to the **Coiled Serpent** is swift. In the privacy of your safe house cellar, you unburden yourself of the caravan's spoils. You combine the hoard, the wealth of the Zhentarim flowing into a single, massive stream of liquidity.

But a monarch does not hoard; she invests. You ascend to the Upper City, the midday sun gleaming on the white stone of the Patriar estates. You move not as a thief, but as a sovereign returning to her court.

**Lyra** is in the solarium, surrounded by ledgers. She looks up as you enter, her face lighting up with a mixture of relief and devotion. She looks tired, the weight of her new Regency pressing on her, but when she sees you, she straightens.

"It is done," she whispers, closing a heavy book. "The creditors are held at bay. The staff is loyal. But the accounts... they are still bleeding."

"Then let us cauterize the wound," you murmur, stepping close. You do not offer coin; you offer a partnership. You place a heavy bag of Zhentarim platinum on the desk—laundered money, now 'investment capital' from the Silent Partner.

"Inject this," you command softly. "Stabilize the House. And take your own tithe, Lyra. A Queen must look the part."

She touches the bag, trembling. "You... you give so much."

"I give to what is mine," you reply. You reach out, your hand cupping her cheek, your thumb brushing her lip. The *Echoes of the Idol* hum beneath your skin, amplifying your natural magnetism into something irresistible. You are beautiful, terrifying, and absolute.

"You have secured the House," you whisper, leaning in until your breath mingles with hers. "Now, secure the future. We are not just partners, Lyra. We are the same creature. Two halves of a new order."

[Persuasion/Seduction Check: 31 (Critical Success)]

Lyra makes a soft sound, a surrender of will. She leans into your touch, her eyes fluttering shut. "Yes," she breathes. "Yours. Always."

She opens a drawer, pulling out a small, velvet sack. "I... I liquidated the last of my mother's jewelry. For the 'consortium'. For you."

[Loot Acquired: 1,000gp (Lyra's Tithe)]

You take it, your fingers interlocking with hers. You have the gold. You have the girl. You have the city by the throat.
