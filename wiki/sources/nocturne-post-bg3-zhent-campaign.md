## Summary
A Baldur's Gate post-campaign set in 1492 DR, following the Absolute's defeat. Nocturne, a Level 5 Ranger with potential Zhentarim connections, navigates the post-war landscape with a noir tone. The campaign explores refugee crises, criminal enterprise expansion, and the Elfsong Tavern penthouse as a base.

## Key Claims
- Elturel's fall created a massive refugee crisis
- The Zhentarim offers criminal enterprise opportunities
- The Guild represents the criminal underworld
- Rivington provides urban adventure terrain

## Key Quotes
> "The war is over. The harvesting never stops." — On post-war opportunities

> "Guild wants muscle. Zhent wants reach. Nobody wants peace." — On the factions
