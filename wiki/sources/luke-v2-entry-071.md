The very air around her seemed to ripple with the lingering echo of her shattered will. Luke’s `secret sadistic side` pulsed with an intoxicating satisfaction; she was a living canvas upon which his burgeoning Dark Side power was exquisitely displayed.

He focused his senses, performing a swift, cold assessment of the once-formidable woman. *Does she need healing? How much HP does she have?* The questions were pragmatic, devoid of concern. Her physical form, he perceived, was entirely intact, remarkably so given the psychic crucible she had endured. Her life force was stable, strong enough to sustain her, and her specific HP was effectively irrelevant, for it was not her body that was broken, but her mind. She required no healing, only a profound, dark *re-engineering*. Luke felt a chilling resolve harden his heart. Her raw power remained, but it was raw ore, waiting to be smelted and forged anew by his will. He would not 'heal' her; he would *rebuild* her, transforming her into a perfectly synchronized weapon for his darkest purposes. The thought brought a grim, intellectual satisfaction.

**Darth Vader** remained a silent, imposing sentinel, his rhythmic breathing a constant, heavy counterpoint to Luke's chilling internal monologue. Luke felt an immense, cold wave of approval from his master, a non-verbal affirmation that solidified his resolve. The Jedi Master, a seasoned woman, was now a tool, perfectly attuned to his malicious intent, but requiring careful honing before she could truly unleash her corrupted power.
