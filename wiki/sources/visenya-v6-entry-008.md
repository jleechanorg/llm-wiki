## Summary
Visenya returns to King's Landing with her Shadow Knights (Robb and Jon), now traveling openly with silver-gold hair. Her father King Rhaegar (Level 20) recognizes the "Apex" he feared. Prince Aegon (Level 5) is publicly humiliated in the throne room and forced to yield. Rhaegar grants "provisional status" for the Shadow Knights, refusing a permanent writ in open court.

## Key Events
- **Identity revealed**: Visenya's silver-gold hair is no longer hidden; the court sees the Blood Dragon
- Rhaegar's melancholy deepens — "For the first time, the King of the Dragon Throne looks truly cold"
- Aegon is forced to yield publicly: "The Blood Dragon's justice is hers to command, Father. I will not speak against her knights again."
- **"Obsession Paradox"** is now fully active: both Robb and Jon are fanatically devoted, competing for Visenya's favor
- **Robb**: "She is the fire at the center of their world"; blue eyes burn with "terrifying, protective devotion"
- **Jon**: Dark gaze fixed on the back of Aegon's head; hand twitching near his blade "as if hoping for a reason to close the equation"
- **Provisional Terms**: Weekly conduct reports required; Shadow Knights serve "under crown review"
