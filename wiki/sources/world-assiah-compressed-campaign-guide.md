## Summary
Compressed campaign guide for the World of Assiah, a D&D 5e campaign centered on the aftermath of the Celestial Wars where Lucifer's rebellion shattered the cosmic order. Covers factions, geography, history, characters, and major plotlines.

## Key Claims
- **Celestial Wars Aftermath**: Lucifer's rebellion created a multi-polar world with the Celestial Imperium controlling east and Shattered Host remnants dominating west
- **Current Date**: Year 11 of the New Peace, Kythorn Day 2 (campaign start)
- **Political Landscape**: Post-Treaty of Nexus, tensions center on succession questions for twin heirs Cassian/Darius and rising power of independent factions
- **Character Ages**: Sariel val Artorius (15), Cassian/Darius (18), Sariel's Companions (16)
- **Core Themes**: Divine rebellion consequences, political intrigue, order vs. chaos struggle, personal redemption/corruption
