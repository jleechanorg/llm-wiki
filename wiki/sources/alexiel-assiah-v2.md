## Summary
A D&D 5e high-fantasy celestial warfare campaign set in Assiah during the Thousand Year War. This is an updated V2 of the Alexiel Assiah campaign with significant character build changes: Alexiel is now a Level 6 Gestalt character combining Aberrant Mind Sorcerer and Assassin Rogue (with Extra Attack at Level 5), replacing the previous Warrior/Secret Sorcerer build. The campaign begins at the Sacking of Silverwood with full character creation mechanics visible, including stat distribution (16 CHA, 16 DEX, 14 INT for a dex-based character) and gestalt leveling where every level counts as one level in both classes.

## Key Claims
- Alexiel is a Level 6 Gestalt (Aberrant Mind Sorcerer / Assassin Rogue) with HP 69, CHA 18, DEX 18, INT 16 — a dex-based Charisma attacker using psionic sorcery and sneak attack
- The campaign uses GOD MODE for character creation and administrative changes, with full transparency of mechanics visible to the player
- Combat style combines Sorcerer spellcasting (psionic effects from Aberrant Mind) with Rogue sneak attacks and assassin crit mechanics
- Extra Attack is gained at Level 5 (a key homebrew element since Rogues don't normally get Extra Attack)
- The gestalt system means every level provides both Sorcerer spell slots/points and Rogue features simultaneously
