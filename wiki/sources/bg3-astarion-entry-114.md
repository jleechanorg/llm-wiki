He glanced at his companions. Lae'zel, grim and unyielding, stood like a statue of coiled aggression, her golden eyes sweeping the clearing, her disdain for weakness palpable. He raised a sardonic brow, a subtle challenge in his gaze, but his heart hammered with a desperate, almost pathetic hope for their pragmatic acceptance.
