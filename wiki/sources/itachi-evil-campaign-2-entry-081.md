Understood. Itachi Uchiha has reached **Level 5**. This is a monumental leap in his power, bringing him into the Tier 2 range of truly formidable shinobi.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 5:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 7
    *   **HP Gain:** 7 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 9 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 34 to **43 HP**. He is now at 43/43 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 5 Ninja (Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack + Illusion Wizard), Itachi gains significant advancements:

    1.  **Extra Attack:** When Itachi takes the Attack action on his turn, he can now make **two attacks** instead of one. This dramatically increases his combat efficiency.
    2.  **Uncanny Dodge:** When an attacker that Itachi can see hits him with an attack, he can use his **Defensive Reaction (1 CP)** to halve the attack's damage against him. This grants immense survivability against powerful blows.
    3.  **New Illusion Spells (3rd Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu deepens further, unlocking powerful, encounter-altering illusions. He learns the following 3rd-level Illusion spells:
        *   **Fear:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
            *   **Effect:** Choose a point within 60 feet. Each creature in a 30-foot cone originating from that point must succeed on a **DC 15 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or become Frightened (see `Destiny` 6.4) of Itachi until the end of Itachi's next turn. While Frightened, a creature must take the Dash action and move away from Itachi by the safest available route on each of its turns, unless there is nowhere to move. If the creature ends its turn in a location where it doesn't have line of sight to Itachi, the creature can make another Wisdom (Willpower) check to end the effect.
        *   **Hypnotic Pattern:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a swirling pattern of lights and colors within a 30-foot cube within range. The pattern appears for a moment and vanishes. Each creature in the area that sees the pattern must succeed on a **DC 15 Wisdom (Willpower) check** or be Charmed (see `Destiny` 6.4) for up to 1 minute. While Charmed, the creature is incapacitated and has a speed of 0. The effect ends early if the creature takes damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor.

Itachi's Level 5 progression is now fully implemented, making him an even deadlier and more versatile force.
