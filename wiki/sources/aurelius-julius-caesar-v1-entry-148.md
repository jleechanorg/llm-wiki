"Praetor Aurelius," Octavian whispers, his voice a dry scrape of wool that barely carries over the scratching quills. "I had expected your return to be marked by a Triumph, not a home invasion. Does the 'Sun of Rome' find the light of the Senate too dim for his liking?"

You lean over Octavian's desk, your liquid amber eyes locking onto his with a terrifying, unblinking focus.
