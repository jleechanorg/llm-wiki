### **Itachi Uchiha: "Ninja" Custom Class Feature Manifest**

**Core Concept:** A prodigy assassin, master of illusion, and strategic genius, combining the lethal precision of an Assassin Rogue with the versatile, mind-bending power of an Illusion Wizard, all infused with Uchiha ocular abilities. The "Extra Attack at Level 5" is a unique feature.

**Progression Philosophy:** Features are primarily drawn from D&D 5e's Assassin Rogue and Illusion Wizard, adapted to the Destiny ruleset. Where a feature is gained from both (e.g., ASI), Itachi gains the stronger benefit or choice. Where similar, they combine. Where distinct, he gains both. Some features may be gained earlier or later to balance his unique prodigy status and Mangekyō/Rinnegan progression.

**Hit Die:** d8
**Aptitude Score Improvement (ASI):** Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19 (as per Destiny Core Rules 3.5.A)
**Energy Point (EP) Pool:** Intelligence Aptitude Score x 2 (as per Destiny Core Rules 4.2.A)
**Combat Points (CP):** Coordination Aptitude Score / 4 (rounded down, min 1) (as per Destiny Core Rules 5.1.A.1)
