## Summary
A D&D 5e dark campaign following Nocturne, the "Silent Blade," a Level 5 Ranger operating in post-Absolute Baldur's Gate under Zhentarim colors. After the Absolute's fall, the Zhentarim ("Black Network") have filled the power vacuum. Nocturne is hired by Zarys to eliminate the Ironhead Legion — twelve Amnian mercenaries who raided a Zhentarim convoy. The campaign features shopping for exotic weapons (titanstring bow, Spider-Bolts), infiltration planning, and psychological warfare against Korg of the Ironhead Legion.

## Key Claims
- Nocturne is a Level 5 Ranger (likely Gloom Stalker or Hunter), HP 44, Gold 400, equipped with studded leather +1 and silvered rapier
- The Ironhead Legion, led by half-orc Korg, is based in a collapsed western wall of an old Flaming Fist outpost near Wyrm's Rock
- Korg is skimming from his Amnian employers — his personal ledger proves the protection racket and is a secondary objective
- The Black Basin: a hidden Zhentarim specialty shop run by "the Stitcher" dealing in exotic weapons (titanstring bow = 5000gp)
- Nocturne negotiates for alchemist's fire and mindfire toxin to make the massacre "psychological warfare"
