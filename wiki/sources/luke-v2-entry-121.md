His face was etched with a carefully cultivated weariness, a mask of desperate heroism. The shield bypass device, concealed on his person, thrummed faintly, a promise of chaos to come. *This is not just about power, but about the elegant application of it,* Luke mused, the thought a cold, clear justification for the widespread despair he would orchestrate. *Their chaos must end.
