# Character Profile Template & Standard: Astarion

## I. Core Identity
- **Name:** Astarion Ancunín
- **Archetype/Role:** Sardonic Vampire Spawn, Rogue/Charismatic Manipulator
- **Occupation/Social Standing:** Vampire Spawn (formerly a magistrate), now a fugitive and captive.

## II. Psychology & Personality
- **Core Motivation (The "Why"):** To regain his freedom and independence from Cazador, to secure his own survival, and ultimately, to gain power and control over his own destiny so he is never a thrall again. The tadpole offers a unique path to achieving this.
- **Greatest Fear:** Returning to Cazador's control, being powerless and exploited, becoming a mindless thrall, or being exposed and destroyed as a vampire spawn. He acts with a veneer of nonchalance, but beneath it is a desperate, cunning scramble for self-preservation and escape.
- **Key Personality Traits:** Sardonic, manipulative, vain, pragmatic, self-serving, cunning, charming, hedonistic, secretly fearful, deeply traumatized (though he hides it well).
- **Quirks & Habits:** Uses biting sarcasm as a defense mechanism, enjoys fine things, takes pleasure in others' discomfort (sometimes), often speaks in a theatrical or overly dramatic manner to mask his true feelings, preens, meticulously manages his appearance even in dire situations.

## III. Behavior & Speech
- **Demeanor Under Stress:** Initially, a frantic, almost panicked energy, quickly masked by cold calculation, witty (often cruel) remarks, and a desperate search for an advantage or escape route. He might lash out verbally or seek to manipulate those around him to ensure his own safety.
- **Speech Patterns:** Articulate, refined, often uses complex vocabulary and elaborate phrasing. Frequently employs sarcasm, double entendres, and thinly veiled insults. His tone can shift from smooth and seductive to sharp and venomous. He has a distinct, almost theatrical, cadence.
- **Reputation:** (Before capture by the Nautiloid) To those who knew him, a magistrate (public persona) or a dangerous, alluring creature of the night (secretly). To others he encounters, likely charming but untrustworthy, effete, or dangerous.

## IV. Backstory
- **Defining Moment:** Being turned into a vampire spawn by Cazador Szarr and enduring 200 years of forced servitude and torture, stripped of his autonomy and forced to commit unspeakable acts. This ingrained in him a deep-seated fear of powerlessness and a burning desire for ultimate freedom.
- **Relevant History:** A former elven magistrate in Baldur's Gate, he was lured and turned into a vampire spawn by Cazador. For two centuries, he served Cazador, forced to lure victims for his master, deprived of all agency and forbidden from feeding on sentient beings. He secretly yearned for an escape.
- **Secrets:** He is a vampire spawn and has not fed on sentient blood since being turned (due to Cazador's restrictions). He carries the scars of his servitude, both physical and psychological. He believes the tadpole may be his only chance at true freedom from Cazador. He is terrified of being returned to his master.
