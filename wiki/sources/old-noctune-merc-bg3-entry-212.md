You navigate the wagons to a secure Guild warehouse in the South Ward, not far from the Coiled Serpent. **Smiley Arvax** meets you at the loading dock. When the bay doors roll up to reveal three wagons packed with Elturian silk, rare spices, and untaxed iron, his jaw drops.

"You... you brought the whole damn caravan," he whispers, running a hand over a bolt of silk. "I thought you'd burn it."

"Waste is a sin, Arvax," you reply, stepping down. "Processing this cargo is your task. I want the liquid value in my vault by tonight."

Arvax laughs, a sound of pure avarice. "Done. The fence network will be busy for a month. Here's the advance on the bulk goods."

He tosses you a heavy sack of platinum—the Guild's immediate buy-in for the merchandise. It is a king's ransom.

The Black Lotus squad dismounts, exhausted but grinning. They have survived a suicide mission and come back rich. You nod to Vesper, dismissing them to the Undercellar for rest and revelry. 

You stand alone in the warehouse office, the dust settling. You have the **Soul Coins**, the **Manifest**, the **Red Wizard's Spellbook**, and a fortune in gold. The Zhentarim martial power in the region has been snapped. Their financial arm is amputated. Their political leverage is yours.

The Shadow Monarch has arrived.
