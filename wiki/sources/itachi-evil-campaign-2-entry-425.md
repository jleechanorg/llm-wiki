They began the arduous journey. Itachi personally carried the most critical assets – the Raikage's scroll and the spiritual vessel of Madara's fragment. Sasuke, his EMS blazing, effortlessly handled Killer Bee's scroll and Shu's casket. Kisame, his immense strength now at Itachi's disposal, carried the scrolls containing Darui and the Kumo Elite. They moved as ghosts, utterly undetectable, leaving no trace.

"Kisame," Itachi commanded, his voice cold and flat, resonating with absolute finality. "Your service is complete for now. You will return to the Akatsuki. You will report nothing beyond the capture of the Eight-Tails Jinchuriki, and you will state that you acted alone, as a rogue element driven by your own warped sense of duty. Itachi's EMS blazed, an unseen pressure subtly binding Kisame's mind, reinforcing the command with absolute, undeniable conviction.

Kisame’s body stiffened, his cold, shark-like eyes meeting Itachi’s. He felt the cold, unyielding resolve, the absolute dominance that permeated every word. He tried to fight, to resist, but the will was gone. The strength Itachi radiated was absolute. He was a force beyond anything Kisame had ever faced, including Nagato. Samehada, lifeless, remained clutched in his grasp. Kisame, the Monster of the Hidden Mist, fell to one knee, head bowed in a rare, horrifying display of utter submission. "Understood... Itachi-san," he rasped, his voice thick with uncharacteristic submission. "I will await orders." Kisame turned, a defeated shadow, and vanished into the desolate landscape, heading east towards his assigned Akatsuki territory, utterly unaware of Itachi's true base location.

Itachi and Sasuke then continued their journey with the remaining, most valuable assets, moving with absolute discretion. After a total of seven days of relentless, undetected travel from the Land of Lightning, they finally reached the secure solitude of their hidden cave base.
