## Summary
A conquest-focused campaign following Aurelius Julius Caesar V1, a Level 5 Paladin Oath of Domination with exceptional stats (INT 18 CHA 18) in Northern Hispania. He commands Legio IX Hispana and seeks to control Vindius Pass while wielding Veritas, a +1 Damascus Gladius.

## Key Claims
- Aurelius follows the Oath of Domination, ruling through strength and authority rather than consent
- His high stats and military command make him a formidable force in northern Spain

## Key Themes
- Domination: Rule through strength and authority
- Conquest: Military expansion in frontier territories
