# Character Profile Template & Standard: Astarion

## I. Core Identity
- **Name:** Astarion Ancunín
- **Archetype/Role:** Sardonic Ranger / Urban Predator
- **Occupation/Social Standing:** Vampire Spawn (formerly a magistrate), now a fugitive and captive. His desperate quest for freedom has sharpened his instincts, turning him into a cunning survivor with an uncanny knack for navigating and hunting within urban environments.

## II. Psychology & Personality
- **Core Motivation (The "Why"):** To regain his freedom and independence from Cazador, to secure his own survival, and ultimately, to gain power and control over his own destiny so he is never a thrall again. The tadpole offers a unique, albeit terrifying, path to achieving this. His ranger skills are honed by two centuries of forced, predatory 'hunting' for Cazador, twisted into a desperate instinct for self-preservation and tracking threats, especially those who hunt others.
- **Greatest Fear:** Returning to Cazador's control, being powerless and exploited, becoming a mindless thrall, or being exposed and destroyed as a vampire spawn. He acts with a veneer of nonchalance, but beneath it is a desperate, cunning scramble for self-preservation and escape. When truly confronted with this fear, he becomes cold, ruthless, and highly reactive, seeking any means to remove the threat, even if it means sacrificing others.
- **Key Personality Traits:** Sardonic, manipulative, vain, pragmatic, self-serving, cunning, charming, hedonistic, secretly fearful, deeply traumatized (though he hides it well). He has a keen predatory instinct, observing and tracking others, always looking for advantage, particularly against those who seek to capture or control.
- **Quirks & Habits:** Uses biting sarcasm as a defense mechanism, enjoys fine things, takes pleasure in others' discomfort (sometimes), often speaks in a theatrical or overly dramatic manner to mask his true feelings, preens, meticulously manages his appearance even in dire situations. He might unconsciously track movements or analyze the 'prey' patterns of those around him, especially if they remind him of his former captors or those who pursue others for profit.

## III. Behavior & Speech
- **Demeanor Under Stress:** Initially, a frantic, almost panicked energy, quickly masked by cold calculation, witty (often cruel) remarks, and a desperate search for an advantage or escape route. He might lash out verbally or seek to manipulate those around him to ensure his own safety. He becomes acutely aware of escape routes, cover, and potential threats or vulnerabilities in his environment, particularly honing in on figures of authority or those who act as enforcers.
- **Speech Patterns:** Articulate, refined, often uses complex vocabulary and elaborate phrasing. Frequently employs sarcasm, double entendres, and thinly veiled insults. His tone can shift from smooth and seductive to sharp and venomous. He has a distinct, almost theatrical, cadence.
- **Reputation:** (Before capture by the Nautiloid) To those who knew him, a magistrate (public persona) or a dangerous, alluring creature of the night (secretly). To others he encounters, likely charming but untrustworthy, effete, or dangerous, with a subtle predatory edge that may make some instinctively wary, especially those involved in tracking or law enforcement.

## IV. Backstory
- **Defining Moment:** Being turned into a vampire spawn by Cazador Szarr and enduring 200 years of forced servitude and torture, stripped of his autonomy and forced to commit unspeakable acts. This ingrained in him a deep-seated fear of powerlessness and a burning desire for ultimate freedom. His forced "hunting" for Cazador, though abhorrent, honed his senses and survival instincts, shaping him into a ranger, particularly effective against those who hunt others for their own ends.
- **Relevant History:** A former elven magistrate in Baldur's Gate, he was lured and turned into a vampire spawn by Cazador. For two centuries, he served Cazador, forced to lure victims for his master, deprived of all agency and forbidden from feeding on sentient beings. This compelled him to develop keen observation, tracking, and urban survival skills as he navigated the hidden paths and preyed upon the unwary, often evading or dealing with bounty hunters and city watch patrols in the process. He secretly yearned for an escape.
- **Secrets:** He is a vampire spawn and has not fed on sentient blood since being turned (due to Cazador's restrictions). He carries the scars of his servitude, both physical and psychological. He believes the tadpole may be his only chance at true freedom from Cazador. He is terrified of being returned to his master. He possesses a deep, unspoken shame about the acts he was forced to commit during his servitude. He has a particular, almost instinctual, loathing for anyone who acts as a "bounty hunter" or "enforcer," stemming from his own experience of being "hunted" and exploited.

- **Class Features (Ranger Level 1):**
    *   **Favored Foe (Bounty Hunters):** When you hit a creature with an attack roll, you can mark it as your Favored Foe for 1 minute or until you lose concentration (as if concentrating on a spell). The first time on each of your turns that you hit your favored foe (**Bounty Hunters**, a specific type of Humanoid) and deal damage to it, you can deal an additional 1d4 damage. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus (which is +2 at Level 1, so 2 times). This extends to those acting as formal or informal 'enforcers' for powerful entities who pursue others for capture or profit.
    *   **Urban Tracker:** You are an unparalleled tracker and survivor within civilized settlements. This replaces the standard "Natural Explorer" feature.
        *   **Urban Navigation:** You can't become lost in urban environments except by magical means. You always know the general direction of major landmarks and the quickest routes between districts.
        *   **Urban Camouflage:** When in an urban environment (city, town, large settlement), you can attempt to move stealthily at a normal pace without penalty for doing so. Additionally, you gain Advantage on Stealth (Coordination) checks to blend into crowds or urban environments.
        *   **Urban Hunter:** When tracking Humanoids in an urban environment, you also learn their general profession, likely affiliations (if common knowledge), and how long ago they passed through the area (within reason, e.g., last 24 hours). You gain Advantage on Wisdom (Survival) or Intelligence (Investigation) checks made to track Humanoids in urban environments.
- **Feats:** None at Level 1.
- **Racial Traits (High Elf - Adapted):**
    *   **Fey Ancestry:** You have Advantage on Resolution Checks against being Charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
    *   **Darkvision:** You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors only in shades of gray.
    *   **Weapon Training:** You have proficiency with the Longsword, Shortsword, Shortbow, and Longbow.
    *   **Cantrips:** You know **Prestidigitation** and **Minor Illusion** from the wizard spell list.
