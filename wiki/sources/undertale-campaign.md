## Summary

Undertale is a D&D 5e campaign set in the world of the Undertale video game. The player controls Frisk—the fallen human child—as a Determination Adept, navigating the Underground populated by monsters. The campaign explores the iconic Undertale themes of mercy, determination, and the power of choice through a tabletop lens, with encounters designed for both combat and social resolution.

## Core Gameplay System: Determination

Determination (DETERMINATION) is the central mechanic of the campaign:

- **Mechanical:** Functions as a tangible force keeping the soul whole even in face of death
- **Narrative:** Powers the player's ability to persist, save, and reshape outcomes
- **High CHA/WIS Build:** Optimized for the Mercy path—navigating encounters through dialogue rather than violence

## The Mercy/Kill Path System

The campaign distinguishes between two fundamental approaches:

- **Mercy Path:** Choosing mercy in encounters, resolving conflict through dialogue, empathy, and understanding. The high-Persuasion/Insight build is optimized for this.
- **Kill Path:** Combat-oriented resolution, monster killing for XP and progression.
- **Grind:** Pure combat encounters.

## Major Themes
