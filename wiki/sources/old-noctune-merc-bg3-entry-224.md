"Exactly," you purr. "We are establishing a new institution. The **Shadow Bank**. We will offer loans to the desperate when the Counting House refuses. We will buy the debts of the merchants. We will own the roofs over their heads."

You look at Arvax. "And the Guild will provide the security. Every business we buy becomes a listening post. Every tenant becomes an informant. We don't just tax the city, Arvax. We *become* the economy."

Arvax reaches out, his hand hovering over the platinum. He looks at you with a mixture of terror and religious awe. "You're not just a crime lord, are you? You're building an empire."

"I am building a system," you reply, your violet eyes glowing. "And you are my architects. Go. Buy me this city."

They leave, burdened with gold and purpose. You are left with 350 gold pieces in your purse, but you have purchased something far more valuable: a permanent, self-sustaining engine of power.
