You move through the city with the urgency of a storm warning. When you enter *The Gilded Cage*, the bell chimes softly. The shop is quiet, the bloodstains from your previous visit scrubbed clean, though the smell of bleach lingers.

Vessic is behind the counter, reviewing a ledger with shaking hands. He jumps when he sees you, his face pale. "Monarch? I... I have the payments ready. The shipment isn't due until—"

Vessic drops his quill. "A... a cleaner?"

"An Arbiter. **Kaelen D'Vray**," you say, the name landing like a stone. "He arrives at the Basilisk Gate at dawn. His mandate is to purge incompetence. You are the first loose end he will look for."

Vessic begins to hyperventilate. "I... I have to run. I have to—"

He nods frantically, stuffing gems and papers into a sack. "Yes. Yes, the safe house. I'm gone. I'm a ghost."
