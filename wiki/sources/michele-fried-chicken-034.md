## Summary
Michele takes Kim to the back office for a private conversation. Using Bardic Insight, she uncovers Kim's true past. When internal memos were leaked proving the owners knew, the internet canceled Kim within 48 hours. She came to Crispy Dreams to build something real without a lie at the center.
