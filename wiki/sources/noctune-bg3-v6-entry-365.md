### 1. Mythic Sovereign Ascension (Tier 5)
This upgrade agent is triggered when a character bypasses the Level 20 capstone through the internalization of Artifact-level power (e.g., the Crown of Karsus). It shifts the game from mortal mechanics to administrative reality-warping.

**Operational Parameters:**
- **Authority Level:** Tier 5 (Mythic). Overrides standard D&D 5e constraints.
- **Infinite Arcanum:** Level 8 and below spell slots are set to 'Unlimited'. The character no longer consumes slots but directs the Weave's local code.
- **Conceptual Magic:** Unlocks 10th (Reality), 11th (History), and 12th (Portfolio) level spells. These are used for narrative-scale effects without standard checks.
- **Somatic Multiprocessing:** Allows concentration on up to 3 spells simultaneously. Feedback control ensures only 1 spell is lost on a failed save.
- **Stat Uncap:** Primary attributes (Charisma) are uncapped to 30, and Spell Save DCs are recalibrated to the 'Impossible Wall' (DC 31-36+).

### 2. Divine Rank Progression (Rank 1-20)
This upgrade agent tracks the character's status within the planar pantheon. It operates on 'Divine Potential %' and specific narrative triggers.

**Rank Tiers and Triggers:**
- **Rank 1-5 (Lesser Deity):** Current Status (0% to Rank 2). Triggered by regional sovereignty and the total subjugation of planetary specimens (e.g., The Netherbrain).
- **Rank 6-10 (Intermediate Deity):** Requires the displacement of a Planar Authority (Demon Lord/God) or the establishment of a multi-continental Divine Realm.
- **Rank 11-15 (Greater Deity):** Requires rewriting a fundamental law of the Weave or the Planes.
- **Rank 16-20 (Overdeity):** Multiversal singularity (Ao Tier).

**Divine Feature Mapping:**
- **Portfolio Sense:** At Rank 1, you sense everything related to your portfolio (Shadow/Solar Sovereignty) within a regional radius.
- **Divine Immunity:** You cannot be killed by mortal means; only entities of equal or higher Divine Rank can permanently erase your essence.
- **Realm Command:** You possess absolute authority over your Divine Realm (currently the Shadow-Cursed Lands), allowing for *Control Weather* and *Static Void* zones at-will.
