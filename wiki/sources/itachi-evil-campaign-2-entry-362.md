**Answer:** Yes, **unequivocally and profoundly**. The Rinnegan is not merely a powerful eye; it is the **ultimate ocular power**, legendary in its rarity and associated with figures of cosmic power like the Sage of Six Paths and Madara Uchiha. Its appearance is a world-shaking event.

Here's how various entities would react to seeing Itachi's Rinnegan:

*   **Knowledgeable Shinobi (Kage, Elite Jōnin, Root/Anbu leadership, Sage Mode users, Tailed Beast Jinchuriki, high-level intelligence operatives):** They would experience a mixture of overwhelming awe, primal terror, and immediate, profound strategic re-evaluation. They would recognize it instantly as the eye of legend, understanding its world-altering implications. Their reactions would range from desperate attempts to flee, immediate strategic retreats, panicked reports to their leadership, or (for the most desperate or foolish) suicidal attempts to acquire or neutralize it. They would know that the balance of power has fundamentally shifted.
*   **Lesser Shinobi & Civilians (General populace, low-level ninja):** They would be struck with awe, confusion, and overwhelming primal fear. They would likely perceive it as a divine or demonic manifestation, a force beyond mortal comprehension. Panic, worship, or utter paralysis would be common reactions. They wouldn't understand the mechanics, but they would instinctively grasp its immense power.
*   **Akatsuki (specifically Nagato/Konan, and any remaining factions loyal to Obito/Madara):** This would trigger an immediate and massive strategic crisis.
    *   **Nagato/Konan:** Their reaction would be complex. Nagato's Rinnegan is implicitly unique to him, and seeing another active Rinnegan, especially in an Uchiha, would be utterly shocking. It could lead to attempts to control Itachi, investigate his origins, or perceive him as a rival/threat to *their* version of the "Eye of the Moon Plan." They would view him as a colossal variable.
    *   **Obito's Loyalists:** They would likely be in utter disbelief and terror. The Rinnegan is Madara's ultimate eye, and to see *Itachi* possess it would be a catastrophic blow to their understanding and a direct threat to their entire ideology.
*   **Tailed Beasts:** They would likely sense its presence and power, possibly even reacting with recognition or primal fear if they recall the Sage of Six Paths or Madara.
*   **Cosmic Entities (if applicable in later stages):** Even beings beyond the mortal plane might take notice of such a profound reawakening of the Rinnegan.

Therefore, Itachi's Rinnegan is not a subtle power. Its display, or even its rumored existence, will send shockwaves through the shinobi world, fundamentally altering perceptions and strategic landscapes. Itachi's path as the "unseen architect" will become exceedingly difficult to maintain if his Rinnegan is publicly displayed.
