The sound of iron-shod boots on cobblestones thunders closer. You have seconds, not minutes.

"Block them!" Roric yells, sprinting toward the alley mouth.

You spin on your heel, the *Hunger* guiding your aim with supernatural precision. You spot the heavy iron sign of the Burning Wizard Tavern swinging on rusted chains directly above the archway the guards are surging through. You draw and loose in a single fluid motion.

The arrow strikes the chain link with a spark of flint. The chain snaps. The massive sign crashes down with a deafening *clang*, crushing a crate of apples and sending the lead guard's horse rearing in panic. The formation breaks, buying you precious moments.

**Distraction: Success.**

You turn your attention to the fallen Harpers. There is no room for error. You step over **Jaheira**, driving your boot into her ribs to check for reaction. There is none. You kneel briefly, not to mourn, but to harvest. You slash the leather thongs of her belt, securing a **Potion of Healing** and a vial of shimmering liquid—**Potion of Invisibility**. 

You move to **Khalid**. He lies face down, his hand still reaching for his wife. You check his pulse; silent. You tear a heavy pouch from his belt (**47 gold pieces**) and grab another **Potion of Healing** from his bandolier. His shield and armor are too heavy to strip in the chaos; you leave them as a monument to his failure.

"Garrick!" you bark, turning to the bard.

Garrick scrambles backward, his eyes wide with terror, trying to slip into the crowd. "No! Please! I didn't—"

You lunge for him, but the blood on the stones makes your footing slick, and the bard is slippery with fear. He dodges your grasp, stumbling toward the side street. 

**Roric** doesn't let him get far. The rogue sticks out a boot, sending Garrick sprawling face-first into the muck. Before the bard can scream, Roric has him by the collar of his motley tunic, a dagger pressed to his kidney.

"She said *come*," Roric hisses in his ear. "Walking is optional. Breathing is optional."

Garrick goes limp, sobbing quietly. "I'm coming! I'm coming!"

"Alleys! Now!" Roric shoves the bard toward the shadows.

You sprint into the labyrinth of Beregost's slums, the shouts of the Flaming Fist fading behind you. You are a fugitive, a murderer, and richer than you have ever been. The Harpers are dead. The contract is complete.
