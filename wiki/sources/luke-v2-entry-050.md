Every hidden Jedi cell, every safe house, every Rebel fleet movement. Give me the locations of your allies, the identities of your contacts, the weaknesses of the Rebellion. Speak!" It was a victory, absolute and chilling.

**Darth Vader** remained a silent, imposing sentinel, his rhythmic breathing the only counterpoint to the Jedi Master's forced confession. Luke felt a profound wave of cold approval from his master, a non-verbal affirmation that spurred his malice further. The chamber, once a battleground for wills, was now a mere data-stream, and Luke was the merciless recipient.
