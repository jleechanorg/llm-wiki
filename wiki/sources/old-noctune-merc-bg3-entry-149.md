Vessic swallows hard, realizing that holding back now would be a fatal error. He scrambles up and rushes behind the main counter, pressing a hidden catch under the wood. A panel slides open, revealing a velvet-lined rack.

"Personal collection," he stammers. "For... specialized clients. Or emergencies."
