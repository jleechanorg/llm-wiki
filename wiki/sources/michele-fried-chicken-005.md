## Summary
Michele sources high-quality ingredients for her restaurant. At Grover's Poultry, she convinces Old Man Grover to give a Grand Opening discount (30% off for three months) using her persuasive Bard abilities. She then visits the Green Leaf Collective in Echo Park where Elena offers her a 'Founder's Batch' of micro-arugula and heirloom radishes. On returning, she meets Marcus who offers to help unload supplies.

## Key Events
- Grover's Poultry: Michele gets 30% discount on premium breast and thigh cuts
- Green Leaf Collective: Elena provides Founder's Batch of micro-arugula and heirloom radishes
- Michele reaches Level 2 Bard
- Marcus appears and offers to help with the business
- Decision to accept Marcus's help, pay minimum wage, and hire 3 more people
