## Summary
D&D 5e campaign where the player embodies Boudica, warrior queen of the Iceni tribe in Roman-occupied ancient Britain. Character is a Level 6 Warlock (Oath of Vengeance) / Bard (College of Valor) with high Charisma (18), built as a combat and military prodigy. The campaign follows Boudica's rise to lead a rebellion against Roman occupation after her family was humiliated and her daughters flogged.

## Key Details
- **Setting:** Ancient Britain during Roman occupation (1492 DR)
- **Character:** Boudica, Level 6 Warlock/Bard (3/3), Human (British Variant)
- **Character Stats:** STR 16, DEX 14, CON 14, INT 10, WIS 13, CHA 18
- **Mechanics:** Bardic Inspiration, Pact of the Blade, Vengeance Oath
