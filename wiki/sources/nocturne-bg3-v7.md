## Summary

A D&D 5e gothic noir psychological horror campaign featuring Nocturne Sosuke — a 16-year-old half-elf Bard with Malcanthet (demon lord of Lust) powers, trapped in Baldur's Gate after the Nautiloid crash. Setting includes The Bloom Shadow brothel, Cassalanter Villa, Upper City. Arc: survival horror, brothel intelligence extraction, infernal politics. Features companion integration (Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Karlach, Lae'zel, Shadowheart) and custom Abyssal Siren Bard subclass.

## Key Claims

- Nocturne is Level 6 contained in a Level 1 cage (tadpole suppressing power)
- Custom class: College of Abyssal Siren — pheromonal/psychic manipulation, Soul Siphon, Thrall mechanics
- Physical evolution stages: Level 0 The Ruin → Level 5-9 The Uncanny Valley → Level 20 The Ascended
- The Bloom Shadow is Madam Zylra's brothel; Zylra is Lawful Evil Tiefling with Black Ledger blackmail
- Cassalanters (Victoro & Ammalia) are villains feeding souls to the Hells; current targets of Nocturne's revenge
- 11 BG3 companions as assets with Approval mechanics and romance buffs
- Horgus Gwent is regular client; Nocturne's goal: marriage long-con to Cassalanter infiltration
