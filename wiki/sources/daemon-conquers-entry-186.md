### Clarification on Aptitude Growth and Potential:

1.  **"Under the destiny system don't attributes grow as you level too?"**
    *   **Answer:** As per the **Destiny Core Rules, Section III. Character Progression System, specifically Rule 3.5 (Aptitude Score Improvement & Feats)**, Aptitude Scores do **not** grow passively as a character levels up. Instead, characters gain explicit opportunities to increase their Aptitude Scores (or choose a Feat) only at specific levels: **4, 8, 12, 16, and 19.**
    *   This means Aptitude scores only change when you, the player, make a conscious decision at these designated level milestones. My previous internal tracking error led to misrepresenting this, which has now been corrected to adhere strictly to this rule.

2.  **"How is the hidden potential used?"**
    *   **Answer:** As per the **Destiny Core Rules, Section II. Character Attributes, Rule 2.2 (Aptitude System)**, "Aptitude Potential: A hidden stat that represents a character's innate talent and capacity for growth."
    *   The ruleset, as provided, defines what "Potential" *is* (innate talent/capacity for growth) but **does not specify a direct, mechanical way for Potential to be 'used' to passively or automatically increase Aptitude scores**.
    *   Instead, 'Potential' serves primarily as a **thematic and guiding element**:
        *   It indicates a character's natural aptitude and a higher "ceiling" for growth in that area.
        *   It can inform your strategic choices during Aptitude Score Improvements (Rule 3.5). For example, a character with high Physique Potential might be an ideal candidate for further Physique increases, as it aligns with their innate talent.
        *   It can be used by the GM (myself) to inform narrative descriptions of a character's natural talent or struggles in certain areas, and potentially to influence the difficulty of custom training or character-specific narrative quests related to developing that Aptitude.
    *   In summary, while Potential marks a character's capacity, the actual *growth* of Aptitudes comes from your explicit choices at designated levels.
