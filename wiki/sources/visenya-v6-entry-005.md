## Summary
Visenya presents Ned Stark with a forged letter supposedly from King's Landing, warning of a "Northern Reckoning" — a military census by the Crown's inquisitors to evaluate the "Ice Cage's" tax compliance. The letter bears the official sigil of the Royal Blackguard and speaks of the Blood Dragon's own men coming to Winterfell.

## Key Events
- The forged letter is described as arriving via "a private courier — a cousin of mine who still serves in the capital's chancellery"
- Ned reads the clinical, threatening prose; Catelyn grips the back of his chair
- The letter claims the Lord Commander (Visenya herself) is "selecting her vanguard"
- Ned's response: "The Blood Dragon's own men coming to Winterfell? They have no jurisdiction here."
- Visenya offers herself as the "friend at court" who understands "the geometry of the southern mind"
- **The Silk Trap is primed** — the family is already isolated; now a catalyst is introduced
- **Social Challenge: Eddard Stark** — Ned's resistance wavers as fear of Crown intervention overrides his honor

## Character Notes
- **Ned Stark (Level 12)**: His rigid morality is being leveraged against him — he cannot conceive that a fourteen-year-old girl is forging letters to manipulate him
- **Catelyn Stark (Level 4)**: Present for the letter reading; fear begins to override her suspicion of Visenya
