## Summary
A Roman Republic alternate history campaign set in 82 BCE. Gaia Julia Caesar is a Level 1 Imperator with the title "Wolf at the Feast" and carries a divine parasite. The campaign explores the Republic's collapse, Fated Miasma metaphysics, and imperator mechanics in an alternate timeline where Julia's rise changes history.

## Key Claims
- The Pax Julia represents a world where the Republic never falls
- Fated Miasma is a metaphysical system tied to fate
- Imperator class drives the campaign mechanics
- Divine parasite provides power at a cost

## Key Quotes
> "The wolf doesn't destroy the feast. The wolf *becomes* the feast." — On the title

> "Fate is a chain. Miasma is the key." — On the metaphysics
