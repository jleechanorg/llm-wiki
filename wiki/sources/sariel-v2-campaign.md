## Summary

Sariel V2 is a high-fantasy D&D 5e campaign set in the World of Assiah, following 15-year-old Sariel Arcanus—the grieving daughter of the legendary "Mortal Queen" Alexiel—in the immediate aftermath of her mother's sacrifice. The campaign blends political intrigue, scholarly investigation, and emotional trauma as Sariel uncovers the truth behind her mother's secret demigod nature and the trap that led to her death.

## Key Details

- **Player Character:** Sariel Arcanus (INTP, True Neutral, Age 15)
- **Starting Level:** 1 (later advanced to Level 2)
- **Setting:** World of Assiah, Imperium era
- **Calendar:** Year 11 of the New Peace, Kythorn
- **Tone:** Tragedy, political intrigue, grief, mystery
- **Campaign Status:** Active (252 scenes)

## Player Character

**Sariel Arcanus** is the daughter of Alexiel Arcanus (the "Mortal Queen," secretly a demigod) and Prince Artorius. At age 15, she was kidnapped by the warlord Mordan and tortured, during which she inadvertently revealed her mother's absolute moral code: protecting children. Mordan exploited this to trap Alexiel at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields, forcing her sacrifice. Sariel now carries crushing guilt, believing her weakness caused her mother's death. She is an intellectual scholar with Starlit Eyes and a data-slate.

**Core Traits:** INT 18, WIS 16, Arcana/History/Investigation/Insight skills.

## Key NPCs

- **Alexiel Arcanus (Deceased)** — The Mortal Queen, secret demigod who ended the Host Wars. Her sacrifice is the campaign's inciting event.
- **Cassian Arcanus** (18) — Twin prince, heir to House Arcanus. Consumed by rage, secretly blames Sariel.
- **Valerius Arcanus** (18) — Twin prince. retreated into cold analytical silence.
- **Titus val Raziel** — High Commander of the Imperial Legions, leader of the Triumvirate, Alexiel's grieving protector.
- **Magister Kantos** — Ancient keeper of secrets in the Deep Archives.
- **Ser Gideon Vance** (25) — Sariel's sworn shield, member of the Starfall Guard.
- **Lady Cressida Valeriana** (16) — Sariel's lady-in-waiting and closest friend.
- **Rowan Thorne** (16) — Sariel's childhood friend, a scholar and moral compass.
- **Sergeant Kaelan** — The Starfall Patient, a witness to events Sariel seeks.

## Key Locations

- **Zenith Spire, Aeterna** — The Imperial capital's central tower, seat of power.
- **Deep Archives** — Secret vault beneath Zenith Spire containing Alexiel's journals.
- **Quiet Ward** — The deepest, fortress-like level of the Starfall Sanitarium.
- **Battle of the Sacrifice Fields** — Site of Alexiel's death, where Mordan's trap was sprung.
- **Starfall Sanitarium** — Medical facility where Sergeant Kaelan is held.

## Major Themes

- **Grief and Guilt:** Sariel's belief that her torture-induced confession directly caused her mother's death.
- **Political Instability:** The Imperium in mourning, factions vying to exploit the power vacuum.
- **The Nature of Power:** Alexiel's hidden demigod status vs. the Imperium's disdain for Nulls.
- **Truth-Seeking:** Sariel's journey to understand her mother's life, powers, and death.
