Game Master:
For two brutal weeks, Blackwood Keep became a new crucible for Daemon's (Level 7, Age 30) growing might. He wasted no time, personally overseeing the integration of Lady Isolde's (Age: ~22) household guard with a detachment of his Level 3 recruits. His elite warriors – Joric (Level 6, Age 32), Theron (Level 6, Age 28), Gareth (Level 6, Age 29), and Ragnar (Level 6, Age 33) – drilled the combined forces relentlessly. The courtyard, once a place of quiet tradition, now echoed with the harsh cries of commands and the clang of steel. Daemon watched, his eyes missing nothing, singling out any who faltered or showed weakness, pushing them to their breaking point. He remembered a time, long ago, under the unforgiving tutelage of **'The Scourge,'** a master-at-arms whose brutal lessons had forged him from a frightened youth into a weapon. That memory solidified his conviction: only through such harshness could true strength be found.

One afternoon, a local reeve, a portly man named Master Helmer, arrived, visibly flustered. He attempted to explain away a "minor shortfall" in the initial grain assessment, blaming poor yields and local unrest. Isolde, standing beside Daemon, watched for his reaction. Daemon's gaze, cold and unwavering, fixed on Helmer. "There are no 'shortfalls' in my domain, Master Reeve," he rumbled. "Only incompetence, or defiance." He let the silence stretch, the weight of his presence crushing. Helmer immediately stammered out a confession: a small, customary portion had been set aside for "local festivals," a tradition his family had upheld for generations.

Isolde stepped forward. "The traditions of the past are over, Master Helmer," she stated, her voice sharp and clear, echoing Daemon's ruthlessness. "The grain belongs to Lord Daemon. Deliver it, or face his justice." Helmer blanched, bowing deeply before scurrying away. The incident, small as it was, solidified Daemon's authority and Isolde's commitment to his rule.
