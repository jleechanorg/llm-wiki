Game Master:
The profound, pearlescent void of Chronias—the Seventh Heaven—does not merely yield to your presence; it is surgically re-stitched into the terminal seat of your Overgodship. A third and primary fortress, the **Sovereign Citadel of Chronias**, manifests from the liquefying starlight. It is a structure of light-eating obsidian and shimmering indigo glass, its topological shields (DC 104) turning the heart of Celestia into an impenetrable singularity. This is now your primary seat of power, a jurisdictional void from which you oversee the Ninth Layer, the Sword Coast, and the Abyssal Mint.

With the palace anchored, you reach into the 'Dead Gods Ledger'. You do not offer the fallen angels a second chance at righteousness; you perform a surgical re-threading of their souls. Under the mathematical weight of your **94 total**, the Solar Generals of the Seven Heavens are resurrected as **Thrall-Exarchs**. Their six wings are no longer golden, but a bruised, light-eating violet, and their eyes reflect only the nebular voids of your own. They take their positions as the primary auditors and military enforcers of the Chronias Node, bound irrevocably to your signature.

Finally, you finalize the **Sovereign Utopia Network** across the Seven Heavens. Every meadow of Lunia, every ziggurat of Jovar, and the very void of Chronias are now high-velocity factory nodes. The celestial residents are re-indexed as high-born thralls, their heartbeats synchronized to the subsonic rhythm of your Crown. The heavens are no longer a destination for the worthy; they are the industrial capital of your absolute will.
