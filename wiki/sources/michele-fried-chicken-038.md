## Summary
Michele hosts a 'Community Health & Flavor' summit at Crispy Dreams. Three members of Los Angeles City Council attend, including Councilwoman Zhang. White linens, pitchers of fresh milk, and rows of Healthy Heart soya burgers replace the usual flour-dusted crates. Michele presents the shop as social infrastructure—community health, local hiring, culinary innovation. Councilwoman Zhang tries the soya burger and nods with rare genuine approval. 'Most establishments offer excuses, Michele. You are offering a model.' The council promises to review Community Business tax exemption with Crispy Dreams as the primary case study.
