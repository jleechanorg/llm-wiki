## Summary
Game mechanic for transitioning player characters from Divine Leverage tier to Sovereign Protocol in a multiversal campaign. The ceremony guides the PC through domain expansion, NPC elevation, stat conversion, and political establishment within the Pan-Substrate.

## Key Claims
- **Trigger Condition**: Activates when `universe_control >= 70` OR specific narrative milestone
- **Six Phases**: Threshold, Sovereign Logic Generation, Sub-Deity Elevation, Stat Conversion, Imperial Context, Sovereign HUD Activation
- **Sovereign Logic**: Unique metaphysical law derived from campaign themes (e.g., "Offensive Entropy", "Celestial Bureaucracy")
- **Resources**: God Power (GP) from highest ability modifier, Substrate Points (SP) as multiversal currency/health
- **Aggro System**: New Sovereigns start at 8/10 lethal velocity, requiring diplomacy or combat reduction
