Despite the strategic rejection, the camp begins to prepare for the midday move. You spend the hour organizing your small detachment, ensuring the Praetorians understand the humility required of them. As you lead the group toward the northern tree line for a preliminary scout, the Subtle Lorica beneath your tunic feels like a heavy secret. You move with a clinical, quiet efficiency, disappearing into the grey-black soup of the mountain fog as the Spires begin their first true dance with the wild North.
