Luke absorbed every nuance, mentally dissecting each principle. He didn't just learn; he immediately began to pervert. He focused on amplifying discord, on planting seeds of paranoia, on twisting love into bitter resentment. He felt the raw emotions of simulated targets in the Force, then, with a surge of Dark Side will, he warped them, enjoying the precise, internal torment he could inflict. His power was not just destructive; it was *corruptive*, an exquisite tool for psychological warfare.

He watched her, a chilling anticipation thrumming within him, eager to see the dark brilliance he had awakened.
