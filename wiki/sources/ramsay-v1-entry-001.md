You lead through fear and magnetism, striking from the dark with a sadistic grace.

**Character Sheet Preview:**
- **Name:** Ramsay (Bolton-Stark)
- **Class:** Dreadblade Shadow (Level 6)
- **Attributes:** STR 9 (-1), DEX 15 (+2), CON 14 (+2), INT 13 (+1), WIS 11 (+0), CHA 16 (+3)
- **HP:** 45 | **AC:** 15 (Studded Leather + DEX)
- **Key Features:** 
  - *Dreadful Flourish:* Use Charisma for attack/damage with finesse weapons.
  - *Umbral Ambush:* Gain a bonus to initiative and extra damage on the first turn of combat.
  - *Sadistic Insight:* Advantage on Intimidation and Insight checks against those you have harmed.

**Starting Equipment:**
- *Bolton's Kiss:* A +1 Flaying Knife (Dagger) that deals extra 1d4 psychic damage on a crit.
- *Stark's Vengeance:* A fine longsword with a wolf-head pommel.
- *Shadow-Stitched Studded Leather:* Dark armor that grants advantage on Stealth in dim light.
- *The Flayer's Kit:* Masterwork torture implements and surgical tools.
- *Purloined Signet Ring:* Your father's ring, taken during your betrayal.

Your own father, Roose, stares at you from the high table with cold, unblinking eyes—eyes that now see his son as his greatest executioner.
