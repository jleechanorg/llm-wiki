Timing in this campaign operates on a Dual-Track Consistency system. Here is the breakdown of how events progress and the consequences of 'ignoring' variables:

**1. The Action-Time Link (Story Mode)**
Every action you take in Story Mode (dialogue, travel, combat, purging) advances the `world_time`. If you spend two weeks on a 'Red Moon Purge,' the rest of the world also experiences those two weeks. NPCs do not wait for you; they pursue their `npc_agendas` during that time. For example, while you were purging the city, Stannis Baratheon used that month to seize the Tyrell grain fleet.

**2. The Frozen State (God Mode & Think Blocks)**
In **God Mode** or when you use a **Think/Plan** block, the world is effectively paused. Time increments by only +1 microsecond for database uniqueness. Events are frozen, and NPCs are static. This allows you to deliberate or perform admin changes without being 'penalized' by the clock.

**3. Urgency and Consequences**
Events marked with `urgency: immediate` (like the currently active hatching of the Green Egg) or `urgency: urgent` require your attention within a specific number of turns or hours. 
- **If you ignore them:** The `refusal_consequence` defined in the game state will trigger automatically. 
- **Example:** If you ignore the 'Amethyst Heartbeat' to go deal with the Stepstones, the egg may petrify or explode because the thermal math was not solved in time.

**4. Background vs. Scene Events**
- **Background Events:** These happen regardless of your presence. You only learn about them if your `discovery_condition` is met (e.g., a Weaver report or a Rank 3 Intelligence secret).
- **Scene Events:** These are immediate prompts requiring player input. Ignoring them (by changing the subject or leaving the room) advances the timeline toward the 'failure' state of that specific event.

**5. Autonomous Progression (Wait X)**
When you use the `wait X` command, the AI simulates your character's autonomous pursuit of goals. During this time, world events progress at full speed. This is the only time the AI will make major strategic assumptions on your behalf, though it is programmed to 'pause' if a crisis point is reached.

**Summary:** The world is a living equation. If you subtract your focus from one variable, the others will continue to solve themselves based on their own logic and agendas.
