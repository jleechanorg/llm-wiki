## Summary
Visenya formalizes an alliance with Roose Bolton (Level 12) at the Dreadfort. The Bolton forces — the most psychologically aligned with Visenya's own sadism — become her primary military instrument. Roose Bolton is described as a "fellow Vivisector" who mirrors Visenya's own approach but lacks her elegance.

## Key Events
- The Dreadfort is established as Visenya's forward command post
- **Roose Bolton's forces**: Psychological warfare specialists and flayers; parallel to Visenya's own methods but cruder
- Roose is described as having "seen your wildfire and calculated that you are a high-risk asset" — he is watching for weakness
- The Dreadfort's intelligence network becomes integrated with Visenya's Weaver spy system
- **The Flayed Writ** is acquired: a Bolton decree written in blood, granting permanent Advantage on Intimidation against Northerners

## Character Notes
- **Roose Bolton (Level 12)**: The "quiet, sadistic terror" that mirrors Visenya's own — but "lacks your elegance"; Ramsay's "rabid-dog behavior can be easily triggered to discredit his calculating father"
- The Bolton alliance is described as "a fellow Vivisector" — a rare peer in cruelty

## Faction Notes
- Bolton forces become the blade of the Northern campaign
- The Dreadfort serves as the seat from which Visenya orchestrates the "Ice Cage Breaks" arc
- Roose is playing both sides (correspondence with Tywin Lannister and the Night King)
