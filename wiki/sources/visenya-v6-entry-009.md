## Summary
Visenya orchestrates the "Emerald Wedding" — a massacre at Winterfell where Wildfire is used to execute the Northern lords (Greatjon Umber, Rickard Karstark, and others) who came to "witness" Robb's wedding. Ned Stark is forced to watch as his entire command structure is deleted. The "Stark Wall" has not just been breached; it has been used to baptize Visenya's Knights in shared, irrevocable sin.

## Key Events
- **The Setup**: Ned was forced to sign letters summoning the Umbers and Karstarks to "witness" the new Warden's marriage — a trap
- The Great Hall of Winterfell is sealed; Wildfire ignites
- The air tastes of "ozone, roasted meat, and the metallic tang of terminal terror"
- Visenya lounges on the high seat as "the stone begins to crack"
- **"Emerald Wedding"** — Lord Greatjon Umber and Rickard Karstark are "shadows dancing in the kiln"
- Some have "collapsed into piles of smoldering fur"; others "claw at the barred doors, their fingernails leaving bloody tracks"
- **Robb and Jon** stand on either side of Visenya, "black cloaks billowing in the updraft"
- **The Obsession Paradox** is absolute — Visenya's knights find the screams of their former allies "the most beautiful song ever sung in this hall"
