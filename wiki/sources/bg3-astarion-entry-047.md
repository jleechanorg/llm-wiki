Astarion's patience, always a thin veneer, finally snapped. "Enough," he snapped, his voice sharp and devoid of its usual melodic lilt. He gestured with his bone blade towards the colossal, fleshy doors at the far end of the chamber. "The engine is inscrutable. These *things* are dead. Unless you fancy being cooked alive as this wretched vessel hurtles towards whatever hellish destination it seeks, we go *through* that." He didn't bother to hide the disdain in his tone; his preference for subtlety had been utterly wasted on this nightmare ship and his blunt companions.

The Githyanki, her golden eyes burning with an eager, almost savage light, merely gave a terse nod. She was already moving, her lithe, muscular form closing the distance to the doors with predatory grace. The burly man, meanwhile, let out a grunt of agreement, his powerful frame vibrating with suppressed energy, eager to unleash his brute force on something other than a Mind Flayer. He hefted his chitinous weapon, its serrated edges gleaming faintly in the dim light.

Astarion watched as his companions positioned themselves before the monumental barrier. The doors, a gruesome fusion of organic tissue and hardened chitin, pulsed faintly, their surface slick and glistening. The Githyanki struck first, a series of rapid, precise blows aimed at the seams where the two halves met, her weapon tearing through membrane with wet, squelching sounds. Black ichor, thin and glistening, began to seep from the wounds. Then, the burly man added his weight, slamming his makeshift blade against the chitinous surface with a thunderous impact that made the entire chamber shudder.

The doors groaned, a sound like a tortured beast, straining against the combined assault. Tendrils of flesh snapped, and the chitinous plates began to crack under the relentless barrage. Slowly, agonizingly, a jagged fissure appeared down the center, widening with sickening pops and squelches. The Mind Flayers were dead, but the ship itself seemed to scream in protest. With a final, agonizing rip, the two halves of the doors pulled apart, revealing a swirling, pulsating tunnel of living, alien flesh beyond. The stench of ozone and raw, otherworldly power surged from the opening.
