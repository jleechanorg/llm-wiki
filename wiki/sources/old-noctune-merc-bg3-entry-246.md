"The truth is a heavy stone, Lyra," you murmur, your voice a soft vibration in the quiet room. "But a rumor... a rumor is a knife in the dark. It cuts deeper because it cannot be seen."

You dictate the poison. She writes. The letters are not explicit warnings; they are inquiries, filled with feigned concern. *"Have you heard of the trouble in Elturel? By mid-afternoon, the city is humming. You walk through the market squares, and you hear it—the hushed conversations, the nervous glances at shadows. The name "Moonrise" is on a thousand lips, a phantom threat you conjured from thin air. The Patriars are not just afraid; they are looking for a savior.

The stage is primed. When the ship arrives in three days, it will not be an invasion; it will be a prophecy fulfilled. And you will be the only one holding the shield.
