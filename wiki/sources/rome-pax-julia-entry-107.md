"Decimus, Papius," you command, tracing a line on the hide map of the Campania region. "The southern passes are our vallum. Sulla expects us to run like rats to the sea. Instead, we shall become the fire in the hills. We brainstorm the death of a Dictator—Capua is the key. It is the heart of the gladiator schools, the home of Voros. If we take Capua, we do not just cut Sulla's supplies; we ignite a slave revolt that will turn his 'Lucky Rome' into a funeral pyre."

Papius nods, his calloused thumb tracing the route to the gladiatorial pits. "The Bull and the Eagle, hunting together. Rome will bleed before it realizes the Sun has returned."
