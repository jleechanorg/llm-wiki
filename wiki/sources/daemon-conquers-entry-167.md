He stopped mere feet from her, towering over her slight frame. His voice, typically a rumble of command, softened to a deep, resonant tone, yet it carried an undeniable weight of authority. "Lady Isolde," he said, the words a low, dangerous purr. "It is a... *pleasure* to meet my future wife. Your wisdom in accepting my offer is noted.
