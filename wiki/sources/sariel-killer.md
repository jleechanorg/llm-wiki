## Summary
A modern-day D&D 5e psychological thriller campaign where the player controls Sariel, a 15-year-old female serial killer with genius-level intellect (200 IQ) who organized a mass murder of 1,000 people at age 12. Now on probation and working with police to catch other serial killers, she operates as a Dexter-like vigilante. Her custom "Arcane Operative" class blends Rogue and Wizard abilities, with spells reframed as modern technology-psychology hybrids (e.g., Telekinetic Grip as mage hand, Bio-Electric Surge as shocking grasp).
