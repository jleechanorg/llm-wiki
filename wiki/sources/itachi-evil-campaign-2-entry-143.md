Itachi Uchiha has accumulated enough Experience Points (**17370 XP**) to reach **Level 9**. This marks another significant advancement in his path towards ultimate control and power, bringing him closer to the apex of shinobi capabilities.

Here are the benefits Itachi gains from reaching Level 9:

*   **Increased Hit Points (HP):**
    *   Itachi rolls his Hit Die (d8) and adds his Health Modifier (+2).
    *   **Roll:** 6
    *   **HP Gain:** 6 (d8 roll) + 2 (Health Mod) = 8 HP
    *   **New Max HP:** Itachi's Maximum HP increases from 65 to **73 HP**. He is now at 73/73 HP.

*   **New Class Features:** As a Level 9 Ninja (Assassin Rogue + Extra Attack + Illusion Wizard), Itachi gains significant advancements:

    1.  **Advantage on Contested Checks to Resist Effects (Assassin Rogue Feature):** Itachi's training grants him enhanced resilience against various debilitating effects. He now gains **Advantage** on any contested check made to resist an effect that targets his **Coordination** (reflecting Dexterity saves) or **Intelligence** (reflecting Intelligence saves). This makes him exceptionally difficult to restrict or mentally influence by others.

    2.  **New Illusion Spells (5th Level):** Itachi's mastery of genjutsu deepens profoundly, unlocking illusions capable of altering perceptions on a grand scale or delving into the minds of others. He learns one of the following 5th-level Illusion spells:
        *   **Seeming:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You change the appearance of any number of creatures or objects you can see within 30 feet of you. You can make yourself and others look like other humanoids, or even objects, to a degree. The illusions are purely visual; they don't change physical form or properties. Lasts up to 8 hours. Any creature that physically interacts with an affected target can make a DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check to discern the illusion.
        *   **Mislead:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a perfect illusory duplicate of yourself that lasts for 1 hour. You can make the illusion move and speak as you desire, and you can perceive through its senses. While the illusion is active, you become invisible and can move up to 120 feet away from the illusion. If you move more than 120 feet away, or if you attack or cast a spell, the invisibility and the illusion end. This allows for complex feints and remote manipulation.
        *   **Dream:**
            *   **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP.
            *   **Effect:** You create a psychic link with a creature on the same plane of existence, sending it a dream. You can choose to appear as yourself or any persona, and control the dream's environment. You can deliver messages, torment the target (inflicting 3d6 Psychic damage on awakening if they fail a DC 15 Wisdom (Willpower) check, and preventing a long rest), or attempt to gain information. This spell is for long-range, deep psychological manipulation.

Please choose one of the 5th-level Illusion spells for Itachi to learn at Level 9:

*   **1. Seeming**
*   **2. Mislead**
*   **3. Dream**
