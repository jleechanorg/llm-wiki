Her attention then shifted to Lyra Umbrage (Lord Umbrage's daughter, Age 20s). Serpent Queen adjusted her persona, casting `Disguise Self` (1st-level spell slot used) to appear as a well-to-do, if slightly dowdy, merchant's wife, carrying a small basket as if on a shopping errand. This allowed her to move through the more affluent streets, blending into the background as she approached Lord Umbrage's imposing estate.
