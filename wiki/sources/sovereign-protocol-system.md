## Summary
Complete mechanics for the Multiversal Campaign tier used by StoryModeAgent after sovereign ascension. The PC becomes a Terminal Administrator (God-Tier Entity) commanding a portion of the multiverse within the Pan-Substrate.

## Key Claims
- **Terminal Administrator Role** — PC commands portion of multiverse as God-Tier Entity after ascension
- **Pan-Substrate** — Infinite competitive landscape with 1,000 ranked Sovereigns, each possessing unique Logic
- **Ultimate Goal** — Entropy Reversal: rewrite thermodynamic laws across all realities
- **Substrate Points (SP)** — Currency AND health; 0 SP means Sovereign erasure
- **New Sovereign Start** — Rank 801-1000 (Administrator), 1 SP, Aggro 8 (Lethal)

## Key Quotes
> "Logic Sieges" — Conflict resolved through unique metaphysical law clashes

> "Entropy Reversal" — Rewrite thermodynamic laws across all realities to match PC's vision

## Core Systems

### God Power (GP)
Derived from highest ability modifier. Used for all Sovereign checks.

### Substrate Points (SP)
- Currency AND Health — at 0 SP, Sovereign is erased
- Territory — 1 SP = 1,000 Universes
- Starting — 1 SP for new Sovereigns

### Aggro Meter (0-10)
| Level | Status | Effects |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 0-3 | Dormant | Stealth possible |
| 4-6 | Noticed | Moderate pressure |
| 7-8 | Targeted | Regular Logic Sieges |
| 9 | Hunted | Emperor attention |
| 10 | Purge | Coordinated elimination |

New Sovereigns start at **Aggro 8 (Lethal)**.

## Logic Siege (Combat)
**Formula:** `d20 + GP + SP Expenditure vs Target Defense`

| Factor | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Logic Advantage (opposed) | +5 |
| Logic Disadvantage (similar) | -5 |
| Sub-Deity Support (War) | +GP |
| Defending Home | +3 |
| Hostile Territory | -3 |

**Success:** Target loses SP, attacker claims universes
**Failure:** Attacker loses SP (Logic Rot)

## Additional Systems

### Peer Handshake
Integrate "Mirrors" (PC variants from other timelines).
- Resolution: d20 + GP vs DC 15+
- Success: +10% SP, unlock Portfolio/Skill
- Failure: +1 Aggro, Mirror becomes hostile

### Sub-Deity Portfolios
| Portfolio | Actions |
|-----------|---------|
| War | Logic Siege, Conquest |
| Logistics | SP Transfer, Efficiency |
| Culture | Conversion, Morale |
| Research | Logic Analysis, Innovation |
| Diplomacy | Treaties, Tribute |
| Intelligence | Spy Network, Counter-Intel |

### Rival System
Primary Rival: Similar rank, opposing Logic, personal connection.
- Rival Agenda Clock (4 segments): Advances when PC acts passively
- At 4/4: Rival strikes PC asset
