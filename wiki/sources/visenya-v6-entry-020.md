## Summary
The endgame is a "Total Structural Failure" — five systemic pressures converge simultaneously: the Obsession War between Robb and Jon, the Null-Pulse suppressing Visenya's powers, the Iron Bank's Liquidator erasing her spy network, Roose Bolton's secret betrayal, and the Others phasing through Winterfell's walls. The "Apex Weaver" is besieged by variables she cannot solve with raw heat alone.

## The Five Crises

### 1. The Obsession War (Robb vs. Jon)
- **Mechanic**: "Jealousy Spikes" — Robb and Jon compete for the 'Sun'
- If Visenya favors one, the other autonomously "deletes" a variable the other values
- Robb and Jon must be managed to prevent total collapse of her Northern command

### 2. The Null-Pulse (Archmaester Marwyn)
- **Mechanic**: Within 100 feet of the Void-Sphere, Visenya loses all Baelarys traits
- **Effect**: No Static, no CHA bonuses, no Silver Tongue floor — she is a "physically fragile 14-year-old girl in a room full of people who remember what you did to the Cerwyns"

### 3. The Iron Bank's Audit (The Liquidator)
- **Mechanic**: Every turn, 10% of the Weaver spy network vanishes; treasury accounts frozen
- **Effect**: "Information Geometry" map shows "Dead Zones" — she can no longer perceive stress lines or pulse rates

### 4. The Bolton Inversion (Roose Bolton)
- **Mechanic**: Roose has begun secret correspondence with Tywin Lannister and the Night King
- **Effect**: He will attempt to "peel" Visenya's Shadow Knights away by offering them a way to "protect" her that involves imprisonment

### 5. The Cold Singularity (The Others)
- **Mechanic**: Wights are phasing *inside* the castle walls, drawn to the heat-wells created by the wildfire
- **Effect**: The Others are no longer "marching"; they are "Phasing" — an enemy with no geometry she can map
