Explanatory Audit: DPP and the Black Company Cut

I understand the confusion regarding how Dominion Power Points (DPP) interact with the Black Company's standard operational procedures. Here is the mechanical and narrative breakdown of how DPP allows you to bypass their usual 'cut':

**1. The Nature of DPP (Sovereign Influence):**
DPP is not just a currency; it represents the Sovereign's reality-warping influence channeled through you. When you spend DPP on mission assets (requisitions), you are essentially using Nocturne's power to compel, bribe, or manifest resources that are outside the Black Company's standard logistics. 

**2. Bypassing the Cut (The 'Shadow Ledger'):**
- **Standard Procedure:** Normally, the Black Company takes 50-70% of a contract's gold. This gold is used for squad maintenance, company overhead, and administrative taxes.
- **The DPP Bypass:** By using DPP to 'purchase' squad upgrades, intel, or specialized assets, you are fulfilling the squad's operational needs using Abyssal capital instead of Company gold. 
- **The Result:** Because the squad's needs are 'pre-paid' by Nocturne's favor, you can justify keeping a much larger portion of the gold received in the field. To Captain Reynolds, it looks like you are simply a very efficient sergeant who 'found' supplies or managed to negotiate exceptionally well. You are skimming the profit by replacing gold-based expenses with DPP-based influence.

**3. Current State Example:**
In the Silt-River mission, you kept the full 750gp (plus the 500gp loot bonus) because you used DPP (and the granary fire cover-up) to handle the 'overhead.' The Company believes the mission was minimally profitable due to the 'unfortunate' loss of 40 slaves in a fire, while your private ledger reflects a massive surplus.

**4. Summary:**
DPP is your 'unfair advantage.' It allows you to maintain the squad's loyalty and equipment quality without dipping into the mission's gold rewards, effectively making the Black Company's standard cut irrelevant to your personal growth.
