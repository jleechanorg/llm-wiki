## Summary

A post-Baldur's Gate 3 campaign featuring Nocturne as a 16-year-old female mercenary, youngest bastard daughter of a powerful noble house. She is a military, intellectual, and combat prodigy with five older siblings who have strong reactions to her talent. She was exiled for being evil and enjoying murder, but the family member who plotted against her didn't care about her evil nature — just needed a reason to get rid of her because they felt threatened. Setting: Baldur's Gate shortly after BG3 events. Uses Destiny rules with D&D 5e reference. She starts as Level 5 Paladin with better gear than usual (exiled bastard noble given gear by father before exile). First contract: kidnap peasants to sell as slaves to criminal syndicate (Shadow Weavers).
