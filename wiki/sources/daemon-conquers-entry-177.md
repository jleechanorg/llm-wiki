After the feast, Daemon (Level 7, Age 30), sated by the excellent meal, dismissed the Blackwood household with a curt gesture, retaining only Lady Isolde (Age: ~22) and Kael (Level 6, Age 25). He led them to a small, austere study, dominated by a large, well-worn map.

"The meal was... satisfactory," Daemon rumbled, his eyes, sharp and calculating, fixing first on Isolde, then on Kael. "Now, to business. Lady Isolde, your lands are rich. Their produce will feed my growing army. Kael informs me of the general situation. What, specifically, do I need to know about the ingrained habits of your retainers, and how best to ensure their... *enthusiastic* contribution to my coffers and granaries? And this Lord Valerick," he added, his voice hardening, "how do we ensure he understands his place?"

Isolde straightened, her earlier relief replaced by a keen focus. "My Lord, the reeves are competent, but their loyalties are old. They manage vast yields, but their methods are slow, and they've always held back a portion for their own benefit. A visible, firm presence will be needed to ensure full compliance. As for Valerick, he is prideful and resentful. A single, decisive display of your might, even at a distance, would cow him into submission. He seeks no true conflict, only petty gains."

Kael stepped forward, indicating points on the map. "My Lord, I propose sending a small detachment of our Level 3 recruits, perhaps twenty, under Sergeant Volkov's command, to oversee the Blackwood reeves. Their presence alone will instill discipline. For Valerick, a stern missive demanding fealty and a symbolic tribute, delivered by Gareth and Ragnar, reinforced by a patrol of your veteran GWMs near his borders for a few days, should suffice. It would send a clear message without necessitating a full campaign."

Daemon listened, a grim nod acknowledging their combined assessments. The pieces were falling into place.
