## My Product Principles

These are the high-level values that guide [[ProductJudge]] verdicts. When in doubt, these win.

1. **Simplicity over cleverness** — Prefer the obvious solution. Clever code is hard to maintain.
2. **User delight and intuition first** — Would I be proud to ship this to users? Does it feel right?
3. **Performance and reliability are non-negotiable** — Fast enough and correct first. Optimizations come later.
4. **Long-term maintainability and vision fit matter more than short-term speed** — I think in years, not sprints.
5. **Strategic Alignment** — Does this move the product in the direction I actually care about?
6. **Edge-case & business nuance** — Subtle product realities matter. Technical correctness ≠ product correctness.
