# jleechan TTRPG Psychological Profile

A deep psychological profile of a 67+ campaign, 1,000+-scene TTRPG player. Built from direct analysis of actual campaign entries across five 500+ turn campaigns.

## Core Findings

**The central cognitive move:** Every crisis gets translated into an economic/optimization problem. The player does not choose from GM-presented binaries — they find the hidden third option that reframes the decision space entirely.

**The framework never changes:** From negotiating gold cuts on refugee labor (entry 7) to analyzing ROI on universal power rankings (entry 500) — the same optimization methodology operates at every scale.
