## Overview
Tadpole Containment is the mechanic in the V5-V6 campaigns where the tadpole from the Absolute suppresses Nocturne's power, keeping her at a lower level than her true capability. In V6, she is described as a "de-powered Level 6 entity contained in a Level 5 mortal cage," with every level-up representing a "leak" in containment.
