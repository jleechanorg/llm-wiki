## Overview
Siren's Call is the overarching thematic framework across multiple Nocturne campaigns, spanning the BG3, Tyranny, and Star Wars settings. It encompasses the use of social dominance, seduction, and manipulation as primary weapons — the "siren archetype" where beauty and charisma are weaponized against those sexually or romantically drawn to the protagonist.

## Key Details
- **Category:** Character Archetype / Theme
- **Campaign(s):** [[nocturne-bg3-v4]], [[nocturne-bg3-v5]], [[nocturne-old-republic]], [[alexiel-swtor-campaign]]

## Key Mechanic: Siren's Leverage
The "Siren's Leverage" trait provides a +2 bonus to Charisma checks against humanoids sexually or romantically attracted to females. This mechanic appears across BG3 Nocturne campaigns and represents the systematic exploitation of attraction as a tactical advantage.

## Siren Archetypes Across Campaigns
- **Nocturne BG3 V4:** College of Perdition Bard, brothel survivor turned manipulator
- **Nocturne BG3 V5:** Shadow Monarch, criminal empire through terror and debt
- **Nocturne Old Republic:** Sith Prodigy (Siren) of House Vitiate — masks "Vaylin" nature with Siren poise
- **Alexiel SW:TOR:** Subject 7, porcelain doll weapon navigating Jedi Academy
