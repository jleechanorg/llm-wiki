The Roman Empire Setting is the 40 BCE low-magic historical fantasy setting of ancient Rome that serves as the backdrop for the Aurelius Caesar campaigns. This setting is characterized by minimal magic, political intrigue, and the military might of the Roman Republic. The timeline places the campaign before the transition from Republic to Empire, capturing Rome at a pivotal moment in history. Gladiatorial combat, political maneuvering, and military campaigns define this setting. The low-magic nature makes martial prowess and political cunning more valuable than arcane power.
