## Definition
Encounter difficulty scaling system for high-magic campaigns where players have access to powerful magical items and spells.

## Tiers by Level
- **T1 (Levels 1-4)**: 3-4 encounters/day
- **T2 (Levels 5-10)**: 5-7 encounters + resource pressure
- **T3 (Levels 11-16)**: Elite groups + counter-buffs
- **T4 (Levels 17-20)**: Set-pieces + artifact-level enemies

## Key Principles
- Increase encounter frequency to pressure resources
- Use elite enemies and counter-magic creatures
- Include environmental challenges and time pressure
- Track spell slots per cast
- Consider exhaustion for forced marches
