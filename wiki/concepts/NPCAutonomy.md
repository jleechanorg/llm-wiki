## Description
Design principle where NPCs operate as independent agents with their own goals, hidden agendas, loyalty hierarchies, and breaking points - they do NOT simply follow player commands.

## Key Aspects
- **Independent Goals**: NPCs pursue objectives unrelated to player success
- **Hidden Agendas**: Secret motivations not visible to player
- **Loyalty Hierarchies**: Competing loyalties that may conflict with player interests
- **Breaking Points**: Inviolable limits beyond which NPC will not comply
- **Refusal/Betrayal**: NPCs may reject requests or act against player
