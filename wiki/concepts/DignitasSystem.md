The Dignitas System is the honor mechanics framework for the v3 campaign, representing the Roman concept of personal dignity and honor through three metrics: Auctoritas (authority/influence), Imperium (command power), and Gratia (favor/charisma). This system tracks Aurelius's standing in Roman society and his ability to influence events. Maintaining dignitas is essential for a Roman noble, and losing it brings shame. The three components must be balanced—excessive focus on one can damage the others. This mechanic drives the political gameplay in Aurelius's merchant cover identity.
