## Overview
The practice of customizing AI-generated content based on user-specific data such as campaign settings, character names, and narrative context. Ensures AI stories feel unique to each user's campaign rather than using generic templates.

## Key Principles
- Use user-provided campaign data for all story generation
- Avoid hardcoded character names or default content
- Include campaign-specific setting and description in prompts
