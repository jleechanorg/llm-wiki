## Definition
Game events requiring player response, generated when switching between campaign modes or when neglect thresholds are exceeded.

## Urgency Levels
- **LOW**: Can be safely ignored
- **MEDIUM**: Should address soon
- **HIGH**: Needs attention this session
- **CRITICAL**: Interrupts adventure immediately

## Trigger Categories

- [[Siege]] — base/settlement under attack
