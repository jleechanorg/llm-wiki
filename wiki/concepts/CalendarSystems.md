## Definition
Multiple calendar systems supported by the World of Assiah world time utilities.

## Supported Calendars
- **Forgotten Realms** (12 months): Hammer, Alturiak, Ches, Tarsakh, Mirtul, Kythorn, Flamerule, Eleasis, Eleint, Marpenoth, Uktar, Nightal
- **Grayhawk** (12 months): Zarantyr, Olarune, Therendor, Eyre, Dravago, Nymm, Lharvion, Barrakas, Rhaan, Sypheros, Aryth, Vult
- **Winterbolt** (12 months): Afteryule, Solmath, Rethe, Astron, Thrimidge, Forelithe, Afterlithe, Wedmath, Halimath, Winterfilth, Blotmath, Foreyule
- **Gregorian** (standard): January–December + abbreviations

## Normalization
All calendars normalized to 1-12 integers for temporal comparison. The LLM decides which calendar to use when displaying time in narrative context.
