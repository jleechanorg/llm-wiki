The process of extracting and formatting debug metadata from story entries. Includes background events, faction updates, rumors, scene events, complications, time-sensitive events, and NPC status changes.

## Event Types
- **Background Events**: Living world updates with actor, action, event_type, and status
- **Faction Updates**: Current objectives and progress for each faction
- **World Events**: Nested structure containing additional event categories

## Formatting
Events are formatted with emoji indicators: ⏳ (pending), ✅ (resolved), 🔍 (in-progress).
