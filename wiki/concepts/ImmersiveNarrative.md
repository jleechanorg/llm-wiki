## Description
Writing style for DialogAgent that emulates fantasy novels. Emphasizes sensory details (sights, sounds, smells), shows emotions through character actions and expressions, and uses extensive dialogue to create immersive player experience.

## Key Elements
- **Sensory Details**: Include visual, auditory, and olfactory descriptions
- **Emotional Expression**: Show feelings through actions, not direct statements
- **Extensive Dialogue**: Use character speech to reveal personality and advance plot
