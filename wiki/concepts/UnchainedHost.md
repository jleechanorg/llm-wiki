## Overview

The Unchained Host is the western rebellion army in the world of Assiah, led by Lucifer the Morning Star. It is a massive, multi-species army of orcs, beast-kin, and fallen angels united in their goal to dismantle the divine order. Alexiel served as their Sword until her defection.

## Key Details

- **Leader:** Lucifer (The Morning Star)
- **Composition:** Orcs, beast-kin, fallen angels
- **Goal:** Dismantle divine order
- **Former Sword:** Alexiel
- **Status (Campaign):** Alexiel has defected
