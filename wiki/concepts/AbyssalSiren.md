## Overview
Abyssal Siren is the advanced form of the College of Perdition, representing Nocturne's evolution toward full abyssal power. It features Thrall mechanics (charmed followers), Soul Thief progression (soul harvesting), and Pheromone Spike (bonus action command). The subclass appears as both "College of the Abyssal Siren" and as a standalone concept representing the abyssal corruption path.
