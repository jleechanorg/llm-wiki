Numeric attribute measuring a player's influence over their campaign universe. Threshold: 70 points triggers multiverse (sovereign) upgrade eligibility. Represents mastery of cosmic-scale narrative elements.

Complementary to [[DivinePotential]] which governs the divine tier transition.
