Large-scale empirical study of coding agent adoption on GitHub. 22-28% adoption rate among 128K projects as of January 2026 — "very high for a technology only a few months old."

## Key Numbers

- **128,018 projects** analyzed
- **22.20%–28.66%** adoption rate
- **48,563 agent commits** identified
- Agent commits focus on **features and bug fixes**

## Key Findings

1. Broad adoption across project maturity spectrum
2. Diverse languages and topics
3. Agent-assisted commits are larger than non-agent
4. Agents leave explicit co-author traces
