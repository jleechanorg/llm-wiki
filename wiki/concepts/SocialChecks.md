## Definition
Skill checks for interacting with NPCs — persuasion, deception, intimidation. Must consider NPC personality, relationship with player, and plausibility of the request. Some actions may be impossible via skill alone regardless of roll.

## Key Considerations
- **NPC Personality**: An NPC's personality affects what arguments work
- **Relationship**: Current standing with NPC influences likelihood of success
- **Plausibility**: Some requests are impossible regardless of roll (e.g., asking a king to give up their crown)
- **Social Norms**: Consider in-world social conventions and context
