## Definition
Strategic combat system for battles involving 20+ forces (armies, warbands, large monster groups). Uses unit blocks, daily upkeep costs, and morale system.

## Unit Block Structure
- 10 soldiers per block
- Infantry = 10gp/day upkeep
- Cavalry = 25-40gp/day upkeep
- War Mages = 100gp/day upkeep per block

## Combat Round Sequence
1. Initiative
2. Ranged attacks
3. Charge attacks
4. Melee combat
5. Morale Check

## Key Rules
- Forces 20+ use strategic mass combat
- Morale system affects unit effectiveness
- Daily resource tracking required
