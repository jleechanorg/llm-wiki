## Overview

The Nullification Field is Alexiel Arcanus's signature power in the [[Sariel V2 Campaign]]. It is a projected field that nullifies magical and demonic phenomena, effectively canceling supernatural effects within its radius. Alexiel was a hidden demigod whose greatest secret was this power—her public disdain for Nulls was actually the perfect cover for her terrifying hidden nature.

## The Fatal Overload

At the [[Battle of the Sacrifice Fields]], Mordan exploited Alexiel's absolute moral code (protecting children) by taking hundreds hostage. Facing an impossible choice, Alexiel overloaded her Nullification Field to stop the demonic ritual and save the children, sacrificing her own life in the process.

## Significance to Sariel

[[Sariel Arcanus]] seeks to understand whether she might inherit or acquire this power, asking Kantos: "Does nullification need to be done on babies or could I do it and possess magic and null power?"

This ability is the reason she was designed as the ultimate weapon against the Celestial Imperium.
