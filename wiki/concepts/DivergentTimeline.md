The Divergent Timeline is the alternative history setting for the v3 campaign, representing a Rome that developed differently from our historical timeline. This divergence creates unique opportunities and challenges for Aurelius as he operates within a familiar yet changed world. The exact nature of the divergence is not specified, but it allows for historical flexibility—the Spartacus revolt may have occurred differently, different political figures may hold power, and the available resources and factions differ from the "canonical" historical record. This concept permits historical fantasy gameplay within a modified Roman setting.
