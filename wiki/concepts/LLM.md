A Large Language Model (LLM) is an AI model trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human language. WorldAI supports multiple LLM providers to give users flexibility in choosing models for their tabletop RPG sessions.

## Key Concepts
- **Model Selection** — Users can choose from different providers and models
- **Context Window** — Maximum tokens the model can process (varies by model)
- **Tool Calling** — Ability to call functions/APIs during generation
- **Structured Output** — Ability to output JSON/structured data reliably

## In WorldAI Context
- Different LLMs suited for narrative-heavy RPG sessions
- Model choice affects response quality, speed, and cost
- Some models better at structured output (Gemini)
- Some offer larger context for long campaigns
