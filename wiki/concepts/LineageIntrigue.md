Lineage track level (0-5) in WorldAI Faction Management that provides intrigue bonuses to intel operations.

## Bonuses
- Reduces detection risk by lineage_intrigue × 3%
- Increases spy strength by lineage_intrigue × 15%

Part of [[IntelOperationsFactionManagement]] formula calculations.
