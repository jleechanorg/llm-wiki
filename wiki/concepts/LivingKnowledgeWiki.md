## Definition

A self-updating, multi-perspective encyclopedia maintained by dedicated agent swarms. Every entry is: continuously updated, backed by debate traces and confidence scores, available in multiple difficulty levels, and proactively expanded when gaps are detected. Project Chimera's killer application.

## Properties

- **Continuously Updated** — Agent swarms maintain and refresh content
- **Debate Traces** — Each entry backed by agent disagreement and resolution logs
- **Confidence Scores** — Quality signals attached to every claim
- **Multiple Difficulty Levels** — Same content available at beginner through expert
- **Proactive Gap Detection** — System identifies missing coverage and dispatches agents
