## Definition
Plan Quality determines the depth and accuracy of the strategic analysis output based on the margin between the roll and the Difficulty Class (DC).

## Success Quality (roll >= DC)

| Margin (roll - DC) | Quality | Effect |
|---------------------|---------|--------|
| Meet to +4 | Competent | Standard analysis, most options covered, reasonable accuracy |
| +5 to +9 | Sharp | Thorough analysis, spot non-obvious options, accurate risk assessment |
| +10 to +14 | Brilliant | Exceptional insight, creative options, anticipate complications |
| +15+ | Masterful | Perfect clarity, optimal strategies, foresee consequences others miss |

## Failure Quality (roll < DC)

| Margin (DC - roll) | Quality | Effect |
|---------------------|---------|--------|
| 1-4 | Incomplete | Miss 1-2 good options, underestimate some risks, analysis has gaps |
| 5-9 | Muddled | Miss obvious options, overlook key risks, 1-2 flawed choices |
| 10+ | Confused | Completely miss the point, wrong assumptions, dangerous misconceptions |

## Note on Margin
Margin is signed: `roll - DC`. Positive means success, negative means failure.
