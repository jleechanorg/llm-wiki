## Description
System ensuring NPCs remember and respond to past interactions with the player.

## What NPCs Remember

### First Impressions
- Modify all future interactions
- Can be overcome but requires significant positive actions

### Promises Made
- Track for betrayal detection
- Broken promises trigger [[Relationship Update Triggers]]
- Kept promises grant trust bonuses

### Favors Owed
- Track both directions (player owes NPC, NPC owes player)
- Outstanding debts affect disposition
- Paying off debts improves relationship

### Insults and Slights
- Even minor ones are remembered
- Accumulate toward disposition changes
- Can be resolved through in-game actions

### Treatment of Friends/Faction
- How player treated NPC's allies affects disposition
- Foundation for [[Cascading Relationships]]

## Prohibited Actions
- NEVER have an NPC forget a significant interaction
- NEVER reset relationships without narrative justification
- NEVER ignore grievances in future encounters

## Usage
Referenced during [[Relationship Check Triggers]] to provide context for NPC behavior. Updates via [[Relationship Update Triggers]].
