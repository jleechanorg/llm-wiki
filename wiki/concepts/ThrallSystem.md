## Overview
The Thrall System is a companion mechanic in the Nocturne BG3 V7 campaign. It represents the ability to bind converted souls as inspired followers, with a maximum of 3 thrall slots.

## Mechanics
- **Slot Limit:** 3 thralls maximum at Level 5
- **Thrall Types:** Can include various creature types
- **Inspiration Economy:** Thralls generate inspiration points

## Context
The thrall system integrates with the Divine Scion's conversion abilities and represents Nocturne's growing power base as she builds her cult and influence.
