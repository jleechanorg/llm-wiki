## Definition

A self-improvement mechanism where three elements co-evolve: agent roles, communication patterns, and graph architecture. Agents can propose changes to any of these; successful proposals are sandbox-tested and promoted automatically, creating a feedback loop that can lead to genuine emergent capabilities.

## The Three Co-Evolving Elements

1. **New Agent Roles** — Agents propose entirely new specializations
2. **New Communication Patterns** — Agents suggest alternative ways of interacting (debate, handoff, blackboard)
3. **Graph Architecture Changes** — The GNN topology itself evolves based on what works
