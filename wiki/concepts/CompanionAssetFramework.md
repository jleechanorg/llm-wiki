## Overview
The Companion Asset Framework is a mechanic in the V6 campaign that frames BG3 companions (Astarion, Wyll, Gale, Karlach, Lae'zel, Shadowheart) as "Assets" with Approval tracking rather than equal party members. This reflects Nocturne's manipulation-focused playstyle where companions are tools to be leveraged rather than allies.
