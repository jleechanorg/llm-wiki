## Summary
Improvements to form input controls including glass morphism backgrounds, focus animations with scale and glow effects, and improved visual feedback.

## Key Features
- **Focus Scale**: `transform: scale(1.02)` on focus
- **Accent Glow**: Box-shadow with accent RGB values
- **Background**: Glass morphism effect
- **Border**: Accent color on focus
