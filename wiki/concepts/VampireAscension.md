Vampire Ascension is the powered-up vampire state that Astarion achieved, transforming from a spawn vampire into a true vampire. This ascension process grants immense power including enhanced physical attributes, supernatural abilities, and immunity to many vulnerabilities that plague lesser vampires. However, ascension also brings new challenges—the eternal hunger becomes more pronounced, and the character must adapt to an immortal existence. In the post-BG3 setting, Ascended vampires represent the pinnacle of vampiric development.
