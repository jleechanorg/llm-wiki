## Definition
Derived from highest ability modifier. Used for all Sovereign checks including Logic Siege combat, Peer Handshake resolution, and Sub-Deity portfolio actions.

## Usage
- **Logic Siege:** d20 + GP + SP Expenditure
- **Peer Handshake:** d20 + GP vs DC 15
- **Sub-Deity Actions:** War portfolio adds GP to Logic Siege

## Derivation
GP = highest ability modifier from character creation
