## Overview

Aizen Thay V1 is a dark fantasy noir campaign set in Thay during the "Century of Silence." The player controls Aizen Vane, the Abyss Knight who is a Genetic Echo of Nocturne Sosuke. The campaign features soul harvesting mechanics, Red Wizard sorcery, and operations from the Obsidian Ziggurat of Bezantur.

## Key Settings

- **Time Period:** Century of Silence (Thay's dark age)
- **Location:** Obsidian Ziggurat of Bezantur
- **Atmosphere:** Metallic tang of slave-pits, ozone of Red Wizard sorcery

## Game Mechanics

- **Character Class:** Abyss Knight
- **Level:** 5
- **Codex:** Codex of the Sovereign (defines mechanics)
- **Abilities:** Soul Thief class abilities
- **Equipment:** Obsidian Mirror (connects to Shattered Rose)
- **Mission:** Tithe of Silt-River (40 fresh souls)

## Key Themes

- **Soul Harvesting:** Tithe system for the Sovereign
- **Dark Fantasy:** No heroic morality
- **Genetic Echo:** Nocturne Sosuke's legacy
- **Black Company:** Mercenary soul-harvest operations
