## Overview
The Celestial Wars were the divine rebellion led by [[Lucifer]] (Morning Star) that shattered the cosmic order, creating the current multi-polar world of Assiah.

## Key Facts
- Leader: [[Lucifer]]
- Outcome: Shattered cosmic order, created multi-polar world
- Result: [[CelestialImperium]] vs [[ShatteredHost]] conflict
- Ended by: [[TreatyOfNexus]]

## Historical Context
- Led to creation of [[SevenApostates]]
- Resulted in current faction landscape
- Post-war period: Year 11 of the New Peace
