Entropy management is the fourth layer of harness engineering, addressing the natural degradation of harnesses over time: documentation drifts from code, constraints get bypassed, prompts stop working as models update.

## Planned Entropy Management Systems

### Self-Improving Prompts (ORCH-04k)
Log which prompts succeed vs fail, build project-specific prompt library

### Autonomous PR Review (ORCH-apr)
OpenClaw reviews PRs using memory, CLAUDE.md rules, and historical patterns before human sees them

### Convergence Intelligence (ORCH-cil)
Learning, anomaly detection, escalation tier tuning based on historical success rates
