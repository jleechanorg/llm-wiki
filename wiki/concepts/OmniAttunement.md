## Definition
Omni-Attunement is a game mechanic in the Nocturne BG3 Continued campaign that allows a character to attune to more magical items than normally permitted. While standard D&D 5e limits attunement to 3 items, this mechanic breaks that limit, allowing the Shadow Monarch to have 5 items attuned simultaneously. This represents extraordinary magical capacity and is a key power source for the character.

## How It Appears in the Campaigns
- [[nocturne-bg3-continued-campaign]]: The Shadow Monarch has Omni-Attunement (Active), with 5 items currently attuned:
  1. Canaith Mandolin
  2. Cloak of Displacement
  3. Ring of Protection
  4. Boots of the Zephyr
  5. Drum of the Dark Heart (+2)

## Mechanics
- **Standard Limit:** 3 items (standard D&D 5e)
- **Omni-Attunement Limit:** 5 items (limit broken)
- **Active Effect:** Allows channeling of multiple magical items simultaneously
- **Synergy:** Works with The Hunger mechanic to enhance overall power
