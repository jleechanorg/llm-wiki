## Definition
The practice of measuring and analyzing service operation timing to identify bottlenecks, slowest operations, and performance trends over time.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Duration**: Sum of all operation durations
- **Average Duration**: Mean operation time per service
- **Slowest Operations**: Top 5 longest operations
- **Fastest Operations**: Top 5 shortest operations
- **Error Rate**: Percentage of operations that failed
