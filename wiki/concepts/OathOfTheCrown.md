Oath of the Crown is a Paladin oath focused on rule through protection, representing Arthur Dayne's commitment to defending the realm and its ruler. This oath emphasizes the responsibility of leadership to protect those under one's care, making the paladin a defender of the crown above all other concerns. Arthur Dayne's dedication to the Kingsguard and his service to the royal family exemplify this oath's principles. The oath requires the paladin to support the legitimate authority and maintain the stability of the realm.
