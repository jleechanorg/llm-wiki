## Description

Great Weapon Master is a D&D 5e feat that enhances the damage potential of two-handed weapons. It allows a character to make an additional attack as a bonus action after a critical hit or killing a creature, and allows them to take a penalty to attack rolls to gain a bonus to damage.

## In This Campaign

Gladius possesses the Great Weapon Master feat, enhancing his already formidable combat potential. Combined with his STR 18 (+4), this makes him an extremely dangerous melee combatant.

## Mechanical Benefits

- When you score a critical hit or reduce a creature to 0 HP, you can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action
- Before you make a melee weapon attack with a heavy weapon, you can choose to take a -5 penalty to the attack roll; if you do so, the attack deals +10 damage

## Significance

Great Weapon Master fits Gladius's "The Hammer" identity — he's not subtle in combat but devastatingly effective. The feat maximizes his damage output, making him the kind of mercenary who finishes jobs quickly and brutal.
