## Overview
Unit upkeep is the recurring cost in gold pieces (gp) that factions pay to maintain military forces. In WorldAI, this follows D&D 5e hireling wage standards.

## Upkeep Rates
| Unit Type | Cost per Week | Rationale |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Soldier | 0.5gp | Conscript/regular hireling pay |
| Spy | 1gp | Specialist/expert pay |
| Elite | 5gp | Elite unit/special forces pay |

## Gameplay Significance
- Factors into faction resource management
- Influences strategic decisions in [[ThinkMode]]
- Enables balance between military strength and economic sustainability
