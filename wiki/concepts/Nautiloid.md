## Summary
The Nautiloid is a mind flayer vessel — a colossal organic ship crewed and controlled by illithids (mind flayers). These ships are living creatures themselves, grown rather than built, and serve as both transportation and mobile bases for the illithid hive mind.

## Key Details
- **Type:** Living organic ship
- **Species:** Created and crewed by Mind Flayers (Illithids)
- **Size:** Massive, capable of transporting dozens of captives

## Key Characteristics
- **Organic construction:** Grown rather than built, like a massive sea creature
- **Illithid control:** Commanded by the hive-mind of mind flayers
- **Captive storage:** Designed to hold prisoners (like the tadpole-infected victims in BG3)
- **Self-propelled:** Moves through planar space

## Campaign Relevance
The bg3-astarion campaign opens on the Nautiloid where Astarion and other potential companions (Shadowheart, Lae'zel, Gale, Wyll, Karlach, Halsin) are held as captives with illithid tadpoles. The escape from this vessel forms the opening sequence and establishes the immediate threat (tadpole ceremorphosis) that drives the party's cooperation.
