## Description
D&D 5e measure of a character's ability to survive damage. Determined by class hit die plus Constitution modifier.

## HP Calculation at Level-Up
When a character gains a level:
1. **Rolled Method**: Roll the class's hit die (d6, d8, d10, or d12) and add Constitution modifier
2. **Fixed Method**: Take the average of the hit die (rounded up) plus Constitution modifier

## Class Hit Dice
- Barbarian: d12
- Fighter, Paladin, Ranger: d10
- Cleric, Druid: d8
- Bard, Monk, Rogue, Warlock: d6
- Wizard: d6 (at first level), d4 thereafter
