## Summary
Legal rights that give creators exclusive control over their original creative works, including copyrights, trademarks, patents, and trade secrets. In service terms, the provider typically owns or licenses all IP.

## Key Types
- **Copyrights**: Original works (source code, databases, functionality, software, designs, text, audio, video, photos, graphics)
- **Trademarks**: Brand identifiers (service marks, logos)
- **Trade Secrets**: Confidential business information
