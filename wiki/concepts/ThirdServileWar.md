## Overview

The Third Servile War (73-71 BCE), also known as the Spartacus Rebellion, was a major uprising of enslaved people against the Roman Republic. It serves as the historical setting for the Gaia Julia V4 and V5 campaigns.

## Historical Context

The war began in 73 BCE when a group of gladiators escaped from the Ludus Magnus (gladiatorial training school) in Capua. Led by Spartacus (a Thracian former soldier), the rebel army grew to include tens of thousands of escaped slaves. The rebellion posed a significant threat to the Roman Republic, requiring multiple armies to eventually suppress it.

## In Gaia Julia V4

In Gaia Julia V4 ("Atrium of rebel headquarters"), the Third Servile War serves as the backdrop. Key elements:
- **Time period:** 73 BCE
- **Character:** Gaia Julia Caesar, daughter of the God-Emperor (alternate history where Julius Caesar already rules as God-Emperor)
- **Cover identity:** "Livia of Capua," a Level 1 captive
- **Actual power:** Level 12 Siren (Bard/Paladin Gestalt) playing a long deception

The campaign features:
- **Spartacus:** "stoic, burdened by his own honor"
- **Crixus:** "blood-drenched, hates Rome," representing brutal side of rebellion
- **Lotus Ten:** fanatical cult forming a "living wall of flesh" around Gaia

## In Gaia Julia V5

Gaia Julia V5 presents the aftermath - Gaia stripped of her divine right and "cast into the filth of the Third Servile War." The pleasure tents in rebel-held countryside represent how far she has fallen from her position as imperial heir.
