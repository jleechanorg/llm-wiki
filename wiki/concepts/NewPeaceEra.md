## Overview

The New Peace Era is the current calendar and political era in the World of Assiah, established after Alexiel Arcanus ended the Host Wars through the Starfall Gambit. The campaign begins in Year 11 of the New Peace, on Kythorn Day 2—the day after Alexiel's death at the Battle of the Sacrifice Fields.

## Political Context

The New Peace is characterized by:
- The Imperium's dominance under a Triumvirate leadership
- Alexiel's martyrdom cementing her legend
- Political instability as factions seek to exploit the power vacuum
- The court's "viper's nest" of factions maneuvering for position

## Calendar

The campaign uses the New Peace calendar rather than the Imperial Calendar (which had a 40-year discrepancy with the lore documents). Key dates:
- **Year 11, Month of Kythorn, Day 2:** Campaign start—the day after Alexiel's sacrifice
- The original lore (Year 1580-1620) was retconned in favor of this immediate aftermath timeline

## Significance

The New Peace Era represents both the stability Alexiel's sacrifice brought and the instability her death now creates. The Imperium was held together by her presence and power; now Titus val Raziel struggles to maintain order as factions move to fill the void.
