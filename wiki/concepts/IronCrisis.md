## Summary
The Iron Crisis was a major economic collapse in the Forgotten Realms setting, centered around the Nashkel mines. It created widespread economic disruption on the Sword Coast, contributing to tensions between Baldur's Gate and Amn, and serves as the backdrop for the bg1-nocturne campaign.

## Key Details
- **Origin:** Economic collapse stemming from the Nashkel mines
- **Impact:** Widespread economic disruption across the Sword Coast
- **Time Period:** 1368 DR (around the events of Baldur's Gate 1)

## Key Consequences
- **Trade disruption:** Major merchant companies affected
- **Political tension:** Fuels rivalries between Baldur's Gate and Amn
- **Instability:** Creates opportunities for mercenary organizations like the Flaming Fist

## Campaign Relevance
The Iron Crisis provides the backdrop for Nocturne's journey. The economic collapse creates an environment of survivalist opportunity where predatory efficiency — Nocturne's specialty — becomes advantageous. The crisis also explains the need for mercenary organizations and the political instability that Sarevok exploits.
