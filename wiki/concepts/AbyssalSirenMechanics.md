## Definition
The Abyssal Siren Mechanics are the specific abilities and balance systems that define the College of the Abyssal Siren Bard subclass. These mechanics transform social interaction into a form of combat through pheromone-based abilities and thrall management.

## Mechanics Overview

### Siren's Gaze
- Primary tool for breaking targets without traditional combat
- Targets the opponent's Social HP (e.g., Minthara's Social HP is 30)
- Successful use reduces target's resolve and opens them to influence

### Gilded Tether (Thralls)
- At Level 5, Nocturne can have up to 3 Thralls
- Each Thrall carries a Max HP penalty equal to their CR (Challenge Rating)
- Thralls are bound through the Gilded Tether ability
- Represents the Siren's control over those she has dominated

### Pheromone Spike
- Bonus Action Command ability
- Allows the Siren to unleash pheromones that affect nearby creatures
- Can be used to enhance Charisma checks or debilitate enemies
