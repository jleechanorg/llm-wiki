## Summary
Vampire Spawn are the thralls of true vampires, bound to serve until they either earn their freedom through service or are destroyed. In D&D 5e/Baldur's Gate 3, they retain some vampire abilities but are constrained by their master's control.

## Key Details
- **Type:** Undead (Vampire Spawn)
- **Origin:** Created when a living creature is killed and raised by a true vampire
- **Servitude:** Bound to serve the vampire who created them
- **Freedom:** Can potentially be freed through quests or circumstances (as in BG3)

## Key Abilities
- **Bite:** Fanged attack that deals damage and can cause piercing
- **Sunlight Sensitivity:** Disadvantage on attack rolls and Wisdom (Perception) checks while in sunlight
- **Regeneration:** Recover HP at the start of each turn if they've dealt damage with their bite that turn
- **Spider Climb:** Can move up surfaces without falling

## Campaign Relevance
Astarion is a Vampire Spawn created by Cazador Szarr. Unlike most spawn, he never fed on sentient blood during his 200 years of servitude, maintaining some measure of his former self. The BG3 campaign follows his journey toward freedom from Cazador's potential return.
