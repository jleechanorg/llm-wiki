## Concept Overview

The Soul-Tithe is an infernal ritual mechanic used by the Cassalanter Family in Baldur's Gate to collect souls and pay their debt to Archdevil Zariel of Avernus. The ritual involves a soul-tithe to a "refined resonance" siphoned into a nexus, with victims held in crystalline cages while their souls are extracted. The Cassalanters used this mechanism to flourish in the economic ruin left by the Absolute's defeat, consuming rival noble houses.

## How the Ritual Works

1. **Victims placed in crystalline cages** -- High-pressure depressurization when the ritual triggers
2. **Soul extraction** -- The "Refined Resonance" (soul energy) is siphoned into a pre-designated nexus
3. **Payment to Zariel** -- The soul-tithe flows to Avernus, satisfying the family's infernal contract
4. **Soul Coins** -- Physical storage of extracted soul energy, usable as currency

## Nocturne's Hijack

In the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, Nocturne hijacked the Cassalanter soul-tithe mid-ritual. She:
- Redirected the nexus through her own palms (gaining the ember-glow infernal sigils)
- Saved her sisters Elara and Lyra from being sacrificed
- Liquidated the Cassalanter Family as the "payment"
- Emerged as a proxy of Avernus with Zariel's attention
