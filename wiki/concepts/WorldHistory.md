Pre-built historical narrative for campaign settings.

## Eras
- Age of Dominion (Ancient past) — The Divine Order under The Creator
- Age of Rebellion (~620 B.G. - 0 A.G.) — First Great War of Ideas
- Age of the Mortal Star (20 B.G. - 10 A.G.) — Rise and fall of Alexiel

## Usage
Provides depth and context for campaign settings without manual worldbuilding.
