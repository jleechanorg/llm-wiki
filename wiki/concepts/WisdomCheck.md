## Definition
Wisdom Check is a mandatory roll in Think Mode for determining plan quality on intuitive and social plans. The character rolls 1d20 + Wisdom modifier, comparing against a Difficulty Class (DC) to determine how good their plan is.

## When Used
Wisdom is used for:
- Intuitive understanding
- Social situation reading
- Reading NPC motivations
- Gut feelings about risks
- Perception-based planning

## Comparison to Intelligence
Use Wisdom when the plan relies on intuition, social dynamics, or perception. Use [[Intelligence Check]] for tactical/logical plans.
