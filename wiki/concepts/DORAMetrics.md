## Summary
DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment) metrics measure software delivery performance. The codebase shows elite performance in deployment frequency and lead time.

## Key Claims
- **Deployment Frequency**: 9.1 deployments/day (elite)
- **Lead Time for Changes**: 2.9 hours median (elite)
- **Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR)**: 2.9 hours (elite)
- **Change Failure Rate**: Not explicitly tracked but implied low

## Context
DORA metrics are industry-standard benchmarks for DevOps performance. Elite performers achieve: >1 deployment/day, <1 hour lead time, <1 hour MTTR, and <5% failure rate.
