The Soul-Tithe is an infernal ritual used by the Cassalanter Family to harvest noble souls and offer them to Archdevil Zariel. In the Faction Nocturne BG3 V3 campaign, Nocturne hijacked this ritual, turning the Cassalanters' own machinery against them.

## Mechanics of the Ritual

The Soul-Tithe requires:
- "Refined Collateral" - nobles held as living sacrifices
- A ritual chamber (the Whispering Cellar at Sunder-Stone Villa)
- Soul coins and ledgers documenting the debts
- Devotion to Zariel as the receiving archdevil

The ritual channels the souls through "Refined Resonance" - a supernatural energy that can be siphoned into the participant's own marrow.

## Nocturne's Hijacking

Nocturne turned the ritual against the Cassalanters by:
1. Using her position within The Bloom Shadow to learn their secrets
2. Subverting the ritual's mechanics to redirect the energy to herself
3. Claiming the "Refined Resonance" for her own use

The result: the Cassalanter Family was destroyed (reduced to ash), Nocturne gained ember-glow palms marking her as a proxy of Avernus (Zariel's realm), and her sisters were freed.

## Consequences

After hijacking the ritual, Nocturne:
- Became marked with infernal ember-glow on her palms
- Now serves as a proxy of Avernus (the layer of the Nine Hells ruled by Zariel)
- Gained legitimate noble standing through claiming Sunder-Stone Villa
- Triggered a power vacuum that the Zhentarim and Flaming Fist are racing to exploit
