## Overview
Project Apotheosis is the central storyline of the Nocturne Old Republic campaign. A Sith Imperial project, it aims to elevate Nocturne through the consumption of multiple essences, achieving a form of transcendence.

## Goals
- Elevate the subject beyond normal Sith capabilities
- Strengthen the Vitiate bloodline
- Achieve galactic-scale power

## Methods
- **Essence Consumption:** Multiple victims to fuel the transformation
- **Golden Lineage:** Preserve pure Vitiate genetics
- **Galactic Harvest:** Scale operations across the Empire
