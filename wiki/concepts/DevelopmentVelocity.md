## Summary
Measurement of software development speed through commit frequency, PR merge rates, and deployment cadence.

## Key Claims
- **Commit Statistics**: 903 commits over 30 days (30.1/day average)
- **PR Statistics**: 273 merged PRs (9.1/day), 28.6 hour average merge time
- **PR Size Distribution**: 65 small (0-50 lines), 114 medium (100-1000 lines), 63 large (1000-10000 lines)
- **Median Lead Time**: 2.9 hours from PR creation to merge
