## Overview

The Determination Adept is a custom D&D 5e class created for the Undertale campaign, based on the Sorcerer/Bard hybrid class framework. It is designed to mechanically and narratively represent the power of DETERMINATION—the central thematic force of the Undertale universe.

## Class Design

### Base Framework
The Determination Adept uses a Sorcerer/Bard hybrid as its base, providing:
- **Sorcerer:** HP, saving throws (CON/CHA), and a "talent inside" metaphor for Determination
- **Bard:** Skills system (Persuasion, Insight, Perception, Survival), social utility, and inspiration mechanics

### The "Determination" Mechanic
DETERMINATION is represented as a tangible force keeping the soul whole even in the face of death. Mechanically, it informs:
- **Save/Load Metaphor:** The ability to persist through failures
- **Red Soul:** Frisk's soul is the only red one—a unique anomaly in the monster world

### Attribute Distribution (Even-Number Optimized)
STR 10, DEX 14 (+2), CON 14 (+2), INT 10, WIS 14 (+2), CHA 16 (+3)

This distribution prioritizes:
- **CHA:** Social interaction, dialogue-based resolution (Mercy path)
- **WIS:** Monster insight, perception, survival
- **CON:** Persistence and "Determination" as HP retention

## Class Features

- **HP:** 8 (lower than typical martial but compensated by save mechanics)
- **AC:** 11 (no armor, relying on dodge/evasion)
- **Skills:** Persuasion (+5), Insight (+4), Perception (+4), Survival (+4)
- **Equipment:** Faded Ribbon, Tough Glove, Stick, Bandage
