## Description

The Apex Paladin is a custom D&D 5e class featured in the Merc Solo Brute Gladius campaign. This class transforms the traditional paladin into a character focused on domination and psychological warfare rather than divine devotion.

## In This Campaign

Gladius is a Level 5 Apex Paladin, using custom class features that emphasize:
- **Perfected Strike:** Expend EP (Energy Points) to add force damage (2d8 for 5 EP, +1d8 per additional 2 EP)
- **Aura of Unyielding Dominance:** Allies within 10ft have Advantage vs. Frightened/Charmed; enemies have Disadvantage vs. your Intimidation
- **Apex Senses:** Use action to gain Advantage on Perception/Investigation and detect invisible creatures within 30ft
- **Willpower Infusion:** Pool of 25 EP for various effects

## Class Concept

The Apex Paladin channels personal will into devastating force and psychological dominance rather than divine power. This fits Gladius's character — his power comes from his determination to dominate, not from any god.
