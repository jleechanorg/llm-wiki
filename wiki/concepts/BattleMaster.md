Battle Master is a Fighter subclass specialized in tactical superiority on the battlefield. Arthur Dayne's Fighter levels utilize this subclass, granting him superiority dice that can be used for tactical maneuvers such as disarming attacks, sweeping swings, and commanding movements. The Battle Master represents the pinnacle of martial skill, combining physical prowess with strategic thinking. This subclass makes Arthur one of the most dangerous combatants in Westeros, as his tactical knowledge complements his legendary swordsmanship.
