The Baldur's Gate Setting refers to the post-Baldur's Gate 3 Faerun setting following the fall of the Absolute. This setting is characterized by political turmoil as the city recovers from the cult's influence and various factions compete for power. The post-Absolute landscape provides opportunities for those willing to exploit the instability, including power seekers like Astarion. The setting combines urban fantasy with the complex politics of the Sword Coast.
