## Description

The Mangekyō Sharingan is the advanced form of the Sharingan, activated by experiencing intense trauma (specifically, the death of someone close to the user). It grants access to incredibly potent, unique visual powers — Amaterasu, Tsukuyomi, and Susano'o — but at a significant cost.

## In This Campaign

Itachi possesses the Mangekyō Sharingan from the start, having activated it during his participation in the Uchiha Clan massacre. He must choose one of three abilities:

1. **Amaterasu - Blazing Scourge:** Unquenchable black flames dealing 6d6 Fire damage each turn. Cost: 1 level of Fatigue per use.

2. **Tsukuyomi - Temporal Nightmare:** Traps target in illusion for up to 1 round per Itachi's INT modifier. Target takes 8d4 Psychic damage and gains 1 level of Fatigue.

3. **Susano'o - Ethereal Colossus:** Summons a spectral avatar granting 10 * Level temporary HP, DR 5, and 2d8 unarmed attacks. Sustaining costs 1 Fatigue per round.

## Significance

The Mangekyō represents Itachi's willingness to pay any price for power. His evil nature means he has no hesitation using these devastating abilities, setting him apart from the canonical Itachi who held back.
