## Definition
Mandatory pauses in combat flow allowing players to use reactions at specific trigger points. Ensures players have opportunity to use Shield, Parry, Counterspell, and Hellish Rebuke appropriately.

## Trigger Types

### Pre-Hit Reactions
- **Shield** — After attack roll announced, before hit/miss determined
- **Parry** — After attack roll, before damage

### Pre-Spell Reactions
- **Counterspell** — When enemy casts spell within 60 ft

### Post-Damage Reactions
- **Hellish Rebuke** — After taking damage from a creature

## Protocol Steps
1. Announce action and roll
2. PAUSE before resolving
3. Ask player for reaction (or confirm no reaction)
4. Then resolve hit/miss/damage
