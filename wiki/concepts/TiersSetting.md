## Overview

The Tiers (formally: Terratus) is the world of the Tyranny campaign, adapted from the Obsidian Entertainment game Tyranny. It is a land conquered by Kyros, the Overlord, whose Edicts have fundamentally reshaped reality itself.

## World Overview

Terratus is a world defined by conquest and the imposition of order through magical law. Kyros has already conquered the known world once; the campaign takes place in Year 431 TR (Terratus Reckoning), known as the Year of the Sword.

## Key Features

### The Edicts
Kyros's Edicts are magical laws that reshape reality. They are not mere proclamations but fundamental changes to how the world works. The Edicts are Kyros's primary tool of control and conquest.

### The Factions
Two major forces contest the conquered territories:
- **Disfavored:** The disciplined, honor-bound military force. They value order, earned glory, and military precision.
- **Scarlet Chorus:** The chaotic, numerous army. They value results over honor, use brute force and numbers, and enjoy cruelty.

### The Conquest
The campaign references Nocturne's history during the original conquest, where they made calculated choices based on "scientific rigor" rather than loyalty—poisoning cities to preserve economic assets, burning libraries after copying forbidden data.

## The "Bad Code" Metaphor

Nocturne views Terratus as "full of bad code"—a world full of inefficiencies, unnecessary suffering, and suboptimal systems. Their goal is to gather forbidden knowledge (from the Spires) to "patch" the universe, treating mass sacrifice as a "simple resource allocation problem."
