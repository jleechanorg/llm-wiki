## Overview

Scalable Oversight is the challenge of verifying AI behavior at scale when humans cannot directly evaluate every decision. Research focuses on methods for maintaining oversight without requiring human review of every action.

## Key Properties

- **Challenge**: Humans can't evaluate every AI decision in high-frequency systems
- **Approaches**: Debate, recursive reward modeling, scalable interpretability
- **Partnership on AI**: Safety-Critical AI Program focuses on scalable oversight
- **Arize/Phoenix**: ML observability enables scalable oversight via automated monitoring

## Connection to Governance

Scalable oversight is the core challenge for AO's governance layer — how to maintain governance over thousands of PRs without human review of each one. Escalation paths and drift detection are mechanisms for scalable oversight.
