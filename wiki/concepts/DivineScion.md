## Overview
The Divine Scion is a prestige class combining elements of a Siren and another divine bloodline. Nocturne embodies this class through her hybrid lineage of Malcanthet (demon lordess) and Bhaal (murder god).

## Class Features
- **Divine Heritage:** Powers scaled from divine or quasi-divine beings
- **Siren Abilities:** Charm, enthrall, and conversion capabilities
- **Hybrid Flexibility:** Features from both bloodlines

## Mother's Mercy
The signature ability of the Divine Scion — a soul conversion mechanic that allows the character to transform others through divine power.
