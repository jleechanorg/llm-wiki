Frontier Models refer to the latest generation of large language models from leading AI companies that push the boundaries of capability, context length, and performance. These models are typically the most capable but may have higher API costs.

## Frontier Models in WorldAI
- Available via OpenRouter aggregator
- Include Llama 3.1, GPT-OSS, GLM-4.6, Grok 4.1
- Larger context windows (131K-2M tokens)
- Optimized for creative storytelling
