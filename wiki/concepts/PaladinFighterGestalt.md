Paladin Fighter Gestalt is the combined martial and divine class progression system used for Aurelius V1, where the character gains levels in both Paladin and Fighter simultaneously. Unlike a standard multiclass, gestalt progression allows the character to gain the best abilities of both classes without the penalty of slowed progression. For a Paladin like Aurelius with high INT and CHA, this allows him to be both a divine champion and a master tactician. The Fighter levels provide extra attacks and battle master maneuvers while the Paladin levels provide oath powers and divine spellcasting, creating a comprehensive warrior.
