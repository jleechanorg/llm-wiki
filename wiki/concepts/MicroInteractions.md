Micro-interactions are small, focused moments of animation that provide feedback to user actions.

## Examples
- Button hover effects (translateY -1px)
- Button click effects (remove translate)
- Loading spinners
- Form focus animations
- Dropdown item indent

## Design Principles
- Subtle movement (1-3px)
- Fast duration (150-300ms)
- Immediate feedback to user input
- Non-blocking to main functionality
