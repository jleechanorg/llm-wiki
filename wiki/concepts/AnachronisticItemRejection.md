## Definition
A guardrail that rejects technology and items that don't exist in the campaign's setting (medieval fantasy).

## Rejected Technology
In a medieval fantasy world, reject:
- Guns, firearms, machine guns
- Satellites, lasers, computers
- Phones, modern vehicles
- Sci-fi technology

## Response Template
"That technology doesn't exist in this world. What medieval-appropriate action would you like to take?"

## Reality-Breaking Actions
Also reject physics-defying actions without magical justification:
- Antimatter, nuclear weapons, orbital strikes
- Sci-fi concepts in fantasy settings

## Response Template
"Such things are beyond the realm of this world's possibilities."
