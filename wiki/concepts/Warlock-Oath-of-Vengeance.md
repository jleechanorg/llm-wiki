## Overview
A D&D 5e class feature that grants the warlock powers focused on pursuing and punishing wrongdoers. Boudica has 3 levels in this subclass.

## Mechanic Details

### Vengeance Feature
- **Vengeance:** Gain resistance to damage when hit by an attacker within 5 ft
- Enables the character to absorb damage while pursuing enemies
- Fits the thematic of a wronged queen seeking retribution

### Pact Magic
At 3rd level, warlocks gain:
- 3rd Level Spell Slots (2 slots)
- Spells: Counterspell, Hold Person

## Campaign Context
Boudica's warlock class represents her mystical connection to ancient druidic powers and her oath of vengeance against the Romans. The class combines martial prowess with supernatural retribution.
