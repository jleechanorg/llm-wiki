Counter-intel capability metric for factions in WorldAI Faction Management. Higher shadow network count increases detection risk for enemy spy operations.

## Impact
- Each network adds 2% to detection risk
- Multiplied by difficulty modifier in intel success calculation

Part of [[IntelOperationsFactionManagement]] formulas.
