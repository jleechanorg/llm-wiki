## Definition
Dual-purpose resource serving as both currency and health for Sovereigns. 1 SP = 1,000 Universes of territory.

## Functions
- **Health:** At 0 SP, Sovereign is erased from the Pan-Substrate
- **Currency:** Expend SP to boost Logic Siege attacks
- **Territory:** SP determines rank and domain size

## Starting Condition
New Sovereigns begin with 1 SP (Administrator tier, Rank 801-1000)

## Scale
| Rank Tier | SP Range |
|-----------|----------|
| Emperor | 100,000+ |
| Conductor | 10,000-99,999 |
| Administrator | 100-9,999 |
