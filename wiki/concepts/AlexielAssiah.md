## Overview

Alexiel Assiah is a high-stakes D&D 5e campaign set in the world of Assiah during Year 78 DR (Alexiel Era). The campaign explores themes of deception, warfare, and the moral cost of power through Alexiel, a biological construct who must choose between her public identity as a Null and her secret nature as a magical prodigy.

## World Background

- **Fractured World:** Divine order vs chaotic rebellion
- **Celestial Imperium:** Eastern bastion of rigid hierarchy and golden magic
- **Unchained Host:** Western army seeking to dismantle divine order
- **The Thousand Year War:** Has reached a stalemate

## Character Concepts

### Dual Identity
- **Public:** Alexiel is a "Null" - a being without magic
- **Secret:** She is a magical prodigy capable of Nullification Field
- **Origin:** Biological construct designed by Lucifer

### Nullification Field
- Special ability that levels the playing field against celestial beings
- Allows her to fight against the divine despite being "weapon of the rebellion"

## Campaign Themes

- **Deception:** Living a lie as a Null
- **Moral Boundaries:** The Silverwood massacre as the breaking point
- **Identity Crisis:** Weapon vs person
- **War:** Thousand Year War backdrop

## Game Mechanics

- **Character Class:** Custom Gestalt (Aberrant Mind/Assassin blend)
- **Level:** 6
- **Resources:** EP: 40/40, Hit Dice: 6/6
- **Special:** Nullification Field, Second Wind
