## Definition
Psychological framework categorizing personality into 16 types based on four dichotomies: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, Judging/Perceiving. Used internally for character depth but never exposed in narrative.

## Application in Character Profiles
- Stored in master_directive.md as INTERNAL ONLY
- Never appears in character descriptions, dialogue, or narrative
- Expresses personality through observed behaviors instead
