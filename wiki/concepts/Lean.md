Lean is Microsoft's interactive theorem prover and programming language. It combines a functional programming language with a powerful dependent type system and built-in automated theorem proving. Lean 4 has emerged as particularly popular in AI research contexts.

## Key Properties
- **Dependent types**: Can express complex mathematical propositions as types
- **Mathlib**: A vast library of formalized mathematics (used for IMO problems, etc.)
- **Autoformalization**: AI research uses Lean for converting natural language proofs to formal proofs
- **Fast evaluation**: Lean's tactic framework enables both interactive and automated proof search
