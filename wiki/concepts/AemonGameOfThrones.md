## Overview

The Aemon Game Of Thrones campaign is a dark, morally corrupt D&D 5e adaptation of George R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire universe. The player operates in "god mode" with chaotic evil alignment, taking on the role of a secret Targaryen heir working as a mercenary for The Mountain.

## Key Themes

- **Moral Corruption:** The campaign explores evil actions through a player's lens
- **Hidden Identity:** Aegon's secret claim to the Iron Throne drives the narrative
- **Political Intrigue:** Setting up encounters with major GOT characters
- **Violence and Power:** The Mountain's Men as a vehicle for dark missions

## Game Mechanics

- **Character Class:** Ranger (Gloomstalker subclass)
- **Level:** Starting at Level 5
- **Play Mode:** God Mode enabled
- **Mission Types:** Pillaging, raiding, enslavement, assassination

## Campaign Structure

- **Total Scenes:** 211
- **Starting Year:** 298 AC
- **Character:** Aegon Targaryen, 18-year-old secret heir
