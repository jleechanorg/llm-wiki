The Kingsguard Oath is the sacred vow taken by members of the elite royal protection order, binding them to lifelong service protecting the king and royal family. This oath is separate from and supersedes all other allegiances, including ties to one's own family house. Arthur Dayne as Lord Commander exemplifies the ultimate fulfillment of this oath, having served faithfully until the fall of the Mad King. The oath requires the knight to defend the monarch at all costs, reveal no secrets of the crown, and take no wife or land.
