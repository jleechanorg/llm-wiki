## Description
The core framework of the Divine Leverage System. Three layers (Mask/Persona/Source) that the player can project. Switching layers is a free action but has narrative consequences. Each layer has its own complete stat block that MUST be tracked independently — the player cannot access stats from a deeper layer without triggering Dissonance.
