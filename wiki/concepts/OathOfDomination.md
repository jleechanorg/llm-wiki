Oath of Domination is a Paladin oath representing rule through strength and authority rather than through the consent of the governed. Aurelius Julius Caesar V1 follows this oath, believing that the strong must rule and the weak must follow. This oath differs from Oath of Conquest in its focus on legitimate authority established through demonstrated power rather than mere tyranny. The paladin following this oath sees themselves as a necessary ruler whose strength benefits those beneath them. The oath's philosophy is that order through strength is more stable than chaos.
