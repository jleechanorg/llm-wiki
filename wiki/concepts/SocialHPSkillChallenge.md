## Definition
A mechanic for resolving social interactions in D&D 5e using an HP-like system based on NPC tiers and request difficulty.

## NPC Tier HP Scale
| Tier | Examples | HP |
|------|----------|-----|
| Commoner | Villagers, peasants | 1-2 |
| Merchant/Guard | Trade NPCs, city guards | 2-3 |
| Noble/Knight | Aristocrats, knights | 3-5 |
| Lord/General | Regional rulers, military leaders | 5-8 |
| King | Monarchs, high royalty | 8-12 |
| God | Deities, divine entities | 15+ |

## Request Difficulty Multiplier
- **1x**: "Teach me", "Alliance"
- **2x**: "Betray beliefs"
- **3x**: "Submit/Surrender"

## Display Requirement
Every interaction with active Social HP challenge MUST show `[SOCIAL SKILL CHALLENGE: NPC]` box in narrative with:
- Objective
- Current HP
- Status

**DO NOT OMIT** the box on continuation turns — show it EVERY time.
