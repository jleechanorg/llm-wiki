## Description
D&D 5e mechanic allowing characters to increase their ability scores at specific levels (4, 8, 12, 16, 19). Players may either:
- Increase two ability scores by +1 each (respecting maximum of 20), OR
- Take a Feat instead of the ASI

## Rules
- Maximum ability score: 20 (unless rules specify otherwise)
- Can increase same ability twice or two different abilities
- Some feats provide +1 to an ability as part of their prerequisites
