## Summary
The Hunger is a unique mechanical system specific to the bg1-nocturne campaign. It's a state-tracking mechanic where Nocturne's stats fluctuate based on her satiation level, creating ongoing narrative tension around resource management and survival.

## Key Details
- **Mechanic Type:** Custom stat modifier system
- **Core Rule:** Satiation state directly impacts ability scores

## Key States
- **Satiated:** +2 to all stats — optimal performance
- **Withdrawal:** -2 to all stats — significant penalty
- **Transition:** Presumably a gradient between states

## Campaign Relevance
The Hunger adds survivalist tension to the campaign. Nocturne must balance resources and ensure she remains satiated for peak performance, while withdrawal creates vulnerability at critical moments. This mechanic reinforces the "Serpent Queen" survivalist theme and creates practical decision points beyond combat.
