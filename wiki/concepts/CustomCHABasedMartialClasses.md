## Description

A defining design pattern in the WorldArchitect campaign universe: custom character classes that use **Charisma as their primary combat stat**, blending martial prowess with social/psychological domination. These classes replace the traditional STR/DEX martial paradigm with CHA-driven attack, damage, and spellcasting — formalizing the narrative idea that force of personality IS force of arms.

## The Pattern

Every CHA-based martial class shares these features:
1. **CHA for Attack/Damage:** Replace STR/DEX with CHA on weapon attacks
2. **Spellcasting via CHA:** Domination, enchantment, and command spells
3. **Aura/Presence Mechanic:** An area-of-effect buff/debuff tied to CHA
4. **Tiered Progression to Level 30:** With apotheosis/ascension capstone
5. **Dual Mask:** A public persona and a true nature, both powered by CHA

## Why It Recurs

The WorldArchitect player consistently gravitates toward characters who dominate through presence rather than muscle. This class design pattern is the mechanical formalization of a consistent playstyle: "I should be able to win by looking at them."
