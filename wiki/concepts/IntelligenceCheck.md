## Definition
Intelligence Check is a mandatory roll in Think Mode for determining plan quality on tactical and logical plans. The character rolls 1d20 + Intelligence modifier, comparing against a Difficulty Class (DC) to determine how good their plan is.

## When Used
Intelligence is used for:
- Tactical assessment
- Logical analysis
- Strategic planning
- Problem-solving
- Recall of factual information

## Comparison to Wisdom
Use Intelligence when the plan involves logic, tactics, and factual recall. Use [[Wisdom Check]] when the plan involves intuition, social reading, or gut feelings.
