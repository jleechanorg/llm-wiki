## Definition
System for determining combatant turn sequence in D&D 5e encounters. Players roll initiative (typically d20 + DEX modifier) to establish order, with all combatants taking turns in strict sequence.

## Key Rules
- All combatants take turns in strict initiative order
- Player CANNOT take consecutive turns
- After player explicitly ends their turn, process ALL ally/enemy turns before returning to player
- Combat status block displays at start of EVERY round showing Level/CR, HP, and full action economy for all combatants
