D&D 5.1 SRD-based combat simulation system for WorldArchitect.AI faction management. Supports three modes: Fast (expected damage), Detailed (full roll-by-roll), and Deterministic (seeded RNG). Implements attack rolls, damage rolls, initiative, and morale rout mechanics following official SRD rules.
