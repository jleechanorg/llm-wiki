Game of Thrones refers to the GRRM (George R.R. Martin) universe setting, specifically the world of Westeros where the Arthur Dayne campaign takes place. This setting is characterized by political intrigue, complex character motivations, and the constant struggle for power among the great houses. The setting draws from historical medieval Europe, particularly the Wars of the Roses, and emphasizes that when playing the game of thrones, winner takes all. Arthur Dayne's service in the Kingsguard places him at the center of these political struggles.
