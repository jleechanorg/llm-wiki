## Definition
Primary Rival mechanics — a Sovereign of similar rank with opposing Logic and personal connection to the PC.

## Rival Agenda Clock
- 4 segments representing rival's planning
- Advances when PC acts passively
- At 4/4: Rival strikes PC asset

## Characteristics
- **Similar Rank:** Close to PC in Sovereign hierarchy
- **Opposing Logic:** Metaphysical law conflicts
- **Personal Connection:** Narrative hook for conflict

## Counter-Play
- Active PC actions stall Rival Agenda
- Defeating Rival in Logic Siege reduces threat
