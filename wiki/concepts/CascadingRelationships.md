## Description
System where relationships with one NPC affect the player's standing with that NPC's allies and faction members.

## Mechanism
When player harms an NPC, relationships with their allies are damaged:
- **Harm to NPC** → -1 to -3 to each ally's trust level
- **Death of NPC's friend/ally** → -3 to -6 with the grieving NPC
- **Helped NPC's ally** → +1 to +2 with the original NPC

## Behavior Chain Example
If player attacks a merchant's friend:
1. Merchant's trust decreases
2. Merchant's trading partners become more cold
3. Local guild may revise disposition
4. Information networks become less helpful

## Usage
Apply after any significant harm or benefit to NPCs with established social connections. Updates via [[Relationship Update Triggers]].
