## Description
Optional D&D 5e character advancement option that provides mechanical benefits beyond ability score increases. Feats often have prerequisites (minimum ability scores, class levels, or other feats).

## ASI vs Feat
At levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19, players choose between:
- ASI: +2 to one stat OR +1/+1 to two stats
- Feat: Specialized ability with prerequisites

## Common Feat Categories
- Combat-focused (Great Weapon Master, Sharpshooter)
- Skill-focused (Skill Expert, Lucky)
- Metamagic (Metamagic Adept, Sorcerer)
- Origin-specific (Fey Touched, Eldritch Adept)
