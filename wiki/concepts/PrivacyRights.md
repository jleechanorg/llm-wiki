## Summary
User rights regarding personal information, varying by geographic location and applicable data protection laws.

## Description
Depending on where users are located geographically, applicable privacy law may grant certain rights regarding personal information. These rights can be exercised by submitting a data subject access request.

## Key Rights Typically Include
- Right to access personal information
- Right to correction
- Right to deletion
- Right to opt-out of certain processing
- Right to data portability
