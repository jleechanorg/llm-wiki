## Overview
The Stigma Score is a social-reputation mechanic from the Nocturne BG3 V4 campaign, tracking how much the broader world knows about Nocturne's true nature and past. As stigma accumulates, the campaign's social landscape shifts — NPCs become more suspicious, hostile, or opportunistic.
