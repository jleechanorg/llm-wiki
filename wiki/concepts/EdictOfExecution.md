## Overview
The Edict of Execution is one of Kyros's overarching laws governing Terratus in the Tyranny campaign. Kyros's Edicts are oppressive decrees that form the backbone of the world's legal and social order. The Fatebinder (Nocturne) navigates, interprets, and sometimes subverts these Edicts as part of the conquest of the Tiers.

## Key Details
- **Category:** GameMechanic / Setting / Law
- **Campaign(s):** [[tyranny-campaign]]
- **Source:** Obsidian Entertainment's Tyranny game
- **Context:** The Edicts of Kyros (including the Edict of Execution) are the mechanism through which Kyros rules the world. The Fatebinder operates within and against these Edicts.
