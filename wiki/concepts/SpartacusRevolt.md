## Description

The Spartacus Revolt (73-71 BCE) was a major slave uprising against the Roman Republic. Led by the Thracian gladiator Spartacus, the rebellion began with escaped gladiators from the ludus (training school) in Capua and grew into a massive army that threatened the Roman Republic itself.

## In This Campaign

The campaign is set during 71 BCE, during the height of the Spartacus Revolt. Gaia Julia's mission is to "stabilize the chaos of the Spartacus revolt" — suggesting she's working to manipulate the outcome or control the narrative around this historical event. The Roman Empire (in its Republican form) seeks to suppress the rebellion while maintaining public order.

## Historical Context

The revolt began in 73 BCE when around 70 gladiators escaped from the School of Gladiators at Capua. Under Spartacus's leadership, they defeated several Roman armies before the final defeat by Marcus Licinius Crassus in 71 BCE. The revolt destabilized Roman control in southern Italy and required the deployment of significant military resources.
