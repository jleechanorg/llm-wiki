## Description

The Sharingan is a Kekkei Genkai (bloodline limit) possessed by the Uchiha clan. This dojutsu (eye technique) grants the user enhanced visual abilities, including enhanced perception, the ability to read movements, and most importantly, the power to copy techniques and cast illusions.

## In This Campaign

Itachi possesses the Sharingan from the start, demonstrating his prodigy status (he gained it at age 12, compared to the family norm of 18). His Sharingan grants:

- **Enhanced Perception (Passive):** Advantage on all Wisdom (Perception) checks, especially for visual details, reading body language, and detecting subtle movements
- **Illusion/Genjutsu Mastery:** As he gains levels, he learns all control/crowd control spells (Hold Person, Confusion, Command, Dominate Person, Suggestion, Fear, etc.)
- **Ability Copying:** When he witnesses another character use an ability, he gains an innate understanding of its workings

## Significance

The Sharingan is Itachi's primary tool for achieving his goals. Combined with his intelligence and strategic thinking, it makes him one of the most formidable shinobi in existence. It allows him to analyze opponents, copy their techniques, and cast devastating illusions.
