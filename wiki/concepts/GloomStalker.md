Gloom Stalker is the Ranger subclass that serves as Aurelius V1's escorts in the northern Spanish campaign. These rangers specialize in operating in darkness and shadows, making them perfect for reconnaissance and flanking missions in Northern Hispania's challenging terrain. They provide tactical advantage through their ability to remain unseen in dim light and deliver devastating first strikes. The subclass represents the shadow aspect of Aurelius's forces—the silent hunters who operate beyond the light of legitimate warfare.
