## Description
Game design principle where NPCs have independent lives, agendas, and agency. NPCs approach the player with missions every 3-8 scenes, pursue their own goals, and may refuse, betray, or come into conflict with the player.

## Key Features
- **NPC Missions**: NPCs approach player with requests every 3-8 scenes
- **Independent Agendas**: NPCs have goals separate from player interests
- **Breaking Points**: NPCs have limits beyond which they will not comply
- **Mandatory Visibility**: `state_updates.scene_event` must appear in narrative
