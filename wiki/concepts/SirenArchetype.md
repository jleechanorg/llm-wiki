## Concept Overview

The Siren Archetype is a specific character class for Gaia Julia Caesar, combining Bard and Paladin in a gestalt configuration. The "Siren" name reflects the character's social manipulation abilities, biological weapon status, and the "gift of Venus" divine bloodline. In Gaia Julia V4, she is Level 12 Siren; in V5, these powers lie dormant at Level 1.

## Core Identity

The Siren is a biological weapon of social engineering -- a being designed at the genetic level to manipulate, charm, and control. The "Siren" name refers to:
- **Charm and Seduction:** Not as romance but as weaponized social influence
- **Genetic Gift of Venus:** Divine bloodline power tied to charm, beauty, and compulsion
- **Social Engineering:** Controlling populations and political outcomes through presence alone

## V4 vs V5 Duality

**Gaia Julia V4 (Active):** Level 12 Siren, full access to Bard/Paladin gestalt powers. Operates as "Livia of Capua" (Level 1 captive cover) while secretly being a biological weapon. The Lotus Ten form a living wall of flesh around her.

**Gaia Julia V5 (Dormant):** Level 1 Bard with Siren powers stripped by poison, trauma, or mystic curse. The same genetic gift lies inactive -- "the gift of Venus" is dormant. She is physically vulnerable, emotionally raw, and must survive on wits alone.
