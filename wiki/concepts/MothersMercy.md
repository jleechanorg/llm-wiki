## Overview
Mother's Mercy is the signature soul-conversion ability of the Divine Scion class. It allows the character to transform creatures through divine power, binding their souls to the caster's will.

## Mechanics
- **Conversion Target:** Willing or unwilling creatures
- **Soul Binding:** Creates a link between converter and converted
- **Thrall Creation:** Converted creatures become thralls

## Context
As Nocturne wields this power through her Malcanthet lineage, it represents the "mercy" of transformation — liberation from one's former self in favor of devoted service.
