## Description
Relationship metric for NPC interactions ranging from -10 (hostile) to +10 (allied). CHECK before ANY NPC interaction, UPDATE after significant actions.

## Priority Order
1. Private trust_override (highest)
2. Private relationship
3. Private reputation
4. Public reputation
5. Default neutral (lowest)

**Principle**: Direct experience beats hearsay.
