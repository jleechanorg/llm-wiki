## Description
The portion of token budget allocated to narrative content (story turns). Approximately 50-60% of max input.

## Allocation
- **Start Turns**: 25% — Opening narrative and character establishment
- **Middle Summary**: 10% — Condensed middle section
- **End Turns**: 60% — Most recent turns for recency
- **Reserved**: 5% — Safety margin
