Web Content Accessibility Guidelines compliance. The Fantasy Theme achieves WCAG AA compliance through:
- Lifted text colors (`--text-primary: #f0ecff`) for 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- 3:1 contrast ratio for non-text elements (borders, UI components)
- Accessibility support for ember particle animation (can be disabled)
