## Overview

The College of Swords is a Bard subclass in D&D 5e that serves as [[Nocturne]]'s mechanical framework in the [[Tyranny Campaign]]. It reimages the Bard as a magical warrior using performance to enhance combat—flavored as Nocturne reciting mathematical proofs that force reality to correct itself.

## Mechanical Framework

### Blade Flourish
The defining feature. Flourishes allow defensive bonuses, slashing damage with area effect, or mobile push effects using Bardic Inspiration dice.

### Roleplay vs. Mechanics Split
Nocturne is mechanically a College of Swords Bard but thematically a Wizard of the Order of Scribes. They recite mathematical proofs and use their blade Discordia as a conductor for force—Bard chassis, Scribe wizard flavor.

College of Swords is a Bard subclass from D&D 5e that transforms the bard into a melee combatant using a blade as their focus. Bards of the College of Swords are simultaneously spellcasters and skilled blade dancers, using their performance to enhance their combat prowess.

## In This Campaign

Gaia Julia is a Level 4 College of Swords Bard. This subclass fits her "Glass Cannon" archetype — high damage output but low durability. The blade center ability allows her to use a weapon (likely a sword) as her Arcane Focus, while the Fighting Style gives her options for either defense or offense.

## Mechanical Features

- **Blade Flourish:** Can use Bardic Inspiration to enhance weapon attacks
- **Fighting Style:** Choose from Dueling, Two-Weapon Fighting, or Protection
- **Extra Attack:** At level 5, can attack twice
- **Defensive Flourish:** Use BI to add to AC
- **Slashing Flourish:** Use BI to deal extra damage and create a fear effect
