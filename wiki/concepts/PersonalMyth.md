## Definition
Deep character framework component defining a character's self-perception narrative. Four elements:
- **Role**: Heroic or tragic self-perception
- **Story**: One-sentence life narrative
- **Comfort**: What provides safety/control
- **Blind Spot**: Negative consequence of the myth

## Usage
- Drives character motivation and reactions
- Creates internal conflict when myth is challenged
- Enables character growth through break-point events
