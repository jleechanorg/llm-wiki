Narrative design pattern where the protagonist faces an impossible choice between two valid but conflicting values.

## Example
Ser Arion's choice: Uphold oath (commit atrocity for security) vs. Break vow (preserve innocence, risk chaos)

## Usage
Used as a campaign hook to drive immediate player engagement.
