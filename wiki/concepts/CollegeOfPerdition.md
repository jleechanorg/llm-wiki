## Overview
College of Perdition is a Bard subclass in the Nocturne campaigns, representing Nocturne's connection to the Abyss and infernal powers. It features Thrall mechanics, Soul Thief progression, and Pheromone Spike abilities. The college evolves into "College of the Abyssal Siren" as Nocturne grows in power.
