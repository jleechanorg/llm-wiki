Character mode is a narrative mode where the LLM responds as a game character, handling player interactions and dialogue. In character mode, the LLM generates responses appropriate to the character's personality and current situation.

## Usage
Used for interactions 1-4 in the Sariel campaign, where players make choices and receive character-driven responses.
