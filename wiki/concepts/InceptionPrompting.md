## Overview

Inception Prompting is CAMEL's core technique for autonomous multi-agent cooperation. Rather than humans specifying task details, agents are given roles and a seed task, and they autonomously guide the conversation toward the goal.

## Key Properties

- **Role-playing**: Agents adopt specific roles (assistant, user)
- **Autonomous cooperation**: Agents guide conversation without human intervention
- **Seed task**: Initial task is minimal; agents elaborate as they go
- **Emergent behavior**: Complex cooperation emerges from simple initial conditions

## Connection to Governance

Inception prompting is relevant to governance because it shows how multi-agent systems can self-organize without centralized control. Governance constraints can be designed to guide emergent cooperation rather than dictating specific behaviors.
