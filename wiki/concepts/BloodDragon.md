## Description

"Blood Dragon" is Sariel's military identity, earned through her exceptional (and brutal) effectiveness as an FBI/Special Forces operative. The name represents her reputation for lethal resolution — a 94% kill rate and 100% case clearance.

## In This Campaign

Sariel's Blood Dragon identity came from the "Dragon's Breath" incident in 2023. During a cartel bunker raid, a magnesium flare backdraft created a deadly situation from which Sariel emerged carrying a killer's severed head. This incident cemented her reputation but also led to her dishonorable discharge for being "too effective."

The V2 version shows an even more extreme version — a 16-year-old with 100 confirmed kills as a masked vigilante. This suggests the Blood Dragon identity exists across multiple campaign versions with different origin points.

## Significance

The Blood Dragon represents the moral ambiguity at the heart of these campaigns. Sariel works outside official channels, using lethal force to achieve results. The campaigns explore whether this makes her a hero (effective justice) or a monster (extrajudicial killer).
