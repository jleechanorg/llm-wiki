Power Ambition is the core theme driving Astarion's character in the post-BG3 setting. After gaining vampiric powers, Astarion's desire for ever-greater strength becomes the defining characteristic of his existence. This theme explores the psychology of power-seeking and the eternal question: when is enough, enough? The post-Absolute world provides fertile ground for this exploration as various entities vie for control in the power vacuum. Astarion's ambition sets him apart from his companions and drives the narrative forward.
