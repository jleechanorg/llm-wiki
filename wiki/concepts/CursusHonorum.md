The Cursus Honorum is the Roman political career path system that tracks a noble's progression through the ranks of Roman political office. Aurelius V1's exceptional stats (INT 18, CHA 18) position him well for this career progression. While his Oath of Domination makes him a military-focused ruler, the Cursus Honorum represents the political advancement he could achieve through his combination of military success and political acumen. The system tracks positions from quaestor through consul, with each step requiring specific qualifications and providing additional power and influence.
