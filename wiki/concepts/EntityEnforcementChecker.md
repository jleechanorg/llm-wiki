EntityEnforcementChecker validates that generated content includes required entities. Part of the entity_instructions module alongside EntityInstructionGenerator.

**Purpose:**
- Verifies generated narratives include mandatory entities
- Ensures player-referenced NPCs appear in output
- Validates location owners are acknowledged in their spaces
