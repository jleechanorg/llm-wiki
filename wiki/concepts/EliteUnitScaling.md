System for creating elite variants of base SRD creatures based on D&D character levels. Provides scaling functions that increase stats proportional to character level.

## Elite Tiers
- **elite_6**: Knight archetype — represents 6th-level character equivalent
- **elite_10**: Gladiator archetype — represents 10th-level character equivalent

## Scaling Approach
Elite units receive increased HP, attack bonus, and damage dice based on the difference between their tier level and the base creature's CR-derived level.

## Purpose
Allows faction systems to represent commander-level units without requiring unique stat blocks for each possible variant.
