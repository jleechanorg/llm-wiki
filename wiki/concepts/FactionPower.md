Metric measuring overall faction strength in WorldAI Faction Management. Calculated from army (soldiers × 1.0, spies × 0.5, elites × 3.0+), territory (5 FP/acre), fortifications (1000 FP each), citizens (0.1), gold (0.001), arcana (0.01), and prestige (100). Used for ranking position calculation.
