## Definition
Strategic game system in WorldAI where players manage factions with resources including citizens, gold, and arcana.

## Resource Types
1. **Citizens** — Population that generates tax revenue and can be recruited as units
2. **Gold** — Currency for buildings and unit recruitment
3. **Arcana** — Magic resource for spellcasting and magical buildings
