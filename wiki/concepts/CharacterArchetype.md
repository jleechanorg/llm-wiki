## Definition
Classic dramatic role pattern for characters (e.g., Cynical Detective, Reluctant Hero, Wise Mentor). Provides starting point for character construction while allowing individual variation.

## Usage in RPGs
- Foundation for NPC development
- Combined with unique quirks, habits, and backstory
- Expressed through behavior, not labels
