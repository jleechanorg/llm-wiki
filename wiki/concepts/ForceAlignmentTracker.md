## Description

The Force Alignment Tracker is a core mechanical element tracking Luke's moral progression between the light and dark sides of the Force. This creates dynamic storytelling where player choices affect the character's powers and story arc.

## In This Campaign

Luke has "light side affinity but dark tendencies, with a secret sadistic side." The Force Alignment Tracker monitors this balance, potentially granting access to different abilities based on alignment and creating narrative tension around his moral choices.

## Significance

The tracker creates:
- Mechanical consequences for narrative choices
- Narrative tension between heroic origins and corruptible nature
- Exploration of the grey area between Jedi and Sith
