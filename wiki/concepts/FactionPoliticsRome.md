## Overview
Faction Politics Rome is the factional power landscape of the Aurelius Caesar V2 campaign, set in 40 BCE Italy during the late Roman Republic. The campaign features Aurelius as a Lawful Good Paladin fighting against slavery, navigating the complex web of political factions including the Roman Republic establishment, Spartacus's rebel army, and the lingering legacy of the Third Servile War.

## Key Details
- **Category:** Setting / Faction System
- **Campaign(s):** [[aurelius-caesar-v2-campaign]]
- **Setting:** 40 BCE Italy, Roman Republic

## Key Factions
- **Roman Republic:** The establishment Aurelius opposes on moral grounds. As a Lawful Good anti-slavery Paladin, he is a target of the Republic's elite.
- **Spartacus Rebels:** The slave army faction, remnants of the Third Servile War. Aurelius's anti-slavery stance puts him in a complex political position relative to this faction.
- **Roman Elite:** Benefited by slavery, hostile to Aurelius's crusade.
