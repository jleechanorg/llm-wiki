## Overview
The Rite of Thorns is a sacred druidic ritual performed at the Emerald Grove, particularly at the Sacred Pool in the Shattered Sanctum. In V6, Nocturne targets this ritual for inversion/siphoning, intending to drain its power for her own ascension rather than simply destroying the druids.
