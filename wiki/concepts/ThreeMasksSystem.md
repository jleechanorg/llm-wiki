The Three Masks System is the class progression mechanic that defines Alexiel's character development in this campaign. This unique system allows her to embody different aspects or "masks" that she wears in different situations: Prodigal Sword (direct combatant), Reluctant Champion (defensive protector), and Joyful Predator (aggressive hunter). Each mask provides different abilities and modifies her playstyle, allowing dynamic character development that reflects her complex identity as bothLucifer's daughter and a being with hidden magical potential.
