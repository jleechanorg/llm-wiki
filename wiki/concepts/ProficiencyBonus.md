## Summary
A D&D 5e mechanic where characters receive a bonus to checks for skills and tools they are proficient in. The bonus is determined by character level: +2 for levels 1-4, +3 for 5-8, +4 for 9-12, +5 for 13-16, +6 for 17-20.

## Key Details
- Calculated from character level (not class level individually)
- Applies to attack rolls, saving throws, and skill checks
- The stats_display module calculates it via get_proficiency_bonus with proper coercion and clamping
