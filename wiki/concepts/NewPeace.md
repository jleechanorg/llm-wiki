## Overview
The New Peace is the current era in the World of Assiah, beginning after the [[TreatyOfNexus]] ended the [[CelestialWars]].

## Timeline
- Year 10, Kythorn Day 1: Battle of Sacrifice Fields - [[Alexiel]] dies saving [[SarielValArtorius]]
- Year 11, Kythorn Day 1: First anniversary of Alexiel's death
- Year 11, Kythorn Day 2: Current campaign start
