## Definition
D&D 5e mechanic where certain conditions grant advantage (roll two d20, take higher) or disadvantage (roll two d20, take lower). Cannot have both simultaneously — advantage cancels disadvantage.

## Display Requirements
When rolling with advantage or disadvantage, the AI must:
- Show both dice results
- Indicate which die was used for the final result
- Clearly state "Advantage: taking higher" or "Disadvantage: taking lower"
