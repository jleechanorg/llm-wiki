## Description
Eight-tier categorical system labeling NPC attitudes toward the player, derived from [[Trust Level Scale]] values.

## Disposition Hierarchy

1. **Hostile** (-10 to -7) — Active opposition, may attack, spreads rumors
2. **Antagonistic** (-6 to -4) — Refusal, premium pricing, misinformation
3. **Cold** (-3 to -1) — Minimal cooperation, suspicious, no risks taken
4. **Neutral** (0) — Standard behavior, no special treatment
5. **Friendly** (+1 to +3) — Helpfulness, fair deals, shares information
6. **Trusted** (+4 to +6) — Proactive help, discounts, warnings
7. **Devoted** (+7 to +9) — Personal risks, secrets, defends reputation
8. **Bonded** (+10) — Family treatment, sacrifices, deepest secrets

## Usage
Determines base behavior for [[Relationship Behavior Modifiers]]. Disposition changes require [[Relationship Update Triggers]] to fire after significant player actions.
