## Overview
DM Mode vs Story Mode is a conceptual framework in the Sariel Assiah campaign that creates a meta-narrative about the nature of the campaign itself. It represents the tension between the game mechanics (DM Mode) and the narrative/story (Story Mode).

## Framework
- **DM Mode:** Mechanics-focused play — stats, dice, rules
- **Story Mode:** Narrative-focused play — character arcs, plot
- **Hybrid:** Both modes inform each other

## Significance
This meta-concept reflects the campaign's themes of hidden identity. Just as Sariel hides her true nature, the campaign itself has layers between what the characters know and what the players engage with.
