## Summary
The collection, storage, use, and sharing of personal information by WorldAI, governed by their Privacy Policy.

## Description
Personal information processing refers to how WorldAI accesses, collects, stores, uses, and/or shares user data when interacting with their services. Processing occurs only when there is a valid legal reason, and users have rights to exercise regarding their data.

## Key Aspects
- **Collection** — Information users disclose when visiting or using services
- **Processing Purposes** — Provide, improve, and administer Services; communicate with users; security and fraud prevention; legal compliance
- **Legal Bases** — Various legal bases relied upon for processing (detailed in Privacy Policy Section 3)
