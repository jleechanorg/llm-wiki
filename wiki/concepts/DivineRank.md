## Description
Level-based divine power tier system. Scales from Mortal (L1-20) through Epic Mortal (21-25) to Quasi-Deity (26-30), Demigod (31-35), Minor God (36-40), Lesser Deity (41-45), Intermediate Deity (46-50), and Greater Deity (51+). Each rank provides increasing bonuses to AC, Attack, Saves, Checks, and Spell DCs, plus cumulative immunities.
