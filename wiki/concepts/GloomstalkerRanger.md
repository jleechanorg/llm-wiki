## Description

Gloomstalker is a Ranger subclass from Xanathar's Guide to Everything, specializing in combat within darkness and shadows. These rangers are drawn to the darkness itself, becoming nearly invisible in dim light and gaining enhanced abilities when fighting in darkness.

## In This Campaign

Sariel is a Level 5 Gloomstalker Ranger in the original campaign. This subclass fits her "Blood Dragon" identity — a deadly operative who operates in the shadows. The Gloomstalker's features include:

- **Dread Ambusher:** Extra damage on first attack in combat
- **Umbral Sight:** Cannot be seen by creatures relying on darkvision while in darkness
- **Iron Mind:** Proficiency in Wisdom saving throws
- **Stalker's Flair:** Can impose disadvantage on enemies

## Significance

The Gloomstalker subclass represents Sariel's identity as an operative who works in darkness — both literally (tactical operations) and metaphorically ( morally gray world of serial killer hunting). Her 44 HP and WIS 16 (+3) make her a capable combatant and survivalist.
