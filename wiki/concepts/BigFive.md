## Definition
Psychological model describing five major dimensions of personality: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism (OCEAN). Used in deep character frameworks for significant NPCs.

## Application
- Provides measurable psychological depth
- "Twist" column shows personal expression of each trait
- Alternative to MBTI for character development
