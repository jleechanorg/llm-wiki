## Definition
A class feature (Bard, Rogue) that doubles the proficiency bonus for specific skills.

## Formula
```
Expertise Check = d20 + Ability Modifier + (Proficiency Bonus × 2)
```

## Example (Level 1 Bard, CHA 16, Expertise in Deception)
- Regular Deception: d20 + 3 + 2 = d20 + 5
- With Expertise: d20 + 3 + 4 = d20 + 7

## Class Availability
- **Bard**: 2 skills at 1st level, more at higher levels
- **Rogue**: 2 skills at 1st level, more at higher levels
