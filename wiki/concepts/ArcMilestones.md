## Definition
Arc milestones are narrative progress markers that track completion of major story arcs within a campaign. Each milestone includes status (completed/in-progress), completion timestamp, and optional phase information.

## Use Cases
- Tracking main quest progression (e.g., "wedding_tour", "faction_quest")
- Conditional narrative branching based on arc completion
- Persistence of narrative progress across sessions
