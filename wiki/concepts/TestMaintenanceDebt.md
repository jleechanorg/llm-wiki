---
title: "Test Maintenance Debt"
type: concept
tags: [testing, debt, technical-debt]
sources: [test-organization-improvements]
last_updated: 2026-04-08
---

Tests that fail due to outdated expectations rather than actual bugs in production code. Occurs when codebase evolves but tests aren't updated to match new architecture. In this case, tests referenced archived constants (Calibration, Destiny systems) that were removed or integrated into other features.
