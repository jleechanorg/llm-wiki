## Summary
Visual and interactive improvements to card components including hover lift animations, glass morphism backgrounds, and accent border highlighting.

## Key Features
- **Hover Lift**: `transform: translateY(-4px)`
- **Shadow**: Elevated shadow on hover via `var(--shadow-elevated)`
- **Border**: Accent color border on hover
- **Glass Background**: `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`
