## Definition
A d20 roll to determine whether a weapon or spell attack hits its target. Compares result against the target's Armor Class (AC).

## Formula
```
Attack Roll = d20 + Ability Modifier + Proficiency Bonus (if proficient)
```

## Resolution
- **Hit**: Roll meets or exceeds target's AC
- **Miss**: Roll falls below target's AC
- **Critical**: Natural 20 on d20 (automatic hit, double damage dice)
