## Overview
Malcanthet Patron is the relationship mechanic where Malcanthet replaced Zariel as Nocturne's infernal patron after inverting the Cassalanter pact. This grants "Malcanthet's Favor (Plot Armor)" — instead of falling unconscious at 0 HP, Nocturne becomes Incorporeal and drops to 1 HP.
