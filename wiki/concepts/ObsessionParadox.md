## Summary
The Obsession Paradox is a core mechanic of the Apex Weaver class in the Visenya V6 campaign. It describes the psychological state of NPCs who have been fully converted by Visenya's Static field — they become fanatically devoted instruments of her will, competing with each other for her exclusive favor rather than pursuing their own goals.

## Mechanics
- **Activation**: When an NPC's Social HP reaches 0 through Visenya's manipulation, they enter the "Obsession Paradox" state
- **Effect**: The NPC becomes a "fanatical instrument" of Visenya's will, finding "the screams of their former allies the most beautiful song ever sung"
- **Jealousy Spikes**: In late campaign, the Paradox escalates to "Competitive" tier — converted NPCs compete for exclusive presence; if Visenya favors one, the other autonomously sabotages the other's valued variables
- **Competitive Tier Example**: Robb and Jon (both Level 8) compete for the "Sun"; Jon kills a Bolton officer Robb relies on, or Robb "cleanses" a spy Jon was using

## Examples in Campaign
- **Robb Stark**: Becomes a "boy unspooled," lying to his father to protect Visenya's secrets; eventually kills a Karstark captain and participates in Ned's parricide
- **Jon Snow/Jaehaerys**: Full surrender — calls himself "the song," wears the Crown of Roses, declares "I would be your sword, Elyse"
- **Shadow Knights**: A unified force of fanatically devoted knights (Robb + Jon) who move in synchronization
