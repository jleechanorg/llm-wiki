## Definition
D&D moral framework mapping characters on two axes: Good-Evil (moral intent) and Lawful-Chaotic (order vs freedom). Starting moral framework that can evolve through story events.

## Application in Character Profiles
- Marked with 🚨 INTERNAL: prefix in profile templates
- Never appears in character descriptions, dialogue, or narrative
- Character behavior expresses alignment through actions, not labels
