The Celestial Imperium is the Eastern divine order faction in the fractured world of Assiah, representing the established celestial hierarchy that maintains order through divine authority. This faction controls Aeterna, the geometric wonder capital, and represents the "correct" celestial authority in opposition to the Unchained Host rebellion. The Imperium operates through a complex system of divine rules and maintains strict order in their territories. They are the primary target of the Nullification Field ability.
