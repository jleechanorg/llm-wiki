Progression system in WorldArchitect.AI representing the cosmic scale of a player's campaign. Three tiers:


| Tier | Description | Upgrade Trigger |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| mortal | Default campaign, local scale | divine_potential ≥ 100, level ≥ 25 |
| divine | Cosmic-scale campaign | universe_control ≥ 70 |
| sovereign | Multiversal-scale campaign | (achieved from divine) |

See [[CampaignDivineMultiverseUpgradeDetectionLogic]] for detection logic.
