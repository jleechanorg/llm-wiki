## Overview
Post-War Reconstruction is the central theme of the Nocturne Post BG3 Zhent campaign. Following the Absolute's defeat, Baldur's Gate faces massive challenges: refugee crises from Elturel's fall, economic disruption, and power vacuum.

## Elements
- **Refugee Crisis:** Elturel's descent created displaced populations
- **Criminal Expansion:** The Guild and Zhentarim exploit the chaos
- **Urban Decay:** Rivington and lower city areas needing rebuilding

## Significance
The campaign uses this theme to explore noir elements — moral ambiguity, opportunistic crime, and survival in a city that "won" the war but still suffers.
