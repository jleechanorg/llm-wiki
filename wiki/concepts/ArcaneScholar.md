## Definition
Arcane Scholar is the fantasy theme for the application, featuring a deep navy-purple palette with gold accents. It creates a mystical, magical aesthetic appropriate for D&D-style campaigns.

## Theme characteristics
- **Background**: #0d0f1f (deep navy-purple)
- **Primary color**: #d4a843 (gold)
- **Card surface**: #161a35 (slightly lighter navy)
- **Accent glow**: Purple-tinted shadows

## Context in this source
The `[data-theme='fantasy']` selector activates this theme, overriding all color tokens while preserving the design token structure.
