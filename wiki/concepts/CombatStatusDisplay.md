Combat Status Display is the UI/feedback mechanism showing combatant resources during combat. The fix makes this a CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - status block must be displayed at START of EVERY round.

## Required Information by Combatant Type
- **PC**: Name, level, HP, AC, actions remaining, turn indicator
- **Allies**: Name, type, HP, AC, status
- **Enemies**: Name, CR, HP, AC, status

## Status Indicators
- [OK], [Wounded], [Bloodied], [Critical], [DEFEATED], [ACTIVE TURN]
