## Overview
Succubus Corruption is the mechanics framework in the V5-succubus campaigns representing how Nocturne's connection to Malcanthet and the Abyss affects her progression and abilities. It involves the Thrall system, Pheromone manipulation, and gradual transformation toward abyssal power.
