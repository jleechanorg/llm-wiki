## Description

"Glass Cannon" is a character archetype describing someone with high offensive capability but low durability. In D&D terms, this typically means high damage dealing or spellcasting power combined with low HP and armor class, requiring careful positioning and protection to survive.

## In This Campaign

Gaia Julia is explicitly described as a "Glass Cannon" — she wields "beauty, brilliance, and a hidden blade with equal lethality" but has low physical defenses (HP 19, CON 8, AC 15). Her stats reflect this: INT 20 (+5) and CHA 18 (+4) for powerful spellcasting and social manipulation, but STR 8 (-1) and CON 8 (-1) for minimal physical resilience.

## Significance

The Glass Cannon archetype defines Gaia's playstyle — she must rely on her Praetorian Hand guards for protection while using her intellect and charisma to control encounters from a distance or behind the scenes. She cannot afford to be caught in direct combat.
