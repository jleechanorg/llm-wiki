The 70% Rule is the foundational political premise of the Gaia Julia V2 campaign (The Golden Republic / Pax Julia Alternate). It describes the Julian family's total dominance over Rome - controlling approximately 70% of the city's power, wealth, and political influence.

## Political Structure

The "70% Rule" establishes a world where:
- The Julian family is the dominant force in Roman politics
- 70% of Rome's resources flow through Julian-controlled channels
- The remaining 30% is contested among rival factions
- "Building a power base in blood" is the explicit campaign goal

This creates a setting where Gaia Julia Caesar, as a scion of the Julian family, operates from a position of immense but not absolute power. There are still rivals to overcome, other families to manipulate, and political battles to win.

## In the Campaign

In Gaia Julia V2, the 70% Rule defines the starting position:
- Gaia is a Level 12 Imperator (gestalt Paladin/Sorcerer)
- She has 100,000 gold and 20 elite disguised guards ("The Twenty")
- Her equipment includes legendary artifacts (Mors Celeris, Shadow of the Palatine, Aegis of the Eagle, Heart of Venus)
- The campaign goal is to expand Julian influence through whatever means necessary

The "low-magic gritty realism" setting contrasts with Gaia's exceptional power, creating dramatic tension between her immense abilities and the grounded Roman political landscape.
