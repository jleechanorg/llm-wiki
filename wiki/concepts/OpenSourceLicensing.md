Legal framework governing redistribution and modification of open-source software. Covers permissions (use, modify, redistribute), conditions (attribution, license preservation), and limitations (no warranty, no liability).

## License Families
- Permissive: BSD, MIT, Apache
- Copyleft: GPL, LGPL, MPL
