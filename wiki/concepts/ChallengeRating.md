D&D 5.1 system for rating creature difficulty. CR values in the SRD mapping range from 0.125 (Guard) to 8.0 (Assassin).

## CR Scale in Unit Mapping
- **0.125**: Guard (basic soldier)
- **0.5**: Scout (recon)
- **1.0**: Spy (infiltration)
- **3.0**: Veteran, Knight (elite soldiers)
- **5.0**: Gladiator (warlord-tier)
- **8.0**: Assassin (highest-tier covert)

## Usage in Faction System
CR values feed into [[FactionPowerRankingsSystem]] calculations for army strength estimation and combat resolution.
