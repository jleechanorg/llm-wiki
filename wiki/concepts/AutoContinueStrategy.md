Auto-continue strategy is a workflow pattern where the system automatically selects option 1 after exhausting provided prompts. Used for continuing campaign interactions when no more player inputs are available.

## Definition
"Always pick option 1 after exhausting provided prompts"

## Usage
Applied in the Sariel campaign example to continue interactions when prompt sequence completes.
