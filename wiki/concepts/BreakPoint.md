## Definition
Psychological framework for significant NPCs defining what breaks a character under pressure. Four components:
- **Catalyst**: What challenges core beliefs
- **Fractures**: What breaks under pressure
- **Immediate Cost**: Negative aftermath reaction
- **Liberation**: Potential growth from overcoming

## Usage
- Creates compelling narrative moments
- Defines character arc turning points
- Provides DM/GM intervention hooks
