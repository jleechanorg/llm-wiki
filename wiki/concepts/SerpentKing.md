## Overview
The Serpent King is a backstory concept for Nocturne in the Old Nocturne Merc BG3 campaign. It represents a philosophical evil identity — a serial killer who commits dark acts with deliberate meaning rather than chaotic cruelty.

## Philosophy
> "Not evil for evil's sake. Evil for *meaning*."

The Serpent King approach to evil is purposeful:
- **Intentional:** Every act has deliberate purpose
- **Meaningful:** Evil serves a goal or philosophy
- **Controlled:** No random cruelty, only strategic darkness

## Significance
This backstory adds moral complexity to the campaign. Rather than a chaotic villain, Nocturne operates with a code — dark, but comprehensible.
