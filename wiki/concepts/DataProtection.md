## Summary
Organizational and technical processes and procedures in place to protect personal information from unauthorized access, collection, storage, use, and sharing.

## Description
Data protection refers to the measures WorldAI implements to keep personal information safe. While no electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure, adequate organizational and technical processes are in place.

## Key Measures
- Organizational security procedures
- Technical security processes
- Protection against hackers, cybercriminals, and unauthorized third parties
