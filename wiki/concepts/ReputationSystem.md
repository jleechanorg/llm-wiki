## Description
Dual-layer reputation tracking for NPCs:

- **Public Reputation**: -100 to +100, visible to all
- **Private Faction Reputation**: -10 to +10 per faction, known only to faction members

## Usage
CHECK before interacting with new NPCs, UPDATE after witnessed deeds.

## Priority
Private reputation beats public (direct experience > hearsay).
