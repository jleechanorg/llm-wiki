## Overview

The Death Cleric is a custom D&D 5e class created collaboratively for the Shadow Heart campaign, based on Shadowheart from Baldur's Gate 3. It is a Cleric subclass focused on necrotic divine magic, the manipulation of death, and powers drawn from Shar—the goddess of darkness, loss, and forgetting.

Unlike standard Cleric builds, the Death Cleric in this campaign has been heavily modified to incorporate martial prowess (Extra Attack from the Fighter class) and soul-harvesting abilities that emphasize the necrotic aspect of the Death domain.

## Custom Class Design

The Death Cleric class was built through a collaborative character creation process:

### Step 1: Class Foundation
Shadowheart selected the "Custom Class" path to fully integrate her unique Level 12 abilities into D&D 5e mechanics.

### Step 2: Martial Integration (Extra Attack)
Shadowheart chose the **Martial Cleric** path—granting the standard Fighter's Extra Attack feature (two attacks when taking the Attack action), allowing Shadowheart to function as both divine caster and martial combatant.

### Step 3: Soul Harvest Path
Shadowheart selected powers centered on draining life force, manipulating souls, and empowering herself through necrotic energy. This path emphasizes the Death Cleric aspect over healing or support.

## Shar's Divine Power

As a Chosen of Shar, Shadowheart's Death Cleric abilities are granted through divine favor. Shar's portfolio includes darkness, loss, and forgetting—themes that inform the necrotic nature of the class's powers.
