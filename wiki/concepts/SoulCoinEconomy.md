## Overview
The Soul Coin Economy is the system of soul-powered currency in Baldur's Gate, powered by souls harvested from "ruined nobility" through infernal pacts. The Cassalanter Family uses Soul Coins as their primary wealth source, bottling noble souls as "vintage currency" for their infernal dealings with Zariel.
