Isabelle is a generic proof assistant developed at the University of Cambridge. It provides a logical framework (Higher-Order Logic, HOL) and is known for its readable proof documents. Isabelle is widely used in academic formal verification projects.

## Key Properties
- **Generic framework**: Supports multiple object logics (HOL, ZFC, etc.)
- **Isar proof language**: Produces human-readable proof documents
- **Academic standard**: Used extensively in academic verification research
- **Isabelle/HOL**: The most widely used instantiation of Isabelle
