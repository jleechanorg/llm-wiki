## Overview
Siren's Leverage is a signature trait of Nocturne Sosuke that provides a +2 bonus to Charisma checks (Deception and Persuasion) against humanoids who are sexually or romantically attracted to females. This makes her a "porcelain weapon" built for social combat in the Nocturne campaigns.
