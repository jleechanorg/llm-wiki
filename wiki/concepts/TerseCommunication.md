Communication style preference: brief, direct, no filler words. User explicitly chose "terse" response style.

Patterns:
- Numbered menu responses (1, 2, 3) over freeform text
- Minimal messages, straight to the point
- No performatively helpful phrases ("Great question!", "I'd be happy to help!")
- Short confirmations like "terse", "keep"
