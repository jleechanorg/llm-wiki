## Description
Derived power metric: Highest Ability Modifier + Divine Rank Bonus. Enables reality manipulation at Layer 2 (The Source). Example: Level 38 Minor God with 22 Wisdom (+6 mod) has Divine Leverage of +9 (+6 WIS + 3 Rank).
