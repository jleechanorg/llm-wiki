## Definition
The six ability scores in D&D 5e: Strength (STR), Dexterity (DEX), Constitution (CON), Intelligence (INT), Wisdom (WIS), and Charisma (CHA). Each character has a score between 1-30 that determines their abilities.

## Calculation
- **Ability Modifier**: `(score - 10) // 2`
  - Score 10 = +0 modifier
  - Score 14 = +2 modifier
  - Score 20 = +5 modifier
