## Description
Practice of selectively including or excluding specific instruction types in agent prompts. DialogAgent filters out mechanics, combat, and SRD instructions to maintain focus on character dialog.

## Filtered Categories
- Mechanics instructions
- Combat instructions
- SRD (System Reference Document) content


## Purpose
- Maintain dialog focus
- Reduce prompt complexity
- Improve response relevance for conversation scenarios
