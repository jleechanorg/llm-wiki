Sanctuary Mode is an AUTONOMOUS detection system that activates when a quest/mission or story arc is completed. It is triggered by contextual evaluation, NOT keyword matching. The system evaluates game state and narrative context to determine completion.

**Activation**: Contextual evaluation of quest/arc completion
