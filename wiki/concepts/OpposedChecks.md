## Definition
A dice roll competition where two parties each roll and compare results. Both rolls, modifiers, and totals must be displayed, with the winner declared based on higher total.

## Examples
- Athletics vs Athletics (grappling)
- Deception vs Insight (lying detection)
- Stealth vs Perception (hiding vs noticing)

## Display Format
```
Party A: Roll = 1d20+modA = [dieA]+modA = TotalA
Party B: Roll = 1d20+modB = [dieB]+modB = TotalB
Result: Party A/B wins (higher total)
```
