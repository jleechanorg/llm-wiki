## Definition
Multi-layer concealment system allowing nascent deities to hide their true power from pantheon detection. Required for survival in [[DivineAscensionCeremony]].

## Layers
1. **Layer 0 - The Mask**: Mortal identity, mortal physics apply
2. **Layer 1 - The Persona**: Fabricated deity identity with church and myth (unlocked at Tier 2)
3. **Layer 2 - The Source**: True cosmic self, revealed only on "Drop the Mask"
## Key Mechanic
Using divine power while wearing the Mortal Mask creates **Divine Dissonance** — detectable by pantheon entities.
