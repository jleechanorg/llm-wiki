## Overview

Soul Harvest is the chosen power path for Shadowheart's custom Death Cleric class in the Shadow Heart campaign. It represents a focus on draining life force, manipulating souls, and empowering Shadowheart through necrotic energy—emphasizing the Death Cleric's necrotic aspect over standard Cleric abilities.

## Power Theme

The Soul Harvest path centers on:
- **Life Force Drain:** Extracting vitality from enemies and creatures
- **Soul Manipulation:** Directly interacting with and controlling souls
- **Necrotic Empowerment:** Converting harvested life force into personal power and abilities

## Campaign Context

Shadowheart selected the Soul Harvest path during character creation (Scene 3), rejecting alternative approaches to the Extra Attack feature in favor of a fully necrotic-themed build. This choice reinforces the Death Cleric's identity as a servant of Shar, goddess of darkness and loss.

The path represents the culmination of Shadowheart's journey as a Chosen of Shar, continuing the evil narrative arc established in Baldur's Gate 3 where she embraced Shar's dark theology.
