## Definition
D&D 5e method for generating ability scores using 27 points distributed across six attributes.

## Rules
- Scores range from 8 to 15 before racial bonuses are applied
- Each score has an associated point cost:
  - 8 = 0 points
  - 9 = 1 point
  - 10 = 2 points
  - 11 = 3 points
  - 12 = 4 points
  - 13 = 5 points
  - 14 = 7 points
  - 15 = 9 points
- Total cost must not exceed 27 points

## Usage in Character Creation
Used in [[CharacterCreationLevelUpMode]] as one of three ability score generation options alongside [[StandardArray]] and custom assignment.
