## Definition
Character creation method where the LLM generates a complete character sheet with all 6 ability scores, HP/AC, skills, equipment, and background. Part of the Game Mechanics Protocol for D&D 5e character creation.

## Key Requirements
- Complete character sheet (name, race, class, level, all 6 ability scores with modifiers, HP/AC, skills, equipment, background)
- 2-3 sentence "Why this character" justification tying to campaign
- Banned-name precheck before proposing any name
- End with approval triad (PlayCharacter / MakeChanges / StartOver)
