## Overview

The Aizen Bg3 campaigns are dark psychological noir campaigns set in post-war Baldur's Gate following the defeat of the Absolute. Sosuke Aizen operates from the shadows as a teen prodigy mastermind, running The White Garden as both an elite brothel and a front for harvesting secrets. The campaigns explore themes of manipulation, hypnosis, and slow-burn political takeovers.

## Campaign Variations

### Aizen Bg3 (Original)
- Player is Level 4 Wizard (Hogyoku)
- Lord Garton Irlingstar is the primary target
- Focus on hypnosis and secret harvesting

### Aizen Bg3 V2 (Revised)
- Player is Level 4 Bard
- Lirael Voss is the primary target
- Expanded network of assets (Kaname, Zylra, Ginora)
- More political intrigue

## Game Mechanics

- **Character Classes:** Wizard (Hogyoku), Bard
- **Levels:** 4
- **Play Modes:** God Mode enabled
- **Key Abilities:** Complete hypnosis, strategic vice, secret manipulation
- **Operation:** The White Garden / The Gardener persona

## Key Themes

- **Psychological Manipulation:** Breaking will rather than brute force
- **Hidden Identity:** Public orphan ward vs secret mastermind
- **Power Vacuum:** Post-war Baldur's Gate
- **Class Struggle:** Upper City spires vs Lower City gutters
