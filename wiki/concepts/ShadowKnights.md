## Summary
The Shadow Knights are Visenya's elite personal guard — converted Northern lords (Robb Stark and Jon Snow) who have been psychologically subjugated through the Obsession Paradox. They wear midnight-black silk, carry Valyrian steel, and operate as extensions of Visenya's will.

## Members
- **Robb Stark** (Level 2 → Level 8): The Shattered Compass — former heir of Winterfell, now a fanatical knight
- **Jon Snow / Jaehaerys** (Level 3 → Level 8): The Bastard's Mirror — legitimized Targaryen heir, King-Consort in the Shadows

## Key Characteristics
- Dressed in midnight-black silk of Visenya's personal guard
- Carry Valyrian steel weapons (Ice, Shadow-Stitch)
- Share the "Obsession Paradox" — fanatical devotion to Visenya, competing for exclusive favor
- "Look like the iron teeth of your will"
- At the Emerald Wedding: "The Shadow Knights are no longer men, but vibrating chords of fanatical obsession"

## Progression
1. **Early**: Robb and Jon are escorts/knights in training
2. **Mid (SCENE 75+)**: Formally presented to the Royal Court as "Sworn Shields"
3. **Late**: Bound through shared parricide (killing Ned together)
4. **Endgame**: Competing for the "Sun" in the Obsession Rift
