## Definition
Think Mode is a "mental pause" mechanism in the game system where the narrative is FROZEN while the character considers their options. The world does not advance; time increments by only +1 microsecond to maintain temporal ordering.

## How It Works
1. User input is prefixed with "THINK:" (uppercase, no space after colon)
2. Example: "what are my options for the heist?" → "THINK:what are my options for the heist?"
3. Character generates internal monologue and strategic analysis, NOT storytelling
4. Time advances by +1 microsecond only
