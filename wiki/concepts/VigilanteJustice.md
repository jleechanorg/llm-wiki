## Description

Vigilante Justice is a central theme in the Hunting Party Sariel V2 campaign, where the 16-year-old Sariel operates as a masked vigilante alongside her official FBI role. The theme explores the moral complexity of justice administered outside official channels.

## In This Campaign

Sariel's V2 version is an FBI prodigy who secretly operates as a vigilante with 100 kills. This creates a fundamental tension: she represents the law (FBI agent) while also being outside the law (masked executioner). The campaign takes place in "The Pit" — a lawless zone where vigilante operations are practical.

## Significance

Vigilante Justice as a theme raises questions about:
- When is extrajudicial killing justified?
- What separates a hero from a serial killer?
- Can the system be reformed, or must it be circumvented?
