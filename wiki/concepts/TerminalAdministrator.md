## Definition
The PC's role in [[Sovereign Protocol]] — a God-Tier Entity commanding a portion of the multiverse after completing [[Sovereign Ascension Ceremony]].

## Starting Position
- **Rank:** 801-1000 (Administrator tier)
- **SP:** 1 (1,000 Universes)
- **Aggro:** 8 (Lethal)
- **Domain:** Minimal — must expand through Logic Sieges

## Progression Path
1. Administrator (Rank 101-1000): Build SP through conquest
2. Conductor (Rank 11-100): Achieve galactic influence
3. Emperor (Rank 1-10): Join multiversal overlords
4. Entropy Reversal: Rewrite thermodynamic laws
