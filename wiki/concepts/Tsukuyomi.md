## Description

Tsukuyomi is one of the two great Mangekyō Sharingan techniques, considered the most powerful genjutsu (illusion technique) in existence. It creates a separate dimension where the user controls time and space, allowing them to torture victims mentally for what feels like days or weeks in mere seconds of real time.

## In This Campaign

Tsukuyomi is Itachi's preferred Mangekyō ability. It represents his cold, intellectual approach to combat — rather than raw destruction (Amaterasu) or defensive power (Susano'o), he prefers to break opponents psychologically.

- **Activation:** Primary Action (2 CP), spend 8 EP
- **Effect:** Target must succeed on DC 15 Wisdom (Willpower) check or be trapped in illusion for up to 1 round per Itachi's INT modifier (minimum 1 round)
- **Damage:** Target takes 8d4 Psychic damage
- **Cost:** 1 level of Fatigue per use

## Significance

Tsukuyomi represents Itachi's evil perfectly — it's not about killing opponents but breaking them. The psychological damage combined with the physical toll makes it his signature technique.
