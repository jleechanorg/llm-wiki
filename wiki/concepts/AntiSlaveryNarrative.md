Anti-Slavery Narrative is the moral theme central to Aurelius Caesar v2 campaign, representing the stance against the institution of slavery in the Roman Republic. This narrative explores the moral implications of slavery as an institution and follows Aurelius's crusade to end it. The campaign draws inspiration from historical events like the Spartacus revolt while exploring more fundamental questions about human rights and dignity. The theme positions Aurelius as a moral crusader in a world where slavery is not only legal but economically essential to the Republic.
