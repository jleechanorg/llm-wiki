## Summary
 tabletop role-playing game system providing character archetypes and campaign settings used for random campaign generation in WorldArchitect.AI. Defines 10 character classes and 10 campaign settings for fallback prompt generation.

## Key Concepts
- **Character Archetypes** — 10 D&D 5e classes: warrior, rogue, wizard, paladin, ranger, bard, cleric, barbarian, monk, druid
- **Campaign Settings** — 10 fantasy settings: Waterdeep, Feywild, Underdark, Icewind Dale, Calimshan, Sword Coast, Barovia, Sharn, Chult, Cyre
- **Fallback Generation** — Random character/setting selection when player provides no input
