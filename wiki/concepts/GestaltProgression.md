Gestalt Progression is a D&D character development system where characters level up in multiple classes simultaneously, combining the abilities of both. In this campaign, Alexiel is a Level 6 gestalt character, combining levels from different classes to gain the benefits of both progression systems. This creates a more powerful and versatile character than a single-class character of the same total level, representing the hybrid nature of Alexiel's identity as both Null and magical prodigy.
