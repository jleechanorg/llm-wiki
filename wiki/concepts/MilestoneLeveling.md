## Definition
Level advancement method where characters level up at narrative milestones rather than accumulating XP. Recommended +1-3 levels per adventure arc.

## Guidelines
- Epic/mythic campaigns may exceed Level 20
- Level ups tied to story progression
- Coordinate with campaign length and scope
