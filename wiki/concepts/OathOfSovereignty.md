Oath of Sovereignty is a Paladin oath representing rule through merit and protection versus tyranny. This oath emphasizes that leaders have a responsibility to protect their people, but that protection must be earned through worthy actions rather than inherited position. Aurelius Julia Caesar V2 follows this oath in his crusade against slavery in the Roman Republic. It differs from Oath of Tyranny in that the paladin seeks to protect rather than dominate, using their power to uplift the oppressed rather than subjugate the weak. The oath represents a middle ground between pure good and pure authority.
