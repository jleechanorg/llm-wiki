The Merchant Cover Mechanics represent Aurelius V3's disguise and deception system operating under the alias Aurelius Varenus. This mechanical framework tracks his merchant identity, the lies he maintains, and the risks of exposure. Success in this system depends on his Bard abilities and the Dignitas System. Maintaining a convincing cover requires ongoing effort and resources. The mechanic adds a layer of political gameplay where Aurelius must balance his covert operations with maintaining his public persona as a respectable merchant.
