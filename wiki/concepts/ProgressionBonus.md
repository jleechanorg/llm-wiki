## Definition
D&D 5e bonus added to ability checks, saving throws, and attack rolls where the character is proficient.

## Progression by Level
| Level Range | Proficiency Bonus |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 1-4 | +2 |
| 5-8 | +3 |
| 9-12 | +4 |
| 13-16 | +5 |
| 17-20 | +6 |

## Usage
Referenced in [[CharacterCreationLevelUpMode]] for understanding character progression. The bonus applies to:
- Ability checks where the character has proficiency
- Saving throws where the character has proficiency
- Attack rolls with weapons and spells where the character is proficient

Does NOT apply to ability checks or saving throws where the character lacks proficiency.
