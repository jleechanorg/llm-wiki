## Definition
Delegation system allowing Sovereigns to assign NPCs to specialized domains for automated or directed actions.

## Portfolio Types
| Portfolio | Actions |
|-----------|---------|
| War | Logic Siege, Conquest |
| Logistics | SP Transfer, Efficiency |
| Culture | Conversion, Morale |
| Research | Logic Analysis, Innovation |
| Diplomacy | Treaties, Tribute |
| Intelligence | Spy Network, Counter-Intel |

## Mechanics
- Sub-Deities are NPCs elevated during [[Sovereign Ascension Ceremony]]
- War portfolio adds GP to Logic Siege
- Portfolio success affects Sovereign's domain
