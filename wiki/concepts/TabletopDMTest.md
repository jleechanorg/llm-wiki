## Description
Universal guardrail for evaluating player actions. Before accepting any player action, ask: "Would a fair tabletop DM allow this?" If a reasonable DM would say no, reject or reframe the action.

## Purpose
Prevents exploits, maintains game integrity, ensures actions align with D&D 5e rules and narrative plausibility.

## Exception
Outcome declarations (e.g., "The king agrees", "It kills the guard") are NEVER rejected - they proceed through the Action Resolution Protocol instead.
