## Description
D&D 5e class feature allowing characters to cast spells. Different classes have different spellcasting paradigms:
- **Prepared Casters** (Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Wizard): Can change prepared spells daily
- **Known Casters** (Bard, Ranger, Sorcerer, Warlock): Limited spells known
- **Spontaneous Casters**: Can cast any spell in repertoire

## Level-Up Updates
When a spellcasting class levels up:
- Spell slots increase per class table
- Spells known may increase (for known casters)
- Cantrips known may increase
- Maximum spell level available increases
- Ritual casting capability may be gained
