## Summary
The Heat System tracks how much attention Visenya's incognito activities are drawing from the Northern establishment. It is a mechanical representation of her cover's integrity and the political danger of her position.

## Levels
| Level | Range | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Low Heat | 0-30 | Ignored as a minor noble; Lady Elyse cover is intact |
| Medium Heat | 31-70 | Northern lords notice her; Ned assigns spies |
| High Heat | 71-100 | Cover blown; North realizes the Blood Dragon is in their home |
| Extreme | 90+ | Immediate hostility or assassination attempts |

## Campaign Progression
- **Entry 001-003**: Low Heat — Visenya arrives as Lady Elyse, minimal suspicion
- **Entry 004-006**: Medium Heat — Ned assigns spies; Catelyn's distrust is noted
- **Entry 007+**: Heat rises as Visenya's manipulations escalate
- **Entry 010 (Emerald Wedding)**: Extreme Heat — the North is aware of the Blood Dragon's presence; Winterfell burns
- **Endgame**: "Regional Heat: Extreme (95)" — the North is in open crisis
