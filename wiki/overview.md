Key tensions include succession questions for twin heirs Cassian and Darius val Artorius, the death of hero Alexiel, and the rise of independent powers including the Tsuran Dominion, Adamant Vault, First-Spear Clans, and Nexus trade hub. Core themes explore divine rebellion consequences, political intrigue among fractured factions, and the struggle between order and chaos.
